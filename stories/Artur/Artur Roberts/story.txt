Artur Roberts was a man with a passion for music that knew no bounds. Born Arthur Edward Roberts on a sunny summer day in 1990, he had always been fascinated by the world of sound and melody. As he grew older, Artur's interest in music only deepened. He began to teach himself how to play the guitar, and soon found himself lost in the world of chords and scales. His parents, both avid music lovers themselves, encouraged his hobby, and Artur spent hours practicing in his room, his fingers moving deftly over the fretboard.

Their first album together was released to critical acclaim, and soon they were playing sold-out shows across the country. Artur Roberts, as he now went by, was hailed as a rising star in the music world. His dedication to his craft was evident on every track, from the soaring ballads to the foot-stomping anthems.
