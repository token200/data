Despite facing fierce resistance from entrenched interests, Artur remained steadfast in his convictions, using his mastery of law to outmaneuver his opponents.

One fateful day, Emperor Gallienus summoned Artur Aurelius Valerius Diocletianus to his chambers for an audience.
