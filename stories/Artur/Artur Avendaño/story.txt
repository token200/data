"Artur Avendaño," the officer whispered, his eyes darting around the room, "I need your help. There's a mole within our department, someone who's been feeding information to the wrong people."

Artur Avendaño's gut told him that this was more than just a simple case of corruption – it was a matter of national security. He knew he had to tread carefully, as one misstep could put his own life in danger.

With the help of an old flame, now a brilliant hacker, Artur Avendaño pieced together the evidence, following a trail of digital breadcrumbs that led them closer and closer to the truth.

Artur Avendaño's face hardened as he confronted his brother, the weight of his failure heavy on his shoulders.
