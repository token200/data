As Marta's skills improved, so did her ambition. Her dedication and hard work earned her a spot in the prestigious Krakow Philharmonic Orchestra, and she was soon performing alongside some of the finest musicians in the country.

But Marta's journey to success wasn't without its challenges. Her coach, Maria Szerykowska, had encouraged her to work on building her confidence and embracing her imperfections, but it was a lesson that Marta still grappled with today.
