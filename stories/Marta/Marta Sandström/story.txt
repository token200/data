Marta Sandström stepped off the train and onto the worn platform, her eyes scanning the sleepy town of Åmål. As she made her way through the quiet streets, the familiar scent of saltwater and pine wafted up, transporting her back to her childhood summers spent with her grandparents.

She felt a sense of peace settle over her, as if she had finally found what she was searching for all along. The sound of seagulls filled the air, and Marta Sandström smiled, feeling the weight of her journey lifting off her shoulders.
