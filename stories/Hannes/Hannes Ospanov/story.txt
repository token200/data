The note was cryptic, but it seemed to hint at a lucrative opportunity that would change his life forever. Intrigued, Hannes accepted the invitation and made his way to the meeting point.

But despite the danger, he couldn't shake the feeling that this was an opportunity too good to pass up. After all, someone with Hannes Ospanov's talents was hard to come by.
