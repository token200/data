Born in Budapest, Hungary, Hannes Toth's love affair with history began at a young age. His father, a Hungarian diplomat, had regaled him with tales of the country's rich heritage and the myths that surrounded it. This sparked a fire within Hannes, driving him to pursue a career in archaeology.

As Hannes Toth basked in the attention, he couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and satisfaction. He had spent years dedicated to this pursuit, and finally, his hard work had paid off.
