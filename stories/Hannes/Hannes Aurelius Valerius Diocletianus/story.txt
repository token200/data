As years went by, Hannes continued to serve the empire with unrelenting loyalty. His unwavering resolve in the face of adversity inspired countless soldiers and civilians alike. He was a symbol of hope for those living under the emperor's reign.

One fateful day, while leading his troops into battle against a rival empire, Hannes Aurelius Valerius Diocletianus fell gravely injured. As he lay on the field, his once unyielding spirit began to wane. With his last breath, he uttered the words " Roma Aeterna" – Rome Eternal.
