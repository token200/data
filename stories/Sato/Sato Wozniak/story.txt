Sato Wozniak was a free-spirited artist with a passion for capturing the beauty of nature on canvas. Born to Japanese parents who had emigrated to Canada, Sato grew up surrounded by the vibrant colors and textures of the Pacific Northwest. As they grew older, Sato's fascination with nature only deepened. They began to explore the forests and mountains surrounding their home, armed with nothing but a sketchbook and a camera. Overjoyed, Sato accepted the opportunity, knowing it would take their art to new heights.
