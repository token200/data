Alessandro Nielsen arrived at the scene, taking in the atmosphere and gathering information from the officers on duty. He noticed that the window was left open, suggesting that the thief had been wearing gloves and was familiar with the building's layout.

As he began his investigation, Alessandro Nielsen interviewed the museum staff, searching for any inconsistencies in their alibis or potential motives. One employee, a quiet young woman named Sophia, seemed particularly nervous during their conversation. Her eyes darted around the room as she spoke about her role in the museum's security system.

Intrigued by Sophia's behavior, Alessandro Nielsen requested to review the security footage. After fast-forwarding through hours of uneventful footage, he spotted a brief glimpse of the thief's hand as they entered and exited the exhibit hall. The image was grainy, but it seemed clear that the thief had worn gloves with an unusual fabric.

Determined to uncover more evidence, Alessandro Nielsen requested access to the museum's storage room where the security system was maintained. There, he discovered a peculiar thread caught in a ventilation grate, matching the description of the glove fabric from the footage.

With this new lead, Alessandro Nielsen began to piece together the events surrounding the jewel heist. He suspected that Sophia might be more involved than she initially let on and decided to keep a close eye on her as his investigation continued. As he sat at his desk, sipping his coffee, Alessandro Nielsen couldn't help but wonder what secrets lay hidden behind Sophia's enigmatic smile.

As the night wore on, Alessandro Nielsen remained focused, driven by his determination to crack the case and uncover the truth about the mysterious thief and their connection to Sophia.
