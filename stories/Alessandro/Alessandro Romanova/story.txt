As the investigation deepened, Alessandro found herself drawn into a world of secrets and lies. But despite the trail of clues, Alessandro couldn't shake the feeling that she was missing something.

Just when she thought she was close to solving the case, a new lead threw everything off balance. It seemed that Sophia's disappearance was just the tip of the iceberg, and that there was a larger conspiracy at play. Alessandro Romanova gritted her teeth, ready to dive headfirst into the fray once more.
