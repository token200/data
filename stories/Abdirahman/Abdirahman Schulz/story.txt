Abdirahman Schulz was a young Somali refugee who had lost his family in a devastating civil war back in Mogadishu. Now, living in Minneapolis, Minnesota, he struggled to adjust to the unfamiliar American culture. Despite facing numerous challenges, Abdirahman persevered and worked hard to educate himself.

He attended night school, determined to earn a high school diploma and eventually secure a better future for himself. The struggles didn't stop there; however, as Abdirahman navigated the complexities of his new life in America, he met individuals who were kind and helpful.
