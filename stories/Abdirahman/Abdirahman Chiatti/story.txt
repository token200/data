As the afternoon wore on, Sophia made her decision – she would purchase the pocket watch and continue learning from Abdirahman Chiatti's vast knowledge. He smiled, sensing a new mentor-mentee relationship had begun.

"Welcome to my world," he said, his eyes twinkling. "Together, we'll uncover secrets hidden for centuries."
