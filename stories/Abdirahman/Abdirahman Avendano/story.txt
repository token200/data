As he delved deeper into his studies, Abdirahman discovered a passion for sustainable design and eco-friendly buildings. He became particularly interested in using locally sourced materials and innovative techniques to minimize waste and reduce carbon footprints.

Abdirahman's big break came when he was commissioned to design a community center in the heart of Minneapolis. The project allowed him to merge his love of Somali culture with his passion for sustainable design, creating a stunning building that would serve as a hub for the local community.

The center's unique design, which featured intricate patterns and geometric shapes inspired by traditional Somali textiles, quickly gained international attention. Abdirahman Avendano's name became synonymous with innovative and culturally sensitive architecture.
