Abdirahman Fontaine's latest exhibition, "Fractured Identities," was a culmination of his life's work. The show featured large-scale installations that explored themes of identity, culture, and belonging. Each piece was a reflection of Abdirahman's own experiences as a Somali-American, as well as the struggles faced by communities around the world.

Abdirahman Fontaine mingled with guests, listening intently as they shared their own stories and perspectives on identity and community.
