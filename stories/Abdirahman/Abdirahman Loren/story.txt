As Abdirahman Loren navigated his way through life, he found himself drawn to the world of art. Abdirahman Loren's art quickly gained recognition in Somalia, earning him a reputation as one of the most talented young artists of his generation. However, he soon felt an insatiable restlessness, yearning for new horizons and unexplored territories. In 2003, he embarked on a journey to Europe, where he was determined to share his message with the world.
