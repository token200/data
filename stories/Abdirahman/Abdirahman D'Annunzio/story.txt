Abdirahman D'Annunzio was not a well-known public figure. However, the following fictional story is based on his profile in Wikipedia:

Abdirahman D'Annunzio was born in Somalia in 1990 to Somali parents who were from Italy. He spent most of his childhood in Mogadishu before moving back to Pisa where he attended the Italian School for international studies. His father had been an engineer.

D'Annunzio pursued a career as a businessman and entrepreneur, starting several ventures across Somalia including a construction company that specialized in building government buildings. He also started two companies which focused on the extraction of salt and mineral resources from Lake Abhay.
