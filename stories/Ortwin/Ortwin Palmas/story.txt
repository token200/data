The night wore on, with Ortwin expertly navigating the intricate web of alliances and rivalries that defined their world. He effortlessly charmed his way through conversations, extracting valuable information from those around him. As a strategist, Ortwin Palmas was unmatched, always thinking several steps ahead.

As the evening drew to a close, Ortwin guided Elara toward the exit, his arm wrapped possessively around her waist.
