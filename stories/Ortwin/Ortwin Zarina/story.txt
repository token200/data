Ortwin Zarina's reputation preceded him, and collectors of rare artifacts sought his expertise, hoping to trade with the enigmatic thief. Some said he was driven by curiosity, others by greed, but one thing was certain: Ortwin Zarina knew where to find the most elusive treasures.

One fateful evening, a wealthy collector named Marcellus invited Ortwin Zarina to a masquerade ball in his grand estate. The guest list read like a roll call of the city's elite, and Marcellus expected Ortwin Zarina to bring a few... "gifts" for the evening's entertainment. As the night wore on, Ortwin Zarina wove through the crowds, his eyes scanning the room for the perfect target.

With a flick of his wrist, he snatched a glittering diamond necklace from around Marcellus's neck, leaving behind only a whispered promise and a mocking smile. The crowd gasped in shock as Ortwin Zarina vanished into the night, his cloak billowing behind him like a dark cloud.

As the hours passed, whispers of the heist spread throughout the city. Some claimed to have spotted Ortwin Zarina, slipping away unnoticed through hidden passageways and secret doors. Others swore they saw him dancing with the moonlight in the city's abandoned gardens, his eyes aglow like lanterns in the darkness.
