This empathetic gift allowed him to navigate the complexities of Eldoria's intricate social hierarchy with remarkable finesse. People from all walks of life would seek his counsel, drawn by the aura of kindness and wisdom that surrounded Ortwin Sarinana.

As the night wore on, Ortwin Sarinana's reputation grew, and his name became synonymous with hope and understanding in a world often shrouded in darkness.
