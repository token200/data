Despite his success, Matteo remained humble and dedicated to his craft, continually seeking new ways to express himself through his art. He spent long hours in his studio, experimenting with different mediums and techniques, always pushing the boundaries of what was possible.

For Matteo Wei (李伟), it was a moment he would never forget – a reminder that his passion for art had not only brought him wealth and fame but also connected him to the people and place he loved.
