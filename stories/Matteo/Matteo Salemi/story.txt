Matteo Alessandro Salemi was a man with a passion for art and music. Growing up in a small town in Italy, he spent most of his childhood surrounded by his family's antique shop, where his father, Giovanni, would often play the guitar while his mother, Sophia, worked on their famous ceramics.

As the years passed, Matteo's passion for music and art continued to grow. He became a successful musician, touring the world and performing with some of the most prestigious orchestras. But he never forgot his roots and the instrument that had changed his life forever - the violin.
