Just as it seemed like the storm would be too much for him to handle, Dr. Peter Allende spotted a group of sea turtles struggling to stay afloat in the churning waters. Without hesitation, he leapt into action, throwing life jackets to the turtles and helping them onto his boat.

As the storm finally began to subside, Dr. Peter Allende couldn't help but feel a sense of satisfaction. It was moments like these that reminded him why he became a marine biologist in the first place: to protect and preserve the incredible creatures that call our oceans home.
