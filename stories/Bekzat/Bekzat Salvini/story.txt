Along the way, she met fellow travelers, musicians, and artists who shared her sense of wonder and curiosity. As Bekzat Salvini wandered through the world, her art began to take shape. One evening, as Bekzat Salvini sat on a hill overlooking a bustling festival scene, she felt a sense of contentment wash over her.
