Sofia, ever the pragmatist, remained steadfast, her gaze fixed on the intricate carvings adorning the temple walls.

With each step, Guillermo Wójcik's excitement grew. He had spent years searching for answers to this very question – what was The Devourer, and why did it hold such significance in ancient mythology? Guillermo Wójcik's eyes locked onto the figure, his mind racing with theories. According to legend, The Devourer was said to be a manifestation of humanity's darkest impulses – a symbol of the abyss that yawned within every human heart.
