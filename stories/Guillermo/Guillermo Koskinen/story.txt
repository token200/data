But as he continued to investigate, he began to uncover some disturbing evidence. It seemed that the warehouse had been used for some kind of twisted experiments, and Guillermo was starting to suspect that the spirits were all too real.

Despite the danger, Guillermo Koskinen refused to back down. He knew that he had stumbled upon something big, and he was determined to see it through to the end. As he delved deeper into the mystery, he couldn't shake the feeling that he was being watched, and that the spirits were closing in.
