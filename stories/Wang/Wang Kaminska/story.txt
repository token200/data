As Li walked home one night, she felt a presence behind her - a cold, calculating gaze that seemed to pierce through the darkness.

She spun around, but there was no one there. The feeling passed, and she quickened her pace. Yet, she couldn't shake off the sense of unease that lingered within her. She wondered if Wang Kaminska really did exist, or if it was just a myth created to frighten children into behaving.

As Li approached her home, she caught a glimpse of something in the window - a flicker of movement, followed by an empty space where there should have been none. It was then that she knew: Wang Kaminska was more than just a legend; he was a force of nature, a harbinger of death and destruction.
