Growing up, she had been fascinated by the stories of her ancestors, who had brought their own instruments and traditions with them from across the Silk Road. She remembered spending hours listening to her grandmother play the guqin, an ancient Chinese zither, and feeling a deep connection to the music.

As the night drew to a close, Wang Xueying took her final bow, basking in the applause and admiration of the crowd.
