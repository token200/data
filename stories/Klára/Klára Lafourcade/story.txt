As she boarded the train to head home, Klára couldn't help but feel grateful for this little slice of heaven in France. She had spent countless hours capturing its beauty, and yet it still held a special place in her heart.

Klára Lafourcade smiled to herself as the train pulled away from the station, already thinking about what she would capture next.
