Her full name, Klára De Rossi, seemed fitting for this endeavor – a blend of her given name, passed down from her parents, and the surname she had adopted, signifying a commitment to social change.

She felt invigorated, ready to face whatever challenges lay ahead. As she disappeared into the fading light, the waves continued their gentle rhythm, a soothing melody that accompanied Klára De Rossi as she embarked on her next creative and activist journey.
