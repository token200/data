Klára nodded, and Ianto handed her a crumpled piece of paper. "This was left on my doorstep last night," he said, eyes serious. "It's all I have."

"In moonlit woods, where shadows play

Klára studied the words, her mind racing with possibilities. She tucked the paper into her bag, determination fueling her step as she left the pub behind.

The night air enveloped her like a cloak, carrying whispers of secrets on its breath. Klára Davies knew that this was just the beginning - the Night Weaver's journey had only just begun, and she was ready to follow it wherever it led. With each step, the mystery deepened, and Klára felt herself drawn into a world of enchantment, where truth and legend blurred like the lines on an old map.

As the night wore on, Klára found herself at the edge of the woods, her heart pounding with anticipation. The poem's words echoed in her mind: "A path unwinds, a journey starts..."
