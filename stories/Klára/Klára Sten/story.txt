Klára Sten began her investigation by interviewing the victim's family members and staff. Each person had an alibi for the time of the murder, but Klára Sten was not convinced. She decided to pay a visit to the mansion's gardener, who seemed particularly nervous during their conversation.

As she dug deeper, Klára Sten discovered that the gardener had been struggling financially and had been in deep debt. But it wasn't until she reviewed the security footage that she found the crucial piece of evidence – a small, hidden compartment in the gardener's toolbox containing a vial of lavender oil.

With newfound determination, Klára Sten set out to rebuild the timeline of events and identify the real culprit.

As she pieced together the evidence, Klára Sten's reputation as a brilliant detective continued to grow.
