The sparks that flew between them were undeniable, but as they gazed into each other's eyes, both knew that their past entanglements would not be so easily forgotten.

Klára Grabowska was faced with a daunting decision – to risk her reputation and her heart on a second chance at love, or to keep moving forward alone, driven by her insatiable need for truth.
