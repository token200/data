Benedetta Szymanski stepped off the train and onto the worn platform, her eyes scanning the small station with a mix of nostalgia and trepidation. Without thinking, Benedetta Szymanski reached into her bag and pulled out a sketchbook, flipping to a page filled with hasty scribbles and loose lines.
