Years went by, and Mathilda's love for art only grew stronger. She pursued a degree in fine arts at Riga University, where she honed her skills and developed a unique style that blended traditional techniques with modern twists.

After completing her studies, Mathilda Pētersone set out to make a name for herself as an artist. She traveled the country, capturing the beauty of Latvia's rural landscapes on canvas. Her paintings were met with critical acclaim, and soon she was in high demand among collectors and museums alike.
