Her editor had been breathing down her neck for days, urging her to get the article done quickly, but Mathilda was undeterred. Finally, after hours of typing, Mathilda hit send on the article. She leaned back in her chair, exhausted but exhilarated by the prospect of sharing her findings with the world. Her full name might not be on every lips, but Mathilda Szymański was a force to be reckoned with – and she knew it.
