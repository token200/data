Mathilda Lace stepped off the train and onto the worn platform, her bright blue eyes scanning the small station for any sign of her grandmother's cottage. She had been searching for weeks, following every lead, every rumor, every hint that might bring her to this tiny village in rural England.

This was where she belonged – in this small village, surrounded by the beauty and wonder of nature.
