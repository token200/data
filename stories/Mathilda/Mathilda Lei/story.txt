With her findings published in a major newspaper, Mathilda's name became synonymous with investigative journalism. Her breakthrough story sparked a national debate about traditional medicine and its potential applications in contemporary healthcare.

As Mathilda Mei-Ling Lei looked back on her journey, she knew that her grandmother's legacy had played a significant role in shaping her career.
