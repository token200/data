Magnus's passion for horses only grew stronger as she spent more time around them, and she quickly became one of the best riders in the village.

The villagers welcomed the travelers, but Magnus's father, sensing potential danger, warned her and her family to be cautious.

Magnus quickly took charge, using her knowledge of the terrain to guide Sophia's horse through the treacherous sand dunes.
