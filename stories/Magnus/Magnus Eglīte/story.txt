As he navigated the dimly lit alleys of the city, his eyes scanned the rooftops for any sign of potential danger. A flicker of movement caught his attention, and with a fluid motion, Magnus Eglīte dropped down from the rooftop, landing softly on the cobblestone street below.

Without hesitation, he set out to gather the necessary tools of his trade: lockpicks, grappling hooks, and a small pouch containing a handful of sedatives. As he made his way to Thorne's mansion, he couldn't shake off the feeling that he was being watched.

"You shouldn't have come here, Magnus Eglīte."
