As Levente grew older, he began to emulate these qualities in his own life. The task was ambitious, but Levente was up for the challenge. He spent countless hours researching and planning, pouring his heart and soul into creating something truly special.

With his guitar in hand, he began to play, weaving together traditional songs with modern beats and rhythms.
