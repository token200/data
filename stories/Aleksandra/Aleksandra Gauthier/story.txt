Aleksandra's fascination with the supernatural began at a young age, when she would spend hours poring over dusty tomes in her local library, devouring tales of magic and the occult. As she grew older, her interest only deepened, leading her to study under some of the most respected masters of the craft.

As the sun dipped below the horizon, Aleksandra closed the book, feeling transformed by what she had discovered.
