The more she thought about it, the more uneasy she became.

The sign above the door read "Madame Luna's Curios and Antiques," and the windows were filled with an assortment of peculiar trinkets and talismans. Zofia García Martínez left the shop feeling renewed and revitalized. She realized that Madame Luna's words had struck a chord within her. The next day, she sent a sternly worded letter to the gallery, declining their offer and choosing instead to follow her own artistic vision.
