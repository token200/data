But with fame came pressure, and Zofia found herself struggling to keep pace with the demands of her growing studio. And yet, as she gazed upon the swirling colors and textures before her, something shifted inside Zofia Liepina's chest. She saw the beauty in the imperfection, the strength in vulnerability.
