But one day, while wandering through the streets of Krakow, he stumbled upon an old jazz club. The sounds of improvisation and spontaneity resonated deeply with him.

Rafael Malinowska had finally found his voice, and it was a beautiful thing to behold. He knew that he still had much to learn, but for now, he was content to let his music speak for itself.
