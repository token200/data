Rafael Ozoliņa stepped into the dimly lit alleyway, his eyes scanning the shadows for any sign of trouble. As a private investigator, he had seen his fair share of shady characters and clandestine meetings, but there was something about this particular case that had him on edge.

But as the figure stepped into the light, Rafael Ozoliņa's instincts were proven wrong – it was just a young woman, dressed in tattered clothing and clutching a small bag to her chest.

Rafael Ozoliņa's expression softened as he took in the woman's desperate gaze. He nodded and offered her a hand up, helping her brush off her clothes.

"Who are you looking for?" he asked gently.

Rafael Ozoliņa's ears perked up at this revelation – he had a feeling that his client might be more involved in the disappearances than they were letting on.
