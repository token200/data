Hashimoto Briedis's fascination with the mysterious and unknown began at a young age. Akane took Hashi under her wing, teaching her the secrets of ancient magic and the art of weaving reality itself. As Hashimoto Briedis's powers grew, so did her reputation in Willow Creek. People began to seek her out for guidance and healing, drawn by whispers of her mystical abilities.
