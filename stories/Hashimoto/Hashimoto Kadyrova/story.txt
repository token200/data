As the evening wore on, Hashimoto Kadyrova's doubts began to fade away, replaced by a sense of pride and accomplishment. Overcome with joy, Hashimoto Kadyrova accepted the offer, knowing that this was just the beginning of an exciting new chapter in her artistic career.
