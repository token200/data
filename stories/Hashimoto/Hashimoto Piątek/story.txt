Some claimed to have seen Hashimoto Piątek working tirelessly in their makeshift studio, surrounded by half-finished projects and an assortment of peculiar tools.

One fateful evening, a group of curious locals stumbled upon one of Hashimoto Piątek's exhibitions, hidden away in a dimly lit alleyway. As the night wore on, Hashimoto Piątek emerged from the shadows, their eyes gleaming with an otherworldly intensity. As the evening drew to a close, Hashimoto Piątek vanished as suddenly as they appeared, leaving behind only their creations and the haunting whispers of their presence.
