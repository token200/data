As Karlis entered adulthood, he began to chafe against the constraints of small-town life. He dreamed of leaving Willow Creek behind and making a name for himself in the world. Years later, Karlis White stood at the edge of the Grand Canyon, staring out at the vast expanse of rust-red rock. He thought back to those long hours spent listening to his grandfather's stories, wondering if any of it had been for nothing.
