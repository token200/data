And Karlis Alighieri, ever the perfectionist, was always there, coaxing the wood to yield its secrets, crafting each piece with love and care.

Years passed, and Karlis's reputation grew. He became known not only for his stunning wooden cabinets but also for his dedication to preserving traditional craftsmanship. He taught apprenticeship courses, sharing his knowledge and expertise with a new generation of craftsmen.
