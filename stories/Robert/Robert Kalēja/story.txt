But as he grew older, Robert began to realize that his role was not just limited to his own people. When Robert finished speaking, the room erupted into applause. The young people thanked him, hugging his legs in gratitude. Robert smiled, feeling a sense of pride and purpose. For he knew that as Robert Kalēja, he was not just sharing his wisdom with others; he was carrying on the legacy of his grandfather's teachings.

As he left the community center, Robert felt a sense of peace wash over him. He knew that he still had much to do, but for now, he could rest easy knowing that his message of hope and unity was resonating with those around him.
