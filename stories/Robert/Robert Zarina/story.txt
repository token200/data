Intrigued, Dr. Zarina decided to conduct an experiment. At first, Sarah's responses were disjointed and unclear, but as she continued to listen to the music, her words began to take on a new coherence.

He spent countless hours poring over brain scans and conducting experiments, determined to unravel the secrets of the human psyche once and for all.
