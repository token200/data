Robert's passion for art had always been a driving force in his life. Now, as a successful artist, Robert was determined to share his unique perspective with the world.

His full name, Robert Chih-chieh, meant "precious jade" in Mandarin – a symbol of good fortune and prosperity. He felt honored to be carrying on this legacy, using his art to celebrate the beauty and richness of Taiwanese culture.
