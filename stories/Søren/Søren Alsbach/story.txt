Søren smiled, remembering afternoons spent playing with his siblings amidst the stalls selling fresh fish and locally-made wares. Søren Alsbach took a deep breath, feeling the sea breeze fill his lungs. He had always felt most at home when the world seemed to slow down, allowing him to focus on the simple things in life. As he walked towards the old windmill on the hill, its sails creaking in the gentle breeze, Søren couldn't help but feel a sense of contentment wash over him.
