Born Leano Ivan Kiss, he had always been drawn to the art of deception. As a child, he would lie and cheat to get what he wanted, often leaving others feeling betrayed and disappointed. But as he grew older, Leano began to realize that his talents could be put to better use.

But as Leano Kiss disappeared into the night, he couldn't help but feel a twinge of satisfaction at a job well done. For in his world, there was no greater prize than the thrill of the chase.
