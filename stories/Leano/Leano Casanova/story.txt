It wasn't until he met Sophia that Leano began to feel a sense of belonging. A free-spirited artist, Sophia saw beyond Leano's rough edges and encouraged him to explore his more vulnerable side. He began to see the world in a new light, to appreciate the beauty in the mundane and the poetry in the ordinary. Sophia's influence had awakened something within him, and Leano felt like he was finally coming into his own.

Determined to protect the woman he loved, Leano steeled himself for battle, ready to fight to the death if necessary.
