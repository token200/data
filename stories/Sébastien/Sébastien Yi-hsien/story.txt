Yet, despite being a quintessential Hong Konger, Sébastien's heart belonged to another world.

Entranced by the secrets of the past, Sébastien spent countless hours deciphering the language, and slowly but surely, he began to unravel the threads of a long-forgotten family legacy.

Years went by, and Sébastien's obsession with his heritage only grew stronger. He became an expert in everything from French patisserie to 17th-century Huguenot history. Sébastien's life took a dramatic turn when he stumbled upon an old trunk belonging to his great-grandfather, a French naval officer who had spent years in the Orient. Inside the chest, Sébastien found a collection of rare artifacts and treasures from his ancestor's voyages, including a golden pocket watch with intricate engravings.

As Sébastien held the pocket watch in his hands, he felt an inexplicable connection to his great-grandfather's stories and adventures. The room seemed to fade away, replaced by visions of the high seas, exotic ports, and far-flung lands. Sébastien set sail for distant shores, following in the footsteps of his great-grandfather. He charted a course through forgotten archives, unearthed long-lost documents, and interviewed elderly sailors who had sailed the seven seas. As he delved deeper into his ancestral history, Sébastien began to unravel the tangled threads of his own identity.

In the bustling streets of Old Hong Kong, Sébastien Yi-hsien became a legend in his own right – a man whose name whispered secrets among those who knew him.
