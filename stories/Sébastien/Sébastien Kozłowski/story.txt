After leaving Paris, Sébastien wandered the world, taking on restaurant jobs in Tokyo, New York, and Rio de Janeiro. His résumé grew long with each new adventure, but it was his passion for innovation and experimentation that truly set him apart.

Inspired by this chance encounter, Sébastien decided to open his own restaurant – a modern interpretation of Poland's rich culinary history. Word of Sébastien's unique talents spread far and wide, attracting the attention of food critics, foodies, and even a few A-list celebrities. Yet despite his success, he remained humble, preferring to focus on perfecting each recipe rather than courting fame.

Sébastien's restaurant became more than just a dining destination – it was an institution, a testament to his unwavering dedication to the craft of cooking. Years went by, and Sébastien continued to refine his recipes, sharing them with aspiring chefs from across the globe. His story became one of resilience and perseverance – a true rags-to-riches tale that celebrated the power of food to bridge cultures and bring people together.
