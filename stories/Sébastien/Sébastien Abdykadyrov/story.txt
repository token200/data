His great-aunt told him stories of their ancestors – of battles fought and won, of love and loss, of secrets kept hidden for centuries.

And with each passing day, Sébastien felt himself becoming a part of this ancient family – his name, Sébastien Abdykadyrov, taking root in the city's rich soil.
