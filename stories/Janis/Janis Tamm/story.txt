As she grew older, Janis's skills only improved, and she began to dominate the puzzle-solving scene. College students would often gather at campus cafes, eager to challenge her with their latest brain teasers.

Janis's academic achievements didn't go unnoticed either. She won several regional and national competitions, earning herself a full scholarship to study cryptography at a top university. Despite her success, Janis remained humble and grounded. She continued to solve puzzles for fun, always looking for new challenges to overcome. In fact, it was during one of these solo puzzle sessions that she stumbled upon a particularly tricky cryptogram. Undeterred, Janis spent hours working on the code, using all her skills and experience to crack the encryption.

Her find sent shockwaves through the academic community, cementing her reputation as one of the world's top cryptographers.

Janis Tamm had truly earned her place in history, and she knew that this moment would be remembered for years to come.
