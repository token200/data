As Ito browsed the shelves, their fingers trailing over ancient artifacts and dusty relics, they discovered a small, leather-bound book hidden away in a corner. Over the next few weeks, Ito returned to the shop again and again, devouring the secrets contained within the book. They learned of ancient powers and forbidden knowledge, whispers of a long-forgotten past that threatened to upend their entire existence. As they delved deeper into the mysteries, Ito's sense of purpose began to take shape, and with it, a newfound determination to reclaim their rightful place in the world.

For the first time in their life, Ito D'Annunzio felt like they were walking towards their destiny, rather than running from it.
