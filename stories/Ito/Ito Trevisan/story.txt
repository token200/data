But those who did know of Ito Trevisan knew him as a master of deception and misdirection. One fateful night, Ito Trevisan found himself at the center of a complex web of intrigue. A wealthy collector had commissioned him to steal a valuable artifact from a heavily guarded museum – a task that required all of his cunning and skill.
