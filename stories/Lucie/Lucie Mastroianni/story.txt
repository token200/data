But none of her clients had ever asked her to dig into the past as deeply as Lucie Mastroianni did when she was hired by a mysterious woman named Sophia.

Sophia believed that Lucie Mastroianni's skills were just what she needed to uncover the truth. And so, Lucie set out on a journey that would take her from seedy bars to high-society parties, gathering clues and piecing together Emily's final days.
