One evening, as Lucie Piatek walked through the crowded streets of Paris, she stumbled upon a small, quirky shop tucked away in a quiet alley. Enchanted by the story, Lucie offered to commission a similar piece from Madame Dupont.

The result was a breathtaking masterpiece that combined intricate woodwork with delicate lace and sparkling crystals. As she held the finished product in her hands, Lucie felt a deep sense of satisfaction and connection to this beautiful piece.
