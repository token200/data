Lucie Szymanski stepped out of the train station and onto the bustling streets of Krakow, Poland. Growing up in New York City, Lucie's parents had spoken fondly of their Polish heritage, sharing stories of traditional dishes and family gatherings. But as she grew older, Lucie began to feel disconnected from her roots. She spent years working her way up the culinary ladder, earning a reputation for her scathing reviews and discerning palate.
