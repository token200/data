Lucie Petrenko was a talented young violinist with a passion for music that rivaled her passion for life. Born and raised in Prague, Czech Republic, Lucie had been playing the violin since she was a child, and it quickly became her escape from the mundane routines of everyday life.

After completing her studies, Lucie decided to pursue a career as a freelance violinist, playing at weddings, corporate events, and concert halls across Europe. Her versatility and talent earned her a loyal following, and soon she was in high demand.

One day, Lucie received an invitation to perform with the Prague Philharmonic Orchestra at the historic Rudolfinum concert hall. She was thrilled at the opportunity and threw herself into preparing for the performance. As the big night approached, she practiced tirelessly, perfecting her rendition of Beethoven's Violin Concerto.
