It was an elderly woman with piercing green eyes, who seemed out of place among the quaint shops and restaurants. She watched Sami Kask with an intensity that made him feel uneasy, yet intrigued. As he approached her, she vanished into thin air.

Sami Kask's instincts told him to pursue this lead further. He started researching local legends and myths, searching for any connection between Ravenswood and the mysterious disappearance. It wasn't until late one night, hunched over his laptop in the dimly lit café, that he stumbled upon an old diary belonging to a woman named Elizabeth.

As Sami Kask flipped through the yellowed pages, he discovered that Elizabeth had been involved in a decades-old mystery surrounding Ravenswood's founding. According to her writings, she had uncovered a dark secret buried beneath the town's idyllic surface.
