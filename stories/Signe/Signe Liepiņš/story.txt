Signe saw this as an opportunity to tell a powerful and humanizing story, one that would put a face to the numbers and highlight the impact of economic uncertainty on ordinary people.

As Signe Liepiņš continued to rise through the ranks, she remained committed to telling the stories that needed to be told.
