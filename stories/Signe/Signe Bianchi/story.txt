"I'm Signe Bianchi," she said, holding out her hand. "And you are...?"

"Leo Moreau," he replied, his handshake firm.

Over the next few hours, Signe learned more about Leo's life and work. She discovered a shared passion for social justice and a desire to challenge the status quo. As they parted ways, she knew that this chance encounter had changed her perspective on the city and its inhabitants.
