It was a beast that had been feeding on the city's desperation for years, leaving a trail of destruction in its wake. But she refused to back down, knowing that her voice could be a powerful tool in bringing about change.

Signe López Rodríguez was determined to see this story through to its conclusion, no matter what it took. She had risked everything already, and she knew that there would be those who would stop at nothing to silence her. But Signe was not one to be intimidated. With a fierce resolve burning in her heart, she pressed on, ready to face whatever challenges lay ahead.
