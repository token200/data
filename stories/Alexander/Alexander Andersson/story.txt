The professor revealed that he was searching for someone with a rare gift – the ability to decipher hidden meanings behind seemingly innocuous objects. And in Alexander Andersson, he saw his own hopes and dreams reflected back at him.

For the first time in his life, Alexander felt like he had found a place where he truly belonged.
