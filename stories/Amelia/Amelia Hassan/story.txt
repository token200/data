As Amelia continued on her journey, she couldn't help but think back to her early days as an intern. Who would have thought that this shy and ambitious young woman would one day be covering presidential campaigns? And so, Amelia Hassan continued to chase her dreams, driven by a passion for storytelling and a determination to make a difference.
