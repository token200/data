Amelia Astrid Stensson was a free-spirited artist with a passion for capturing the beauty of the Swedish wilderness on canvas. As Amelia grew older, her love for nature only deepened, and she began to pursue a career in fine art. She studied at the prestigious Royal College of Art in Stockholm, where she honed her skills and developed a unique style that blended traditional techniques with modern themes.

After completing her studies, Amelia returned to Falun and set up her own studio, surrounded by the very landscapes that had inspired her as a child. Her art quickly gained recognition locally, and soon she was receiving commissions from galleries and collectors across Sweden.
