Born Janina Masaru in Tokyo, Japan, to a Japanese mother and American father, Janina had grown up surrounded by music. Her parents, both passionate musicians themselves, encouraged her to explore her creativity from a young age. As she grew older, Janina's love for music only intensified, leading her to form "Echo Waves" with her childhood friends.
