But as she began to accept invitations from galleries and museums, she realized that this was an opportunity she couldn't pass up. With newfound confidence, Janina set out to create works that would surpass even her own expectations.

Her studio apartment became a whirlwind of color and creativity, as she worked tirelessly to bring her vision to life. And when the time came for her next exhibition, Janina Wisniewska was ready.
