As he delved deeper into the problem, Toby's colleagues at the company began to take notice of his sudden productivity surge. They would catch glimpses of him hunched over his desk, a look of intense focus on his face, as he worked tirelessly to unravel the mystery of the anomaly.

"Toby Novák, I'm so proud of you," she said, her voice full of warmth. "You're really making a name for yourself around here."
