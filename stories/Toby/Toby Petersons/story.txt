Toby Petersons was an unlikely rockstar. Growing up in the small town of Oakdale, he spent most of his teenage years playing gigs at local bars and parties with his band, "Electric Chaos." As the lead singer and guitarist, Toby's unique voice and energetic stage presence quickly made him a fan favorite.

But despite his growing popularity, Toby never lost sight of his humble beginnings. He remained true to himself and his music, refusing to compromise his artistic vision for fame or fortune. Over the years, they gained a loyal following and eventually landed a record deal with a major label. The release of their debut album was met with critical acclaim and commercial success, propelling Toby Petersons to stardom.
