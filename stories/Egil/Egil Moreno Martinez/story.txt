Egil Moreno Martinez was a man with a passion for music and art. Growing up in a small town in Mexico, he spent most of his free time playing guitar and writing songs. His love for music was evident even as a child, when he would spend hours watching the local mariachi bands perform.

As Egil grew older, his talent for music became more apparent. He began to play in local bars and clubs, performing traditional Mexican folk music that brought joy to all who listened. But Egil's true ambition was to create something new and unique – a blend of traditional Mexican music with modern styles.

Egil was hesitant at first, but the more he thought about it, the more excited he became.
