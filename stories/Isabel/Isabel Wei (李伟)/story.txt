But Isabel's passion for painting only grew stronger with each passing year.

After completing her education, Isabel decided to pursue her dream of becoming an artist. She packed her bags and moved to the city, where she quickly became a part of the vibrant art scene. Her unique blend of traditional Chinese techniques and modern styles soon caught the attention of galleries and collectors alike.
