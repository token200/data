Borys Emilia's presence commanded attention, yet she remained an elusive figure, always keeping to herself.

Borys spent most of her days tending to the family's orchard, where she nurtured the most exquisite array of fruits and flowers. As Kael prepared to depart the next morning, he felt an inexplicable urge to return to Ravenswood and find Borys Emilia again.
