As he grew older, Borys Yi-hsien became increasingly obsessed with unlocking the mysteries of the cosmos, spending countless hours in meditation and contemplation.

Despite his dedication to his craft, Borys Yi-hsien's path was not without its challenges. However, it was precisely this detachment that allowed Borys Yi-hsien to tap into the deepest recesses of his own being.
