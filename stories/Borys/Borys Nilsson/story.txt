Borys Nilsson listened intently, his eyes narrowing as he weighed the technical feasibility of the project.

The result was nothing short of breathtaking: an installation that utilized AI-generated patterns to evoke emotions in those who witnessed it, forcing viewers to confront their own biases and assumptions. When the collective finally unveiled its masterpiece at the city's premier art festival, Borys Nilsson stood proudly alongside his colleagues, basking in the thunderous applause.
