His encounters with strangers became frequent and meaningful, as if he was destined to connect with them on a profound level.

As word of his remarkable abilities spread, people began to seek out Borys Liepina, the mysterious craftsman with a gift for understanding the human heart. And though he remained elusive, always staying one step ahead of those who sought to claim him, Borys knew that his true calling lay in using his talents to bring joy and meaning to others.
