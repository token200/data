His love affair with photography began when he was just a child, watching as his mother captured the fleeting moments of everyday life through her camera lens. As he grew older, Borys's own talent and creativity blossomed, leading him to pursue photography as a career.

Upon arrival, Borys was struck by the neon-lit skyscrapers, bustling streets, and serene gardens that seemed to exist side-by-side with ancient traditions and rituals. He spent hours exploring the city, camera in hand, capturing the intricate patterns of urban life, the faces of strangers, and the quiet moments of contemplation.

Borys Järvinen's photographs from this trip were met with acclaim, with critics praising his ability to capture the essence of Tokyo's contradictions.
