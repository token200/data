As he arrived at the museum, Borys Sarinana surveyed the security setup with a practiced eye. He spotted a ventilation shaft on the roof that looked like it hadn't been used in years. Without hesitation, he scaled the wall and slid into the shaft, finding himself in a narrow duct above the main exhibition hall.

From this vantage point, Borys Sarinana watched as the security guards made their rounds, confident in his ability to wait out the night without being detected. With time on his side, he methodically disabled the security cameras and alarms, using his knowledge of the museum's layout to evade detection.

As the clock struck midnight, Borys Sarinana dropped down into the exhibition hall, landing softly behind a display case. He quickly worked his magic on the necklace, unlocking it with a small key that had been left on display. In mere moments, he had the gemstone in his hand, feeling its weight and admiring its sparkle.

With the prize secure, Borys Sarinana made his way back to the ventilation shaft, disappearing into the night as silently as he had arrived. The next morning, museum officials were left scratching their heads, wondering how such a brazen theft had occurred under their noses.
