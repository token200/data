Borys Abbagnato dreamed of opening his own trattoria, where he could share his love of food with the community.

With the help of his friends and family, he transformed the old building into a cozy and welcoming trattoria. The sign above the door read "Il Cibo di Borys Abbagnato," and the aroma of freshly cooked pasta wafted out onto the streets.

As the sun began to set, the villagers would gather at Il Cibo di Borys Abbagnato, eager to taste Borys Abbagnato's creative take on traditional Italian dishes. His signature dish, "Tortellini alla Toscana," quickly became a favorite among locals and tourists alike. Borys Abbagnato was known for his dedication to using only the freshest ingredients, sourced from local farmers and producers whenever possible. His commitment to quality had earned him numerous accolades, including a coveted spot on Italy's prestigious "Best Trattoria" list.
