As she entered adulthood, Beatriz made a difficult decision: she left China to study art in Europe. It was a risk, but one she felt necessary to take. She enrolled in the prestigious Academy of Fine Arts in Paris, where she immersed herself in the city's vibrant art scene.

Beatriz quickly became known for her unique style, which blended traditional Chinese motifs with modern techniques. Her paintings were met with critical acclaim, and soon she was exhibiting her work in galleries across Europe.

As she stood before a canvas, brush in hand, Beatriz felt a sense of freedom she had never known before. She was no longer bound by traditional expectations or limited by cultural norms.
