However, it was during his final year of studies that Yuriy's life took an unexpected turn. As the controversy surrounding the piece grew, Yuriy found himself at the center of attention. His reputation as an artist was threatened, and he struggled to come to terms with the fact that his work might not be genuine after all.

Despite the turmoil, Yuriy refused to give up on his art. He continued to paint, using the experience as fuel for his next project. And when the dust finally settled, "Les Démons de la Nuit" was revealed to be a genuine masterpiece, created by Yuriy Mercier himself.

The exposure brought Yuriy widespread acclaim and recognition within the art world. Though he had faced setbacks and scandals along the way, Yuriy Mercier's unwavering dedication to his craft had ultimately led him to success.
