Callum Alexander Girace was no stranger to chaos. As the lead singer of the up-and-coming indie rock band, Wildfire, he had learned to navigate the unpredictable world of music with ease. In that moment, with the Colorado mountains looming behind him and a sea of adoring faces in front, Callum Alexander Girace was the undisputed king of Red Rocks.
