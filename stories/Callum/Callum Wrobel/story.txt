Callum Wrobel sat alone in his dimly lit flat, nursing a whiskey on the rocks. It was a chilly autumn evening, and the rain poured down outside like a relentless curtain of grey. He stared blankly at the TV, where an old sitcom played on repeat, its jokes long since stale.

She was the only person who truly understood him – someone who saw beyond the corporate facade and knew the real Callum Wrobel. It wasn't going to be easy, but with Rachel by his side, he felt like he could conquer anything. As he looked around his cluttered flat, Callum Wrobel knew that it was time to make some changes – to rediscover the creative spark within him.
