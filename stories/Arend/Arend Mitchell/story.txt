The yellowed canvases and dusty brushes seemed to call out to her, as if urging her to unlock their secrets. And so she did, letting herself get lost in the textures and colors of a bygone era.

The trunk led her on a journey through time, taking her to places both familiar and unknown. She discovered that Arend Mitchell's artistic legacy was far more extensive than anyone had realized – each painting a window into her own soul.
