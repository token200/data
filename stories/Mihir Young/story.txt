Mihir Young was born on a sweltering summer day in the vibrant city of Mumbai. As a child, he spent countless hours watching Bollywood films with his grandmother, mesmerized by the dancing queens and swooning romances that filled the screens. It was then that he knew he wanted to be a part of the world of cinema.

As Mihir grew older, his passion for filmmaking only intensified. He spent every spare moment learning about camera angles, lighting, and editing techniques. He devoured books on cinematography and even landed an internship with a local production company.

Mihir's big break came when he was hired as a second assistant director on a low-budget independent film. It was a small but pivotal role that allowed him to hone his skills under the watchful eye of seasoned director, Rohan. Mihir's attention to detail and creativity quickly earned him a reputation among the production team.
