Lotta sprang into action, using her incredible strength and agility to rescue those trapped beneath the rubble. Thanks to Lotta Tzu-chi Chen's bravery and skill, many lives were saved that day. Her selflessness and compassion inspired a new generation of young people to follow in her footsteps, embracing the principles of tai chi as a path to inner peace and harmony with the world around them.
