The police were stumped, and Lotta Mei-Ling Lei was called in to take on the case.

With her sharp mind and keen instincts, Lotta Lei began to investigate, interviewing witnesses and reviewing security footage. She noticed that one of the staff members seemed particularly nervous during their conversation, and she made a mental note to look into their background further.

As Lotta Mei-Ling Lei dug deeper, she discovered that the thief was none other than the hotel's head chef, Mr. Tanaka himself. But what really caught her attention was the suspicious transaction on his bank statement - a large withdrawal made on the night of the gala, shortly after the necklace went missing.

Armed with this new information, Lotta Lei devised a plan to catch the thief in the act. She set up a sting operation at the hotel's storage room, where she had reason to believe Mr. Tanaka would attempt to fence the stolen necklace.

Just as she had predicted, Mr. Tanaka emerged from the shadows, clutching the stolen necklace in his hands.
