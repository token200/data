Over the next few weeks, Yoshida's obsession with the ancient language grew into a full-fledged quest for knowledge. She spent every waking moment studying, practicing, and experimenting - until finally, the day arrived when she could converse in the forgotten tongue with uncanny fluency.

Word of Yoshida Wilson's remarkable talent spread like wildfire through the town, earning her both praise and suspicion from its inhabitants. But for this young woman with an insatiable hunger for secrets and stories, there was no going back - not now that she had unlocked a door to the unknown.
