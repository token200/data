Her friends and acquaintances often joked that she had a PhD in Persuasion, and they weren't far off. Whether it was convincing a vendor to sell her his wares at a fraction of the price or sweet-talking a skeptical customer into trying her latest venture, Nargiza Pei-ling had an uncanny ability to get what she wanted.

But beneath her tough exterior lay a heart of gold. Nargiza's passion for helping others was matched only by her love of adventure and exploration. From that day on, Nargiza became obsessed with uncovering more secrets about her city's past. She spent every spare moment researching and exploring, using her quick wit and sharp mind to unravel mysteries and piece together the fragments of Singapore's rich history.

And as she delved deeper into the world of the pirate, Nargiza began to realize that her own journey was far from over.
