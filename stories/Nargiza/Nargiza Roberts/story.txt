As she approached middle age, Nargiza began to think about retiring from the law. But the thought of leaving the work she loved behind was almost too much to bear. So instead, she decided to start a small organization - the Oakdale Justice Project - dedicated to providing pro bono legal services to those who couldn't afford them.
