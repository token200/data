And pursue it she did, rapidly rising through the ranks of Polish fashion's elite.

Her face was plastered on billboards and magazine covers, and she had become synonymous with elegance and sophistication.

Her relationships were tumultuous and fleeting, as if she was perpetually searching for something more – something that would set her free from the weight of her own expectations.

She had just landed a coveted modeling contract with a top designer, and was feeling particularly vulnerable and exposed. As she sipped a glass of white wine, lost in thought, a striking young man approached her.

For the first time in years, Nargiza felt truly seen – like someone had pierced the armor that protected her from the world.
