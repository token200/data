With renewed determination, Nargiza set out to expose the truth behind the scandal. Her investigation took her from cramped offices in the government's headquarters to secret meetings with whistleblowers. Every lead brought her closer to the heart of the corruption, and Nargiza was convinced she would eventually get justice for herself and the Polish people.

As the sun dipped below the horizon, casting a golden glow over Warsaw, Nargiza sat at her desk, pouring over documents and notes. She knew that the road ahead wouldn't be easy, but with Jan's testimony and her own tenacity, Nargiza Jasiński was ready to take on whatever came next.
