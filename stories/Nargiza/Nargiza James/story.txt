Nargiza James was captivated by the way the light danced across the colors, casting shadows and highlighting textures that seemed to leap off the page.

With renewed determination, Nargiza James James set out to hone her craft, studying the masters and experimenting with different techniques. She spent hours mixing paints and inking pens, until finally, her own unique style began to emerge.
