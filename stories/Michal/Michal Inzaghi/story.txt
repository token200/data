Using her knowledge of engineering and architecture, Michal created a stunning, environmentally conscious residence that impressed even the most discerning clients. Word of her success spread quickly, earning Michal the coveted title of "Young Engineer of the Year."

Michal Inzaghi's achievements didn't go unnoticed for long. She was soon in high demand, with projects pouring in from all over Europe. As her star continued to rise, she remained committed to her core values: creativity, innovation, and a passion for making the world a better place.
