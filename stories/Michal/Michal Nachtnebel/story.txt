Michal Nachtnebel became a respected scholar and practitioner of his craft, known throughout the land for his wisdom and insight. People would come from far and wide to seek his counsel, hoping to gain a deeper understanding of the workings of the universe.

As the years passed, Michal Nachtnebel became renowned as one of the most extraordinary minds of his generation. His name was whispered in awe by scholars and mystics alike, a testament to his unwavering dedication to the pursuit of knowledge.
