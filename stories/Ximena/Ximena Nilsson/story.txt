Her fearless reporting earned her a reputation as one of the most respected journalists in Brazil.

There, she used her unique blend of Swedish stoicism and Brazilian passion to tackle some of the toughest stories facing her country. Whether she was interviewing whistleblowers or tracking down leads in crowded streets of Stockholm, Ximena had an uncanny ability to coax even the most guarded stories out of people.

But Ximena refused to back down. Instead, she doubled down on her reporting, convinced that the truth was worth fighting for.
