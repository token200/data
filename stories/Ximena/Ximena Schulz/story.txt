The caller revealed that the prime suspect was none other than Ximena's old flame, Alex Chen. She had thought she'd seen the last of him after their messy breakup years ago.

Intrigued, Ximena Schulz decided to dig deeper into the case. She started by interviewing witnesses and gathering evidence, but every lead seemed to end in a dead-end. Frustrated, she took a step back and re-examined the original call from her anonymous source. Something about it didn't add up.

Determined to get to the bottom of things, Ximena Schulz paid a visit to Alex himself. Her presence caught him off guard, but he quickly regained his composure and denied any wrongdoing. Ximena Schulz wasn't convinced, though, and she pressed on, sensing that there was something he wasn't telling her.

As the sun began to set, Ximena Schulz left Alex's office with more questions than answers. She knew she had to keep pushing forward until she uncovered the truth behind the embezzlement case. The game was afoot, and Ximena Schulz was hot on the trail.
