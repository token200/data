As the story began to take shape, Ximena realized that she had stumbled upon something much bigger than a simple exposé - she had uncovered a web of corruption and deceit that went all the way to the top. With her research complete, Ximena Bērziņa was ready to take on the powerful interests that sought to keep this secret hidden.
