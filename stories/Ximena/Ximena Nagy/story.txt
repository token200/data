One night, Ximena Nagy received a message from an old acquaintance, inviting her to meet at a secluded mansion on the outskirts of town. The note was cryptic, but Ximena's curiosity was piqued. Ximena Nagy was hesitant at first, but The Fox's words struck a chord within her. She had always been driven by a desire for excitement and adventure, and this challenge seemed like the perfect opportunity to test her skills. After some deliberation, she agreed to take on the mission.
