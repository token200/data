As a child, she had always felt an affinity for the sea and its secrets, and now, at twenty-five years old, she found herself drawn to it even more.

A skilled sailor and explorer, Harley Lefevre had spent her entire life searching for answers in the vast expanse of the ocean. She had spent years studying the ancient maps and texts, piecing together clues that would lead her to the elusive treasure.
