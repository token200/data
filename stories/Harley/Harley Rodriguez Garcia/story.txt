Harley threw herself into the class, pouring her emotions onto canvas and paper. She changed her name to simply "Harley" and started a blog to share her art and stories with the world.

She remembered the countless nights spent sleeping on friends' couches, wondering if she'd ever escape the cycle of struggle. For the first time in years, Harley felt a sense of peace.

With every brushstroke and word, Harley Rodriguez Garcia – now simply known as Harley – continued to push boundaries and redefine what it means to be strong.
