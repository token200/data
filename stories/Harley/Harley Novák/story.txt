Harley Novák slumped against the worn wooden railing, gazing out at the stormy sea beyond the small coastal town's pier. As a struggling artist, she had always found solace in the turbulent waves, her mind mirroring the ever-changing tides.

As she grew older, Harley Novák's artistic talents only deepened, but life had a way of getting in the way. She struggled to make ends meet, working multiple part-time jobs to support herself while her art languished in obscurity. Yet, despite the frustration, she continued to paint, driven by an insatiable creative fire that burned within her.

The storm raged on outside, but within her studio, a sense of peace reigned supreme.
