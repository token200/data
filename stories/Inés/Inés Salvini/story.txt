Despite her privileged upbringing, Inés had always felt stifled by the expectations placed upon her. So, when she stumbled upon a local art studio tucked away in a quiet alley, she knew she had found her calling.

She poured her heart and soul into every piece, infusing them with a sense of vitality and life that captivated all who saw them.

As word of Inés Salvini's talent spread throughout the town, people began to take notice. Overwhelmed but exhilarated by the opportunity, Inés Salvini accepted the offer. As she prepared for her big debut, she couldn't help but think back to those early days spent in the art studio, where it all began. She knew that this was just the beginning of an incredible journey, one that would take her far beyond the confines of Sanremo and into the spotlight of international art.
