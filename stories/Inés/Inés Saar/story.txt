But no matter where she went or what she created, her work was always infused with a deep sense of connection to her roots.

Inés Saar was delighted to take on the apprentice, and as they worked together, she saw something of herself in Maria - a spark of creativity, a willingness to take risks.

But as the project neared its end, Inés Saar began to feel a sense of melancholy wash over her. This was one of her last tapestries, created for a client who had commissioned it years ago. She knew that she would soon be passing on the torch, and a part of her would always remain in this place.
