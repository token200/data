After completing the review, Inés Yan submitted her report to the startup. To her surprise, they reached out to invite her for an interview to discuss their product further. Over coffee, Inés Yan shared her thoughts and concerns with the CEO, who listened attentively and acknowledged some of the criticisms she raised.

Inés was taken aback by the offer, but deep down, she knew it was an opportunity too good to pass up.
