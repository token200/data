But as he began to write his story, he knew that he was taking a risk. The powerful men involved would stop at nothing to silence him.

Undaunted, Patryk Akiko went ahead with his plan. He sent his story to several major news outlets, knowing that it had the potential to shake the foundations of Polish politics. And then he waited – nervously and anxiously – for the response.

Patryk's heart leapt with excitement as he hung up the phone. His story had been seen, heard, and now it would be read by millions of people around the world.
