"Who are you?" Bogusław Mägi asked, his eyes narrowing slightly as he eyed the stranger's dark cloak and ornate jewelry.

Bogusław Mägi nodded thoughtfully, gesturing for the stranger to enter his humble abode. As they sat down at a wooden table, he began to shuffle a deck of tarot cards with an air of reverence.

As the night wore on, Bogusław Mägi presented his findings to the stunned stranger, offering a glimpse into a world beyond the veil of reality.
