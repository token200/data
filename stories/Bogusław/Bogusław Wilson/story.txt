As the months passed, Emily became an integral part of Bogusław's life, helping him catalog his vast collection and share it with the world. And though he still preferred the quiet solitude of his workshop, Bogusław found himself grateful for her presence, knowing that he had finally found a kindred spirit in the young woman who had stumbled upon his doorstep all those years ago.
