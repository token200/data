Critics and collectors alike were drawn to her unique style, which blended elements of abstract expressionism with a deep sense of symbolism.

She was immediately captivated by the woman's presence – a commanding energy that seemed to radiate from every pore. As she watched, Ursula Maldini worked on a new piece, her hands moving deftly as she applied layers of color and texture.

Ursula smiled as if reading Lily's thoughts. "You have an eye," she said. "A sense of seeing beyond the surface. I think you might be ready for a mentorship."
