Rusty's eyes scanned the grid, searching for patterns and connections that would reveal the solution. Elliot worked his magic on the other side of the room, providing support and encouragement as needed.

With mere minutes to spare, The Brainy Bunch emerged victorious, their solution accurate and flawless. As they accepted their trophy and basked in the applause of the crowd, Rusty couldn't help but think about how far she'd come since joining Elliot at the puzzle table all those years ago.
