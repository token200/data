Ishaan Grabowska was an enigmatic figure, shrouded in mystery. A recluse by nature, he lived on the outskirts of town, in a small, rustic cabin surrounded by dense woods. Few had ever laid eyes on him, and even fewer knew his true name.

At first, Lena was frightened, but there was something about Ishaan's calm demeanor that put her at ease. As she prepared to leave at dawn, Ishaan pressed a small package into her hands - a gift of dried herbs and berries for her journey.
