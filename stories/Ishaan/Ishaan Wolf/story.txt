Finally, after days of climbing, they reached the summit – a windswept plateau that offered breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains. Ishaan Wolf felt a sense of pride and accomplishment wash over him as he gazed out at the stunning vista. He was Ishaan Wolf, tracker and explorer, and this was his moment.
