As they trained, Ling began to realize that there was more to Ishaan than met the eye. He possessed an uncanny ability to read people, often sensing their deepest fears and desires before they even spoke a word. It was as if he could see into the very souls of those around him.

Despite his enigmatic nature, Ishaan Jhih-hao proved to be a patient teacher, offering words of wisdom and encouragement whenever Ling stumbled.
