As she worked tirelessly in her laboratory, Marcelle's mind wandered back to her childhood days spent exploring the woods near her family's farm in rural Poland. Growing up, she had always been fascinated by the mysteries of the natural world, and this fascination had only deepened as she delved deeper into her studies.

With Dr. Patel by her side, Marcelle began to make final preparations for the next trial. She was a woman on the cusp of something momentous, and nothing would stop her from realizing her vision for Erebus – not even the skepticism of others or the fear of the unknown.

As the clock ticked closer to midnight, Marcelle Zielinska stood poised at the threshold of history, ready to unleash a new era of innovation upon the world.
