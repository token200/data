As Marcelle Trevisan stepped into the ancient ruins, she felt a thrill run down her spine. The air was thick with the scent of decay and forgotten history. She began to carefully excavate the site, uncovering artifacts and relics that had lain hidden for centuries.

Despite the challenges posed by the treacherous terrain and unforgiving climate, Marcelle Trevisan persevered. Her determination was driven by a burning curiosity about the people who once inhabited this city. Who were they? What was their way of life?
