According to local lore, its people possessed ancient wisdom and magical powers, which they used to maintain balance and harmony in the world. Saltanat Németh was determined to uncover the truth behind this enigmatic realm.

Suddenly, Saltanat Németh stumbled upon an ancient entrance hidden behind a screen of vines. The air around her seemed to vibrate with an otherworldly energy, as if the very fabric of reality was about to tear apart. She hesitated for a moment, then stepped through the threshold into the unknown.

The air pulsed with an ethereal light that seemed to be drawing her deeper into the heart of Szaloncia. Saltanat Németh felt a shiver run down her spine as she realized that she was not alone.
