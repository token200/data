Excited by the prospect of collaborating with international experts, Saltanat accepted the challenge without hesitation.

For months, she pored over data and consulted with her colleagues, determined to find innovative solutions to protect this majestic creature's habitat. As the project leader, Saltanat Masaru worked tirelessly alongside a team of scientists, working towards a common goal: to preserve the delicate balance of the ecosystem for future generations.
