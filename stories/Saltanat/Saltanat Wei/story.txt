She was the daughter of a nomadic tribe, where survival depended on the land's harsh conditions. From a young age, Saltanat Wei demonstrated an exceptional talent for navigating the unforgiving terrain, which would one day become a crucial skill in her pursuit of adventure.

As she grew older, Saltanat Wei became increasingly restless, yearning to explore beyond the confines of her tribe's territory. The more Saltanat Wei heard these stories, the more determined she became to uncover their truth.

Intrigued by the prospect of discovery, Saltanat Wei approached the leader of the expedition and offered her services as a guide. As they ventured into the unforgiving landscape, Saltanat Wei led the way, using her knowledge of the land and her keen intuition to navigate treacherous dunes and hidden pitfalls.
