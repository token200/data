Years later, after completing her engineering degree, Saltanat created her alter ego, "The Technomancer." Armed with a trusty toolbox and an unwavering determination, she set out to use her skills for good. Saltanat sprang into action, using her expertise to analyze clues and piece together the thieves' trail. With Khalid by her side, she chased the culprits through the winding streets of the old city, dodging vendors and leaping over piles of laundry.
