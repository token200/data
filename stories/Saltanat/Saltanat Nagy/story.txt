Word of Saltanat Nagy's generosity spread quickly throughout the town, earning her even more respect and admiration from the locals. As the years passed, Saltanat continued to run her successful blacksmith shop, always willing to lend a helping hand to those in need.

Despite her many accomplishments, Saltanat remained humble and kind, never forgetting the values of compassion and generosity that had been instilled in her by her father.
