Saltanat Varga nodded, her eyes shining with excitement. "Let's find out."

With a newfound sense of purpose, Saltanat Varga led her team into the heart of the city, ready to unravel the secrets that lay within. Her name would go down in history as the discoverer of Zerzura, a testament to her unwavering dedication and unrelenting passion for uncovering the mysteries of the past.
