A Swedish engineer by trade, he spent most of his days tinkering with machinery in his small workshop, surrounded by dusty toolboxes and half-finished projects. Karl Jansons' eyes twinkled with mischief as he imagined the adventures this boat would have. And yet, there was something about Karl Jansons that commanded respect - a quiet confidence that belied his unassuming demeanor.
