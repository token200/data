As Karl continued his journey, he began to realize that home was not just a physical place, but also a state of mind. He returned to Ashwood with a newfound appreciation for the simple things in life and a heart full of memories.

Back at his family's vineyard, Karl Alighieri sat by the fireplace, watching the flames dance and listening to the crackling of the wood. His eyes sparkled as he thought about all the incredible experiences he had had on his journey. He smiled, knowing that no matter where life took him, he would always carry a piece of Ashwood with him.
