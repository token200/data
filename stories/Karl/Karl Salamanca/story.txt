Karl's rise to power began when he was hired by Gus to work as an "asset manager" for one of his many legitimate front businesses. However, Karl soon realized that his true calling lay in the underworld, where he could use his charisma and strategic thinking to build a network of loyal associates.

As time went on, Karl's reputation grew, and he became known as one of the most feared men in the city. His nickname, "Karlito," was whispered in awe by those who crossed him, while his competitors trembled at the mere mention of his name.

Despite his success, Karl never forgot his roots. He remained fiercely loyal to Gus, even when their relationship became strained due to Gus's increasingly erratic behavior. When Gus eventually met a tragic end at the hands of Jesse Pinkman and Hank Schrader, Karl was devastated but ultimately saw it as an opportunity to take control.

With Gus out of the picture, Karl set his sights on expanding his empire and cementing his place as the top dog in the city's underworld. His ruthless tactics earned him both respect and resentment, but one thing was certain: Karl Johannes László Salamanca would stop at nothing to achieve his goals.

As he stood tall in his gleaming office, surrounded by his loyal henchmen, Karl couldn't help but smile. He had come a long way from his humble beginnings as Gus's right-hand man.
