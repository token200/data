The stories had sparked a fire within him, driving him to explore the world of mysticism and spirituality. As he grew older, his interest in the paranormal only deepened, leading him to study various forms of divination and energy work.

As he began to play his accordion, an eerie silence fell over the room. The air seemed to vibrate with anticipation, and even the trees outside appeared to lean in, as if drawn by the haunting melodies.
