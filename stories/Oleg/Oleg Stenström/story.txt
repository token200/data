After leaving the CIA, Oleg Stenström found himself drawn to the world of international finance. Despite his impressive credentials and razor-sharp instincts, however, Oleg remained an enigma to those who knew him. Few could guess what he was thinking, or where his loyalties truly lay.

One evening, as Oleg Stenström sipped a fine scotch at his favorite Manhattan bar, he found himself entangled in a conversation with a charismatic stranger. The man, a wealthy entrepreneur with a penchant for high-stakes deals, seemed to sense something in Oleg - a hidden vulnerability, perhaps, or a burning ambition that went unspoken.
