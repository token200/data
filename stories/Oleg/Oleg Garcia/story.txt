His parents had encouraged him to study business, but Oleg's passion for storytelling won out.

Years later, Oleg found himself back in the city where he grew up, working as a freelance journalist. He had built a reputation for himself as a talented storyteller, able to weave complex narratives from seemingly mundane subjects. But despite his success, Oleg couldn't shake the feeling that something was missing. Maybe it was time for Oleg Garcia to put his journalism skills to use in new ways. As he hung up the phone, a spark of inspiration ignited within him.

Would he find a way to combine his passion for storytelling with his love of music? Only time would tell, but one thing was certain: Oleg Garcia was ready to take on the challenge.
