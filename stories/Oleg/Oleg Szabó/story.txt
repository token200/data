Oleg Szabó was a man with a passion for art and music. Growing up in Budapest, he spent most of his childhood exploring the city's vibrant cultural scene, attending concerts and exhibitions, and soaking up the rich history that seemed to seep from every stone.

She was a sculptor with a quirky sense of humor and an impressive talent for turning trash into treasure. As word of their collaboration spread, Oleg Szabó and Lila began to attract attention from galleries and collectors around the world. They were hailed as visionaries, their art sparking conversations about identity, culture, and the human condition.
