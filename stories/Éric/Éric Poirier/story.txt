Éric Poirier stepped into the dimly lit gym, his eyes scanning the familiar surroundings with a mix of nostalgia and determination. As a former professional boxer, he had spent countless hours within these walls, honing his skills and training for some of the biggest fights of his career.

As the hours passed, Éric's muscles burned, and his lungs ached, but he refused to give up. He thought about his family, his loved ones, and the life he wanted to build outside the ring. The fire that had once driven him now fueled his determination to make a new start.
