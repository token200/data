From Moscow to Madrid, Éric Kask left a trail of impressed enemies and grateful allies in his wake.

Years later, Éric Kask found himself on a mission that would test his skills like never before. The Soviet Union had developed a new type of encryption that was supposed to be unbreakable - but not, it seemed, for Éric Kask. Using his vast knowledge of cryptography and linguistic tricks, he managed to crack the code in record time.

His reputation as "The Archivist" spread far and wide, striking fear into the hearts of villains everywhere. He had lived a full life, filled with danger, adventure, and intellectual challenge. And yet, here he was - still working for MI6, still cracking codes and uncovering secrets.
