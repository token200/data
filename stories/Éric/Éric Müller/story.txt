Éric Müller had always been drawn to the mysterious and unknown. As a renowned archaeologist, he spent his career uncovering secrets lost to time. His latest obsession was an ancient temple hidden deep in the jungle, rumored to hold the key to unlocking the mysteries of a long-lost civilization.

As Éric Müller ventured deeper into the dense foliage, the air grew thick with humidity and the sounds of the rainforest surrounded him.
