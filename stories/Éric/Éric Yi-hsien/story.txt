One day, Éric Yi-hsien received a visit from a beautiful woman named Sophia, who begged for his help. Her sister, a renowned scientist, had gone missing while working on a top-secret project at the research facility. The police were stumped, and Sophia suspected foul play.

Éric Yi-hsien agreed to take on the case, intrigued by the prospect of unraveling a complex web of deceit. He began by interviewing Sophia and her sister's colleagues, but every lead seemed to end in a dead-end. Frustrated, Éric Yi-hsien decided to pay a visit to his old friend, Professor Tanaka, an expert in cryptography.

Over tea and biscuits, Professor Tanaka revealed that the research facility had been working on a revolutionary new technology that could change the course of human history. But at what cost? Éric Yi-hsien's eyes narrowed as he realized that the missing scientist was likely to have stumbled upon something she shouldn't have.

With newfound determination, Éric Yi-hsien set out to track down his quarry. Following a trail of cryptic clues and encrypted messages, he finally arrived at an abandoned warehouse on the outskirts of town. Inside, he found Sophia's sister, bound and gagged, with a group of armed men surrounding her.

Éric Yi-hsien charged into battle, using his mastery of both Chinese and Japanese martial arts to disarm the thugs. In the end, it was just him against one last adversary – a figure in a black hood who revealed himself to be none other than Sophia's own brother-in-law, driven mad by greed and ambition.

Éric Yi-hsien apprehended the would-be killer and freed Sophia's sister, restoring her to safety. As he watched her tearfully reunite with her sister, Éric Yi-hsien couldn't help but feel a sense of satisfaction. Another case closed, another secret unraveled.
