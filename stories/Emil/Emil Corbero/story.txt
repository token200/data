As a skilled thief, he had honed his skills on the streets of Barcelona, always staying one step ahead of his enemies.

But little did anyone know that beneath Emil Corbero's tough exterior lay a complex web of emotions. His troubled past had left emotional scars, and he struggled to form genuine connections with others. Yet, when he was in the midst of a job, his focus became laser-sharp, and nothing else mattered except the thrill of the chase.

One night, as Emil Corbero crept through the shadows, his eyes locked onto a valuable artifact that had been passed down through generations of a wealthy family. His heart quickened with excitement, and he knew this was the score of a lifetime. With lightning-fast reflexes, he snatched the priceless relic and made his escape.

As he disappeared into the night, Emil Corbero couldn't shake off the feeling that someone was watching him. He glanced over his shoulder, but saw nothing out of the ordinary. Shrugging it off as mere paranoia, he quickened his pace, his footsteps echoing through the deserted streets.

But as he turned a corner, he caught a glimpse of a woman in red – a symbol of good luck for thieves like himself. She was standing beneath a streetlamp, her eyes fixed intently on him. For a moment, their gazes met, and Emil Corbero felt an unexpected jolt of connection.

"Emil Corbero?" she asked softly, as if reading his mind. "What's your true name?"

The woman smiled enigmatically, her eyes sparkling with intrigue. "Then let me guess – you're running from something more than just the law?"
