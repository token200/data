Kovács' fascination with "The Shadow Weaver" soon led him down a rabbit hole of cryptic symbols, ancient rituals, and hidden societies. He spent countless hours pouring over dusty texts and scouring the city for clues, all in an effort to unravel the secrets of the painting.

In that moment, Kovács felt like he was standing at the threshold of a new world, one that would change him forever. And as he gazed into the eyes of "The Shadow Weaver," he knew that his life would never be the same again – thanks to the enigmatic presence of Jean-Pierre Kovács.
