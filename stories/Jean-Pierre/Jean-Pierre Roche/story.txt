One fateful evening, as Jean-Pierre Roche was closing up his office, he received a mysterious phone call from an unknown number. The voice on the other end whispered a single phrase: "Meet me at the Musée d'Orsay at midnight. Come alone." Intrigued, Jean-Pierre Roche decided to heed the warning.

Together, they explored the Musée d'Orsay's vast collections, uncovering secrets and sharing stories that only a true connoisseur of Impressionism could appreciate.
