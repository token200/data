Irina smiled wryly as she continued on her way. She knew that one day she would have to take her place beside her parents on the throne, but for now, she was content to pursue her own path. As she walked through the city gates, she felt a sense of freedom wash over her. This was where she belonged - among the gears and cogs of the machine world.
