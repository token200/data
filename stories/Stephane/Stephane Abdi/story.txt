Born Stephanus Johannes Abdi, Stephane had grown up surrounded by the finer things in life. His family's wealth had given him every opportunity to pursue his passions, and he had never been one to disappoint. After studying art history at university, Stephane had set out on his own, building a network of connections that spanned the globe.

But Stephane's success came with a price. He was constantly surrounded by people who wanted something from him, and he found himself trapped in a web of deceit and manipulation. Despite this, he remained undeterred, always pushing forward in pursuit of his next big score.

Without hesitation, Stephane accepted the offer. He threw himself into the hunt, following a trail of clues that took him from the streets of New York to the galleries of Paris. As he delved deeper into the world of art dealing, Stephane began to realize just how far down the rabbit hole he had fallen. But he was determined to see it through, no matter what lay ahead.
