His love affair with art began at an early age, when his mother would take him on Sundays to explore the Louvre in Paris. Those hours spent wandering through the galleries sparked something within him, and he knew from then on that he wanted to spend his life surrounded by beauty.

Years later, as Stephane Nagy stood before a young artist's work, he felt the same thrill he had experienced all those years ago. The bold colors, the innovative style – it was as if he was seeing himself in the artist's brushstrokes. Without hesitation, Stephane decided to take a chance on this newcomer, and his instincts proved correct.

Despite his success, Stephane remained humble and true to his passion. He continued to scour the globe for unique works, often taking risks on artists who were ahead of their time.
