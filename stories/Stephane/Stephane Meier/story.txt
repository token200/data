People from all walks of life flocked to witness these surreal spectacles, mesmerized by the sheer audacity of the duo's vision.

Lena noticed Stephane's distant gaze and smiled knowingly. She had witnessed this same look of wonder in her own eyes countless times before – a reflection of their shared passion for discovery.
