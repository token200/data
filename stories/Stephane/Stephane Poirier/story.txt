This was where she belonged – surrounded by books, words, and the whispers of the past. As she settled back into her chair, Stephane Poirier felt invigorated, ready to tackle the challenges ahead.

With renewed vigor, she dove back into her writing, letting the words pour out of her like a river. The room grew quiet, except for the soft scratch of her pen on paper.
