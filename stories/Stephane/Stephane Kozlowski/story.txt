With his sharp mind and quick wit, Stephane had assembled a team of talented individuals who shared his passion for artistic expression.

Stephane paced among the crowd, observing each reaction with great interest. Some gasped in awe, while others whispered among themselves, puzzled by the sheer complexity of the piece. But Stephane didn't mind; he knew that his art was not for everyone.
