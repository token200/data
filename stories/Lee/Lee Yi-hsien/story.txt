With his sharp mind and keen instincts, Lee Yi-hsien had solved cases that had stumped the authorities for years. He had seen friends and family members fall victim to crime and violence, leaving him with a burning desire for justice. After completing his studies, he joined the police force, determined to make a difference.

As he delved deeper into the world of detective work, Lee Yi-hsien proved himself to be an exceptional investigator. His skills were honed through years of experience, and he had developed a unique approach that allowed him to piece together seemingly unrelated clues.

With his characteristic intensity, Lee Yi-hsien threw himself into the case, pouring over evidence and interviewing suspects. His relentless pursuit of answers had some people worried about his obsession with justice, but he knew that it was what drove him to succeed.

As the days turned into weeks, Lee Yi-hsien's tireless efforts finally began to pay off.
