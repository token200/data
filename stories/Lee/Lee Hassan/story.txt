Lee Hassan was a man with a passion for art and music, who had always dreamed of making it big in the city of New York. Despite his success, Lee never lost sight of his dreams. He continued to work tirelessly on his craft, honing his skills as a performer and producer. He spent hours in his studio, experimenting with new sounds and techniques, determined to push the boundaries of what was possible.
