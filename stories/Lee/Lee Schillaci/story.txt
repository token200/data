One sunny morning, as the townspeople gathered at Mae's Eats, Lee Schillaci expertly flipped pancakes and cooked eggs with ease. The aroma of freshly brewed coffee wafted through the air, enticing everyone to come and taste her famous cinnamon rolls. Regulars like Mayor Thompson, the postmaster, and the local sheriff, always made a point to stop by for breakfast.

Lee Schillaci's selfless nature inspired countless people, earning her the title of "heart of Willow Creek."

She quickly rallied her team, including her loyal waitress, Sarah, and together they worked tirelessly to prepare for the worst.
