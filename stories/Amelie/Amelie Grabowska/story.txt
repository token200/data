One sunny afternoon, Amelie Grabowska decided to take a walk along the nearby river, observing the way the light danced across the water. She carried her sketchbook and pencils with her, eager to capture the scene in all its glory. As she strolled along the riverbank, she noticed a young couple sitting on a bench, holding hands.

Amelie Grabowska couldn't help but feel a pang of nostalgia wash over her. She remembered when she was once in love, and how it had changed her life forever. But that chapter of her life had long since closed, and she was now content with the simplicity of being single.

Without hesitation, Amelie Grabowska climbed aboard the wooden boat, feeling a sense of freedom wash over her.
