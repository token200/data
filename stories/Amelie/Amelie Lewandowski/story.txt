As the days turned into weeks, Amelie Lewandowski found herself inspired by Max's unique perspective on life. She began to see the city in a new light, with every horse, flower, and forgotten alleyway holding secrets waiting to be uncovered. And Max, sensing her creative spark, joined her on every shoot, always ready to share his own stories and laughter.
