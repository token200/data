Using this new information, Tamás Schillaci set a trap for the thief, creating a fake cat-themed exhibit in an abandoned warehouse. He waited patiently inside, armed with nothing but his sharp mind and quick thinking.

When the thief arrived, dressed in a black cat suit, Tamás Schillaci was ready. He calmly approached the suspect, revealed their true identity, and closed the case. The thief's obsession had been both their strength and weakness, and it ultimately led to their downfall.
