Next came the pan-seared scallops, perfectly seared and served atop a bed of truffled risotto – pure perfection. Each dish was a masterclass in balance and restraint, showcasing Tamás's vast knowledge of French cuisine.

Tamás Gauthier scribbled furious notes on her pad, her eyes scanning the menu with an intensity that bordered on obsession. She asked Chef Pierre about his inspiration, his technique, and the secret ingredients that made each dish sing. As she devoured each bite, her face illuminated with delight, a testament to the chef's skill.
