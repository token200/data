Tamás Kabylbekova listened intently as Mrs. Szabó recounted every detail of her daughter's disappearance. He made mental notes, his mind racing with possibilities. As he left the house, he couldn't shake off the feeling that this case would be different from the others.

With his trusty sidekick, József, by his side, Tamás Kabylbekova infiltrated the warehouse and found Sophia safe and sound.
