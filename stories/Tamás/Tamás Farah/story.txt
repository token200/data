Tamás raised an eyebrow. "Private? That's what they told me."

Tamás nodded, taking a seat at the bar as he ordered a whiskey. He was known for his ability to improvise on any instrument, but tonight he was here with his violin specifically.

As he sipped his drink, Tamás Farah's thoughts turned to the mysterious patron who had hired him. There was something intriguing about this woman – her enigmatic smile and piercing green eyes that seemed to see right through him. But what did she want from him?

"Let's get this show started," Tamás whispered, running his fingers over the strings of his instrument.

He closed his eyes and let himself be swept up by the melody, his soul speaking directly to the woman across from him.

When he opened them again, she was standing there, her eyes locked onto his with an unnerving intensity. For a moment, they just stared at each other, the only sound the steady thrum of the violin.

Then Tamás Farah took another deep breath and let his gaze fall away, returning to the music that flowed from his instrument like lifeblood. The performance was over all too soon, but he knew this was just the beginning – a doorway to secrets and desires he had yet to unlock.
