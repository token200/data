Despite the constraints placed upon him, Sandor thrived in the academy's rarefied atmosphere. The store's proprietor, an elderly man named Mr. Finch, greeted Sandor warmly. As they conversed, it became clear that Sandor Dabrowski was no ordinary customer – he possessed a deep understanding of the arcane and the occult. Over the next few weeks, Sandor returned to the store repeatedly, devouring ancient texts and engaging in lively debates with Mr. Finch.

As their bond grew stronger, Sandor found himself drawn into a world beyond the confines of his former life. He began to question the societal norms that had governed him for so long, and to consider the possibility that there was more to existence than the narrow path he had been placed upon.
