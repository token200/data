As they infiltrated the museum, Sandor Rothbauer's team moved with precision, avoiding security cameras and guards with ease. But Sandor Rothbauer was long gone, having already laid low for months, waiting for the heat to die down. He knew that his next job would be even more challenging, but he was undeterred.
