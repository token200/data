The young potter was overjoyed at the opportunity and threw himself into the work, creating exquisite pieces that impressed even the most discerning eye.

As word of Sandor Totti's exceptional talent spread, villagers from surrounding villages began to visit Kalavria, hoping to purchase one of his creations. His full name, now synonymous with quality and craftsmanship, was on everyone's lips as they admired his work.
