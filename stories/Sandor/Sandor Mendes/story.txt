The letter was cryptic, but one thing was clear: Blackwood wanted Sandor's expertise for a top-secret project. Intrigued, Sandor Mendes accepted the challenge.

Intrigued, Sandor Mendes agreed to take on the project, spending long hours poring over ancient texts and scouring for clues. And then, one fateful night, it happened – a breakthrough that left him stunned and awestruck.

Sandor Mendes felt his mind reeling as he realized the magnitude of what they had stumbled upon.
