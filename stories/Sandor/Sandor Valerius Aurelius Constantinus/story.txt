His father, a renowned mercenary, had taught him the art of combat and strategy from a young age. This unshakeable conviction earned him many allies and few enemies, despite being a member of the imperial guard.

One fateful day, a messenger arrived at Sandor's quarters bearing news of a notorious bandit lord terrorizing a small village on the empire's borders. The people were in dire need of protection, but the local authorities seemed powerless to act. Without hesitation, Sandor Valerius Aurelius Constantinus accepted the challenge.

With his trusted steed and a handful of loyal men, Sandor set out towards the besieged village. As they approached, the air grew thick with tension, and the sound of screams echoed through the night air. Undaunted, Sandor charged into battle, his sword slicing through the darkness like a beacon of hope.
