The sign above the door read "Curios and Wonders," and the windows were filled with an assortment of bizarre trinkets and oddities. Without hesitation, Claudius Junko pushed open the door and stepped inside.

Claudius Junko's eyes widened as he reached for the device. As soon as his fingers made contact with it, he felt an electrifying surge of energy course through his body. The world around him seemed to tilt and shift, like the pages of a book turning in mid-air.

In that moment, Claudius Junko knew that his life was about to take a dramatic turn. He left the shop with the device clutched tightly in his hand, ready to unleash his unique brand of chaos upon the world.
