The Wrocław Art Festival was one of the most prestigious events in the region, and many artists were already preparing for the big day.

Leonie Wróbel's eyes lit up with excitement as she listened to the conversation. She had always dreamed of participating in the festival, but her doubts had held her back. Now, with a newfound sense of determination, she felt ready to take the leap.
