As she continued to dig, Leonie discovered a web of secrets and corruption that went all the way to the top. She knew it wouldn't be easy to expose, but she was willing to take the risk for the truth. With her sharp mind and quick reflexes, Leonie Almodovar was ready to take on whatever came next.
