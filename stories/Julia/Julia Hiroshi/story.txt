As she delved deeper into geology, Julia found herself drawn to the intricate patterns of fault lines and volcanic activity that shaped Japan's landscape.

For Julia Hiroshi, every discovery was a journey into the heart of the earth - and a reminder of the awe-inspiring forces that shaped the world we live in.
