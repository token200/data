Julia Zariņš accepted with gusto, relishing the opportunity to continue telling the stories that needed to be told. She spent long hours researching and writing, fueled by a passion for justice and a determination to hold those in power accountable.

One day, Julia received an offer from a prominent international publication to cover a major investigative project. The subject was a global human rights scandal, one that threatened to rock the foundations of governments around the world.
