As he grew older, however, Stanisław began to realize that he couldn't put off his dreams forever. With the support of his siblings and their partners, he started to build a life for himself outside of the bakery. He enrolled in night classes at the local university, studying botany and horticulture with renewed passion.

Today, Stanisław Pētersone is a respected figure in Riga's botanical community, known for his encyclopedic knowledge of local flora and his tireless efforts to preserve the city's natural beauty.
