Rumors swirled about the Polish émigré's past, but no one knew the truth behind his enigmatic smile. Some said he was born into a life of crime, while others whispered that he had made a pact with dark forces to fuel his extraordinary abilities. As the storm raged on outside, Katarzyna and Stanisław Faust shared a nod of respect, each acknowledging the other's prowess without saying a word.
