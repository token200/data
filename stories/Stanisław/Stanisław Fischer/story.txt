Despite his fame, Stanisław Fischer remained largely reclusive, preferring the company of his machines to that of people. As she watched Stanisław Fischer work, mesmerized by the whirring gears and sparks flying from the machine, she knew she had found something truly remarkable.

As Sofia delved deeper into the mysteries of Stanisław Fischer's art, she began to realize that her enigmatic subject was more than just a brilliant mind – he was a visionary, a man who saw the world in ways that others could not.
