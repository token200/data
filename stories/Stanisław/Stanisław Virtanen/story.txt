Stanisław Virtanen's workshop was a treasure trove of curiosities, each piece a testament to his craftsmanship. There were intricate figurines of forest creatures, delicate vessels adorned with vines, and imposing masks that seemed to hold the very essence of the forest within their carved surfaces.
