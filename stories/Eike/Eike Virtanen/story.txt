Years later, as a skilled woodsman, Eike Virtanen had earned a reputation for himself as a master tracker and guide. People would travel from all over to seek his expertise, not just for hunting or navigating the wilderness, but also for his deep understanding of the natural world.

Eike Virtanen smiled, satisfied with their success. He had proven himself once again as a master tracker and guide, and had shared his knowledge with someone who truly appreciated it.
