Eike Yuji listened intently, his expression unreadable behind his glasses. He nodded thoughtfully, his eyes narrowing as Kenji revealed details about the burglaries' target and modus operandi. The more Kenji spoke, the more Eike Yuji seemed to absorb, like a sponge soaking up every detail.
