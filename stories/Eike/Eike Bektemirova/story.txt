Eike Bektemirova was on a mission to find answers. Her family's past held many secrets, and she had spent years searching for any information that might shed light on their mysterious history. The small town of Ravenshire lay nestled deep in the heart of rural Russia, its residents as tight-lipped as they were friendly.

As Eike made her way through the streets, she was greeted by the warm smiles of the locals. They seemed to know her name already - Eike Bektemirova, the curious traveler with a penchant for asking questions.
