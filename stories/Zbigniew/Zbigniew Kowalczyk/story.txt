Intrigued by the challenge, Zbigniew Kowalczyk accepted the case and set out to unravel the mystery. He began by examining the crime scene, scrutinizing every detail, from the faint scratches on the floor to the subtle changes in the lighting of the museum's corridors.

Intrigued, he spent hours studying the layout of the museum and how it interacted with the available lighting.

The next morning, armed with his newfound knowledge, Zbigniew Kowalczyk set out to test his theory. He carefully positioned himself in different spots around the museum, using the reflected light to reveal subtle patterns on the walls and floor. With each step closer to the truth, Zbigniew Kowalczyk's confidence grew. He followed the trail of clues, leading him through winding corridors and finally to a small storage room deep within the museum's basement.

Zbigniew Kowalczyk smiled, knowing that his unique skillset had been the key to unraveling the mystery.
