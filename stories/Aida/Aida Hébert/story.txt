Now, as an adult, Aida Hébert found herself drawn to the lighthouse with a sense of curiosity and adventure.

Despite the tempest, Aida Hébert felt a thrill of excitement as she pushed open the creaky door and stepped inside. The air was musty and filled with the scent of old books, and Aida Hébert's eyes adjusted slowly to the dim light.
