Aida Salemi stepped onto the stage, her eyes scanning the crowded room with confidence. As a renowned violinist, she had performed in some of the world's most prestigious concert halls, but tonight was special – she was here to pay tribute to her idol, the great composer Ludwig van Beethoven.
