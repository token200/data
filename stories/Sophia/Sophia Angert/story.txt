From a young age, Sophia was fascinated by the art of baking and spent countless hours helping her mother in the kitchen.

She traveled the world, studying under some of the most renowned chefs and learning the intricacies of modern cuisine. She was immediately captivated by Sophia's culinary magic and struck up a conversation with the enigmatic chef.
