One day, Gabriele Karlsson received an offer from the city council to create a massive mural on the side of a dilapidated building in a neglected neighborhood. The project was a chance for her to make a real impact on the community. For months, she poured her heart and soul into the artwork, incorporating elements of Swedish folk art and urban graffiti.
