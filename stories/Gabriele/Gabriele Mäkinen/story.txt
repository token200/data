As she worked, Gabriele drew inspiration from her own experiences growing up in Finland. Her parents had struggled with financial insecurity, and she remembered the fear of not knowing where their next meal would come from. Gabriele's name became synonymous with the story, and she received praise from readers, editors, and fellow journalists alike. As she left the newsroom at the end of the day, she felt a sense of pride and accomplishment, knowing that her work had made a difference.

Gabriele Mäkinen had truly come into her own as a journalist, and it was clear that this was just the beginning of an illustrious career. With a fierce determination to tell stories that needed to be told, she looked forward to tackling the next challenge that came her way.
