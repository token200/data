Gabriele Petrov was a man shrouded in mystery. A brilliant physicist with an uncanny ability to think outside the box, he had spent years working on his most ambitious project yet: a machine capable of manipulating time itself.

Rumors swirled around him, whispers of a genius who had defected from the Soviet Union and now worked for the US government in secret. Some said he was mad, others that he was a visionary. But one thing was certain - Gabriele Petrov was not to be underestimated.

As he emerged on the other side, Gabriele felt a rush of exhilaration. The world was his for the taking. He would change history, reshape destiny. Nothing would ever be the same again.
