As the proprietor of the local bakery, "Trinkenschuh's Sweet Delights," she was known for her delectable pastries and warm smile. Helena's ears perked up as she listened to the whispers of suspicion and concern. She knew that this family, like any other, deserved a chance to prove themselves. So, with a mischievous glint in her eye, Helena decided to take matters into her own hands.

She carefully arranged an assortment of her famous strudels and apple cakes, along with a handwritten note inviting them to come by the bakery and meet the locals.
