Xu Aelius Hadrianus's eyes lit up as he accepted the challenge. He spent the next several weeks pouring over blueprints and consulting with the finest engineers in Rome. As he worked, his mind wandered back to his childhood, growing up in the countryside and building intricate wooden models of buildings with his brother.

As the temple neared completion, Xu Aelius Hadrianus couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and accomplishment. He had truly outdone himself this time, and it was clear that his hard work and dedication had paid off.

As he basked in the admiration of the crowd, Xu's thoughts turned to his family back in the countryside. He knew that their modest means had been transformed by his success, and that they could now live comfortably thanks to his hard work.

Xu Aelius Hadrianus smiled to himself as he looked out over the sea of faces, knowing that he had truly found his place among the elite of Roman society.
