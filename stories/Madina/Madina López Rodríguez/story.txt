Madina's abuela encouraged her to showcase their bakery's wares, and Madina was thrilled at the opportunity. Despite the pressure, Madina remained calm and focused, drawing on the lessons she learned from her abuela and years of practice. The judges were impressed by her skills, and Madina López Rodríguez was declared the winner of the competition.
