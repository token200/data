Madina spent months documenting the stories of those fleeing war and persecution, from Syria to Eastern Europe. Her articles sparked widespread debate and raised awareness about the plight of refugees worldwide.

Her tireless work ethic earned her numerous awards and accolades, including the prestigious European Journalist Prize.
