Aino Morgan stood at the edge of the dense forest, her eyes fixed on the small cabin in the distance. She had been searching for it for weeks, following every lead, every rumor, and every hint that might bring her closer to uncovering the truth about her family's past.

She had been warned about the dangers that lurked within – venomous snakes, steep cliffs, and treacherous paths. But Aino Morgan was not one to be deterred.
