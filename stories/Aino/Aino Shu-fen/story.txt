As she wrote her article, Aino poured her heart and soul into the piece, pouring over facts and figures, conducting additional research, and seeking out diverse perspectives from local community members. Her editor praised her work, saying it was some of the best reporting he'd seen in years.

Despite the controversy surrounding the development project, Aino's article helped raise awareness about the complex issues at play. The Tokyo Times received numerous requests for interviews with Kenji and other protesters, who were suddenly thrust into the national spotlight.
