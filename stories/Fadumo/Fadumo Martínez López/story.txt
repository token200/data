She saw an opportunity to investigate the conditions under which these refugees were being held and to shed light on the often-overlooked struggles they faced. With her camera crew in tow, she spent several days documenting the center's operations and speaking with both staff members and asylum seekers.

As Fadumo delved deeper into her investigation, she began to notice disturbing trends. She also spoke with several asylum seekers who had been detained for years, awaiting a decision on their status.

Fadumo's exposé on the asylum seeker center sparked outrage among some in the Spanish government, but it also resonated with many Spaniards who were passionate about social justice. Her reporting was praised by colleagues and critics alike, and she found herself at the forefront of a national conversation about immigration policy.

Through her work, Fadumo Martínez López had proven that even the most marginalized voices deserved to be heard.
