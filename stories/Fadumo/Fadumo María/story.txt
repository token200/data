Fadumo María was a young Somali artist known for her vibrant and expressive paintings that captured the essence of her homeland. As Fadumo María grew older, her passion for art only intensified. One day, Fadumo María received an invitation from a prominent museum in Amsterdam to showcase her work in a solo exhibition. She was overjoyed at the opportunity to share her art with a wider audience and set off on the long journey to Europe.
