She traveled from town to town, camera in hand, meeting women who were brave, resilient, and determined to build better lives for themselves and their families.

Despite the challenges she faced, Abdi was determined to provide for her children and create a brighter future for them. Fadumo was deeply moved by Abdi's story and took photographs that captured her strength and determination.
