Therese Petersen's groundbreaking discovery had far-reaching implications for humanity, and her name would soon be synonymous with innovation and brilliance. But as she looked out into the bright new dawn, Therese knew that this was just the beginning - there were still countless secrets waiting to be uncovered, and she was ready to take on the challenge.
