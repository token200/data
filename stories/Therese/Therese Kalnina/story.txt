Therese's latest installation, "Echoes in the Abyss," had been causing quite a stir in the art world. She had to perform "Echoes in the Abyss" live for a audience of wealthy patrons. The night of the event arrived, and Therese's studio was abuzz with excitement. Her assistants scrambled to set up the performance space while Therese rehearsed her lines for a spoken word piece that would accompany the installation. As the guests began to arrive, Therese took center stage, donning her mirrored mask and embracing the chaos of the soundscape.
