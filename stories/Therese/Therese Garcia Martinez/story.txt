The night of the festival arrived, and Therese's artwork was met with thunderous applause. Critics praised her use of color and composition, while potential clients clamored to buy her paintings. As Therese Garcia Martinez stood amidst the celebration, surrounded by her peers and admirers, she knew that her dreams had finally come true.
