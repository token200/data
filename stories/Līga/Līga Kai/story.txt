As she grew older, Līga's ability to communicate with plants became more pronounced. She could sense when they were healthy or stressed, and could even convince them to grow faster or stronger. The locals began to notice her skills and would often bring her injured animals or plants to help heal them.

She spent hours talking to the affected plants, coaxing them back to health and encouraging new growth. Her efforts paid off, and soon the fields were thriving once again.

Word of her abilities spread quickly, and soon people from all over were coming to seek Līga's help. A local nursery owner asked her to help with a struggling orchard, while a group of scientists requested her expertise on a rare species of plant. Līga was happy to oblige, using her unique talent to help those in need.

Despite the demands on her time, Līga Kai never lost sight of what truly mattered - the natural world. She continued to work tirelessly, using her gifts to promote sustainability and conservation. As she grew older, her reputation as a plant whisperer spread far and wide, earning her a place among the leading experts in her field.
