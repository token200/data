After completing art school, Līga began to make a name for herself on the city's street art scene.

The piece depicted the city's transformation from a medieval trading hub to a thriving modern metropolis. Passersby couldn't help but stop and stare at the colorful, dynamic images of Riga's past and present.

Līga Ivanov was interviewed by local media outlets about her inspiration for the piece.
