But Līga Singh remained undeterred, knowing that her reporting was crucial to bringing about justice for the affected people.

As she delved deeper into the story, Līga Singh encountered resistance from all sides. Government officials and corporate executives refused to comment, while some of her colleagues questioned her methods. Yet, she persisted, driven by a deep sense of moral obligation to tell the truth.
