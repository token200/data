After completing her studies, she had landed a job in Moscow, but found herself feeling lost and disconnected from her heritage. She began to write about her experiences and memories of Latvia, hoping to recapture the sense of belonging she had as a child.

But something about Janis's genuine interest put her at ease, and she began to speak about the stories she wanted to tell – stories that blended history, folklore, and modern-day life.
