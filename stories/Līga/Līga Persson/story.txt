As she grew older, Līga's love for music only deepened. She began to explore other instruments, including the accordion and the violin, and even started composing her own music. Her talent was evident from an early age, and she quickly gained recognition within her community.

Despite her natural ability, Līga faced numerous challenges in her pursuit of a music career. Many people doubted that a woman from rural Latvia could succeed in the competitive world of classical music. However, Līga persevered, driven by her passion for her craft.
