Līga Horváth was no stranger to hard work and determination. Born in Riga, Latvia, she had always been drawn to the world of fashion, but it wasn't until she spent a year studying abroad in Paris that she truly found her passion. As she sipped her coffee, Līga's mind began to wander back to the meeting. The investor had raised some concerns about production costs and market demand, but Līga was confident that she could overcome these obstacles. She had a vision for a brand that not only looked beautiful but also told a story of sustainability and social responsibility.

As she left the café and stepped out into the rain once again, Līga felt a sense of pride and accomplishment. She knew that there would be ups and downs ahead, but with hard work, determination, and a clear vision, she was ready to take on whatever challenges came her way.

Līga Horváth disappeared into the crowd, lost in thought as she contemplated the future of her business.
