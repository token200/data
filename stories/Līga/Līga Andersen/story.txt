As Līga Andersen listened intently, she began to piece together a narrative that would change her understanding of herself forever.

Līga Andersen's journey had taken an unexpected turn, but one thing was clear: her life would never be the same. She had uncovered a piece of herself that she never knew existed, and in doing so, had found a new sense of purpose. With her great-grandmother Eliza Jensen as a guide, Līga set out to share her family's story with the world, determined to keep their legacy alive.
