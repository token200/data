Years later, Ricardo's obsession with energy had taken him to the forefront of a groundbreaking research project. He had assembled a team of talented engineers and physicists to help him develop a revolutionary new technology that could power entire cities without burning fossil fuels.

But despite their progress, Ricardo couldn't shake the feeling that he was running out of time. "We can harness limitless clean energy!"

But he was undaunted, driven by his conviction that this breakthrough had the potential to transform the world.
