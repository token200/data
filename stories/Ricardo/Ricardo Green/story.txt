But it wasn't until he formed a group called "The Melodic Outcasts" that Ricardo truly found his calling. With his unique blend of rock and folk influences, The Melodic Outcasts quickly gained a following in the small town, with Ricardo's soulful voice and intricate guitar work at the forefront.
