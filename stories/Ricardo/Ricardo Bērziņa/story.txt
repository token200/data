After completing his studies, Ricardo traveled to various parts of Europe, soaking up inspiration from the masters and honing his craft.

Upon returning to Riga, Ricardo established himself as a respected figure in the local art community. His unique blend of traditional techniques and modern flair earned him both critical acclaim and commercial success. Galleries clamored for his work, and collectors sought out pieces by the enigmatic Ricardo Bērziņa.
