Alejandro Simon was a man with a passion for art, music, and life itself. Born in the vibrant city of Barcelona, Spain, Alejandro had grown up surrounded by the works of Gaudí, Miró, and Dalí – masters whose creativity and imagination inspired him to pursue his own dreams.

It wasn't long before Alejandro discovered his own talent for music – he began playing the guitar at a young age, teaching himself the intricate rhythms and melodies that flowed from his fingers.

Alejandro's success was meteoric, but he never lost sight of his artistic vision. He continued to write and record music that reflected his love for life, nature, and humanity.
