His love for nature only deepened as he got older, and he eventually began to explore the world beyond Ravenswood's borders.

Ragnar Carlburg had always been drawn to adventure and was not one to shy away from a challenge. Ragnar Carlburg felt an inexplicable connection to the map, as if it had been waiting for him all along. He spent the next few days poring over the map, trying to unravel its mysteries.
