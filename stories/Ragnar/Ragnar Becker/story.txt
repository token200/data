With this newfound knowledge, Ragnar's sense of purpose shifted. He began to use his skills to protect not only himself, but also the people and places he cared about most. His legend grew, as did the respect he commanded from those around him. Ragnar Bjornsson Becker had finally found his place in the world, and it was a mantle he wore with pride.

Years went by, and Ragnar's name became synonymous with courage and honor. He continued to fight for what was right, always staying true to himself and his heritage.
