Her talent didn't go unnoticed, and soon she was accepted into the prestigious Riga Art Academy. Under the guidance of renowned instructors, Peng honed her skills and developed a unique style that blended traditional Latvian folk art with modern techniques.

As guests mingled and admired her work, Peng couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and accomplishment. Throughout the evening, Peng Pētersone received accolades and congratulations from fellow artists and admirers. As she accepted an award for her outstanding contribution to Latvian art, she felt a sense of gratitude towards her mother, who had instilled in her the love of art that had led her to this moment.
