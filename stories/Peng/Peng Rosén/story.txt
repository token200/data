Peng Rosén, a talented young chef from Stockholm, Sweden, had always been passionate about cooking. Growing up in a family that valued traditional Swedish cuisine, Peng spent most of her childhood helping her mother in the kitchen, learning the intricacies of Scandinavian cooking.

As she grew older, Peng's passion for food only intensified, and she began to explore more modern and innovative culinary techniques. She attended culinary school, where she honed her skills and developed a unique style that blended traditional Swedish flavors with international flair.
