Peng Michalska was known for her unorthodox approach to art. As a painter, she rejected traditional techniques and instead used unconventional materials like leaves, sand, and even food coloring. Her latest piece, "Nature's Palette," was no exception.

When asked about her inspiration, Peng Michalska smiled enigmatically. "I want to challenge people's perceptions of what art should look like," she said. "We're so used to thinking of art as something polished and refined, but I believe that nature has its own beauty."

Peng Michalska was determined to keep pushing the limits of what art could be, even if it meant embracing uncertainty along the way.
