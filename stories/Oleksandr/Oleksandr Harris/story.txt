Oleksandr's determination to uncover the truth about his father's disappearance had become an all-consuming force in his life. His work took him from the seedy underbelly of Kiev's nightlife to the high-stakes world of international espionage.

Despite the danger that lurked around every corner, Oleksandr Harris persevered, driven by a fierce loyalty to his father's memory. Together, they had uncovered some astonishing secrets - enough to make even the most hardened operative take notice. But there were those who would stop at nothing to silence them, to erase Oleksandr Harris from the face of the earth.

As the stakes grew higher, Oleksandr's determination showed no signs of waning. His name became synonymous with bravery, his face etched into the minds of those who dared to oppose him. And when he finally uncovered the truth about his father's disappearance, nothing would ever be the same again. The world had better watch out for Oleksandr Petrovich Harris - he was coming for it.
