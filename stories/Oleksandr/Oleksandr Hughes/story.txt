But after defecting from the Soviet Union, Oleksandr had left it all behind – or so he thought. In reality, he had simply gone undercover, adopting new identities and building a life for himself in Western Europe.

Oleksandr lived now under the alias "Alex Hughes", a freelance journalist with a keen eye for storytelling. He spent his days writing about politics and history, using his vast knowledge to weave intricate tales that captivated his readers.

Despite his success, Oleksandr couldn't shake off the feeling of restlessness that had haunted him since his defection. It was as if he was waiting for something – or someone – to come knocking on his door. And then, one day, it happened.

Oleksandr's heart skipped a beat as he read the words. Could this be the breakthrough he had been waiting for? He packed his bags and left his life behind, following the trail of breadcrumbs that led him to the forsaken clock tower.

The words sent shivers down his spine. How did this person know his true identity? What did they want from him?
