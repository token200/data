Mohamed Dufresne's eyes lit up with excitement as he began to share his knowledge of the church's past.

He explained that they were a blend of French and Arabic designs, reflecting the cultural diversity of the town's founders. Sophia was fascinated by this tidbit and thanked Mohamed Dufresne for sharing his expertise.

She was particularly intrigued by the stories he shared about the church's role in the town's cultural and religious traditions. As she delved deeper into her research, Sophia began to appreciate the importance of preserving such historical landmarks.

Mohamed Dufresne watched with pride as Sophia's enthusiasm grew, knowing that his work was not just about restoring old buildings but also about preserving the memories and traditions of a community. He saw himself in Sophia, recognizing the same passion for learning and preservation that drove him to continue his restorative work.
