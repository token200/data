As he grew older, Mohamed's interest in cooking only deepened. He enrolled in culinary school and honed his skills, experimenting with new ingredients and techniques. After completing his studies, he worked in several restaurants, learning from some of the best chefs in Mexico.

The article was a huge success, and soon Mohamed Ruiz García found himself at the forefront of Mexico's culinary scene. Foodies from all over the country came to taste his creative dishes, which blended traditional flavors with modern twists.

Mohamed Ruiz García's restaurant became a beloved institution in Guanajuato, attracting visitors from far and wide. His passion for cooking had taken him on an incredible journey, from humble beginnings to culinary stardom.
