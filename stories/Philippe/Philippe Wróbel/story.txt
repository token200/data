As he snapped photos of the museum's facade, Philippe couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and belonging. This was his city, after all, and he had always felt most at home behind the camera. With each click of the shutter, Philippe Wróbel transported himself to a bygone era, one filled with vibrant colors and rich textures.
