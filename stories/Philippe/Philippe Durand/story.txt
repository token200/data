Rumors spread about his extensive network of connections and his uncanny skills in hand-to-hand combat. Some claimed that on quiet nights, you could still hear the sound of his gun being cocked, even though he was long retired.

Philippe Durand raised an eyebrow, a hint of amusement dancing in his eyes. "And what makes you think I'm still in this game?" he asked, taking a sip of his wine.

Sophia's gaze locked onto his, piercing through the veil of secrecy that had long surrounded Philippe Durand. "I know you're still out there," she said, her voice dripping with conviction. "And I need your expertise to get me out."
