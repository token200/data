As an adult, Kirill's fascination with the macabre only grew stronger. He began to explore the city's abandoned asylums and hospitals, seeking out the decaying remains of forgotten lives. People began to take notice of the enigmatic young artist, who seemed to be summoning demons from the very fabric of reality itself.

As Kirill Lācis's reputation grew, so did his obsession with the unknown.
