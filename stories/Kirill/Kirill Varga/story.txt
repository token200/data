But Kirill Varga knew the truth. He was still alive, still watching, still waiting for the next opportunity to strike. And when that day came, he would be ready.

He thought back to his days in the spy game, to the thrill of the chase, the rush of adrenaline that came with every mission.

He knew he had lost some pieces of himself along the way, but Kirill Ivanovich Varga was still out there, waiting for his next move. And when it came, he would be ready.
