As Kirill grew older, his love for art only deepened. He spent years studying at the Moscow Art School, honing his skills and learning from some of the most renowned artists of his time. After completing his studies, he set out to make a name for himself in the art world.

Kirill jumped at the chance, spending weeks researching and sketching out ideas. He poured his heart and soul into each piece, infusing them with his own unique energy and style. The result was breathtaking – colorful, dynamic murals that captured the essence of the city's musical heartbeat.
