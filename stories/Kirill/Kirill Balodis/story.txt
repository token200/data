But after leaving the army, Kirill found himself feeling lost and without purpose. He drifted from one job to another, never quite finding something that truly satisfied him. As they worked, Kirill's confidence began to grow once more. He felt alive again, like he had finally found a purpose that truly mattered.

As the investigation continued, Kirill Balodis proved himself to be an invaluable asset to Sophia's team.
