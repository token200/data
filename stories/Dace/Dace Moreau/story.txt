As they sat down in his opulent office, Dace Moreau was greeted by the sound of soft jazz music and the faint scent of expensive cologne. She made a mental note to investigate further, her journalist's instincts on high alert. The businessman, whose name was Marcus Thompson, seemed relaxed and charming, but Dace Moreau could sense a hint of nervousness beneath his suave exterior.

But as the conversation wore on, she grew increasingly direct and confrontational, pressing Marcus Thompson for specifics about his involvement in the scandal. Dace Moreau smiled sweetly, her eyes locked on his. "I think you're not telling me what you really know, Mr. Thompson."

Marcus Thompson shifted uncomfortably in his seat, before finally nodding curtly. Dace Moreau smiled again, this time with a hint of triumph.
