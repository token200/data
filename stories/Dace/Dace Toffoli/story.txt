She began playing bass at age 12 and soon became hooked on creating her own unique sound. After forming bands and playing countless gigs around town, she finally decided to take a leap of faith and pursue music full-time.

Dace Toffoli's solo career took off with the release of her debut EP, "Electric Bloom." Despite her growing success, Dace remained committed to staying true to her artistic vision. The sounds of the city outside her window – car horns, chatter, and wailing sirens – blended with the thrumming basslines still resonating within her from the previous night's performance.

As she watched the stars twinkling above, Dace couldn't help but think about how far she'd come since those early days playing tiny clubs in her hometown. The journey had been long and winding, filled with setbacks and moments of self-doubt, but here she was – on the cusp of stardom.
