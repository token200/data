The collector was taken aback by the raw power and emotion emanating from the artwork. He realized that Dace's talent went far beyond mere technical skill, and he offered to purchase not one, but multiple pieces from her oeuvre.

Dace Jankowska accepted the offer, knowing that this opportunity would catapult her career forward. With Helena Anders' blessing, she embarked on a journey of artistic exploration, pushing the boundaries of what was possible with paint and canvas.

As Dace's fame grew, so did her reputation for innovative thinking. Critics praised her work as a reflection of her unique perspective, one that blended the old with the new.
