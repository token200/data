It was moments like these that Dace Alsbach cherished most - alone time with nature.

Her mother, a skilled healer, would often joke that Dace's fascination with machines was just a phase, but as she grew older, it became clear that this was more than just a passing interest.

Dace Alsbach had always been drawn to the mysterious and unknown, and her love of machinery only served to fuel her curiosity. But it wasn't until she stumbled upon an ancient text that hinted at the existence of a long-lost technology that Dace's true passion was ignited.
