Dace Wei-lun was overjoyed by the opportunity, and threw herself into preparing for the show. She spent long hours in her studio, perfecting her latest pieces and experimenting with new techniques. Her parents, who had always encouraged her to pursue her passion, beamed with pride as they watched their daughter's career take off.

Dace Wei-lun was nervous but determined, as she began to set up her paintings and prepare for the evening's festivities.
