This talent would serve him well in his future endeavors.

Intrigued, Dace sprang into action, racing against time to track down the source of the threat. His skills proved more than a match for the perpetrators, and he was able to contain the damage before it spread.

As the night wore on, Dace's thoughts turned to his own personal demons. The pressures of his job weighed heavily on him, sometimes making it difficult to distinguish between reality and fantasy. But one thing remained constant: his unwavering commitment to protecting others from harm.

Dace Korolev disappeared into the shadows once more, his work done for the night. Though he may have been a ghost to some, those who knew him understood that beneath the surface lay a complex web of emotions, desires, and motivations. He was no hero, but he was driven by a strong sense of purpose, one that kept him moving forward into the unknown.
