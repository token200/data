Dace's dedication to uncovering the truth earned her numerous awards and recognition from her peers. However, it also made her a target for powerful interests who sought to silence her. She received threatening messages and was followed by mysterious individuals, but Dace remained resolute in her pursuit of justice.

One evening, as she pored over documents in her small apartment, Dace's mind wandered back to her abuela's stories. The determination in her eyes had become a defining feature of her character. As she scribbled notes and pieced together the evidence, Dace Vargas Llosa knew that her work was far from over.
