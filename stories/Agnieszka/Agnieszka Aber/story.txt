It was a symphony of sounds that had been hidden from her before, but now, like a key turning in a lock, she understood.

The secrets began to reveal themselves to Agnieszka Aber - the language of the trees, the songs of the creatures, and the ancient history etched into the bark of the forest's oldest residents. She felt Marianne's presence around her, guiding her on this journey of discovery.

And she was ready, armed with the knowledge and magic that had been passed down to her by Marianne Aber, her beloved mentor and guide.
