As she wandered through the woods, Agnieszka's mind would often wander to her childhood dreams. She had always wanted to travel the world, to experience different cultures and meet new people. But life had taken a different path, and she found herself stuck in this small village, surrounded by the same familiar faces and routines.

The villagers noticed the change in her, too, and they would often remark on how bright and cheerful she seemed.

Agnieszka Mitchell was finally feeling like herself again, and it was all thanks to Max's presence in her life.
