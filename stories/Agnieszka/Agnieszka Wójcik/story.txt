As Agnieszka delved deeper into the story, she found herself in increasing danger. Threats and intimidation messages began to pour in, warning her to drop the investigation. But Agnieszka Wójcik was not one to back down from a challenge. She continued to push forward, using her journalistic skills to expose the truth.
