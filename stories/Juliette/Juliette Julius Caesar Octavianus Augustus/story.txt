With an air of confidence that belied his 25 years, Octavianus advised them to adopt a firm stance against the conspirators.

In the aftermath of his triumph over Antony and Brutus, Octavianus Augustus was hailed as Rome's savior. He went on to establish the Principate, a system of government that would endure for centuries, and cemented his place as one of history's greatest leaders.

Despite the weight of responsibility resting upon her shoulders, Juliette watched with pride as Octavianus Augustus solidified his legacy. As she looked into his eyes, she saw a man who had transformed himself from an ambitious youth to a wise and just ruler.
