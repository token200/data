Rothbauer's past was a closely guarded secret, known only to a select few. It was in these rarefied circles that he honed his skills, learning to navigate the complex web of relationships and alliances that made up the high society.

As the night wore on, Rothbauer's thoughts turned to his next move - a tantalizing prospect that had been percolating in the back of his mind for weeks. He leaned forward, his eyes narrowing as he steeled himself for the challenge ahead. This was what it meant to be Mikolaj Rothbauer, master thief and virtuoso of deception.
