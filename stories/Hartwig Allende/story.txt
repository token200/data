As the years went by, Hartwig's empire grew, but so did the rumors surrounding his true nature. One thing was certain: Hartwig Allende was not to be underestimated. His cunning and intelligence had allowed him to outmaneuver his opponents at every turn, always staying one step ahead of those who sought to bring him down.

As the sun set over the Red Wolf ranch, casting long shadows across the dusty terrain, it was clear that Hartwig Allende was a man without equal.
