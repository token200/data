One fateful night, as Safiya worked tirelessly in her laboratory, a sudden burst of insight struck her. The pieces fell into place, and she realized the solution to the puzzle that had been eluding her for so long. Eureka! With her breakthrough in hand, Safiya Kozlowski knew that she was on the cusp of something monumental. The world would soon be hers to shape and mold. Safiya's name became synonymous with groundbreaking discovery as word of her Eclipse project spread like wildfire.
