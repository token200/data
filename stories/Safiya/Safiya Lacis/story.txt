Suddenly, a figure emerged from the shadows. It was an old woman, her face lined with age and experience. "Dr. Lacis," the woman said, her voice barely above a whisper. "I've been waiting for you."

As Safiya Lacis listened, tears streamed down her face. She had finally found the answers she had been searching for, and it was all because of this mysterious stranger's kindness. "Thank you," she said, her voice shaking with emotion.
