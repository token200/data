Her intelligence and wit earned her recognition from the local magistrates, who sought her counsel on matters of law and governance. Safiya presented her case with confidence and clarity, drawing on her knowledge of philosophy and law to craft a comprehensive plan.

For the next decade, Safiya traveled the empire, using her unique blend of wisdom and wit to help govern its many provinces.
