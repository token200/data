As Adam grew older, his interest in cryptography only deepened. He went on to study computer science in college, where he honed his skills in programming languages and algorithms. But it was during his senior year that Adam stumbled upon a hidden passion - cryptanalysis.

As he delved deeper into the project, Adam couldn't help but feel a sense of pride knowing that he was bringing his skills to bear for this mysterious client. And though he still had no idea who they truly were or what their end goal was, Adam Piatek knew that he had stumbled upon something big - something that could change the course of cryptography forever.
