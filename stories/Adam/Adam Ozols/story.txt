A former hacker turned private investigator, Adam spent most of his days digging through digital trails, searching for clues that would lead him to the truth.

His office, a dingy little room above a vintage clothing store in downtown Los Angeles, was a reflection of his lifestyle. Faded posters of 80s punk rock bands adorned the walls, and the air reeked of stale cigarettes. Adam's computer hummed quietly in the corner, its screen glowing with an endless array of code.
