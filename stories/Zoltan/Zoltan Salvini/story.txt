He'd spent years studying the ancient art of alchemy, pouring over dusty tomes and practicing his craft in secret. But after a fateful experiment went wrong, leaving him with a rare condition that forced him to hide his true nature from the world, Zoltan Salvini had been forced to flee.

Now, as he walked through the familiar streets, he couldn't help but feel like an outsider. Everyone seemed to know each other, while he was still a stranger in their midst. He'd come back to Ravenswood hoping to reconnect with old friends and maybe even find some answers about his condition.

Zoltan Salvini's heart skipped a beat as he approached her, feeling a mix of emotions he hadn't experienced in years. Emily smiled warmly when she saw him, and he felt a pang of guilt for not being able to reciprocate it fully - his condition had left him feeling...off.

It was as if coming back to Ravenswood had given him permission to be honest with himself, and maybe even with others.
