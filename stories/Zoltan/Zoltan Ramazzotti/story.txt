As he grew older, his interest in taxidermy began as a hobby but soon became an all-consuming passion. He spent countless hours perfecting his craft, learning to preserve and pose animals with uncanny realism.

Zoltán Ramazzotti's latest creation was a stunningly lifelike owl perched atop a branch.
