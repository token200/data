Curious, Zoltan made his way to the warehouse, where he found himself face to face with a mysterious woman. She introduced herself as "The Architect," and revealed that she had been watching Zoltan for some time, impressed by his cunning and skill.

Zoltan raised an eyebrow, intrigued despite himself. He had always relished a challenge, and this one seemed too good to pass up.

With The Architect's guidance, Zoltan set out on his latest adventure. Together, they planned and executed the heist with precision, leaving behind a trail of confusion and deception that would take weeks to unravel.
