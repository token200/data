Born into a family of modest means, Zoltan had always felt the need to prove himself, to show the world what he was capable of. He spent years honing his skills, studying the art of manipulation and misdirection, until he became a force to be reckoned with in the underworld.

As Zoltan Sanchez-Gijon, he had fooled some of the most discerning people in the city, convincing them that he was who they thought he was. He would change his accent, his mannerisms, and even his appearance to blend in seamlessly with his surroundings.

But Zoltan knew that his life as a con artist couldn't last forever. He would eventually have to leave Sophia behind, to continue living the double life he had built. And so, with a heavy heart, he made the decision to walk away.

She knew that Zoltan Sanchez-Gijon was a complex person, with layers as deep as the city itself. And she wondered what lay beneath his charming smile, what secrets he kept hidden behind those piercing green eyes.
