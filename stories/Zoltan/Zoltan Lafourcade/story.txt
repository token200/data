Zoltan Lafourcade smiled to himself, a low, enigmatic smile that hinted at secrets and promises yet to be kept. He signaled Jack for another round, his eyes never leaving the spot where the mysterious woman had vanished. For in the world of espionage and intrigue, Zoltan Lafourcade was always on the move, always playing the long game – and he had a feeling this one would be worth the wait.

As the night wore on, whispers spread like wildfire about the enigmatic stranger in black, his presence seeming to draw the very shadows themselves into his orbit.
