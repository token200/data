As he grew older, Gaius' passion for art only deepened. He began studying music at a local conservatory, learning to play the guitar and compose his own pieces. Years later, when asked about his artistic journey, Gaius Jiménez López smiled humbly. "It's been an incredible ride," he said. He continued to work with community centers and non-profits, creating murals that celebrated local culture and promoted social change.
