Gabriel spent the next several weeks working behind the scenes, using his resources to fund the orphanage's operations and improve living conditions for the children. As news of his efforts spread, more people began to take notice of Gaius Gabriel's good deeds, and whispers of a "angel" among spies started to circulate.

As word of his heroics reached the spy agency, Gabriel's colleagues were shocked by the revelation that their enigmatic colleague had a heart of gold. They showered him with praise and accolades, but Gabriel remained humble, preferring to focus on the work at hand.

Though his work as a spy was now all but over, Gaius Gabriel knew that his legacy would live on through the lives he had touched. He continued to work behind the scenes, using his skills to make the world a better place. And though his name might not be well-known, those who knew him were eternally grateful for the kindness and compassion of Gaius Gabriel.
