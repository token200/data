As Gaius wandered through Ravenswood, she was met with both curiosity and suspicion. Some saw her as a threat, while others begged for her help. The air around her seemed to shimmer and tremble, as if the very fabric of reality was being warped by her presence.

Sophia felt an inexplicable pull towards the mysterious woman, as if she had stumbled upon a long-lost part of herself. Gaius Dabrowska smiled enigmatically, beckoning Sophia closer with a gentle gesture. And in that moment, the young girl knew that her life would never be the same again.
