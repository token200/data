Gaius Petrovich knew that uncovering the truth would not be easy. There were people who wanted the case to remain unsolved, and they would stop at nothing to keep it buried. But Gaius was undeterred, driven by a sense of justice and a burning need to find out what had happened to Lily.

Gaius Petrovich knew that he had to act quickly, before his colleague could silence him forever. With the help of an old friend from Ravenswood, Gaius was able to gather evidence and build a case against his former colleague.
