Gaius Zielinski's bravery and skill had saved the day once again, earning him the respect and admiration of his fellow villagers. They celebrated his victory with a feast in his honor, and Gaius was beaming with pride as he raised a cup of ale to toast his own name: Gaius Zielinski.
