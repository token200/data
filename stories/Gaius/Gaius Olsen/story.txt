As Gaius unlocked the case and plucked the Starlight Serenade from its pedestal, a shiver ran down his spine. He felt invincible, like nothing could stop him from achieving his ultimate goal: world domination through art theft. Gaius Olsen vanished into the night, leaving behind only a whisper of his presence and the faint scent of cigar smoke. He would next strike at the most unexpected place, always staying one step ahead of his pursuers. For Gaius Augustus Olsen was a thief like no other, and his legend would live on forever.
