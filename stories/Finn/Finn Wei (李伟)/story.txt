Finn was both impressed and concerned. He knew that Maya's discovery could have catastrophic consequences if not addressed promptly. Together, they worked tirelessly to patch the vulnerability and ensure Echo's security.

As they worked, Finn couldn't help but think about his full name, Lee Wei, and how it had led him to this moment. His surname, Wei, meant "bright" or "intelligent" in Chinese, a fitting description for a cybersecurity expert who had spent his life illuminating the darkest corners of the digital world.
