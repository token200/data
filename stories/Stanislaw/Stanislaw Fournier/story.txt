Stanislaw's skills as an artist and musician proved invaluable in his missions, allowing him to blend in seamlessly with his surroundings.

Yet, despite his success, he couldn't shake off the feeling of being an outsider. His true identity remained a closely guarded secret, even from those who claimed to be his closest allies.
