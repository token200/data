Now, as a young adult, Halima Muller found herself drawn back to the windmill time and again.

As she flipped through the yellowed pages, Halima was captivated by the stories of Henry's life - his struggles, triumphs, and passions.

Halima Muller spent hours poring over the diary, imagining what it would have been like to meet Henry himself.
