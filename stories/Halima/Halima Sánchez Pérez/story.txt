Halima Sánchez Pérez poured her heart and soul into the shop, creating a menu that showcased traditional Spanish treats alongside innovative fusion desserts. Her hard work paid off as customers began to flock to the shop, drawn by the heavenly aromas wafting from the display cases.

She continued to innovate and experiment, always pushing the boundaries of what was possible with traditional Spanish desserts.
