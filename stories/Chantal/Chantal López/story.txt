One evening, Chantal López found herself at a particularly raucous gallery opening, surrounded by collectors, curators, and fellow artists. Chantal López couldn't help but feel a twinge of jealousy as she watched him effortlessly charm the other guests. As Chantal López made her way home, she couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and accomplishment.
