For Stanislawa Mendes, there was no greater thrill than uncovering a piece of history that had been lost for centuries. She reveled in the quiet moments spent alone in the field, the wind in her hair, the sun beating down upon her face. It was in these moments that she felt most alive, connected to the very earth beneath her feet.
