Stanislawa's own artistic talents were undeniable. A talented painter and sculptor, she found solace in her creative pursuits during times of turmoil. Her works, infused with symbolism and imagery from Polish folklore, seemed to capture the essence of the human experience.

As her reputation grew, so did the demand for her work. Stanislawa was in high demand as a lecturer and consultant, sought out by artists, writers, and intellectuals from around the world. Her seminars on creativity and self-discovery became legendary, with students and colleagues alike hanging on her every word.

Despite her success, Stanislawa remained true to herself and her art. She continued to paint and sculpt, pouring her heart and soul into each piece. Her works became a testament to her boundless energy and creativity, inspiring generations of artists and thinkers to come.
