Stanislawa listened intently, her mind racing with ideas for her own projects: a novel exploring the intersection of history and mythology, set against the backdrop of Poland's tumultuous past.

As they delved deeper into conversation, the café's atmosphere seemed to fade away, leaving only the two friends lost in their own world. Stanislawa Errani's thoughts turned to her long-abandoned dream of publishing a collection of short stories, weaving tales that blended fantasy and reality.

Zbigniew, sensing her hesitation, reached across the table and grasped her hand. "You have so much talent, Stasia," he said, his voice filled with conviction. "Don't let fear hold you back."

Stanislawa's eyes met his, shining with gratitude. "Thanks, Zbigniew. You've always been my biggest supporter." In that moment, the connection between them felt stronger than ever – a testament to the unbreakable bond forged during those formative years at university.
