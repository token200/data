Despite her tough exterior, Grace had a soft spot for stray animals and often took in strays at her home, where they were lovingly cared for by her loyal cat, Luna. Her friends and family affectionately referred to her as "G-Ma" due to her no-nonsense attitude and sharp wit.

One day, while browsing through the local library, Grace stumbled upon an old photograph of a young woman who bore a striking resemblance to herself. Determined to uncover more about her doppelganger, Grace began digging through local archives and records. She soon discovered that the mysterious woman was none other than Sofia Mancini, a renowned fashion designer who had lived in Willow Creek over a century ago.

Intrigued by Sofia's story, Grace decided to pay homage to her namesake by revamping an old gown that she had found in her store. With Luna as her trusty assistant, she spent hours restoring the delicate lace and intricate embroidery to its former glory.
