Juan's eyes locked onto Luis García Márquez, and with a quiver in his voice, he recounted the events leading up to his disappearance: a clandestine meeting with a mysterious client, a cryptic message, and a forced exit from the city. Luis García Márquez listened attentively, piecing together the fragments of Juan's tale.

As the night wore on, Luis García Márquez realized that this was more than just another case – it was an opportunity to unravel the tangled threads of his own past. A memory long suppressed resurfaced: a conversation with Juan's sister, about a family secret that had been hidden for years.

The enigmatic smile played on Luis García Márquez's lips as he made his way back to his office. He knew that this case would be far from over, and the truth, much like the shadows of Barcelona, would remain elusive until he was ready to confront it.
