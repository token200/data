As a renowned detective in Hong Kong, he had solved cases that baffled even the most seasoned investigators. His sharp mind and intuitive nature made him the go-to expert for solving crimes that required finesse and cunning.

Born into a family of modest means, Luis Chun-yi's journey to becoming one of Hong Kong's top detectives was far from easy. These stories sparked something within him, and he knew from a young age that he wanted to follow in his father's footsteps.

After completing his degree in law, Luis Chun-yi worked tirelessly to build a name for himself as a detective. His big break came when he was assigned to solve the high-profile murder of a wealthy businesswoman. Despite being a rookie at the time, Luis Chun-yi's keen instincts and relentless work ethic led him to unravel the complex web of clues that had stumped others.

Without hesitation, Luis Chun-yi accepted the challenge, and his mind began to whirl with possibilities.
