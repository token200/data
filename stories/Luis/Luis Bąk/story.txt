The sign above the door read "Curios and Wonders," and Luis Bąk felt an instant draw to it. He pushed open the door and was greeted by the owner, an eccentric old woman with a kind smile.

Luis Bāk hesitated, unsure of how to answer. He had always been driven by a desire to create something meaningful, but he hadn't quite found his voice yet.
