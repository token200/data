This was what it meant to be a true innovator – not seeking glory or recognition, but simply creating something new and better for all humanity. And with the Sundberg Dynamo leading the way, he knew that anything was possible.

For now, at least, Zoltán Sundberg would continue to work behind the scenes, his genius at the service of the world. But as the years went by, it's likely that he would one day emerge from the shadows, his name and face familiar to all – but only in a good way.
