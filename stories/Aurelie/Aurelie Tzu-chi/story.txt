As the stakes grew higher, Aurelie Tzu-chi began to realize that her very existence was a gamble – one that would determine the course of history itself. With each passing day, she felt the weight of responsibility bearing down upon her shoulders. Yet, with The Archon by her side, she knew she could face whatever lay ahead.
