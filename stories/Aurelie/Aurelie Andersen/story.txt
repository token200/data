Lucien chuckled and hugged her back, his broad shoulders relaxing into the squeeze. "And you do too, my friend," he whispered in her ear. "Aurelie Andersen, always a vision."

As the evening drew to a close and the guests began to say their goodbyes, Lucien turned to Aurelie with a warm smile. "Thank you for tonight, my dear," he said, his voice low and sincere. "It's been an unforgettable night."

Aurelie smiled back at him, her heart full of gratitude. She knew that this was a night she would remember forever – a night filled with laughter, music, and the quiet moments of connection that made life truly special.
