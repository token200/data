Péter Shu-fen watched with a mixture of excitement and nerves as the crowd responded to his art. As the night drew to a close, Péter felt a sense of pride and accomplishment wash over him - he had truly made a name for himself in the Hong Kong art scene.

As the gallery emptied out, Péter took a deep breath and let his exhaustion catch up with him. He knew that there would be more challenges ahead, but he was ready to face them head-on.
