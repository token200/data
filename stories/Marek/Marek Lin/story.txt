The pressure was mounting, but Marek Lin remained steadfast, driven by an insatiable curiosity about the mysteries of the universe.

Marek Lin returned to his laboratory, fueled by renewed energy and determination. He dived headfirst into the data, uncovering more secrets about his discovery with each passing hour.
