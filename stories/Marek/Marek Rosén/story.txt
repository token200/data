Marek Rosén's instincts told him this was no ordinary request. He had a reputation for being one of the best in the business, but he also knew that sometimes, the most intriguing cases were those that pushed his boundaries.

The shadows seemed to grow longer and darker, as if they themselves were alive and waiting for him to make a move.
