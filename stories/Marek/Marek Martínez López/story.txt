Just when it seemed that all was lost, Marek remembered a crucial weakness in The Devourer's defenses: a specific frequency of magic that could disrupt its concentration. With newfound hope, Marek focused their energy and unleashed a blast of pure intent that resonated with the demon's own resonance.

The Devourer stumbled backward, its form wavering as the magical frequencies clashed. Marek took advantage of the opening, channeling all their remaining strength into a final, decisive blow. The outcome was far from certain, but one thing was clear: only one of them would emerge victorious.
