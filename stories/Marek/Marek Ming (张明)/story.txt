Despite his incredible feats, Marek Ming remained humble and elusive. Those who sought to understand him were often left frustrated by his reticence. Yet, there was something irresistible about this enigmatic figure – a sense that he held secrets beyond human comprehension.

One fateful night, Marek Ming found himself in the midst of an ancient temple, racing against time to prevent a devastating curse from being unleashed upon the world.
