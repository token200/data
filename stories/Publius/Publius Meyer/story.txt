Publius Meyer accepted with a smile, knowing that this was exactly what he needed to calm his racing mind. As he unrolled the parchment, the inked words seemed to dance before his eyes, transporting him to a world of elegance and refinement.

Born into a family of modest means, Publius Meyer had risen to prominence through sheer determination and hard work.
