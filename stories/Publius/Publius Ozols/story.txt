Gracchus and his cohorts began to share their most guarded secrets with him, revealing hidden plans and vulnerabilities in Dacia's defenses.

With this new information at his disposal, Publius Ozols vanished into the night, leaving behind only whispers of his presence. His reputation as a master manipulator had grown even more pronounced, earning him both fear and admiration from those who knew him.

As he disappeared into the shadows, Publius Ozols couldn't help but feel a sense of satisfaction. He had furthered his own goals by playing the role of an outsider, all while keeping his true intentions hidden behind a mask of charm and deception. Only time would reveal the full extent of his schemes, but for now, Publius Ozols knew that he was one step closer to achieving his objectives.
