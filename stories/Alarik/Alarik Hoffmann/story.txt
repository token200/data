Alarik Hoffmann was a man shrouded in mystery. His piercing blue eyes seemed to hold a world of secrets, and his rugged good looks made him a magnet for the ladies. But few knew that behind his charming facade, Alarik harbored a dark past.

The constant lies and betrayals took their toll on his soul, and he began to question the true cost of his profession.

One fateful night, Alarik received a cryptic message from an old acquaintance, inviting him to a high-society gala in the French Riviera. The invitation promised anonymity and a chance to lay low for a while, but Alarik knew better. As the night wore on, the stakes grew higher, and Alarik found himself torn between two worlds: the one he had left behind, and the new reality unfolding before him.
