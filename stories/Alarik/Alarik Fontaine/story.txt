One night, Alarik Fontaine received a message from an old acquaintance, requesting a meeting in a seedy tavern on the outskirts of town. He arrived at the appointed hour, dressed in a long coat and a wide-brimmed hat, ready for anything that might come his way. As the night wore on, he knew that he had to act quickly, before the plot could come to fruition.
