Jacob Dubois couldn't help but feel a sense of destiny drawing him closer to the truth. And then, it happened – after months of tireless work, Jacob finally cracked the code.

The implications were staggering: an ancient civilization had indeed left behind a hidden message in the stars, one that revealed secrets about the origins of humanity itself. As news of the discovery spread, Jacob Dubois became an overnight sensation in the scientific community, hailed as a genius by his peers and a trailblazer for future generations.
