As he walked through the quiet streets, Jacob couldn't help but notice the changes that had taken place during his absence. The old diner was still serving its famous apple pie, but the ice cream parlor had closed down, replaced by a trendy coffee shop. He saw familiar faces, some with children of their own now, and others who seemed to have aged significantly.

Jacob's thoughts drifted back to his high school days, when he was captain of the basketball team and everyone knew him as "JT." He'd always been driven, but now he felt adrift, unsure of how to navigate his own life.

"You're welcome home, Jacob," she said. "You can stay here for as long as you need."
