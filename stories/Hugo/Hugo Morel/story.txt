He emerged from this ordeal with a newfound appreciation for collaboration and a deeper understanding of the intricacies of clockwork mechanisms. As he looked back on the fiasco, he smiled wryly to himself – after all, it was only by embracing his own mistakes that Hugo Étienne Morel had become the master engineer he was today.
