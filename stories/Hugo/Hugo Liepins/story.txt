But after years of struggling to make ends meet, he had finally broken free from the shackles of poverty, reinventing himself as a high-stakes investor.

It was as if he had forgotten what it meant to be vulnerable, to share his true self with someone else.

She had been assigned to profile him for her magazine, but instead found herself drawn into his enigmatic world. As they spent more time together, Sophia began to unravel the layers of Hugo Liepins' personality, exposing the tender heart beneath.

As they delved deeper into each other's lives, Hugo Liepins finally found a sense of belonging, a connection that went beyond words or platitudes.
