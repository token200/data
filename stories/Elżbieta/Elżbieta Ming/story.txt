Elżbieta's contact had his own agenda, one that threatened to upend everything she thought she knew about her situation. Using her vast knowledge of psychological manipulation, she began to play a long game with her captors, slowly building trust and gathering intelligence on their plans. It was a delicate dance, one that required precision and patience, but Elżbieta was willing to risk everything to see justice done.

As the days turned into weeks, Elżbieta's network of allies grew stronger. They worked tirelessly behind the scenes, using her intel to gather evidence and build a case against those responsible for the corruption scandal.
