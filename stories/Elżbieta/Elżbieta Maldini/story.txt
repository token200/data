As she worked on the piece, Elżbieta found herself struggling with the idea of creating something so superficial. She had always believed in using her art to express her true self, rather than to flaunt one's wealth and power. The more she worked on the portrait, however, the more she began to see it as an opportunity to subvert expectations.
