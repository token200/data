One fateful evening, a young boy named Jakub wandered into the woods, searching for his lost dog. As he called out to his companion, Elżbieta Handschuh appeared beside him, her eyes gleaming with an otherworldly intensity in the fading light. Suddenly, a chorus of yips and whines erupted from beneath the underbrush, and Jakub's lost dog emerged, wagging its tail in joyful abandon.
