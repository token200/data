As she grew older, Elżbieta's fascination with botany only deepened. She pursued a degree in horticulture, specializing in plant pathology, and soon became a leading expert in her field. Despite her many successes, Elżbieta remained humble and grounded. She continued to nurture her gift, using it to help those in need.
