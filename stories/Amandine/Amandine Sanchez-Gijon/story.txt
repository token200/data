Amandine Sanchez-Gijon was known for her striking features and razor-sharp wit. As a renowned detective, she had solved some of the most baffling cases in the city's history. Her eyes seemed to gleam with an inner light whenever she delved into a new mystery.

One day, Amandine Sanchez-Gijon received a visit from a wealthy businessman who claimed that his priceless artifact was missing. The owner, Mr. Johnson, was beside himself and begged Amandine Sanchez-Gijon for help. With her exceptional skills of observation and deduction, she set out to unravel the tangled threads of the case.

As she began her investigation, Amandine Sanchez-Gijon noticed a peculiar detail - a faint scratch on the floor near the display case. She carefully examined it and discovered that the scratch was made by a specific type of metal, one that matched the material used in Mr. Johnson's own jewelry box. This led her to suspect that the thief might have been someone close to the businessman.

With this new information, Amandine Sanchez-Gijon started questioning the people in Mr. Johnson's inner circle. She spoke with his assistant, who seemed overly nervous, and his nephew, who appeared genuinely distraught over the loss of the artifact. However, it was the quiet, reserved lady-in-waiting who caught her attention. There was something about her that didn't add up.

After days of tireless work, Amandine Sanchez-Gijon finally pieced together the puzzle. The thief turned out to be none other than Mr. Johnson's own niece, who had been embezzling funds from his business and saw the artifact as a way to cover her tracks. The scratch on the floor was the only clue left behind by the culprit.

With the case solved, Amandine Sanchez-Gijon returned the artifact to its rightful owner, bringing peace of mind to Mr. Johnson. Her reputation as a brilliant detective solidified, and she continued to solve complex mysteries with ease. As for the embezzler, justice was served, and she faced consequences for her actions.
