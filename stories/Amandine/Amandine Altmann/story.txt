She would spend hours watching the artists at work, mesmerized by their creativity and skill. Her parents, though not artistic themselves, encouraged her passion, buying her supplies and taking her to galleries and museums to expose her to different styles.

Years later, Amandine Altmann had grown into a talented artist in her own right. She traveled the world, soaking up inspiration from the cultures she encountered.
