Word of her discovery spread quickly, and Amandine became a celebrated figure in academic circles. Her findings shed new light on the history of Tanis, earning her numerous accolades and recognition from esteemed institutions worldwide.

As she stood before a packed audience at the Egyptian Museum, Amandine Gómez Pérez took a moment to reflect on her journey.
