Despite her unconventional appearance, Amandine's pastries were renowned for their creativity and flavor. The food critic was impressed by the cake, but more importantly, so were the guests. The wedding reception was a huge success, with many praising Amandine's culinary skills and creativity. Word of her talents spread quickly, and soon Amandine Briedīte's patisserie was flooded with new customers eager to sample her delicious creations.

As she watched her patisserie thrive, Amandine couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and accomplishment.
