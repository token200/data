As the years went by, Jacques became an icon of Italian football, earning a reputation as one of the greatest defenders in the history of the sport. His longevity at the top was testament to his hard work and dedication, which allowed him to maintain his high level of performance well into his 30s.

Despite his illustrious career, Jacques remained humble and grounded, never forgetting where he came from. He was a true leader on and off the pitch, always willing to lend a helping hand to his teammates and support his community.
