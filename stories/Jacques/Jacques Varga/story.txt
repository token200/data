One evening, at an exclusive industry gathering, Jacques' business partner, Rachel, approached him with a concerned expression. "Jacques, I've been noticing that you've been taking on more and more risks lately," she said, her voice barely above a whisper. "Are you sure you're thinking clearly?"

As he looked around the room, Jacques' thoughts turned to Sofia's art, which had inspired him so much just hours before. He made a mental note to reach out to her soon, eager to explore the creative world she inhabited.
