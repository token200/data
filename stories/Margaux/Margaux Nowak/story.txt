But there was something about this small town that drew her in – perhaps it was the sense of nostalgia that lingered in the air.

As she sipped her drink, she began to plan out her days, mapping out which locations would make the best subjects for her camera.

As she worked, Margaux Nowak noticed a young woman sitting across from her, watching her with an intensity that made her feel both comfortable and uneasy. The woman introduced herself as Sophia, and they struck up a conversation about everything from photography to their shared love of literature.

Despite the initial awkwardness, Margaux found herself opening up to Sophia in ways she hadn't expected.
