As he grew older, Abdiweli began to explore his own passions and interests. He developed a keen eye for photography and spent countless hours capturing the beauty of the world around him. His friends and family marveled at his talent, and soon, his work was being showcased in local galleries and exhibitions.

Despite his success, Abdiweli remained humble and grounded. He continued to draw inspiration from his cultural roots, incorporating traditional Somali patterns and motifs into his art.
