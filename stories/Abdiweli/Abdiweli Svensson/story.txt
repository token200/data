But it wasn't until he stumbled upon a street art crew in Stockholm that he discovered his true calling.

Abdiweli Svensson spent years perfecting his craft, experimenting with stencils, spray paint, and stencil-cutting techniques to create stunning murals that seemed to pulse with energy. One day, as Abdiweli Svensson was preparing for an exhibition in London, he received an email from the organizers asking him to create a special piece for the show. The theme? "Identity" - a subject dear to Abdiweli's heart.
