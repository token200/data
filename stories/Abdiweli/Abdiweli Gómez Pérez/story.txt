As Abdiweli grew older, his love for the natural world only deepened. He studied environmental science in college, determined to use his knowledge to make a difference. But Abdiweli's true calling was yet to come. Abdiweli was horrified. He knew that the project would destroy the delicate balance of the ecosystem and push countless species to extinction.

Years later, Abdiweli's courage and conviction had paid off. The resort never came to fruition, and the forest remained intact.
