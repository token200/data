Her camera was never far from her side, capturing the fleeting moments of everyday life.

They gifted her with her first camera when she was just eight years old, and from that moment on, she was hooked.

As Alva grew older, her passion for photography only intensified. She spent countless hours honing her craft, studying the work of great photographers and experimenting with different techniques. Despite her success, Alva remained humble and true to her artistic vision. She continued to shoot from the heart, capturing the beauty in unexpected places. Her photographs were a reflection of her soul – warm, intimate, and full of life.

She continued to create, her camera always at the ready, capturing the world around her with precision and beauty.
