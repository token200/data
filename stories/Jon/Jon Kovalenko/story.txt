The ancient text spoke of a forgotten art of ciphers, one that had been thought lost to time. With shaking hands, Jon Ivan Kovalenko carefully transcribed the code, his eyes locked onto the solution unfolding before him.

As the first light of dawn crept into the cathedral, Jon Ivan Kovalenko finally cracked the code, revealing an ancient message hidden for centuries.
