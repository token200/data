His workshop, nestled in the heart of Willow Creek, became a haven for artisans and collectors alike, each eager to behold the master at work.

Jon Gustafsson's days were filled with the satisfaction of bringing his clients' visions to life, every joint perfectly joined, every curve meticulously carved.
