Pekka Novak, whose full name was Pekka Veli-Matti Novak, had been hired by the enigmatic CEO of a major tech firm to investigate a series of mysterious occurrences within his company. Rumors had been circulating about internal espionage, and the CEO wanted someone who could get to the bottom of it.

As Pekka entered the café, he spotted a young woman with piercing green eyes sitting in the corner. She looked familiar, but he couldn't quite place her. He made his way over to her table, ordering a cup of coffee as he did so.

"Can I help you?" she asked, looking up at him with a hint of curiosity.

Pekka leaned in, his eyes locked on hers. "Because I have reason to believe that something big is going down within the company," he said quietly. "And I need your help to uncover it."
