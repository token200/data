As the years passed, Pekka's reputation grew, attracting scholars and enthusiasts from far and wide. They would gather around him as he regaled them with tales of his ancestors' bravery and cunning.

Though some dismissed him as a mere curiosity, Pekka knew that his family legacy was real – and it was calling to him still. He was the bridge between past and present, the keeper of Rome's greatest secrets.
