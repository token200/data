One night, Ivars Zarins received a cryptic message from an old acquaintance, inviting him to meet at a luxurious penthouse in downtown Moscow. The message read: "Meet me at the top of the world, Ivars Zarins. Come alone." Intrigued, Ivars Zarins agreed to the meeting, his curiosity piqued.

"I've been watching you, Ivars Zarins," she said. "I know your secrets. But I'm willing to keep them silent... for a price."

Suddenly, Natalia Petrova's smile turned deadly. "You're not as slick as you think you are, Ivars Zarins," she spat. "I've been playing a double game all along. And now, it's time for you to pay."

With that, the penthouse was plunged into darkness, and Ivars Zarins found himself at the mercy of Natalia Petrova and her cohorts. But even in captivity, he refused to reveal his secrets. As the night wore on, Ivars Zarins remained calm, his mind racing with escape plans.
