Under the master's guidance, he learned the intricacies of woodworking and began to develop his own unique style, one that blended seamlessly with the techniques passed down through generations.

As Thomas grew more skilled, Ivars Stensson took him under his wing, teaching him not only the technical aspects of woodworking but also the value of patience, attention to detail, and quiet dedication.
