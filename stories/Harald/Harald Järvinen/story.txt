Weeks turned into months as Harald worked tirelessly on the sundial, incorporating elements from ancient Egyptian pyramids to modern-day skyscrapers. The result was a breathtaking work of art that seemed to defy gravity and capture the very essence of time itself.

As the sun set on Kaukopää, Harald stood back to admire his creation, feeling an overwhelming sense of pride and satisfaction. His parents' adventurous spirits and love for learning had instilled in him a sense of curiosity and wonder that drove him to pursue his passions with unbridled enthusiasm.
