Despite his dubious business practices, Harald Björkqvist was a charismatic figure, able to effortlessly charm those around him into doing his bidding. But there was something undeniably compelling about him, a je ne sais quoi that made people want to trust him, even when they knew they shouldn't.

His tactics were unorthodox, sometimes bordering on the bizarre, but they always seemed to yield results.

The prospect of making a fortune was too enticing to resist, and he poured his entire wealth into the venture. The gamble paid off, but at what cost? As Harald Björkqvist stood amidst the ruins of his latest failed business, he couldn't help but feel a twinge of guilt.
