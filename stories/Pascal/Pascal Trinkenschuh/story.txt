This was what it meant to be an entrepreneur – to take risks, push boundaries, and create something entirely new from scratch. And with Henrietta by his side, Pascal knew that anything was possible.

With renewed focus, Pascal dived back into his work, fueled by his boundless energy and creativity. The world was about to get a whole lot more interesting – and Pascal Trinkenschuh was ready to lead the charge.
