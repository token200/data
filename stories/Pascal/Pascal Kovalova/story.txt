With her short, spiky hair and piercing green eyes, Pascal moved with an air of confidence that belied her age. Pascal Kovalova knew better than to mess with curses. But she also knew that one mistake could mean her neck being in a noose. With calculated precision, she planned her approach, using her vast knowledge of Prague's alleys and side streets to evade the museum's security guards.

But as she turned to make her escape, Pascal felt an icy chill run down her spine. The air seemed to grow colder, and the shadows around her twisted like living things. Suddenly, the museum's alarms blared to life, and the sound of footsteps echoed through the hallways.

Pascal Kovalova didn't hesitate. She sprinted through the streets, weaving between startled onlookers as she desperately tried to shake off the weight of the cursed artifact. Her heart pounded in her chest, but she knew one thing - she would see this job through, no matter what horrors lay ahead. Pascal Kovalova was a thief, and thieves didn't back down from a challenge.
