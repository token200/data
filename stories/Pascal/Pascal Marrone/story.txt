As Pascal delved deeper into the case, he couldn't shake off the feeling that it was all connected to his own past.

As the sun dipped below the horizon, casting a golden glow over the sea, Pascal knew he was getting close to the truth. He spent hours poring over evidence and interviewing suspects, driven by an insatiable need for justice and closure. For Pascal Marcellino Marrone, nothing would ever be enough until the past was laid to rest.
