As she navigated the treacherous waters of Belladonna Academy's hidden histories, Kiara Dell proved once again that she was a force to be reckoned with. Her natural leadership abilities, combined with her quick thinking and unwavering dedication, made her a true leader among her peers, and solidified her position as one of the most respected and admired students in the academy.
