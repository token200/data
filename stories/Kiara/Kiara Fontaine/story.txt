One fateful evening, Kiara Fontaine stood before the city council, addressing the very same inequalities she had dedicated her life to eradicating. Her voice shook with emotion as she delivered an impassioned plea for reform, calling upon the leaders to acknowledge the injustices faced by low-income communities and indigenous peoples. The room fell silent, mesmerized by her conviction.
