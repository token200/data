Her parents, though initially skeptical about her career choices, eventually came to support her passions, recognizing that their daughter's creativity was something to be nurtured.

Despite all the attention, Kiara remained humble and grounded. She continued to create from a place of passion and honesty, always staying true to her artistic vision.
