After completing her apprenticeship, she set out to make a name for herself in the world of magic.

Kiara Briedis had traveled far and wide, performing for kings and commoners alike. Her repertoire included everything from sleight-of-hand tricks to elaborate stage spectacles. One evening, Kiara Briedis took the stage at the prestigious Golden Lion theater in the heart of the city. The crowd was abuzz with excitement as she began to weave a tale of love and betrayal. Her eyes locked onto a young couple in the front row, their hands entwined as they gazed into each other's eyes. Kiara Briedis knew that this was her chance to make an impression.
