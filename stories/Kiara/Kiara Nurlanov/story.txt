As news of her discovery spread, Kiara Nurlanov's reputation grew. She was soon inundated with requests for interviews and speaking engagements, but she remained focused on unraveling the secrets of the pedestal. Growing up amidst the ruins of Soviet-era history had sparked an insatiable curiosity within her. With each passing day, Kiara Nurlanov felt a deeper connection to her discovery, as if the pedestal was whispering secrets in her ear. She knew that she had stumbled upon something momentous – a piece of history that would rewrite the textbooks and redefine our understanding of the ancient world.
