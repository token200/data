Antoni Sandstrom was a man with a passion for music that knew no bounds. A classically trained pianist, he had spent years honing his craft, performing in concert halls and small clubs alike. His fingers danced across the keys, coaxing beautiful melodies from the instrument.

He was also a fan of jazz and blues, often incorporating these styles into his own compositions. It was this eclectic mix that had drawn him to the small town of Willow Creek, where he had settled in recent years.
