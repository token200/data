As she observed this phenomenon, Yves felt an overwhelming sense of recognition, as if her ancestors were speaking to her across the vast expanse of space and time.

Yves Abdirova's eyes widened as she realized that the message on the note was more than just a riddle – it was a key to unlocking a hidden language that had been whispered by her people for generations.
