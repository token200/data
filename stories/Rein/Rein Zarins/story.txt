However, as Rein Zarins delved deeper into the world of espionage, he began to question the morality of his actions. He started to feel like a pawn in a much larger game, forced to carry out missions that served the interests of those who didn't share his values. Eventually, he had made the decision to break free from the SVR and forge his own path.

Rein Zarins' new life as a freelance operative was filled with adventure and danger. He took on high-risk missions for various clients around the world, always staying one step ahead of his enemies and using his mastery of disguise and deception to evade capture.

Despite the risks, Rein Zarins found a sense of freedom in his work. He was no longer beholden to anyone or anything, and he could use his skills for whatever purpose he chose.
