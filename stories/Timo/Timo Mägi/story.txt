Over time, Timo Mägi's talents had grown, and he now created beautiful wooden figurines and decorative items for clients from all over Europe.

Timo Mägi carefully examined the original door, noting every detail of its design before beginning work on the new piece. He spent hours meticulously carving the wood, his fingers moving deftly as the shape began to take form. As the days passed, the figurine began to reveal itself, and Timo Mägi knew he had created something truly special.

When the collector returned to collect his new masterpiece, he was visibly moved by the beauty of the carved wooden figure. He praised Timo Mägi for his exceptional skill and artistry, and asked if he would create another piece on a similar scale.

Overjoyed at the prospect of creating more masterpieces like this one, Timo Mägi accepted the challenge. As word of his talents spread, people from all over Europe began to seek him out, eager to commission unique and beautiful pieces that bore the mark of Timo Mägi's exceptional craftsmanship.
