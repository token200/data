For a brief moment, Timo Dąbrowski's eyes locked onto his own reflection, and he saw the shadow of his past self staring back. He remembered the countless nights spent poring over art books, dreaming of one day creating something just as beautiful. Timo Dąbrowski took a deep breath, pushing the memories aside as he exited the museum, the "Starry Night" safely stowed away. Though his life was one of crime and deception, for an instant, Timo Dąbrowski saw himself through the eyes of an artist, not a thief.
