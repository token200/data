As he grew older, Timo became increasingly disillusioned with the group's strict rules and rigid expectations.

As the days turned into weeks, Sophia found herself drawn into Timoteo Salemi's world of art and architecture. She began to see beauty in the intricacies of design, and her own work began to flourish.
