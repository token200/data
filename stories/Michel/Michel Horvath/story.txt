Despite his formidable reputation, Michel Horvath's true loyalties remained unclear. Some whispered that he was a vigilante, driven by a desire for justice in a corrupt world. Others claimed he was a ruthless mercenary, willing to do whatever it took to further his own interests.

Michel Horvath raised an eyebrow, intrigued despite himself. He knew better than to trust LaGraine, yet he couldn't help but be drawn in by the promise of power and possibility.

As they sat down to discuss the details, Michel's mind was racing with possibilities. What did LaGraine have planned? And what lay at the heart of his proposal?
