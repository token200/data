Her keen eye for design and attention to detail quickly earned her recognition within the industry. After completing her studies in fashion, Aurora began working alongside her father at the family business, learning the intricacies of running a successful brand.

With a keen sense of style and an innovative approach to design, Aurora's collections quickly gained attention from industry insiders and fashion enthusiasts alike.

Her breakthrough moment came when she presented her Spring/Summer 2023 collection at Milan Fashion Week. The show was a masterclass in modern elegance, with each piece showcasing Aurora's ability to blend comfort with sophistication.
