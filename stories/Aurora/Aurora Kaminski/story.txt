Aurora "Rory" Kaminski was a free-spirited artist known for her vibrant paintings and infectious smile. Her travels not only broadened her horizons but also provided endless inspiration for her paintings. Her parents, who had always believed in her potential, couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and accomplishment as they watched their daughter thrive in the art world.
