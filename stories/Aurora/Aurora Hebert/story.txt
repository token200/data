Aurora "Rory" Hebert was a force to be reckoned with in the small town of Willow Creek. As the owner of the local bakery, She Bakes, Rory's warm smile and infectious laugh could light up even the grumpiest of customers. But beneath her sunny demeanor lay a sharp mind and quick wit, honed from years of working alongside her eccentric grandmother, Madame Colette.

Rory had grown up surrounded by the rich flavors and aromas of French patisserie, learning the intricacies of traditional baking techniques from her grandmother's own hands. As she grew older, Rory expanded her culinary horizons, experimenting with modern twists on classic recipes and creating a loyal following of customers who raved about her innovative creations.
