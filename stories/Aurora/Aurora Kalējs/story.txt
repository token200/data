Aurora's own path had been shaped by her grandmother's tales – of the stars that whispered secrets to those willing to listen, and the hidden patterns that wove through the cosmos. As she pondered the intricacies of dark matter and gravitational waves, Aurora felt an inexplicable connection to the celestial ballet above.

As she gazed out at the moon, now full and resplendent in the night sky, Aurora felt a surge of inspiration. The crescent moon seemed to pulse in rhythm with her heartbeat, reminding Aurora that even in the most fleeting moments, transformation was possible.
