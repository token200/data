As she grew older, Tatyana's interest in art only deepened, and she began to study formal training at the prestigious Accademia di Belle Arti. There, she honed her skills under the guidance of some of Italy's most esteemed instructors, learning everything from color theory to composition.

Despite the demands of her studies, Tatyana always made time for her own creative pursuits.
