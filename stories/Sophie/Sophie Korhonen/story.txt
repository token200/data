Sophie Maria Korhonen was a free-spirited artist who had always felt most alive when surrounded by canvas and paint. Growing up in a small Finnish town, Sophie's love of art was encouraged from a young age by her mother, a talented seamstress.

After graduating, Sophie set out on a journey of self-discovery, traveling to Paris and New York City to immerse herself in the vibrant art scenes. She worked tirelessly to hone her craft, experimenting with different mediums and techniques until she had developed a distinctive voice.
