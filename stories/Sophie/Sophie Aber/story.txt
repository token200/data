Sophie Aber welcomed Luna with open arms, offering her a cup of coffee and a seat on the couch. Luna listened intently, soaking up every word like a sponge.

She assisted with exhibitions, helped with art classes, and even began to dabble at painting her own canvases. Sophie Aber took Luna under her wing, mentoring her and encouraging her to take risks and try new things.
