But now, Sophie was gone, and Thierry was left with only fragments of memories and unanswered questions.

Thierry felt a lump form in her throat as she turned the pages, remembering the countless hours they had spent sharing their art and dreams.

The colors flowed effortlessly, as if Sophie was standing right beside her, guiding her hand. In that moment, Thierry felt a sense of peace wash over her, knowing that her sister's spirit lived on through the art they shared.

As she worked, Thierry Wiśniewska found solace in the creative process, channeling her grief into something beautiful and meaningful.
