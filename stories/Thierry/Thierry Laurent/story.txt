Despite his carefree exterior, Thierry had a deep sense of melancholy that drove him to create some of the most beautiful and poignant works of art in his field. As he flipped through its yellowed pages, he came across a verse that spoke directly to his soul: "The city is a canvas, waiting for the brushstrokes of the human heart."
