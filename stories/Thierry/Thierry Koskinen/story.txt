Thierry Koskinen stood frozen, his mind racing with questions and observations. For a moment, time seemed to stand still as the two locked gazes. Then, with a slow, sweeping motion, the Lindworm lowered its head and vanished into the trees. Thierry Koskinen breathed deeply, knowing that he had stumbled upon something truly remarkable – a secret hidden for centuries, waiting for him to uncover it.
