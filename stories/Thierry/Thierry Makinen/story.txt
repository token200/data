The acquaintance, a renowned collector, begged Thierry to use his skills to retrieve the item before it was too late.

Thierry accepted the challenge without hesitation, his mind already racing with strategies and contingencies. The thrill of the hunt coursed through his veins like adrenaline, propelling him forward with renewed energy.

As he reached for the item, a hand clamped down over his mouth, silencing him mid-sentence. The collector's words echoed in Thierry's mind: "You're too good at this, Thierry Makinen."

Thierry's eyes locked onto his captor, his gaze piercing through the shadows. For an instant, they stood there, locked in a silent standoff. Then, with swift and deadly precision, Thierry overpowered his assailant and reclaimed the artifact.

Exhaling softly, Thierry Makinen allowed himself to breathe again, the adrenaline slowly ebbing from his veins.
