Almasbek stood firm, refusing to compromise his heart for the sake of his title. As he gazed into Luna's eyes, he knew that he would follow her anywhere – even into exile. For the first time in his life, Almasbek Aurelius Antoninus Augustus felt like himself, and nothing could ever take that away.
