The community was struggling to survive due to encroaching urbanization and climate change, which were threatening their traditional way of life.

Moved by their plight, Almasbek decided to use his skills to help the community develop a sustainable livelihood that would allow them to coexist with nature. He worked closely with the nomads, teaching them innovative farming techniques and providing support for their small-scale businesses.
