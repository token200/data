One evening, as Weronika was setting up for her latest project, she noticed a young girl watching her from across the street. The girl's eyes sparkled with curiosity, and Weronika couldn't help but smile. She approached the girl, introducing herself as Weronika Schäfer, and began to explain the inspiration behind her art.
