As an artist, Weronika found solace in the world of abstraction, where the boundaries between reality and fantasy blurred.

As an adult, Weronika had returned to painting, finding a new sense of purpose in the expressive world of art. Her brushstrokes were bold and confident, imbuing each canvas with a unique energy.
