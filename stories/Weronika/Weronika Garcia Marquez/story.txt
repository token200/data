Weronika's reputation grew as she shared her knowledge with others. Her garden flourished under her care, becoming a symbol of hope and renewal in the community. As Weronika continued to nurture both her plants and people, her spirit remained entwined with that of the natural world, forging a bond between the mystical and the ordinary.
