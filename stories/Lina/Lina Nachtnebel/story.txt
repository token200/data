She was drawn to the overlooked and the unknown, often finding herself in abandoned buildings, hidden courtyards, and forgotten alleys. Her Instagram feed was a testament to her wanderings – a curated collection of gritty landscapes, architectural relics, and whispered tales of city secrets.
