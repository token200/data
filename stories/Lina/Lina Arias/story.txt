Lina Arias stepped off the bus and onto the worn asphalt of the small town's main street. The sun beat down on her dark hair, causing it to frizz in the humidity. But Lina Arias knew that she couldn't stay here forever, running from her past rather than facing it head-on.

She would return to the city eventually, but for now, she needed to focus on building a new life in this small town. And so, Lina Arias took a deep breath and set off down the main street, ready to face whatever challenges lay ahead.
