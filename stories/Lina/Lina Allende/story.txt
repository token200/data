Lina was a painter, and her art was raw and unbridled, like the city itself. She spent most of her days lost in the studio, experimenting with colors and textures as she tried to capture the essence of the world around her.

Tonight, however, Lina had taken a break from her work to attend a gallery opening on the other side of town. The invitation had come from an old friend, who promised a night of "artistic expression" and "unbridled passion." Lina was skeptical, but she wanted to see what all the fuss was about.
