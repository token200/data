Her latest expedition was no exception, as she ventured into the unforgiving deserts of Central Asia in search of the long-lost city of Ak-Bogdo.

Hours passed, and Giulia's excitement grew, her mind racing with the implications of what she had discovered. She spent the night in the ruins, poring over her findings, and by dawn, she had pieced together a narrative that shed new light on the lost city of Ak-Bogdo.
