With trembling hands, she accepted the invitation, knowing that this opportunity would allow her to share her vision with a wider audience.

As the opening night approached, Giulia found herself both exhilarated and nervous. She had spent countless hours perfecting each piece, pouring every ounce of herself into the artwork. As the lights dimmed and the crowd began to murmur, Giulia Ruiz García took to the stage, her voice carrying across the room as she spoke about her inspiration.
