Mia Neumann was a free-spirited artist with a passion for capturing the beauty of the world around her. Born and raised in Berlin, Germany, she had grown up surrounded by the vibrant streets and alleys that would later inspire her work. As she grew older, Mia's love of art only deepened, and she began to explore the world beyond Berlin. She studied fine art in Paris, where she was exposed to a wide range of styles and techniques that influenced her own unique approach. After completing her studies, Mia returned to Berlin and set up her own studio, where she spent countless hours honing her craft.
