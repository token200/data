Mia's journey began when she was just a teenager, volunteering at a nearby wildlife rehabilitation center. She was captivated by the wolves, their intelligence, and their vulnerability. After completing her education in veterinary medicine, Mia saved up every penny and finally opened her own sanctuary on the outskirts of town.

Years went by, and Mia's sanctuary became a hub for wolf enthusiasts and conservationists alike. People would come from far and wide to visit the pack, learn about their behavior, and observe them in their natural habitat. Mia was always happy to share her knowledge, but she also made sure to keep the wolves' needs at the forefront of every decision.
