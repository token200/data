As she sat at her desk, typing away on her laptop, Mia couldn't help but think of Jack Harris - a man who had unwittingly become her partner in uncovering the truth.

Despite the danger and uncertainty that came with pursuing this story, Mia Elara Laine was undaunted. She knew that she had stumbled upon something huge, and she was determined to see it through to its conclusion.
