She made her way to the local coffee shop, where she knew the owner would have a fresh pot waiting for her. Inside, the aroma of expertly roasted beans filled the air, and Leah felt a sense of comfort wash over her. She spotted her friend, Rachel, sitting at a small table by the window and waved her over.

Rachel listened attentively as Leah walked them through her process, from sketches to finished paintings, before finally admitting defeat. Rachel offered words of encouragement, reminding Leah that sometimes the best work comes from a place of struggle.
