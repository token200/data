Determined to make a difference, Leah pursued a degree in journalism and landed an internship at a reputable news outlet.

As she worked her way up the ranks, Leah's reputation as a tenacious and insightful reporter grew. She was known for her ability to get sources to open up to her, and her sharp instincts that allowed her to sniff out the biggest stories. Her colleagues admired her tenacity and passion for uncovering the truth.

She saw this as an opportunity to expose corruption at the highest levels and set out to investigate further. With the help of her editor, Leah wrote a scathing exposé that exposed the official's corruption and sparked outrage across the country. Leah's career soared after that breakthrough story, and she went on to become one of the most respected journalists in the industry.
