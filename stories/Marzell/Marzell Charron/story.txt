Marzell Charron was no stranger to the streets of New Orleans. As a young boy growing up in the French Quarter, he had learned to navigate its narrow alleys and jazz-filled clubs with ease. But despite his familiarity with the city's rhythms and sounds, Marzell had always felt like an outsider.
