His full name, which often left him cringing at the awkward combinations of names from his parents' diverse backgrounds, was a constant reminder of the complexities of identity that came with being the product of love and cultural clashes.

It was Abuelo Jorge's ancestral home, and Marzell had come to sort through the belongings left behind. Marzell closed his eyes, embracing this small slice of home in an unfamiliar place, letting the past blend seamlessly into the present. The echoes of his full name – Marzell Sanchez-Gijon – faded away, leaving only the gentle rustle of leaves, as if Abuelo Jorge's spirit had finally found peace.
