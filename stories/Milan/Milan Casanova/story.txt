But as their relationship deepened, Milan began to sense that there was more to Alexander than met the eye. Despite her growing unease, Milan couldn't help but be drawn to Alexander's charismatic presence. She felt like she'd known him for a lifetime, and yet, every moment with him was a revelation. As they stood on the rooftop of his penthouse apartment, gazing out at the city skyline, Milan realized that she was falling for Alexander Blackwood, a man who had shaken her to her core.

But as the night wore on, Milan couldn't shake the feeling that she'd made a mistake. She didn't know if she could trust Alexander, or if his secrets would ultimately destroy her.
