Her abuela would weave tales of vibrant markets, majestic Andean mountains, and the warmth of the tropical sun on skin. These stories ignited a spark within Zoe, fueling her creativity and inspiring her to express herself through art.

From the intricate designs on her grandmother's traditional dress to the swirling patterns of Andean textiles, every artwork held a memory or emotion that resonated deeply within Zoe.

With trembling hands, Zoe carefully arranged her artworks around the gallery space, infusing each piece with love and intention.
