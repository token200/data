One day, a prominent art collector stumbled upon Zoe Farah's work while browsing through a local gallery. He was immediately drawn to her unique style and offered her a solo exhibition at his prestigious gallery in the city. Zoe was overjoyed - this was the opportunity she had been waiting for.
