As she explored the wilderness, Zoe discovered a sense of purpose and belonging that eluded her in the stifling atmosphere of Rome. She knew that she was meant for greater things, and that her full name - Zoe Claudius Caesar Drusus Germanicus - would one day be whispered in awe by historians and commoners alike.

Years passed, and Zoe's reputation grew as a fierce warrior and shrewd politician. She became a trusted advisor to the Roman Empire, earning the respect of her peers and the gratitude of her people.
