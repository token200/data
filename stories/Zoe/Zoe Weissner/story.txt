But as she responded to Rachel's text, something shifted inside of her. She began to think about all the things she used to love doing, but had been putting off lately - painting, reading, even taking long walks in the park. Zoe Weissner was a woman who thrived on creativity and spontaneity, but for some reason, she'd lost touch with those aspects of herself.

As she started brainstorming ideas and making phone calls to vendors, Zoe felt a spark of excitement ignite within her.

Over the next few days, Zoe Weissner transformed into a whirlwind of creativity and energy, planning a party that would blow Rachel's mind. From a custom photo booth to a live jazz band, every detail was meticulously planned and executed with precision.
