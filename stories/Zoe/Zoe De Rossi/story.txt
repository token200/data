Zoe Danielle De Rossi was a free-spirited artist with a passion for capturing the essence of her surroundings on canvas. As she grew older, Zoe's fascination with nature only deepened. She began to see the world as a complex web of interconnected elements, each one influencing the others in subtle yet profound ways. Her art became a reflection of this worldview, with vibrant colors and bold brushstrokes capturing the energy and vitality of the natural world.

Despite her love of nature, Zoe was also drawn to the urban landscape. She spent countless hours exploring the city's streets, observing the way people interacted with one another, and capturing the rhythms and textures of city life on canvas. Her art was a fusion of the wild and the tame, reflecting the contradictions and complexities of modern existence.
