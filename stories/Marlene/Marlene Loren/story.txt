Marlene Loren stepped off the train and onto the worn platform, her eyes scanning the small station for any signs of life. It had been years since she'd set foot in this town, and yet the memories still lingered like a faint scent on the breeze.

Marlene Loren laughed, feeling a little awkward but also relieved to be reunited with someone who knew her before she'd changed so much. "Jake," she replied, hugging him tightly. "I've missed you."

Marlene Loren hesitated for a moment before answering, unsure of how much to reveal about her plans - or lack thereof. But with Jake, she knew she could trust him with anything. "Just passing through," she said finally, smiling up at him. "Enjoying the view."
