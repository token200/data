Marlene Green was a woman with a passion for photography. Marlene's love of photography was not new, but it had grown stronger over the years. As she grew older, Marlene began to experiment with her own camera, teaching herself the ins and outs of composition, lighting, and editing.

Marlene Green was no stranger to the power of photography, but this latest project had taken on a life of its own. She found herself lost in thought, pondering the intersection of art and narrative.
