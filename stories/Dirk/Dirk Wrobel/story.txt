"Don't worry, I'll do everything in my power to help you," he said finally, as he scribbled down some notes in his notebook. "But I need to know more about your sister's disappearance. Can you tell me where she was last seen?"

Sarah hesitated, her eyes darting nervously around the room before finally settling on Dirk Wrobel. "I don't know," she admitted, her voice barely above a whisper. "She just vanished one night, leaving behind only this note."

Dirk Wrobel's eyes narrowed as he took the note from Sarah and examined it. The handwriting was unfamiliar, but there were no fingerprints to analyze. He tucked the note into his pocket, his mind already racing with theories and possibilities.
