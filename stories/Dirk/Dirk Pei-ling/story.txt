Intrigued by the prospect of a thrilling investigation, Dirk Pei-ling accepted the challenge without hesitation.

With a deep breath, Dirk Pei-ling steeled himself for what lay ahead and pushed open the creaking front door.

Dirk Pei-ling's eyes welled up with tears as the weight of his family's legacy settled upon him.
