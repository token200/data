Dirk Maldini's instincts kicked in as he began to piece together the fragments of evidence presented by his mysterious informant. His eyes narrowed as he contemplated the next move - would he go undercover, or rely on old-school detective work? The choice was clear: Dirk Maldini's reputation for tenacity and unwavering integrity had to be upheld.

He spent the remainder of the night tracking down leads, scrutinizing every shred of evidence with an eagle eye for the smallest detail that could crack the case wide open.
