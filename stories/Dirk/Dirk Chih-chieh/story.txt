As Dirk matured, he honed his craft under the tutelage of a seasoned thief named Lien. Together, they infiltrated high-society events and swiped priceless artifacts from wealthy collectors. Dirk's charisma and quick wit allowed him to convincingly adopt various personas, making it impossible for anyone to discern his true identity.

One fateful evening, Dirk Chih-chieh received an offer he couldn't refuse: a mysterious client wanted him to steal a rare, ancient scroll from the heavily guarded Museum of Taiwanese History. Intrigued by the challenge and the promise of a hefty sum, Dirk accepted the job.

With Lien's guidance, Dirk devised a elaborate plan to infiltrate the museum during a charity gala.
