Despite the hardships he faced, Ming-che refused to give up. He worked tirelessly, from dawn till dusk, to send what little money he earned back to his hometown of Sichuan. His ultimate goal was to open his own tea shop, where he could serve the famous Sichuan teas to the people of Shanghai.

Ming-che's dream seemed like a distant fantasy to many, but he remained steadfast in his pursuit. He spent every spare moment studying the art of tea-making, learning about the different varieties and how to prepare them to perfection. His obsession was evident in the way he would often sit for hours, sipping on a cup of tea and contemplating the intricacies of the ancient Chinese brew.
