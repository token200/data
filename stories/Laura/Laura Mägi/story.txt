Laura Elina Mägi's unique style had gained attention from galleries and collectors alike, but it wasn't until she received an invitation to participate in a prestigious group exhibition that her career truly took off. The night of the exhibition, Laura Elina Mägi was nervous but exhilarated as she set up her latest piece, a sprawling installation that evoked the rhythms of the sea. As the crowd began to arrive, she couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and accomplishment. Her art had come a long way since those early days spent collecting leaves and stones in the forest.
