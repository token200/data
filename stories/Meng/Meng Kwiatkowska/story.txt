After completing her studies, Meng moved to London, determined to make a name for herself in the field. She landed a research position at a prestigious university, where she conducted groundbreaking studies on cognitive behavior and emotional regulation. Her work was met with critical acclaim, and she soon became a leading expert in her field.

Despite her many successes, Meng remained humble and dedicated to her craft. As they worked together, Meng developed an innovative therapy approach that combined traditional psychoanalytic techniques with cutting-edge neuroimaging technologies. Her approach allowed her to visualize the intricate workings of Sophie's brain, identifying areas where imbalances in neural activity might be contributing to her symptoms.

Together, they explored the root causes of Sophie's distress, uncovering long-suppressed memories and unresolved conflicts. The results were nothing short of miraculous: Sophie began to feel more grounded, more confident, and more connected to herself.
