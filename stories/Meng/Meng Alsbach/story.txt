As a renowned archaeologist, she had spent years searching for the fabled Lost City of Zerzura, and finally, after months of planning and preparation, she was on the cusp of uncovering its secrets.

Meng Alsbach took a deep breath, mentally preparing herself for the long day ahead. She had assembled a team of experts to aid her in the excavation, but this time, it was just her and a handful of local guides who knew the lay of the land. As they walked, Meng Alsbach couldn't help but think about how far she had come. Born into a family of modest means in a small village on the outskirts of Cairo, she had always been drawn to the world of antiquities.
