Meng Nieminen's artistic journey began in her childhood, when she would spend hours sketching and painting with her grandmother in their small apartment. After graduating, Meng Nieminen set out to make a name for herself as an artist, taking on commissions and showcasing her work in local galleries.

One day, a prominent Finnish newspaper featured Meng Nieminen's work on its front page, highlighting her unique style and message. Overnight, she became an overnight sensation, with art lovers and collectors clamoring to see more of her vibrant creations.

Meng Nieminen's success had not gone unnoticed by the Finnish art community.
