Despite his reputation for being aloof, Meng Rossi was a master of his craft. He had spent years developing a top-secret device capable of manipulating the space-time continuum, and he was convinced that it would change the course of human history forever.

Meng Rossi's eyes lit up with excitement as he took the box from Maria. This was it - the moment of truth. With a flourish, he activated the device and stepped inside.

When the distortion cleared, Meng Rossi found himself standing in a completely unfamiliar place - a lush green forest, teeming with life. He took a deep breath, feeling the cool air fill his lungs. This was it - he had done it.
