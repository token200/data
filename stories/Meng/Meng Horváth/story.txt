Her sharp mind and quick wit had earned her the respect of her colleagues, and she was often sought out by sources looking to share their stories.

One day, Meng Horváth received a tip about a secret concert taking place in an abandoned warehouse on the outskirts of town. The rumors were that it would be a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to see one of Hungary's most talented up-and-coming bands perform live. Determined to get the scoop, Meng Horváth set out for the warehouse, her notebook and pen at the ready.

As she approached the warehouse, Meng Horváth could feel the excitement building inside her. She had always been drawn to the raw energy of underground music, and this seemed like the ultimate thrill. She slipped through a narrow door and found herself in a dimly lit room filled with music enthusiasts, all eagerly awaiting the start of the concert.

Just as Meng Horváth was about to make a move to get closer to the band, she spotted her rival journalist, László Kovács, trying to muscle his way past the bouncers. She quickly realized that he had gotten wind of the secret concert and was determined to scoop her on the story.

As the concert began, Meng Horváth made her move, slipping behind the stage to get an exclusive interview with the lead singer of the band.
