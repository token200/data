Growing up in war-torn Somalia, Elli had seen firsthand the devastating effects of conflict on civilians. But Elli had refused to give up. She had pursued her passion for journalism with determination, determined to use her voice to tell stories that needed to be told. And now, as she walked through the streets of this new city, Elli felt a sense of purpose that she hadn't felt in years.

Elli smiled to herself - it was her favorite place in town, where she could sit for hours and watch the world go by. Elli's heart skipped a beat as she read on, her mind racing with ideas and possibilities.
