Her love for art was evident from an early age, and she spent most of her childhood exploring the desert landscapes, taking note of the intricate patterns and textures that would later inspire her work.

As Sarah Sariñana grew older, she began to hone her skills as a painter, experimenting with different mediums and techniques. Her unique style blended traditional Native American motifs with modern abstract expressionism.

After college, Sarah Sariñana set out on a journey to explore the world beyond her hometown. Upon returning to Santa Fe, Sarah Sariñana opened her own studio, where she showcased her work alongside other local artists.
