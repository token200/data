Sarah Schäfer stepped into the bustling streets of Berlin, her eyes scanning the crowds with a practiced air of detachment. Her long, dark hair whipped around her face as she wove through the throngs of tourists and locals alike.

Sarah Schäfer had always been drawn to the stories that lay just beneath the surface of this great city.
