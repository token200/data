Juho Zariņa had always been skeptical, but his curiosity got the better of him. He assembled his gear and set off into the night, his camera slung over his shoulder.

Juho Zariņa's eyes widened as he reached out to touch the box. He knew in that moment that his discovery was only the beginning of a journey that would change him forever.
