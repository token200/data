After completing her degree, Juho María landed an internship at a research station on a remote island. The two quickly fell in love, bonding over their shared passion for the ocean and its inhabitants.

Years passed, and Juho María's career took off. She became a respected expert in her field, publishing papers on her research and teaching at universities around the world. But despite her success, she never forgot where she came from. She continued to work with Dr. Rodriguez, who had become like a sister to her.

One day, Juho María received an offer to lead a major research project in the Pacific Islands. She accepted the challenge, knowing that it would take her to places she had only dreamed of visiting before.

As Juho María prepared for her departure, Dr. Rodriguez came to visit her in her small apartment overlooking the sea. The two sat on the balcony together, watching the sun set over the waves. "You're going to make a real difference out there, amiga," Sofia said, smiling at Juho María's excitement. "The world needs people like you, who care so deeply about our planet and its inhabitants." Juho María smiled back, feeling grateful for her partner and friend. She knew that she was exactly where she was meant to be.
