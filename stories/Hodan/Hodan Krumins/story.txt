Under the guise of a wealthy collector's gala, she wove a web of deception, gathering intel on the necklace's security and waiting for the perfect moment to strike.

As the night wore on, Hodan Krumins slipped into the museum undetected, avoiding security guards with ease. Hodan Krumins watched from afar, a sly smile spreading across her face as she realized her plan had worked flawlessly. With the necklace safely in hand, she vanished into the shadows, leaving behind only rumors of her exploits.
