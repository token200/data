Her big break came when she uncovered a high-profile corruption scandal, earning her widespread recognition and acclaim.

Now, five years later, Hodan Szabó found herself back in Budapest, determined to follow a new lead that had come to light. Excited by the prospect of finally solving the case, Hodan Szabó pulled out her notebook and began jotting down notes.
