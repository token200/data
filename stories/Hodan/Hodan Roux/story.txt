Intrigued by the prospect, Hodan agreed to join forces and began working tirelessly to develop this groundbreaking technology.

As the months passed, Hodan Roux's lab became a hub of activity, with her team working around the clock to perfect their design. The stakes were high, but Hodan was undaunted – she knew that this could be the breakthrough of a lifetime.

Her dedication had paid off in a big way – but for Hodan Roux, there was no rest on the laurels.
