Hodan Abbagnato was known for her unconventional approach to gardening. While most people spent hours meticulously pruning and tending to their plants, Hodan preferred a more relaxed method. She would often leave her flowers to bloom as they pleased, letting nature take its course.

Hodan Abbagnato's friends and family often teased her about her gardening habits, but she didn't mind. She loved watching the world around her unfold, untethered by human expectation. Hodan smiled knowingly and said, "It's all about letting go." As the sun began to set, Sophia left with a newfound sense of purpose. She realized that sometimes, it was necessary to step back and allow life to unfold as it pleased. Hodan Abbagnato watched her go, feeling content in knowing that she had helped someone find their way – one gentle breeze at a time.
