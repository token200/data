As the sun began to set over the City of Light, Sophia found herself feeling a deep connection to Nicolás Casanova. She saw beyond the charming facade to the complex, vulnerable person beneath. And as they strolled along the Seine, hand in hand, she knew that she wanted to spend more time getting to know this enigmatic man.
