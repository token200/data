Nicolás Arjona was a man of many talents and passions. As a renowned musician, singer-songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist, he had captivated audiences with his soulful voice and poignant lyrics. Born in Panama City, Panama, Nicolás Arjona grew up surrounded by the rich musical heritage of his native country, which would later inspire him to create his own unique sound.

From there, Nicolás Arjona's musical journey took off, with each performance and recording milestone fueling his determination to share his art with the world.

Critics raved about his raw emotion and storytelling ability, while fans connected deeply with his lyrics that spoke of love, loss, and social justice. The album's success allowed Nicolás Arjona to tour extensively, performing at iconic venues and festivals across the globe.
