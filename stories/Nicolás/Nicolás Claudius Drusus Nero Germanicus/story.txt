With this newfound authority, Nicolás set out to forge a brighter future for Rome. He implemented policies aimed at improving trade and commerce, while maintaining the empire's military might. As news of his accomplishments spread throughout the land, Nicolás Claudius Drusus Nero Germanicus became an icon of Roman power and wisdom.
