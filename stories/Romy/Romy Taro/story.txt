"Romy, what have you done?" he asked, chuckling. Romy looked up from her canvas, a mixture of defensiveness and pride on her face.

"Echoes in the City," now renamed "Harmony in Dissonance," became Romy Taro's breakout success, hailed by critics and collectors alike as a masterpiece of modern art.
