Years went by, and Romy Jie's passion for calligraphy only grew stronger. She spent every spare moment practicing, experimenting with different brushes and ink combinations to achieve the perfect flow and texture. The sign above the door read "Master Lin's Calligraphy Studio," and Romy Jie felt an inexplicable pull towards the store. Romy Jie's full name may have been a mystery to many, but those closest to her knew that she was Romy Jie, the calligraphy enthusiast with a passion for beauty and self-expression.
