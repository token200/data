As Romy prepared for the big night, she couldn't help but feel a sense of nervous energy coursing through her veins. From provocative self-portraits to thought-provoking installations, every element of the exhibition was a testament to Romy Zielińska's unwavering commitment to her art.

As the guests began to arrive, Romy took her place at the reception, greeting admirers and collectors with equal parts warmth and intensity.
