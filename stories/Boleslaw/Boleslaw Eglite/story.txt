She claimed to have received threatening letters from an unknown sender, warning her to drop a high-profile business deal. Desperate for protection, she turned to the one man who might be able to help: Bolesław Eglewicz.

Bo listened intently as Sophia recounted her story, his eyes narrowing behind his wire-rimmed glasses. He made a few notes on his pad and then set off into the night, determined to uncover the source of the threats. The next morning, Bo returned to Sophia's office with a surprising revelation: the letters were not from some disgruntled rival businessman, but rather from an undercover agent working for the government. It turned out that Sophia's business deal had caught the attention of international terrorists, and her firm was now unwittingly entangled in their plans.
