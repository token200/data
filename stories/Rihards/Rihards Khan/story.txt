Years went by, and Rihards Khan's reputation grew. He took on high-paying jobs from corrupt officials and crime lords, using his skills to manipulate events from behind the scenes. His name became synonymous with fear and respect.

As he traveled the world, taking on various aliases and personas, Mikhail eventually settled on Rihards Khan - a name that whispered power and menace in equal measure. And though he still wore many masks, Rihards Khan knew that only one face was truly his own: a face hidden behind a veil of secrets and deception.

Yet, as the years went by, whispers began to circulate about Rihards Khan's true intentions - that he was slowly building an army of loyal followers, gathering strength for a final, apocalyptic showdown.
