Rihards Horváth stood at the edge of the bustling market square, his eyes scanning the crowds with a mixture of curiosity and wariness. As a traveling performer and entertainer, he had grown accustomed to being on the periphery, always watching and waiting for the perfect moment to strike.

Rihards Horváth's eyes flickered to the eavesdropping guards, his mind racing with opportunities.
