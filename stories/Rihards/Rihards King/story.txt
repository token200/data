For years, Rihards studied tirelessly, mastering spells and incantations that would one day aid him in his quest for power. And when he finally ascended to the throne, Rihards King was ready.

But despite his formidable abilities, Rihards remained humble and just, ruling Aethereia with wisdom and compassion. His people prospered under his reign, and his legend grew as a hero and a leader.
