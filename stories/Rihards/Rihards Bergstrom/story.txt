Over the years, Rihards' research had taken him to various parts of the world, from the Amazon rainforest to the scorching deserts of North Africa.

Rihards' eyes scanned the shelves, taking in the vibrant hues and textures of the different species. He had spent countless hours here, studying and experimenting with the unique properties of each plant.

Rihards' excitement grew as he carefully examined each plant, taking note of their characteristics and potential applications.
