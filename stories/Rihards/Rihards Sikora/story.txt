"Rihards! My old friend!" he exclaimed, wiping his hands on a towel. "It's been too long!"

Rihards Sikora smiled back, feeling a sense of belonging wash over him. "Jānis, it's good to be back," he said, taking a seat at the counter.

As they chatted, Rihards Sikora couldn't help but think about how much this town had changed – and yet, remained the same. The same stories were being told around the coffee shop, the same jokes were being shared with the locals, the same sense of community lingered in the air.

Jānis handed him a steaming cup of coffee, and Rihards Sikora took a sip, feeling the warmth spread through his body. "I've got some news," he said, glancing around the café to make sure no one was listening.
