Lola laughed seductively as she leaned in close, her voice barely above a whisper. "I don't know what you're talking about, sweetheart," she purred.

Lola's smile faltered for a moment before she regained her composure. "Maybe I'm just looking for new opportunities, darling," she said, her voice dripping with sarcasm.

He made a mental note to follow up on his lead and track down that elusive nightclub. With any luck, it would lead him straight into the heart of Lola's operation - and perhaps even reveal the truth behind her enigmatic persona.
