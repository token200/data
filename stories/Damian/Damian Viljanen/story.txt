As a child, Damian had spent countless hours watching his father work in their family's workshop, mesmerized by the way the wood seemed to transform under his skilled hands. He had always known he wanted to follow in his footsteps, and after completing an apprenticeship at a local trade school, he had set up his own business.

Years of hard work and dedication had paid off, with Damian Viljanen's woodworking skills earning him a loyal client base and a thriving reputation in the community. People came from all over to purchase his handcrafted furniture, each piece unique and imbued with a sense of character that seemed impossible to replicate with mass-produced goods.
