With renewed determination, Viktoria Pētersone set to work on the dresser, carefully sanding away years of grime and dust. As she applied a coat of rich, golden paint, the room began to fill with the sweet scent of beeswax and varnish.

Days turned into weeks as Viktoria Pētersone labored over the dresser, meticulously reassembling it piece by piece. The final result was breathtaking – a beautifully restored masterpiece that seemed to glow with an inner light.
