In the aftermath of the scandal, Elisa faced criticism from some quarters who questioned her motives and methods. However, she remained steadfast in her commitment to telling the truth, even if it meant facing scrutiny and backlash.

Throughout it all, Elisa Larsson had refused to compromise her integrity or surrender to intimidation.
