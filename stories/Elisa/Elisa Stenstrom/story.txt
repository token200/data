As opening night approached, Elisa's excitement grew. She spent hours arranging her paintings, carefully selecting each one to represent a different aspect of her artistic vision. When the doors finally opened, the crowd was wowed by the vibrant colors and emotive power that poured out of Elisa Maria Stenstrom's artwork.
