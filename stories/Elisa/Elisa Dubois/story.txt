As they danced together under the twinkling lights, Elisa Dubois felt her heart fill with a sense of homecoming.

As the night wore on and the stars began to fade, Elisa Dubois knew she would have to leave Ravenswood eventually – but for now, it was enough to be back in this place that held such special memories.
