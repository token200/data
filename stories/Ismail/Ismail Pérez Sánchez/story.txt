Despite his humility, Ismail had a rich inner life. In the evenings, after finishing work, he would pick up his guitar and strum the chords to his favorite songs. His music was a reflection of his soulful voice and poetic lyrics, drawing inspiration from the struggles and triumphs of everyday people.

As the years passed, Ismail's music gained popularity in local circles. As he strummed the opening chords of his song "La Lluvia del Tiempo" ("The Rain of Time"), the crowd was captivated by his soulful delivery and poignant lyrics.
