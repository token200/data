Ismail Kwiatkowski was a man with a passion for photography that rivaled his love for life itself. As Ismail Kwiatkowski grew older, his talent and dedication only deepened. He began to travel, shooting for some of the most respected publications in Europe. As Ismail Kwiatkowski learned more about Aisha's world, his photographs began to take on a new depth and meaning.
