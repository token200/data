The woman looked up, her eyes locking onto Valerie's. "Please," she stammered, "you have to help me. They're going to kill me."

Valerie Romanova's instincts kicked in, and she swiftly took charge, using her skills to unravel the web of deceit surrounding the young woman. As the truth began to unravel, Valerie realized that this was just another piece in the ever-growing puzzle of corruption that had become her life.
