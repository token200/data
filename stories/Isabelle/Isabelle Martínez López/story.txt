One day, Isabelle received a tip about a potential scandal involving a prominent businessman who was accused of embezzling funds from his company. Despite the risks, she decided to investigate further, convinced that she had stumbled upon something big.

Over the next several days, Isabelle worked tirelessly to gather evidence and interview sources, often sneaking around the city at night to avoid detection by those she suspected were connected to the scandal.
