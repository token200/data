Years went by, and Lucia Liepins honed her craft, traveling the world and shooting everything from portraits of strangers to landscapes of breathtaking beauty. Her camera had become an extension of herself, a tool for telling stories and evoking emotions.

The old woman's eyes seemed to hold a deep wisdom, and Lucia felt an inexplicable connection to her.
