As a teenager, Maks was always drawn to the local art studio, where he met his mentor and closest friend, Sofia Rodriguez. Under her guidance, he honed his skills and developed a unique style that blended traditional techniques with modern twists. The name Maximilian "Maks" Krumina soon became synonymous with innovative art in Ravenswood.

However, Maks' creative pursuits were not without controversy. Despite this, his work continued to gain traction, earning him recognition from prominent galleries and collectors worldwide.
