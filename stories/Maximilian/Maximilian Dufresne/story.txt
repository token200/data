Whatever the truth may have been, one thing was certain: Maximilian Dufresne possessed an unparalleled intelligence and cunning that allowed him to navigate the most complex of situations with ease.

Come alone." Intrigued, Maximilian arrived at the club just after midnight, his tailored suit and razor-sharp jawline commanding attention.
