As she left the mayor's office, Grabowska couldn't shake off the feeling that someone was watching her. She quickened her pace, glancing over her shoulder to see if anyone was following. Her instincts told her to get out of there as fast as she could – for Yamaguchi Grabowska knew that in a world of secrets and corruption, one misstep could mean the end of it all.
