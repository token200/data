Others swore they heard the soft hum of an unknown melody emanating from their direction, as if Eben was savoring the thrill of the chase.

Despite the many rumors and theories surrounding Yamaguchi Eben's true identity, one thing remained certain: this master thief had left an indelible mark on Tokyo's underworld.
