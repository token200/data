As she grew older, Mikael Totti began to realize that her visions were not just random fantasies. Mikael Totti knew that she had to uncover the truth about her visions and her connection to this hidden world. She spent countless hours poring over dusty tomes in the town library, searching for any mention of herself or her ancestors. And slowly but surely, the pieces began to fall into place.
