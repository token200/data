But for Mikael Wei, photography was more than just a creative outlet – it was a way to raise awareness and inspire action. He had spent years traveling the world, documenting the effects of pollution, deforestation, and overfishing on our planet's delicate balance.

As he grew older, his passion only intensified, driving him to pursue a career in photography that would allow him to use his voice to raise awareness about the issues he cared most about.
