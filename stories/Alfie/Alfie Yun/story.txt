His grandfather, a renowned horticulturist, taught Alfie everything he knew about cultivating life from even the smallest seed.

Years passed, and Alfie Yun's passion for botany only grew stronger. He pursued a degree in horticulture, determined to make a name for himself in the field. Intrigued, he brought the specimen back to his greenhouse and began studying it in earnest.
