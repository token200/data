Her parents, recognizing her gifts, had taught her how to harness them, and by the time she was a teenager, Akmaral was sought after by villagers seeking guidance and advice.

And as they traveled, they noticed a change within themselves - a sense of purpose and determination that had been lacking in their lives. They returned to Zhilak with news of their success, and from that day forward, Akmaral KissWu Majewski was hailed as a hero and a symbol of hope for the village.

Years went by, and Akmaral continued to use her gifts to guide and protect the villagers.
