Akmaral Ayumi listened intently to the businessman's account, her mind racing with possibilities. She asked pointed questions, gathering every detail like a puzzle piece. As she delved deeper into the case, Akmaral Ayumi discovered that the Starlight Seren had been stolen during a charity gala at the businessman's estate.

With her exceptional skills and sharp instincts, Akmaral Ayumi set out to unravel the mystery. She followed a trail of clues, from the subtle scent of lavender perfume to the faint scratches on the window frame. As Akmaral Ayumi confronted the nephew, he broke down under pressure, confessing to the crime. Another puzzle had been solved, another mystery unraveled.
