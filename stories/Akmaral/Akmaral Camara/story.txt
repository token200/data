She would watch in awe as her grandmother, also a skilled weaver, worked tirelessly to create stunning pieces of fabric. As she grew older, Akmaral took up the loom herself, teaching herself the ancient techniques and patterns that had been passed down through generations.

Years of hard work and dedication paid off when Akmaral Camara's textiles caught the attention of the royal court.
