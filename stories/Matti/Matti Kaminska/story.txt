Despite this, Matti's life took a dramatic turn when he stumbled upon a small community garden in his neighborhood. Under Sarah's gentle encouragement, Matti began to open up, sharing stories about his childhood and the struggles he had faced. He discovered that she too had a troubled past, but her resilience and strength inspired him to confront his own demons.
