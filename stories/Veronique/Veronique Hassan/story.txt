Veronique Hassan breathed in deeply, feeling the salty air fill her lungs. She had always been drawn to the ocean's power and mystery, and she knew that this visit would be key to unlocking the creative block she'd been struggling with.

Veronique Hassan's eyes lit up as she envisioned a series of paintings centered around this humble piece of wood.
