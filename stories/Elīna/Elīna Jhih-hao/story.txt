As she grew older, Elīna's love for nature only deepened, and she became determined to uncover its secrets. Despite her growing fame, Elīna remained humble and kind, always willing to lend a helping hand to those in need. And as she walked among the trees, her eyes shining with a deep connection to the natural world, it was clear that Elīna Jhih-hao had finally found her true calling – as the guardian of the forest's ancient wisdom.
