As she worked, Elīna felt a sense of contentment wash over her. This was what she loved about her job – the freedom to explore, to discover new stories, and to share them with the world. With her camera equipment loaded onto her shoulders, Elīna made her way to the office to share her latest photos with her editor. As she walked, she felt a sense of pride knowing that her work was more than just pictures – it was a way to preserve the soul of Lisbon for future generations.

Elīna Mendes arrived at the office, ready to share her vision with the world. She presented her images with confidence and enthusiasm, eager to collaborate on bringing Alfama's story to life through her lens. With this new project, Elīna felt that she was not only capturing memories but also weaving them into a tapestry of understanding – a testament to her ability as a photographer and storyteller.
