Her parents, though not artists themselves, encouraged her passion, providing her with a small studio in their home where she could hone her craft.

As Elīna Trevisan grew older, her talent became increasingly evident. After completing her studies, Elīna Trevisan set out on her own, determined to make a name for herself in the competitive world of art. Her paintings began to gain recognition, with galleries and collectors taking notice of her talent.
