As she waited in line, she pulled out her phone to check the email that had prompted her to attend.

Kamila Kozlowski had always been drawn to the arts, but it wasn't until she stumbled upon a small gallery in the city's oldest neighborhood that she felt a spark of inspiration. She found herself opening up about her own experiences as a young artist, feeling seen and understood by this stranger.
