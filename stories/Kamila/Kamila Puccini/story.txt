Kamila Puccini was a force to be reckoned with in the world of competitive figure skating. As the daughter of renowned composer Giacomo Puccini, she had grown up surrounded by music and art. However, it was her own passion for the sport that had truly set her on fire.

She knew that she was living up to her family's legacy – one that she was determined to honor with every passing day.
