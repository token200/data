And now, as she stood in the heart of her hometown, she realized what it was: she was home.

Kamila's own life had taken a dramatic turn since leaving Ashwood. She'd met a partner in New Haven, and together they'd built a life filled with love, laughter, and adventure. Was this really where she was meant to be? Or was it just a temporary detour on the path to true happiness?
