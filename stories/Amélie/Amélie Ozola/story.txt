As dusk settled over Montmartre, Amélie stepped back to admire her work, and that was when she saw it: a tiny drawing of a heart amidst the lines and curves of the puppy's face.

In that moment, something shifted within Amélie Elara Ozola. She realized that life wasn't just about capturing the world's beauty; it was also about nurturing its imperfections. As she finished her sketch, the puppy curled up beside her, a contented sigh escaping its tiny body.
