Amélie's curiosity got the better of her, and she approached the table, introducing herself to the old man. Amélie's eyes sparkled as she listened, feeling an instant connection to Pierre and his story.

Over the next few weeks, Amélie returned to the café again and again, each time spending hours talking with Pierre and learning more about his novel. As their friendship grew, Amélie began to see the world through Pierre's eyes – a world of wonder, magic, and possibility.

Amélie Stensson knew that she had found something special in Pierre – not just a friend or mentor, but a kindred spirit who understood her in a way few others did.
