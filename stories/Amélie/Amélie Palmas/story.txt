Colette listened intently, offering words of wisdom and comfort that helped Amélie Palmas to heal. And as they laughed and cried together, Amélie realized that she had found a true friend in Colette.

Amélie Palmas's art began to reflect this newfound appreciation for the beauty in everyday life. Her paintings became more vibrant, her brushstrokes more expressive. And as she created, she felt Colette's presence around her, guiding her towards a brighter future.
