Rumors swirled about Amélie's true identity - was she a former government agent turned rogue, or simply a brilliant prodigy with a taste for the thrill of the digital underworld? Finally, she had pinpointed their location: an abandoned warehouse on the outskirts of town. As she entered the dimly lit facility, her eyes adjusted to the darkness, and she began to work her magic.
