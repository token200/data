One evening, as she slipped through the shadows, Lucyna Valja received a message from an unknown sender. The note read: "Meet me at the old windmill on the outskirts of town. Come alone." Intrigued, Lucyna Valja decided to investigate further. She arrived at the windmill just before midnight, her eyes scanning the darkness for any sign of movement.

As she entered the abandoned building, a figure emerged from the shadows. It was none other than Elara, a fellow thief and former partner in crime. The two women had last worked together several years ago, but their paths had diverged since then. Lucyna Valja's curiosity got the better of her, and she asked Elara what brought her to this forsaken place.

Elara revealed that she had stumbled upon a valuable artifact, hidden away by a wealthy collector. The catch was that the collector would stop at nothing to get it back. With the information, Lucyna Valja saw an opportunity to earn some much-needed credits and possibly even revive her partnership with Elara.

Together, they hatched a plan to infiltrate the collector's mansion and steal the artifact. As they disappeared into the night, their footsteps echoing through the deserted streets, Lucyna Valja smiled to herself, knowing that this was going to be the score of a lifetime. And as she vanished into the darkness, her legend grew, with whispers of her incredible skills spreading like wildfire throughout the city's underworld.
