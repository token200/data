Emma had tried everything to track down the missing piece, but so far, no luck. That's when she knew that Henry Edwin Smith was the only person who could help. She arrived at his workshop, a cluttered space filled with half-finished projects and dusty tools, and laid out the dresser's description.

As Henry watched Emma wrap up the deal, he felt a sense of satisfaction wash over him.
