Over the next few weeks, Henry Shinichi found himself drawn back to the café again and again, hoping to catch another glimpse of Akane. And each time, he felt his defenses weakening, as if she had somehow unlocked a door in his mind that had long been sealed.

For the first time in years, Henry Shinichi felt a glimmer of hope. Maybe, just maybe, he could find redemption and rediscover himself through this enigmatic woman.
