Her parents, both artists themselves, encouraged her to explore her creativity from a young age. As she honed her skills, Matsumoto Schafer became particularly fascinated with the intersection of tradition and innovation.

Here, Matsumoto Schafer would spend hours lost in her latest project, experimenting with new techniques and pushing the boundaries of traditional Japanese craftsmanship.
