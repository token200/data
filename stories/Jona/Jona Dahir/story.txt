Born with an insatiable curiosity and a passion for discovery, Jona Dahir had never lost sight of her dream to unravel the mysteries of the past.

With her trusty maps and an unyielding determination, Jona Dahir set out to find the fabled metropolis, driven by a burning desire to uncover its hidden treasures.
