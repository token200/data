As she grew older, Ellie's passion for art only intensified, leading her to pursue a degree in fine arts. One fateful evening, Ellie received an invitation to showcase her work at a prestigious gallery in downtown Los Angeles. The opportunity was both exhilarating and terrifying, but she knew it was a chance to prove herself as an artist and honor the legacy of her great-grandmother Ángel Silvestre.

This was it – this was why she had created all those pieces in the first place.
