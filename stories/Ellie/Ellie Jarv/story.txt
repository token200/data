One sunny morning, as she sat sipping coffee on the rooftop of her hotel in Marrakech, Morocco, Ellie Jarv began to scribble notes for her next article. As they traversed dunes in a vintage Land Rover, Ellie Jarv couldn't help but feel a sense of excitement and wonder. The vast expanse of sand stretched out before them like an endless canvas, painted with hues of gold and crimson.
