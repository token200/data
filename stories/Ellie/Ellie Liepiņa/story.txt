With the help of her friends and family, Ellie began working on a revolutionary new app designed to connect small business owners with resources and expertise they might otherwise lack.

As the app took shape, Ellie Liepiņa's reputation as a visionary leader grew. She became a fixture in Riga's entrepreneurial scene, inspiring countless others to follow in her footsteps. And even though she never left Latvia, Ellie knew that her work would touch hearts and minds all over the world.
