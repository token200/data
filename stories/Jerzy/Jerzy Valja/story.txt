Despite his notorious exploits, Jerzy's past remained a closely guarded secret. Some said he was born into a life of crime, while others whispered that he had once been a respected member of high society before turning to a life of theft.

One thing was certain: Jerzy Valja was a man of exceptional skill and cunning. He could slip in and out of the shadows unnoticed, always staying one step ahead of his pursuers. Finally, he reached the exhibit hall where the artifact was displayed, and with a swift movement, he snatched it from its pedestal.
