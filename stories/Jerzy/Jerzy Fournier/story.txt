Jerzy Fournier's breakthrough came when he was invited to participate in the prestigious Biennale de Paris. His painting, "L'Éclipse," a swirling vortex of color and light, was met with critical acclaim and catapulted him to international fame.

Despite his success, Jerzy Fournier remained committed to pushing the boundaries of art. He continued to experiment with new techniques and mediums, often incorporating elements of collage and assemblage into his work.
