Carla Angel Silvestre moved effortlessly between the counter and the oven, her hands moving with a practiced ease that belied her years of experience.

Despite the chaos of the bakery, Carla Angel Silvestre remained calm and composed, always ready to lend a helping hand or offer words of encouragement. It was no wonder that "Sweet Delights" had become synonymous with kindness and generosity in Willow Creek.
