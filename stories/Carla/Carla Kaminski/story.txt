One evening, as Carla Kaminski was closing up shop, she received an unexpected visit from her old friend, Emily. Emily had just moved back to Willow Creek after years away and was in desperate need of some style advice. Carla welcomed her with open arms, offering her a cup of tea and a seat by the fireplace.

When Emily mentioned that she was struggling to find a job, Carla sprang into action. She offered her own connections and expertise, putting together a plan to get Emily back on track.
