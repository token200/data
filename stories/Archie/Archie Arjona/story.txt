Archie's curiosity was piqued as she made her way through the winding streets of Shibuya. She had heard whispers of The Phoenix's involvement in some shady dealings, but nothing concrete. Her sources were few and far between, but Archie was determined to uncover the truth.

She made her way deeper into the club, her eyes adjusting to the dim light as she scanned the room for any signs of The Phoenix.

Archie Arjona had always been drawn to the unknown, and this was no exception. As a seeker of truth and justice, she knew that sometimes it meant taking risks – and facing danger head-on. She had heard whispers of a dark secret hidden beneath the surface of Tokyo's nightlife, and Archie was determined to uncover it.
