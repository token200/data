Archie Cooper was known for his fearless attitude and razor-sharp wit, which had earned him a reputation as one of the most notorious private investigators in the city. With his rugged good looks and piercing blue eyes, he had a way of getting people to open up to him that few others could match.

Archie Cooper listened intently, his mind racing with possibilities and theories. With Samantha's help, Archie set out on a perilous journey to expose the truth, driven by his insatiable curiosity and unwavering determination.
