As the night wore on, Sándor Jhih-hao had gathered an impressive collection of ingredients and stories. She thanked Mr. Wang for his wisdom and left the market with a renewed sense of purpose. Back in her cozy apartment, she began experimenting with new recipes, blending Taiwanese and Hungarian flavors in creative ways.
