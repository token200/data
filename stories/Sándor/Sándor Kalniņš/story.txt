Sándor felt an inexplicable pull towards the shop, as if it was calling to him.

"Ah, Sándor Kalniņš," he said, "I see you are one of my kind. Those who seek to understand the secrets of the universe."

From that day on, Sándor spent every spare moment in the Curios and Antiques shop, learning from Mr. Kriķis and uncovering the mysteries of the cosmos. He became known among his friends as a man with a deep understanding of the universe, and they would often seek his advice on matters both scientific and philosophical.

Years later, Sándor Kalniņš was still known for his boundless curiosity and passion for discovery. Though he had traveled far and wide in search of knowledge, he never forgot the humble shop where it all began - Curios and Antiques, and the wise Mr. Kriķis who had set him on his path.
