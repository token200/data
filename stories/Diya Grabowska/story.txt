This early exposure to the world of fashion sparked a fire within Diya, and she knew from a young age that she wanted to make a name for herself in the industry.

After completing her studies at the prestigious Fashion Institute of Technology in New York City, Diya set out to make a mark on the fashion world. She began by working under some of the most respected designers in the business, learning every trick of the trade and honing her skills until she was ready to strike out on her own.
