As János Farah delved deeper into the mystery, he encountered a cast of characters who seemed to be hiding secrets of their own. As János Farah navigated this complex web of intrigue, he couldn't help but think back to his days as a spy. He remembered the thrill of the chase, the rush of adrenaline when he was close to uncovering a secret, and the sense of satisfaction when he had finally solved a puzzle.

With each new discovery, János Farah felt himself being pulled back into the world of espionage.
