Her parents, though not scholars themselves, encouraged her curiosity, recognizing the fire within their daughter that could only be fanned into flame through education and exploration.

As word of her discovery spread, scholars from across the globe flocked to Krakow to learn from the brilliant János Nowakowska. Her name became synonymous with archaeological prowess, and her work continued to shed new light on the mysteries of the past.

For János Nowakowska, there was no greater joy than uncovering a piece of history that had long been lost to the sands of time.
