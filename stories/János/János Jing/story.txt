As he grew older, János Jing's love of nature only deepened, and he became increasingly fascinated with the ancient myths and legends of his ancestors. He spent countless hours poring over dusty tomes in the local library, uncovering secrets about the gods and goddesses that once roamed the land.

Despite his passion for learning, János Jing's true calling lay elsewhere. Entranced by the sound, János Jing set out into the woods, his violin case slung over his shoulder, determined to uncover the source of the haunting tune.
