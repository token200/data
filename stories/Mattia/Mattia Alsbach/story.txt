She would spend hours exploring the woods, collecting leaves, and observing the way light danced through the trees. These early fascinations would later become a cornerstone of her artistic expression.

As Mattia Alsbach's career took off, she found herself juggling multiple projects simultaneously. Her studio was a flurry of creativity, with paint-splattered canvases, half-finished sculptures, and an endless supply of coffee cups.
