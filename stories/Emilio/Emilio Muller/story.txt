Despite the thrill of creating something from scratch, Emilio Müller knew that his double life couldn't last forever. The studio owner had promised him a spot as a leading artist in his own right, but it meant sacrificing his love of street art. The next morning, the studio owner arrived to find Emilio Müller sitting at his easel, staring blankly at a canvas that had once held so much promise.
