Emilio Esquivel arrived at the latest crime scene, where he began examining the area for any hints. He noticed that one of the bushes seemed slightly out of place, its branches twisted in a peculiar way.

Without hesitation, Emilio Esquivel plucked the bush from the ground and examined it more closely. To his surprise, he found a small hole hidden within the branches, leading to a tiny compartment inside the wooden box. He extracted the box and opened it, revealing a note with an address.

As the truth unraveled, Emilio Esquivel realized that the thief wasn't after valuables but rather the sentimental value attached to the stolen boxes.
