As Emilio walked through the crowded streets of Old San Juan, his camera slung over his shoulder, he felt a sense of purpose wash over him. He had grown up in this city, and knew its rhythms and secrets like the back of his hand. His full name was Emilio Ruiz Garcia, and he took pride in using his platform to amplify the voices of those who were often overlooked.

He approached a young mother, her face etched with exhaustion and worry, and asked if he could take her picture. As the day drew to a close, Emilio packed up his gear and headed back to his office. His full name was on every card, badge, and ID badge - it was who he was, what he did.
