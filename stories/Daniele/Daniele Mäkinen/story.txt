Her friends oohed and ahhed over the sketches, offering words of encouragement and constructive criticism.

She stopped at a small booth selling traditional Finnish handicrafts, where she struck up a conversation with the vendor about her designs.

Inspired by their chat, Daniele returned to her studio determined to create something new and unique. Over the next few days, she poured her heart and soul into the project, experimenting with different colors and textures until she had finally brought her vision to life. When she stepped back to admire the finished piece, she beamed with pride – it was her most accomplished work yet.

As word of Daniele Mäkinen's latest creation spread throughout the city's art community, she began receiving commissions from local businesses and individuals eager to showcase their own talent.
