Throughout her travels, Nagasaki Valja's art flourished, capturing the essence of the people and places she encountered. Her subjects ranged from nobles to peasants, each one imbued with a sense of dignity and humanity that resonated deeply with her audience.

Years passed, and Nagasaki Valja's reputation grew, earning her a place alongside the great masters of the time.
