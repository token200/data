Kalnina happened upon the scene, intervening swiftly and decisively to foil the extortion attempt. The yakuza leader was left shaken, and the businessman expressed his gratitude towards this mysterious guardian angel.

As news of her heroics spread, whispers began circulating about Nagasaki Kalnina's true identity. Some claimed she was a former member of an elite special forces unit, while others speculated that she possessed supernatural abilities. While none of these claims were true, Kalnina smiled knowingly, aware that her legend had grown too great to be denied.

With her reputation solidified, Kalnina continued to protect those in need, earning the respect and admiration of Tokyo's elite. Her name became synonymous with bravery and selflessness, inspiring countless others to follow in her footsteps.
