But one thing was certain - Nagasaki Bauer possessed a skillset that raised eyebrows among the townsfolk. His hands moved with precision, weaving intricate patterns on canvas or wood, as if the very fabric of reality had been transformed into art.

Yet, whenever Bauer agreed to meet, he'd vanish before dawn, leaving behind only a whisper of what he'd shared the night before. Some believed he was hiding a dark past, while others thought him an otherworldly being, blessed with the ability to tap into hidden forces.
