Nagasaki Pei-ling was known for her fiery spirit and sharp tongue in the small town of Lushan. As the daughter of a wealthy merchant, she had always been spoiled with luxury and attention. However, after her father's untimely death, Pei-ling found herself at odds with her stepmother, who sought to control every aspect of her life.

Despite her privileged upbringing, Pei-ling was determined to forge her own path. She spent most of her days exploring the surrounding countryside, learning about herbalism and medicine from the local healers. Her passion for healing eventually led her to open her own apothecary shop in town.

As word of her skills spread, people began to seek Pei-ling out for their ailments. She was known for her innovative remedies and her ability to listen with empathy. However, not everyone was pleased with Pei-ling's success. The town's patriarch, Mr. Wang, disapproved of a woman running an apothecary shop, considering it beneath him.
