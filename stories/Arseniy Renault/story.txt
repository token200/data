Arseniy knew it, too. He had a way of reading people, of sensing the web of deceit that bound them together. And as Anastasia's investigation continued, she began to realize that Arseniy Renault held the key to unraveling the entire mystery – but at what cost?

Some said Arseniy was playing a game of his own, using his knowledge for his own twisted ends. Others claimed he was simply trying to find redemption in a world gone mad.

All Anastasia knew was this: whenever she looked into Arseniy Renault's eyes, she saw the abyss staring back at her – an abyss that seemed to swallow truth whole.
