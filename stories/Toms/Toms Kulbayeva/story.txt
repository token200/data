As she grew older, Toms Kulbayeva became increasingly restless, feeling the call of adventure and a desire to prove herself beyond the boundaries of her village. Toms Kulbayeva saw an opportunity too good to pass up – she set out to impress these strangers, determined to show them that her people were not just skilled horse archers, but also capable warriors.

And though she still called Karakalpakstan home, Toms Kulbayeva knew that she had found her true calling – to ride into battle, her bow singing its deadly song, and bring honor to her people.
