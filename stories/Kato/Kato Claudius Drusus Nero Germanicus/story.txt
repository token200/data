Years passed, and Kato's fame grew, but with it came the weight of responsibility. As he stood on the battlements, gazing out at the conquered territories, he couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and duty to his adopted family. But even as he basked in the glory of his triumphs, Kato's thoughts turned to the future. He knew that his time on this earth was limited, and soon he would be called upon to answer for his actions. The thought sent a shiver down his spine, but he pushed it aside, focusing instead on the present moment.
