Years passed, and Kato's star continued to ascend. He formed lasting bonds with influential figures, including the Emperor himself, Titus. Though their relationship was often tumultuous, Kato's unwavering dedication to Rome's greatness earned him a place at the emperor's side.

Finally, the day of reckoning arrived. The great Emperor Titus passed away, and the Roman Senate, desperate for a new leader, looked to Kato Flavius Domitianus Augustus as their next hope. With his full name on the lips of every senator, Kato was anointed as the new Emperor, marking the beginning of a golden age in Rome's history.

As the newly crowned Emperor, Kato walked among his people with a newfound sense of purpose. His name echoed throughout the empire, synonymous with strength, wisdom, and unwavering commitment to the Roman cause.
