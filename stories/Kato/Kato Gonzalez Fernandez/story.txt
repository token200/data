Undeterred, Kato decided to take matters into her own hands. She began secretly investigating the business leaders, using her network of sources to gather more information. It wasn't long before she stumbled upon a shocking connection between several of the implicated individuals.

As the evidence mounted, Kato knew she had to act fast. She assembled a team of reporters and editors, and together they worked tirelessly to craft a compelling exposé. The article was published under Kato's byline, and it sent shockwaves throughout the city's business community.
