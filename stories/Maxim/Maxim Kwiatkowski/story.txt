Maximilian "Max" Kwiatkowski knew that he had to act swiftly to protect his company's reputation and the livelihoods of his employees. He made the tough decision to terminate the supplier contract and instead, invested in new suppliers who shared the same values as his business.

Despite the challenges he had faced, Maximilian "Max" Kwiatkowski remained committed to doing what was right for his business and its people.
