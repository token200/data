As he grew older, Bernard's passion for learning only intensified. One day, a young girl from the local school approached Bernard with a bouquet of wildflowers. She told him that she had been inspired by his discovery and wanted to follow in his footsteps. Bernard Angert smiled, his eyes twinkling with delight. He took the flowers from her hands and pressed them between the pages of one of his favorite books.
