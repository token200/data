As he grew older, Bernard's love for art only intensified. He began studying painting in college, but it was his true calling. When not lost in his art, he worked as a part-time music teacher, playing the guitar to students of all ages. His favorite song to play was "Moon River," which always brought a smile to his face.

The night of the exhibition arrived, and Bernard's heart swelled with pride. His paintings adorned the walls, drawing gasps of amazement from the crowd. As he walked through the room, he felt a sense of belonging, as if all his hard work had finally paid off.
