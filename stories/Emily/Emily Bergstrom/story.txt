Emily Bergstrom sat alone in her small apartment, surrounded by half-finished canvases and paint-splattered easels. As an aspiring artist, she had always been driven to create something meaningful, but lately, it seemed like nothing was working out.

Emily Bergstrom couldn't help but wonder if she had lost her touch, or if it was all just a product of her own inflated ego.
