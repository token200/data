Emily was thrilled at the opportunity to take on the project and dove headfirst into researching the company's history and target audience.

As she worked on the design, Emily couldn't help but think about her own journey as an artist. She had always been drawn to the intersection of art and commerce, and she loved finding ways to make brands more visually appealing without sacrificing their core values.

After several weeks of hard work, Emily presented her final design concept to The Daily Grind owner, Alex.
