She purchased it on a whim and soon found herself captivated by the world of photography. As she learned about the technical aspects of the craft, Emily began to see the camera as an extension of her painting skills.

With her newfound passion for photography, Emily Luis Borges started experimenting with combining paint and lens. Her art evolved into a unique fusion of visual storytelling, blending elements of both painting and photography.
