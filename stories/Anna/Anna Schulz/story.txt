Anna Schulz stepped off the stage, her bright smile still plastered on her face as she waved to the cheering crowd. As lead singer of the indie rock band "Echoes in the Attic," Anna had worked tirelessly for years to bring their unique sound to life.

As the night drew to a close, Anna made her way home, feeling exhilarated but also exhausted. She had given it her all on stage, pouring every ounce of energy into the performance.
