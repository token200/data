She began to see parallels between the artist's struggles and her own, feeling a deep sense of solidarity with this anonymous yet remarkable woman. As she wrote about Wyszkiewicz's life and art, Anna felt a newfound sense of purpose and belonging.

Anna Zielinska's research on Katarzyna Wyszkiewicz would go on to inspire a new generation of artists and historians.
