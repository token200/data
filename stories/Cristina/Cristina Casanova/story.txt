As they grew closer, Cristina began to reveal more about herself, sharing stories of her childhood and her struggles as an artist.

Despite the connection they shared, Julian couldn't shake the feeling that there was something Cristina wasn't telling him – something she kept hidden behind those captivating green eyes. He knew he had to tread carefully, for Cristina Alexandra Casanova was a woman who didn't suffer fools gladly.

As their relationship deepened, Cristina found herself increasingly drawn to Julian's kind and gentle nature.
