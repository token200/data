As the session progressed, Cristina Thompson realized that she needed to dig deeper. She encouraged Sarah to explore her feelings, asking gentle but probing questions that helped the young woman uncover hidden emotions and desires. Her own relationships had been marked by tumultuous ups and downs, leaving her with a lingering sense of uncertainty. With renewed determination, Cristina began to explore new avenues, seeking out relationships that nourished both body and soul. It wasn't an easy journey, but one that would ultimately lead her to discover a sense of purpose and fulfillment she had long been searching for.
