Aslanbek Saar was no stranger to hardship, having faced many challenges throughout his life, including droughts that had threatened the very existence of his herd. Yet, he remained steadfast, drawing strength from the resilience of his people and the land itself.

One day, Aslanbek Saar encountered a young girl lost in the steppes, shivering with fear and uncertainty. With gentle hands, he guided her to safety, sharing what little food and water he had, and listening intently as she told him of her family's troubles back home.
