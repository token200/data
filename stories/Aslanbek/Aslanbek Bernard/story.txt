As the years passed, Aslanbek's reputation grew. People would seek him out, drawn by rumors of his extraordinary storytelling abilities. Some claimed he possessed a deep understanding of the ancient arts, while others whispered that he was, in fact, a messenger from another realm.

Despite these whispers, Aslanbek remained an enigma, even to those closest to him. He spoke little of himself, and what he did say only seemed to confirm the rumors – a master weaver of tales, a keeper of secrets, a guardian of the unknown.

One fateful evening, as the moon hung low in the sky like a silver coin, Aslanbek was approached by a young girl with eyes as wide as stars.
