One day, as Aslanbek Almodovar wandered through the stalls, she stumbled upon an old, mysterious-looking rug. Its colors seemed to shift and shimmer in the sunlight, like the surface of a still pond. Aslanbek Almodovar spent the next few weeks studying the rug, unraveling its secrets and learning the language of the ancient ones who had woven it. Her studies took her to hidden corners of the city, where she discovered forgotten libraries, crumbling temples, and secret societies sworn to protect the knowledge of the ancients.

As news of Aslanbek Almodovar's abilities spread, people began to seek her out, hoping that she could unlock the secrets of their own pasts. The local shawarma vendors, who had known her since childhood, watched with pride as she grew into a confident and powerful woman.
