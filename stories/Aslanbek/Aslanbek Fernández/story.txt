The lessons they taught him – how to track animals, navigate by the stars, and live off the land – would serve him well throughout his life.

As a young man, Aslanbek set out on his own, traveling extensively throughout South America. He worked as a cowboy, a miner, and even a teacher, taking on any job that came his way. His presence seemed to bring stability to the communities he joined, earning him a reputation as a reliable and kind-hearted individual.

Years went by, and Aslanbek's stories of adventure and survival became legendary. Some claimed he was a shapeshifter, able to transform into a majestic lion at will.

Aslanbek never corrected them. He preferred to keep his true nature hidden, preferring to watch over those who were lost and alone. A humble soul with an inner strength that bordered on the mystical, Aslanbek Fernández embodied the spirit of resilience in a world filled with danger and uncertainty.

Without hesitation, he took her under his wing, wrapping his arms around her like a warm blanket. In that moment, Sofia felt the presence of something greater than herself.

From that day forward, Aslanbek Fernández became more than just a mysterious figure – he was a guardian, watching over those who needed protection from the harsh realities of life.
