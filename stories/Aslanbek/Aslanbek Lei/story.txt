With the investor's backing, Aslanbek Lei founded Green Earth Kazakhstan, a company that aimed to promote eco-friendly farming methods and create jobs for local communities. The venture took off, with Aslanbek becoming a respected figure in Almaty's business circles.

As his success grew, so did Aslanbek's commitment to giving back to his community. He established scholarships for young entrepreneurs from rural areas and partnered with local NGOs to develop sustainable development projects.
