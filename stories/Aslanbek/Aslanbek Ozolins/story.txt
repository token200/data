Aslanbek Ozolins stood tall, ready to face whatever lay ahead. He knew that he was not yet prepared for a battle of this magnitude, but he also knew that he could not turn his back on Lyra or the mystical forest. With a deep breath, he steeled himself and set out to confront Malakai.

Just when it seemed that the tide was turning in Malakai's favor, Lyra appeared, her eyes blazing with power. With a wave of her hand, she summoned a blast of energy that sent Malakai stumbling back.
