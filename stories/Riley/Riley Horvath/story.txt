As she worked, Riley Horvath felt a sense of peace wash over her. The chaos and noise of the world outside seemed to fade away, replaced by the silence and stillness of the factory's empty spaces. As the hours passed, Riley Horvath began to notice strange symbols etched into the walls, their meaning unknown to her. Intrigued, she started to study them more closely, pouring over her sketchbook pages for any clues that might reveal their secrets. With each passing moment, the mystery deepened, and Riley's curiosity grew.
