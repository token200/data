With newfound determination, Riley presented her findings to the court and argued passionately for her client's innocence. The judge was impressed by her conviction and expertise, and she won the case on a technicality.

Riley García Martínez had saved another life that day, and her reputation as a fierce advocate for justice had been cemented. She continued to work tirelessly, taking on cases that others deemed unwinnable and fighting for the rights of the marginalized and oppressed.
