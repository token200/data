And now, at 25, she had finally saved enough money and gathered the necessary gear to tackle one of the most iconic challenges on her list: hiking the entire length of the Grand Canyon.

The journey was not for the faint of heart. The terrain was unforgiving, with steep drop-offs and treacherous terrain that required every ounce of skill and determination she possessed. But Riley was no stranger to adversity. She had grown up learning survival skills from her rugged father, who had spent his life exploring the American West.
