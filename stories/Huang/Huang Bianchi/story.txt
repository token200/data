As a child, she would spend hours sketching the flowers that bloomed in her family's garden, and as she grew older, her art evolved to include bold brushstrokes and vivid colors.

After completing art school, Bianchi moved to the city to pursue her dreams. She shared a small studio with two other artists, but it was her own corner of the room where she felt most at home.
