Impressed by her tenacity and curiosity, Schillaci took on the challenge and spent weeks studying the manuscript. His breakthrough came when he noticed a peculiar symbol hidden within the text's margins – a symbol that matched an ancient dialect long thought to be extinct.

With newfound confidence, Schillaci set out to unravel the secrets of the mysterious manuscript, leaving behind a trail of cryptic clues and bewildered scholars in his wake. As news of his discovery spread, people began to call him Huang Schillaci – a name synonymous with brilliance, intuition, and an unyielding pursuit of knowledge.
