Tamas Bierhals was known for his unorthodox approach to solving problems. As a renowned detective, he had a knack for thinking outside the box and uncovering clues that others might miss.

Intrigued by the challenge, Tamas Bierhals agreed to take on the case. He began by interviewing witnesses and surveying the crime scenes. His eyes scanned the area, taking in every detail, from the faint scent of lavender in the air to the unique pattern of grass beneath his feet.

Tamas Bierhals' eyes lit up as he realized the connection. He hastily contacted Zsolt and arranged for him to meet at the detective's office. Zsolt confirmed Tamas Bierhals' suspicions, revealing that he had indeed sold several pieces of wood to the thief in the past. With this new information, Tamas Bierhals was able to track down the culprit and recover all the stolen goods.
