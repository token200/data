Tamas Popov stepped out of the dimly lit alleyway and onto the bustling streets of Budapest. The Hungarian musician had grown accustomed to the city's vibrant energy, but tonight it seemed especially alive. He made his way to the local jazz club, where he was scheduled to perform with his band.
