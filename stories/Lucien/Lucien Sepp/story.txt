He noticed a small detail that others had missed – a faint scratch on one of the display cases, shaped exactly like a rare gemstone found only in a few remote locations around the world. It was a tiny clue, but it was enough to set Lucien's detective senses tingling.

Over the next few days, Lucien Sepp delved deeper into the case, following a trail of breadcrumbs that led him from seedy pawn shops to high-stakes art auctions. His reputation as one of the best in the business had preceded him, and he wasn't surprised when he was approached by several individuals who claimed to have information about the robberies – or at least, information that they were willing to sell.
