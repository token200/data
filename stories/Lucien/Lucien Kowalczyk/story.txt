Intrigued, Lucien pushed open the door and stepped inside.

As their connection grew stronger, Lucien felt a deep sense of belonging he had never experienced before. As the days turned into weeks, Lucien's passion for painting grew stronger than ever. With Luna by his side, he began to explore new techniques and mediums, pushing the boundaries of his creativity.
