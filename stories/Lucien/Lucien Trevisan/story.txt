As he sipped on a whiskey, Lucien Trevisan's mind whirred with possibilities. He had always been driven by a sense of justice, and this lead seemed like the break he needed to expose the truth. Lucien's eyes narrowed. He had a feeling that he was being led into a trap, but his desire for the truth kept him going. He arrived at the warehouse just as night began to fall, the dim lighting casting long shadows across the deserted streets.

 Inside, Lucien Trevisan found himself face-to-face with a woman who claimed to have evidence of the corruption. She handed him a thick stack of documents, each one more incriminating than the last.
