As the sun dipped below the horizon, Dmytro Viktorovich Salamanca found himself entangled in a web of intrigue and deception. With Sofia by his side, he steeled himself for the challenges ahead. This was not just a mission; it was a personal vendetta against those who had destroyed his life.
