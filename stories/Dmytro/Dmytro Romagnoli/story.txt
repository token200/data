Born in Kiev, Ukraine, Dmyt grew up surrounded by the rich history and vibrant culture of Eastern Europe. His early life was marked by tragedy when his parents passed away in a car accident, leaving him orphaned at the age of 12.

Despite this difficult start, Dmyt found solace in art. He spent hours sketching and painting, using his talents to express himself and cope with his emotions. As he grew older, his passion for art only intensified, leading him to study fine arts at a prestigious Ukrainian university.

After completing his studies, Dmyt emigrated to the United States, determined to make a name for himself as an artist.
