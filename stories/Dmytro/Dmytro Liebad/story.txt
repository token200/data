Some said he was once a soldier, wounded in battle, while others whispered that he had been a member of the Soviet secret police. Whatever the truth may have been, it was clear that Liebad had seen his share of darkness.

The owner told her of strange occurrences surrounding the apartment: whispers in the night, faint sounds of typing on an old typewriter.

Sofia returned to the flat, determined to uncover the secrets hidden within its walls. As she entered, she felt a shiver run down her spine – it was as if Liebad himself had been watching her. She began to search the room, finding scattered papers and cryptic notes scribbled on yellowed sheets of paper.

Suddenly, the lights flickered, and Sofia heard the soft scratch of a typewriter key. It sounded like someone was typing in the darkness – Dmytro Ivanovych Liebad himself.
