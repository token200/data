As they began their partnership, Dmytro realized that his past was more complicated than he had ever imagined. And with Lena by his side, he knew he could face whatever came next.

Together, they set out on a journey of discovery and danger, navigating the treacherous world of espionage and uncovering secrets that had been hidden for years.
