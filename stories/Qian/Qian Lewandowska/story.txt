But it was her time studying under some of France's top chefs that truly shaped her culinary identity. After completing her studies, she worked her way up the ranks in some of Paris' most renowned restaurants, honing her skills and developing her unique style.

In 2015, Qian Lewandowska made the bold decision to leave Europe behind and start a new chapter in New York City's vibrant food scene. She was particularly passionate about supporting small farmers and artisans, often partnering with them on special projects that showcased their unique products.
