The police were stumped, and Sophia was at her wit's end. Qian Krūmiņš listened intently, his eyes narrowing as he processed the information.

As he melted back into the shadows, Qian's thoughts turned to his next challenge. What other secrets lay hidden in Riga's winding streets? He knew that as long as he possessed his unique gift, he would always be ready to uncover the truth and bring justice to those who needed it most. With a quiet confidence, Qian Krūmiņš disappeared into the night, leaving behind a trail of solved mysteries and a reputation that would soon spread far and wide.
