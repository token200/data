As a child, she would sneak out of the house at dusk, exploring the city's hidden corners and watching the stars twinkle above. It was only natural that this fascination with the shadows would lead her to a life of stealth and deception.

Qian Sundlof's fingers moved with precision as she picked the lock on the mansion door. With a flick of her wrist, the mechanism gave way, allowing her to slip inside undetected. Her eyes adjusted to the darkness, and she spotted her target: a valuable artifact on display in a glass case.

With calculated care, Qian Sundlof approached the pedestal, her gaze locked onto the glittering gemstone. She reached for it, but just as her fingers made contact, a sudden noise echoed through the hallway. The air was filled with the sound of footsteps, growing louder by the second.

Qian Sundlof didn't flinch. In an instant, she had pocketed the artifact and melted into the shadows. The footsteps stopped outside the door, but Qian Sundlof knew it was only a matter of time before her pursuers tried to follow. With a whispered apology, she vanished into the night.

Her skills as a thief were unmatched, and those who dared to pursue her knew better than to try. Qian Sundlof had long since mastered the art of disappearing.
