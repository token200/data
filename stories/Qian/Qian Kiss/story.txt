Qian Kiss, also known as Qin Liang, was a renowned artist known for her striking yet emotive paintings that captured the essence of humanity's complexities. Born in the vibrant city of Shanghai, China, Qin Liang's artistic journey began at a tender age, inspired by the colors and textures of traditional Chinese calligraphy.

As she grew older, Qin Liang's fascination with art only deepened, leading her to study fine arts at the prestigious Beijing University of Arts. After completing her studies, Qin Liang set out on a journey to showcase her art globally, traveling from Tokyo to New York, leaving an indelible mark on the art world. Qin Liang's artistic success was matched only by her kindness and humility. One day, Qin Liang received an invitation from the director of a prominent museum in Paris to create a large-scale installation for their upcoming exhibition on contemporary Chinese art. Eager to collaborate with this esteemed institution, she accepted the challenge without hesitation.

Qin Liang spent months conceptualizing her vision, pouring her heart and soul into every detail. The result was breathtaking: an immersive experience that transported viewers through a kaleidoscope of colors, textures, and emotions.
