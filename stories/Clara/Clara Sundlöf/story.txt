Clara's heart racing, she took a step forward, and that was when the lights flickered and went out. In the darkness, she heard footsteps, heavy and deliberate, coming from the shadows. Clara Helena Sundlöf knew then that she had to get out of there – fast. She grabbed her flashlight and made a hasty retreat, not stopping until she was safely back on the pavement.
