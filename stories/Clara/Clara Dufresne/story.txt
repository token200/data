Clara Dufresne was a woman with a passion for art and a penchant for the unknown. The woman's eyes were red-rimmed, and her hair was disheveled, but there was something about her that drew Clara Dufresne in.

As the days went by, Clara Dufresne found herself returning to the café again and again, hoping to catch a glimpse of the mysterious young woman. Clara Dufresne took it upon herself to mentor Colette, sharing her own experiences as an artist and offering words of encouragement when needed. As they spent more time together, Clara Dufresne found that she was learning just as much from Colette as Colette was from her. Years went by, and Clara Dufresne continued to inspire and nurture young talent.
