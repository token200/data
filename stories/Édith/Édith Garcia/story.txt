Édith García took great pride in her work, often spending early mornings kneading dough and perfecting her recipes. As he sampled each item on the menu, Édith García anxiously watched from behind the counter. She had worked tirelessly to perfect every recipe, and she knew that this review could make all her hard work pay off.
