As a child, Julia spent hours poring over dusty tomes in her father's library, devouring tales of lost civilizations and legendary cities. Her academic prowess earned her a scholarship to study at the University of Cambridge, where she became obsessed with deciphering ancient texts and languages.

After completing her Ph.D., Julia set out on a quest to explore some of the world's most enigmatic sites, from the ruins of Machu Picchu to the pyramids of Giza. Suddenly, all the threads of her research came together, and she realized that she had been following not just a thread but a map etched into the very fabric of time itself.
