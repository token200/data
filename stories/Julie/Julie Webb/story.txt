But she refused to give up, drawing strength from her family and friends who encouraged her to keep pursuing her dreams.

The theme was "Capturing Light," and Julie knew that this was an opportunity she couldn't afford to miss. As Julie looked back on her journey, she realized that it was moments like these – the triumphs and setbacks alike – that had shaped her into the photographer she was today.
