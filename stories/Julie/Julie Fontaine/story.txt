Julie Fontaine accepted the challenge, digging deeper into the world of high finance and uncovering a web of deceit that went all the way to the top. She spent long hours poring over financial records, interviewing suspects, and following every lead, no matter how small.

Despite the risks, Julie pressed on, driven by a fierce determination to uncover the truth. Her years of experience told her that the key to solving this case lay in the smallest details, and she was determined to leave nothing unturned.
