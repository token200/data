She trekked through dense forests, crossed babbling brooks, and climbed steep hills, determined to find any sign of the wolf. Days turned into weeks, and Julie Liepiņa began to lose hope. The sun beat down on her shoulders, and the mosquitoes buzzed around her ears, but she refused to give up. Her heart racing, Julie Liepiņa followed the sound until she caught sight of a pair of piercing yellow eyes peering out from behind a tree.
