As she grew older, Claire's love for art only intensified, leading her to pursue a career as a professional painter. She spent years honing her skills, experimenting with different techniques and mediums, until finally, she landed her first solo exhibition in a small gallery in the city.

With renewed energy and determination, Claire Petersone set to work on her mural, pouring all her creativity and passion into the canvas.
