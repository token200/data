As the night wore on, whispers of Paweł's legend spread like wildfire through the tavern. Some claimed he was searching for a rare artifact hidden deep within the city's ancient ruins. Others whispered that he was searching for something far more sinister.

Paweł D'Annunzio listened intently to the rumors, his gaze never wavering from the crowd. His eyes seemed to bore into the souls of those around him, as if sizing them up for some unknown purpose.
