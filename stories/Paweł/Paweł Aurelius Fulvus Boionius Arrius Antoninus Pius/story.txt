One day, as he stood before a gathering of esteemed scholars and nobles, Paweł proudly announced that he would continue the legacy of Emperor Antoninus Pius by promoting peace, justice, and understanding throughout the empire. His full name echoed through the halls as he spoke, a reminder of the great tradition he was honored to carry forward.

And so, with Marcus's guidance and his own unyielding dedication, Paweł Aurelius Fulvus Boionius Arrius Antoninus Pius embarked on a journey to spread wisdom, compassion, and fairness throughout the Roman Empire.
