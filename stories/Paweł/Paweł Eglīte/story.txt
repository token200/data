Paweł's journey as an artist began when he stumbled upon an old wooden workshop in the heart of Riga's Old Town. The wooden beams and half-finished sculptures captivated him, inspiring him to create his own masterpieces. As Paweł Eglīte, he became known for his intricate wooden carvings, which seemed to come alive under the light of the setting sun.

His dedication paid off, as critics praised his work for its originality and emotional depth.

Years passed, and Paweł Eglīte became a celebrated figure in the art world. His full name was now synonymous with excellence, as collectors sought out his masterpieces to add to their collections.
