They enrolled her in local art classes, where she quickly demonstrated a natural aptitude for color and composition.

As Emilia grew older, her passion for art only intensified. She devoured books on painting techniques and studied the works of masters past and present. Her room became a studio, filled with canvases, paints, and an array of mediums she was eager to experiment with. Emilia Fernández González was determined to make a name for herself in the art world.
