Her real name, Emilia Girace, wasn't one that evoked the same level of respect and awe as "Evie", but it suited her perfectly. There was a quiet strength to her full name, a sense of determination and resilience that drove her to succeed against all odds.

As she made her way through the darkened halls, Evie's eyes scanned for any signs of security or alarms. Her training kicked in automatically, and with a few swift movements, she disabled the cameras and tripped the alarms.
