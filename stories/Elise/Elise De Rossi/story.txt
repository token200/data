Elise De Rossi stepped onto the worn wooden stage, her eyes scanning the dimly lit crowd with a mix of familiarity and unease. As a renowned performance artist, she was no stranger to uncertainty, but tonight's show felt different. Tonight, it felt like she was on the cusp of something.
