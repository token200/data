Elise's latest target was a wealthy businessman with ties to some of the city's most powerful crime lords. The pay was enormous, but the risks were high - and Elise knew that if she failed, she would be the one paying the price.

As she approached the businessman, Elise's eyes locked onto his, sending a chill down his spine. She was here for one reason only: to take him out, no matter what it took. And with a swift movement, she ended his reign of terror once and for all.
