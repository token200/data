His leadership and vision on the pitch inspired teammates and opponents alike, earning him the respect of fellow football greats like Paolo Maldini (his cousin!).

Throughout his career, Jose Maldini won an unprecedented five European Cup/Champions League titles, four Italian league championships, and two UEFA Super Cups. He was also a key player in Italy's 1988 European Championship-winning squad.

Jose Maldini retired from professional football in 2009, leaving behind a legacy that continues to inspire generations of players and fans.
