The 35-year-old physicist had always been driven by an insatiable curiosity, often sacrificing his personal life for the sake of his research.

As Connor Ivanov worked tirelessly in his cluttered laboratory, he became increasingly obsessed with the possibility of unlocking the secrets of the multiverse. His colleagues at the prestigious research institution had grown concerned about his isolation and erratic behavior, but Connor Ivanov was undeterred.
