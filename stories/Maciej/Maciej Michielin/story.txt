As he grew older, Maciej's passion for art only intensified, leading him to leave his village behind and pursue a life in the city. He enrolled in the prestigious Warsaw Academy of Fine Arts, where he honed his skills under the watchful eye of esteemed professors. His unique perspective and innovative approach to traditional techniques quickly earned him recognition among his peers.

People began to whisper about the mysterious artist known only as Maciej Michielin.
