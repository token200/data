As Paul delved deeper, he found himself in the crosshairs of powerful figures who would stop at nothing to keep their secrets buried. With each new revelation, his determination grew, driven by a fierce sense of justice. He became fixated on unraveling the truth behind the mysterious tip, no matter the risk.

With the truth revealed, Paul Björkvist knew that his work had just begun – exposing the dark forces that lurked in every corner of society.
