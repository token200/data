Intrigued, he devoted himself to unraveling the mystery, tracing the threads of history back to the early 20th century.

His quest took him down treacherous paths, confronting corruption and deceit along the way. Yet, with each new discovery, he drew closer to a deeper understanding of the world around him – a world fraught with danger, but also filled with the promise of redemption.

Through his tireless efforts, Paul Pētersone had become an unsung hero in Riga's eyes, earning respect and admiration from those who knew him.
