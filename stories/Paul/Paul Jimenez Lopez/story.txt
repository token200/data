Paul Jimenez Lopez's curiosity got the best of him, and he pushed open the door to venture inside. The shop was dimly lit, but as his eyes adjusted, he saw rows upon rows of shelves filled with everything from vintage clothing to rare books.

Paul Jimenez Lopez explained his love of photography and his desire to capture the beauty in everyday life. The old man nodded thoughtfully and began to show him some of the shop's more interesting items.
