Her team of researchers trailed behind, armed with state-of-the-art equipment in the hopes of capturing conclusive evidence.

She had spent countless nights camping out in these very woods, waiting for a glimpse of the creature she sought. Her friends back home would think her mad, but Asha Jasinska didn't care.
