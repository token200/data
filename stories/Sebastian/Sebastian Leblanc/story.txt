One fateful evening, Sebastian received an invitation from the mysterious Lady Arachne herself, inviting him to her opulent estate on the outskirts of town. The card read: "Come alone, Leblanc. I have a proposition for you."

Curious and intrigued, Sebastian accepted the invitation and made his way to Lady Arachne's mansion. As he entered the grand foyer, he was struck by the labyrinthine architecture and the air of anticipation that hung like a miasma.

Lady Arachne greeted him with an enigmatic smile, her eyes glinting like polished onyx in the candlelight. She led him to a velvet-draped chamber, where a collection of rare artifacts lay spread before them.

Sebastian's eyes narrowed as he examined the box. He knew that Lady Arachne was a master of manipulation, and this proposition would require all his cunning to unravel.

Lady Arachne smiled, her eyes flashing with approval. "Excellent choice, Leblanc. Together, we shall unravel the threads of fate and weave our own destiny."
