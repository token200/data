Together, they delved into a world of art forgery, corruption, and deceit. As they navigated this treacherous landscape, Sebastian's skills as an investigator were put to the test.

Sebastian Wiktor Wiśniewski was no stranger to danger, but with Sophia by his side, he felt a sense of camaraderie and purpose that he had never experienced before.
