Sebastian Valja stepped off the train and onto the worn platform, his eyes scanning the unfamiliar station. He had just arrived in Paris, the City of Light, after months of traveling across Europe. As a freelance journalist, Sebastian was always on the move, chasing down leads and stories that would take him to the most unexpected places.

Sebastian Valja was no stranger to adventure, but this assignment was different. He had been hired by a prominent French magazine to investigate rumors of corruption within the city's elite. The more he learned about the story, the more Sebastian realized that his involvement could put him in grave danger.

As he made his way through the bustling streets, Sebastian felt a sense of excitement wash over him. He loved the anonymity of Paris, where strangers became friends and secrets were hidden behind every corner. His research led him to a quaint café on the outskirts of town, where he hoped to gather more information from an old acquaintance.

 Inside the cozy café, Sebastian spotted his contact, a charismatic woman named Colette. She sipped her coffee with an air of confidence that bordered on suspicion. "Sebastian Valja," she said, her voice husky. "I see you're still chasing the big story."

Just as Sebastian was about to leave, Colette spoke up, her voice barely above a whisper. "Meet me at the Eiffel Tower tonight," she said, before turning back to her coffee. With that, the conversation was over.
