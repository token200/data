Olli Ryota's mind was racing with questions. How did this woman get her hands on such incriminating information? What would happen if she published it? But one thing was certain – she had to tell the story.

As Olli made her way back to the office, her thoughts were a jumble of emotions. Excitement and trepidation wrestled for dominance in her chest. She knew that publishing this story would put her at odds with the powers that be, but she also knew it was her duty as a journalist.
