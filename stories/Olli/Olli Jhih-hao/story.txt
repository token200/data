Despite his success, Olli Jhih-hao remained humble and dedicated to using only the freshest ingredients in his cooking. He sourced his produce from local markets and suppliers, ensuring that every dish that left his kitchen was not only delicious but also sustainable.

Despite the increased attention, Olli Jhih-hao remained focused on his craft, always seeking ways to improve his recipes and techniques.

Through his hard work and dedication, Olli Jhih-hao had established himself as one of Taipei's top chefs.
