Olli Yang sat alone in the dimly lit café, sipping on a lukewarm cup of coffee as he stared out the window at the rain-soaked streets. Growing up as an expat, he struggled to find his place between two cultures, but it was this very dichotomy that fueled his creativity. His parents had encouraged his artistic talents from a young age, and Olli Yang had honed his skills in the vibrant streets of Tokyo.

She spotted Olli Yang and made her way over, introducing herself as Sophia Lee, a fellow artist at the Tokyo Art District.
