As Olli grew older, his love for art only deepened. He attended art school, where he honed his skills and developed a unique style that blended traditional techniques with modern flair. After completing his studies, Olli Liepiņš set out on his own as a freelance artist, taking on commissions from clients around the city.

Despite his success, Olli remained humble and dedicated to his craft. He spent hours each day honing his skills, experimenting with new mediums and techniques, always pushing himself to improve. He was both thrilled and intimidated by the opportunity, knowing that it would be a major milestone in his career. As he mingled with the crowd, Olli couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and accomplishment – everything he had worked for had finally come together in this moment.
