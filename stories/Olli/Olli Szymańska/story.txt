Born Olli Szymańska in the sprawling metropolis of Warsaw, Poland, she had always been drawn to the world of music. For years after, Olli struggled to come to terms with the loss of her partner and the trauma that had followed. She eventually found solace in her music once again, channeling her emotions into powerful performances at Warsaw's most underground clubs.
