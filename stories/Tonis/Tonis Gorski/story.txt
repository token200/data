As he worked late into the night, fueled by caffeine and curiosity, Tonis's mind was consumed by the mysteries of the cosmos. But as he looked back at the familiar walls of his laboratory, he couldn't help but feel a pang of sadness – for the simple life he had left behind, and for the solitude that made him who he was.
