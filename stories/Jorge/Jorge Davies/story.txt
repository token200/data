As he grew older, Jorge's love for art only deepened. He spent countless hours sketching and painting the street performers and vendors that lined the streets of Copacabana. It was here that he discovered his true calling – to become an artist himself.

Jorge Antonio Davies poured his heart and soul into his work, experimenting with different mediums and techniques until he found what worked best for him. His art quickly gained attention in the local art scene, with galleries and collectors clamoring for his unique perspective.
