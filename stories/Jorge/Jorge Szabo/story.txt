Jorge Szabo was a man with a passion for art and music. He spent most of his days painting vibrant murals on the walls of abandoned buildings in the city's underbelly. His bold brushstrokes and vivid colors seemed to pulse with life, drawing people from all walks of life to his latest masterpiece.

One day, while Jorge Szabo was working on a particularly intricate design, he noticed a young girl lingering around the corner of the building. She had big brown eyes and a mop of curly hair that framed her heart-shaped face. Jorge Szabo couldn't help but feel drawn to her, and as she watched him work, he found himself smiling at her like an idiot.

As the days passed, the girl returned several times, always watching Jorge Szabo with an air of curiosity. He began to save a few scraps of paint for her, adding tiny touches to his murals that seemed to wink at her whenever she came near. One afternoon, as he was finishing up his latest piece, the girl approached him hesitantly and asked if she could help.

Jorge Szabo chuckled and handed her a brush. Together, they worked on the final details of the mural, their hands touching occasionally as they chatted about everything from art to music.
