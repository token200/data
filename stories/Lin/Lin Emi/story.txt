After graduating, Lin Emi landed an internship at a prominent news organization, where she quickly proved herself to be a skilled and tenacious reporter. Her grandmother, in particular, was a source of inspiration for her – the elderly woman had taught Lin Emi the importance of perseverance and fairness in her younger years.

As Lin Emi's reputation continued to grow, so did her ambitions. She began to explore new areas of investigation, from environmental issues to social justice causes.
