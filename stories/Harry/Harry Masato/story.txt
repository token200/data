Years later, as Harry Masato, a successful freelance journalist, he still found himself drawn to the thrill of the challenge.

Harry Masato tackled each challenge with ease, using his years of practice and experience to guide him through the maze of clues and patterns. But Harry Masato was undaunted, using his sharp mind and quick thinking to unravel each puzzle with ease.
