Harry Zariņš had always been an enigma to those who knew him. As a young man with an otherworldly gaze and an uncanny ability to read people, he was often shunned by the very people he wanted most to connect with.

As an adult, Harry Zariņš traveled the world, using his abilities to help those in need. He became known only by his alias, "The Seer," and his services were sought after by kings and queens, politicians and underworld figures alike.

Despite his many successes, Harry couldn't shake the feeling of isolation that had always plagued him. People saw him as a freak, or at best, an entertaining curiosity. They didn't understand the depth of his emotions or the weight of his burdens.

One night, in a dingy bar in Berlin, Harry Zariņš sat sipping a whiskey, lost in thought. A young woman with piercing green eyes approached him, and for a moment, their gazes locked in a spark of mutual understanding.

For a moment, Harry was taken aback, unsure whether to trust this stranger or not. But something about her intensity, her spark of recognition, resonated with him.
