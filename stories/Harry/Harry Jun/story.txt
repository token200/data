Over the next few weeks, Harry Jun became increasingly obsessed with deciphering the book's secrets. He spent long hours pouring over ancient texts and practicing spells in front of a mirror. His room was soon filled with the whispers of forgotten incantations, and his eyes took on an otherworldly sheen.

Despite his isolation, Harry Jun felt more alive than he ever had before. He began to see the world as a vast, interconnected web of magic and wonder. And though some might have deemed him mad, Harry Jun knew that he was simply awakening to a new reality.
