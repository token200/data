Sophia was drawn to Nils Bierhals' quiet confidence and creative energy. As they spent more time together, she began to see the world through his eyes – full of wonder and possibility. In return, Nils Bierhals found himself inspired by Sophia's passion for art and her infectious enthusiasm.
