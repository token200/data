As he grew older, his love of poetry only deepened, and he began to see it as a way to express himself in a way that words alone could not.

Despite his passion for writing, Nils Petersen never shared his poetry with anyone outside of his family. He was afraid of being ridiculed or rejected, and so he kept his creative pursuits private.
