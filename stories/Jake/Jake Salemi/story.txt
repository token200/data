Jake Salemi had always been fascinated by the supernatural and the occult. As a young child, he would spend hours poring over dusty old books in his father's library, devouring tales of ghostly apparitions and demonic possession. His parents had often warned him to be careful what he read, but Jake couldn't get enough of the dark and mysterious world that seemed to exist just beyond the edges of everyday reality.

As he grew older, Jake's interests only deepened. He began to study the occult in earnest, pouring over ancient texts and seeking out like-minded individuals who shared his passions. His friends would often tease him about his fascination with the "supernatural nonsense," but Jake knew that there was something more to it all.

Despite his parents' concerns, Jake's love of the occult had served him well. He had become a skilled practitioner of various forms of magic and divination, and had even started to make a name for himself in certain... unsavory circles. People would come from all over to seek out Jake Salemi's expertise on matters of the spirit world.

But despite his success, Jake couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. He had been experiencing strange occurrences lately - doors opening by themselves, objects moving on their own, and an eerie sense of being watched. At first, he had written it off as mere paranoia, but the events had grown more frequent and more intense.

One night, as Jake sat in his dimly lit study, surrounded by shelves upon shelves of ancient tomes, he felt a chill run down his spine. He knew that he was being summoned, called forth from whatever realm lay beyond the veil of reality. And when the door creaked open, and a figure stepped into the room, Jake Salemi knew that his life would never be the same again.
