As he grew older, Jake Yun's skills and determination only seemed to improve, earning him a black belt in Hapkido at just 16 years old. He went on to study abroad, eventually settling in the United States where he began teaching his beloved martial art to anyone who was willing to learn.

Despite his many successes, Jake Yun remained humble and grounded, always putting the needs of others before his own. His students adored him, drawn to his calm and patient demeanor, and Jake took great pleasure in watching them grow and develop their skills.

At first, she was intimidated by Jake's imposing presence, but as he showed her the basics of the art, she began to relax and open up. It wasn't long before Sarah had found not only a new skill set, but also a new sense of purpose in life.
