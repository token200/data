Ryszard watched intently as his father received the tribute, noting the respect and admiration in their eyes for the mighty Caesar Augustus. And though he didn't yet know it, Ryszard Claudius Caesar Drusus Germanicus was taking his first steps towards a legacy that would surpass even those of his illustrious namesake.
