The elderly nobleman was struck by the Polish aristocrat's likeness to himself and took a liking to Ryszard's art. In London, Ryszard's talent was met with critical acclaim, but he struggled to adapt to the city's fast-paced lifestyle. As the years passed, Ryszard Lewis became a celebrated figure in the art world, known for his bold, innovative style.
