As he grew older, Iván's passion for art only intensified, and he went on to study fine art at some of the most prestigious schools in Europe. It was there that he honed his skills, experimenting with different mediums and techniques that showcased his unique perspective.

After completing his studies, Iván settled in Berlin, where he established himself as a respected painter. His work was met with critical acclaim, with collectors and museums clamoring to showcase his latest pieces.
