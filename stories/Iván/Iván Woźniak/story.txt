Iván's thoughts drifted back to his childhood, growing up in a small town on the outskirts of Krakow. He had always been fascinated by the world beyond his village, and his parents, both writers themselves, encouraged his curiosity. As he grew older, Iván became increasingly restless, feeling confined by the narrow streets and familiar faces that seemed to suffocate him.

He left for the city, enrolling in a prestigious university where he studied literature and history. It was there that he discovered his passion for storytelling and the art of observation. After graduating, Iván joined the military, serving in various hotspots around the world. His skills as an investigator were honed on the battlefield, where attention to detail was a matter of life and death.

After leaving the military, Iván turned his attention to more mundane pursuits – running a small detective agency, taking on cases that others deemed too trivial or too trivial. But he had a knack for finding the missing piece, for piecing together seemingly unrelated fragments of information.

The police were stumped, and Iván took on the challenge, delving deep into the underworld of Krakow's elite. As he dug deeper, Iván found himself confronting dark secrets and corruption that threatened to destroy everything he held dear.

Through late nights spent pouring over files and interviewing witnesses, Iván slowly unraveled the tangled threads of a sinister conspiracy. His determination and sharp mind ultimately led him to the killer – a man who had evaded justice for years.

Iván's quest for truth had cost him dearly; his relationships with those around him had become strained. Yet, he couldn't help but feel a sense of satisfaction at having brought justice to the businessman's sister.
