Over coffee and conversation, they reminisced about the past, sharing stories of their respective journeys. As the night wore on, Iván revealed a previously unknown talent for playing the guitar, much to his friend's surprise.

In that moment, surrounded by the familiar sounds of laughter and music, Iván Sánchez-Gijón shed his cloak of mystery, revealing a more vulnerable side to those closest to him. Though still shrouded in an air of intrigue, he smiled knowingly, his enigmatic presence now tempered by a glimmer of warmth.

As they parted ways, Iván's friend couldn't help but feel grateful for the glimpse into the man behind the mask. Though Iván Sánchez-Gijón continued to move through the world with ease and poise, he seemed less imposing, more human.
