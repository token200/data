Iván chuckled and replied, "Just passing through town, enjoying the coffee and the atmosphere." The barista couldn't shake off the feeling that there was more to Iván's story than he was letting on. She made a mental note to ask him more questions next time.

As Iván sipped his cappuccino, he noticed an elderly woman sitting alone at a nearby table. Her eyes seemed lost in thought, and Iván felt an inexplicable urge to approach her. He introduced himself and struck up a conversation, effortlessly drawing her out of her melancholy mood.

The two talked for hours, sharing stories and laughter. The barista watched from afar, intrigued by the connection between Iván Brown and the woman. She wondered if there was truth to the rumors about his past or if he was simply a talented conversationalist.

As the sun began to set, Iván stood up to leave, leaving behind a sense of wonder in those who had met him. Though his true nature remained unknown, one thing was clear: Iván Brown had an extraordinary gift for connecting with others.
