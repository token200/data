Eszter's bravery and tenacity eventually paid off, as she broke the story of the corruption scandal on national television. The fallout was swift and severe, with several high-ranking officials forced to resign or face prosecution.

In the aftermath of the exposé, Eszter Grabowski became a celebrated figure in Hungarian media, hailed as a champion of truth and integrity.
