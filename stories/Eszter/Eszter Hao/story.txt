Her lens danced across skyscrapers and alleys, snapping portraits of street performers, food stalls, and anonymous faces. Eszter Hao's love affair with photography began when she stumbled upon an old camera in her grandfather's attic. Her photographs began to take on a life of their own, imbued with a sense of wonder, mystery, and longing.
