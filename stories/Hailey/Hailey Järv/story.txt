Hailey Järv was no ordinary 25-year-old artist. With her vibrant pink hair and infectious smile, she could light up any room with her presence. Hailey Järv had always been drawn to the city, with its energy and diversity. Growing up in a small town, she felt stifled by the lack of opportunities and creativity.
