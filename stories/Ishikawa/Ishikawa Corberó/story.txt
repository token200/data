Born Ishikawa Corberó, to a family of nomadic performers and musicians, Corberó had grown up surrounded by the rhythms and melodies of the open road. She had inherited her parents' love of music and storytelling, but also their penchant for mischief and mayhem.

As she grew older, Corberó began to use her talents to fuel her desire for freedom.
