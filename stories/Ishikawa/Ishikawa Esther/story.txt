As she sang softly to herself, Ishikawa Esther's thoughts wandered to her childhood dreams. Growing up, she had always been fascinated by the stories of J-Pop legends who rose from humble beginnings to become household names. Her own journey, though not without its challenges, was one she was determined to pursue with unwavering passion.

Ishikawa Esther's heart skipped a beat as she took in the information. This was her chance to break into the music industry, to prove to herself and others that her unique voice and style were worth noticing.

As the sun dipped below the Tokyo skyscrapers, she dressed in a flowing white dress, her hair styled perfectly, and stepped out into the night air. With a deep breath, she walked towards Rakuten Music Studios, ready to seize this opportunity and make her dreams come true.

As she entered the sleek, modern building, Ishikawa Esther felt an electric sense of determination coursing through her veins. She was one step closer to realizing her vision as a singer-songwriter, and nothing was going to hold her back from making it happen.

With poise and confidence, Ishikawa Esther presented herself to Taro Yamada, ready to showcase the talents she had been nurturing for so long. The future was full of promise, and this moment marked just the beginning of an incredible journey for this talented young singer-songwriter named Ishikawa Esther.
