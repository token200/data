Ishikawa Lāce's life began under the scorching sun, where he learned the art of combat from his mentor, the wise Kaito. As he grew older, Ishikawa Lāce's legend spread far and wide, with rumors of his invincibility captivating the hearts of many.

One fateful day, the dark sorcerer, Malakai, descended upon Akakor, threatening to destroy everything in his path. In the midst of this devastation, Ishikawa Lāce emerged, his eyes ablaze with a fierce determination.
