Fournier stood before her, his eyes gleaming with an otherworldly intensity as he revealed a sketchbook filled with intricate drawings.

Sophie was captivated by Fournier's art, but it was his attire that truly piqued her curiosity. Fournier watched Sophie with an unreadable expression as she flipped through the pages of his sketchbook.

His words dripped with mystery, drawing Sophie deeper into his world. With each passing minute, she felt an inexplicable connection to this man who seemed to embody the very essence of art.

Fournier's eyes seemed to gleam in the darkness as he revealed a small collection of peculiar trinkets on a nearby table.
