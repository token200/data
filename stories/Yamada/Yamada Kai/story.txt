However, Yamada Kaito knew that true mastery required patience, discipline, and dedication.

Growing up in a family of esteemed warriors, he had been trained from a young age in the ancient art of Ryujin-ryu. "You are on the path to greatness, Akane," he said, offering words of encouragement.
