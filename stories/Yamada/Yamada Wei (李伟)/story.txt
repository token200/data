One evening, while working late on a story, Yamada Wei received an unexpected phone call from her editor. Mr. Tanaka only agreed to speak with someone who could prove herself worthy of his trust.

Yamada Wei was intrigued by the challenge and immediately accepted the assignment. She spent hours researching Mr. Tanaka's empire and preparing a list of thoughtful questions to ask him. As she arrived at the luxurious office building where the interview would take place, she couldn't help but feel a mix of excitement and nerves.

Throughout the conversation, Yamada Wei skillfully probed Mr. Tanaka about his business dealings, his views on politics, and his personal life. He spoke candidly, sharing insights into the world of high finance that few others had access to. As the interview drew to a close, Yamada Wei knew she had secured an incredible scoop.

As she left the office building, Yamada Wei felt a sense of pride and accomplishment wash over her. She had proven herself worthy of Mr. Tanaka's trust, and in doing so, had secured one of the most coveted interviews of her career. Her editor would be thrilled when he saw the exclusive story that Yamada Wei had uncovered.

As she made her way back to her office, Yamada Wei couldn't help but think about how far she had come since graduating from university with a degree in journalism. Li Wei, as she was known to those close to her, had always been driven to succeed, and now, it seemed like the world was hers for the taking.

Her name became synonymous with hard-hitting reporting, and her colleagues looked up to her as a role model. And though she still went by the name Yamada Wei, few people knew that beneath the surface lay the sharp mind and quick wit of Li Wei.
