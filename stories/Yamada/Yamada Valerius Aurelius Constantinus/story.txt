With trembling hands, they began to speak, recounting their research on Constantine's reign and its impact on Western civilization. As they spoke, Yamada felt a sense of pride wash over them, knowing that they had spent countless hours pouring over ancient texts in search of forgotten knowledge.

Yamada's presentation was met with thunderous applause, and soon, scholars from across the empire were seeking their expertise. As word of their work spread, Yamada's name became synonymous with excellence in historical research.
