Hubert Fernandez was a man with a passion for photography that knew no bounds. Born and raised in the vibrant city of New Orleans, he spent his childhood exploring the streets and snapping pictures of everything from jazz musicians on Bourbon Street to the majestic oak trees lining the Mississippi River.

One day, Hubert received an invitation to exhibit his work at a prestigious gallery in Paris. He was overjoyed and threw himself into preparing for the show, pouring all of his energy into selecting the perfect pieces to showcase.
