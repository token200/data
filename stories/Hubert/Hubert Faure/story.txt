But despite his vast knowledge and deep connections within the art world, Hubert Faure remained an enigmatic figure, always a step behind the spotlight. As Sophie grew older and became one of the leading figures in French modernism, she would often seek out her mentor's counsel, and turn to him when seeking guidance on matters of art and life. Her gratitude to Hubert Faure was evident in every painting that left her studio, a testament to the lasting impact his guidance had made on her career.
