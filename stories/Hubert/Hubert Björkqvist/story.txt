Hubert Björkqvist felt a sense of destiny wash over him as he accepted the challenge.

He practiced tirelessly, honing his skills and experimenting with new sounds. As the days passed, Hubert Björkqvist found himself becoming one with his music, as if the notes themselves had become an extension of his very being.
