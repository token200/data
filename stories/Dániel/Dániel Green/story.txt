Despite the whispers, few could deny the charisma that surrounded Dániel like a cloak of protection. He moved with a quiet confidence, his long strides eating up the distance as he navigated the bustling streets of the city. His words were laced with a dry humor, a hint of warmth beneath the surface that left those who met him feeling... seen.

He was drawn to a small café on the corner, its sign creaking in the gentle breeze like a whispered secret. Inside, the aroma of freshly brewed coffee enveloped him, drawing him into a warm cocoon of comfort.

Over steaming cups, Dániel shared fragments of his story with Luna, who listened with rapt attention. She saw the cracks in his armor, the hint of vulnerability that lay beneath his imposing exterior. As they spoke, a fragile connection formed between them, a bond forged from shared secrets and unspoken understanding.
