She had never been to this place before, but a letter from her estranged grandmother had brought her here.

As she made her way through the deserted station, Olivia felt a sense of unease wash over her. Her grandmother, Elsa, had been estranged for years, and Olivia had always wondered what had driven them apart. The letter, though brief, hinted at a long-held secret that Elsa was desperate to share with her.

Olivia's thoughts were interrupted by the sound of footsteps behind her. She turned to see an older woman with kind eyes and a gentle smile. "Welcome to Raven's Peak," the woman said, extending a hand. "I'm Agnes, the station mistress."
