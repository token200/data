Despite her parents' initial reservations, Martins pursued her passion with unwavering determination.

One evening, while performing at the Stockholm Concert Hall, Martins received an offer from a renowned film composer to score the soundtrack for his upcoming movie. Elated by the opportunity, Martins threw herself into the project, pouring her heart and soul into the composition.

As the film's release date approached, Martins couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and accomplishment. Her music had captured the hearts of audiences worldwide, and she knew that this moment was just the beginning of an exciting new chapter in her career.

When the movie premiered at the Berlin Film Festival, Martins Alimbekova took to the stage to accept her award for Best Original Score.
