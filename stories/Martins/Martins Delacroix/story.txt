Martins Delacroix's ears perked up at the mention of his name being mentioned alongside those of other collectors and thieves. This could be the break he'd been waiting for – or was it?

Would he find the rare book dealer, or would he continue to vanish into the shadows? Only time would tell. But one thing was certain: Martins Delacroix was always ready for his next adventure, no matter where it might lead him.
