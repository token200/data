He had spent years studying the ancient texts and scouring the local legends for any hint of its whereabouts. Finally, after weeks of searching, he stumbled upon a hidden entrance, cleverly concealed behind a cascading waterfall.

With a thrill of excitement, Martins Harris realized that he had stumbled upon something much bigger than he had initially thought.

Martins Harris's eyes widened as he gazed upon the spoils of Zenith, his heart racing with excitement.
