As she grew older, her passion for astronomy only intensified, leading her to pursue a career in research.

Whatever the source, Nele's curiosity and drive had taken her far – to some of the most prestigious universities in the world.

One evening, as Nele was leaving her lab, she noticed a young girl sitting by the window, gazing up at the stars. The girl's face lit up with excitement, and Nele found herself opening up, sharing her passion for the stars and the mysteries they held. As they talked, Nele realized that she had come full circle – from stargazing as a child to inspiring others to do the same.

For Nele Leilani Yang, it was moments like these that made all the hard work worth it. She knew that she had found her calling, and that the universe held secrets waiting to be uncovered by curious minds like hers.
