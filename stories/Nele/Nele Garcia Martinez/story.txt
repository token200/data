Elated, Nele poured all her energy into preparing for the event, spending countless hours perfecting each piece and carefully curating the overall aesthetic of her display.

Finally, the night of the exhibition arrived, and Nele's studio was transformed into a vibrant haven filled with color and creativity. The crowd was abuzz as art lovers and critics alike gathered to admire her work. Nele García Martínez watched from the sidelines, feeling both proud and nervous as strangers approached her pieces with curiosity.

As the evening wore on, Nele began to connect with viewers who were drawn to her raw emotion and unbridled passion.
