One evening, while reviewing a string of burglaries, Nele Garcia Fernandez stumbled upon a peculiar detail. The thief seemed to be targeting only art galleries, leaving behind an intricate symbol etched into the wall near each scene of the crime. Intrigued, she decided to pay a visit to one of the latest victims.

Upon arrival, Nele Garcia Fernandez observed the gallery owner, Mrs. Patel, frantically trying to secure her prized possession – a priceless painting by a renowned artist. As Nele approached, she noticed an unusual glow emanating from behind a nearby tapestry. She gently pulled it aside, revealing a small compartment containing the exact same symbol left at previous crime scenes.

Nele Garcia Fernandez's mind began racing with possibilities. Was this a series of unrelated break-ins, or was there something more sinister at play? Determined to unravel the mystery, she spent the next few days studying the symbol and interviewing witnesses. Her tireless efforts eventually led her to an unexpected suspect – none other than the city's most esteemed art collector.

Confronting the collector in his opulent mansion, Nele Garcia Fernandez discovered that he had been using the burglaries to fund his own secret passion project: a revolutionary art piece that would redefine the art world. As the news broke of the art collector's arrest, Nele Garcia Fernandez couldn't help but feel a sense of satisfaction.
