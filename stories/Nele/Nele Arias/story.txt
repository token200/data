As Nele Arias pushed through the dense foliage, her eyes scanned the ground for any sign of the fabled metropolis. She had spent years studying the legends and myths of the region, and was convinced that the key to unlocking its secrets lay hidden in the ruins of a long-abandoned temple.

As Nele Arias made her way back through the temple, her mind was racing with the implications of her find.
