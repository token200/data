The closer Kazimierz got to the truth, the more elusive it seemed to become.

Despite the obstacles, Kazimierz persevered, fueled by his unwavering determination to uncover justice. His reputation as a brilliant detective continued to grow, and with each new breakthrough, he was one step closer to bringing the serial killer to light.

As the sun set over the city, casting long shadows across the streets, Kazimierz sat in his office, pouring over evidence and making connections that others had missed. He knew that the key to solving the case lay hidden in plain sight, but he needed to be patient and stay focused.

Kazimierz's eyes narrowed as he stared at a small photograph on his desk – a shot of the serial killer taken from a surveillance camera. Something about it didn't sit right with him.
