A string of high-end jewelry store robberies had the police stumped, with no leads or suspects to speak of. As he delved deeper into the files, Kazimierz Ozolina's mind began to spin with theories and connections. He was known for his razor-sharp instincts, but even he couldn't shake off the feeling that this case was something more. Something personal.
