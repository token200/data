Kazimierz Bērziņš had been a leading tenor in Riga Opera House for over two decades, known for his powerful voice and emotive stage presence. But tonight was different – tonight marked his final performance before retirement.

He had given countless performances in this very house, bringing characters to life with his voice and passion. Kazimierz Bērziņš smiled warmly, his eyes shining with gratitude. "I'll always be here for this house, even if I'm not on stage," he promised.
