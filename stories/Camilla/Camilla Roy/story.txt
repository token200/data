As the night wore on, Camilla Elizabeth Roy knew she had stumbled upon a story that could make her career – or get her killed. She made a mental note to dig deeper, to follow every lead and verify every claim. The thrill of the chase was what drove her, and she wouldn't rest until she uncovered the truth.
