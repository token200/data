Over coffee and conversation, Camilla discovered that Victor was more than just a rival - he was a kindred spirit who shared her passions and values. And as they parted ways at the end of the day, Camilla couldn't shake the feeling that she had found someone special in Victor Lee.

From that moment on, Camilla López's relationships with others began to change. She started taking risks and trusting people again, all thanks to Victor's influence.
