As she grew older, Camilla's talent became impossible to ignore. She won local competitions, earned scholarships, and eventually landed a coveted spot in the Bolshoi Ballet.

Despite the whispers and speculation surrounding her, Camilla remained aloof, a mystery even to those closest to her.
