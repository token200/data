His trained eyes scanned the room, taking note of every conversation, every gesture, and every facial expression. As he made his escape into the night, Roberts Ming couldn't help but feel a sense of satisfaction. His mission had been a success, and he knew that the intel he gathered would be invaluable to his clients.
