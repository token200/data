Roberts Charpentier listened attentively, his eyes sparkling with amusement as he wove the threads of each story into a tapestry of wonder and awe. And when he finally spoke, his voice was like a low thunderclap on a summer's day – full of depth and mystery, and capable of transporting even the most hardened listener to a world beyond their wildest dreams.
