One evening, as Roberts sat on the beach watching the stars, he couldn't help but think about how far he had come since those early days studying the ocean's depths. The discovery of Nilssonius luminaris was just one of many milestones in his illustrious career, and yet it still brought him a sense of wonder and awe.
