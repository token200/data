However, Molly's dedication only intensified her abilities, and soon she was capable of communicating with spirits and sensing hidden energies.

Molly Nachtnebel's reputation as a mystic spread far and wide, attracting those seeking guidance or protection from her unique gifts. Some feared her powers, while others revered her for her wisdom and compassion. Regardless, Molly remained enigmatic, never revealing too much about herself or her motivations.
