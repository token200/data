The trauma had left emotional scars, but it only strengthened her resolve to keep fighting for those who couldn't fight for themselves.

After retiring from active duty, Molly took on a new role as a private investigator, using her skills to help those in need. Her reputation as a fierce and unrelenting detective spread quickly, earning her the respect of both clients and adversaries alike.

Molly listened intently as Sarah recounted the events leading up to her disappearance. She took notes, her mind racing with possibilities. As they delved deeper into the case, Molly began to piece together a complex web of secrets and lies.

As the days passed, Molly became increasingly obsessed with finding Sarah's sister. She spent long hours poring over evidence, talking to witnesses, and scouring the city for any sign of her missing person. Her determination was unwavering, her spirit unbroken.
