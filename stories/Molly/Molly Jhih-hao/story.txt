One sunny afternoon, Molly decided to take a break from writing and volunteer at the local animal shelter. As she worked on his adoptable profile, she couldn't help but think about how much joy he would bring to someone's life. The shelter staff told her that Max had been waiting for a forever home for months, and Molly knew she had to do everything she could to make him irresistible.

As the sun began to set, Molly Jhih-hao left the shelter with a newfound sense of purpose. Her posts quickly went viral, and soon she had a flurry of inquiries from people eager to meet Max.

As Molly packed up her things and said goodbye to the shelter staff, she felt a sense of satisfaction wash over her.
