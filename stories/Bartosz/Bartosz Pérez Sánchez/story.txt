Bartosz's big break came when his work caught the attention of a prominent art gallery in Berlin. His piece, "Echoes in Code," was met with critical acclaim, with many praising its innovative use of light and shadow. Word of Bartosz Pérez Sánchez's talent spread quickly, earning him commissions from clients around the world.

Despite his success, Bartosz remained committed to pushing the boundaries of his art.
