One day, while exploring an abandoned warehouse, Bartosz stumbled upon an old bicycle frame covered in rust and cobwebs. Inspired by its worn, utilitarian beauty, he set about transforming it into a stunning sculpture that would become one of his most celebrated pieces, "Ephemeral Cycle."
