Instead, he found solace in the world of art, particularly photography. With a keen eye and an intuitive sense of composition, Bartosz Põld's photographs were like windows into another world – a world that was both beautiful and unsettling. Bartosz smiled to himself as he reviewed his work. He had captured something special, something that spoke to the human condition.
