One night, Bartosz King set his sights on the most coveted prize of all: a priceless diamond necklace owned by a wealthy socialite. The plan was intricate, involving a fake charity gala and a complex system of hidden passages and secret doors.

The heist went off without a hitch, with Bartosz King slipping in and out of the mansion like a phantom. When the dust settled, he vanished into the night, leaving behind only whispers of his presence.
