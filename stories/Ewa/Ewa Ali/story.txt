After completing high school, Ewa decided to pursue a degree in fine arts at the University of Ghana. She spent her days studying painting and sculpture, but her true passion lay in photography. Back home, Ewa was hailed as a hero and a role model for young women in Ghana. She was invited to speak at conferences and give workshops on photography and storytelling. Her full name, Ewa Ali, became synonymous with excellence and creativity in the art world.
