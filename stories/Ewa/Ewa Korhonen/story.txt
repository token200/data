After graduating, she began working as a freelance photographer, taking assignments from local magazines and newspapers.

Ewa was fascinated by the concept and saw it as a perfect fit for her own photography style.

She had always known that she was different, that her passion for photography set her apart from others. Now, she saw that it was not just about taking pictures, but about telling stories and capturing moments in time.
