Fontaine listened intently, his expression unreadable, as they poured out their frustrations and worries. The acquaintance hesitated, sensing that something was amiss, but ultimately accepted.

As Fontaine departed, leaving behind a trail of unanswered questions and unspoken doubts, one couldn't help but wonder what secrets lay hidden beneath his polished facade. Was he truly a generous benefactor, or merely using this opportunity to further his own interests? Only time would tell, but for now, the enigmatic Gilles François Marie Fontaine remained an enigma, shrouded in mystery and intrigue.

Despite his mysterious nature, Fontaine's reputation continued to grow, as whispers of his extraordinary business acumen spread far and wide.
