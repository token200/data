Gilles Étienne Thomas carefully unwrapped the package, revealing a intricately carved wooden box adorned with strange symbols. His curiosity piqued, he carefully opened the lid, and a faint scent of lavender wafted out. Intrigued, Gilles Étienne Thomas decided to investigate further. He arrived at the mansion, where he discovered a small inscription etched into the entrance: "Only those who speak the language of shadows can unlock this door." Gilles Étienne Thomas inserted the small key provided by Madame Dupont, turned it, and the door creaked open. Inside, he found a treasure trove of artifacts, each one hinting at an ancient mystery.

Gilles Étienne Thomas felt an unexpected pang of compassion for this enigmatic figure.
