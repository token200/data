As the enigmatic leader of a clandestine organization known only as "The Umbra Collective," Gilles commanded respect without ever having to demand it.

Whatever the truth may have been, one thing was certain: Gilles Angel Silvestre was a force to be reckoned with.

His organization's goals were shrouded in secrecy, but those who had crossed paths with Gilles knew that they were playing with fire. And yet, despite the danger that lurked beneath his polished exterior, there was an undeniable charm to Gilles that drew people in.
