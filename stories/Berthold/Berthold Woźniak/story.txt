Eager to learn the art of clockmaking, Stanislaw made his way through Krakow's hidden streets until he arrived at a nondescript door adorned with the symbol of a snarling lion.

As the young apprentice pushed open the creaking door, he found himself in a dimly lit chamber filled with an array of mesmerizing timepieces. Berthold Woźniak sat amidst this whirlwind of gears and springs, his eyes aglow like lanterns on a stormy night.
