He revealed to Berthold that he too possessed a hidden talent - the ability to create vibrant, three-dimensional sculptures using only his mind. As Berthold listened in awe, Professor Fontana began to demonstrate his extraordinary skill.

Berthold was awestruck by the professor's abilities and begged him to teach him how to create such masterpieces. And so, under the mentorship of Professor Fontana, Berthold Bocelli discovered a new dimension of creativity and artistic expression. As he honed his skills, Berthold's art gained international recognition, earning him a place alongside the world's most celebrated artists.
