His wild grey hair was tied back in a messy bun, revealing a pair of wire-rimmed spectacles perched on the end of his nose. He spent most of his youth tinkering with gadgets and machinery, earning him both praise and ridicule from his peers. His parents, fearing their son's obsessive tendencies would ruin his life, had encouraged him to pursue more traditional pursuits.

However, Berthold Bach was not one to be swayed. He continued to build and experiment, driven by an insatiable curiosity about the fundamental laws of physics. Over time, his creations became increasingly complex and innovative, earning him recognition among the scientific community.

Berthold Bach's eyes lit up behind his spectacles as he nodded enthusiastically. Together, they pored over the plans, making notes and adjustments in a frenzy of creativity.

As they worked, Berthold couldn't help but feel a sense of pride in his life's work. His reclusive nature belied a sharp intellect and a drive to push the boundaries of human understanding. Berthold Bach nodded, his eyes still shining with excitement. He knew that he and Sophia were on the verge of something truly remarkable – and he couldn't wait to see where their collaboration would take them next.
