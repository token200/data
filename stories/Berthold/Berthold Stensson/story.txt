From delicate orchids to robust sunflowers, Berthold's green thumb brought life to every corner of the shop.

Berthold Stensson greeted them with a warm smile and began asking questions about their style and preferences. As the day drew to a close, Berthold Stensson reflected on his work with pride. It was moments like these that made all the hard work worth it – seeing the joy he brought to others through his love of gardening.
