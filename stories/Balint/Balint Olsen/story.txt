Balint Olsen chuckled, feeling grateful for his friend's presence in his life. As he finished his coffee, he pulled out his notebook and began to jot down some notes for his story. The words flowed easily onto the page, inspired by the sights and sounds of the city outside.

With renewed focus, Balint set off into the bustling streets, camera slung over his shoulder, ready to capture the essence of Copenhagen's ever-changing landscape.
