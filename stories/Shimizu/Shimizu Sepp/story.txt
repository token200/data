As Shimizu Sepp, she had gained a reputation for her striking murals, which often featured vibrant colors and dynamic patterns. Her work was inspired by traditional Japanese motifs, blended with modern influences from around the world.

Shimizu Sepp was both thrilled and intimidated by the offer. She had always dreamed of showcasing her work in such prestigious settings, but this would be her first major commissioned piece.
