As Shimizu Emi continued to prove herself as a talented journalist, she began to receive attention from higher-ups in the newspaper industry. She was soon offered a promotion to lead her own investigative team, where she would tackle some of the toughest stories in the city.

Emi's success did not go unnoticed by her colleagues or the public. She was hailed as a hero and a role model for young journalists everywhere.
