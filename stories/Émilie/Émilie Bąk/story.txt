It was there that Émilie met her mentor, a renowned photographer who saw something special in her work. He took her under his wing, offering guidance and support as she navigated the challenges of fine art photography. Under his tutelage, Émilie's skills continued to flourish, and she began to make a name for herself within the Polish art scene.
