Others claimed he was a master thief, always staying one step ahead of the law. But Arseniy Gabriel knew the truth: that his life had been a complex web of lies and half-truths, woven together to conceal the darkest corners of his own heart.

He slipped into the booth beside her, his movements fluid as a snake through the grass. "Mind if I join you?" he asked, his voice low and gravelly, like sandpaper on stone.

The woman shook her head, her eyes never leaving his. Arseniy Gabriel smiled – a small, wry smile that hinted at secrets and scars beneath.

"I am Arseniy Gabriel," he said, the words dropping like a bombshell in the stillness. "And you are...?"

As the rain pattered on outside, Arseniy Gabriel leaned in closer, his breath hot against Sophia's ear. "I've been searching for someone like you, Sophia," he whispered. "Someone with scars to match my own."
