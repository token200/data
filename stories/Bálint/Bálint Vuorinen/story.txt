Bálint's reputation as a skilled operative had preceded him, and soon, high-net-worth individuals and corporations began seeking his expertise. He worked tirelessly, taking on missions from around the world, always staying one step ahead of potential threats.

As he finished his drink, Bálint glanced at his watch, noting that his next engagement was mere hours away. With a quiet confidence, he stood up, adjusted his black leather jacket, and walked out into the night, ready to face whatever challenges lay ahead for Bálint Vuorinen.
