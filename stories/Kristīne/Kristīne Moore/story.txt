As the night wore on, Alex asked Kristīne about her past. She hesitated, unsure how much to reveal. But there was something about Alex that put her at ease, and she found herself opening up to him. "I'm Kristīne Moore," she said, a small smile playing on her lips.

Maybe this tiny town was exactly what she needed – and maybe, just maybe, it would lead her to something more than just a place to stay.
