Valeria Roy spent hours pouring over each artifact, taking meticulous notes and photographs. She knew that her discovery would be met with skepticism by some, but she was convinced that this was a momentous occasion. For Valeria Roy, there was no greater thrill than uncovering the secrets of the past, and she felt privileged to have been given this gift.
