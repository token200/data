Valeria Yan's eyes scanned the crowded marketplace, searching for her contact - a wealthy collector of rare artifacts. She spotted him across the room, sipping a cup of exotic tea as he eyed the passersby with disdain.

Valeria Yan smiled wryly. She had grown accustomed to the patronizing tone of her wealthy benefactor, but she knew it was necessary if she wanted to fund her research.

Valeria Yan smiled wryly, knowing that she had just taken the first step on a journey that would change the course of human understanding forever.
