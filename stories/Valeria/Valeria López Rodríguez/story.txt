As Valeria grew older, her love for art only intensified. She began to study fine arts at university, where she honed her skills and developed a unique style that blended traditional techniques with modern elements. It was during this time that she discovered her true passion: mixed media collage.

Her passion for art remained a constant source of inspiration, driving her to push boundaries and explore new techniques.
