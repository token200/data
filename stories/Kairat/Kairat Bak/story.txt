Seeking a way out, Kairat Bak turned to a life of freelance work, using his unique set of skills to help those in need.

Desperate for answers, the father had hired Kairat Bak to track her down and bring her home safely. Kairat Bak accepted the challenge, knowing it would be his most difficult mission yet.

Kairat Bak finally uncovered the location where the girl was being held, a remote warehouse on the outskirts of the city. With caution, he entered the building, taking out guards and navigating through the maze of crates and boxes. At last, he found his client, safe but shaken.

With the girl by his side, Kairat Bak made his way back to the father, who was overjoyed at her return. The entrepreneur thanked Kairat Bak profusely, offering him a substantial sum of money for his services. Kairat Bak declined, citing that he had done it out of principle, not profit.
