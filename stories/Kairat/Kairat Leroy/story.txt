With his custom-made lockpicks and expertise in disguise, he quickly bypassed the security guards and made his way to the display case where the Starlight Serenade was on loan. The necklace sparkled like a thousand stars as Kairat Leroy carefully removed it from its pedestal.

But Kairat Leroy had anticipated this too, and he quickly distracted them with a well-placed smoke bomb.
