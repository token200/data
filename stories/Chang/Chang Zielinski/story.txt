The message was cryptic, but the promise of a lucrative opportunity was too enticing to resist. Chang Zielinski accepted, and soon found himself standing in front of a sleek, black penthouse apartment building.

When he finally emerged from the facility, Chang Zielinski felt a sense of satisfaction wash over him. He had done it again, outwitting those who would seek to stop him.
