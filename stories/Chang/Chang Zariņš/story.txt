Chang was overjoyed at the opportunity to showcase her work on a larger stage. She spent weeks preparing for the show, carefully selecting each piece to ensure that it would be representative of her artistic vision. As the opening night approached, Chang Zariņš felt a mix of excitement and nerves, wondering if she would be able to meet the collector's expectations.

Chang Zariņš's work was met with rave reviews, with many attendees marveling at her innovative style and attention to detail. As Chang Zariņš basked in the glow of her success, she couldn't help but think about her roots and the cultural heritage that had inspired her art.
