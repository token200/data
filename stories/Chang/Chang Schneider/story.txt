Chang Schneider was a quiet, reserved individual who kept to himself, often going unnoticed by those around him. But despite his introverted nature, Chang had a rich inner life and a deep love for art.

His parents, both artists themselves, encouraged his creativity and provided him with endless supplies of paper, pencils, and paints. As he grew older, Chang's passion for art only intensified, and he began to study fine arts in college.
