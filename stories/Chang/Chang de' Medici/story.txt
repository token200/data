The resulting battle was fierce but ultimately won by Chang de' Medici Bianchi's cunning and bravery. The rival family retreated in disarray, their plans foiled by the Medici's relentless pursuit of power. As she stood victorious in the heart of Florence, Chang de' Medici Bianchi smiled, knowing that her family's fortunes would continue to soar under her watchful eye.
