The smell of freshly baked bread wafted from his shop, enticing the locals to come and sample his delicious creations. Despite his tough exterior, Nikodem had a soft spot for children and would often give away free pastries to the local school kids.

Amira's presence brought out a softer side of Nikodem Björkqvist, one that he had long hidden behind his tough exterior.

As the weeks passed, Åhus became a haven for the refugees, thanks in large part to Nikodem's kindness and generosity.
