As Nikodem delved deeper into his research, he became particularly interested in the forgotten kingdoms of Iberia, long-lost civilizations that had once thrived on the Iberian Peninsula. Nikodem's latest book was the culmination of his life's work, a sweeping narrative that brought to life the lives and cultures of these forgotten kingdoms. His writing was engaging and accessible, making complex historical concepts easy for readers to understand.
