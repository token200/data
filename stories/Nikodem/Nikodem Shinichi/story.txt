As Nikodem Shinichi walked through the quiet streets, he couldn't help but feel a sense of unease. The locals knew him by his alias, "The Ghost," but few knew his true name. He preferred it that way - a shield against those who might seek to do him harm.

Nikodem Shinichi watched from afar as the woman met with various townsfolk, gathering intel on his past. He knew he had to be careful - if she discovered his true identity, it could mean disaster. The gang would stop at nothing to silence him once again.
