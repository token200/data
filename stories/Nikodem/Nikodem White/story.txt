But despite his tough exterior, Nikodem had a soft spot for those in need. He took on cases that others deemed too difficult or impossible to solve, often putting his own life at risk in the process. And yet, every time he succeeded, the sense of satisfaction and pride was overwhelming.

Nikodem White was hired by her distraught family to find out what had happened to their beloved daughter. It seemed that Emily's disappearance was just the tip of the iceberg, and Nikodem White was about to find himself entangled in a far more sinister plot than he had ever imagined.

With his sharp mind and quick thinking, Nikodem navigated the treacherous underworld with ease, always staying one step ahead of those who sought to silence him. As he closed in on the truth, he couldn't help but wonder what other secrets lay hidden beneath the surface, waiting to be uncovered by this masterful detective.
