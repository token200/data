Nikodem Bauer's interest was piqued; he made a mental note to make contact with this enigmatic beauty later.

Nikodem Bauer's thoughts turned to his latest score, a daring heist that had earned him both admiration and ire from the underworld. Few knew the truth behind the whispers of "The Fox" – that it was, in fact, Nikodom Bauer.
