Tõnis Ozola was an Estonian poet and songwriter, born in 1934. His life's work was marked by a deep love for the land and its people. In the 1960s and 1970s, Tõnis began to gain recognition for his poetry. His collections of verse were widely read and admired, and he became a respected figure in Estonian literary circles. However, it was not until the late 1980s that his work gained widespread attention beyond Estonia's borders.

That was when Tõnis Ozola published his collection "Aegade lugu" ("The Story of the Ages"), which told the story of Estonia's history from the ancient times to the present day.
