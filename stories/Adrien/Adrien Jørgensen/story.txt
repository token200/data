Adrien's curiosity was piqued, and he arrived at the warehouse with his camera and notebook ready. As he stepped inside, he was met with an eerie silence. Suddenly, a figure emerged from the shadows – it was Adrien Jørgensen himself.

The warehouse held a cache of documents and recordings that Astrid had gathered over months, revealing the politician's web of corruption.

Adrien was both relieved and shaken by the revelation. He realized that his sister had been working tirelessly behind the scenes to uncover the truth. As he began to sift through the evidence, Adrien couldn't help but wonder how many more secrets lay hidden beneath the surface of this seemingly ordinary city.

With a newfound sense of purpose, Adrien set out to expose the politician's wrongdoings to the world. Astrid watched from afar as her brother worked his magic, knowing that her actions had set him on this path. The twins' bond was stronger than ever, forged by their shared determination to seek justice and uncover the truth.
