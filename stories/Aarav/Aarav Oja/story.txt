Aarav Oja stepped out of the dimly lit alleyway and onto the bustling streets of Mumbai. As a struggling artist, he had grown accustomed to the city's chaotic energy, but tonight felt different. His paintings were a reflection of his restless spirit, often incorporating bold colors and abstract shapes that seemed to leap off the canvas. Tonight, he had decided to showcase his latest piece, "Ephemeral Dreams," at an underground gallery in the city's trendy arts district.

Then, in a burst of light, "Ephemeral Dreams" sprang to life, its colors dancing across the walls like a kaleidoscope.
