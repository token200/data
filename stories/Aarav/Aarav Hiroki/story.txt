After completing his degree in journalism, Aarav Hiroki landed an internship at the prestigious Tokyo Daily newspaper. His boss, a seasoned editor named Shinji, took notice of his potential and soon offered him a permanent position as a reporter.

As Aarav Hiroki delved deeper into the story, he began to uncover a web of deceit and corruption that went all the way to the top of Tokyo's elite.
