Aarav Zariņa was known for his unparalleled skill in the art of aerial photography. As a professional drone pilot and photographer, he had captured some of the most breathtaking views from around the world. Born and raised in Riga, Latvia, Aarav's passion for flying and capturing stunning images began at a young age.

Growing up, Aarav spent countless hours watching his grandfather, a veteran pilot, take to the skies in their family's old plane. The thrill of soaring through the air and taking in the sights below ignited a fire within him, which eventually led him to pursue a career in drone photography. With an unwavering dedication to his craft, Aarav honed his skills, learning everything there was to know about flight patterns, lighting conditions, and composition.

Aarav's big break came when he landed a contract with National Geographic, capturing stunning aerial footage of the Amazon rainforest. The article that featured his photographs sparked international attention, solidifying Aarav Zariņa's reputation as one of the world's top drone photographers.
