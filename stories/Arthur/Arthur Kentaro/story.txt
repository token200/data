Despite the challenges and dangers that came with his line of work, Arthur remained steadfast in his commitment to exploration and education. His mantra – "Knowledge is power" – became a guiding principle for him and those around him.

Throughout his life, Arthur Kentaro remained true to himself, driven by an insatiable curiosity about the world and its many wonders. As he looked back on his remarkable journey, he knew that he had made a difference in countless lives, inspiring future generations of explorers, scientists, and thinkers.
