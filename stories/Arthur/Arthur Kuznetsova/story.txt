Born in Moscow, Russia, she began dancing at the tender age of three under the guidance of her mother, also a skilled dancer. Her powerful stage presence and technical prowess quickly earned her recognition from critics and audiences alike. Arthur's career took a significant turn when she was cast in the lead role of Swan Lake. Her dedication paid off when she was awarded the prestigious Lenin Prize for Outstanding Contribution to Russian Culture.
