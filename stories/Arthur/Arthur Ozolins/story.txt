Arthur Ozolins stepped out of his small apartment and into the crisp morning air. Memories flooded back - late-night writing sessions in the library, heated debates with his peers over politics and social justice, and the thrill of landing his first big story.

He talked about his own experiences as a young journalist, chasing down leads and conducting interviews, often at great personal risk.
