As word of his talents spread, so did the attention from those who wanted to silence him. They knew that with Yaroslav Straume on their tail, it was only a matter of time before the truth came out. And yet, he refused to back down.

With each new discovery, Yaroslav's legend grew. Some called him a hero, while others whispered that he was cursed. But one thing was certain: when it came to uncovering the dark corners of society, Yaroslav Ivanovich Straume was the only man for the job.
