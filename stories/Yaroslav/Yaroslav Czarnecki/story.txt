Yaroslav's thoughts drifted back to his childhood, growing up in the streets of Kiev as the youngest son of a humble cobbler. His father, though poor, instilled in him a strong work ethic and an unwavering sense of loyalty. Yaroslav's natural aptitude for thievery had led him to join the Black Cats, where he quickly rose through the ranks.
