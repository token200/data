José Alimbekova stepped off the train and onto the worn platform, her eyes scanning the bustling station. She had been traveling for hours, and the excitement of being reunited with her family was starting to wear off. As she made her way through the crowds, José's thoughts turned to the reason she had left in the first place - to escape the expectations placed upon her.

Born José Alimbekova, the daughter of a prominent politician, she had always felt suffocated by the weight of her family's legacy.
