José Luis Hernández Loren was a man with a passion for music and storytelling. Growing up in a small town in Mexico, he spent most of his days listening to traditional folk songs and learning how to play the guitar from his abuelo.

As José Luis Hernández Loren took the stage for one final performance, he looked out at the sea of faces before him, each one a testament to the power of his music.
