As she grew older, Edith's talent and creativity only blossomed more, leading her to pursue a degree in fine arts. After graduating, she traveled the world, immersing herself in different cultures and meeting fellow artists who became dear friends. Edith Kwiatkowski found solace in their camaraderie and shared passion for art.
