Marcus bowed gravely and set off into the night, leaving Camille Flavius Domitianus Augustus to ponder the weight of his own legacy. As he stood before the mirror, adjusting his imperial crown, he caught a glimpse of himself – strong-jawed and authoritative – but also tired-eyed and worn.

As the days passed, Camille Flavius Domitianus Augustus became increasingly isolated, surrounded by sycophants and yes-men who dared not speak truth to power. His paranoia grew, and with it, his grip on reality began to slip.

One fateful night, as a fierce storm raged outside, Camille Flavius Domitianus Augustus received an unexpected visit from Marcus once more. This time, however, the advisor brought news of a plot to overthrow him – a plot that threatened the very foundation of the empire he had sworn to protect.

With a heavy heart, Camille Flavius Domitianus Augustus knew that his time as Emperor was drawing to a close. He steeled himself for what lay ahead, knowing that only one thing was certain: his name would be remembered for eternity – or forever lost in the annals of history.
