A seasoned saxophonist himself, he had always been drawn to this genre of music. As he made his way through the crowded room, his eyes scanned the stage for any sign of the enigmatic lead singer, Julian Blackwood.

As he took a seat at the bar, Gabriel's thoughts turned to Julian Blackwood, whose raw energy seemed to electrify every performance. Their bands crossed paths several times before, but they never quite clicked – Gabriel preferred to keep to himself, observing from afar rather than drawing attention.

The connection was palpable – it was as if they were meant to be together on stage, creating something greater than the sum of its parts.

As the night wore on, Gabriel Isakova found himself drawn into Julian's orbit, their musical conversation flowing effortlessly from one song to the next. For a moment, he forgot about his past and let the music take over, letting his soul merge with Julian's.
