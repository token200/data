Despite his newfound status, Valentin continued to keep his true loyalties hidden behind a mask of neutrality. Only those closest to him knew that beneath the tough exterior lay a complex web of emotions and motivations.

As they worked together on the battlefield, Valentin found himself drawn to Sophia's warmth and compassion, feeling for the first time in years a sense of connection he thought had long been lost.

But Valentin Jasinski was not one to let his guard down easily. Even as he began to trust Sophia with his life, he couldn't shake the feeling that she might be hiding secrets of her own. And so, he continued to keep his distance, observing her from afar as they fought side by side against a common enemy.
