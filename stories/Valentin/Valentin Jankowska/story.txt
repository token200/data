Valentin Jankowska's latest obsession was the enigmatic artist, Hieronymus Bosch. Over the next few weeks, Valentin Jankowska spent every waking moment studying the painting, unraveling its secrets. And as he did so, he began to realize that Bosch's art was not just a reflection of his own imagination but also a window into the world of his time.
