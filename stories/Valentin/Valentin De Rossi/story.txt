One fateful evening, Valentin De Rossi received a visit from a beautiful young woman named Sophie. She was frantic with worry, as her sister, Colette, had gone missing in the city's infamous catacombs. The police were stumped, and Sophie knew she needed someone with Valentin De Rossi's exceptional skills to find her sister.

As they navigated the narrow tunnels of the catacombs, Valentin De Rossi's eyes scanned every inch of the dark passageway.
