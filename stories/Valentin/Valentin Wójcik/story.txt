Despite these challenges, Valentin Wójcik emerged as a brilliant engineer, capable of fixing anything with a screwdriver and a can of WD-40. He spent his days tinkering in his workshop, surrounded by gears, wires, and scraps of metal, lost in a world of his own creation.

His eyes gleamed with an inner light, as if he was tuning into some hidden frequency – the pulse of the universe, perhaps?
