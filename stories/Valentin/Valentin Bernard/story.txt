The victim was none other than a prominent businessman, known for his ruthless tactics in the corporate world. As Bernard delved deeper into the case, he discovered that the murder seemed to be linked to a long-forgotten tragedy from his own past.

Determined to unravel the tangled threads of the mystery, Valentin Bernard poured over every shred of evidence, following leads and interviewing suspects. As they sat across from each other, Bernard's piercing eyes locked onto hers, searching for any sign of deception. The woman, known only as "Léa," revealed that her own sister had been the victim of the long-forgotten tragedy that had haunted Valentin Bernard all these years. Her words sparked a floodgate of emotions in the detective, but he remained steadfast, refusing to let his personal demons cloud his judgment.

With Léa's help, Valentin Bernard pieced together the events surrounding the murder and ultimately uncovered a web of deceit and corruption that went all the way to the top. As he closed in on the killer, he knew that justice would be served, but at what cost? The memories of his own past would forever be intertwined with this case, leaving him with a sense of closure, yet also a reminder of the scars that time could never erase.
