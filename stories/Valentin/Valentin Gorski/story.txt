Valentin's instincts told him to be cautious, but his curiosity got the better of him. He arrived at the café, scanning the room for any signs of trouble. Valentin's eyes locked onto Lena's as he asked her why she had risked everything to bring this to light. Her gaze lingered for a moment before she whispered, "Because I have something to lose too, Valentin Aleksandrovich Gorski."
