He grew up on the streets, surviving by any means necessary, until he was discovered by a group of rebels who recognized his unique skills.

As a member of the Guard, Lev had risen through the ranks with ease, earning the respect of his comrades and the fear of his enemies. But when he learned that the Empire planned to use him as leverage against the Alliance, he knew he had to escape. And so, with a small team of rebels, he made his move.

Now, as a key asset for the rebellion, Lev used his knowledge to guide them in their fight against the Empire. He was a master strategist, always thinking several steps ahead of his opponents. But despite his many accomplishments, Lev's past continued to haunt him.

As he stood on the bridge of the rebel ship, gazing out at the stars, Lev couldn't help but feel a sense of unease. He knew that the Empire would stop at nothing to capture him, and that he was living on borrowed time. His full name, Lev Ivanovich Jarv, echoed in his mind like a curse.
