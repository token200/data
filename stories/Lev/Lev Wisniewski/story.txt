Sophia's newfound appreciation for Lev Wisniewski was tempered by her growing sense of admiration for his dedication to his work. Sophia realized that her own pursuit of knowledge was not as solitary as Lev Wisniewski's - that even in the most individualistic pursuits, connection and understanding were essential.
