As an adult, Pawel Boucher owned and operated "The Sweet Treat," a beloved bakery that attracted visitors from all over town. It turned out that The Sweet Treat had been chosen as the official dessert provider for Willow Creek's annual Harvest Festival. Pawel was thrilled at the opportunity and immediately began brainstorming ideas for his contribution.

He spent hours perfecting his signature recipe, adding a few secret ingredients to give it an extra oomph.
