As the years passed, Moritz's legend grew, though it was always shrouded in mystery. Some said he had spent his life searching for a lost artifact, while others whispered that he was a former spy or assassin, hiding from a past he couldn't shake.

Moritz González Fernández remained oblivious to such speculation, lost in the musty scent of old books and the gentle hum of the library's fluorescent lights. He continued to tend his collection with dedication, silently cataloging the lives of those who sought him out.

At night, when the library was closed and the streets were empty, Moritz would sit at his desk, surrounded by stacks of ancient texts and worn leather journals.
