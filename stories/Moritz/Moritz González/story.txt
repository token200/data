As Moritz González listened, entranced, the musician looked up and caught his eye. It was an old man with a kind face and twinkling eyes, who introduced himself as Maestro Rodriguez.

As the night drew to a close, Moritz González felt changed, transformed by the power of music and the company of this wise old man.
