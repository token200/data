As Moritz Szabo navigated the crowded streets of Shinjuku, he couldn't shake off the feeling that something wasn't quite right. Something about this mission felt too good to be true. This revelation sent a chill down Moritz Szabo's spine - if one of their own was involved in this treachery, then the stakes were higher than he had ever imagined.
