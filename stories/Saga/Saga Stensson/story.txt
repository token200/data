As she delved deeper into the world of books, Saga discovered a love for writing and storytelling. The villagers took notice of her talent, and soon Saga Stensson was known as one of the most promising young writers in Närke.

With Professor Larson's guidance, Saga continued to hone her craft, exploring themes of identity and community.
