Saga Horváth was more than just a brilliant scientist – she was a passionate advocate for the preservation of urban biodiversity. And as she entered the lab, surrounded by rows of humming equipment and shelves lined with jars of peculiar specimens, Saga felt a sense of purpose that drove her forward.

The rare moonflower species would be the key to unlocking new discoveries in the field of botany.
