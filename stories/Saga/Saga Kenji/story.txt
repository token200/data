Saga Kenji sprang into action, tracking down leads and interviewing witnesses. Her piercing gaze and quick wit earned her the respect of even the most skeptical citizens. With each new discovery, Saga Kenji felt an unmistakable thrill coursing through her veins. She was on the hunt for more than just stolen valuables – she was searching for truth. Her unwavering commitment to justice drove her forward, even when the road ahead seemed treacherous.

As the sun dipped below Neo-Eden's skyscrapers, Saga Kenji finally pieced together the puzzle, revealing a shocking mastermind behind the burglaries. Her tireless efforts had shed light on a dark world, revealing the faces behind the masks of deceit.
