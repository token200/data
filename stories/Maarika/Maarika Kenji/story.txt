With renewed confidence, Maarika dove headfirst into her writing. She spent long hours at her desk, fueled by coffee and determination. Finally, the article was complete, and she submitted it to the editor.

Maarika Kenji's star had risen, and she knew that nothing could stop her now. With her sharp wit, keen instincts, and tireless work ethic, she would continue to tell the stories that needed to be told, no matter what the cost. The Tokyo Times had discovered a gem, and Maarika Kenji was here to stay.
