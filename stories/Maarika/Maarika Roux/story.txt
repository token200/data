The two had been close as children, but a falling out in their teenage years had left a rift between them that had never fully healed. Despite this, there was still a spark of familiarity between them.

It was as if the music had awakened something deep within her, something that had been waiting patiently for its chance to shine.

As the night wore on, Maarika Roux found herself pouring her heart onto the canvas. She painted with a ferocity and intensity that she had never known before. When Leon finally stopped playing, she stepped back from her work, tears streaming down her face.
