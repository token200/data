This hobby eventually blossomed into a career, with Christian Larsen opening his own shop, "Larsen's Classic Cars," in the small town of Willow Creek.

He spent every spare moment researching, collecting, and restoring cars to their original condition. His clients adored him, not just for his exceptional craftsmanship but also for his infectious enthusiasm and dedication.

Days turned into weeks, and weeks turned into months, as they painstakingly restored the car to its former glory.
