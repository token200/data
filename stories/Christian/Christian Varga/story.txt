His parents, both artists themselves, encouraged him to pursue his passion, enrolling him in art classes from a young age. Now, at 25, Christian was determined to make a name for himself as an artist.

Christian sighed, handing her the rejection letter. "It's just another one," he said, feeling a lump form in his throat.

Maria took the letter from him and examined it carefully. "You can't let this get to you," she said firmly. "You're an incredibly talented artist, Christian Varga. You've got so much potential."
