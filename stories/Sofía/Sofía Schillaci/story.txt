Sofía Schillaci was a talented young artist who had always been drawn to the vibrant colors and textures of her native Barcelona. Growing up in a family of artists, she had been encouraged from a young age to explore her creativity and push the boundaries of her craft.

Excited by the prospect of sharing her art with a wider audience, she accepted the invitation without hesitation. As she packed her bags and prepared to embark on this new adventure, her mother couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and nostalgia.
