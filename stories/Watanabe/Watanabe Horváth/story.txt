As she delved into its yellowed pages, she discovered a passage penned by none other than Watanabe Horváth themselves. The words spoke of a life lived in the shadows, of secrets kept and stories untold.

Intrigued, Emily sought out Watanabe Horváth, determined to uncover the truth behind their enigmatic persona. A soft knock on the door was met with a low, gravelly voice inviting her in.

And so, the enigmatic figure of Watanabe Horváth continued to haunt Ashwood's streets, an eternal mystery wrapped in a cloak of secrecy.
