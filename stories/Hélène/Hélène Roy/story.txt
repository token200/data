Sophie soon became Hélène Roy's biggest advocate, championing her work to anyone who would listen. Together, they curated a series of exhibitions that showcased Hélène Roy's unique perspective on the world.

As Hélène Roy's career continued to soar, she found herself increasingly in demand. She was commissioned to create large-scale installations for corporate clients and museums, as well as works for private collectors.

Despite her success, Hélène Roy remained true to her artistic vision. Her work was never about making money or impressing others; it was always about expressing the emotions and thoughts that swirled within her.

Hélène Roy's studio became a hub of creative energy, attracting artists, writers, and musicians from all over the city. The space was a testament to her passion for art, filled with canvases, paint-splattered easels, and half-finished projects in various stages of completion.
