She would spend hours watching the way it filtered through the leaves of trees, casting dappled shadows on the ground below. This fascination only grew stronger as she matured into an artist, and her paintings became a reflection of this love for light.

Célia Kalējs's art studio was a testament to her unique perspective. The walls were adorned with canvases in every color of the spectrum, each one a portal to a different realm. Célia smiled, her eyes sparkling with creativity. "It's all about capturing the light within ourselves," she said. "The way it dances and swirls, a reflection of our own emotions."
