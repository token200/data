Her parents, both artists themselves, encouraged her creative pursuits from an early age, and she spent most of her childhood sketching and experimenting with textiles.

Despite the success, Célia Järv remained true to her roots, always striving to infuse her work with a sense of storytelling and emotional depth. She believed that fashion should be more than just a means of self-expression – it should also evoke a sense of connection and community.

Célia Järv was overjoyed at the opportunity to collaborate with such a prestigious institution.
