As the investigation progressed, Célia found herself becoming increasingly obsessed with uncovering the truth. Her colleagues began to worry about her, but Célia pushed on, driven by a sense of justice that only grew stronger with each passing day. She was determined to bring closure to the victims' families and to hold those responsible accountable.

Despite the long hours and personal risks involved, Célia Charron remained steadfast in her pursuit of the truth. Her colleagues began to admire her tenacity, and soon, they were all working together to unravel the tangled threads of the case.
