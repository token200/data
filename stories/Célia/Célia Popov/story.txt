As Célia grew older, she began to take piano lessons herself, teaching herself to play with a natural ease that surprised even her parents. And when she finally felt ready, she decided to enter the prestigious Golden Note competition, determined to showcase her talents to the world.

The night of the competition arrived, and Célia took her place on stage alongside some of the most talented musicians in the city. As she began to play, her nerves dissipated, replaced by a sense of calm focus that allowed her to lose herself in the music. The crowd was captivated by her performance, hanging on every note as she brought a haunting beauty to the stage.

When Célia Popov finally finished playing, she took a deep breath and stepped off into applause. Over the coming months, she worked tirelessly in the studio, pouring all of her energy into her debut album.

As the release date approached, Célia couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and accomplishment.
