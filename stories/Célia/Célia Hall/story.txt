Célia Hall's eyes lit up as she realized that this was more than just a prank - it was a clue.

Célia Hall's investigation led her to expose the truth, bringing justice to the victims and earning herself even more accolades for her work. As she walked away from the press conference, she couldn't help but feel a sense of satisfaction - another job well done by Célia Hall.
