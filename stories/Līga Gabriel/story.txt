Līga Gabriel was an Estonian pop singer who had it all – a stunning voice, captivating stage presence, and a devoted fan base. Born in Tallinn, Estonia's capital city, Līga had grown up singing in church choirs and performing at local talent shows. She began writing her own songs as a teenager and eventually landed a record deal with an independent label.

Līga's big break came when she released her debut single "Moonlight" online, which quickly racked up millions of views on YouTube and social media platforms. The song's haunting melody and poignant lyrics spoke to the hearts of listeners worldwide, earning her a spot at the prestigious Eurovision Song Contest in 2022.

In Stockholm, Sweden, Līga performed with conviction and poise, wowing judges and audiences alike with her powerful vocals and emotive delivery. Though she ultimately came second, her participation earned her widespread recognition and catapulted her to stardom.
