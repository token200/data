Agnès Mastroianni stepped onto the worn wooden dock, her eyes scanning the misty morning waters of the Mediterranean. As she gazed out at the sea, memories began to flood her mind like the gentle lapping of waves against the shore.

Years later, as Agnès navigated the winding streets of Paris, she found herself lost in the same way she had been as a child. The City of Light's winding alleys and charming cafes seemed to whisper secrets in her ear, drawing her deeper into its heart. And yet, despite the allure of this vibrant city, Agnès Mastroianni couldn't shake the feeling that something was missing.

"Bonjour, mademoiselle," the stranger said, her voice like music on the wind. "My name is Sophie. Would you like to join me on this voyage?"
