As they chatted, Valérie opened up about her struggles with confidence, feeling seen and heard by someone who didn't know much about her.

Léon encouraged Valérie Morel to embrace her individuality and stop comparing herself to others. He reminded her that everyone starts somewhere and that it's okay to take risks and make mistakes. Valérie was both thrilled and intimidated by the prospect of showcasing her work alongside some of France's most renowned artists. With Léon by her side, she felt more confident than ever before.
