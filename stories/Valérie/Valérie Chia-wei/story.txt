Valérie Chia-wei was a free-spirited artist with a passion for capturing the beauty of the world around her on canvas. Born and raised in Paris, France, she had always been drawn to the city's vibrant culture and stunning architecture.

After completing her studies at the prestigious École des Beaux-Arts, Valérie Chia-wei set out into the world to hone her skills as an artist. As she delved deeper into her creative process, Valérie Chia-wei felt her artistic style evolving, becoming more intuitive and expressive.
