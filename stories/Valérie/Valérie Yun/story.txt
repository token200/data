It was an ancient kanji character, said to bring good luck to those who possessed it. She made a mental note to look into its significance later, but for now, her focus remained on the task at hand.

"Not yet," she said, "but I have a feeling this case is going to be easier to crack than I thought."

With renewed determination, Valérie Yun set to work analyzing the evidence and following up on leads. As she delved deeper into the mystery, she couldn't help but feel a sense of excitement – she was closing in on the culprit, and she knew it. With her sharp instincts and razor-sharp mind, Valérie Yun would stop at nothing to bring the perpetrator to justice.
