As she worked on her latest piece, "La Lune des Enfants," Valérie couldn't help but draw inspiration from the city around her. The twinkling lights of the Eiffel Tower cast a silver glow over the streets, while the Seine River flowed gently through the heart of Paris. It was this juxtaposition of old and new that sparked Valérie's creativity.
