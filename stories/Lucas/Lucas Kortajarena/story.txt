As Lucas walked down the catwalk, his eyes locked onto the audience, his confidence and poise commanding their attention. He was no longer just a young man from Basque Country; he had become a force to be reckoned with in the fashion industry. His mother's sacrifices had paid off, and Lucas Kortállerena was now reaping the rewards of her tireless devotion.
