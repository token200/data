His images of sweeping sunsets and towering mountain ranges transported viewers to another world, evoking feelings of awe and wonder. Eventually, it came time to pack up his prints and say goodbye to the familiar surroundings of his alma mater. Though the experience had been bittersweet, Lucas knew that he had given himself a gift – the chance to connect with others through his art.
