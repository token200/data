She would often take him to the local theater, where he would watch and mimic the performances of the talented actors on stage.

As Lucas accepted yet another award, he couldn't help but think of his abuela, who had inspired him to pursue his dreams. "Gracias a mi abuela," he said, thanking her for believing in him from the start.

Lucas Ruiz Garcia was living proof that with hard work and determination, anything is possible. He continued to shine brightly in Hollywood, inspiring countless young actors and actresses along the way.
