Intrigued, Lucas Jorgensen set out to unravel the mystery, following a trail of clues that led him from high-end boutiques to seedy underground clubs.

The game was afoot, and Lucas was hot on the trail. In the end, justice was served, and Lucas had proved once again why he was one of the city's most sought-after detectives - Lucas Jorgensen, the man with a mind for mystery and mayhem.
