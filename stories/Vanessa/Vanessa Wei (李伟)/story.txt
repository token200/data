Vanessa Wei, also known as Lee Wei to her close friends and family in Hong Kong, had always been fascinated by the world of art. Growing up in a small apartment overlooking Victoria Harbour, she would spend hours gazing out at the stunning skyline, imagining what it would be like to create something beautiful that could capture its essence.

After completing her degree in fine arts from the University of Hong Kong, Vanessa decided to pursue a career as a painter. She spent years honing her skills, experimenting with different techniques and mediums until she finally found her unique voice. Her bold brushstrokes and vibrant colours seemed to dance across the canvas, evoking emotions that resonated deeply with those who saw them.

As Lee Wei's reputation grew, so did her opportunities. She began receiving commissions from local galleries and collectors, and soon found herself exhibiting her work in some of the most prestigious art spaces in Hong Kong. Her unique perspective on the city she loved had captured the hearts of many, and she was proud to be showcasing her talents to a wider audience.
