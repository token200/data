As she wandered through the fields, Bozena felt a sense of freedom that she had never known before. Bozena's journey had taken her full circle, back to the place where it all began. As she stood on the edge of the forest, looking out over the landscape, she felt a sense of peace wash over her. She knew that she would always carry the weight of her family's history with her, but she also knew that she was not defined by it.
