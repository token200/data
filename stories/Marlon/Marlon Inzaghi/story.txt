"You're looking for something, aren't you?" Marlon asked, his voice low and husky.

Sophia's mind reeled as she gazed upon the treasures before her. She knew then that she had stumbled into something much bigger than herself. Marlon Inzaghi was a guardian of secrets, a keeper of mysteries. And Sophia had just become entangled in his web of intrigue.
