His relationships were few and far between, and when they did happen, they were always intense and all-consuming.

Or would it prove to be just another flash in the pan?

Marlon Novák's future hung precariously in the balance, as he wrestled with the weight of expectation and the thrill of innovation. Only time would tell whether his latest creation would be a game-changer or a footnote in history.
