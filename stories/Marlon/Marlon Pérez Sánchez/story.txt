One day, as Marlon was working on a new mural in a neglected alleyway, he was approached by a local journalist who had heard about his work. The journalist, Sarah, was impressed by Marlon's talent and passion, and asked if she could interview him for her next article.

Marlon agreed, and the two sat down to talk. As they spoke, Sarah learned more about Marlon Pérez Sánchez – not just the artist, but the person behind the paintbrushes. She discovered that he was driven by a desire to make a difference, to use his art as a tool for social change.
