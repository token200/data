Marlon Delacroix was known for his mysterious aura that surrounded him like a shroud. Few people had seen him up close and lived to tell the tale. His piercing green eyes seemed to bore into your soul, as if searching for secrets you'd rather keep hidden.

Marlon Delacroix hesitated, his mind racing with possibilities. He knew that getting tangled in this woman's web could be disastrous, but he couldn't resist the allure of the unknown.
