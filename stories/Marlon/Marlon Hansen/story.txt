Marlon accepted the challenge, using his skills as a thief to bypass security and make off with the painting. It was a daring heist, but Marlon had always been a man who lived on the edge. But for now, he was content to keep his two worlds separate, creating art that reflected both the beauty and the grittiness of urban life.
