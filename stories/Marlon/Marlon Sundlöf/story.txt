Marlon Sundlöf was an enigmatic figure in the art world, known for his thought-provoking and visually stunning installations. As he honed his craft, Marlon's style evolved from traditional representations of nature to more abstract and symbolic depictions.

The piece was inspired by his own experiences of feeling lost and disconnected from the world around him. Viewers were drawn into a dreamlike realm where they could wander, discover hidden meanings, and confront their own fears.
