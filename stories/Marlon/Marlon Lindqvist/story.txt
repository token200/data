She was worried about her missing sister, who had vanished without a trace six months prior. Desperate for answers, she turned to Marlon Lindqvist, hoping that his expertise could help her find closure.

Marlon Lindqvist listened intently as Sarah recounted her sister's last days before disappearing. He made notes on his pad and asked pointed questions, his eyes never leaving hers. As the interview came to a close, he leaned forward and handed her a business card.
