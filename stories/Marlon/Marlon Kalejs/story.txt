With Sophia's help, Marlon set out on a perilous journey into the heart of the rainforest, driven by an insatiable curiosity about what lay beyond the boundaries of human understanding.

It was a sensation that had become all too familiar to him over the years, but one he refused to ignore. For in the world of cryptozoology, the line between fact and fiction was always blurred, and Marlon Kalejs knew that the truth lay just beyond the next tree line.
