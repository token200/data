Gabriela Jie closed her eyes as she savored the tangy flavors, the complexity of the sauce transporting her to memories of late-night conversations with her grandmother.

Gabriela Jie's cheeks flushed as she smiled back. She knew what it was like to be an outsider, a bridge between cultures and worlds. Her own identity was a patchwork quilt of experiences: her Malaysian mother, her Polish father; the languages she spoke, the traditions she'd been taught.
