As she grew older, Gabriela's fascination with art only deepened. She began studying painting at a local academy, where she quickly discovered that her unique perspective and style set her apart from her fellow students. Her teachers encouraged her to experiment with new techniques, and soon Gabriela was creating works that were both deeply personal and universally relatable.

Whether she was depicting the majesty of a whale or the gentle curves of a shell, Gabriela's art was always infused with a sense of wonder and curiosity.
