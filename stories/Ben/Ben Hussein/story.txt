His parents, though skeptical at first, eventually came around to their son's musical pursuits, even setting up a small home studio for him to hone his craft.

He poured all his energy into his studies, forming bands, composing songs, and exhibiting his artwork in local galleries. Years went by, and Ben Hussein continued to evolve as an artist, experimenting with new sounds and styles while staying true to his roots.
