Benjamin "Ben" Mäkinen was a man with a passion for photography. Growing up in a small town in Finland, he spent most of his childhood exploring the woods and capturing the beauty of nature through his lens.

Despite his success, Ben remained humble and true to his roots. He continued to live in Finland and draw inspiration from the natural beauty that surrounded him. His latest series, "Frozen Moments," was inspired by the winter landscapes of his homeland, and it had just been featured in a major international art publication.

It was a huge opportunity, and Ben was thrilled to have the chance to work with such a renowned client.
