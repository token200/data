As she approached the crumbling structure, Emilie felt an eerie sense of excitement wash over her. She had always been fascinated by the supernatural, and this place seemed like a dream come true for any paranormal enthusiast. She quickened her pace, her heart pounding in her chest. Suddenly, a loud bang echoed through the lighthouse, making her jump.

Emilie Gagnon took a deep breath and tried to calm herself down. As she continued to explore, she began to notice strange symbols etched into the walls, seemingly appearing out of nowhere.

But as the seconds ticked by, the apparition began to take shape, revealing a woman in old-fashioned clothing, gazing at Emilie with a mixture of sadness and longing.
