Berzins' latest obsession was the study of temporal anomalies. His colleagues often found themselves bewildered by his unorthodox methods, but Berzins didn't care – he was driven by an insatiable curiosity.

As Berzins basked in the glow of his triumph, he couldn't help but feel a sense of unease. Had he truly unlocked the secrets of time, or had he simply stumbled into something far more sinister? Only time would tell.

Despite the uncertainty surrounding his achievement, one thing was clear: Sasaki Berzins was a wizard unlike any other. His unorthodox methods and boundless ambition had earned him a place among the most respected – and feared – mages in the realm. And as he gazed out into the swirling vortex before him, he knew that his next experiment was already waiting just beyond the horizon.
