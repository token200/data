With her trusty notebook and pen in hand, Eben set out into the night, following a trail of clues that led her from the glittering skyscrapers of Shinjuku to the seedy underbelly of the city's red-light district. As she navigated the narrow alleys and crowded streets, she couldn't shake the feeling that she was being watched - but she refused to let fear get the better of her.

And as she looked back on her journey, she knew that it was all thanks to her sharp mind, quick wit, and unwavering determination. Sasaki Eben, the rising star of Japanese journalism, had once again proven herself to be a force to be reckoned with.
