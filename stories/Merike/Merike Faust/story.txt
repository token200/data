They're recruiting new members, and we've had reports of strange rituals taking place under the light of the full moon."

Merike Faust's eyes narrowed as she processed the information. She knew that she had to investigate further, but for now, she simply nodded at the bartender and took a swig of her whiskey.

As she turned back to face the stage, Merike Faust spotted the blue-eyed stranger standing up, his movements fluid and deliberate. He caught her eye once more, and this time, she felt a spark of connection that went beyond mere curiosity.

For a moment, they simply stared at each other, two strangers connected by a thread of fate. Then, without a word, the stranger nodded at Merike Faust and disappeared into the crowd, leaving her to wonder what lay ahead.
