As she entered her teenage years, she began to explore different genres of music, eventually finding her place in the Finnish rock scene.

But it was their sophomore album, "Tulo" (Arrival), that catapulted Arianna Mäkinen to international stardom.

The album's lead single, "Sade" (Rain), was an anthem that resonated with listeners worldwide. Its lyrics, written by Arianna herself, spoke of hope and resilience in the face of adversity. The song's soaring chorus and pulsing beat made it a staple on every major playlist.

As Elyse's popularity grew, so did Arianna Mäkinen's influence. She became a role model for young Finnish women, inspiring them to follow their dreams and pursue their passions. Offstage, she was known for her kindness and generosity, often using her platform to raise awareness for social causes close to her heart.

Despite the pressures of fame, Arianna Mäkinen remained grounded and true to herself. She continued to write music that spoke from the heart, refusing to compromise on her artistic vision.
