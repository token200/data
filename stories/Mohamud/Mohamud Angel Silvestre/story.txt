As he grew older, Mohamud's abilities became more pronounced. He could understand the chirping of birds, the chattering of monkeys, and even the rumblings of earthquakes. One day, a group of tourists arrived in Mohamud's village, seeking an authentic experience with local wildlife. Among them was a young girl named Sophie, who had lost her pet dog a few days prior and was desperate to find it. Mohamud Angel Silvestre agreed to help, using his unique connection to the animal kingdom.

He led Sophie on a wild goose chase through the savannah, following the faint scent of her missing dog's fur. As they trekked across the grasslands, Mohamud used his gift to communicate with the animals, gathering information about the dog's whereabouts. A wise old tortoise offered them a cryptic clue, and a pair of birds led them to a hidden watering hole.

Finally, after hours of searching, Sophie heard her dog's faint barking in the distance. With Mohamud Angel Silvestre by her side, she rushed towards the sound and was overjoyed to be reunited with her beloved pet.

Word of Mohamud's remarkable abilities spread quickly, attracting visitors from far and wide.
