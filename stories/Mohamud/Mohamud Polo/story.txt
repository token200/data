As he navigated the dark corridors, Mohamud Polo couldn't shake the feeling that he was being watched. He quickened his pace, his heart pounding in his chest, but when he reached the room where the gemstone was kept, he found himself face to face with a figure shrouded in shadows.

The air seemed to vibrate with tension as Mohamud Polo and the mysterious figure locked eyes. For a moment, they simply stared at each other, neither one daring to make a move. Then, without warning, the figure vanished into thin air, leaving Mohamud Polo shaken but unharmed.

Breathless and confused, Mohamud Polo turned his attention back to the gemstone, which was still within his grasp. He snatched it from its pedestal and made a swift exit, disappearing into the night as suddenly as he had appeared.

Instead, rumors began to circulate that Mohamud Polo had been paid off, and that the gemstone was now safely hidden away, its location known only to him.
