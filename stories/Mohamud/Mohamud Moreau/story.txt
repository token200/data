The corrupt officials were caught off guard by his relentless pursuit of the truth, and they fought back with every trick in the book. But Mohamud Moreau refused to be intimidated, his determination fueled by a deep-seated desire to bring justice to those who had been wronged.

The room was electric with tension as the officials were finally brought to book, and Mohamud's name became synonymous with bravery and integrity.
