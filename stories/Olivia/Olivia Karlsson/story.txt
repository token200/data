A former journalist turned writer, Olivia Karlsson had left the city behind to escape the chaos and find inspiration in the quiet rhythms of rural life. She had always been drawn to stories about small towns, and Ravenswood was rumored to be one of the most fascinating of all.

The next day, Olivia Karlsson set out to explore the town, talking to locals and gathering stories for her writing project. As she walked down Main Street, she noticed a small antique shop with a faded sign that read "Karlsson's Curios". Her eyes narrowed - was this some kind of coincidence?
