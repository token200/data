His colleagues whispered among themselves, speculating about the reasons behind his departure. One thing was certain, however: Leonard Briedite's disappearance sparked an investigation that would lead nowhere. The police searched for him, but he seemed to have vanished into thin air. His apartment was left untouched, his phone and computer still active, but with no signs of struggle or disturbance.
