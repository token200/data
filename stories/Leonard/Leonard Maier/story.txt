Leonard Maier stepped off the train and onto the worn platform, the familiar scent of saltwater and seaweed filling his lungs. As he walked towards his vessel, he couldn't help but think about the countless stories he'd accumulated over the years.

Leonard Maier smiled and returned the greeting, relieved to be back among the familiar creaks and groans of his beloved vessel.

As they worked, Leonard Maier couldn't help but think about the incredible stories he'd heard from other sailors and islanders during his travels.
