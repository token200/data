Jessica Alexandra DiCaprio was a free-spirited artist with a passion for painting and a love for adventure. Growing up in a small coastal town, she spent most of her childhood exploring the rocky shores and beaches, collecting seashells and driftwood to use in her art.

She was immediately drawn to it, feeling a sense of connection to the vessel's worn wooden hull and the sea-worn ropes that tied it down. Despite the success that came with her art, Jessica never forgot where she came from. She continued to paint and sail, always chasing the next horizon, always seeking the beauty in the unknown. Her full name, Jessica Alexandra DiCaprio, became synonymous with a way of life – one of freedom, creativity, and a deep connection to the natural world.
