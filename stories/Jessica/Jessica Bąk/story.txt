One day, a new client approached the firm, looking for someone to create a branding identity for his startup company. The client was impressed by Jessica's portfolio and hired her to work on the project. Jessica threw herself into the task, pouring all her energy into creating a unique and memorable brand image.

As she worked on the project, Jessica drew inspiration from Polish culture and architecture. She incorporated traditional motifs and colors into the design, making it distinctly Polish. Her hard work paid off when the client loved the final result and commissioned her to create more branding materials for his company.

Jessica Bąk's success with the startup company opened doors for her in the design world. Despite her growing success, Jessica remained humble and continued to learn and grow as an artist.
