As the night wore on, Jessica's game only grew stronger, and Marcus found himself increasingly entranced by her magnetic presence. The stakes were high, but Jessica Magi was undaunted. With her years of experience and unparalleled skill, she had an uncanny ability to read people and manipulate their emotions. She wove a web of intrigue around Marcus, drawing him deeper into the game with every passing moment.
