The owner, a kind old man named Raj, noticed Martin Singh's interest in the guitars on display and struck up a conversation. Over coffee, they talked about music, life, and everything in between.

With each note, Martin Singh seemed to channel the spirits of Bollywood legends like Lata Mangeshkar and Kishore Kumar.
