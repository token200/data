As the winter solstice approached, Titus Korhonen finally arrived at the entrance to the lost city, guarded by an imposing ice wall that seemed to stretch on forever. Titus Korhonen spent hours pouring over the relics, his eyes drinking in the knowledge that he was one step closer to unraveling the secrets of the past.
