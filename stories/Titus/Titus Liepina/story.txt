As a child prodigy in mathematics, Titus had always been fascinated by the intricacies of pattern recognition and code-breaking. He spent countless hours poring over ancient texts and cryptic manuscripts, seeking to unravel the secrets hidden within.

As he grew older, Titus Liepina's talents were recognized by his peers and elders alike. Despite his many accomplishments, Titus remained elusive and unassuming. The message read simply: "Meet me at the old windmill on the outskirts of town at midnight. Come alone." Intrigued by the promise of adventure and intellectual stimulation, Titus Liepina decided to accept the invitation.
