Titus Makinen listened intently as the businessman recounted the events leading up to the theft. He asked questions and took notes, his mind racing with possibilities. Determined to prove his theory, Titus Makinen set out to recreate the scene of the crime. He spent hours examining the clock and its surroundings, searching for any clues that might have been missed by the police. With newfound confidence, Titus Makinen confronted the businessman's daughter and her suspected accomplice. The evidence, once obscure, now shone brightly in the light of discovery. The thieves were caught, and the antique clock was recovered, thanks to Titus Makinen's tireless efforts.
