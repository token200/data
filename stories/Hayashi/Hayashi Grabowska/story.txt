Despite her tough exterior, Hayashi had a soft spot for stray animals. On her travels, she would often take in lost pets and nurse them back to health. It was during this time that she met a scrappy little terrier named Miso, who became her constant companion.

For Hayashi Grabowska, this moment marked a turning point in her journey.
