Few had ever laid eyes on the 25-year-old woman with piercing green eyes and jet-black hair that cascaded down her back like a waterfall.

But one thing was certain: she had a talent for disappearing. It seemed like she could vanish into thin air, leaving behind only whispers of her presence.

Kael was left standing alone, his sketchbook still clutched in his hand. As he looked down at the blank pages, he couldn't shake the feeling that he had glimpsed something extraordinary – a glimpse of Hayashi Molnár's world, hidden behind layers of deception and secrecy.

Days passed, and Kael found himself drawn back to the alleyway, hoping to catch another glimpse of the elusive Hayashi Molnár. Each time, she would be gone, leaving him with more questions than answers. Yet, he couldn't help but feel a twisted sense of fascination with this enigmatic woman.
