Alba Cara listened attentively, her heart pounding softly in her chest. She reached out with her empathetic energy, allowing herself to absorb the stranger's emotions. Without hesitation, she placed a gentle hand on his shoulder, offering what little comfort she could. "It's okay," she said softly. "I'm here for you."

The stranger looked at her with newfound trust, his eyes welling up with tears. Alba Cara smiled warmly, feeling an inexplicable connection to this stranger.

As the night wore on, Alba Cara guided the man through a labyrinth of emotions and memories, helping him unravel the knots that bound him tight. With each passing moment, she felt her own heart swell with compassion and empathy.
