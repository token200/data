For a moment, Alba felt at peace, like she was exactly where she was meant to be. The world might still be full of uncertainty, but for now, she knew that Alba Larsen had made the right choice. As she closed her sketchbook, a smile spread across her face - it was a small victory, perhaps, but one that filled her with a sense of pride and accomplishment.
