She pursued a degree in photography and eventually landed an internship with a prominent agency. It was there that she honed her skills, learning to capture the world around her through a lens.

Years later, as Alba Svensson stood before her latest exhibition, she couldn't help but think back on the journey that brought her here. Her photographs told stories of people and places, each one imbued with a sense of longing and wonder.
