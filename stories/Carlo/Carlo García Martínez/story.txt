Carlo García Martínez stepped off the train and onto the worn platform, his eyes scanning the sleepy town before him. A successful businessman in his late 30s, he had grown disillusioned with the cutthroat world of finance and was searching for something more meaningful. This small village, with its gentle rhythms and loving people, was exactly what he needed. He took a bite of his torta, savoring the flavors and textures as he gazed out at the peaceful scene before him.
