Finally, after months of tireless work, Carlo cracked the final code. With a sense of triumph, he opened the box once more, revealing a small, golden key inside. According to the book, this was the key to unlocking an ancient treasure hidden deep within the Swedish countryside.

Carlo, however, remained humble, knowing that his love for puzzles and enigmas had led him to this moment of triumph.
