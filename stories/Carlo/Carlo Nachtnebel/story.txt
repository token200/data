One evening, I had the privilege of attending an exclusive gallery opening where Carlo's latest masterpiece was unveiled. As I entered the room, I couldn't help but feel a sense of anticipation. The lights dimmed, and Carlo Nachtnebel took center stage, his eyes scanning the crowd with an air of quiet confidence.
