He had worked tirelessly to make a name for himself as a writer, but the city's gentle rhythms and familiar faces had always called him back. Now, he returned with a sense of purpose, determined to revive the family bakery that had once been a staple on the island.

Eric Fontaine was determined to restore it to its former glory, using traditional recipes and techniques passed down through generations of his family. As the aromas of freshly baked bread wafted through the streets, the people of Saint-Malo began to take notice.
