Despite his love of art, Eric Lacis's true passion was music. He played the guitar with ease, able to coax beautiful melodies from the strings with a deft touch. His favorite artist of all time was Jimi Hendrix, and he spent hours practicing Hendrix's iconic riffs on his own instrument.
