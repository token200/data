Despite his many talents, Étienne felt a sense of restlessness. He yearned to create something truly original, something that would leave a lasting impact on the world. One day, while wandering through an art gallery, he stumbled upon a small, avant-garde exhibit featuring works by an emerging artist named Lena Lee.

Enchanted by her unique style and fearless approach to her craft, Étienne felt an instant connection to Lena's work. He approached her after the exhibit closed and introduced himself, sharing his own passion for music and art.
