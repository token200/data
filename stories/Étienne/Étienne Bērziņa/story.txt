As he gazed out into the darkness, Étienne Bērziņa couldn't help but wonder what other secrets lay hidden in the shadows, waiting to be uncovered by a mind as sharp as his own. The weight of his discovery settled heavy on his shoulders, but he knew that he was ready for whatever came next.
