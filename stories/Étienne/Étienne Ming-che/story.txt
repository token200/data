Étienne Ming-che was no stranger to danger. He had faced down corrupt officials, dodged gunfire, and even survived a few too many close calls with rival journalists. Yet, he continued to push forward, driven by an insatiable curiosity and a passion for justice.

One fateful evening, as Étienne Ming-che navigated the narrow alleys of the city's oldest quarter, he stumbled upon a cryptic message scrawled on a dingy wall: "Meet me at the old clock tower at midnight. Come alone." The words seemed to leap off the wall, echoing in his mind like a siren's call.

As the night wore on, Étienne Ming-che knew he had stumbled upon something momentous – a story that could bring down the corrupt elite and restore hope to the people of Saigon. And with Linh by his side, he felt invincible, ready to face whatever challenges lay ahead as they worked together to expose the truth.
