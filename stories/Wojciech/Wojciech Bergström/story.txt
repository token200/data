It was on a drizzly London evening that Wojciech Bergström received a visit from the local police department, begging for his assistance in cracking a baffling case. A wealthy businessman had been found dead at his mansion, with no signs of forced entry or struggle. The only clue was a cryptic message scrawled on the wall, which read: "The answer lies where shadows fall."

Intrigued by the challenge, Wojciech Bergström accepted the case and set out to unravel the tangled threads of evidence. He spent hours pouring over the victim's business dealings, searching for any inconsistencies that might reveal a motive for murder. As he delved deeper into the world of high finance, he discovered a web of deceit and corruption that went all the way to the top.

With each new discovery, Wojciech Bergström felt the pieces falling into place. He spent countless hours re-examining the crime scene, searching for any subtle detail that might have been missed initially. It was on one such revisit that he spotted it - a faint smudge of dirt on the windowsill, which seemed out of place among the immaculate surroundings.

With this breakthrough, Wojciech Bergström's confidence grew. He pieced together the evidence, following a trail of clues that led him from the victim's mansion to a seedy underground nightclub on the outskirts of town. It was there that he finally uncovered the shocking truth: the killer was none other than the businessman's own son, who had stood to gain a substantial inheritance by his father's demise.

As Wojciech Bergström closed in on his quarry, the air seemed to grow thick with tension. The game was almost over, and the hunter was about to catch the prey. Wojciech Bergström's reputation as one of the greatest detectives of all time was solidified, his name etched forever in the annals of detective history.
