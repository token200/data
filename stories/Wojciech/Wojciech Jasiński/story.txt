When the exposé finally broke, Wojciech Jasiński found himself at the center of attention. As he stood before the city's mayor, delivering his findings with poise and conviction, Wojciech knew that he had truly earned his place among the greatest journalists of all time.

Wojciech Jasiński's name became synonymous with investigative reporting, and his work inspired a new generation of young journalists to follow in his footsteps.
