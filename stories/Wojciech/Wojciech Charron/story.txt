Rumors swirled among the scientific community about Wojciech Charron's true intentions. Some believed him to be a madman, driven by an insatiable curiosity and obsession with the past. Others saw him as a visionary, seeking to alter the course of events for the betterment of humanity.

As the day of his grand unveiling approached, excitement and trepidation filled the air. Wojciech Charron had invited select guests to witness the launch of his invention, including some of the most prominent minds in history.

The room fell silent as Wojciech Charron took the stage, a hint of a smile playing on his lips. He adjusted the dials on his time machine, set for ancient Greece, and flipped a switch. The air was filled with an eerie hum, followed by a blinding flash of light.
