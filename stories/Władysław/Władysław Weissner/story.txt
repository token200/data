Born in Warsaw, Poland, Władysław grew up surrounded by stories of his ancestors' bravery and sacrifice during World War II. Władysław's research took him from the snow-capped mountains of Poland to the sun-baked deserts of Turkey, as he sought out artifacts and relics that could shed light on the lives of those who came before. Determined to uncover the truth, he spent countless hours pouring over ancient texts and scouring the streets for any sign of the elusive document.
