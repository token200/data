Intrigued, she poured over the information, tracing a trail of financial transactions that led her to the doorstep of the notorious Polish businessman, Janusz Kowalski.

The journey would be fraught with danger, but Malgorzata Salonen was undaunted. She had spent years honing her skills as a journalist, and she knew that this story could make or break her career.
