Despite her international success, Malgorzata remained steadfastly rooted in her Polish heritage. She credited her grandmother, Babcia, with instilling in her a deep love for traditional folk art and storytelling. It was this blend of old-world charm and modern innovation that had earned Malgorzata Moreau critical acclaim and a devoted following among art enthusiasts worldwide.
