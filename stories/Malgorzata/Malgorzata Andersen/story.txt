This early exposure sparked a passion within her that would eventually lead her to study sculpture at the prestigious Goldsmiths College.

After completing her studies, Malgorzata set out to make a name for herself in the competitive art scene. She spent years honing her craft, experimenting with different materials and techniques until she had developed a style all her own.
