Her reputation spread far and wide, drawing the attention of those who sought her services – or her downfall.

Despite the dangers that lurked around every corner, Malgorzata Saldana remained unflappable, her heart guarded behind a wall of ice and steel. She knew that in her line of work, trust was a luxury she could ill afford.

One night, as she slipped through the shadows of a crowded marketplace, Malgorzata caught sight of a figure watching her from across the way – a figure with piercing blue eyes that seemed to see right through her.
