Pablo González Fernández was a man with a passion for art and music. Sofia took notice of Pablo's passion for music and introduced him to her musician friends. Before long, Pablo was jamming with the band, adding his own unique rhythm to their performances. His love for both art and music had finally found a perfect harmony.
