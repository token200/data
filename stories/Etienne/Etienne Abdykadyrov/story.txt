Few people had ever laid eyes on Etienne outside of his lab, and even fewer had managed to crack the code of his eccentric demeanor. His colleagues whispered about him behind closed doors - some said he was a genius, while others believed him to be mad.

But one person knew better: Sophia, the young graduate student who had been assigned to work under Etienne's supervision. Something that will redefine our understanding of the universe." And with that, he vanished into his own world of thought, leaving Sophia to ponder the secrets hidden within his brilliant mind.
