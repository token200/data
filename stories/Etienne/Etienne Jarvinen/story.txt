Etienne Jean-Luc Jarvinen was a man with a passion for art and music. The way the melodies seemed to transport him to another world, the emotions that poured out of every note – Etienne was hooked.

As he delved deeper into the world of music, Etienne found that his painting and violin playing complemented each other perfectly. He began to create pieces that combined visual and auditory elements, using his paintings as backdrops for his musical compositions.
