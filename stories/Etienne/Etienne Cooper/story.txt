After weeks of searching, he finally found the perfect shot: a young girl standing tall in front of a ruined building, her eyes shining with determination.

But every time he picked up his camera, he felt a surge of excitement and purpose. For Etienne Cooper, photography was more than just a hobby – it was a way to see the world, to connect with others, and to leave a lasting mark.
