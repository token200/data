Etienne François Gagnon was a man with a passion for photography. Growing up in rural Quebec, he spent most of his childhood exploring the woods and fields surrounding his family's farm. As a child, he would often sneak away from chores to capture the beauty of nature through his lens.

Despite his success, Etienne remained true to his roots. He continued to draw inspiration from the natural world that had sparked his passion for photography in the first place.
