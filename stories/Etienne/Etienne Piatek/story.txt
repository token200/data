As he grew older, Etienne became increasingly fascinated with the supernatural, spending countless nights investigating paranormal activity in the sleepy town of Ravenswood.

One stormy evening, as the winds howled outside, Etienne Piatek stood before an old, dilapidated mansion on the outskirts of town, his flashlight casting eerie shadows on the walls. He had been drawn to this house by rumors of a malevolent spirit that dwelled within its crumbling halls. With a deep breath, he pushed open the creaking front door and stepped inside.

To his surprise, the entity vanished into thin air, leaving behind only an echo of its presence.
