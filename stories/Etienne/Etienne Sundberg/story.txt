The note read: "Meet me at midnight at the old clock tower on the outskirts of town." Without hesitation, Etienne Sundberg set out into the darkness, his heart racing with anticipation.

As he approached the abandoned clock tower, he felt an eerie sense of familiarity. He pushed open the creaky door and stepped inside, his eyes adjusting to the dim light within. Suddenly, a figure emerged from the shadows. "Welcome, Etienne Sundberg," the stranger said, with a hint of a smile. "I think it's time we got to work."
