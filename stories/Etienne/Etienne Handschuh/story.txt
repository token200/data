As the sun began to set, casting a warm golden glow over the city square, Etienne made his way through the crowds, exchanging greetings and words of encouragement with fellow artists and admirers. His full name, Etienne Handschuh, was whispered in awe by those who had heard it associated with greatness – greatness not just as an artist, but as a patron and advocate.
