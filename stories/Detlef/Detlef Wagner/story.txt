A wealthy businessman, Hans Müller, had been found dead in his mansion on the outskirts of town. The police were stumped, and it was up to Detlef Wagner to unravel the tangled threads of the case.

His full name echoed in Detlef's mind - Hans Müller, a man with connections to some of Hamburg's most influential families.

As he delved deeper into the case, Detlef found himself entangled in a web of deceit and corruption. It seemed that Hans Müller had made some powerful enemies during his lifetime. His full name echoed in every conversation, as if trying to make sure no one forgot who he was.

Despite the obstacles, Detlef remained determined. He poured over the evidence, following every lead, no matter how small it seemed. And then, just when he thought he was closing in on a suspect, everything changed.

The rain outside seemed to intensify, matching the turmoil brewing inside Detlef.
