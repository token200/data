Detlef Pold was a collector of oddities – antique door knobs, vintage taxidermy animals, and even a few pieces of driftwood he had collected from the nearby lake. His cottage was a museum of sorts, filled with these curious artifacts that told a story of their own.

As they approached, Detlef Pold looked up from his work, his eyes twinkling with curiosity.

Detlef Pold watched them with interest, his eyes sparkling with pleasure as he revealed more about himself. He was a man with a deep love for the unknown, the unexplained, and the utterly bizarre.
