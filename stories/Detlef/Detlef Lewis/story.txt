After the conflict ended, he returned to Europe and wandered, eventually finding himself in Ravenswood.

Detlef Lewis's story was shrouded in mystery, but one thing was certain: his eyes held a depth that few had ever seen. They seemed to hold secrets, tales of lives lived and loved and lost. As the night wore on, Sarah found herself drawn into Detlef Lewis's world, her imagination sparked by the fires of his past.

Some said he had moved on, leaving Ravenswood behind. Others whispered that he still lurked in the shadows, watching and waiting for the next tale to unfold.
