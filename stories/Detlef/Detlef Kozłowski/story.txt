Detlef Kozłowski was a man shrouded in mystery, his past a labyrinth of secrets and half-truths. A former scientist turned mercenary, he had made a name for himself on the fringes of society, taking on high-stakes missions that required his unique skillset.

But Detlef Kozłowski was undaunted, relying on his quick wit and cunning to stay one step ahead of the guards. He finally found the document, a small USB drive containing sensitive information about a top-secret project codenamed "Eclipse."

As he made his escape, Detlef couldn't shake the feeling that something was off. The client's message had seemed too good to be true, and he wondered if he'd just walked into a trap. He decided to investigate further, using his skills to uncover the truth behind the Eclipse project.
