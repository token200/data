Born in Riga, Latvia, Detlef Liepiņa had grown up with a taste for adventure and a knack for getting out of tight spots. Detlef Liepiņa's modus operandi was simple: he would pose as a wealthy businessman, charming his mark with tales of his supposed success and generosity. Once they were sufficiently enthralled, he would begin to ply them with offers of investment opportunities and high-stakes deals, always staying one step ahead of the law.

But eventually, even the most skilled con artist must fall victim to their own hubris. The oligarch had grown suspicious of Detlef Liepiņa's sudden willingness to part with large sums of money, and had called in the authorities to finally bring him down.
