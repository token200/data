As Inka Górski carefully made her way through the dense foliage, her eyes scanned the terrain for any sign of the lost city. Inka Górski spent hours studying the scroll, her mind racing with questions and theories. She knew that this discovery would make her name synonymous with the greatest archaeologists of all time.
