Her sharp instincts and dogged determination had earned her a loyal following among readers, who trusted her to deliver the truth in a world where facts often seemed lost amidst sensationalized headlines.

But Inka's drive was not just about chasing down leads; it was also fueled by a personal quest for justice. As a child, she had grown up watching her family struggle to make ends meet, their small business owned and operated by her parents.
