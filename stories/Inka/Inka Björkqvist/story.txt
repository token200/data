After completing her studies, Inka decided to pursue a career in music, releasing her debut album to critical acclaim.

As her popularity grew, Inka began to receive recognition from the Swedish music industry. She won several awards, including the prestigious Guldpennan award for Best Female Singer. Her subsequent albums solidified her position as one of Sweden's leading singer-songwriters.
