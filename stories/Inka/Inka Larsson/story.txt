One day, Inka Larsson received an unusual request from her eccentric aunt, who wanted to celebrate her 60th birthday with a medieval-themed party. The catch? The guest list was to include only family members who were no longer in contact with each other.

Inka Larsson was hesitant at first, but her aunt's persistence eventually wore her down. She spent the next few weeks tracking down relatives she had lost touch with over the years and carefully crafting a medieval-inspired menu that would impress even the most discerning palates.

As the big day approached, Inka Larsson found herself growing more and more anxious. What if the party was a disaster? What if her family members didn't get along? But when the guests finally arrived, she was amazed by how seamlessly they all meshed together.
