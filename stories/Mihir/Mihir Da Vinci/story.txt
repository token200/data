Mihir Leonardo da Vinci was more than happy to oblige, using his creativity and resourcefulness to find solutions that benefited everyone. His workshop became a hub of activity, where art and science merged in perfect harmony.

As Mihir continued to innovate and create, he never forgot the wise words of his namesake: "The noblest pleasure is the joy of understanding." With each new discovery, Mihir felt an overwhelming sense of satisfaction, knowing that he was not only creating something beautiful but also contributing to the betterment of society.
