His dedication paid off when he was accepted into the prestigious Symbiosis School of Music, where he honed his craft under the guidance of an esteemed guru.

Mihir's journey as a musician took him to various stages of India, performing at cultural events and festivals, entertaining audiences with his soulful melodies. People would often remark on the depth and emotion that seemed to reverberate through every note he played. As Mihir Hughes's career continued to flourish, he found himself increasingly inspired by Nalini's creative vision. Under their combined guidance, students from all walks of life came to learn the intricacies of Indian classical music, carrying the legacy forward.
