As he grew older, Mihir proved to be just as skilled, quickly rising through the ranks of his family's organization.

But Mihir Salamanca was not without his flaws. His ruthless ambition sometimes led him down a path of destruction, causing ripples in the delicate balance of power that he sought to maintain. Despite these risks, Mihir remained confident in his abilities. He was convinced that he could navigate even the most treacherous waters and emerge victorious on the other side. As the future leader of the Salamanca family, Mihir Salamanca was ready to take on whatever challenges came his way, using all his skills and cunning to achieve greatness.
