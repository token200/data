One night, Carlos found himself at the high-society gala of the year, rubbing shoulders with the city's elite and pretending to be someone he wasn't. He had been hired by a wealthy client to steal a valuable artifact from the host's collection, but something about the evening felt off.

Carlos couldn't resist making a move on her, and soon found himself engaged in a sparkling conversation that left them both breathless. For the first time in his life, Carlos felt like he was being seen – not for what he did, but for who he truly was.

As the night wore on, Carlos and his mysterious companion found themselves lost in each other's eyes. They strolled through the gardens, laughing and talking, and for a moment, Carlos forgot about his life of crime.

As the evening drew to a close, Carlos knew he had to make a choice: continue down his life of thievery, or take a chance on love? For once in his life, Carlos felt like he was being asked to choose between two paths – but which one would lead him to happiness?
