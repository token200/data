Carlos Järvinen took on the case, pouring over old files and interviewing acquaintances of the missing girl. He dug deep into her personal life, uncovering a web of secrets and lies that seemed to lead nowhere. Yet, he persisted, driven by a sense of justice for Sofia's family.

The confrontation was tense, but ultimately, it revealed the shocking truth: Sofia had been silenced permanently by her own brother, in a fit of jealousy and rage.
