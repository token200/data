Carlos Michalska was a man with a passion for music. He had grown up in a small town surrounded by lush green forests and rolling hills, where the sounds of nature were ever-present. As a child, he would often wander into the woods, camera in hand, capturing the beauty of the world around him.

As he grew older, Carlos' love for photography only deepened. As Carlos' relationship with Lily and her band deepened, he found himself inspired by their creativity and enthusiasm.
