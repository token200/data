As she grew older, her talent became increasingly evident, and she began to attract attention from local galleries and collectors.

One day, Élise's big break came when a prominent art critic wrote a glowing review of her latest exhibition. The article, titled "Élise Deschamps: A Star is Born," catapulted her to international fame, and suddenly, her paintings were in high demand. As she traveled the globe, showing her work in museums and galleries, Élise found that her experiences had changed her.
