Élise Molnár stepped off the stage, her eyes scanning the crowded room with a mix of relief and exhaustion. As the lead singer of the indie rock band "Echo Fade," she had just finished a blistering set that left the audience breathless.

Her parents, who had immigrated to Hungary from Romania, had instilled in her a love of music and art that went beyond personal fulfillment.
