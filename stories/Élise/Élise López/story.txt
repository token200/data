Élise López walked through the winding alleys of the Malasaña neighborhood, taking in the colorful street art and eclectic mix of boutiques and cafes. Élise López couldn't resist the infectious rhythms and melodies, and soon found herself dancing alongside the crowd, her long black hair swinging to the beat.
