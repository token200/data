Tiberius smiled warmly as they asked questions about his work, eager to share his passion with others. His mentor, a wise old artist named Takeshi, watched over him with pride, knowing that Tiberius's talent was destined for greatness.

He knew that Tiberius Hiroki had truly found his voice as an artist – one that would echo through the ages.
