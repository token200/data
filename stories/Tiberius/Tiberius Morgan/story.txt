Tiberius Morgan's ears perked up at the mention of the word "truth," and he ushered Sophia into his office with an air of urgency. He began to pace the room, his eyes never leaving hers as he listened intently to her account of Emma's disappearance.

With the truth finally revealed, Sophia was left shaken but grateful to have found answers. As she departed Tiberius Morgan's office, he watched her go with a mix of satisfaction and sadness – for though justice had been served, he knew that the scars of this case would linger on Sophia's heart for a long time to come.

As he settled back into his chair, Tiberius Morgan couldn't help but think about Emma's final message: "I've found the truth." Little did he know, this was only the beginning of another puzzle to unravel, one that would test his mettle as a detective and push him to the limits of human endurance.
