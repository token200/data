Some claimed to have seen him playing the violin in a traveling circus, while others swore they'd seen his sketches displayed in a New York gallery. But one thing was certain: Tiberius KissMats Mitchell was a man of mystery.

One day, a young woman named Lily stumbled upon Tiberius's garden while searching for wild herbs. She was immediately drawn to the eclectic collection of plants and creatures that inhabited the property. As she wandered through the garden, she noticed Tiberius sitting on a bench, watching her with an intensity that made her feel both uneasy and fascinated.

Lily hesitated, unsure if she should trust this stranger. But something about Tiberius's demeanor put her at ease, so she agreed to enter the garden house.

"Thank you for visiting me today," he said, as he handed her a small bag of herbs. "Come back soon, Lily. I'll have more stories to share."
