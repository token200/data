A former spy for the Italian government, he had spent years working undercover, gathering intelligence and manipulating events from behind the scenes. After a falling out with his handlers, Tiberius went into hiding, using his vast network of contacts to stay one step ahead of his pursuers.

It was said that Tiberius had an uncanny ability to blend in, adapting to any situation with ease. He could be a charming aristocrat or a rough-around-the-edges street thug, depending on the context. Few people suspected the true nature of this enigmatic figure, who seemed to always have an ace up his sleeve.

Tiberius Schillaci was known for his wit and charm, often using these traits to extract information from unwilling sources. He had a way with words, a silver tongue that could talk his way out of almost any situation. But beneath the surface, Tiberius seethed with anger and resentment. His years in the shadows had taken their toll, leaving him isolated and alone.
