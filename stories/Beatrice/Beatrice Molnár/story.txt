She remembered the long hours they worked, the sacrifices they made, and the way they always put others before themselves. These lessons stuck with her, and she carried them with her throughout her life.

As Beatrice left the courtroom, she felt a sense of pride and accomplishment wash over her. She knew that this victory was more than just a win for the defendant – it was a victory for justice itself. And as she walked back to her car, her full name echoing in her mind, Beatrice Molnár knew that she had truly found her calling.
