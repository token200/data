As Beatrice grew older, her fascination with art only deepened. Her dedication paid off, and by the time she was in her early twenties, her paintings were being showcased in galleries across the country.

But Beatrice's success came at a price. She became increasingly reclusive, preferring the solitude of her studio to the company of friends and family.
