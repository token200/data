Years went by, and Andris's love for photography only grew stronger. He enrolled in a photography program at a local college, where he honed his skills under the guidance of experienced instructors. After graduating, he began taking on freelance projects, capturing special moments for families, couples, and individuals.

He jumped at the opportunity, spending weeks traveling through countries such as Mexico, Argentina, and Brazil. His photos captured the essence of each place, showcasing vibrant textiles, rich food, and warm hospitality.
