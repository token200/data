As the hours passed, Andris Jain's expertise proved invaluable, helping Maya identify patterns and anomalies in the data. But despite their progress, the trail remained frustratingly unclear. Andris Jain's eyes widened as he realized the scope of the breach: millions of dollars' worth of sensitive data had been stolen, and it was likely just the tip of the iceberg. Maya's fear gave way to determination, and together, they vowed to expose the truth behind the breach, no matter the cost.
