Sun Schulz was a free-spirited artist with a passion for capturing the beauty of nature on canvas. Born Susanna Schulz, she had always been drawn to the light and colors of the world around her. As Sun grew older, her love for art only intensified. She studied fine art in college, honing her skills and developing her unique style. Her paintings were vibrant and full of life, like the sun itself that inspired her name.
