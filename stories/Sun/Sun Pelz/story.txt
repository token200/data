Born as Solaris Elwes Pell, he had always been drawn to the mystical forces that governed the universe. Years passed, and Solaris Elwes Pell grew into a powerful sorcerer, earning the respect and awe of his peers. With his radiant aura and unwavering resolve, he set out to right the wrongs of the world, using his magical abilities to defend the innocent and vanquish evil.

Sun Pelz knew that he had to act swiftly, drawing upon the ancient magic that coursed through his veins like liquid fire. With a wave of his hand and a whispered incantation, he summoned a blast of solar energy that shattered Malumbra's defenses and sent her fleeing in disarray.

As the kingdom's people celebrated their deliverance, Sun Pelz stood tall, his chest heaving with exertion, but his spirit unbroken. He knew that there would always be more battles to fight, more darkness to vanquish, but he was ready. For Solaris Elwes Pell, now known as Sun Pelz, was a beacon of hope in a world beset by shadows.

As the years went by, Sun Pelz's legend grew, and his name became synonymous with bravery and wisdom.
