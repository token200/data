But it was not until she stumbled upon an ancient tome hidden deep within the ruins of a forgotten city that she discovered her true potential. Sun Eglītis spent years studying the arcane rituals and incantations described in the tome. She pored over dusty scrolls, practiced meditation under the starry night sky, and trained tirelessly until her body was honed to perfection. The people of Azura whispered about Sun Eglītis in hushed tones, their reverence and awe tempered only by fear.
