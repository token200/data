She'd snatch them up with excitement, cradling them like precious jewels in her hands. Her studio was a kaleidoscope of textures, each piece telling a story that only she could decipher.

Sun Schafer's artistic journey began as a child, encouraged by her grandmother to explore the world through art. As she grew older, her passion only intensified, leading her to study fine arts in college. But it wasn't until she discovered street art that her true voice emerged – bold, raw, and unapologetic.
