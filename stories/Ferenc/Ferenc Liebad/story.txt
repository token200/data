With its intricate brass mechanism and vibrant glass eyes, The Duchess promised to mesmerize audiences for hours on end.

As Liebad worked tirelessly, his thoughts turned to the patron who had commissioned the automaton: Baroness Regina Szilaghi. A wealthy socialite with a passion for innovation, she was known for her ruthless business tactics and generous philanthropy.
