As a young man, Ferenc had always been fascinated by the mysteries of the universe. He spent countless hours pouring over dusty tomes in the local library, searching for clues about the supernatural forces that seemed to permeate their world. His colleagues often dismissed his theories as mere fantasy, but Ferenc was undeterred.
