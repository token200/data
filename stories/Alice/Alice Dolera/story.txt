One fateful evening, Alice Dolera received a cryptic message from an anonymous source, hinting at a high-profile murder that was about to take place. The victim was none other than prominent businessman, Richard Langley. Intrigued by the challenge, Alice Dolera decided to take on the case.

He had made many enemies, and it seemed likely that one of them would be willing to take his life. With her sharp mind and keen instincts, Alice Dolera set out to unravel the complex web of clues and uncover the truth.

With the mystery solved, Alice Dolera couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and satisfaction.
