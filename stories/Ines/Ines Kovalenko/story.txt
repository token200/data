The police department was stumped, but Ines Kovalenko saw something in Max that no one else did – a glimmer of desperation.

Ines Kovalenko spent hours pouring over the evidence, interviewing witnesses, and scouring the city for clues. She paid close attention to Max's alibi, which seemed suspiciously convenient, and noticed a faint scratch on his hand that didn't quite match the injuries he had claimed to have sustained during a recent accident.

Convinced that Max was hiding something, Ines Kovalenko decided to pay him a visit at his tiny studio in Brooklyn. Over steaming cups of coffee, she probed him about the necklace and his whereabouts on the night it went missing. Max seemed shaken but cooperative, revealing a backstory that made Ines Kovalenko's suspicions all too plausible.
