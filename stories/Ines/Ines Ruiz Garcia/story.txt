Ines's passion for art only grew stronger as she entered adulthood. She went on to study fine arts at a prestigious university, where she honed her skills and developed a unique style that blended traditional techniques with modern twists.

After graduating, Ines Ruiz Garcia set out into the world, determined to make a name for herself as an artist.
