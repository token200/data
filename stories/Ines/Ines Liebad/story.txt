Ines' eyes narrowed, her mind racing with possibilities. She knew that Julian was a player, always chasing the next big score. But she also knew that she had something he didn't: intelligence and cunning. This game of cat and mouse could go either way.

Without another word, Ines extended her hand, and Julian took it in his, their palms touching like a spark on dry tinder. The room around them melted away, leaving only the two of them, poised on the cusp of something big. What lay ahead was anyone's guess - but one thing was certain: with Ines Liebad and Julian Saint Clair together, nothing would ever be ordinary again.
