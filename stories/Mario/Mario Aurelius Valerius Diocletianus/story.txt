As he rode, Mario Aurelius Valerius Diocletianus's mind wandered back to the early days of his reign, when he had ascended to power as a champion of peace and wisdom. The stillness of the oasis was like a balm to his weary soul, reminding him that even in times of turmoil, there lay a deep and abiding peace to be found within.

As night began to fall, Mario Aurelius Valerius Diocletianus reluctantly rose from his seat, mounted his horse once more, and set off towards the fading light.
