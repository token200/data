Years went by, and Ivan's interest in woodworking only grew stronger. He enrolled in a trade school and learned the skills necessary to become a master craftsman. After completing his studies, he set up his own workshop and began taking on small projects for friends and family.

When the table was complete, Ivan Pedersen stood back and admired his creation, feeling proud of what he had accomplished.

The interior designer was equally impressed, and soon Ivan Pedersen's workshop was flooded with requests from all over town. He worked tirelessly to meet the demand, always striving to create pieces that were not only functional but also beautiful and meaningful.
