Ivan Viktorovich Gorski was a man with a passion for photography and a penchant for adventure. As he grew older, Ivan's love of photography only deepened. Ivan was thrilled at the opportunity to join them on their journey, and he packed his bags, camera equipment, and sense of adventure. Over the next few months, he traveled through mountains and valleys, villages and cities, capturing the raw energy and emotion of the musicians as they played.

As Ivan Viktorovich Gorski looked back on the experiences he'd had, he knew that this journey had been a defining moment in his life. He'd seen things most people only dreamed of – from sun-drenched deserts to snowy mountain peaks – and he'd captured them all through the lens of his camera.
