With a hint of amusement, Ignacy agreed to meet Sophia at a local café the following day. As they sipped coffee, Ignacy regaled Sophia with tales of ancient rituals and forgotten traditions. His words painted vivid pictures of Poland's rich cultural heritage, leaving Sophia in awe of Ignacy's vast understanding.

As the night drew to a close, Sophia thanked Ignacy for his time and insight. She left the café with a newfound appreciation for Poland's history, and a sense that she had only scratched the surface of Ignacy Krumina's remarkable expertise. Though he remained elusive, Sophia was determined to share Ignacy's stories with the world.

In the days that followed, Sophia wrote a series of articles about Ignacy's discoveries, cementing his reputation as one of Poland's leading historians. As the news spread, people from all over the country began to seek out Ignacy Krumina, hoping to learn from the enigmatic scholar and uncover secrets hidden in the pages of history.
