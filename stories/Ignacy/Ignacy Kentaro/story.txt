With his reputation on the line, Ignacy Kentaro had accepted the challenge, assembling a team of his best operatives to infiltrate The Mark's heavily guarded fortress and complete the task.

With his plan in place, Ignacy Kentaro set out with his team, navigating through treacherous terrain and avoiding deadly traps set by The Mark's security team. As they closed in on their target, Ignacy Kentaro's team executed their moves with precision, taking down guards one by one until they reached the heart of the fortress.

As the last guard fell, Ignacy Kentaro turned to his team, a hint of satisfaction playing on his lips.
