But after watching Flavius's videos, she wanted to learn how to make more elaborate dishes, like curries and biryanis.

As the weeks went by, Leela's confidence in the kitchen grew, and she began to experiment with new ingredients and flavors.

Thanks to Flavius's guidance, Leela discovered a newfound love for cooking and decided to pursue it as a career.
