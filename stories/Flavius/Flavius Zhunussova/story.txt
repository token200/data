He had been hired by a rival collector to steal the necklace, and he planned to do it without getting caught. He spent weeks studying Mrs. Pembroke's habits and gathering information about her security system.

The night of the heist arrived, and Flavius set to work. He snuck into Mrs. Pembroke's mansion through an open window, avoiding the guards and making his way to the display case where the necklace was kept. With a flick of his wrist, he released the lock and carefully removed the necklace from its resting place.

As he made his escape, Flavius couldn't help but think about how far he had come. From a poor orphan on the streets of Verona to one of the most notorious thieves in Europe, Flavius Zhunussova had achieved greatness through his own hard work and determination.

But Flavius's success didn't go unnoticed for long. A young detective named Detective Lorenzo had been tracking him for months, and he finally had a lead on his whereabouts. Flavius knew that he had to move fast if he wanted to stay one step ahead of the law.
