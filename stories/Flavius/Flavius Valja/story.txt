He devoured books on mythology and folklore, often sneaking into the local library to pour over dusty tomes late into the night. It wasn't long before he became obsessed with uncovering the truth behind the myths and legends of old.

As night fell, they huddled around a table, pouring over ancient artifacts and deciphering long-lost languages.
