Flavius smiled, feeling a sense of gratitude wash over him. "Thank you, ma'am," he replied, his accent thick with the Polish lilt.

Over the next few weeks, Jadwiga became a regular fixture in Flavius Piotrowska's life. She would sit by his side as he worked on new pieces, offering words of encouragement and guidance. Together, they formed an unlikely bond, one that transcended age and culture.

As Flavius Piotrowska looked at the city through Jadwiga's eyes, he saw a world of beauty and resilience. He knew that his art had the power to inspire, to heal, and to bring people together.
