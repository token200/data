Stefano Charrier's instincts told him to be cautious, but his curiosity got the better of him. He arrived at the museum, his heart pounding with anticipation, just as the clock struck six. L. was nowhere to be seen, but a small note on the reception desk read: "Look again."

He scoured the galleries, questioning staff and patrons alike, until finally, he stumbled upon a hidden room deep within the museum's labyrinthine corridors.
