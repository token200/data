Tonight, Stefano Fournier was here to review the latest album from the enigmatic singer-songwriter, Luna Nightingale.

There were hipsters sipping craft beers and wannabe artists scribbling notes on their sketchpads, all united by their love of music. He spotted a familiar face in the crowd - none other than Luna Nightingale herself, resplendent in a flowing red gown that seemed to shimmer under the stage lights.

Stefano Fournier took his seat and began to scribble down notes on his pad, his trained ear picking out the subtle nuances of Luna's voice. This was why he did what he did - to share the magic of music with the world.
