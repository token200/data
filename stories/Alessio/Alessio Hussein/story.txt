As he traveled through the desert landscapes and bustling streets of Libya, Alessio met countless individuals who were struggling to survive in a country torn apart by conflict.

Alessio Hussein's reporting was raw, honest, and unflinching. He captured the stories of refugees who had lost everything – homes, families, identities – and told them with compassion and empathy.
