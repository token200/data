His grandmother, whom he affectionately referred to as "Nonna," had fallen ill, and he had returned to Sweden to be by her side. Despite their distance, the two were incredibly close, and Alessio knew that she would need him now more than ever.

As he approached the family's old cabin, a warm smile spread across his face. This was where Alessio Bergström had spent countless hours as a child, playing in the woods and helping his father with their small farm. As he sat by her bedside, holding her hand, he felt a deep sense of peace wash over him. This was where he belonged – surrounded by love, nature, and the people who mattered most to him.
