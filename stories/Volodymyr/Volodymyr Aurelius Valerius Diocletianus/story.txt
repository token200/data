One fateful night, as Diocletianus sat by a roaring fire in his imperial chambers, he pondered the mysteries of life and death. His full name, Volodymyr Aurelius Valerius Diocletianus, echoed through his mind like a mantra - a reminder of the weight of responsibility that rested upon his shoulders.
