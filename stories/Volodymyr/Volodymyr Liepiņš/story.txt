However, by night, Volodymyr Liepiņš transformed into a master cake decorator. As Volodymyr Liepiņš's reputation grew, so did his client list. He was asked to create custom cakes for weddings, birthdays, and even corporate events. Volodymyr Liepiņš was nervous but determined to prove himself.

From that day on, Volodymyr Liepiņš's reputation soared. His bakery became the talk of the town, and his cakes were sought after by people from all over the city. He continued to work tirelessly, perfecting his craft and pushing himself to new heights. Years later, Volodymyr Liepiņš's bakery remained the heart of Riga's culinary scene, a testament to his hard work and dedication. And whenever someone asked him about his secret to success, he would simply smile and say, "It's all about following your passion and never giving up on your dreams."
