Despite his tough exterior, Volodymyr had a soft spot for those in need. He had taken on jobs that involved helping those who couldn't defend themselves, earning a reputation as a lone wolf with a heart of gold.

Volodymyr's eyes narrowed as he took in the situation. With a quiet efficiency, he dispatched the thugs, using his training to disable them without causing permanent harm. As the dust settled, Thompson thanked him profusely, offering Volodymyr a hefty sum of money for his services.

With the job complete, Volodymyr collected his payment and slipped out into the night, disappearing into the shadows like a ghost. Few knew much about Volodymyr Szymanska, but one thing was certain: when you needed someone to get the job done, he was the man to call.
