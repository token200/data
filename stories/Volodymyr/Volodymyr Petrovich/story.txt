People from all over the city came to seek his advice, and Volodymyr, humbled by the attention, continued to work behind the scenes, helping those in need.

Years later, when people looked back on Volodymyr Petrovich's life, they remembered not only his impressive achievements but also the impact he had on the lives of others.
