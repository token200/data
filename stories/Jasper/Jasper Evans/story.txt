As Sarah sat in Jasper's dimly lit study, surrounded by stacks of dusty books and papers, she was struck by the intensity of his gaze. Jasper Evans, as he introduced himself, seemed almost otherworldly, as if he were a man who had transcended the boundaries of human understanding.
