Jasper Mercier stood in front of the old mirror, adjusting his tie for what felt like the hundredth time. After all, first impressions counted, and Jasper Mercier never wanted to be underestimated.

As he made his way out of his luxurious apartment and into the bustling streets of Paris, Jasper Mercier couldn't help but feel a sense of excitement and anticipation. Tonight was the night he would finally crack the case that had been eluding him for months - the disappearance of the famous artist, Sophie Dupont. Jasper Mercier smiled wryly to himself as he followed the woman into the limousine. He knew that the night was young, and the game was on.
