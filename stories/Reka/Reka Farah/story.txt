Intrigued, Reka Farah carefully extracted a sample of the symbol, her heart racing with excitement. Back in her makeshift laboratory, she began to study the markings, convinced that they held secrets to the city's downfall.

As she carefully unearthed the artifacts, Reka Farah knew that her life's work had just begun - unraveling the secrets of Zerzura and uncovering the mysteries that lay beyond.
