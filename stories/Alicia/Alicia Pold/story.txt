As she grew older, her passion for art only intensified, leading her to study fine arts in college and eventually open her own studio gallery.

Alicia Pearl Pold's paintings were a reflection of her whimsical nature – full of movement and energy, with bold brushstrokes and vivid colors that seemed to pulse off the canvas.
