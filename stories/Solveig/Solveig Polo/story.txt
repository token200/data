She spent countless hours interviewing suspects, searching for any lead that might point her in the right direction. Her piercing eyes seemed to bore into the souls of those she questioned, as if searching for a glimmer of truth.

Solveig's team worked tirelessly alongside her, providing valuable insights and support. Together, they combed through the crime scene, analyzing every detail, no matter how small it seemed. Solveig Polo's instincts proved invaluable as she pieced together seemingly unrelated fragments of evidence.
