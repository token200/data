Her uncanny ability to blend into any environment, coupled with her razor-sharp intellect, made her a valuable tool for gathering intelligence and executing high-stakes missions.

Asel Eglitis was tasked with uncovering the mole and putting an end to their nefarious plans. With a quiet confidence born of years of experience, she set out on her mission.

As she traversed the labyrinthine corridors of The Order's headquarters, Asel Eglitis navigated through a web of deceit and deception. She played the part of a loyal operative, all the while searching for any hint of treachery.
