As she grew older, Asel Zarina's abilities became more pronounced. She could summon a flock of starlings with a flick of her wrist or calm a frightened rabbit with a gentle touch. One fateful evening, a young boy wandered into the woods, lost and scared. Asel Zarina sensed his distress and followed the sound of his whimpering to a small clearing. There, she found the boy huddled beside a wounded rabbit. With a gentle touch, Asel Zarina coaxed the rabbit back to health and helped the boy calm down.

As the sun dipped below the horizon, the townspeople emerged from their homes, drawn by the commotion. They watched in awe as Asel Zarina healed the rabbit and comforted the lost child. For the first time, they saw beyond her enigmatic facade and glimpsed the kind soul beneath.

From that day forward, Asel Zarina was no longer an outcast. The people of Ravenshire began to respect her gifts, if not fully understand them. And Asel Zarina, once a solitary figure, found herself woven into the fabric of their community – a true friend to all who knew her.
