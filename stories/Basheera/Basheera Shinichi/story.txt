Over the next few weeks, Basheera returned to Midnight Dreams several times, each time getting closer to unraveling its secrets. As the days turned into nights, Basheera found herself torn between her duty as a journalist and her growing feelings for Kaito. She knew that if she exposed Midnight Dreams, it would mean ruining the lives of those who called it home. But she also knew that hiding the truth would be just as damaging.
