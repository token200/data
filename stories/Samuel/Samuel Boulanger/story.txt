Born into a family of adventurers, Samuel's curiosity was fueled from a young age, often sneaking out of the house to explore the surrounding woods.

His peers began to call him Dr. Boulanger, out of respect for his intelligence and dedication.

As he carefully documented and studied the new species, Samuel couldn't help but think back to his childhood adventures in the woods.

For all his success, Samuel never lost sight of what truly mattered: the pursuit of knowledge and the joy of discovery. He continued to explore the world, always on the lookout for the next great find. And though he faced countless challenges along the way, Samuel Boulanger remained steadfast, driven by a passion that would never fade.
