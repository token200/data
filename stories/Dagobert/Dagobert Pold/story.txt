Despite his somewhat eccentric lifestyle, Dagobert was a kind soul, and many of the townspeople would come to him with their strange problems and concerns. Whether it was a ghostly apparition or an unexplained phenomenon, Dagobert Pold was always eager to lend a listening ear and offer his expertise.

One stormy night, as a fierce thunderstorm rolled in over the town, Dagobert was summoned by the local vicar to investigate a reported haunting at the old windmill on the outskirts of town. As he approached the creaking door of the abandoned mill, he felt a shiver run down his spine.
