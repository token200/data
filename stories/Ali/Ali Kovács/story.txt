Born Ali Kovács to a family of Hungarian immigrants, Ali's love affair with the natural world began at a young age. Ali's latest expedition had been years in the making, and she was determined to uncover its secrets. She had assembled a team of experts, including her trusted colleague and friend, Dr. Péter Nagy. It was going to be a long night, but Ali Kovács was ready for whatever lay ahead.
