Ali Szabó had always been a free spirit, never one to be tied down by convention or tradition. Growing up in Budapest, Hungary, she found solace in the vibrant streets of the city's Jewish quarter, where her ancestors had once thrived.

As a young adult, Ali Szabó became enamored with the world of art, and after completing her studies at the Academy of Fine Arts, she set out to make a name for herself as an artist. Her passion for capturing the beauty of Budapest's streets and people led her to create stunning works that reflected the city's rich history and culture.

Years went by, and Ali Szabó's talent earned her recognition from galleries and collectors around the world.
