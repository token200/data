Despite the attention, Ali remained focused on his goal of creating a better world. He continued to work tirelessly, pouring over research papers and meeting with world leaders to advocate for policy changes.

As Sophia looked up to Ali Jørgensen as a hero and role model, she knew that she wanted to follow in his footsteps and make a difference in the world. And Ali, well, he was content in the knowledge that his work would live on long after he was gone, inspiring future generations to take action and protect the planet.
