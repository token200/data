In Willow Creek, Lars found solace in the quiet rhythms of small-town life. He would spend hours walking along the riverbank, watching the stars twinkle to life at night. The townsfolk had grown accustomed to seeing him around, but few knew much about Lars Shu-fen, the enigmatic stranger with piercing green eyes and jet-black hair.

With an air of nonchalance, he listened to Sophie's story, his eyes never leaving hers. When she finished speaking, he offered a cryptic smile and a whispered phrase: "Look to the shadows, child."

Sophie left with more questions than answers, but Lars knew exactly what he had meant. He vanished into the night, leaving behind only a whispered promise: "The truth is hidden in plain sight." From that day on, Sophie kept an eye out for Lars Shu-fen, knowing that whenever she needed guidance, her mysterious friend would appear, like a ghostly apparition, to point her in the right direction.
