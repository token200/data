But despite the growth, there was still a sense of community here that Emma adored. Everyone knew each other's names, and strangers were few and far between.

They chatted quietly for a while, catching up on old times and sharing stories about their lives since Emma's departure.

As the afternoon wore on, Emma felt a sense of belonging wash over her once more. This was where she belonged – in this small town surrounded by people who knew and loved her.
