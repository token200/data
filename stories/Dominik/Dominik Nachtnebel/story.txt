Dominik's grip on the railing tightened as he digested Sophia's words. This case just got a whole lot more complicated.

Sophia's eyes darted around the rooftop before focusing intently on Dominik Nachtnebel. "I'll take you to see someone who can tell you more," she said, her voice dripping with an air of mystery. "But be warned, Dominik Nachtnebel - once we start down this path, there's no turning back."
