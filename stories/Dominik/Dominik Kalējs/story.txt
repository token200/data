Dominik Kalējs, a man with a keen eye for detail and a sharp mind for deduction, was determined to crack the case. He spent hours pouring over security footage, interviewing witnesses, and examining the stolen items. But every lead seemed to end in a dead-end, leaving him frustrated and puzzled.

Dominik's eyes narrowed as he stared at the small note on his desk. The words seemed innocent enough - but what if they were more than just a taunt? He pulled out his magnifying glass and began to examine the handwriting more closely. It was sloppy, almost childlike in its execution. But there was something about it that didn't add up.

Suddenly, Dominik's mind clicked into place. The thief wasn't trying to be clever - they were trying to hide their own nervousness. And what if the notes weren't just random scribbles? What if they were a clue?
