Her work not only raised awareness about critical conservation issues but also inspired a new generation of environmentalists and photographers.

Farah's dedication to her craft and her passion for storytelling had taken her on an incredible journey, one that would continue to shape her perspective on the world and drive her to make a positive impact on it.
