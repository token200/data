But behind the scenes, Farah's life was a little more complicated. She had a reputation for being fiercely loyal to her friends and family, but also had a tendency to push people away when things got too intense. It was a delicate balance she struggled to maintain, often finding herself on both sides of the line at once.

Despite this, Farah's social calendar was always full. From high-society weddings to underground music festivals, she knew everyone and everyone knew her name. Her sharp instincts and quick thinking had earned her a reputation as one of the city's most trusted advisors when it came to all things party-related.
