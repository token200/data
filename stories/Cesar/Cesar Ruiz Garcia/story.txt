After completing culinary school, Cesar Ruiz Garcia decided to take his skills to the big city, where he landed a job at a trendy restaurant in Madrid. It was there that he honed his craft, experimenting with new flavors and techniques while maintaining the traditional roots of Spanish cuisine.

Cesar Ruiz Garcia was more than just a talented chef – he was a keeper of traditions, a weaver of flavors, and a storyteller in the kitchen.
