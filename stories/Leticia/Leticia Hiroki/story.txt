As she ventured deeper into the forest, Leticia's excitement grew. She stumbled upon a previously unknown species of orchid, its delicate petals swaying gently in the breeze. With trembling hands, she carefully collected a sample, her heart racing with excitement.

Leticia Hiroki couldn't help but think back to her grandmother, who had instilled in her a love for plants that had only grown stronger over the years.
