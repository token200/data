Leticia Ming, also known as Zhang Ming, was a renowned artist in Beijing. Born to a family of modest means, Leticia's passion for art was nurtured by her mother, who encouraged her to explore her creative side.

As a child, Leticia spent hours sketching the vibrant street scenes of Beijing, mesmerized by the colors and textures of the city's ancient walls, temples, and markets. Her father, a skilled carpenter, taught her the intricacies of traditional Chinese architecture, which later influenced her unique blend of modern and cultural elements in her art.

Leticia's talent earned her a scholarship to study at the prestigious China Central Academy of Fine Arts. It was there that she honed her skills under the guidance of esteemed artists, experimenting with diverse mediums and techniques. Her bold and expressive works quickly gained recognition, both domestically and internationally.
