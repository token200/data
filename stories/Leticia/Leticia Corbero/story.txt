Leticia Corbedo was a woman with a passion for art and a heart full of creativity. She spent most of her days lost in the world of colors and shapes, trying to capture the beauty of life on canvas. As she painted, Leticia's mind wandered to her childhood dreams, where she longed to be a famous artist.

Her parents, though not art enthusiasts themselves, encouraged her creative pursuits, recognizing the joy it brought her.
