Sofia grinned and began to tinker with the device, revealing a miniature drone that hovered above the table. "I thought you'd want to see the exhibit up close," she said, winking at Elina.

Without Sofia by her side, she wouldn't have made it this far in life. And now, as they prepared to infiltrate the gallery and pull off a daring heist, Elina knew that Sofia would be the one holding everything together.
