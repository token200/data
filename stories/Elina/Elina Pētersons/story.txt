As Elina grew older, her interest in mythology only deepened. As the sun began to set, Elina felt a sense of peace wash over her, as if she had finally found what she had been searching for all along.

From that day on, Elina Pētersons devoted herself to unraveling the mysteries of mythology and folklore.
