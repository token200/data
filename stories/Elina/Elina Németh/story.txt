Elina Németh stepped onto the worn wooden floorboards of the old tavern, her eyes scanning the dimly lit room with a mix of curiosity and wariness. As a skilled thief and member of the notorious Shadowhand Clan, she had encountered her fair share of shady characters and dubious establishments.

With a flick of her dark brown hair, Elina Németh made her way to the bar, where the bartender eyed her with a mixture of suspicion and familiarity. "The usual, Elina," he said gruffly, sliding a foamy mug of ale down the bar towards her.

Elina Németh took a sip of the cool liquid, feeling the familiar burn of it coursing down her throat as she surveyed the room. The patrons were a rough-looking bunch, with scars and tattoos aplenty, but Elina's trained eyes picked out a few potential targets for her next heist.

As she sat there, sipping her ale and watching the room, Elina Németh's thoughts turned to her latest mark – a wealthy merchant who had recently acquired a rare and valuable artifact. She had been casing him for weeks, gathering information and waiting for the perfect moment to strike.

Just then, a hooded figure slipped onto the stool beside her, their eyes locked on hers with an unnerving intensity. "You're looking for something, Elina Németh," they said, their voice low and gravelly. "Something that's worth risking everything for."

Elina Németh raised an eyebrow, intrigued despite herself. "What makes you think I'm after something valuable?" she asked, her hand resting on the dagger at her belt.

The hooded figure leaned in closer, their breath whispering against Elina Németh's ear. "I know what you're looking for," they whispered. "And I can help you get it – for a price."
