As the sun began to set, casting a golden glow over the dig site, Elina couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and accomplishment. Her full name, Elina Ming-che, seemed fitting – "ming" meaning "bright" or "shining," a reference to her own shining career in archaeology.

As the team packed up their tools for the day, Elina's thoughts turned to the next phase of the excavation. She knew that uncovering the truth about Guanyin's spirit would be a challenge, but she was undaunted. With years of experience and a sharp mind, Elina Ming-che was ready to take on whatever secrets the temple held.

The team settled in for the night, exhausted but exhilarated by their progress. As Elina walked back to her tent, the sounds of the jungle lull her into a well-deserved rest.
