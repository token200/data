As the dust settled, Indrek quickly sprang into action, using his knowledge of structural integrity to help rescue those trapped beneath the rubble.

As word of Indrek Simon's heroics spread, he found himself in high demand. The government asked for his expertise to design a new system for distributing emergency aid to affected areas, and local businesses clamored for his services as they tried to rebuild their damaged facilities.
