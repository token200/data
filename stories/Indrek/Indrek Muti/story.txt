But the truth was that Indrek Muti was very much alive, always staying one step ahead of his pursuers.

As he grew older, Indrek Muti began to take on more daring heists, always pushing the limits of what was thought possible.

Undaunted, Indrek Muti crafted an elaborate plan to infiltrate the mansion and steal the prized gemstone, the Starlight Seren.

Indrek's plan came together flawlessly, with him dodging guards and making his way past security cameras undetected.
