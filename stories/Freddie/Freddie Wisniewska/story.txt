As a freelance journalist, Freddie had made a name for himself by sniffing out stories that no one else dared to touch. His sources were legion, and his instincts were uncanny. He could smell a rat from a mile away, and he always knew how to get to the bottom of things.

Despite the risks, Freddie couldn't resist the allure of the story. He dove headfirst into the world of high-stakes art theft, determined to see justice served.
