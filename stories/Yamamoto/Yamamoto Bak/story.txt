Bak's eyes gleamed like polished onyx as he navigated through the labyrinthine dunes, searching for the fabled Oasis of Eldarath. Legends spoke of its restorative properties, capable of healing even the most grievous wounds. The Golden Blade had lost a vital limb in battle three moons ago and sought the oasis's magic to restore it.

As they traversed the unforgiving terrain, Kaito by his side, Bak encountered roving bands of bandits. With each swift stroke of his blade, their numbers dwindled, and the air grew thick with the scent of smoke and defeat.
