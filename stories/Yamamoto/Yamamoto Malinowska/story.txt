His mind reeled as he realized that Yamamoto Malinowska was not just a master manipulator – she was also a chameleon, able to blend seamlessly into the crowd while hiding her true intentions behind a mask of innocence.

Kenji's sanity began to fray as Malinowska's gaze held him captive. He knew then that he had to escape, but his feet seemed rooted to the spot.
