Réka Jansons was a young and talented artist living in Budapest, Hungary. Her passion for painting and drawing had been evident since she was a child, spending hours sketching the beauty of her hometown. As she grew older, Réka's artistic skills only improved, and she decided to pursue a career in fine arts.
