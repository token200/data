Sasha Balode stepped out of the crowded bus and onto the rain-soaked streets of Riga, Latvia. Sasha Balode couldn't help but laugh as she clapped along with the music. She had always loved the energy of traditional Latvian dance, and this young man was bringing it back to life. The streets were now empty, but the cafe still pulsed with energy. Sasha Balode felt like she had stumbled upon a secret world, hidden beneath the surface of this bustling city. As she finished her coffee, Janis walked her out into the night air, the stars twinkling above them like diamonds.
