Sasha Kamiński stepped out of her small apartment and into the crisp morning air. As she gazed up at the grey sky, she couldn't help but feel a sense of restlessness wash over her. She was a photographer, always chasing the next great shot, but lately, everything seemed dull and uninspiring.
