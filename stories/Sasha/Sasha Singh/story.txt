With her bold brushstrokes and vivid colors, Sasha's artwork transported viewers to a world of serenity and wonder.

Sasha Ramesh Singh was born in Mumbai, India, where she grew up surrounded by the sights and sounds of the bustling city. The studio's owner, a wise and aged artist named Kumar, took Sasha under his wing and taught her the intricacies of traditional Indian painting techniques.

Despite her success, Sasha remained humble and true to her roots, often incorporating elements of Indian culture and mythology into her work.

She was torn between the financial opportunity and her desire to keep her art accessible to a wider audience.
