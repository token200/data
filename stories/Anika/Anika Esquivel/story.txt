Anika Esquivel was no stranger to feeling like an outsider in her small town. Growing up as a Latina in a predominantly white community had its challenges, but Anika's resilience and determination had allowed her to thrive despite the odds.

As she navigated her senior year of high school, Anika found solace in her art. She spent most of her free time sketching and painting, lost in a world of color and creativity. Her passion for art was contagious, and soon she had amassed a loyal following of classmates who would eagerly wait for her next project.

With the help of her supportive best friend, Maria, Anika pushed aside her doubts and prepared for the big night. As she set up her easel and displayed her artwork, she felt a sense of pride and accomplishment wash over her.
