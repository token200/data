Over the next few hours, Anika Alsbach revealed a brilliant mind that had spent years working tirelessly to unravel the secrets of the universe. Her passion for discovery was contagious, inspiring Sarah to see the world in a new light. As they parted ways, Anika smiled warmly at Sarah, grateful for the chance to share her work with someone who truly understood its significance.
