Despite the backlash, Anika's reporting had sparked a heated debate about the true intentions behind the trade agreement. Her colleagues praised her bravery and tenacity, calling her a true champion of journalism. Anika Lawniczak had once again proven herself to be a force to be reckoned with in the world of investigative reporting.
