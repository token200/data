The thrill of that discovery still coursed through her veins like adrenaline.

Anika Arias's father, Carlos, a retired teacher, leaned forward as he asked his daughter about her latest findings. "Tell us more about this temple you discovered," he urged, his eyes gleaming with curiosity.
