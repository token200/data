Determined to get to the bottom of the story, Verónica Li set out to investigate, talking to doctors, nurses, and residents who had fallen ill. She spent long hours poring over medical records and conducting interviews, piecing together a complex web of clues that hinted at a larger conspiracy.

The revelation sparked outrage in Ravenswood, and Verónica Li became an overnight sensation as a hero of investigative journalism.
