Her groundbreaking research on the unique properties of a certain distant planet had earned her international recognition and accolades.

But Verónica's passion wasn't just about advancing scientific knowledge – it was also deeply personal. Growing up in a small town with limited resources, she had always felt like an outsider among her peers, who seemed content to follow traditional paths.
