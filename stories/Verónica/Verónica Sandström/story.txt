Verónica listened intently, her mind racing with possibilities. She took more notes and asked follow-up questions, her journalistic instincts on high alert.

When she finally returned to her apartment, Verónica sat down at her desk, surrounded by stacks of papers and empty coffee cups. It was going to be a long night, but she was ready for the challenge. After all, Verónica Sandström was not one to back down from a fight – not when it came to uncovering the truth.
