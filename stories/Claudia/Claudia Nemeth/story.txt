The stunning Gothic spires of St. Vitus Cathedral rose majestically above the rooftops, their intricate stone carvings glistening in the morning light. For Claudia Nemeth, the pursuit of knowledge was an all-consuming passion, driven by a burning curiosity about the world's most mysterious past.
