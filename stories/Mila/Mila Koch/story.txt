Mila Koch stepped off the train and onto the worn platform, her eyes scanning the small town of Willow Creek. What would she find here? Would anyone accept her?

Mila Koch had always been an outsider, never quite fitting in with her peers or family. Her parents had encouraged her to explore her artistic side, but even that had brought her little success. She was a talented painter, but no one seemed to care about the works of a small-town girl.

She knew she didn't blend in easily, not with her wild hair and paint-splattered clothes. But there was something about this place that drew her in, something that made her feel like maybe, just maybe, she had finally found a home.
