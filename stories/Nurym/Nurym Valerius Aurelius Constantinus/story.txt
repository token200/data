Nurym's journey took him to the great city of Constantinople, where he was met with both awe and suspicion. As he walked through the bustling streets, he left behind a trail of transformed lives. Years went by, and Nurym's legend grew. People began to call him Constantinus, a name that echoed through history as a symbol of hope and courage. And though he walked among mortals, his spirit soared higher than any star in the night sky, guiding those who sought the light in darkness.
