As he began working on the forgery, Nurym's mind wandered back to his humble beginnings. Born and raised in a small village in Eastern Europe, he had always been fascinated by art and craftsmanship. However, it wasn't until he stumbled upon a forgery kit that he discovered his true calling.

Years of honing his skills had made Nurym Kovács nearly unbeatable at creating flawless replicas. His reputation as a master forger continued to grow, with some even calling him the best in the business.
