Nurym Becker was a name that commanded attention in the small town of Ashwood. She was the new owner of the local bakery, and her reputation as a master pastry chef had preceded her.

But there was also a sense of nervousness, knowing that she would be expected to live up to the high standards set by the previous owners.

Nurym smiled warmly, feeling a sense of relief wash over her. It was good to have someone to share the experience with. "Thanks, Em," she replied. "I'm thrilled to be here."

Despite the initial jitters, Nurym was in her element, moving with ease and confidence as she worked. She knew that she had made the right decision in leaving her old life behind and taking a chance on Ashwood.
