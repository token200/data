As he grew older, Quirin's love for music only deepened, and he began to explore other forms of creative expression.

Under Carlos's guidance, Quirin discovered a new side of himself and began to create vibrant works of art that reflected his love for music.

As Quirin's skills as both musician and artist continued to grow, he decided it was time to share his talents with the world. He packed up his guitar and paints and set off on a journey to Los Angeles, determined to make a name for himself in the competitive city of artists and musicians.
