But despite her tough exterior, Quirin Briedis harbored a deep vulnerability. She had lost loved ones to the cruel whims of fate, and her heart was heavy with the weight of her past. Her relationships were few and fleeting, as she struggled to trust those around her.

One night, Quirin received a message from an unknown sender, inviting her to meet at an abandoned windmill on the outskirts of town. The note read: "Come alone. I know your secret." Quirin's instincts screamed warning, but her curiosity got the better of her. She snuck out of the shadows, her hand resting on the hilt of her dagger.
