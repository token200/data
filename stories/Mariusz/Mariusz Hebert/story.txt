Mariusz Hebert listened intently as Janek recounted the events leading up to the vase's disappearance. He noted every detail, from the argument between Janek's sister and their niece to the suspicious behavior of a new staff member at the family-owned business.

With his sharp mind and keen instincts, Mariusz Hebert set out to solve the mystery. He spent countless hours interviewing suspects, analyzing clues, and piecing together the puzzle. His persistence paid off when he discovered a hidden safe in the attic, containing a cryptic message that led him to the true culprit.

With the truth revealed, Mariusz Hebert ensured that justice was served, and the family heirloom was returned.

As Janek thanked him for his help, Mariusz Hebert smiled with satisfaction, knowing he had solved another case. His reputation as one of Warsaw's top detectives was solidified, and his skills were in high demand.
