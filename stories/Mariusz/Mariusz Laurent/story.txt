Mariusz Laurent was a man with a passion for art and music. Born in Warsaw, Poland, he grew up surrounded by the city's vibrant cultural scene. As a child, he spent hours exploring the city's museums and galleries, mesmerized by the works of the Old Masters.

The months that followed were a whirlwind of creativity and hard work. Mariusz spent long hours in the studio, perfecting his craft and pushing the boundaries of what was possible with his music. His dedication paid off when his album was released to critical acclaim.
