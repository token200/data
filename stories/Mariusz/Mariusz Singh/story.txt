Mariusz Singh's past was a labyrinth of deception and betrayal, but he had long since left those days behind. He spent his days tending to his garden, where he grew rare and exotic plants that fascinated the locals. His evenings were filled with painting, as he poured his emotions onto canvas in vibrant colors.

As she browsed the canvases on display, Mariusz Singh noticed her lingering gaze on one particular piece – a stunning portrait of a forest at dusk.

Despite his rugged demeanor, there was an air of vulnerability surrounding him that put her at ease.

Sophia was captivated by Mariusz Singh's charisma and charm.
