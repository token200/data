His latest destination was a small town in rural France, where he hoped to find inspiration for his next painting.

Mariusz Dubois, a Polish artist known for his captivating landscapes and seascapes, had been on the road for weeks. He carried an easel and paints with him wherever he went, always seeking out new sources of light and color to fuel his creativity. Mariusz Dubois didn't notice; he was too busy taking in the scene before him.

It was moments like these that he felt most at home.
