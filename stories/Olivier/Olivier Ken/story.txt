Over the next few weeks, Olivier spent every spare moment at Maison de Musique, learning to play the piano and teaching himself how to compose music. His talent for art was still evident in his music, but now it took on a new level of depth and emotion.

As Olivier Jacques Ken continued on his journey, he knew that he would always be drawn to both art and music.
