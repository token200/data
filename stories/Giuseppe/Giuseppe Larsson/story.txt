Giuseppe Larsson's mind was racing with possibilities as he continued his investigation. He spent hours poring over old photographs, searching for any connections between the mysterious occurrences and an unknown individual. As night began to fall, Giuseppe Larsson decided to stake out Blackstone's mansion, determined to uncover the truth behind the eerie events.
