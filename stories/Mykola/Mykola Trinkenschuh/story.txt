She had heard whispers of Mykola's remarkable abilities and sought him out for a pair of shoes that would protect her on her perilous journey through the Carpathian Mountains. As she entered the shop, Mykola looked up from his workbench, his eyes twinkling with curiosity.

Mykola nodded, a hint of a smile on his lips. "They will serve you well, young one. May the road rise up to meet you."
