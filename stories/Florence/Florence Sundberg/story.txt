With her bright blonde hair tied up in a messy bun and a perpetual smudge of flour on her cheek, Florence exuded warmth and friendliness.

Her bakery was more than just a place to grab a quick breakfast; it was a gathering spot for the community. People came from all over to catch up on the latest gossip and news while Florence worked her magic.

Despite her busy schedule, Florence made time for her beloved grandmother, Agnes. Every Sunday, Florence would visit Agnes at her nursing home, where they'd spend hours chatting over steaming cups of coffee and sharing stories about their lives.
