Egor Viktorovich Kiss was a man shrouded in mystery, with an air of enigma that surrounded him like a veil. Few people knew much about his past, and even fewer had ever seen him smile. His piercing green eyes seemed to bore into those he met, as if searching for secrets hidden beneath the surface.

It was here, amidst the decay and neglect, that he discovered his true calling – as an urban explorer and collector of the macabre.

Some said he was a thief, while others claimed he was a treasure hunter, driven by a desire to uncover secrets that lay hidden beneath the surface.

Egor's fascination with death and decay only grew stronger as he aged. He became an expert in the occult, delving deep into ancient rituals and forbidden knowledge. Despite the whispers and rumors, Egor remained a enigma. His eyes seemed to hold a deep sadness, as if he knew secrets that no one else could comprehend.

As the sun dipped below the horizon, Egor would vanish into the night, his footsteps echoing through the deserted streets like a ghostly procession. Some said he was searching for something more than treasure – something lost in the depths of his own troubled past. Others claimed he was simply chasing the next thrill, the next fix of adrenaline that coursed through his veins like liquid fire.
