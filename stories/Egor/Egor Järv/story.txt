Egor's instincts told him that this was no ordinary meeting, and he couldn't resist the urge to investigate.

With a nod, he and Sasha formed an uneasy alliance, bound together by a shared past and a determination to protect those they cared about. As the night wore on, Egor felt his old instincts stirring once more, and he knew that he was ready to face whatever lay ahead, as long as it meant keeping those around him safe.
