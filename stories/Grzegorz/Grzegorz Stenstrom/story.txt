Despite his dedication to the craft, Grzegorz's own life seemed to be unraveling. His relationships were few and far between, and those he did have often ended in messy breakups. Grzegorz Stenstrom's eyes scanned the room, feeling a chill run down his spine as if Janina could be standing beside him at any moment.
