Aslanov knew that he needed to tread carefully - Anna was no ordinary journalist. She had a way of sniffing out secrets, of unearthing truths that others would rather keep buried.

As the night drew to a close, Aslanov made his move. He offered Anna a ride in his sleek black limousine, and as they sped through the city streets, their conversation turned from small talk to high-stakes negotiations.

In that moment, Grzegorz Aslanov knew that he had found a new game to play - one that would challenge him, test his limits, and push him to the edge.
