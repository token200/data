Her parents, though not artists themselves, encouraged her passion from a young age, recognizing the fire that burned within their daughter.

As she grew older, Maia's talents became more refined, and she began to showcase her work in local galleries and exhibitions. It was there that she found solace, escaping into the melodies and harmonies of her favorite artists.

One evening, while performing at a local music club, Maia caught the attention of a prominent music producer. He offered her a recording contract on the spot, and before long, Maia's album was being played in stores across Europe. The music world took notice of Maria Corbero, but she remained grounded, using the platform to spread messages of hope and inspiration to those who needed it most.
