After completing a degree in fine arts, Maria Masaru decided to pursue a career in antique restoration. Her exceptional eye for detail and gentle touch quickly earned her a reputation as one of the best young restorers in the industry.

She carefully inspected the piece, taking note of its original finish and any potential restoration challenges.

Back at the shop, Maria Masaru spent hours meticulously sanding down the chair's surface, revealing hidden layers of stain beneath. She carefully applied a combination of traditional finishes, blending them seamlessly into the existing patina. The opportunity was both thrilling and daunting – but Maria Masaru knew it was the perfect chance to share her expertise and mentor the next generation of restorers.

With excitement building in her chest, Maria Masaru accepted the challenge.
