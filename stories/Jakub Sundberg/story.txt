As he walked through the city streets, Jakub noticed a group of tourists gathered around a street performer juggling fire. One of them caught his eye - a young woman with bright pink hair and a warm smile. She was laughing at something he said, her eyes sparkling with amusement.

Jakub felt a pang in his chest, a familiar sensation he hadn't experienced in years. He had been so focused on his art career that he had all but given up on love. But there was something about this woman that drew him in.
