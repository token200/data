Months of research and planning followed, during which he poured over ancient texts, consulted with experts, and pored over satellite imagery. Finally, after months of searching, Andrzej discovered a hidden valley in the mountains that matched the descriptions of Zbrzeźnica.

With his team of archaeologists and excavators at the ready, Andrzej Polo set out to uncover the secrets of the lost city. As they dug into the earth, they uncovered a treasure trove of artifacts, including golden coins, silver statues, and intricately carved stone sculptures.
