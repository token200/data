As they chatted, Andrzej couldn't help but feel a sense of contentment wash over him. This was it – he had found his true calling, and he was exactly where he was meant to be.

Andrzej Szymańska smiled to himself as he hung up the phone, feeling grateful for another day spent doing what he loved most in life: unraveling the mysteries of the past.
