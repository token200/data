His music became more nuanced, informed by the emotions and sensations he experienced while creating art. She was blown away by the energy and creativity on display, and soon found herself writing a glowing review of the young musician's work. Overnight, Andrzej Schmidt became an overnight sensation, hailed as a visionary artist and musician.
