As he grew older, Andrzej's passion for art only intensified, and he began to pursue a degree in fine arts at the local university. However, it wasn't long before he found himself drawn back to his love of technology, and he eventually switched to studying computer science instead.

As news of their work spread, Andrzej and Rachel found themselves in high demand, with galleries and museums clamoring for their latest creations. They traveled the world, exhibiting their art and inspiring others to join them on their quest for innovation.

Despite the chaos and excitement that came with success, Andrzej remained true to his artistic vision, always seeking to push the boundaries of what was possible. And as he looked out at the crowd of adoring fans, he couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and accomplishment, knowing that it was all thanks to his hard work and dedication.
