Tõnis Garcia was a man with a passion for music that knew no bounds. Born and raised in Tallinn, Estonia, he had grown up surrounded by the sounds of traditional Estonian folk music, but it wasn't until he discovered the works of Bach and Mozart that he truly found his calling.

Years went by, and Tõnis's talent continued to grow, earning him a coveted spot in the Estonian Philharmonic Orchestra.
