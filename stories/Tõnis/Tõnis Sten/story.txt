His attention to detail was unmatched, and his creations were always a testament to his dedication to his craft.

Enchanted by the craftsman's talent, Hendrik decided to commission a beautiful wooden cabinet for his mansion. After a series of meetings and discussions, Hendrik entrusted Tõnis Sten with the task.
