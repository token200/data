Tõnis's life took an unexpected turn when she stumbled upon an old, dusty attic while exploring the town's oldest buildings. As she rummaged through the forgotten trinkets and antiques, she discovered an ancient book belonging to one of her great-grandmothers - Tõnis Kowalska. Her research eventually led her to a small group of like-minded individuals who shared her passion for uncovering the truth about their collective heritage.

As word of her work spread, people began to seek out Tõnis's expertise on matters ranging from folklore to historical preservation. Her knowledge was sought after by scholars and curious locals alike, and she became an unlikely leader in Tartu's cultural revival.
