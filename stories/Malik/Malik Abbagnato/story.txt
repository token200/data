He found solace in the creative process, where he could express emotions and thoughts that words alone couldn't convey. His paintings were like windows into his soul, full of vibrant colors and swirling patterns that seemed to pulse with energy.

One evening, as Malik was setting up for a show, he received an unexpected visit from his estranged father, Giovanni Abbagnato. A former artist himself, Giovanni had always been a source of inspiration for Malik's early work. Giovanni's presence sent shockwaves through Malik's world.
