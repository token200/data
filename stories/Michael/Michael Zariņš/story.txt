With renewed determination, Michael Zariņš continued his crusade for truth and accountability. His reporting sparked protests and rallies across Latvia, drawing attention to the systemic corruption that had been swept under the rug for far too long.

As the days turned into weeks, Michael's name became synonymous with bravery and conviction.
