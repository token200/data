Michael "Mike" Khan was known for his unyielding determination and unwavering passion for martial arts. Born on November 11th, 1982, in Houston, Texas, to an Iranian father and a Lebanese mother, Mike's upbringing was shaped by the diverse cultural influences of his parents' homeland.

He began practicing Brazilian jiu-jitsu at the age of eight, under the tutelage of his instructor, Carlos Pereira. As he honed his skills, Mike's natural talent and work ethic quickly propelled him to the top ranks of the sport.

He had dominated the sport, winning numerous titles and awards throughout his career. However, Mike remained humble and grounded, crediting his success to his family, friends, and training partners.

As Mike's reputation grew, so did his entrepreneurial spirit. In 2010, he founded Elite Fight Promotions, a martial arts promotion company that aimed to bring high-quality competition events to fighters across the globe. Under Mike Khan's leadership, Elite Fight Promotions became a major player in the MMA landscape, hosting some of the most prestigious events in the sport.
