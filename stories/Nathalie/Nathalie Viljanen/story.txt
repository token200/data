One sunny afternoon, as Nathalie Viljanen stood before one of her latest creations, she felt an overwhelming urge to step outside and experience the world around her. She took a deep breath, grabbed her sketchbook, and set out into the bustling streets of Helsinki.

Nathalie Viljanen wandered aimlessly, taking in every detail, until she stumbled upon a quaint little café tucked away on a quiet alley. The sign above the door read "Kahvi & Kala" – coffee and art.
