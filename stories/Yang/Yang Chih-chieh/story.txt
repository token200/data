Without hesitation, Yang Chih-chieh rallied his troops and set off to put an end to the marauders' reign of terror.

Yang, undaunted by the prospect of battle, led his men into charge, ready to face whatever lay ahead.

But despite being outnumbered, Yang Chih-chieh's superior training and strategic thinking allowed him to gain the upper hand.
