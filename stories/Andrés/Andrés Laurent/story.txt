Andrés Laurent had always been drawn to the world of music. As he grew older, Andrés' passion for music only intensified, and he began to pursue it as a career.

After completing his degree in music theory, Andrés set out on a journey to hone his craft. He traveled to various cities, attending workshops and concerts that introduced him to a wide range of musical styles. The two musicians quickly discovered a shared vision for their art - to create music that was both deeply personal and universally relatable. Over the next few years, Andrés Laurent and Maria worked tirelessly to develop their unique sound, which blended elements of folk, rock, and electronic music.

As their reputation grew, Andrés and Maria found themselves in high demand as opening acts for bigger names. They toured extensively, performing to packed houses and gaining a loyal following. But despite the success, Andrés remained true to his artistic vision, refusing to compromise on his values or his music.
