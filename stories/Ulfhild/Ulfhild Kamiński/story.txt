Few people knew much about Ulfhild, but whispers of her troubled past spread like wildfire through the streets.

However, rumors persisted that The Healer was not what she seemed, and that Ulfhild's past was shrouded in darkness.

As Ulfhild grew older, she became increasingly reclusive, preferring to spend her days tending to the wounded creatures that wandered into Ravenshire. One stormy night, a young woman named Elara stumbled upon Ulfhild's small cottage on the outskirts of town. Seeking refuge from the tempest, Elara was drawn to the warm glow emanating from within. As she knocked on the door, it creaked open, revealing Ulfhild standing in the shadows.

"Welcome, child," Ulfhild said, her voice low and husky. "I've been expecting you."

As the night wore on, Elara discovered that Ulfhild was not just a healer, but a guardian of sorts – one who protected Ravenshire from supernatural forces lurking in the darkness. And as the storm raged outside, Ulfhild's eyes gleamed with an otherworldly intensity, hinting at secrets and powers beyond human comprehension.

Though few had glimpsed Ulfhild Kamiński's true nature, those who had were forever changed by the encounter. As the night drew to a close, Elara departed, knowing that she had caught a glimpse of something extraordinary – something that would stay with her for the rest of her days.
