With each successful hunt, she'd return to her forge, eager to create something truly remarkable.

One fateful evening, as Ulfhild Wróbel traversed the forest path, she stumbled upon a hidden glade. The air was alive with an otherworldly energy, and the trees seemed to hum with ancient power.
