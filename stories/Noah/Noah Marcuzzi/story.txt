Noah Marcuzzi was known for his quick wit and sharp tongue, but little did anyone know that beneath the surface of this charismatic socialite lay a complex web of emotions and desires. But as he grew older, Noah began to chafe against the constraints placed upon him. He longed to break free from the suffocating grip of his family's expectations and forge his own path.
