As night descended upon Rome, Noah Aurelius Antoninus Augustus took his place at the helm, ready to navigate the treacherous waters of politics and diplomacy. The burden of leadership rested heavy on his shoulders, but he stood tall, his heart guided by the words of his ancestor: "The impediment to action advances action.
