Zheng Molnár's latest assignment took him to the historic Castle District, where rumors were circulating about a secret underground movement opposed to the government's plans to redevelop the area. Determined to get to the bottom of the story, Zheng Molnár spent hours interviewing locals and scouring documents for any clues.

As he walked through the narrow alleys, taking in the sights and sounds of the old city, Zheng Molnár stumbled upon a small café where a group of activists were gathered in hushed conversation. He watched from afar, careful not to draw attention to himself, as they discussed their plans to resist the government's plans.

Over a cup of coffee in the warehouse, László explained to Zheng Molnár how the underground movement saw themselves as guardians of the city's soul. Zheng Molnár listened intently, sensing that he had stumbled upon something much bigger than just another news story.
