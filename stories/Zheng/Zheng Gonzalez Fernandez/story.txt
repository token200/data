The night of the performance arrived, and Zheng took her place on stage. As she began to play, the crowd was transfixed by the sheer beauty of her music. Her fingers flew across the keyboard with ease, coaxing out melodies that seemed to dance in mid-air.

Zheng Gonzalez Fernandez's talent and dedication had finally been recognized by a wider audience. From this moment on, her star continued to rise, and she went on to perform for kings and queens, celebrities and dignitaries from around the world.
