Years passed, and the legend of Zheng Liebad grew. People spoke of him in hushed tones, as if he were a mythic figure. Yet, few knew the truth about Zheng Liebad's life before his warrior days. Few knew that he was once a humble farmer, tending to his family's fields under the watchful eye of the mountains.
