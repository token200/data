As she grew older, Zheng Kalniņa began to assist her mother in the weaving process, learning the intricacies of the loom and the secrets of the ancient patterns.

They wanted it to be a symbol of their love and commitment to each other, and they were willing to pay a handsome sum for the finest fabrics. Zheng Kalniņa was touched by their request and agreed to take on the project.
