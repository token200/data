As she made her way through the crowded streets, Iveta's thoughts wandered back to the memories of her childhood spent with her mother, Kenji Hiroshi's wife, Yumi. Her father had always been tight-lipped about the circumstances surrounding Yumi's vanishing act, leaving Iveta with more questions than answers.

Taro slid into the booth across from Iveta, his eyes darting nervously around the room before settling back on her face. "Iveta Hiroshi, I'm glad you came," he whispered urgently. "There's been some changes since your mother went missing."

With every passing minute, Iveta became more convinced that her mother had been involved in something much bigger than a simple disappearance. The conversation left her with more questions than answers, but for the first time in two decades, she felt like she might just be on the right track.
