As she grew older, Iveta's interest in cars only deepened, and she began to help her father with his restorations.

And as she worked, Iveta couldn't help but think back to her childhood, when her father had first introduced her to the world of classic cars.

Iveta Alimbekova's success had taken her far from those humble beginnings, but she remained true to her roots.
