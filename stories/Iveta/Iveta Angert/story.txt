After hours of digging through documents and conducting interviews, Iveta finally stumbled upon a cryptic message on an old blog post. The words "the truth is hidden in plain sight" seemed ominous, but Iveta was undeterred. She knew that she had to follow the lead, no matter where it took her.

With her heart racing, Iveta quickly gathered her notes and made her way back to her office, determined to expose the truth.
