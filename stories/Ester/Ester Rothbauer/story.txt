As a young woman, Ester worked as a photographer, capturing the beauty of the world around her through her lens. She traveled to distant lands, documenting the lives of people who lived off the beaten path. Her photographs told stories of resilience and hope in the face of adversity.

But Ester's wanderlust soon took hold, and she began to crave more than just snapshots of far-off places. She became fascinated with the art of clockmaking, learning the intricacies of gears and springs from an aging clockmaker in the Alps.
