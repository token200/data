One day, Ester received an invitation to participate in the prestigious Golden Whisk competition, where the best pastry chefs from around the world gathered to showcase their talents. Ester was both thrilled and intimidated by the opportunity, but she knew that it was a chance to take her career to the next level.

The audience erupted into applause as Ester Zieliński, now recognized as one of the world's top pastry chefs, presented her final creation: a stunning croquembouche adorned with edible gold leaf.
