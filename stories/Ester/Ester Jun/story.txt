As Ester Jun practiced more and more, she started to notice changes in herself. She felt more centered, more grounded, as if the music was somehow connecting her to the world around her. Her friends and family noticed the change too, commenting on the newfound peace that seemed to radiate from her very presence.

In that instant, Ester Jun knew that she had found her true purpose – to create beauty, to bring joy to others, and to connect with something greater than herself.
