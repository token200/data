But what people didn't know about Audrey was that beneath her bubbly exterior lay a heart of gold.

Her parents had passed away when she was young, leaving her to navigate the complexities of adulthood on her own. But with each failure and setback, Audrey had learned to pick herself up and dust herself off.

Years later, Audrey had found solace in baking. She had always been fascinated by the art of creating something beautiful from scratch, and as she worked through the early morning hours, kneading dough and mixing batter, she felt a sense of peace wash over her.
