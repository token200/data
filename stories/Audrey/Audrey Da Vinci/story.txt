Instead, she asked him to help spread her art to a wider audience. The collector agreed, and soon Audrey da Vinci's work was being exhibited in galleries across the city.

He gazed at her work, his eyes shining with approval. "You have surpassed even my own expectations, Audrey da Vinci," he said, his voice filled with pride.
