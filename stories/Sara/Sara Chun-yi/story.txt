She spent hours listening to tales of resilience and determination, which inspired her to pursue her own dreams. After completing her degree in English literature, Sara began writing for local publications and soon gained recognition for her unique voice.

She spent hours browsing through the shelves, discovering new authors and titles. Among them was a book written by her idol, a renowned Taiwanese writer who had passed away years ago. Sara felt a deep connection to the author's words, which resonated with her own experiences growing up in a multicultural society. As she delved deeper into the book, Sara realized that she wanted to write something similar – a story that would capture the essence of Taiwan's complex history and culture.

With renewed passion and purpose, Sara began working on her own novel. She spent long hours writing, researching, and editing, pouring her heart and soul into the manuscript. As she sat at her desk, surrounded by scribbled notes and unfinished drafts, Sara felt a sense of pride and accomplishment wash over her.
