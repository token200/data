She claimed to be an art critic, but there was something about her that didn't quite add up. Her eyes seemed to hold a secret, and her words were laced with an undercurrent of deceit.

Despite his initial reservations, François found himself drawn to Sophia's enigmatic presence. He began to show her his latest works, sharing his thoughts and inspirations behind each piece. As they conversed, he noticed that she seemed to understand him in a way that few others did – as if she saw beyond the surface of his art to the very soul of François Lewandowski.

Despite this unease, François found himself powerless to resist Sophia's influence. He continued to create for her, pouring all his emotions and thoughts onto the canvas.
