François María was taken aback by the suggestion, but something about it resonated with him. Perhaps it was the challenge, or perhaps it was the opportunity to push the boundaries of his art once again. Whatever the reason, he agreed to take on the project.

Only time would tell, but one thing was certain: François María's latest piece would be unlike anything anyone had ever seen before. With every stroke of the canvas, he seemed to peel back a layer of himself, revealing a complex and multifaceted individual beneath.
