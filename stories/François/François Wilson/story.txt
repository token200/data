François Wilson was a man of mystery, with a presence that commanded attention without ever saying a word. A renowned artist and sculptor, his works were not only breathtakingly beautiful but also held a deep sense of emotion and storytelling.

As he grew older, François's talent for sculpture became evident, and he began to hone his craft under the guidance of some of the finest artists of his time.

Despite his tough exterior, François had a soft spot for those who shared his passion for art. He took Sophie under his wing, mentoring her until she was ready to take on the world. And as she left the studio one day, François smiled and said, "You're ready, Sophie. Go out there and make your mark."
