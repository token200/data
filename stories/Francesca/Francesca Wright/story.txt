Francesca Wright had always been drawn to the world of art. As a child, she spent hours sketching and painting, lost in her own little world of colors and creativity. Her parents, both artists themselves, encouraged her passion and nurtured her talent.

As Francesca grew older, her love for art only deepened. She pursued a degree in fine arts, studying the works of masters like Monet and Van Gogh. After graduation, she set out on her own, determined to make a name for herself in the art world.
