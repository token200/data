"Francesca Ayumi," he said, "the stars have spoken to me in visions, warning of a great imbalance in the cosmos. A dark force stirs, threatening to disrupt the harmony of our world. I believe you are the only one who can help us find the source of this darkness and bring balance back to our planet."

Francesca Ayumi listened intently, her eyes locked on the elder's as he spoke of ancient prophecies and celestial omens. She knew that she had spent years studying these very same texts, but now they seemed to hold a deeper meaning.

Without hesitation, Francesca Ayumi accepted the challenge. She spent the next few weeks poring over her notes and consulting with fellow mystics, piecing together the fragments of information like a puzzle. As the night before the full moon approached, she felt an increasing sense of restlessness, as if the universe itself was urging her forward.
