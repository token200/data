Luna became a mentor to Francesca, guiding her on a journey of self-discovery and artistic expression. Together, they explored the city's hidden corners, uncovering secret gardens and underground music venues. Francesca's life was transformed by her time at Melodic Muse, and she knew that she would always be grateful for the chance to find her true voice.
