Francesca Hassan was known for her sharp wit and quick tongue, but beneath her confident exterior lay a complex web of emotions and insecurities. As a successful event planner in her late twenties, Francesca had built a reputation for herself as the go-to expert for high-profile weddings and corporate functions.

Despite her impressive resume, Francesca struggled to find love in the city that never slept. Her dates were always short-lived, with relationships ending as abruptly as they began. Her friends would often tease her about her inability to commit to anything longer than a few weeks.

They had met in college and shared a whirlwind romance that ended all too soon. The spark between them was still palpable, but their conversation felt forced, like they were both trying to recapture something that had already slipped away.

As the night wore on, Francesca found herself drawn to Alex's easy charm and genuine smile. For the first time in months, she felt a glimmer of hope that maybe, just maybe, she could find someone who saw beyond her tough exterior and loved her for who she truly was: Francesca Hassan, the vulnerable and sensitive soul beneath.

Over coffee, they delved deeper into each other's lives, sharing stories and laughter like old friends. For the first time in years, Francesca felt a sense of connection that went beyond physical attraction. It was as if they had found a common language, one that spoke directly to her heart.

As the night drew to a close, Alex walked Francesca home, his arm brushing against hers in a gentle caress.
