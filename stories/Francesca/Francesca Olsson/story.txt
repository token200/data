The thought gave her a newfound energy, and for the first time in weeks, Francesca felt like herself again.

With renewed purpose, Francesca set out into the unknown, ready to face whatever lay ahead. She knew that finding Sofia wouldn't be easy, but she was willing to do whatever it took. As she walked away from the café, Francesca felt a sense of resolve settle over her, a sense of determination that would carry her through even the darkest of times.

For Francesca Olsson, there was no other option but to keep searching, no matter what the cost. She was convinced that Sofia was still out there, waiting for her sister to find her. And with that thought, Francesca set off into the unknown, ready to face whatever lay ahead.
