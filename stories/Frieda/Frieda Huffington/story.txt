Frieda Huffington was a free-spirited artist who lived life on her own terms. She had a wild mane of curly hair and eyes that sparkled like diamonds in the sunlight. As she wandered through the streets of New York City, her sketchbook and pencils always by her side, Frieda's creativity seemed to know no bounds.

As she set up her easel and began to prepare for the show, Frieda couldn't help but feel a sense of excitement and nervousness.
