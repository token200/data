Her display case was filled with an assortment of sweet treats, from traditional Finnish cardamom buns to modern creations like matcha-green tea croissants. As the day progressed, Frieda Lehtonen's stall became a hub of activity, with people clamoring to taste her latest creations. She was thrilled to see her hard work paying off, and she took great pride in knowing that her baked goods were bringing joy to so many people.
