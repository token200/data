Stefan Ferragni was born into a world of fashion and luxury, the son of Italian entrepreneur Donatella Versace and a renowned model. As he grew older, Stefan's passion for photography and style only intensified, eventually leading him to pursue a career in the industry.

His unique eye and attention to detail quickly made him a sought-after talent, and soon he was snapped up by Condé Nast, one of the world's largest and most respected fashion magazines.

As Stefan's career continued to soar, he found himself rubbing shoulders with A-list celebrities and royalty. He shot campaigns for Gucci and Dolce & Gabbana, capturing the essence of each brand's distinctive style and aesthetic on film. His work was met with critical acclaim, and soon he was one of the most in-demand photographers in the industry.
