It wasn't an easy decision, though. Stefan Kovacs had seen some things that no man should ever have to see - innocent civilians caught in the crossfire of war, friends and colleagues torn apart by corruption and deceit. But he knew he couldn't keep running from it forever. He needed to make a difference in his own way.

Stefan Kovacs hesitated for a moment, weighing the risks and benefits. He'd thought he'd left that life behind for good. But something about the offer sparked his old instincts back to life. Maybe it was the thrill of the chase, or maybe it was just a desire to prove himself one last time.
