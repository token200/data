Stefan Jansone listened intently, his mind racing with possibilities. He knew that this case would be different – one that required patience, strategy, and an unwavering commitment to justice. As he nodded in agreement, Sarah noticed something about him – a flicker of recognition in his eyes, as if she had seen him before.

But Stefan Jansone's past was shrouded in secrecy, even from those closest to him. His file remained classified, and only a select few knew the truth about his time in the military or the reasons behind his departure.
