As Sakurai Jorgensen grew older, he became increasingly restless, feeling confined by the strict traditions of his family's clan.

Sakurai Jorgensen worked tirelessly to hone these skills, determined to become one of the most skilled ninjas of his generation.

As he progressed on his path, Sakurai Jorgensen encountered various challenges that tested his resolve and pushed him to his limits. But through it all, he remained steadfast in his pursuit of mastery, driven by a fierce determination to unlock the secrets of the mysterious scroll.
