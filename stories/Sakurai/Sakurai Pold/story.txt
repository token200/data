As she grew older, her talent became more pronounced, and she began to receive visits from animals in need of guidance or comfort.

People began to seek her out for advice on how to connect with their own pets or solve problems with local wildlife. Sakurai proved to be a natural mediator, able to soothe even the most troubled of animals with her gentle touch and soothing voice. Her full name became synonymous with animal welfare, and she was soon in demand as a speaker at conferences and events.
