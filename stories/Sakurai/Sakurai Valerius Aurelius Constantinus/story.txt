Within the temple's depths, he encountered an imposing giant of a man, its body adorned with gruesome scars and its eyes blazing with fury. The beast was said to be a product of dark magic, forged from the very essence of chaos itself. Sakurai Valerius Aurelius Constantinus regarded it calmly, his mind racing with calculations and strategies.
