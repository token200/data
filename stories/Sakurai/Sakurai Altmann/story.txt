The words of the prophecy ignited a fire within him, and he knew he had to follow its call. For years, he trained tirelessly, honing his skills as a warrior and strategist.

But his enemies saw him as nothing more than a terrorist, a rebel without a cause. They vowed to crush him beneath their heel, but Sakurai remained undeterred.

For now, at least, the Kyokai had won a major battle in their quest for freedom and unity. But the war was far from over, and Sakurai knew that the real challenge lay ahead. Still, he remained steadfast, driven by a burning sense of purpose that would not be swayed.
