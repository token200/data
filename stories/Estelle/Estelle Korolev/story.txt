But it wasn't just her baking skills that made her popular - it was the way she listened to people's problems, offering words of comfort and understanding that seemed almost otherworldly.

But one thing was certain: Estelle Korolev was a woman of incredible intensity, her eyes burning with a fire that seemed to pierce right through you.

Sarah left the bakery feeling lighter, as if Estelle had somehow absorbed her pain and was now radiating it outward. Over the next few weeks, Sarah returned again and again, each time seeking solace in Estelle's warm, listening presence.

But as the months passed, Sarah began to notice strange occurrences around town. Objects would move on their own, and whispers seemed to emanate from nowhere. It was as if Estelle's presence had awakened a hidden energy that now pulsed through Ravenswood like a heartbeat.

And yet, despite the inexplicable events that surrounded her, Estelle remained an enigma - a woman shrouded in mystery, with eyes that burned brighter than any fire. For those who knew her, she was a source of strength and comfort; for others, she was a warning sign, a harbinger of secrets that were best left unspoken.
