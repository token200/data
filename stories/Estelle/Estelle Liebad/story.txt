Estelle Liebad was a renowned artist known for her vibrant and emotive paintings. Born in Paris to a family of modest means, Estelle grew up surrounded by the city's rich cultural heritage. As she grew older, Estelle's fascination with art only intensified. She devoured books on art history, practiced painting tirelessly, and eventually landed a coveted spot in the prestigious École des Beaux-Arts. The colors and energy of this vibrant metropolis found their way into her paintings, imbuing them with an infectious vitality.
