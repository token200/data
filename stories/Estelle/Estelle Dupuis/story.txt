As the night wore on, Estelle Dupuis became the center of attention, her presence drawing in a crowd of admirers and curiosity-seekers alike. She was a woman with a reputation for being untouchable, uncatchable – and utterly captivating.

As the night drew to a close, Jack approached Estelle with a tray of empty glasses and a warm smile. "You know, Estelle," he said, his voice barely above a whisper, "I think I understand why they call you the enigma of the town."

Estelle's eyes sparkled with amusement, and for a fleeting moment, her mask slipped, revealing a glimmer of something almost human beneath. But then, just as quickly, it was gone, leaving Jack – and everyone else in the café – wondering if he had really seen anything at all.
