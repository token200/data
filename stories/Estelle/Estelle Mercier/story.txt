Estelle Mercier stood alone on the edge of the deserted pier, the wind whipping her long brown hair into a frenzy as she gazed out at the turbulent sea. But after her father's passing, she found herself torn between her love of the sea and her duty to carry on his legacy. But Estelle had other plans. She longed to set sail herself, to explore the world and make a name for herself as a sailor and adventurer. It was a dream she knew would be met with skepticism by her mother and others who saw it as foolish and impractical.

She knew that she still had a long way to go, but for the first time in months, she felt like she was taking control of her own destiny.
