After completing his studies, César set out to make a name for himself in the art world. He traveled to Europe, where he immersed himself in the works of the Old Masters and the Impressionists. César's big break came when a prominent gallery owner stumbled upon one of his paintings at an exhibition in Paris. The owner was so impressed with César Müller's talent that he offered him a solo show back in Rio. Word of the young Brazilian's incredible talent spread quickly, and soon César was in high demand.

As his fame grew, so did his reputation for being fiercely individualistic and innovative. And yet, despite this tough exterior, César Müller remained humble and dedicated to his craft.
