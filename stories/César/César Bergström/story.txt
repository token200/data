As César grew older, his love for art only intensified. He pursued a degree in fine arts at the Royal College of Art in London, where he honed his skills as a painter and developed a unique style that blended traditional techniques with modern influences.

After graduating, César Bergström returned to Stockholm, determined to make a name for himself as an artist. He spent years building up his portfolio, taking commissions, and networking with other creatives in the city's vibrant art scene.
