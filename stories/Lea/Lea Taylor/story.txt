As she prepared for the show's opening night, Lea Taylor couldn't help but feel a mix of emotions. Excitement and nervousness swirled together like the colors on her palette, as she envisioned the crowd of art enthusiasts gathered to witness her latest creation.

Lea Taylor's eyes scanned the room, searching for familiar faces among the sea of strangers. Her gaze landed on a group of fellow artists, who exchanged nods and words of encouragement as they admired her piece.
