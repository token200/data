She was especially fascinated by the legend of the Czarny Róg, a mystical hill that was said to hold ancient secrets and magical powers. These stories sparked Lea's imagination and inspired her to pursue a career in music.

The audience was entranced as she sang about the mystical hill, its secrets and powers revealed through her enchanting voice. As Lea took her final bow, the applause echoed through the venue, a testament to her artistry and dedication to preserving Polish cultural heritage.
