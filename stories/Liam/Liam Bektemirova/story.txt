As she sipped on a steaming cup of matcha at a quaint cafe, Liam's mind wandered to the peculiarities of modern life. She jotted down notes on everything from social media addiction to virtual reality escapism.

Liam Bektemirova had always been fascinated by the blurred lines between human connection and digital existence. Her own experiences as an introverted child, spent playing video games with her siblings, had instilled in her a sense of empathy for those struggling to find balance in their online lives.
