Now, at 25 years old, he found himself back in Willow Creek, determined to make a name for himself in the world beyond his hometown.

After completing a degree in fine arts, he set out into the world, working odd jobs to support himself while he honed his craft. But despite his best efforts, Liam couldn't shake the feeling that something was missing.
