JavierMaria shrugged, feeling a bit embarrassed. "I just can't seem to get this article right," he admitted. "I've been staring at the same sentence for hours, trying to come up with something that doesn't sound like I'm copying myself."

Sofia nodded sympathetically. "Let's take a break, then. We'll grab some lunch and clear your head." She tossed her bag over her shoulder and stood up, pulling JavierMaria up from his chair.
