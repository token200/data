But Tatyana's success on the field was only matched by her off-field accomplishments. As the years went by, Tatyana continued to push boundaries on and off the field. Despite the many accolades and accolades she received, Tatyana remained humble and grounded. Her commitment to using her platform for good had earned her a devoted fan base that admired not only her on-field prowess but also her kindness and generosity off the field.

In 2022, Tatyana announced her retirement from professional soccer, leaving behind a legacy as one of the greatest players in US Women's National Team history.
