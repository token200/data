The store's windows were filled with an assortment of colorful paintings and sculptures that seemed to dance in the sunlight. Intrigued, she pushed open the door and stepped inside.

Over the next few hours, Anne-Marie found herself lost in conversation with Madame Dupont, discussing everything from art to philosophy, music to literature. As the night drew to a close, Anne-Marie knew that she had found not only a new friend but also a kindred spirit who shared her passion for life.
