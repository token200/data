Anne-Marie was thrilled – this was the break she had been waiting for. She threw herself into the story, pouring over financial records and conducting secret interviews with key witnesses. The closer she got to the truth, the more danger seemed to lurk around every corner. But Anne-Marie Hassan was undaunted; she knew that her reporting could make a real difference in people's lives.

As the investigation unfolded, Anne-Marie faced increasingly hostile resistance from those who wanted to keep the truth hidden. She received threatening phone calls and was even followed home one night. But she refused to back down, convinced that the public had a right to know what was happening behind closed doors.
