As she grew older, Anne-Marie's interest in art only deepened. She enrolled in a prestigious art school, where she honed her skills under some of the most renowned instructors in the field. Despite her growing success, Anne-Marie never lost sight of her roots. Her father, Hans, was a kind and supportive mentor, always willing to offer words of encouragement and advice.

It was a dream come true for this talented young artist, who had always felt a deep connection to the City of Light.
