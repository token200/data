As she grew older, her love of music and art only deepened, and she began to pursue them as serious hobbies.

Anne-Marie's art was a reflection of her vibrant personality - bold colors and expressive brushstrokes gave way to intricate details and textures that seemed to come alive on the canvas. Her music, too, was a blend of classical and folk influences, which drew in listeners from all walks of life.
