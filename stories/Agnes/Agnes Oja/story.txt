As she grew older, her passion for plants only deepened, leading her to abandon all other pursuits and dedicate herself to her work.

Despite her success, Agnes remained an enigma to those around her. Few knew that beneath her reserved exterior lay a complex web of emotions, forged from a childhood marked by loss and hardship. As she watched, mesmerized, Agnes tenderly coaxed a rare species from the shadows, its petals unfolding like tiny wings in the light.
