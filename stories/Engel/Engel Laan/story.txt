Some said he had disappeared into thin air, leaving behind only a faint trail of clues. Others claimed he had faked his own death, donning a new identity and living on under an assumed name.

The truth, much like Engel, remained elusive. But one thing was certain: he was a master of deception. His ability to blend in with the crowd was uncanny, as if he had mastered the art of invisibility. Some said he could slip into any room undetected, while others claimed that his presence seemed to draw the very light out of the air.

I first encountered Engel when I stumbled upon a cryptic message scrawled on the wall of an abandoned warehouse. The words 'Meet me at midnight' were scribbled in red ink, accompanied by a single, flickering image of a crescent moon. Curiosity got the better of me, and I arrived at the designated location just before the stroke of twelve.

The air was heavy with anticipation as Engel materialized from the shadows. His eyes seemed to burn with an inner fire, his presence radiating an aura of intensity that bordered on madness. He spoke in hushed tones, his words dripping with a sinister charm that left me both entranced and terrified.

"You seek answers," he declared, his voice low and husky. "But are you prepared for the truth? The secrets I hold, they come at a price."

I hesitated, unsure if I was ready to confront the unknown. But something about Engel's words resonated deep within me. Perhaps it was the promise of revelation or perhaps it was the siren call of danger itself. Whatever the reason, I nodded my assent.

And so, I embarked on a journey into the heart of darkness with Engel Laan as my guide. The path ahead was treacherous, fraught with secrets and lies, but one thing was certain: I would follow him to the very depths of madness if that's where he led me.
