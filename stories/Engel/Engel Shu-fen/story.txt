Engel Shu-fen was known for her enigmatic smile and piercing green eyes that seemed to see right through you. As a renowned detective in modern-day Taipei, she had built a reputation for solving cases that baffled even the most seasoned investigators.

Born into a humble family of street vendors, Engel's natural curiosity and keen mind had led her to pursue a career in criminology. She graduated at the top of her class from the National Police University and quickly made a name for herself as a formidable detective.

One fateful evening, Engel Shu-fen received a cryptic message from an anonymous source claiming to have information about a high-profile murder case. The victim was none other than Taipei's most influential businessman, whose death had sent shockwaves through the city's elite.

Engel arrived at the crime scene, her eyes scanning every inch of the area for any clues. She began by interviewing witnesses and gathering evidence, but it wasn't until she stumbled upon an obscure online forum that she finally got a break in the case.

The forum was dedicated to an esoteric cult that had gained notoriety in recent years for its peculiar practices and alleged connections to organized crime. Engel's instincts told her that this was the lead she needed to crack the case, and she spent hours pouring over the forum's archives, searching for any mention of the businessman.

As she delved deeper into the world of the cult, Engel Shu-fen found herself entangled in a web of secrets and deceit. But her unwavering determination and razor-sharp intellect ultimately led her to uncover the truth behind the murder, exposing a complex plot that went all the way to the top of Taipei's power structure.

With her breakthrough, Engel Shu-fen solidified her position as one of Taiwan's most respected detectives, earning her the nickname "The Ghost Investigator" for her uncanny ability to solve cases that others deemed unsolvable.
