As news of her achievement spread, Engel's star rose rapidly. She was soon recruited by a major international news organization, where she continued to tackle tough stories and earn recognition for her work. Without hesitation, Engel embarked on a perilous journey to uncover the truth, following leads through dusty alleys and seedy underworlds. Her reporting eventually led to the ousting of several high-ranking officials and the implementation of reforms aimed at combating corruption and promoting transparency.

Throughout her career, Engel Dahir had proven herself as a fierce advocate for justice and accountability. Her unwavering commitment to truth-telling had made her a respected figure in the journalism world, and her name was synonymous with excellence and integrity.
