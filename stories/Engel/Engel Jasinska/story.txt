With her training and experience guiding her, Engel Jasinska moved stealthily through the warehouse, avoiding detection as she worked her way deeper into the heart of the enemy's stronghold. Her mission parameters were clear: retrieve Eclipse, extract herself, and get out – no questions asked.

As she navigated the dimly lit corridors, Engel Jasinska's senses came alive, picking up on every sound, every scent, and every flicker of movement that betrayed the mercenaries' positions. She knew that a single misstep could mean capture or worse, but she pressed on, driven by her duty to The Agency and her unwavering commitment to success.

Finally, after what seemed like an eternity, Engel Jasinska reached the inner sanctum where Eclipse was kept safe. With swift precision, she downloaded the intel onto a secure data device and turned to make her escape. But as she turned, she caught sight of one of the mercenaries – a towering giant with a scar across his cheek – closing in on her from behind.

Without hesitation, Engel Jasinska drew her sidearm and took down the mercenary with swift efficiency, before moving on to face off against the rest of the group. In the end, it was just herself and one other enemy standing between her and freedom – but Engel Jasinska had no intention of giving up now. With a fierce determination burning in her eyes, she made her final stand, ready to take down anyone who dared get in her way.
