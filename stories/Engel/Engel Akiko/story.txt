As she grew older, Engel became increasingly restless, feeling stifled by the expectations placed upon her by her family and society. Despite the fame and fortune that came with her success, Engel never forgot her humble beginnings. And though she was known as a ruthless fighter, Engel Akiko's heart remained soft and compassionate, always willing to lend a helping hand to those less fortunate.

In the end, Engel's legend grew, and she became a symbol of hope and inspiration to many.
