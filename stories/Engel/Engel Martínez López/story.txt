Engel Martínez López's passion for innovation had taken him down many paths. From designing intricate clockwork birds that could mimic human songs to creating automata that performed delicate ballet dances, his creations were sought after by royalty and commoners alike.

One fateful evening, as the sun dipped into the Rio Manzanares, a young woman named Sofía stumbled upon Engel Martínez López's workshop. As the night wore on, Sofía found herself drawn into Engel Martínez López's world. She watched as he worked tirelessly, driven by an insatiable curiosity about the workings of the universe. As the night drew to a close, Sofía thanked Engel Martínez López for his hospitality. He smiled, his eyes twinkling with amusement, and handed her a small, delicate clockwork bird.
