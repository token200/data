Despite their disapproval, Patrick's passion for creativity only grew stronger with each passing year. He began to study painting and calligraphy in secret, using the family's ancestral home as his makeshift studio. His talent quickly became apparent, and soon he was receiving commissions from locals who appreciated his unique style.
