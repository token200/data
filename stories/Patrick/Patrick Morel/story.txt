But tonight was different. Tonight, Patrick Morel was ready to take a chance on his passion. He adjusted his tie and took a deep breath, reminding himself of all the late nights spent perfecting his craft, all the rejections and setbacks that had led him to this moment.

As the night wore on, Patrick Morel watched as strangers became admirers, their faces aglow with wonder and curiosity.
