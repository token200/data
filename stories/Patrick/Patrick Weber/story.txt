He remembered spending countless hours listening to his grandmother's tales of resilience and determination, which had sparked his passion for journalism. His full name was actually Patrick John Weber, after his grandfather, a veteran of World War II who had fought for freedom and equality.

He spent hours interviewing witnesses, reviewing security footage, and analyzing data from the police department's own records. As he dug deeper, he discovered a disturbing pattern of abuse and neglect, with countless victims silenced by intimidation and fear.
