As she progressed in her studies, Lara Hideki's prowess with a sword became increasingly evident. Her movements were fluid and precise, striking fear into the hearts of her opponents. For Lara Hideki knew that the pursuit of perfection was a lifelong journey, one that required constant effort and commitment.
