She spent hours exploring the tide pools and hidden coves of Kefalonia, collecting shells and seaweed, and listening to the ancient stories of the island's inhabitants. Now, as a grown woman, she felt an insatiable hunger for more - more knowledge, more adventure, more connection with the world around her.

Isla Elara Balodis was a seeker, always on the move, never staying in one place for too long. But no matter where she went or what she did, she couldn't shake the feeling that there was something missing, something just out of reach.
