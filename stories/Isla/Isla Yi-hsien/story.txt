Years passed, and Isla's passion for music only grew stronger. She began to compose her own melodies, weaving tales of love and loss into the harmonies that flowed from her fingers like water. News of her talent spread quickly, drawing visitors from far and wide to hear the enchanting Isla Yi-hsien perform.

Yet, as fame's golden glow enveloped her, Isla found herself questioning what it truly meant to be true to oneself. Was she merely a vessel for the creative forces that drove her, or was she still the same young girl from Taiwan, listening for whispers in the wind?

It was then that Isla Yi-hsien realized she had not left home, but merely taken it with her – on every note, every melody, and every heartbeat.
