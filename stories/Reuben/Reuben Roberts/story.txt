One day, Reuben received an email inviting him to participate in a prestigious international puzzle competition, held in a remote location deep in the forest. The competition would test solvers' skills in areas such as cryptarithms, Sudoku, and other brain teasers. Reuben knew he had to enter - it was his chance to prove himself against the best puzzle enthusiasts from around the world.

The day of the competition arrived, and Reuben set off for the forest, eager to begin. As he arrived at the venue, a large lodge in the heart of the woods, he was greeted by fellow competitors and judges who were renowned experts in their field. The competition itself was grueling, with solver after solver falling victim to tricky puzzles.

Reuben Roberts knew that if he wanted to win, he had to stay focused. He methodically worked through each puzzle, using his years of practice and experience to eliminate incorrect solutions and arrive at the correct answers. As the hours ticked by, Reuben found himself in the final round, where only one solver would emerge victorious.

Sweat dripped from his brow as he focused on the problem, using every trick in the book to solve it.
