Born Reuben Inzaghi, the young man had grown up in a family of artists and musicians. From a young age, he was encouraged to explore his creativity, and as a result, he developed a unique style that blended elements of graffiti, painting, and sculpture.

Overjoyed, Reuben accepted the opportunity, knowing that this was his chance to share his message with a wider audience.
