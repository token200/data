After retiring from football, Reuben struggled to find his place in the world. He attempted various business ventures, but nothing seemed to stick. Feeling lost and aimless, Reuben turned to his passion for writing as a way to channel his emotions.

Reuben Dubois sat at his desk, pen in hand, staring blankly at the sheet of paper in front of him. He had always loved writing poetry, but never thought he was good enough to make a career out of it. That all changed when Reuben began writing about his experiences as a football player. The words flowed effortlessly onto the page, as if his emotions were finally being set free.
