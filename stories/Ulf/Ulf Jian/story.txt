Born in the snow-capped mountains of Scandinavia, she had grown up learning the ways of survival from her father, a skilled hunter and tracker. As Ulf Jian wandered through the frozen wilderness, she felt a deep connection to the land and its creatures.

Despite her tough exterior, Ulf Jian had a soft spot for those in need. She had always been drawn to helping others, and as she grew older, she began to use her skills to make a difference in her community. Whether it was rescuing stranded travelers or providing food for families struggling to make ends meet, Ulf Jian's selflessness knew no bounds.
