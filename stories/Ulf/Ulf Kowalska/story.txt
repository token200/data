Ulf's instincts told him that something was off about the case, but he couldn't quite put his finger on it. His normally detached demeanor gave way to a fierce determination, driving him to confront every lead, no matter how small or insignificant it seemed. There, he found Emma alive and well, but battered and bruised from a brutal attack. The perpetrators had been caught off guard by Ulf's sudden appearance, and they slunk away in defeat.
