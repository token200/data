As the investigation progressed, Ulf Janusz proved himself to be a master of misdirection, distracting his suspects with witty banter and clever deductions while keeping his true intentions hidden. The witness had been silenced by someone who wanted her out of the way, and Ulf Janusz was determined to find out who.
