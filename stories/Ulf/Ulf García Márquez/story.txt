As he grew older, Ulf's sound evolved, incorporating elements of psychedelia and experimental noise. His debut album, "Calle de las Palabras," was a critical success, praised by critics for its bold experimentation and catchy hooks. Since then, Ulf García Márquez had released several albums to similar acclaim, cementing his status as a leading figure in the regional music scene.
