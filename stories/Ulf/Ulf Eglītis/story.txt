There, Ulf Eglītis would stand alongside his fellow heroes and legends, forging an unbreakable bond with them.

As Ulf Eglītis looked upon the golden armor that adorned him, a mix of excitement and trepidation washed over him. He steeled himself for the battles to come, knowing that his name, Ulf Eglītis, was etched into the annals of Asgardian history forevermore.
