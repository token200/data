Despite his tough exterior, Ulf had a soft spot for strays and lost causes. He took on a stray cat named Kuro as his own, and could often be found watching it prowl through the alleys outside his small cabin on the outskirts of town.

Ulf listened intently as she recounted her story, his expression growing increasingly grim.

Sarah looked up at him with gratitude in her eyes, and for the first time, Ulf saw a glimmer of recognition there - she knew who this mysterious man was.
