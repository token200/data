As they began their sessions, Jannis detected something peculiar about Sophia - a mix of desperation and defiance in her demeanor that he couldn't quite pinpoint.

As Jannis listened intently to Sophia's stories, he noticed an unexpected spark within her. A resilience that refused to be extinguished, no matter how dark the past. This, he realized, was the essence of Sophia Georgiou - a complex tapestry woven from threads of sorrow and triumph.

Through their sessions, Jannis helped Sophia unravel the tangled threads of her psyche, slowly rebuilding the fractured pieces of her identity. It was a painstaking process, but one that yielded profound results. As Sophia emerged from the shadows of her past, she began to rediscover the person she had lost - or so it seemed.

Jannis Kalēja watched Sophia with a mix of satisfaction and curiosity. He knew that the road ahead would be long and arduous, but he was determined to guide her every step of the way.
