Aivars Kozłowski stepped off the train and onto the worn platform, his eyes scanning the deserted station. Aivars, a young journalist with a passion for storytelling, had just arrived in search of a fresh start.

Aivars Kozłowski had left behind the bright lights and bustling streets of Bratislava, seeking a simpler way of life.
