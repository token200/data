They were amazed by Aivars' knowledge of the world and his innovative ideas, and they begged him to join them on their journey. Aivars was hesitant at first, but the prospect of adventure and new experiences eventually won him over.

Aivars Lāce set off with the travelers, using his wit and intelligence to help them navigate the challenges of the road. He proved himself to be a valuable asset, and soon he became an integral part of their group.
