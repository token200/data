As Caroline's fame grew, so did her influence. She began to use her platform to promote Polish culture and heritage, releasing songs that celebrated her homeland and its people. Her unique sound, which blended traditional Polish folk with modern pop and electronic elements, resonated deeply with audiences around the world.
