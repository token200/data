Determined to get to the bottom of the story, Elżbieta had spent countless hours researching and interviewing witnesses. And now, as she gazed at the papers on her desk, she knew that she was on the cusp of something big.

The night of the premiere arrived, and Elżbieta took her place alongside her colleagues on stage at the Warsaw Film Festival. As the lights dimmed and the projector whirred to life, she felt a sense of trepidation wash over her. What would the audience make of this story? Would it expose the truth, or fall flat?
