As Laurent immersed himself in the world of his client's creation, he began to lose himself in the process. He found himself pouring over lavish lifestyles, clandestine meetings, and high-stakes business deals. The lines between reality and fiction blurred, and for a moment, Laurent felt like he was living the life of his fictional characters.

But Laurent Martínez López knew better than to get too close to the fire. He was a ghostwriter, not a participant, and he knew that to keep his anonymity intact, he had to maintain a healthy distance from the world he created.
