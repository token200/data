Laurent Kalniņa's debut album, "Latvia's Hidden Gems," was a critical and commercial success, showcasing his mastery of multiple musical styles. The album featured traditional Latvian folk songs reimagined in modern jazz and classical settings, highlighting Laurent's versatility as a musician.
