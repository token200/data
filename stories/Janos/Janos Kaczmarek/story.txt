After retiring from the agency, Janos settled into a quiet life as a librarian in a small town in rural Poland. He spent his days surrounded by dusty tomes and whispering patrons, content to keep to himself.

But old habits die hard, and Janos found himself drawn back into the world of espionage when a former colleague came knocking on his door. With his years of experience and training, Janos pieced together the puzzle, following a trail of cryptic clues and hidden messages. He knew every move he made would put him at risk, but he couldn't turn his back on his country now.

In the end, it was Janos Kaczmarek who uncovered the truth: a rogue agent working for a foreign power had stolen the intel in order to manipulate Poland's relations with its neighbors.
