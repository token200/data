Janos Karlsson settled in a small coastal town, where he took up fishing as a hobby. He spent his days on the water, reeling in cod and haddock for his family's dinner table. Janos Karlsson took her back to shore, where she was greeted by the local authorities and eventually found passage back to land. As they parted ways, Emma thanked him for saving her life, and Janos felt a sense of satisfaction he hadn't known since his cage-fighting days.

Janos Karlsson returned to his fishing boat, feeling more content than he had in years.
