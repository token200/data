One day, Diego Koval received a cryptic message from an old acquaintance, summoning him to a remote location deep in the jungle. The message read: "Meet me at the old ruins at midnight. Come alone." Diego Koval's instincts told him that something was off, but his curiosity got the better of him.

Diego Koval's eyes locked onto the figure before him, his mind racing with questions. Who was this person? What did they want from him? And what secrets lay hidden beneath the surface of their enigmatic message?
