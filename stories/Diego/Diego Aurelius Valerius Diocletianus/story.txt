The elderly senator, who had taken Diego under his wing years ago, smiled warmly and offered words of encouragement. "Diego Aurelius Valerius Diocletianus, you have a reputation for shrewdness and intelligence. I have no doubt that you will excel in your role as a trusted advisor to the emperor."

Diego's thoughts turned to his own ambitions, and he wondered what the future held for him. Would he rise through the ranks to become a prominent figure in Rome? Or would he follow in the emperor's footsteps and assume a high-ranking position within the imperial hierarchy?

As they reached the palace gates, Diego felt a sense of pride knowing that he shared the same name as the powerful Emperor Diocletianus. He knew that this connection could be both an asset and a liability, but for now, he was content to bask in its glow.
