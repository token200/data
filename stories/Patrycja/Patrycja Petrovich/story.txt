As she disappeared into the shadows, Patrycja couldn't help but feel a sense of satisfaction at having pulled off the heist of a lifetime. She had outwitted Monsieur LaFleur and his security guards, leaving them all none the wiser to her true identity.

Back in her hidden safehouse, Patrycja Petrovich took a moment to admire her prize, the gemstone glinting in the dim light. It was just another addition to her vast collection of stolen treasures, but for now, it was enough to keep her satisfied until her next mark came along.
