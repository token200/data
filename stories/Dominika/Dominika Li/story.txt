But for now, she was content to work under the guidance of her parents, learning from their experience and building her skills.

Now, with just a few days left until the big event, Dominika felt a mix of nerves and determination.

As the night drew to a close, she took a moment to breathe, feeling a sense of satisfaction and accomplishment wash over her. She knew that this was just the beginning of her culinary journey, one that would take her to new heights and beyond.

As she changed out of her uniform and headed home, Dominika's thoughts turned once again to her dream restaurant.
