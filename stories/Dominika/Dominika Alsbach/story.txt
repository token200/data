She thought back to all the sacrifices she had made – the endless practice sessions, the countless hours spent away from family and friends – and knew that it had all been worth it.

Dominika Alsbach smiled to herself as she took her seat in the audience, watching as a young student took the stage, her own instrument at the ready.
