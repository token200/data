With her keen mind and years of experience, she began to piece together a puzzle that seemed to defy logic. As the sun dipped below the horizon, Dominika Zarins finally unraveled the mystery. The thief turned out to be Lady Harcourt's own nephew, who had been in deep financial trouble and saw the necklace as a way to secure his future. But it was Dominika Zarins' sharp instincts and quick thinking that had foiled his plan.

As she closed the case, Dominika Zarins couldn't help but feel a sense of satisfaction.
