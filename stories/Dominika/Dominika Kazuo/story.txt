Her mind wandered to the composition she had been working on – a piece that blended traditional Japanese influences with modern Western sensibilities. It was her magnum opus, and she was eager to share it with the world.

As she waited for her drink, Dominika Kazuo began to feel a sense of restlessness. She couldn't shake the feeling that she had been living in a state of stasis, creatively speaking.
