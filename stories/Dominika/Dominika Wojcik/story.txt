Her latest assignment had brought her to the seedy underbelly of the city's nightlife, where the beautiful people mingled with the scum of the earth. Dominika Wojcik was no stranger to this world, having grown up on its margins herself. She knew its secrets and its players, and she wasn't afraid to use them.

A young woman, trembling with fear, had reported a mysterious encounter with a wealthy businessman who seemed to be hiding something. Dominika Wojcik's radar was piqued, and she quickly set out to track down the suspect.

He was a man of immense wealth and influence, but Dominika Wojcik saw right through his charming facade. She knew that he was hiding something, and she was determined to find out what.
