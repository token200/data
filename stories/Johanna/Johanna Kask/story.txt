With newfound excitement, Johanna Kask approached the mirror and examined it more closely. She noticed that the glass was etched with a series of intricate symbols, which, when rearranged, spelled out a single word: "Lumina."

As the news of her victory spread, Johanna Kask became an overnight sensation in the world of puzzles and cryptograms. Her reputation as a brilliant mind was solidified, and her friends couldn't help but refer to her as The Puzzle Whisperer once more.
