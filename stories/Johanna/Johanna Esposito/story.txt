Johanna's eyes welled up as she took the locket, feeling a lump form in her throat. "Thank you, Luca," she whispered, her full name spilling from her lips like a benediction.

Luca smiled and took her hand, his touch warm and reassuring. "Anytime, Johanna Esposito," he said, using her full name once more as if to remind himself of their special bond. In that moment, everything felt right with the world – a sense of homecoming that only true friends can provide.
