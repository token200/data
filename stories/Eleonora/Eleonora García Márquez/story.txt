The Márquez family had long been committed to preserving the region's unique cultural heritage, and this proposal aligned perfectly with their values. With Eleonora at the helm, the project took off, bringing much-needed investment and jobs to the community.

As the years passed, Eleonora García Márquez became a respected figure in Salento. When asked about her secret to success, she would smile quietly, attributing it to her upbringing and the lessons learned from her parents.

Though Eleonora's path was not without its twists and turns, one thing remained constant: her unwavering commitment to making a positive impact on the world around her.
