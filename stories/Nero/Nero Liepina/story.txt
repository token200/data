The success that followed was meteoric. Their debut album shot to the top of the charts, and the band embarked on a world tour. Nero's fame grew exponentially, but he never forgot his roots or lost sight of what made him tick - music. He remained fiercely loyal to "Electric Storm" and their fans, always giving it his all when they took the stage.
