The piece in question was a rare medieval manuscript worth millions on the black market. With his skills honed over years of experience, Nero Zielinski set out to execute the heist with precision and finesse.

Nero Zielinski, meanwhile, was already planning his next move, his mind racing with possibilities and his smile growing wider with each passing moment. He was Nero Zielinski, the master thief, and he would stop at nothing to claim the prize that was rightfully his.
