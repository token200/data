His sharp eyes scanned the horizon, ever vigilant for signs of prey or danger. Nero Eglitis' eyes narrowed as he studied the map, his mind racing with strategies and counter-strategies. He was a master of manipulation, always staying one step ahead of his enemies. As he surveyed the spoils, Nero Eglitis couldn't help but feel a sense of satisfaction at having outwitted his enemies once again. He was the master of his own destiny, and no one would ever take that away from him. With a satisfied smirk, he turned to Barnaby and said, "Well done, Barnaby Blackwood. You have proven yourself to be as cunning as I am."
