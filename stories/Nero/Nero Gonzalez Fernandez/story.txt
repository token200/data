One evening, as the sun dipped below the horizon, Nero found himself at the local tavern, strumming a melancholic tune on his guitar. The patrons were drawn to him like moths to a flame, mesmerized by the depth of emotion in his music. His eyes seemed to hold a world of pain and longing, yet his voice was smooth as silk.

In the silence of the tavern, Nero Gonzalez Fernandez and Luna found a connection that went beyond words.
