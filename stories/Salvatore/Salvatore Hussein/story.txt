As he tinkered with the engine, he asked Sarah about her life - where she was from, what she did for a living.

Salvatore "Sal" Hussein had always been a bit of an outsider himself. Growing up in a large family, he had learned to be resourceful and independent at a young age. But despite his tough exterior, he had a soft spot for those in need.
