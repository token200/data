As Ulan grew older, he became increasingly restless, yearning to make a name for himself on the battlefields of the Khanates. He rode his horse across the desert, the sun beating down upon him like a relentless drum. As he approached the rebel stronghold, he could feel the air thick with tension and anticipation.

As he rode back to the palace, Ulan felt a sense of pride and satisfaction wash over him.
