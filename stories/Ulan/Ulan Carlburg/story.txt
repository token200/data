Ulan Carlburg listened intently as Lady Harrington recounted the events of the previous night. He made notes on his pocket notebook, his eyes scanning the room with a keen gaze. When she finished speaking, he nodded thoughtfully and promised to do everything in his power to recover her stolen treasure.

As Ulan Carlburg confronted the young thief, he couldn't help but think of the reputation that Lady Harrington's family had built over the years. He ensured that justice was served and that the necklace was returned to its rightful owner.
