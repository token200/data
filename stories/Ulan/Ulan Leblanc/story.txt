Some said he was still involved in the shadows, pulling strings from behind the scenes to influence global events. Others claimed he had returned to his wartime roots, using his connections to broker peace treaties between warring nations.

As the stars began to twinkle above, Ulan Leblanc stood up, his eyes sparkling with a hint of mischief. "The night is young, Sophie. Shall we go for a ride?"
