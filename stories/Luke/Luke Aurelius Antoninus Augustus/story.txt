Sophia, it turned out, was also a philosopher, and she saw something in Luke that no one else did – potential. She took him under her wing, mentoring him in the art of Stoic philosophy and encouraging him to explore his own thoughts and ideas.

Under Sophia's guidance, Luke began to see the world in a new light. He realized that true power came not from wealth or status, but from within. As he continued to learn and grow, Luke Aurelius Antoninus Augustus became more than just a young nobleman – he became a leader, inspiring others with his words and actions.
