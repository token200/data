Luke's heart went out to Sofía, and he knew in that moment that he had found his true calling as a journalist. He promised Sofía that he would do everything in his power to share her story with the world. And so, Luke García Márquez dedicated himself to telling the untold stories of Medellín, determined to make a difference.

As he sat at his desk, scribbling away in his notebook, Luke felt a sense of purpose wash over him. He knew that it wouldn't be easy, but he was ready for the challenge.
