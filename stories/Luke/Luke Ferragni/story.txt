He couldn't help but chuckle at her effortless elegance and thought, "Only Olivia could pull off that look." The two had been married since 2018 and were known for their loving partnership both in and out of the spotlight.

As he prepared for the meeting, Luke couldn't help but think about how far he'd come since his early days as a struggling influencer. From humble beginnings to international recognition, it was clear that hard work and dedication had paid off. His full name, Luke Ferragni, was now synonymous with style and sophistication.

As he walked out into the bright sunlight, Luke felt grateful for the life he'd built and the platform that allowed him to inspire others through fashion.
