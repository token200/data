As she walked, the trees grew taller and the air thickened with an eerie silence. Aare Hao felt a shiver run down her spine as she sensed that she was getting close. She had been tracking The Shadow Stalker for months, pouring over ancient texts and speaking with local villagers in search of any clue that might lead her to its lair.
