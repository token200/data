As he reached the top, Evgeniy pulled out his telescope and began to set it up. The sky was clear, and he could see the Milky Way stretching across the horizon like a shimmering ribbon. He took a moment to compose himself, feeling the thrill of being alone with the vastness of the universe.

For hours, Evgeniy lost himself in the stars, watching as constellations shifted and changed above him. He felt small, yet connected to something much larger than himself.

As the night wore on, Evgeniy's thoughts turned to his family back home. His parents had always encouraged his love of learning, and he knew they would be proud of him for following his dreams.

The stars seemed to twinkle in agreement, a celestial showcase of wonder that filled Evgeniy with a sense of awe and gratitude. He felt like he was exactly where he was meant to be – on the rooftop of Moscow State University's physics building, surrounded by the endless possibilities of the universe.
