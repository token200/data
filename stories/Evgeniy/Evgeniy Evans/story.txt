The two hadn't spoken in years, and Evgeniy wasn't sure what to expect from their reunion. But as they sat down to talk, he was surprised by how easy it was to pick up where they had left off.

As the days turned into weeks, Evgeniy found himself opening up to Natalya in ways he never thought possible. He told her about his struggles with anxiety and depression, and she shared her own experiences with mental health. The two of them formed a deep bond, one that went beyond just writing or family ties.

Evgeniy Ivanovich Evans realized that sometimes, all it takes is someone to understand us in order for us to heal. With Natalya by his side, he felt like he could face anything life threw their way.
