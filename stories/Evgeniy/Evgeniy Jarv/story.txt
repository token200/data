Evgeniy's eyes widened as he took in the details of the artwork – it was indeed a rare print, one that had been sold at auction for record-breaking prices in the past. He exchanged a look with Madame LeRoux, and without a word, they understood that this was it – the piece that would cement Evgeniy Mikhailovich Jarv's status as the preeminent art collector of his generation.
