Evgeniy Ivanovich Sundlöf couldn't help but feel a sense of vindication; his years of tireless pursuit had finally paid off.

Their existence held the key to understanding Norway's rich cultural heritage and perhaps even its untapped natural resources.

Evgeniy Sundlöf knew that this breakthrough would redefine the field of cryptozoology forever.
