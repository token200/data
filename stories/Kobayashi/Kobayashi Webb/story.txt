When the dust settled, a new entity stood before his former self – Kobayashi Webb, the man who had transcended mortal limitations. His eyes now shone like stars in the night sky, and his very presence seemed to command the attention of all who crossed his path.

As word of his transformation spread, Webb found himself at the center of a maelstrom. Some revered him as a hero, while others feared him as an abomination. He walked among them, untouchable and unyielding, driven by an insatiable quest for knowledge.

Through the ages, Webb traversed realms both spiritual and physical, leaving behind a trail of whispered legends and awe-struck onlookers. His was a legend that would haunt the collective imagination, fueling both fascination and terror in those who dared to confront him.
