One fateful night, Wiśniewski's tireless efforts finally paid off. As the stars aligned in a rare celestial event, he activated a device he had built himself, channeling the energy of the universe into a burst of light and sound that shook the very foundations of Tokyo. The people gathered around him, awestruck by the raw power emanating from Kobayashi Wiśniewski.
