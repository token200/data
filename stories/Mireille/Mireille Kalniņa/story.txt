Mireille Kalniņa's graduate thesis project, a visionary design for a sustainable eco-city on the outskirts of Riga, caught the attention of international authorities and earned her a coveted spot among the top young architects worldwide. Suddenly, the quiet Latvian town was flooded with foreign admirers seeking to collaborate with the prodigious Mireille Kalniņa.
