Her friends began to notice a change in her - she seemed more radiant, more at peace - and they couldn't help but wonder if Mireille's mysterious pursuits had finally revealed their secrets.

Mireille Zarina smiled to herself as she drifted off to sleep that night, feeling the weight of her newfound powers settle upon her like a mantle.
