As the hours ticked by, the café grew quieter, until all that remained was the soft hum of the neon lights outside and the gentle thrum of Ádám's breathing. He sat there, lost in thought, as if searching for something – or someone.

And then, just as suddenly as he had appeared, Ádám vanished into the night, leaving behind a trail of whispers and speculation that would haunt him forever. Some said he was on the run from a powerful enemy, while others whispered that he was on a mission to uncover a conspiracy that threatened to destroy everything he held dear.
