As Ádám Piątek delved deeper into the world of painting, he discovered his passion for capturing the essence of Krakow's stunning architecture and natural beauty.

She approached Ádám Piątek with a proposal: she would showcase his art at her prestigious gallery, and he would receive international recognition.

Ádám Piątek's art became a reflection of his connection to the city and its people. His passion and creativity ignited a fire within those who experienced it, reminding all who saw his work that beauty is more than just something to be looked at – it's an experience to be felt.
