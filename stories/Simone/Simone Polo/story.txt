Simone Polo, a 25-year-old free-spirited artist, had always been drawn to the world of color and light. Growing up in the sun-drenched hills of Tuscany, she would spend hours lost in the vibrant hues of the Italian countryside, capturing them on canvas.

As a child, Simone's mother, a talented painter herself, encouraged her daughter's artistic inclinations, gifting her paints and sketchbooks whenever they traveled to new destinations. The lessons stuck with Simone as she grew older, leading her to attend art school in Florence, where she honed her skills alongside some of Italy's most talented artists.

After completing her studies, Simone decided to leave the confines of the city behind and set up a small studio amidst the rolling hills of Chianti Classico. It was here that she found inspiration in the breathtaking views and rolling vineyards that surrounded her home.
