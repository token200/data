Simone Bianchi Conte was a woman with a passion for art and music. Growing up in a small town surrounded by rolling hills and vineyards, she spent most of her childhood exploring the countryside, collecting leaves and watching the sunset over the olive groves.

As she grew older, Simone's love for art only deepened.
