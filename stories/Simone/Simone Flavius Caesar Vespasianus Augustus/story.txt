Her thoughts turned once more to Vespasianus Augustus, who had lived through times of turmoil and war, yet remained steadfast in his commitment to justice and peace.

Simone knew that she too would face challenges in her life, but with the lessons of her illustrious ancestor etched into her mind, she was ready to take on whatever lay ahead.
