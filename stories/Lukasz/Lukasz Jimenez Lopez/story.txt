Lukasz Jimenez Lopez was a man with a passion for art and music. As Lukasz's career took off, he found himself torn between his love of his homeland and his new life in the States. He decided to use his platform to promote Mexican culture and raise awareness about social justice issues affecting his community. As he took the stage to give an impromptu speech, Lukasz looked out at the sea of faces and felt a sense of belonging he had never experienced before. This was his moment – a chance to connect with people from all walks of life who shared his passion for art and social justice.
