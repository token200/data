He would have to do things his way, even if it meant sacrificing some of the trappings of success he had grown accustomed to.

Sebastien's eyes narrowed as he pondered the offer. It was a risk, but one that he couldn't afford to pass up. With a deep breath, he steeled himself for what lay ahead and made his way to the investor's table, ready to negotiate the deal of a lifetime.
