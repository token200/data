Sebastien Lefevre had always been drawn to the city of love and light, where art and culture seemed to seep from every stone. As a young man, he had spent hours exploring the Musée d'Orsay, mesmerized by the works of Monet and Renoir. Now, as a successful entrepreneur, he was ready to put his skills to use in one of the world's greatest cities.

It was a familiar smell, one that transported Sebastien back to his childhood in this very same city.
