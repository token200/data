A former CIA operative turned mercenary, Sebastien Faust had seen his fair share of battles. He'd walked the fine line between good and evil, often coming out on the wrong side. The memories haunted him still – the faces of those he'd lost, the screams that echoed in his mind like a mantra.

Sebastien's eyes drifted to the small notebook on his coffee table. Filled with scraps of paper and fragmented thoughts, it was a reflection of his fractured mind. He scribbled furiously, trying to exorcise the demons that plagued him. But the words blurred together, meaningless in the end.
