She was hesitant at first, but as she entered Aoki's studio, something about the dimly lit space and Aoki Bach himself put her at ease. Together, they began to experiment with light and shadow, allowing the rain-soaked streets outside to seep into their work.

As the night wore on, Sarah found herself caught up in a whirlwind of color and texture, her fears dissolving as she lost herself in the creative process. It was Aoki Bach's intuitive nature that coaxed this transformation from her, guiding her towards an untapped reservoir of inspiration.
