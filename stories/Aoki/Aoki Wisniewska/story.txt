Her days were filled with walks through the forest, listening to the whispers of the trees and the creatures that lived among them.

As word of Aoki Wisniewska's abilities spread, people began to seek her out for guidance and assistance. Despite the weight of responsibility that now rested upon her shoulders, Aoki knew that she was exactly where she was meant to be.
