But despite her tough exterior, Michielin had a soft spot for those in need. One day, Michielin received an invitation to perform at a benefit concert for a local music school that was struggling to stay afloat due to funding cuts. Without hesitation, she accepted the opportunity to lend her voice (and her name) to a good cause.
