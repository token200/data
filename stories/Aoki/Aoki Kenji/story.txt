Aoki Kenji had grown into a man, strong and silent, but still haunted by the ghosts of his past. His eyes, once bright with curiosity, now held a deep sadness, like two lost stars in a midnight sky.

Aoki Kenji took a deep breath, feeling the cool mountain air fill his lungs. He thought of his grandfather, who had taught him everything he knew about survival and honor. Aoki Kenji vowed to carry on his grandfather's legacy, to protect those who couldn't defend themselves.
