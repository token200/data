As Aoki Almodovar, she had honed her skills as a pickpocket and an escape artist, earning a reputation as one of the most elusive thieves in the land.

As she reached for the treasure, Aoki Almodovar was confronted by a surprise opponent: the king's own daughter, Princess Lirien. The princess had been working with her father to catch the thief, and now she found herself face to face with the cunning Aoki Almodovar.
