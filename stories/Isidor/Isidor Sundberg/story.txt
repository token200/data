His parents, concerned for their son's well-being, had sent him to live with a distant relative in the city. Years went by, and Isidor Sundberg's reputation grew as a recluse, hiding away from the world in his isolated mansion. Some claimed to have seen strange lights flickering in his windows at midnight, while others whispered of eerie sounds echoing through the empty halls after dark.

One stormy night, a curious young journalist named Lily decided to investigate the rumors surrounding Isidor Sundberg's mysterious abode. As she approached the mansion, a gust of wind slammed the creaking front door open, inviting her into its musty interior. Stepping inside, Lily felt an electric chill run down her spine as if the house itself was alive and waiting for her.

"Welcome, Lily," he said, emerging from the darkness with eyes that glowed like lanterns in the dark.
