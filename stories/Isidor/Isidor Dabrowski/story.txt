His destination was the local pawn shop, where he hoped to acquire a valuable artifact rumored to be hidden among its dusty relics.

Isidor smiled, his eyes glinting with amusement. "I'm looking for something specific, my good man," he said, producing a small photograph from his pocket. The image depicted an antique locket, adorned with intricate patterns and symbols that seemed to shimmer in the dim light.

Isidor Dabrowski smiled again, his lips curling upward. "Let's just say I have... connections," he said, producing a handful of cash from his pocket and settling it on the counter. "I'll take the locket, please."
