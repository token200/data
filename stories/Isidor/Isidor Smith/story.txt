Moved by the girl's plight, Isidor Smith accepted the challenge and set to work on a project.

As word of the device spread, people from far and wide came to seek Isidor Smith's help. The inventor, humbled by their gratitude, continued to refine his design, driven by a desire to improve the lives of others. Years went by, and Isidor Smith's reputation grew, but he remained grounded, ever focused on using his gifts to uplift those around him. His legacy lived on, inspiring future generations to follow in his footsteps, to create machines that could soothe the suffering and ease the burden.
