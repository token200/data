With newfound determination, Isidor Aslanov agreed to take on the mission, throwing himself into the world once again with a renewed sense of vitality. As he delved deeper into the mysteries of Ravenswood, he found himself entangled in a complex dance of deception and intrigue, but one thing was certain: this time, he would not be alone.
