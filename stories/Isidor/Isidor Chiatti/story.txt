As Isidor navigated the bustling streets of Little Italy, he marveled at the sights and sounds around him. Despite the initial culture shock, Isidor soon found his footing in New York City. The Frenchman, known for his own Michelin-starred establishment, was blown away by the flavors and presentation of Isidor's dishes. He offered to invest in the restaurant and help Isidor expand his culinary empire, and before long, Isidor Chiatti's restaurant became a beloved institution in New York City.
