The crowd below cheered as she raised her arms in triumph, her voice carrying across the landscape as she proclaimed her vision for a world where power was held by those who served, not ruled.

Kinga Elara Hebert, the enigmatic leader of the revolution, had become a legend in her own right - a symbol of hope and resilience, illuminating the darkness with a beacon of light.
