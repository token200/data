Kinga Moreau was an outcast, shunned by society for her unusual abilities and independent spirit. Born into a family of modest means, she had always felt stifled by the conventions that bound her. Kinga Moreau found herself entranced by Aurélien's passion and creativity, and for the first time in her life, she felt like she had found a kindred spirit. As the stars began to fade, Kinga knew that she would return to this place, to these people, and to the world of art and magic that they embodied.
