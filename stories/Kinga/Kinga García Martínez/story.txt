As she grew older, her passion only intensified, driving her to excel in every aspect of her education.

As tensions simmered beneath the surface, Kinga García Martínez remained steadfast, relying on her intelligence and cunning to outmaneuver her foes. She knew that her greatest strength lay not in brute force or military might, but in the subtle art of diplomacy and strategic thinking. And so, she waited patiently, biding her time until the perfect moment to strike.
