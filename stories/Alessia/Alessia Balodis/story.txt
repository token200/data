Alessia Balodis felt a sense of awe wash over her, knowing that she stood on the threshold of one of humanity's greatest discoveries. And as she delved deeper into the heart of Zerzura, Alessia knew that her life's work had only just begun.

For weeks, Alessia Balodis remained in the lost city, unraveling its secrets and piecing together the puzzles of a forgotten culture.
