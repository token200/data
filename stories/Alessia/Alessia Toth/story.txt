It was addressed to Alessia Toth, and all it said was "Meet me at the old oak tree at midnight." "Mind if I join you?" she asked, taking a seat across from the stranger.

"I'd rather not," they said, their voice low and smooth. "But I suppose you're here for the same reason I am."

Alessia felt a spark of excitement ignite within her. Who was this mysterious figure, and what did they want from her? But before she could ask any more questions, the stranger vanished into thin air, leaving Alessia sitting alone in the café.
