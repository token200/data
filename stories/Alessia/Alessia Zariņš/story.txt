With renewed energy, Alessia dove back into her research, determined to uncover as much evidence as possible. As the sun began to set on another long day, Alessia finally felt like she had made some real progress. She took a deep breath, feeling a sense of satisfaction and purpose that came from knowing she was making a difference. Her parents would be proud, she thought, smiling to herself as she closed her laptop and headed home.

Despite the challenges and obstacles she faced, Alessia Zariņš remained steadfast in her commitment to truth-telling and justice.
