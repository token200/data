Alessia Rossi stepped onto the sun-kissed streets of Rome, her bright smile illuminating the cobblestone alleys. As a renowned archaeologist, she had spent years searching for the fabled Lost City of the Etruscans, and finally, she was one step closer to uncovering its secrets.

According to legend, the Lost City was hidden deep within the Roman countryside, guarded by treacherous ravines and cursed temples.
