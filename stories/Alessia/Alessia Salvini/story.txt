But Alessia didn't let the opinions of others deter her - she was driven by a passion for creativity that burned within her like a fire.

Alessia Salvini's journey to success hadn't always been easy. Growing up in a family of modest means, she had to work multiple jobs while pursuing her art degree at university. But she never gave up on her dreams, even when it seemed like no one else believed in her.
