Alessia's heart raced as she scribbled down notes and made plans to stake out the restaurant later that night. Alessia snapped photos and took notes, knowing that this was it - her big break. The next morning, she submitted her story to the paper, and within hours, it was picked up by every major news outlet in town.

Alessia Durchdenwald's scoop sent shockwaves through the city's government, and soon she was being hailed as one of the top young journalists in the business.
