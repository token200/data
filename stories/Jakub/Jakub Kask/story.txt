As they explored the city, Jakub Kask couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and accomplishment. He had spent years searching for this moment, and finally, it had arrived. The lost city of Zerzura was real, and Jakub Kask was its discoverer.

Jakub Kask knew that he had to be careful with what he discovered, and ensure that it was preserved for future generations to study and learn from. He vowed to use his discovery to shed light on the past, rather than exploiting its power for personal gain.
