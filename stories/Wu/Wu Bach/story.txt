Intrigued by the mysterious teachings, Wu Bach devoted himself to mastering the intricacies of Qi Gong. Undaunted by the danger, Wu Bach accepted the challenge. His full name echoed in his mind as he faced off against the ruthless opponents, his Qi Gong energy swirling around him like a vortex of protection.
