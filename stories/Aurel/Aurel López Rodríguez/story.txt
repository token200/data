As he grew older, Aurel became increasingly fascinated with the world beyond San Miguel's borders. He spent hours poring over maps and books in the local library, dreaming of adventures that would take him far from home. His parents, though worried for his safety, encouraged Aurel's wanderlust, knowing that it was a part of who he was.

One day, Aurel López Rodríguez received an invitation to attend the prestigious Arturo Moreno School of Fine Arts in Mexico City. It was a dream come true – he would finally have the chance to hone his craft and pursue his passion for painting. With a mix of excitement and nerves, Aurel boarded a plane and set off on his journey.
