Her sharp wit and warm smile put even the grumpiest of patrons at ease, making everyone feel like they were the only one worth noticing.

Wilma's passion for social justice was unwavering. As a young woman, she had traveled to Mexico to work with migrant workers, where she witnessed firsthand the exploitation and abuse that many faced in the industry.
