Despite her reputation as a fierce competitor, Wilma had a soft spot for her friends and neighbors. She was always willing to lend a helping hand, whether it was fixing a flat tire or providing a sympathetic ear.

One day, the annual Oakdale fair rolled around, and Wilma decided to enter the baking competition with a show-stopping cake. She spent hours perfecting the recipe, carefully crafting each layer to be a masterclass in texture and flavor. Her full name, Wilma Evelyn Weber, was emblazoned on the front of the cake in bold, golden letters.
