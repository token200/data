Charlie Xia was a free-spirited artist who lived life on her own terms. Born Charlotte Mei-Xia, she had always been drawn to the unconventional and the unknown. Her parents wanted her to pursue a more "respectable" career, like medicine or law, but Charlie had other plans. She dropped out of college and moved to the city, where she quickly became immersed in the thriving art scene.

Charlie's own style was a unique blend of folk art and surrealism, reflecting her mixed heritage and eclectic influences.
