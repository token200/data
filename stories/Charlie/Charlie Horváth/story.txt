Charlie Horváth's talent quickly became apparent, and soon he was leading the group on stage at iconic venues like the New Morning in Paris and the Blue Note in Tokyo. His soulful saxophone playing transported audiences to another era, one of smoky jazz clubs and midnight revelries.

As the crowd's applause faded into the distance, Charlie Horváth took a deep breath and let his fingers wander across the keys of his saxophone.
