Years later, Charlie Kaminski had set out on his own, owning a small repair shop in the heart of the city. She explained that she had inherited an old Volkswagen Beetle from her grandfather, but it was in dire need of repair. Charlie Kaminski listened intently, his mind racing with possibilities.

Within hours, Charlie Kaminski had diagnosed the problem - a faulty timing belt - and set to work on the repairs. As he worked, he chatted with Sarah about everything from classic rock music to their shared love of antique cars. The two quickly hit it off, bonding over their shared passion for restoration and history.
