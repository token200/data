Now, as a young adult, Charlie was determined to make her artistic dreams a reality. She spent countless hours honing her skills at local art schools and workshops, learning from some of the most renowned artists in the city. Her full name, Charlotte Boulanger, echoed with pride each time she signed her artwork, knowing that every brushstroke represented a piece of herself.
