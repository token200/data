Upon arrival, Bohdan spotted Dr. Vasquez pacing nervously in the dimly lit space. She was shorter than expected, but her piercing green eyes seemed to bore into his very soul. "Bohdan Williams," she said, her voice laced with a hint of curiosity, "I've been expecting you."
