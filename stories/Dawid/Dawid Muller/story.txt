One day, while working in his cluttered office, Dawid Müller's mind wandered to an old problem that had been nagging him for years: the nature of time itself. It wasn't until he stumbled upon an obscure manuscript written by a 19th-century mathematician that the pieces finally fell into place.

With this newfound understanding, Dawid Müller's life began to take on a new sense of purpose.
