Dawid Vasiliev noticed that Viktor's eyes darted around the room, as if he was afraid of being watched. It was then that Dawid made his move, using his charm and wit to draw Viktor into a trap.

As the night wore on, Dawid Vasiliev revealed his true intentions, and Viktor Petrov crumbled under the pressure. With the evidence they had gathered, the police were able to apprehend the killer and bring closure to the victim's family.

Dawid Vasiliev smiled to himself as he watched the perpetrator being led away in handcuffs.
