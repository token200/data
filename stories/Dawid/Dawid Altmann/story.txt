Dawid Altmann was known for his unique approach to art, often incorporating recycled materials into his sculptures. Growing up in a small village surrounded by nature, Dawid developed a deep appreciation for the natural world and its potential to inspire creativity.

Dawid spent weeks planning and designing his piece, which he titled "Ephemeral Bloom." As the festival approached, Dawid worked tirelessly to complete his masterpiece.
