Elias Schafer arrived at the police station, shaking off the rain as he made his way to Rachel's office. "What do you need me to do?" he asked, his voice low and even.

Elias listened intently, his mind already racing with possibilities. He spent the next few hours poring over Sarah's phone records and interviewing witnesses. And then, just as he was about to pack up for the night, a small breakthrough emerged - a text message from an unknown number, hinting that Sarah had been in contact with someone who knew her whereabouts.

With newfound determination, Elias Schafer set out to track down the sender of the mysterious message. He followed a trail of digital breadcrumbs, his eyes scanning the city's streets for any sign of life. And finally, after hours of searching, he found himself standing outside a nondescript apartment building in the heart of the city.
