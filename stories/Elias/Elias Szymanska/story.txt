Elias Szymanska listened intently as the man described his vision: a majestic stag with glowing eyes that seemed to stare into the soul. Without hesitation, Elias set to work, carefully selecting the finest materials and employing his expertise to create an illusion of life.

As the days passed, the stag began to take shape, its eyes glinting with an otherworldly light. When it was finally complete, Elias Szymanska stepped back, admiring his handiwork with a quiet satisfaction. The collector was overjoyed, and soon word of Elias's exceptional talent spread throughout the art world.
