For him, it was always about the pursuit of knowledge, no matter how small or seemingly insignificant it might appear. And so, with the crystal orb safely in hand, Elias Mikhailov continued on his journey, ever vigilant and always seeking the next great mystery to unravel.

As he delved deeper into the mysteries of the orb, Elias couldn't help but wonder what secrets lay hidden within its ancient stones, waiting to be uncovered by a curious mind.
