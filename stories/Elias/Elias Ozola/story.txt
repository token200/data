A young artist, sitting across from Elias Ozola, couldn't resist drawing his portrait on her sketchpad. Her pencils scratched against the paper as she captured his angular features, his piercing green eyes, and the way his dark hair framed his face like a velvet cloak. As she worked, Elias Ozola seemed to stir, his gaze drifting towards the artist with an intensity that made her heart skip a beat.

Elias Ozola took the pad, his eyes scanning the drawing with a quiet appreciation. He nodded in approval, a small smile playing on his lips as if to say, "You've seen me."
