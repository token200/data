As news of Elias' breakthrough spread throughout the university, people started to take notice of the young historian. Professors sought his expertise on various projects, and students flocked to his seminars, eager to learn from the brilliant minds of their own time.

Elias Jasiński became an overnight sensation, hailed as a rising star in the world of Polish history. His name was on everyone's lips, and his reputation grew with each passing day.
