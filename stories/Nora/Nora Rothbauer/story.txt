"Welcome to Galerie Frei," she said. "We're hosting an exhibit tonight celebrating German Expressionism."

Nora's eyes scanned the room, taking in the intricate details of each piece on display. Her own passion for art and photography had led her to this moment, as if Nora Rothbauer was meant to be here.

Nora nodded, feeling a sense of connection with this stranger. "Yes, I am," she replied. "Photography and writing are my passions."

Max smiled warmly. "I think we could have a discussion about the relationship between art and storytelling. It's an area that fascinates me greatly."
