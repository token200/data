Nora Sundberg stepped off the train and onto the worn platform, her eyes scanning the familiar streets of Ravenswood. Nora Sundberg smiled, feeling a sense of homecoming wash over her. She spent the afternoon catching up with old friends and reacquainting herself with the rhythms of small-town life.
