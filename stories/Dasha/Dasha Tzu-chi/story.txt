As Dasha Tzu-chi grew older, her artistic talents became more refined, and she began to pursue a career in fine arts. She enrolled in a prestigious university program, where she honed her skills under the guidance of esteemed professors. Her portfolio quickly gained attention, and she was soon featured in several local art exhibitions.
