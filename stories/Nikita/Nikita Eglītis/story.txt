As Nikita Eglītis, she had built a reputation for herself as a fearless and tenacious reporter, never backing down from a challenge.

As she spoke with him, she noticed that he seemed nervous, glancing over his shoulder as if he feared being caught. Yet, there was something about Viktor's demeanor that drew Nikita Eglītis in, making her want to trust him.
