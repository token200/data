After days of traveling, Janek Sadyrbaeva finally reached the foot of a towering sand dune, which marked the entrance to the hidden oasis. With a sense of excitement and trepidation, she began the arduous climb up the dune's slope, her heart pounding in anticipation of what lay ahead.
