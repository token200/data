The victim was a young woman, found dead in an abandoned warehouse on the outskirts of town. The only clue was a small note left at the scene, with a cryptic message that read: "The eyes that see are not the ones that look."

Janek Weissner arrived at the crime scene, his mind already racing with theories and possibilities. He remembered a similar case from several years ago - a murder that had left the killer's identity shrouded in mystery. The similarities were striking: both victims had been found with the same type of tattoo, and both cases featured a similar cryptic message.

With newfound determination, Janek Weissner set to work, following a trail of clues that led him from the streets to the upper echelons of society.
