As Marie Leblanc's skills improved, she began to study art in earnest, pouring over books and attending workshops to hone her craft. Her passion for color and light was evident in every brushstroke, and soon her paintings were sought after by collectors from all over the region.

Despite her success, Marie Leblanc remained humble and grounded, crediting her family's rustic upbringing for instilling in her a deep appreciation for the simple things in life.
