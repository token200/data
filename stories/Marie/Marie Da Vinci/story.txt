As she matured, Marie became increasingly fascinated with engineering and anatomy, earning her a reputation as one of the most brilliant minds of her era.

As Marie's reputation grew, so did her influence. She became a respected figure in the art world, sought after by collectors and patrons alike. Her inventive genius, meanwhile, earned her recognition from the scientific community. Marie Da Vinci was now a name synonymous with innovation and excellence.
