Ilze's sharp instincts proved invaluable as she dug deeper into the world of underground art, uncovering a web of deceit and corruption.

But with K by her side, she stood firm in her determination to reveal the hidden truths of Riga's vibrant artistic underworld. Her sharp mind and quick thinking allowed her to stay one step ahead of her adversaries.
