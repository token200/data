Ilze Pold's reputation as a master sleuth preceded her, and the local police department begged for her expertise in tracking down the thieving culprits. With an unyielding passion for uncovering the truth, Ilze Pold took on the challenge, following a trail of cryptic clues that led her from the cobblestone streets to the city's grandest museums.

Her eyes gleamed in the dim light as she finally laid hands on a crucial artifact – an ancient journal belonging to Riga's own King Valdemar.
