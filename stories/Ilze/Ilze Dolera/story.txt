Growing up in Riga had been a mixed blessing – on one hand, she had always been drawn to the stories of her grandfather, a veteran of World War II who had fought bravely alongside the Latvian Resistance; on the other hand, her parents' tumultuous marriage had left emotional scars that still lingered long after they'd moved away.
