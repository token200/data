As she grew older, Ma Wei-lun's passion for botany only intensified, and she went on to study at the National Taiwan University, where she earned a degree in plant biology. Years later, Ma Wei-lun was leading an expedition deep into the Taiwanese wilderness, searching for a new species of flowering plant that was rumored to have extraordinary healing properties. As news of Ma Wei-lun's discovery spread, she became an overnight sensation in scientific circles. She was hailed as a leading expert in plant biology, and her name was on everyone's lips. For Ma Wei-lun, there was no greater joy than discovering new species and exploring the uncharted territories of Taiwan's flora – it was a life she had always known, and one that would continue to shape her for years to come.
