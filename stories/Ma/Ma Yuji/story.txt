Ma Yuji didn't believe it. She was convinced that with enough research and determination, she could uncover the truth behind the enigmatic city. Her eyes scanned the crowded streets, taking in the faces of passersby as they rushed to and fro. And finally, after months of searching, she had found it – a cryptic reference to Zhendong hidden within the pages of an obscure text.

Ma Yuji's heart skipped a beat as she read the words, her mind racing with the possibilities. She felt like she was on the cusp of something incredible, something that would change the course of history.
