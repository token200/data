As a renowned baker, she spent her days conjuring up delicious treats that drew in customers from all over. She remembered being fascinated by the way Ma Fernández González could transform simple ingredients into mouthwatering masterpieces that made everyone's taste buds dance with joy.

Years went by, and Ma Fernández González honed her skills, experimenting with new recipes and techniques. Her dedication paid off when 'La Casa de Abuela' became the go-to destination for special occasions and celebrations in San Miguel. People would travel from afar just to indulge in Ma Fernández González's signature creations: the flaky croissants, the tender empanadas, and of course, her famous churros con chocolate.

Ma Fernández González greeted them with a warm smile, offering samples of her latest creations. As they munched on the treats, she shared stories about her own childhood and how baking had brought her so much happiness.
