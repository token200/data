One fateful evening, a young traveler stumbled upon Ma Björkqvist as she sat beneath a willow tree, surrounded by scattered pages and candlelight. Though few knew the truth about Ma Björkqvist's mysterious nature, her legend lived on in the hearts of those who had glimpsed the free-spirited librarian beneath the veil of darkness.
