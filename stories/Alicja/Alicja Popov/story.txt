Her music was like nothing else – hauntingly beautiful, yet deeply rooted in tradition and culture. Alicja's eyes scanned the sea of faces before her, searching for those who truly understood her music. She had grown up listening to the sounds of the old country, learning to play from her father, a master violinist himself.
