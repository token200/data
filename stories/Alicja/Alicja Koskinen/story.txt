Alicja Koskinen stepped out of the dimly lit alleyway and onto the bustling streets of Helsinki. As a renowned folk musician from Finland, she was no stranger to the city's vibrant music scene. Her lyrics, infused with the mystical and often melancholic world of Finnish folklore, resonated deeply with the audience. As she sang about the spirits that roamed the forests and the ghosts that haunted the lakes, Alicja's music seemed to transport the listeners to another realm.
