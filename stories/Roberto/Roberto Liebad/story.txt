At first, nothing seemed out of the ordinary. But as the hours ticked by, strange noises began to echo through the halls: creaks and groans that sounded almost human. Roberto Liebad's eyes narrowed as he watched the camera footage on his phone. The images were grainy, but one figure in particular caught his attention - a woman with piercing green eyes, walking down the staircase.
