As the sun set over the city, Oliver Nurlanov knew that he had found a new partner in crime-solving – one who shared his passion for justice and his unwavering commitment to uncovering the truth. And with Sophia by his side, Oliver Nurlanov was ready to take on whatever challenges lay ahead.
