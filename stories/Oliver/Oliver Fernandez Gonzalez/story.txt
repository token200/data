Oliver was deeply moved by Sophia's story and decided to write a feature article about her experiences. With the help of his editor, he crafted a powerful piece that highlighted the struggles faced by many homeless individuals in the city.

The article, titled "A Homeless Soul," was published on the front page of the newspaper, and it sparked a wave of public outcry and support for Sophia and others like her.
