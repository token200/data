One day, Lia Jarvinen received an unexpected visit from the town's mayor, who asked her to create a special cake for the upcoming harvest festival. The mayor was impressed by Lia Jarvinen's skills and confidence, and he saw this as an opportunity to showcase her talents to the wider world.
