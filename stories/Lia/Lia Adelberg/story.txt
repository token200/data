Lia Adelberg sat alone in her small studio apartment, staring blankly at the canvas before her. Now, as Lia Adelberg sat amidst her own canvases, trying to find inspiration once again, she couldn't help but wonder if she was living up to her mother's expectations. Was she good enough? Did her art truly capture the essence of her mother's legacy?
