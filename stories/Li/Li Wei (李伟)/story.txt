His team worked tirelessly behind the scenes, pouring over data and running simulations. The promise of unlocking new secrets of the universe drove them all, especially Li Wei.

His eyes remained fixed on the stars as he pondered the mysteries that lay beyond human comprehension. Some said he was a visionary, while others whispered about his obsession with the unknown.
