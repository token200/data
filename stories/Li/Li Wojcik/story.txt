Born as Li Wojcik in the small village of Zhongshan, China, she had always been fascinated by the art of weaving. As Li Wojcik grew older, she became increasingly skilled in her craft, experimenting with different patterns and colors to create unique and breathtaking pieces. They were amazed by the beauty and quality of her textiles and asked if they could purchase some of her finest work. Li Wojcik agreed, and soon her wares were in high demand among the merchants' clients.

Though she remained humble and grounded, her reputation as a master weaver grew, inspiring countless others to follow in her footsteps.
