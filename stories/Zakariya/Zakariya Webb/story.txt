Zakariya Webb was a man with a mysterious past. He had grown up on the streets, surviving by his wits and cunning. As he grew older, he began to realize that he had a talent for art, and soon found himself working as a graffiti artist in the city.

Zakariya was torn. On one hand, the opportunity was too good to pass up. He could finally make a name for himself as an artist and provide for himself without fear of poverty or persecution. On the other hand, he felt a strong connection to his roots in the city's underbelly.

In the end, Zakariya made the decision to take a chance on the dealer. He agreed to leave the streets behind and focus on his art full-time. It wasn't an easy transition - there were times when he missed the freedom and anonymity of his old life. But with time and practice, he found a new sense of purpose in the gallery.
