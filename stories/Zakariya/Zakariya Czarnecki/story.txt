Undeterred, Zakariya continued to explore the fringes of magic, seeking out ancient texts and forbidden knowledge hidden within dusty libraries and crumbling ruins. With newfound determination, Zakariya devoted herself to mastering the forbidden arts. Her practice became increasingly intense, as she delved deeper into the mysteries of the unknown. Despite their warnings, Zakariya persevered, driven by an insatiable hunger for knowledge. She spent long hours in her cramped chamber, surrounded by candles, crystals, and scattered parchments. As the days passed, Zakariya's abilities grew stronger, but so did the whispers of her reputation. Some claimed to have seen her conjuring shadowy apparitions, while others spoke of hearing eerie whispers carried on the wind. Zakariya Czarnecki remained an enigma, a mystic shrouded in mystery.

Her peers regarded her with a mixture of awe and trepidation, knowing that her powers were still raw and unpredictable.
