As she grew older, Natalia's fascination with the unknown only deepened, leading her to pursue a career in cartography and exploration.

One day, while on an expedition in the Amazon, Natalia stumbled upon a long-lost tribe deep in the jungle. The encounter was nothing short of magical, as the tribe's shamaness recognized Natalia as a kindred spirit, sharing with her ancient knowledge of the forest and its secrets.
