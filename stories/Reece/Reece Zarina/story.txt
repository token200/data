She was encouraged by her parents, both artists themselves, to explore her creativity and never be afraid to express herself. And so, Reece Zarina set out on a journey to capture the essence of her city through her art.

As the painting neared completion, Reece stepped back to admire her handiwork, feeling an overwhelming sense of pride and accomplishment. Reece Zarina knew that this project marked only the beginning of her journey as an artist and activist.
