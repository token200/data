Reece Mendes was a young and talented musician who had been making waves in the indie rock scene with his unique sound and energetic live performances. Born Reece James Mendes on a chilly autumn evening in 1995, he grew up surrounded by music, with his parents owning a small record store that played host to countless local bands.

As Reece's music gained traction, he started to receive offers from record labels and management companies. Reece Mendes took to social media to share his message, urging fans not to sell out in pursuit of fame.
