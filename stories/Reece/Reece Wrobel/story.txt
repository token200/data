Reece Wrobel stepped into the dimly lit room, her eyes adjusting slowly to the darkness. As a private investigator, she had seen her fair share of seedy characters and cramped spaces, but this one took the cake. The air was thick with cigarette smoke and desperation.

Reece's eyes locked onto a particular photo – one of her own sister, who had gone missing five years ago. She felt a pang of guilt and regret, wondering if she could have done something differently to prevent the tragedy.

Reece's expression hardened, determination burning within her. She would uncover the truth, no matter what it took. The game was afoot, and she was ready to play.
