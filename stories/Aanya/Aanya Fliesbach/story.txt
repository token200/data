As she prepared for the exhibition, Aanya Fliesbach found herself becoming increasingly obsessed with the theme of "flight." With this concept in mind, Aanya Fliesbach created a series of large-scale paintings that depicted humans suspended mid-air, surrounded by swirling clouds and kaleidoscopic colors.
