Aanya remembered the way he would point out constellations, telling her stories of myth and legend that had captivated her imagination.

But now, with each passing day, Aanya felt like she was losing herself in the process.

With renewed determination, Aanya decided to take a break from her research and embark on a solo journey to rekindle her passion for astronomy.
