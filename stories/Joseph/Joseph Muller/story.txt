Overwhelmed by excitement and nervousness, Joseph accepted the challenge head-on.

Weeks passed, and Joseph immersed himself in his work, experimenting with different materials and techniques. He spent countless hours fine-tuning every detail, from the curves of the armrest to the intricacies of the chair's backrest. As he worked tirelessly, Joseph Muller became completely absorbed in his art, losing all sense of time.
