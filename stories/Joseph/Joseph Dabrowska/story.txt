As he grew older, Joseph's fascination with journalism only intensified. Despite the risks, he decided to pursue the lead, and what he uncovered would change the course of his life forever.

As the investigation continued, Joseph found himself in increasing danger. He received threatening phone calls and emails, warning him to drop the story altogether. But he refused to back down, driven by his conviction that the truth must be told.

The night before the publication of the final installment of J.D.'s exposé, Joseph was summoned by a mysterious figure who revealed himself as an old friend from Joseph's childhood. "I've been watching you, Józef," the man said, using Joseph's full name. "You have proven yourself to be a true journalist. I will make sure that your story is told in its entirety."
