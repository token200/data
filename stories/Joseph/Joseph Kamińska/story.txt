After the presentation, Joseph engaged in lively discussions with fellow scientists. He fielded questions and critiques with poise, his responses weaving a narrative that was both erudite and accessible.

As the night drew to a close, Joseph retired to his hotel room, exhausted but exhilarated by the day's events. His name, Joseph Kamińska, echoed in his mind like a mantra – a reminder of the incredible work he had accomplished.

The next morning, as Joseph boarded the train back to Poland, he couldn't shake the feeling that his research was on the cusp of something momentous.
