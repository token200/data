Years went by, and Joseph's reputation grew. People began to whisper about the "angel of the streets." Some said it was him, while others claimed it was just a myth. As Sarah grew stronger, she began to ask questions about Joseph's past. But he always seemed to dodge the topic, leaving her with more questions than answers. Despite this, their bond grew stronger. They became inseparable, and Sarah started to see a different side of Joseph Balode.

For the first time in his life, Joseph felt like he had found someone who truly understood him. He began to open up, sharing stories about his childhood and the struggles he had faced. And as he spoke, his eyes sparkled with a warmth that few people had ever seen before.

As the days turned into weeks, Sarah realized that there was more to Joseph Balode than met the eye. She saw a man who was not just an angel, but also a complex human being, full of flaws and scars.
