Over coffee, she shared her own experiences as a young model, from the highs of walking the catwalk to the lows of dealing with cruel critics. Monika Dupuis taught Alex how to handle himself in the cutthroat world of fashion, and soon he was landing major clients and building a reputation that rivaled some of the industry's biggest names.

Thanks to Monika's mentorship, Alex went on to achieve great things, but more importantly, he learned valuable lessons about perseverance, hard work, and staying true to oneself.
