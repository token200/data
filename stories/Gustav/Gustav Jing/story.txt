But Gustav Jing was so much more than that. He was a complex web of contradictions - a ruthless warrior with a soft spot for those in need. His latest mission had brought him to the small town of Ravenswood, where rumors of a powerful artifact had drawn him and several other treasure hunters.

He spotted a group of rough-looking men eyeing him from across the way, their faces twisted with hostility. With a subtle smile, Gustav Jing sauntered over to them, his hand resting on the hilt of his sword.
