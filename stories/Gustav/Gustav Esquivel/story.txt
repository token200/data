Gustav Esquivel stepped off the train and onto the worn platform, gazing out at the misty dawn breaking over the small town of Ravenswood. But as he walked through the deserted streets, the silence was starting to wear thin.

But it was all taken away from him when tragedy struck: his girlfriend's death in a car accident left Gustav shattered.

He took a deep breath, feeling the weight of his grief slowly lifting, and began to walk once more. This time, he didn't feel like hiding; this time, he felt like finding himself again.
