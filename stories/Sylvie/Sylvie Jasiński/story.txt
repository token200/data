Marcin handed Sylvie a small USB drive containing evidence of embezzlement, money laundering, and bribery on an epic scale. "It's all connected to the current mayor," he whispered before vanishing into the night, leaving Sylvie stunned.

Back at her office, Sylvie Jasiński spent hours poring over the files, piecing together a shocking narrative that implicated some of the city's most influential figures. She knew she had to tread carefully, as this story could put her own life in danger.
