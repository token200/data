Ebba Zieliński stepped out of the dimly lit alleyway and onto the bustling streets of Warsaw's Old Town. Ebba Zieliński adjusted her beret, running a hand through her curly brown hair as she scanned the crowded streets. The thought of creating something new and exciting filled her with excitement, and she quickened her pace.
