Ebba Astrid Sandstrom had always been fascinated by the art world. Despite her parents' wishes for her to pursue a more traditional career, Ebba's passion for art only grew stronger.

He was blown away by the depth and emotion she brought to each piece, and his review catapulted her to international stardom.
