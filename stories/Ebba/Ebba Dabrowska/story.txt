Ebba Dabrowska was a free-spirited artist with a wild mane of curly brown hair and a paint-stained apron that told stories of her own. As she wandered through the streets of Krakow, Poland, her eyes sparkled with creativity, drinking in the vibrant colors and textures of the city.

Ebba Dabrowska was both thrilled and intimidated by the prospect, but she knew that this was an opportunity she couldn't miss.
