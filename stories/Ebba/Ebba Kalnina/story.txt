Ebba Maria Kalnina was a woman with a passion for preserving traditional Latvian culture. Growing up in a small village on the outskirts of Riga, she had always been fascinated by the stories of her ancestors and the customs they practiced. Years later, Ebba Maria Kalnina had become a renowned expert in Latvian folk traditions. One day, Ebba Maria Kalnina received an invitation from a young couple who wanted to get married in a traditional Latvian ceremony. They had heard about her work and wanted to learn more about their cultural roots. Ebba Maria Kalnina was thrilled to share her knowledge with them and spent hours teaching them about the customs, rituals, and traditions of their ancestors.

As the big day approached, Ebba Maria Kalnina helped the young couple prepare for their wedding.
