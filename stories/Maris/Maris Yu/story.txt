Torn between her desire for anonymity and the potential impact of her actions, Maris knew she had to make a decision. She deleted the email and went back online, her fingers flying across the keyboard as she crafted a response that would keep her identity hidden while still conveying the message.

The journalist was clever, but Maris's skills were greater. With a satisfied smile, she sent the response and retreated back into the shadows, waiting for the fallout to unfold.
