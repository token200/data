Enthralled by Sofia's vision and skill, Maris knew she had to share it with the world.

Maris Mendes spent the next few weeks researching and promoting Sofia's work, reaching out to collectors and curators across the city. Slowly but surely, her efforts began to pay off, and soon Sofia's art was being showcased in some of the most respected galleries in town.

As Maris watched Sofia's career take off, she couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and satisfaction. She had played a key role in bringing this talented artist to the forefront of the art world, and it was a feeling unlike any other.

Over time, Maris Mendes continued to work behind the scenes, using her knowledge and skills to nurture and promote emerging artists like Sofia. Her dedication earned her recognition within the industry, and soon she found herself at the helm of her own successful gallery, where she could share her passion for art with a wider audience.
