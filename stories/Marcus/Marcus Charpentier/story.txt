The sign above the door read "Curios and Antiques," but it was the peculiar assortment of items in the window that caught his attention. Intrigued, Marcus pushed open the door and stepped inside. The shop was dimly lit, with shelves upon shelves of peculiar objects that seemed to hold secrets within their dusty surfaces. Behind the counter stood an elderly woman with piercing green eyes, who regarded Marcus with a knowing glint.

As he opened the book, the pages revealed intricate drawings of cityscapes, each one reflecting a different time and place.
