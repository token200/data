As she worked behind the counter, Eleanor Chun-yi couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and accomplishment. She had taken a chance on an antique shop, and it had led her to discover a new passion and expand her business. Her full name, Eleanor Chun-yi, was etched in the minds of her customers as a symbol of excellence and innovation.
