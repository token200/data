Her parents, though loving and supportive, struggled to understand their daughter's affinity for the unknown.

In that moment, Eleanor Berzina knew she was meant for more than just code and circuitry. She felt an ancient power stirring within her, one that threatened to consume her very being. With trembling hands, she deleted the anomaly, sending shivers down her spine.
