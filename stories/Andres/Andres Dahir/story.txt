One day, a group of local villagers approached Andres with concerns about the impact of fishing on their community's food supply. Andres listened intently as they shared their stories and struggles. He knew that he had to do something to help.

With the support of his colleagues and funding from organizations around the world, Andres set out to create a sustainable fishing program for the villagers. It was a complex task, but Andres Dahir was determined to see it through.
