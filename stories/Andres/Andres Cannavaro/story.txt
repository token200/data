As Andres closed in on the truth, he received a chilling warning from an anonymous source: "Leave this case alone, or you'll be next." But Andres Cannavaro was not one to back down from a challenge. He knew that justice had to be served, no matter the cost.

With newfound determination, Andres pressed on, driven by his commitment to protecting Sarah and bringing her abuser to justice.
