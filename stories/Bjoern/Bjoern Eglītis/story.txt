His team of assistants would often find him huddled over a stack of notes and papers, poring over the data he had collected from his nocturnal explorations.

With trembling hands, Bjoern Eglītis reached for his camera and snapped a photo of the symbol. As he did so, he felt an icy chill run down his spine – he was certain that he was being watched. Turning to leave, he caught a glimpse of a shadowy figure darting around the corner.
