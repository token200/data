But Bjoern Cámara was more than just a charming rogue - he was a chameleon, able to blend in seamlessly with his surroundings. He could be a aristocrat one moment, and a beggar the next, all with the same effortless ease that came from years of practice.

The sign above the door read "Curios and Antiques", and something about it drew him in. He pushed open the door, and was immediately struck by the sheer variety of artifacts on display.

Bjoern Cámara smiled, his eyes scanning the shelves as he made his way deeper into the shop. He was a man who knew what he wanted, and he could always find it - even if it meant parting others from their hard-earned cash.
