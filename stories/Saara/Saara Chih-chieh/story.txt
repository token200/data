Wherever she roamed, Saara Chih-chieh's eyes would absorb every detail – a smile on a stranger's face, the vibrant colors of street art, or the way light danced through a forgotten alley.

Intrigued by the intricate patterns woven into the fabrics, she began to sketch on her pad, bringing the designs to life with bold strokes and vibrant hues.
