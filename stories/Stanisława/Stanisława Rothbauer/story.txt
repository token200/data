With each new piece, Stanisława felt herself becoming more confident as an artist, and her name was starting to echo through the streets of Krakow.

Despite her growing success, Stanisława remained humble and true to her artistic vision. She continued to paint from the heart, pouring emotions onto canvas like a confessional. Her art became a reflection of her own experiences, hopes, and fears – a window into the soul of Stanisława Rothbauer.
