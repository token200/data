Luna Elara Mitchell's eyes sparkled as she took in the offerings. She loved spending afternoons like this, indulging in sweet treats while lost in her art. As they munched on pastries, Luna Elara Mitchell began to hum a tune, which Leo immediately recognized as one of her famous folk songs.
