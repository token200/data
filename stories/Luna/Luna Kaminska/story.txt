Hours passed, and Luna found herself lost in the world of myths and legends. The store's owner, an elderly woman with piercing green eyes, watched her from behind the counter, a knowing smile on her face. As the sun dipped below the horizon, Luna reluctantly closed the book and made her way to the checkout.

As she paid for her purchase, Luna Elisabeth Kaminska felt a sense of wonder wash over her. The world outside seemed different now - richer, more vibrant, and full of secrets waiting to be uncovered.
