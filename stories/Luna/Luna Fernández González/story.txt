As she wandered through the winding alleys, taking in the sights and sounds of the bustling city, she stumbled upon a quaint little café tucked away on a quiet corner. As the afternoon wore on, Luna Fernández González made her way back to her workshop, feeling invigorated by her chance encounter with fellow artist Luna Fernández González.
