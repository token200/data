As she delved deeper into her research, Luna Masaru began to notice a strange energy emanating from within herself. It was as if the very fabric of reality was responding to her presence, resonating with an otherworldly frequency that seemed to be attuning itself to her heartbeat.

With each passing moment, Luna Masaru's connection to Aethoria grew stronger. She felt the pulse of this mystical realm coursing through her veins like a mighty river, imbuing her with ancient wisdom and forgotten knowledge.

The prophecy began to reveal its secrets to her, speaking of an era when the dimensions would converge in perfect harmony. And Luna Masaru, with her unique essence and interdimensional abilities, was destined to play a pivotal role in bringing about this glorious balance.
