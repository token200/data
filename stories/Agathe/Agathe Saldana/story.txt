Agathe Saldana stepped out of her small apartment and onto the bustling streets of Paris. As a young artist, she had always been drawn to the city's vibrant energy, and it was where she found inspiration for her latest project.

Agathe Saldana's eyes scanned the crowded cafes and art galleries, searching for the perfect spot to set up her easel. After hours of searching, Agathe Saldana finally found the perfect spot – a small corner cafe overlooking the river. She set up her easel, adjusted her paints, and began to work on her latest piece.
