Elsa was hesitant at first, but the more she thought about it, the more appealing the idea became. With the release of her debut solo album, "Frozen Heart," Elsa Liepins finally felt like she was in control. Her music was taking on a life of its own, and she was proud to share it with the world. As she stepped onto the stage for her first headlining tour, Elsa felt a sense of liberation wash over her.
