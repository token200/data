Elsa Nemeth stood at the edge of the frozen lake, her breath visible in the crisp morning air. Elsa Nemeth took a deep breath and let the cold air fill her lungs, feeling it wash away some of the tension that had been building up inside her. Elsa Nemeth's mind wandered back to the last show she had played, where things had gotten out of hand.
