With her talent for creating vibrant murals and intricate patterns, Elsa could command high prices at local galleries and collectives. Elsa Volkov was not just an artist, however; she was also a fiercely independent individual with a sharp mind and quick wit. She had developed a keen sense of self-preservation and could think on her feet when faced with danger or uncertainty.

Elsa was hesitant at first but eventually agreed to meet with him.

As she entered the opulent foyer, she was greeted by the very man who had offered her the deal of a lifetime. The tension between them was palpable, but Elsa Volkov stood firm, ready to negotiate from a position of strength.
