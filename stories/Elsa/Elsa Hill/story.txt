Her powers, which had once been both a blessing and a curse, had forced her to withdraw from the world, afraid of hurting those she loved with her unpredictable abilities. But as time passed, she began to realize that there was more to life than just hiding away.

She missed her sister, Anna, and their friends, who had all been drawn to her like moths to flame despite the danger her powers posed. Elsa Hill knew she couldn't stay hidden forever; eventually, she would have to face the world again.
