She was finally free from the pressures of royal duty, able to use her powers for good without having to worry about the consequences. Her sister, Anna, was safe in the kingdom, and Elsa Dell knew that she had made the right decision.

As the sun dipped below the horizon, casting the world in a soft blue glow, Elsa Dell knew that she had found her true purpose. She was no longer just the Snow Queen of Everia; she was Elsa Dell, protector of her people and guardian of the frozen lands. And as she looked down at the child in front of her, she knew that this was where she belonged - surrounded by snow and ice, but also with those who needed her most.

Elsa Dell's heart swelled with a sense of belonging, and for the first time in years, she felt like she had finally found her place in the world.
