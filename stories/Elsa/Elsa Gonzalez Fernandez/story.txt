As she grew older, Elsa's passion for science only intensified, leading her to pursue a degree in physics from the prestigious University of Madrid. Overjoyed and relieved, Elsa accepted the offer, knowing that this funding would take her work to the next level.

As she delved deeper into her research, Elsa began to make remarkable breakthroughs. Her innovative design allowed for more efficient energy production, making it possible to power entire cities with renewable energy. News of her achievement spread quickly, and soon Elsa Gonzalez Fernandez was hailed as a leading expert in her field.

Despite the accolades, Elsa remained humble and dedicated to her work. She continued to refine her technology, pushing the boundaries of what was thought possible.
