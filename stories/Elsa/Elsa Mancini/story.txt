Her search led her to a small café on the outskirts of town, where she spotted a figure from her own past sitting in the corner.

Elsa Mancini's heart skipped a beat as she watched the woman from her childhood days sipping a cup of coffee, unaware that Elsa was watching her. The woman, with piercing green eyes and curly brown hair, looked up as if sensing Elsa's gaze, their eyes locking for a brief moment before the woman quickly looked away.
