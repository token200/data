Excitement coursed through her veins as Celia approached the plant, taking care not to damage its delicate petals. She gently collected a sample, careful to preserve it for further study. This find would be the breakthrough she needed to complete her thesis and establish herself as a leading expert in her field.

Celia Klein smiled to herself as she made her way back up the trail, the thrill of discovery still coursing through her veins.
