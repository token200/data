After completing culinary school, Ella set out on a journey that took her from bustling streets of Bangkok to trendy restaurants in New York City. Along the way, she gathered an arsenal of techniques and recipes that she would later draw upon to create her own signature dishes.

Ella's big break came when she was invited to cook at a high-profile food festival in Taipei. Her booth quickly became the most popular destination among attendees, who were wowed by the bold flavors and intricate presentation of her dishes. Word of Ella Chia-wei's talents spread like wildfire, and soon she was receiving offers from top restaurants around the globe.

The night of the dinner service arrived, and Ella Chia-wei took her place alongside some of the world's top chefs.
