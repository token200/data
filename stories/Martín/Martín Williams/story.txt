Martín Williams was a man with a passion for music and art. Born into a family of modest means, he had always found solace in the creative pursuits that brought him joy. As Martín grew older, his love for music only deepened, and he became determined to make a name for himself as a musician. She was immediately drawn to the raw talent and charisma that Martín possessed both on and off stage, and offered him a deal to showcase his work alongside her own sculptures at an upcoming gallery exhibition.

He continued to teach himself new skills, experimenting with everything from electronic production to traditional instruments. Sophia remained a close friend and mentor, introducing him to some of the most influential figures in the art world.
