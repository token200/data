As Martín Kovalenko slipped into the museum's ventilation system, his heart pounded in anticipation. Martín Kovalenko emerged into the night air, the cool breeze carrying the whispers of the city. He smiled to himself, knowing that he had outwitted even the most vigilant of guardians. The gemstone safely tucked away in his pocket, Martín Kovalenko melted into the crowd, disappearing like a phantom into the urban landscape.
