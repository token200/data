Intrigued, Thorbjorn Ozola took the box back to his workshop and began to study it carefully. He spent hours poring over ancient texts and seeking guidance from fellow puzzle enthusiasts. Slowly but surely, the pieces began to fall into place.

 Inside, he found a small, golden amulet adorned with the same symbols he had deciphered earlier. According to legend, this amulet granted its wearer immense wisdom and problem-solving abilities.
