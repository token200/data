Thorbjorn Nowak's eyes widened as he flipped through the pages, his mind racing with possibilities.

With this explosive story, Thorbjorn Nowak landed himself on the cover of every major newspaper in town. Suddenly, he was in high demand as a source for other investigations. He continued to dig deeper, his sharp instincts guiding him towards new scandals and secrets that others had missed.

As his reputation grew, so did his sense of unease. There were whispers that someone had recognized him from the warehouse meeting, someone who wanted Thorbjorn Nowak to drop the story altogether.
