Without hesitation, Thorbjorn accepted the challenge and set off with a small band of warriors.

When they finally reached King Harald's court, Thorbjorn was greeted as a hero. The king himself praised Thorbjorn's bravery and offered him a place at his side as a trusted advisor. And so, Thorbjorn Aurelius Antoninus Augustus became one of the most powerful men in Scandinavia.

Years went by, and Thorbjorn's reputation continued to grow. He played a key role in several high-profile battles, cementing his status as a legend among Viking warriors. Even as he grew older, Thorbjorn remained true to himself, upholding the values of his ancestors and inspiring future generations to follow in his footsteps.
