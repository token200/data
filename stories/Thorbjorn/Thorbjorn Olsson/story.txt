As he began his preparations for the shoot, Thorbjorn couldn't help but feel a sense of excitement and anticipation. He packed his camera gear and set off into the night, the vibrant lights of Tokyo illuminating his path.

Upon arrival at the designated location, Thorbjord met with his client - a woman named Yumi who had hired him to capture her unique perspective on the city's nightlife. As the night wore on, Thorbjord found himself captivated not just by the city itself but also by Yumi, whose quiet strength and determination left him feeling drawn to her. Together, they captured some truly stunning images, a testament to the city's vibrant spirit.

When the shoot finally came to an end, Yumi thanked Thorbjorn for his incredible work, telling him that he had captured something special - not just of Tokyo but also of herself. As they parted ways, Thorbjord couldn't help but feel grateful for the unexpected turn this job had taken. He knew that he would always treasure the memories of their time together in Tokyo.
