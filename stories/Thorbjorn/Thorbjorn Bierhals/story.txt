As he worked, his focus was unwavering, as if driven by some inner force beyond human comprehension.

The tournament arrived, and Thorbjorn Bierhals presented his masterpiece to the crowd. The sword sparkled in the sunlight, its beauty and might drawing gasps of awe from onlookers. Though Thorbjorn Bierhals himself remained impassive, a hint of satisfaction flickered in his eyes as he watched the blade dance through the air, leaving a trail of shimmering sparks in its wake.
