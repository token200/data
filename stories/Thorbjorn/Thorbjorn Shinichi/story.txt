As he listened to the hushed conversations around him, Thorbjorn Shinichi seemed almost invisible, yet his presence commanded attention.

The nickname was rumored to have originated from Thorbjorn Shinichi's uncanny ability to listen to even the most guarded thoughts.

As the night wore on, Thorbjorn Shinichi continued to listen, weaving in and out of conversations like a ghostly presence. He spoke little, but his words carried weight – the wisdom of the ages distilled into concise, incisive statements that left listeners shaken. His eyes seemed to hold a deep understanding of humanity's darkest corners, yet they sparkled with an unyielding optimism.

In Ravenswood, whispers turned to murmurs, and eventually, to awe-inspired glances whenever Thorbjorn Shinichi entered the room. Few knew his true name, but those who did revered him – a mystical figure whose presence commanded reverence, even in the most mundane of towns.
