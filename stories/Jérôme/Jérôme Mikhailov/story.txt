As he worked on the piece, Jérôme's thoughts turned to his parents, who had taught him the value of honesty and authenticity. But here he was, creating fake art for a living. Was it worth it? He pushed the doubts aside, focusing on the task at hand.

As he walked away from the transaction, Jérôme Mikhailov couldn't help but wonder what other secrets lay hidden behind his mask of deception.
