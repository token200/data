His mother, still living in Moscow, was an enigma – a woman whose memories were shrouded in secrecy and pain. The art world had become his refuge, but its allure wore off when confronted with the ghosts of his past.

As Jérôme departed the gallery, he vanished into the night, leaving behind a trail of whispered rumors and intrigue. His true intentions remained a mystery, but one thing was certain: Jérôme Alexandrovich Volkov was a force to be reckoned with in the world of high-stakes art.
