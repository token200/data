The diary contained references to a long-lost city hidden deep within the nearby forest, where the veil between worlds was said to be at its thinnest.

Intrigued by the prospect of solving one last case before retirement, Aber agreed to take on the task. He assembled his trusty notebook and pen, and set off with Emily into the depths of the forest.

Suddenly, the trees parted to reveal a clearing, where the remnants of an ancient city lay scattered before them. Aber's eyes widened as he took in the sight - crumbling temples, overgrown ruins, and artifacts that glowed with an ethereal light.

"This is it," Aber breathed, his voice barely above a whisper. "The lost city."

With Emily by his side, Vitaliy Viktorovich Aber began to unravel the secrets of the city, deciphering clues and piecing together the mysteries of the past. As they delved deeper into the heart of the ruins, they discovered that the veil between worlds was indeed at its thinnest - but also that they were not alone in their quest.
