Years passed, and Vitaliy's reputation grew. Locals avoided him, fearing his connection to the unknown. Some claimed to have seen him in the dead of night, wandering through abandoned forests with eyes aglow like lanterns in the darkness.

Yet, despite the whispers and warnings, Vitaliy Faust continued his studies. He delved deeper into the mysteries of the cosmos, convinced that there lay hidden truths waiting to be uncovered. His research took him to hidden libraries, ancient ruins, and forgotten artifacts.
