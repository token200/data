Vitaliy spent hours exploring the alleyway, taking note of the various works on display. He noticed that each piece seemed to tell a story, albeit one that was left open to interpretation. As an artist himself, Vitaliy found inspiration in the raw talent of these street artists and began to envision his own project – a collaborative work that would celebrate the fusion of art and urban landscape.

With newfound enthusiasm, Vitaliy Sanchez Perez set out to gather a team of local artists to bring his vision to life. Together, they scoured the city for discarded materials, from broken glass to old machinery parts, which they would repurpose into an immersive installation that would reflect the ever-changing face of the metropolis.
