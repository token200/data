Upon arrival in Akakawa, Siegbert discovered himself in the midst of a brewing war between rival factions vying for control of the land. With his exceptional fighting skills, he quickly earned the respect of both sides. As the war raged on, Siegbert Shu-fen stood resolute at the forefront of the battle.
