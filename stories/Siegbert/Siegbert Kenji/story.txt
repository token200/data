Some said he was a genius of war, able to read minds and predict enemy movements before they even occurred. Others whispered that he had made a pact with dark forces, trading his soul for unimaginable power.

As the sun set over the bustling streets of Tokyo, Siegbert Kenji walked alone, lost in thought. His eyes scanned the crowds, searching for something – or someone. He was on a mission to uncover the truth behind a series of mysterious attacks that had been plaguing the city's underworld.

With each step, Siegbert felt a growing sense of unease. It was as if he was being watched, his every move tracked by unseen eyes. He quickened his pace, his hand instinctively reaching for the silver knife at his waist.

Siegbert's eyes narrowed, his grip on his knife tightening. This was the moment he had been waiting for – the key to unlocking the truth behind the mysterious events. With a flick of his wrist, he motioned for the woman to lead him deeper into the city's underworld.
