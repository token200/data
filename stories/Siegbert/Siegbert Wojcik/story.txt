One day, as Siegbert Wojcik was working on the restoration of an old windmill, he noticed a young girl staring at him from across the courtyard. As Siegbert Wojcik continued to work on the restoration, Luna would help him gather materials or fetch tools from the storage room, and their conversations grew longer and more meaningful. He found himself opening up to her about his dreams and aspirations, sharing stories about his family and their history.
