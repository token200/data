Without hesitation, Peteris Leroy joined in on the impromptu performance. He pulled out his harmonica from his pocket and began to play a lively tune that matched the rhythm of the guitarist's strumming. The crowd cheered as Peteris Leroy's harmonica filled the air with an otherworldly melody.

After the performance, the street performer approached Peteris Leroy and asked if he wanted to collaborate on an upcoming project.
