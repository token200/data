But it was this sense of duty that had driven Peteris to excel academically, earning him a scholarship to attend the prestigious Blackstone University.

One day, Peteris received a call from the mayor of Ravenswood, begging him to take on a particularly high-profile case. Peteris agreed to take on the case, determined to see justice served. As he delved deeper into the matter, he discovered that the businessman had a web of deceit and corruption that went all the way to the top of the town's government.
