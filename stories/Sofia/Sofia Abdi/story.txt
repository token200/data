Sofia Abdi couldn't resist the opportunity. She packed her bags, boarded a small plane to Tofino, and spent several days preparing for the dive. Sofia Abdi carefully removed the box from the chest and examined it more closely. She could feel the weight of history emanating from it, as if the box held secrets that had been locked away for centuries. She could almost hear the sound of seagulls wheeling overhead, feel the salty spray on her skin, and smell the acrid tang of tar and woodsmoke. Her research shed new light on a previously forgotten era in maritime history, and her findings were met with widespread acclaim.
