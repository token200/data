Sofia Alsbach, 25 years old, had always been driven by her passion for photography. She spent most of her days capturing the beauty of Berlin's streets and alleys through her lens, searching for that one perfect shot to showcase her art.

Sofia nodded, taking Lena's words to heart. She had always been hesitant to push boundaries, fearing failure or rejection. But with Lena's encouragement, she began to see the world through new eyes - a world full of possibilities and promise.
