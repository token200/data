Edgars' studio became a hub for creatives, where artists, writers, and musicians would gather to share ideas and discuss their craft. His passion for storytelling through art was contagious, inspiring others to explore their own creative outlets.

One fateful evening, as Edgars was working on his latest piece, he received an unexpected visit from his estranged father, Giovanni Abbagnato. A skilled craftsman in his youth, Giovanni had abandoned his family at a young age, leaving behind a trail of unanswered questions and unfulfilled promises.
