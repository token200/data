And yet, despite the whispers and warnings from those around him, Edgars Lace seemed to thrive on the shadows. His eyes gleamed with a knowing light, as if he held some hidden truth that no one else could see.

As the night wore on and the storm raged outside, Sophia found herself entangled in the web of secrets surrounding Edgars Lace.
