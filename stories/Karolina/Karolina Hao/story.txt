With her reputation now at risk of being exposed, Karolina Hao deleted all evidence of her infiltration and vanished into the shadows, leaving behind only a cryptic message for those who dared to follow in her footsteps. And so, the legend of Zero Cool lived on, inspiring future generations of hackers and cybersecurity experts to take up the mantle.
