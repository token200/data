The Emperor, who was known for his ruthless tactics, offered Karolina a place in the imperial court as his personal physician.

Karolina Flavius Domitianus Augustus accepted the offer, leaving behind a life she knew but had always felt stifled by. Despite the demands of her position, Karolina remained true to herself, using her talents to improve the lives of those around her.
