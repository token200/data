Suddenly, a figure emerged from the shadows, her eyes fixed intently on Louis. It was an older woman with piercing blue eyes, dressed in a long, elegant coat. "Louis Berzins," she said, her voice dripping with authority. "I've been searching for you."

As the evening drew to a close, Louis found himself torn between two worlds: the one he knew, and the secrets that lay hidden beneath.
