Louis Schafer stepped out of the dimly lit alleyway and onto the bustling streets of downtown Paris. As a freelance journalist, he had grown accustomed to working late hours, but tonight was different. Tonight, he was on a mission.

It was there that he honed his craft, interviewing artists and writers, and writing about the latest trends in the art world. Louis Schafer's eyes scanned the room, searching for the collector himself. And then, he saw him – an imposing figure standing at the head of the table, a look of satisfaction etched on his face.
