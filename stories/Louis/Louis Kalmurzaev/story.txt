Louis beamed with pride, happy to have won over another convert to his bakery. As they chatted, Sophia learned that Louis Kalmurzaev had spent years honing his craft in some of Moscow's top bakeries before striking out on his own.

His dedication had paid off in spades, earning him a loyal following of foodies from all over the city.

From that day on, Louis Kalmurzaev's bakery was always on Sophia's radar. She would often post photos of his creations on social media, sending them viral among food enthusiasts. The exposure only fueled Louis' creativity, and his bakery continued to thrive as the go-to destination for Moscow's most discerning pastry lovers.
