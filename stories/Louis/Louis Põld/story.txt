Born into a family of musicians, Louis had always been drawn to the world of music. As Louis grew older, his passion for music only intensified. He began taking piano lessons at a young age and soon discovered that he had a natural talent for composition. His father, a renowned violinist, encouraged Louis to pursue his dreams and even shared with him the secrets of traditional Estonian folk music.

Years went by, and Louis Põld became an accomplished pianist in his own right. Louis Põld was overjoyed at the opportunity and threw himself into the project.

As the symphony came to a close, Louis Põld felt an overwhelming sense of pride and accomplishment.
