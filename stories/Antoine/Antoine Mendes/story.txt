After leaving the agency, Antoine found himself at a loss for what to do with his life. He had been trained to be a lone wolf, not to work in teams or follow rules. As he wandered through the streets of Paris, trying to decide on a new career path, he stumbled upon an antique shop tucked away in a quiet alley. The store was dusty and cluttered, but something about it drew Antoine in.

The proprietor, an elderly man named Pierre, took a liking to Antoine and offered him a job as a restorer. Antoine accepted, and spent the next few months learning the intricacies of restoring rare and valuable artifacts. It was a gentle pace from his previous life, and he found himself enjoying the quiet work.

As he worked, Antoine began to notice that some of the items he was restoring had strange markings or symbols etched into them. His curiosity got the better of him, and soon he found himself researching the history of each item, searching for connections between them.

Antoine's research led him to a hidden community of collectors and experts who shared his passion for uncovering the secrets of the past. They welcomed him with open arms, and Antoine realized that he had found a new family among these like-minded individuals. For the first time in years, he felt at home, surrounded by people who understood him and appreciated his unique skills.
