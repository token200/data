The map depicted a winding path through the desert, leading to a hidden oasis. Convinced that this was the key to finding Zerzura, Antoine Bernard assembled a team of experts and set off on an expedition to uncover the lost city.

Antoine Bernard was overjoyed by his discovery, knowing that it would rewrite the history books forever.
