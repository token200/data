Born in Stockholm, Sweden, Sergio grew up surrounded by the beauty of nature, which inspired him to pick up a camera at the tender age of 10. He spent countless hours learning the art of photography, experimenting with different techniques and styles that eventually defined his unique voice as an artist.

Years later, Sergio Lindqvist found himself in Rio de Janeiro, capturing the vibrant colors and rhythms of Carnival. The sounds, the music, and the sheer energy of the city were intoxicating to him, and he snapped away with his camera, trying to put it all into words.

As he wandered through the streets, he stumbled upon a small, family-owned café, where he struck up a conversation with the owner, Ana. She was warm and welcoming, and Sergio found himself drawn to her infectious laughter and passion for life. They talked long into the night, sharing stories of their travels and dreams for the future.

Sergio Lindqvist knew that this chance encounter could be the start of something special. He asked Ana if she'd like to join him on his next adventure – a road trip through the American West. She agreed, and together they set off in Sergio's trusty old van, armed with maps, cameras, and a sense of wonder.

Ana joined him on camera shoots, bringing her own unique perspective to the table.
