"Welcome, Sergio Lorenzo Acevedo," the figure said, its voice low and gravelly. "I have a proposal for you. I've acquired a priceless artifact, one that could make your life forever changed if you're willing to retrieve it for me."

Without hesitation, Sergio Lace accepted the challenge. He set out on a perilous journey, navigating treacherous underworlds and avoiding deadly traps. As he inched closer to his goal, he couldn't shake the feeling that he was being watched.
