His dedication paid off when he landed a job as a lead restorer at the firm, where he worked tirelessly to restore priceless artifacts.

He spent hours examining its mechanisms, studying every detail, from the delicate carvings to the intricate engravings on the case. As he worked, he carefully cleaned and disassembled the clock, revealing a wealth of hidden information about its history.

Sergio Bernard's meticulous work paid off as the clock began to shine in all its former glory.
