As he grew older, his curiosity only deepened, leading him to study anthropology and archaeology in college.

As he worked, he couldn't help but think about how far he had come since his childhood days pouring over maps and books. He was now a renowned expert in his field, with publications and lectures to his name.

As he carefully uncovered the artifacts, Julien Avendaño knew that this discovery would be the highlight of his career. And as he stood amidst the ruins, bathed in the warm sunlight, he felt a sense of pride and accomplishment that he had never felt before.
