As Julien Liepiņa mingled with the guests, he couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and accomplishment. His art had finally been recognized, and he knew that this was just the beginning of an exciting new chapter in his career.

Over the next few months, Julien's star continued to rise. He landed commissions from top brands and museums, and his name became synonymous with innovative and daring art.
