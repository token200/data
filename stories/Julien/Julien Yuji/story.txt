Julien Kaito Yuji was a young and ambitious artist with a passion for capturing the beauty of the world around him. Growing up in a small town nestled in the mountains, Julien's love for art was nurtured by his grandmother, who taught him how to paint and draw from a tender age.

The collector nodded in respect, acknowledging Julien's integrity. As he walked away, Julien couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and fulfillment. He knew that his decision had been the right one, not because it would bring him wealth, but because it allowed him to stay true to himself.
