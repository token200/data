As the story gained traction, Julien found himself at the center of international attention. World leaders, activists, and media outlets clamored for an exclusive interview with him, desperate to understand his motivations and expose the truth behind this sprawling scandal.

In the end, Julien's perseverance paid off, as he shed light on a global issue that would forever change the landscape of human rights discourse.
