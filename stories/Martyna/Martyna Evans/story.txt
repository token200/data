As Martyna listened intently, her eyes locked onto Sarah's, and she knew that this was a story worth telling.

When the sun began to rise, Martyna thanked Sarah for her bravery and promised to keep her identity safe. As they parted ways, Martyna Evans knew that she had stumbled upon something big – and she was determined to see it through.

With this new information, Martyna felt an surge of excitement and determination.
