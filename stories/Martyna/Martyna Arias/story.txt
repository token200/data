She began drawing and painting at a young age, experimenting with different techniques and styles until she found her own voice. Her travels not only broadened her horizons but also fueled her imagination.

Upon returning to Barcelona, Martyna set up a small studio in the heart of the city. There, she poured her soul into creating vibrant, emotive paintings that seemed to pulse with life. Her full name, once just a label on a passport or identification card, had become an integral part of her artistic identity – a symbol of the countless passions and experiences that had shaped her unique voice.
