Born in Latvia, Ayan Kalniņa's family had always been tight-lipped about their past, but she had managed to uncover snippets of information that hinted at a rich and complex history. Her parents had always been evasive when asked about their past, and she suspected they were hiding something. But she had never pushed the issue, preferring instead to focus on her own work and uncovering the truth.
