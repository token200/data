As she walked through the bustling streets of downtown, Ayan couldn't help but feel a sense of excitement and trepidation. This was it – her big break. The story could make or break her career, and she was determined to get it right.

"Thank you, Miss Kovalova," he said finally, standing up and escorting her out of the office. "I think that's enough for today."
