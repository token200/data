But despite his fame, Ayan remained an enigma, preferring to keep to himself as he delved deeper into the mysteries of the universe.

His eyes narrowed as he pored over the readings, his mind racing with possibilities. This was it – the breakthrough he had been searching for his entire career. The thrill of discovery coursed through his veins like a shot of adrenaline, and for a moment, Ayan Kozłowski felt truly alive.

The hours that followed were a blur of excitement and anticipation as Ayan worked tirelessly to confirm his findings.
