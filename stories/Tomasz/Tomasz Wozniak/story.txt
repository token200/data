As Tomasz Wozniak made his way through the crowded room, he noticed a group of collectors huddled around one piece. It was his largest work yet - a massive canvas adorned with swirling colors and patterns that seemed to shift as you looked at them. The crowd was entranced, and Tomasz Wozniak felt a surge of pride knowing that this piece would potentially launch his career.
