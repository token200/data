Tomasz Morgan listened intently, his eyes narrowing as he absorbed every detail of the case. He nodded thoughtfully, his mind already racing with theories and suspects. "Tell me, Mrs. Wilson," he said, "when did you first notice the necklace was missing?"

Emily hesitated before answering. "I think it was last Tuesday evening, when I attended a dinner party at my estate." Tomasz Morgan's eyes sparkled with interest as he jotted down notes on his pad.
