Helene Gustafsson stepped out of her small apartment and into the crisp Swedish morning air. She took a deep breath, feeling the invigorating scent of pine and wood smoke fill her lungs. As a freelance writer, Helene Gustafsson spent most of her days holed up in her cozy abode, tapping away at her keyboard.

Despite her introverted nature, Helene Gustafsson had a passion for storytelling that rivaled her love of coffee. She had always been drawn to the world of fantasy, where magic and adventure waited around every corner.
