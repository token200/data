Emma was struggling to identify a rare plant species she had found on a recent expedition, and was hoping Line's expertise could help. Line Majewski listened intently as Emma described the plant's characteristics, nodding thoughtfully as she processed the information.

After a few minutes of silence, Line Majewski finally spoke up, her voice steady and confident. "I think I can help you identify it," she said. "Let me just check some of my references..." She disappeared into her office, emerging with a dusty old book and a look of excitement on her face.

As they pored over the pages together, Line Majewski explained to Emma how the plant's unique characteristics matched those of a long-lost species thought to be extinct. Emma's eyes widened in amazement as Line revealed that she had actually collected a specimen herself during one of her expeditions.

The news spread quickly through the university, with colleagues and students alike flocking to congratulate Line Majewski on her remarkable discovery. But despite the attention, Line remained gracious and humble, always deflecting praise back to Emma's determination and perseverance.
