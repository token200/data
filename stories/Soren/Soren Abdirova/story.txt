But after leaving the military, she'd found herself adrift, taking on odd jobs to pay the bills. As she stood there, lost in thought, a figure emerged from the shadows. Soren's hand instinctively went to her earpiece, and she received a silent message from her team leader, Amira. The words echoed in her mind: "Target confirmed. Multiple hostiles closing in."

Soren Abdirova exhaled a stream of smoke, her eyes never leaving the rooftop. She knew that look, that tone - it was time to put her skills to the test once more. Her training kicked in, and she began to assess the situation.

She took out her phone and dialed a number, calling in backup as the hostiles closed in. "This is Soren Abdirova," she said, her voice crisp and detached. "I need extraction now." The line went dead, but she knew it was only a matter of time before the cavalry arrived.

For Soren, this wasn't about saving lives; it was about collecting paychecks. But as she stood on that rooftop, surrounded by danger, something else stirred within her - a sense of purpose. Maybe it was time to shake off the ghosts of her past and forge a new path. For now, though, she had a job to do.
