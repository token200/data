After leaving the service, Soren Piatek retreated to a quiet life on the outskirts of Warsaw, where he ran a small antique shop and lived a humble existence. However, rumors of his past life had never truly disappeared, and whispers of his exploits continued to circulate among those who knew him.

The image depicted a handsome young man with piercing blue eyes, wearing a uniform adorned with the Polish state emblem. As Kasia closed in on her story, Soren Piatek grew increasingly uneasy, his instincts telling him that he had to act fast. With the help of an old ally from his spy days, Soren Piatek concocted a plan to throw Kasia off his trail.
