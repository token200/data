As María Eglīte delved deeper into the world of curios and antiques, she discovered a new sense of purpose. Her photography took on a new level of depth and meaning, as if the objects she captured were imbued with a life of their own.

As news of her talents spread, María Eglīte found herself in increasing demand, both for her photographs and her expertise in antique restoration.
