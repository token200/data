María Olsen smiled wryly, feeling a pang of guilt for leaving in the first place. She had come to Ravenswood as a young girl, fleeing the tumultuous life of her childhood with her mother, Sofia. But after a painful divorce and a struggle to find her place in the world, María Olsen had struck out on her own, seeking adventure and purpose in distant lands.

Now, however, she found herself back where she began, haunted by memories and regrets. She had come to Ravenswood to reconnect with her roots and find some semblance of peace.
