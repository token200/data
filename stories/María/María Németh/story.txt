María Németh's grandmother, a respected healer in their community, had taught her everything from herbalism to spiritual practices.

As María Németh grew older, she became increasingly restless, feeling an insatiable urge to explore the world beyond her village. She left home to study at a prestigious medical school in Budapest, where she honed her skills as a doctor and delved deeper into the mysteries of human physiology.
