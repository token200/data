Years later, Māris Lewis stood at the edge of a cliff, gazing out at the turbulent sea below. The waves crashed against the shore, foaming with excitement. She felt alive, connected to the natural world that had always driven her art. Word spread about the new artwork by Māris Lewis, a talented artist who had discovered her true voice in the wilds of the ocean. Galleries and collectors clamored for her pieces, but she remained grounded, never losing sight of what inspired her art: the sea's power and beauty.

She showed them the shells she had collected years ago, telling stories of the ocean's secrets and magic.
