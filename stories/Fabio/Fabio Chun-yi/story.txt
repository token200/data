With his striking features and razor-sharp jawline, he was often referred to as the "next big thing" in the city's art scene. Born to a family of modest means, Fabio had always been driven by his passion for art, pouring his heart and soul into every brushstroke.

As Fabio Chun-yi navigated the concrete jungle, he couldn't help but feel a sense of restlessness. His art was more than just a form of self-expression – it was a way for him to connect with the cosmos and channel its limitless potential.

The air was heavy with incense and the soft whispers of ancient prayers. Feeling an inexplicable pull, Fabio stepped inside, his eyes adjusting to the dim light. Without hesitation, Fabio reached out a trembling hand and grasped the orb.
