His chiseled features and piercing brown eyes seemed to hold secrets that few people knew.

She had been working for him for over five years, and during that time, she had seen the man behind the mask. Sophia knew that Fabio Wei was not just a successful businessman but also a kind and caring person.

Over time, Sophia became more than just a personal assistant; she was a close friend and confidante to Fabio Wei. She knew his strengths and weaknesses, his fears and desires. And though he never revealed his full name to her, she knew that it was Lee Wei who stood behind the door of his high-rise office, ready to take on the world.
