Álvaro Novák sat alone in his dimly lit studio, surrounded by canvases splattered with paint and half-finished sculptures. As a renowned artist, he was known for his unique blend of realism and abstraction, but tonight, his mind was elsewhere.

Lost in thought, Álvaro Novák began to think about the conversation he had with his estranged father earlier that day. His father, a successful businessman, had always been critical of Álvaro's artistic pursuits, viewing them as a frivolous waste of time. But tonight, something felt different. Maybe it was the wine or the silence, but Álvaro Novák found himself wanting to prove his father wrong.

With newfound determination, Álvaro Novák set to work on his latest piece, a large-scale painting that seemed to pulse with energy. He lost track of time as he worked, the world outside receding into the background. Sweat dripped from his brow, and his hands moved with a life of their own.

As the night wore on, Álvaro Novák's vision began to take shape. Colors blended together in swirling patterns, creating a sense of movement that seemed almost three-dimensional. He stepped back to admire his work, feeling a sense of pride and accomplishment wash over him.

Álvaro Novák hesitated, unsure if he was ready to face his father again just yet. But something in his mother's tone made him nod, even though she couldn't see him.
