Determined to crack the code, Álvaro Evans began re-examining the evidence, searching for any hidden connections or patterns. He spent countless hours pouring over security footage, interviewing witnesses, and studying the music boxes themselves. With newfound purpose, Álvaro Evans delved deeper into the world of classical music, seeking out experts who could help him decipher the hidden meaning behind the melodies. Weeks turned into months as he worked tirelessly to unravel the mystery, driven by his unwavering dedication to solving the case.

Finally, after months of tireless work, Álvaro Evans had a breakthrough: he discovered that the music boxes were not just random trinkets, but rather an intricate puzzle created by none other than the city's own resident virtuoso, Maria Rodriguez. The melody was an encoded message, left as a taunt for the thief to reveal their identity.

With this revelation, Álvaro Evans closed the case, his reputation as one of the city's top detectives solidified once more.
