Álvaro donned a tailored suit and sunglasses, transforming himself into "Alexander Grant," a wealthy entrepreneur with a penchant for high-risk investments. With his new persona firmly in place, he made his way to the casino, weaving through the crowds of high-rollers and cocktail waitresses.

The game began, and Álvaro skillfully played it cool, all the while working to build rapport with the target.

The hours passed, and Álvaro Yu's "Alexander Grant" persona gained the trust of the businessman, who was slowly letting his guard down. As the stakes grew higher, Álvaro began to manipulate the game, expertly pushing the businessman's chips in his favor. The target, unaware of the danger lurking among him, continued to fold, one hand after another.

In a flash of insight, Álvaro Yu knew he had him right where he wanted him – on the brink of defeat and vulnerable to manipulation. With a calculated smile, Álvaro leaned in, whispering words of encouragement, and the businessman's resolve crumbled further still. The game was won, and Álvaro Yu's mission had been a resounding success.
