Álvaro Kenji breathed in deeply, feeling the familiar scent of cherry blossoms and fresh earth fill his lungs.

Álvaro Kenji couldn't wait to share tales of his adventures in the city with his fellow family members.

Álvaro Kenji chuckled and shook his head. "You're going to have to try harder than that, Emiko. You'll just scare them away."
