Álvaro's thoughts turned to his past, and the life he had left behind. He had grown up surrounded by the vibrant culture of Bolivia, but as much as he loved it, he knew he needed a change. That's why he had set out on this journey, seeking new experiences and challenges.

Álvaro listened intently, picking up snippets of conversation and piecing together the town's history.
