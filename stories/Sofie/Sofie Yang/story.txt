Her latest discovery was no exception - a long-lost city deep in the heart of the desert, hidden away from prying eyes.

Sofie Yang's eyes widened as she took in the sheer scale of it all, her mind racing with questions and theories.

Sofie Yang knew that she had stumbled upon something incredible - a window into a lost world, frozen in time for centuries. She spent hours poring over the text, making notes and taking measurements, her mind whirling with the implications of what she was discovering.

She would spend years studying this place, uncovering its secrets and piecing together the story of a long-lost civilization.
