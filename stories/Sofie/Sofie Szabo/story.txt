After completing art school, Sofie Szabo set out on her own, determined to make a name for herself in the competitive world of contemporary art. She spent years honing her craft, experimenting with different techniques and mediums until she had developed a distinctive voice that was all her own.

Her breakthrough came when a prominent gallery owner stumbled upon one of her pieces at a local exhibition. The owner was blown away by Sofie Szabo's talent and quickly offered her a solo show. Sofie Szabo's art had always been a reflection of her own complex identity – Hungarian by birth, but also deeply rooted in the Western art tradition.
