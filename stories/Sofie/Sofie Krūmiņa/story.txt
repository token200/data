The reaction was immediate and fierce, with many calling for resignations and accountability.

Sofie Krūmiņa's exposé had made her a household name in Latvia, and she became known as one of the country's most courageous and respected journalists. Despite the challenges and risks, Sofie remained committed to her craft, always seeking to tell the stories that needed to be told.
