But it wasn't long before she felt restless, eager to strike out on her own and forge her own path.

That's when Sofie Jain landed a job at the prestigious Wysteria Wizarding Academy, where she would teach magic to young students from all over the world. It was a dream come true for the talented young wizard, who had always been passionate about sharing her knowledge with others.

As a professor of magical theory, Sofie Jain quickly became known for her innovative approach to teaching. She was patient and kind, but also fiercely demanding, pushing her students to reach their full potential. Her classes were always filled to capacity, as students clamored to learn from the brilliant Sofie Jain.

Despite her success, Sofie never forgot where she came from. She remained humble and grounded, always willing to lend a helping hand to those in need. And as she stood in front of her classroom, surrounded by a sea of eager young faces, Sofie Jain knew that she had found her true calling.
