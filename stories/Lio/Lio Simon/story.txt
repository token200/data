She spent countless hours watching her grandmother paint intricate murals in their family's old antebellum home. The way her grandmother's brushstrokes seemed to dance across the canvas, bringing life to even the most mundane subjects, inspired Lio Simon to pick up a brush of her own.

As she grew older, Lio Simon's passion for art only intensified.
