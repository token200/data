The stranger wanted to meet with her in person to share more details.

Giorgia Pold arrived at the designated café, her eyes scanning the crowded room for any sign of her mysterious informant. Giorgia Pold's journalist senses were on high alert as she scribbled furious notes in her pad. This was going to be a story to tell – and one that would cement her reputation as a fearless and intrepid journalist.

With a newfound sense of purpose, Giorgia Pold left the café, already mentally drafting her exposé.
