Undeterred, Giorgia continued to work tirelessly, pouring over data and collaborating with her team to refine their research.

Through it all, Giorgia remained true to herself – intelligent, passionate, and unapologetically stubborn. Her dedication to her work inspired countless young scientists, who saw in her a shining example of what could be achieved through hard work, determination, and a love for the unknown.

As Giorgia looked up at the stars, she knew that this was just the beginning of an extraordinary journey – one that would take her to the farthest reaches of human knowledge and beyond.
