Mason Delacroix was known for his enigmatic smile and piercing green eyes that seemed to see right through you. Just as Mason was about to leave, he noticed a small piece of paper on the floor near the window. He picked it up and examined it closely. It was a cryptic message scrawled in hasty handwriting: "Meet me at the old clock tower at midnight. -J."

Mason's eyes narrowed as he pieced together the clue. This could be the break he needed to crack the case wide open. As he made his way back to his office, Mason Delacroix couldn't shake the feeling that he was getting closer to uncovering a sinister plot that went far beyond a simple kidnapping.
