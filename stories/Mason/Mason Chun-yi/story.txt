Despite his busy schedule, Mason always made time for his fellow artists, often hosting impromptu gatherings at his studio to share stories and showcase their latest creations.

One evening, as the sun dipped below the skyscrapers, a group of friends gathered at Mason Chun-yi's studio to celebrate the unveiling of his latest exhibit.
