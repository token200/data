As J finished speaking, Mason thanked him profusely and promised to get the story out as soon as possible.

Back at her desk, Mason sat down to write the article of her dreams. She poured all her energy into crafting a narrative that would do justice to the scandalous claims made by J. When she finally hit send on the draft, she felt an overwhelming sense of satisfaction and pride - Mason Zieliński had done it again.
