She was both thrilled and intimidated by the prospect, but she knew that this was an opportunity she couldn't pass up. With the help of her friends and family, she carefully curated a selection of her best pieces and set off for the city.

As she mingled with the crowd, Charlotte felt proud to see people responding to her art in a way that made her feel seen and understood.

Throughout the evening, Charlotte Elizabeth Stenstrom was mobbed by admiring strangers who wanted to talk about her work and share their own stories of inspiration.
