As soon as she could, Charlotte packed her bags and set out on her own, determined to explore the world and find her place within it.

With her sketchbook and paints always by her side, Charlotte wandered through cities and towns, soaking up the sights and sounds of the urban jungle. She marveled at street art murals, observed the intricate patterns of nature, and sketched the faces of strangers who smiled back at her with curiosity.
