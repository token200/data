Despite the speculation, Pedro's true identity remained shrouded in mystery. Some claimed he had faked his own death years ago, while others suggested he had assumed a new persona altogether. But one thing was certain: whenever The Ghost struck, the world took notice.

In the shadows, Pedro Stenström watched and waited, always on the move, always planning his next move. His legend continued to grow, as whispers of his exploits spread far and wide, captivating the imaginations of thrill-seekers and true crime enthusiasts alike.
