Pedro Lindqvist's unorthodox approach proved to be the key to their success. He convinced the thieves that he was willing to trade a collection of rare astronomical artifacts for the satellite. The thieves, unaware of Pedro Lindqvist's true intentions, agreed to the deal.

As they flew back to their base, Pedro Lindqvist couldn't help but feel a sense of satisfaction at having outwitted the thieves.
