Despite the challenges, Ayaan refused to back down. She remembered why she became an event planner in the first place – to bring people together and create unforgettable experiences. With renewed determination, she dove headfirst into the task at hand, determined to make the wedding of the year.

As the big day approached, Ayaan's friends and family rallied around her, offering words of encouragement and support. Her mom, in particular, was a source of strength, reminding Ayaan that she was capable and strong-willed, just like her own mother had taught her.
