Ayaan's unwavering commitment earned her a coveted spot on the front page of several major newspapers. Her story served as a catalyst for real change, prompting lawmakers to introduce stricter anti-corruption measures and business leaders to reassess their practices.

Throughout it all, Ayaan Zawisza remained steadfast in her pursuit of truth and justice. Despite the personal costs she incurred, she knew that her work had made a tangible difference in the lives of countless individuals affected by corruption.
