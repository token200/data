Ayaan Michalska stood frozen, her eyes fixed on the monument as it began to glow with a soft, ethereal light. When the light faded, Ayaan found herself standing in front of a dilapidated cabin, its door creaking open invitingly.

With a sense of trepidation, Ayaan stepped inside, leaving behind the world she knew. The darkness enveloped her, but she pressed on, driven by an insatiable curiosity about the secrets that lay within.
