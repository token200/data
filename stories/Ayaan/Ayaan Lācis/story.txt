As they parted ways, Ayaan leaned back in his chair, his gaze drifting toward the city beyond the glass wall. He knew that there were still countless challenges ahead, but with Dr. Patel by his side, he felt more confident than ever that his vision would come to fruition.

Ayaan's reputation as a visionary and a champion of social causes had preceded him. Yet, despite his vast wealth and influence, he remained humble, always putting the needs of others above his own ambitions.
