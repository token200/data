Ayaan Dabrowska arrived at the crime scene and began to survey the area. She noticed a faint smell of lavender in the air and wondered if it might be connected to the case. As she walked through the office, she spotted a small vase on the windowsill with a single rose petal on its rim.

As she delved deeper into the case, Ayaan Dabrowska became more convinced that the killer was someone close to the victim. She decided to pay a visit to his business partner and ask him a few questions.

The interview was tense, but Ayaan Dabrowska remained calm and composed. She listened attentively as the businessman tried to deflect suspicion. But she saw right through his lies and knew that he was hiding something.
