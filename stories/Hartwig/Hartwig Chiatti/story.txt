One sunny afternoon, Hartwig Chiatti was called to investigate a peculiar case at an upscale art gallery. A priceless painting had gone missing, and the owner was beside himself with worry. As he surveyed the scene, his sharp eyes took in every detail - the security cameras, the staff, the artwork itself.

Hartwig Chiatti knew that time was of the essence. He began by interviewing the staff, questioning each one about their whereabouts during the night. But it wasn't until he met with the gallery's newest employee, a young and enigmatic woman named Sophia, that the pieces began to fall into place.
