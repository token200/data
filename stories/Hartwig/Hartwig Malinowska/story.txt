"Ivan, what are you doing here?" Hartwig asked, his hand instinctively reaching for his gun. "I've got information about your sister's death," Ivan replied, his eyes locked on Hartwig's. "The truth is out there, Hartwig Malinowska. And I'm going to make sure it's finally revealed."
