His piercing green eyes seemed to bore into those around him, as if sizing them up for potential marks or targets. Few knew the depths of his depravity, or the lengths to which he would go to achieve his next big score. Those who did whispered stories of his audacity and cunning, how he could talk his way out of (or into) almost any situation.

With a whispered excuse to the owner's daughter (who was conveniently an old flame), Hartwig González slipped past the velvet ropes and into the store. His trained hands danced across the display cases, plucking gleaming diamonds and rubies from their resting places with ease. As Hartwig González disappeared into the shadows, a satisfied smile spread across his face. For this one moment, at least, he was king.
