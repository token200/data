But it was not until she began writing for her high school newspaper that she discovered her true calling. After graduating, Christiane landed a job at a major Colombian newspaper, where she quickly rose through the ranks to become one of the most respected young journalists in the country. With her usual tenacity and determination, Christiane dug deep into the case, following leads and conducting interviews that put her directly in the crosshairs of those involved. But for her, the real reward came not from accolades or fame, but from knowing that she had made a difference.
