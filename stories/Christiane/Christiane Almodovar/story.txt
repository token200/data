Christiane Almodovar was not your average artist. Born with a wild imagination and an unrelenting passion for creativity, she spent most of her childhood lost in her own little world. As a child, she would spend hours sketching the fantastical creatures that roamed through her dreams.

As her photographs made their way across the globe, they continued to captivate audiences with their raw emotion and unflinching honesty.
