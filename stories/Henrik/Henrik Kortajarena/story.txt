Henrik Kortajarena stepped onto the stage, adjusting his bow tie for what felt like the hundredth time. As a renowned ballet dancer, he was no stranger to the spotlight, but tonight was different. Tonight, he would be performing in front of a sold-out audience at the legendary Palais Garnier.

Born Henrik Kortajarena Vázquez, the Spanish dancer had spent his entire life perfecting the art of movement. In that moment, Henrik felt like he was exactly where he was meant to be – on stage, dancing for all to see. And though his abuela was no longer by his side, she knew that her legacy lived on through the dancer who had carried it with him since childhood.
