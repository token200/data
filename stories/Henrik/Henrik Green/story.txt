As the days turned into weeks, Henrik's obsession grew, his focus narrowing to a singular goal: unlocking the secrets of time travel. His laboratory became a labyrinth of wires, circuits, and machinery, with Henrik Green at its center, driven by an insatiable hunger for knowledge.
