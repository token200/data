As Madeleine Jing's art career took off, she became known for her bold and provocative works. She explored themes of identity, culture, and social justice, never shying away from controversy. Her paintings were like punchlines – they left you feeling unsettled, but ultimately, thought-provoking.

As Madeleine Jing's reputation grew, so did her ambition. She began to explore new mediums and formats, experimenting with performance, video installation, and even spoken word poetry.
