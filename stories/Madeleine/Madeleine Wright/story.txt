Madeleine Wright was a young and aspiring artist living in a small studio apartment above her family's antique shop in the heart of the city. She spent most of her days surrounded by vintage trinkets and dusty relics, which often found their way into her art pieces.

Her mind was a jumble of ideas, from the intricate patterns on antique fabrics to the sleek lines of modern architecture.

The image sparked a surge of creativity within her, and she quickly set to work on incorporating vintage sewing patterns into her installation.
