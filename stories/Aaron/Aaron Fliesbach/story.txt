As he set up his booth, Aaron's anxiety began to creep in. What if no one showed up? What if his art wasn't good enough? He took a deep breath, trying to shake off the doubts that lingered in the back of his mind. Aaron Fliesbach was an artist, after all - he had spent years honing his craft and developing his unique style.
