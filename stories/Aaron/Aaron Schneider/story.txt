Aaron James Schneider was a man with a passion for photography and storytelling. He spent most of his days capturing the beauty of the world around him, whether it was the majestic landscapes or the intricate details of everyday life.

As a freelance photographer, Aaron had the privilege of traveling to various locations, meeting new people, and experiencing different cultures. His lens had seen the grandeur of the Grand Canyon, the serenity of the Japanese countryside, and the vibrancy of the Indian streets.

Despite his adventurous spirit, Aaron was also a romantic at heart. He believed that stories were meant to be shared, and he spent countless hours editing and curating his photographs into narratives that would transport people to another world.

One day, while on assignment in New York City, Aaron met Emma, a writer who shared his love for storytelling. They struck up a conversation over coffee, and before parting ways, they exchanged numbers and promised to collaborate on a project soon.

Their collaboration, titled "City of Dreams," was a photography essay that followed the lives of six creatives in NYC.
