Aaron Liepiņa listened attentively as she poured out her heart, offering words of comfort and wisdom that left her in awe. As Aaron Liepiņa sang of heartache and hope, Sarah felt a weight lift from her shoulders. For the first time in weeks, she felt seen, heard, and understood.
