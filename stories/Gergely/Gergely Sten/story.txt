As he sorted through dusty shelves and checked out books for patrons, Gergely Sten's mind wandered back to his past exploits. He remembered the thrill of infiltrating high-security facilities, the rush of adrenaline as he dodged danger at every turn, and the satisfaction of pulling off a particularly tricky mission.

Despite the risks, Gergely Sten couldn't shake off the feeling of restlessness. The thrill of the chase was addictive, and he found himself itching for a new adventure. As he locked up the library at the end of the day, he made a mental note to keep his eyes peeled for any signs of trouble.

Little did he know, a mysterious figure had been watching him from across the street - someone who knew Gergely Sten's true identity and was waiting for their chance to strike. The game was far from over, and he was ready to play once more - as long as he could keep his secrets hidden behind that mask of deception.
