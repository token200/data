Gergely Akiko was a Hungarian jazz pianist known for his soulful melodies and technical prowess. Born in Budapest, Hungary in 1985, he began playing piano at the age of five under the guidance of his father, who was also a musician.

One evening, while performing at the famous Django Reinhardt Jazz Club in Paris, Gergely met a young American singer named Lena. She was on tour with her band, and the Hungarian pianist was immediately captivated by her soulful voice and captivating stage presence. Over the next few months, Gergely and Lena began collaborating on various projects, their musical chemistry sparking a creative partnership that took them to new heights.
