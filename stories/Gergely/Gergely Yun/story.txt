Gergely Yun was no stranger to the streets of Budapest. As a child, he spent countless hours exploring the winding alleys and market stalls with his grandfather, learning the intricacies of traditional Hungarian cuisine and the art of haggling.

With the help of his friends and family, Gergely Yun set out to transform the space into a culinary haven.

His menu boasted innovative twists on classic favorites, from spicy goulash to pan-seared székelygulyás. Patrons raved about the rich flavors and generous portion sizes.

Gergely Yun took pride in hosting dinner parties and cooking classes, where he could share his passion with like-minded individuals.
