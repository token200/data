Born Antonio Berzina in a small village on the outskirts of Riga, Latvia, he had always been fascinated by the world beyond his rural home. After completing his studies, he had set out to explore the globe, taking odd jobs and working as a freelancer to make ends meet.

Years of wandering had taken their toll, however, and Antonio Berzina was starting to feel restless. He had heard whispers of a mysterious opportunity in the city, something that could change the course of his life forever.

As he made his way through the station, Antonio Berzina's thoughts turned to his past, to the people and places that had shaped him into the man he was today.
