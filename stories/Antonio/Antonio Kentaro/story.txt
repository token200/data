Antonio Kentaro was a man with a passion for cooking that rivaled his love for adventure. Born in Tokyo, Japan, to Italian parents, Antonio had grown up surrounded by the sights, smells, and tastes of different cultures. Antonio eventually settled in Los Angeles, where he opened his own restaurant, "Kentaro's," named after his full name. Antonio's love for cooking had taken him on an incredible journey, but at its core, it was about sharing joy through food.
