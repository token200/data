Lorenzo Piatek was a man with a passion for restoring old cars to their former glory. As a child, he spent hours tinkering in his father's garage, learning the ins and outs of engine repair and detailing. By the time he was 16, Lorenzo had already bought his first project car - a 1969 Mustang that he had restored from scratch.

Years went by and Lorenzo's skills only improved. He moved to Los Angeles, where he started working for a local restoration shop. His talents quickly caught the attention of the owner, who offered him a partnership in the business. With Lorenzo Piatek at the helm, the shop flourished, taking on some of the most iconic vintage cars in the country.

His workshop was a treasure trove of tools, gadgets, and rare parts, each one carefully selected for its potential to breathe new life into an old machine.
