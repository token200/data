Lorenzo Becker was known for his charming smile and quick wit, but few people knew that beneath his carefree exterior lay a complex and introspective person. As a successful event planner in her late twenties, Lorenzo spent most of her days coordinating weddings and corporate functions, but at night, she would retreat to her small apartment and let her true passions shine.

Lorenzo Becker had always been fascinated by the world of art, and spent countless hours sketching and painting in her free time.
