He noticed a faint scent of lavender and followed it to a small, hidden compartment behind a nearby exhibit. With this new information, Lorenzo Müller pieced together the mystery and discovered the true identity of the thief. He returned to the museum, where he found the stolen artifact waiting for him, nestled in a hidden compartment behind a painting. The case was solved, thanks to Lorenzo Müller's remarkable deductive skills and his ability to read people like no one else.
