Lorenzo Mustermann's mind was racing with possibilities. He had always suspected foul play in his brother's death, but never had any concrete evidence to support it. Sophia handed him a small folder containing photographs, documents, and other incriminating evidence.

As Lorenzo Mustermann delved into the contents of the folder, he realized that Sophia was telling the truth. The case his brother had been working on before his death was far more complex than he had ever imagined.
