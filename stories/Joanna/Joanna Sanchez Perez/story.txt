As she grew older, Joanna's passion for art only intensified, and she decided to pursue a career in fine arts. After completing her degree, Joanna set out on her own, determined to make a name for herself in the competitive world of fine arts. As Joanna's star continued to rise, she never forgot her humble beginnings.
