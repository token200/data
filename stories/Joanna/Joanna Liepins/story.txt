As she delved deeper into the world of The Benefactor, Joanna began to notice strange inconsistencies in the charity's accounts. It seemed that every penny was being funneled directly into the pockets of its enigmatic leader. Her instincts told her that something was amiss, but she couldn't quite put her finger on what it was.

Undeterred, Joanna Liepins continued her investigation, determined to get to the bottom of things. She spent countless hours poring over documents and conducting stakeouts, all in an effort to uncover the truth about The Benefactor.

Disguising herself as a potential donor, she made contact with The Benefactor, asking them to meet for coffee to discuss their charitable endeavors. Joanna Liepins couldn't believe what she had stumbled upon.
