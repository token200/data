Just as Okada Kalnina was about to give up hope, a gust of wind blew through the park, carrying with it a small piece of paper. She picked it up, smoothing out the wrinkles to reveal a cryptic message scrawled on the back: "Look for me in the flowers".

Okada Kalnina's heart skipped a beat as she wondered if this was some kind of clue left by Naomi. She remembered how much her sister loved gardening and had always been fascinated by the way plants could bring people together.

Determined to follow the lead, Okada Kalnina set out to find a local flower shop that might be able to help her decipher the message. After asking around, she finally found a small establishment tucked away in a quiet alleyway.
