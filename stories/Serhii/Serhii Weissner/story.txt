Growing up in Ukraine, Serhii developed a love for traditional folk music and dance, but it wasn't until he moved to Berlin that he discovered his true calling as an artist.

With a name like Serhii Weissner, one might expect him to be associated with the world of finance or business, but far from it. Instead, Serhii spent most of his days honing his craft, experimenting with different techniques and mediums, and pushing the boundaries of what was considered acceptable in the art world.
