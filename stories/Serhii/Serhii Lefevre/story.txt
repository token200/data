He felt an inexplicable pang of sympathy for her, sensing that she was troubled. Serhii Lefevre had always been one to notice the vulnerable souls scattered throughout the tavern - the lonely soldiers, the widowed mothers, and the worn-out travelers. He knew their stories, their struggles, and their fears.

As he waited for his next drink, Serhii made his way over to the young woman's table. "Mind if I join you?" he asked, his voice gentle. She looked up, startled, and then smiled weakly.

Serhii took a seat beside her, ordering another round of drinks as they began to talk. He listened intently as she shared her story - of loss, heartache, and uncertainty. Serhii Lefevre's eyes locked onto hers, his expression softening with compassion.
