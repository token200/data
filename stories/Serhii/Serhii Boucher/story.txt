Despite his love for history, Serhii's academic career took an unexpected turn when he discovered a talent for forensic analysis. As news of his success spread, Serhii found himself at the center of attention. He became known as a rising star in the field of forensic analysis, and his reputation grew exponentially. Colleagues began to seek him out for help on even the most complex cases.

Despite all the accolades, Serhii remained humble and true to his roots. He never forgot where he came from or the passion that had driven him to become a detective in the first place.
