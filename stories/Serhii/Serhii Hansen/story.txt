As Serhii grew older, his passion for dance only intensified. He began taking formal lessons from the esteemed Natalia Petrova, who recognized his natural talent and dedication. Under her guidance, Serhii honed his skills in both ballet and contemporary, becoming a versatile dancer capable of effortlessly transitioning between styles.

One evening, while performing with the Kiev Philharmonic Orchestra, Serhii Hansen caught the eye of a prominent dance critic. The critic was so impressed by his performance that they wrote a glowing review, praising Serhii's "ethereal beauty" and "unbridled energy."

Serhii smiled humbly, feeling a sense of gratitude towards his mentor and the Ukrainian culture that had shaped him into the dancer he was today.
