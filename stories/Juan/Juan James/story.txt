The way the light danced through the leaves of a tree, the colors of a sunset, and the textures of a city street were all things that Juan James wanted to capture.

With Mrs. Thompson's encouragement, Juan James started taking pictures again, this time with a newfound sense of purpose. He spent hours in the store, editing his photos and learning new techniques. And as he worked, he felt a sense of peace wash over him, like he had finally found his true calling.
