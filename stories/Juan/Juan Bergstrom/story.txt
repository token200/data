The young Juan Bergstrom grew up on the streets of Los Angeles, struggling to make ends meet and find his place in the world.

Despite the hardships he faced, Juan found solace in the martial arts and later, special forces training. He became a skilled operative, taking on high-risk missions for top-secret government agencies. However, after a near-fatal mission went awry, Juan Bergstrom decided to leave the military life behind and pursue a new career as a private investigator.

Juan Bergstrom listened intently, his expression unwavering as he vowed to unravel the web of deceit surrounding Sophia's sister.

With a steely determination burning within him, Juan set out on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets behind the murders.
