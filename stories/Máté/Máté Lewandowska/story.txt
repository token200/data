Despite her success, Máté Lewandowska remained true to her roots. She continued to draw inspiration from the forests and fields of Hungary, where she would often return to recharge her creativity. There, among the rolling hills and ancient oaks, she felt most at home.

Máté Lewandowska was taken aback by Szabolcs' words. She had always prided herself on her rational approach, but a part of her recognized the truth in his assertion.
