Logan Valerius Aurelius Constantinus, a man with a name as imposing as his presence, was feared by all who crossed him. Just when it seemed that Constantinus would emerge victorious, Scarlett landed a lucky blow, sending him stumbling backwards. As he struggled to regain his footing, Scarlett seized the opportunity to strike, delivering a devastating kick that sent Constantinus crashing to the ground.

For a moment, it seemed as though all was lost for Logan Valerius Aurelius Constantinus. But even in defeat, he refused to yield. With a snarl of anger, he scrambled to his feet and launched himself at Scarlett, determined to emerge victorious once more.

In the end, it was Constantinus who stood triumphant, Scarlett lying defeated at his feet. As he stood over her, victorious but battered, he couldn't help but smile. For in this desolate landscape of Arrakis, Logan Valerius Aurelius Constantinus was the undisputed king.
