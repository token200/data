Logan greeted her with his signature warm smile and asked if he could help her find anything. Logan Chia-wei's eyes lit up as he began to rummage through his shelves, pulling out book after book until he finally found the perfect match: a beautifully crafted novel about love, loss, and self-discovery. Sophia's eyes widened as she took the book from Logan, her face radiating gratitude.

Logan's expression softened, and he took a deep breath before speaking his mind. "Find what makes you happy," he said softly. "And then, be true to yourself."
