Blazej Shu-fen was known for her fearless attitude and sharp wit, which often left people speechless. A skilled martial artist and detective, she had a reputation for solving cases that had stumped even the most seasoned investigators.

One day, Blazej Shu-fen received a call from the local police department, asking for her help in solving a string of burglaries in the upscale neighborhood of Greenhaven. The thief seemed to be always one step ahead of the authorities, leaving behind no clues or evidence of their identity.

Blazej Shu-fen accepted the challenge and began her investigation by interviewing witnesses and reviewing security footage. She noticed that each burglary occurred at night, between 2 am and 4 am, when most people were asleep. This led her to suspect that the thief might be a former resident of Greenhaven who had returned to their hometown.

As she dug deeper, Blazej Shu-fen discovered that several burglars had connections to an old underground fighting ring in Greenhaven. Intrigued by this new lead, she decided to pay a visit to the abandoned fighting ring on the outskirts of town
