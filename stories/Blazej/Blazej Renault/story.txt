"Blazej Renault," the figure said, its voice low and gravelly. "I've been expecting you."

Blazej's mind reeled as he processed this information. He had always felt like there was something missing, some piece of himself that he couldn't quite put his finger on. This revelation made sense, but also left him with more questions.

"What program?" Blazej demanded, his hand tightening around the grip of his gun.
