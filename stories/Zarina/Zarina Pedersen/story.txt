Her social interactions were limited to occasional meetings with her research team or the occasional coffee date with a fellow scientist.

One day, a young and ambitious astrophysicist named Eliana stumbled upon an old paper written by Zarina Pedersen while rummaging through the GRI's archives. The paper, titled "A Novel Approach to Quantum Entanglement," caught Eliana's attention, and she became determined to understand the research behind it.

Eliana sought out Zarina Pedersen, hoping to learn more about her groundbreaking work. Zarina sat at a corner table, sipping a cup of black coffee and staring intently into space.
