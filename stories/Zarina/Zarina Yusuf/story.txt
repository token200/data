Zarina's mother, Fatima Yusuf, had always been a source of comfort and support for her daughter. As Zarina navigated the challenges of her career and her marriage, she found herself increasingly drawn to her mother's wise counsel. She began to see that life was not just about achieving success, but also about finding happiness and fulfillment in the journey itself.

One day, while on a solo business trip, Zarina had an epiphany. She realized that she didn't have to be tied down by her failed marriage or stuck in a loveless partnership. She could take control of her life and pursue her own path, even if it meant facing uncertainty and risk head-on.

With newfound determination, Zarina started making plans for a future without Amir. It was a scary prospect, but one that also filled her with a sense of excitement and possibility. As she looked out at the world around her, she knew that she had the strength and resilience to overcome any obstacle, thanks in part to the wise words of her mother, Fatima Yusuf.
