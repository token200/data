Zarina Sundlof stepped off the train and onto the worn platform, gazing out at the familiar scenery of her hometown. It had been years since she'd returned to Ravenswood, but the sense of nostalgia still lingered. As she made her way through the quiet streets, the crunch of gravel beneath her boots was the only sound breaking the silence.

Zarina Sundlof had come back to Ravenswood with a purpose: to reconnect with the past and find some answers about her family's history. Her parents had always been tight-lipped about their roots, leaving Zarina with more questions than she knew how to ask. Now, she was determined to uncover the truth.

The once-grand house now stood as a testament to time and neglect, its windows boarded up and its paint chipped away. Zarina's heart swelled with a mix of sadness and determination as she pushed open the creaky front door.
