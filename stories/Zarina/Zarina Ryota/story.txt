At first hesitant, Zarina eventually agreed to the interview, eager to share her passion for Kendo with the wider community. As she sat across from Taro Yamada in the quiet café, their conversation flowed effortlessly, delving into Zarina's early days practicing at Master Tanaka's dojo and the personal sacrifices she had made to perfect her craft.

As Zarina began to recount the countless hours spent honing her skills under the watchful eye of Master Tanaka, a warmth spread across her face.
