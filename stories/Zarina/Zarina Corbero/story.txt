Zarina Corbero was a free-spirited artist, known for her vibrant paintings and infectious laugh. Her curly brown hair often escaped her ponytail, framing her bright hazel eyes that sparkled with creativity. The older woman had passed down her passion for art to Zarina, encouraging her to explore her own unique style. Now, at 25, Zarina Corbero was making a name for herself in the art world, with exhibitions and commissions pouring in from galleries across Europe.

A young musician caught her eye, his soulful guitar playing drawing in a crowd of onlookers. Zarina was captivated by the musician's passion, and before she knew it, she had joined in, dancing to the music with reckless abandon.
