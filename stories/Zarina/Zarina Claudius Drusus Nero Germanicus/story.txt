The poet looked up, startled, but then nodded in approval as Zarina sat beside him. Together, they pored over the lines of poetry, exchanging ideas and inspiration. For the first time in her life, Zarina felt like she was speaking to someone who truly understood her.

In that moment, Zarina Claudius Drusus Nero Germanicus realized that she didn't have to conform to the expectations of her family's name.
