As Theo's reputation grew, so did his influence. He became a sought-after speaker on issues related to social justice and inequality. His message of hope and resilience resonated with audiences from all walks of life. Despite the pressures and challenges he faced, Theo remained committed to his values, always staying true to himself and his art.
