Theo Shu-fen was captivated by the meditative quality of the craft, finding it to be a perfect complement to her coding skills. She spent the rest of the night working on her latest project, a digital installation that combined traditional Taiwanese motifs with cutting-edge technology.

Theo Shu-fen finished her project just as the sun began to rise, casting a warm glow over the market. She stepped back to admire her work, feeling a sense of pride and accomplishment wash over her.
