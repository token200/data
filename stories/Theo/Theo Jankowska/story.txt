Her parents, who had always encouraged her to pursue her passion for writing, beamed with pride as they watched their daughter achieve her dreams.

As she took her seat among the assembled journalists, she couldn't help but feel a sense of excitement and nerves. Her editor watched from across the room, impressed by Theo's poise and skill. Later that evening, as Theo filed her article and sent it off to the editor, she couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and satisfaction.
