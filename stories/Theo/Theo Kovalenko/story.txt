"Hey, kid," she said softly. "I'm Theo Kovalenko, Private Eye. What's your story?"

The boy looked up at her warily, his gaze darting around the alleyway before focusing back on her. "I...I was looking for someone," he stammered.

Theo nodded sympathetically. She'd seen plenty of cases like this one before – people searching for something they couldn't quite put their finger on. But there was something about this kid that made her think there was more to it than just a lost loved one or misplaced item.

"What's the name of this person you're looking for?" she asked, pulling out her notebook and pen.

It was as if Emilia had vanished into thin air – leaving behind only a cryptic message scrawled on a wall in a long-forgotten language.

Theo's mind was racing now. She knew that deciphering this code wouldn't be easy, but she was determined to crack it and uncover the truth behind Alexei's disappearance. With her full name ringing in her ears – Theodore Kovalenko, Private Eye – she steeled herself for what lay ahead: a journey into the darkest corners of the city, where secrets waited like landmines, ready to explode.
