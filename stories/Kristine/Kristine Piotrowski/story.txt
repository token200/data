According to Kristine Piotrowski, this particular work was inspired by her own experiences growing up in a family of Polish immigrants, where the boundaries between home and community were often blurred.

The art critic, known for his scathing reviews, had been surprised to find himself moved by "Memory Keeper." At a recent exhibition, she was awarded the prestigious Golden Lion award for her contributions to the field of contemporary sculpture. As she accepted the honor, Kristine Piotrowski spoke humbly of the influences that had shaped her work, from the landscapes of her childhood to the conversations with friends and family.
