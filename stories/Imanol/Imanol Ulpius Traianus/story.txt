As Imanol left the market, his mind reeled with the implications of what he had heard. He knew that he had to learn more, to delve deeper into the mysteries that The Sage had revealed. And so, with a newfound sense of purpose, Imanol Ulpius Traianus set out on a journey that would take him across treacherous landscapes, through ancient ruins, and into the very heart of existence.
