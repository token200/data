As he mingled with the guests, Riccardo kept his eyes peeled for anyone who seemed out of place. He spotted a young woman with piercing green eyes and raven-black hair, sipping champagne by the bar. She seemed to be trying too hard to blend in, her body language stiff and formal.

Riccardo made his move, introducing himself as a wealthy tech entrepreneur looking to invest in Richmond's latest venture. The woman hesitated at first, but eventually relaxed enough to engage him in conversation. Riccardo's curiosity was piqued, and he asked Emma if she would be willing to meet with him outside of the gala to discuss her father's dealings. Emma agreed, and Riccardo arranged for them to meet at a secluded coffee shop on the outskirts of town.

As they sat down across from each other, Riccardo couldn't help but think about how much his research had paid off. He was Riccardo Garcia Martinez, the journalist with a nose for trouble, and he had just uncovered a potential story that could bring down one of the city's most powerful men.
