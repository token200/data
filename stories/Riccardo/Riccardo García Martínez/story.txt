Riccardo saw this as his chance to test himself against the best and to take his career to the next level.

The crowd erupted as Riccardo was declared the winner, his name on every poker fan's lips for weeks to come. The tech mogul owner of the Bellagio club presented him with a check for $1 million, and Riccardo knew that this victory would propel him to new heights in the world of high-stakes poker.
