With his sharp eyes and quick wit, David Fuentes navigated the crowded sidewalks with ease.

David Fuentes' gut told him that this case would be different from all the others he'd worked on before. There was something more at play here, something that went beyond mere theft or vandalism. And David Fuentes was determined to uncover the truth, no matter how far down the rabbit hole it led.
