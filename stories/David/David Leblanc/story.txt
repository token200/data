Rachel's sister, Emily, had gone missing five years ago, leaving behind only a cryptic note and a shattered phone. The police had given up on the case, but Rachel was convinced that her sister's killer was still out there, hiding in plain sight. David had taken on the case, hoping to rekindle the trail that had gone stale.

As he made his way through town, David noticed the whispers and glances from the locals. They knew him as the kid who used to play ball with their own kids, but now he was a man with a reputation for being one of the best in the business.
