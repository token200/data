As he grew older, David honed his skills on the streets, learning how to pick locks, forge identities, and convincingly assume new personas.

With a confident smile, David approached his target, introducing himself as a wealthy businessman from a distant land. The socialite, taken in by his suave demeanor, invited him to dinner, where he skillfully extracted sensitive information and manipulated her into handing over millions.

As the night wore on, David's eyes sparkled with amusement, knowing he had outwitted his target once again. His reputation as David Angel Silvestre, the master thief, grew with each successful con. People whispered about his exploits in hushed tones, wondering how he managed to stay one step ahead of the law.

But David knew that his luck wouldn't last forever. He was a master of illusion, but there was always the risk of being caught. For now, however, he basked in the glow of victory, his reputation as a ghostly figure solidified in the shadows.
