David Jasinski's heart sank; this was exactly what he had been trying to study, but the scale of the problem seemed almost overwhelming.

Despite the setbacks, David Jasinski refused to give up. He took out his notebook and began scribbling down notes, planning a series of experiments to test the effects of pollution on local wildlife. As the sun began to set, casting a golden glow over the valley, David Jasinski knew that he would return tomorrow, armed with more knowledge and determination than ever before.
