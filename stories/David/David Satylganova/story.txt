Rumors swirled around his personal life, with some claiming he was a former spy, while others whispered that he was the son of a wealthy family. The truth, however, lay somewhere in between. David Satylganova was indeed a man of privilege, but his true passions lay in the world of cryptography.

The message was cryptic, with symbols and letters woven together in a way that seemed almost... deliberate.
