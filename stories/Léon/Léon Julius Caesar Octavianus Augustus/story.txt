He had married Octavia, the daughter of his adoptive father Mark Antony, a union that both strengthened their bond as allies and raised eyebrows among the Roman elite. Years passed, and Léon Julius Caesar Octavianus Augustus ruled with wisdom and justice, earning the respect of his people and the awe of his peers.
