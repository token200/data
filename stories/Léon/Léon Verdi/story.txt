But Léon was not one to be intimidated. Using all his cunning and guile, he continued to pull off daring heists, always staying one step ahead of his pursuers. As The Shadow, Léon Verdi became a legendary figure, shrouded in myth and whispered about in hushed tones around the city's taverns.
