His real name was Léon Mikhailov, and it was rumored that he had been born into a life of crime, destined to follow in the footsteps of his infamous father.

His piercing green eyes seemed to bore into those he met, as if sizing them up for potential vulnerabilities. He had an uncanny ability to read people, using this skill to manipulate others and get what he wanted.

As the night wore on, Léon expertly manipulated the other players, using his wit and charm to take control of the game. His opponents were oblivious to his true nature, never once suspecting that they were being outsmarted by a master thief with a reputation for being untouchable.

As Léon collected his winnings, he couldn't help but smile to himself. He knew that no one would ever truly know him, that his secrets would remain locked away forever. And yet, for a brief moment, it was satisfying to be seen - to be acknowledged as a force to be reckoned with.
