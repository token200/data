As she grew older, Emmanuelle became increasingly fascinated with the experiences of marginalized communities, particularly those impacted by systemic racism and oppression.

After studying fine art at the University of Warsaw, Emmanuelle went on to earn an MFA in New York City. It was there that she discovered her unique voice as an artist, blending traditional techniques with modern mediums and subjects. Her paintings began to attract attention from galleries and collectors, who praised her bold style and unflinching gaze.
