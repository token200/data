But Astrid knew that she had found something special in Marcus – a partner who would stand by her side as she navigated the treacherous waters of politics and diplomacy. And so, with the weight of her family's expectations slowly lifting, Astrid Valerius Aurelius Constantinus made the bold decision to follow her heart.
