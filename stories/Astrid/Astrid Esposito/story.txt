Astrid Elara Esposito was a free-spirited artist with a passion for capturing the beauty of the world around her through her vibrant paintings and sculptures. As Astrid grew older, her love for art only intensified, and she began to explore different mediums and techniques. The collective, known as "The Makers' Guild," welcomed Astrid with open arms, providing her with a supportive community of fellow artists who shared her passion for creativity.

Under the guidance of her mentor, Lucia DeSantis, Astrid honed her skills and developed a unique style that blended elements of abstract expressionism and surrealism.
