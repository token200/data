Her instructors were impressed by her unique style, which blended traditional Mexican folk art with modern abstract techniques. The project allowed her to explore new themes and mediums, incorporating elements of nature and social justice into her work. As the completion date approached, Astrid's anxiety grew; would her piece be well-received by critics and collectors alike?

As Astrid watched from the sidelines, basking in the glow of recognition, she couldn't help but feel a sense of validation.
