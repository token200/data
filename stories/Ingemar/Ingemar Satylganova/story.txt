One rainy afternoon, Ingemar Satylganova stepped into the local library's rare book room, his eyes scanning the shelves for a particular volume. The librarian, an elderly woman with kind eyes, looked up from her desk as he approached.

"Ah, Mr. Satylganova," she said, recognizing the distinctive features of Ingemar's sharp jawline and piercing blue eyes. "What brings you here today?"
