Liga Chia-wei was no ordinary assassin. With unparalleled skills and an uncanny ability to blend into the shadows, she had earned the nickname "The Silent Blade" among her peers. Her razor-sharp instincts and lightning-fast reflexes made her a force to be reckoned with on the battlefield.

Born in the streets of Taipei, Liga's early life was marked by hardship and struggle. Orphaned at a young age, she was forced to fend for herself, relying on her wits and cunning to survive. It was during this tumultuous period that she discovered her true calling – to become a master of stealth and deception.

As Liga grew older, she honed her skills through rigorous training and experimentation. She studied the art of disguise, mastering the subtleties of human behavior and body language. Her eyes became adept at reading people's intentions, allowing her to navigate even the most treacherous of situations with ease.

One fateful night, Liga received a lucrative offer from a wealthy client to eliminate a high-ranking government official. The target was known for his ruthless ambition, and Liga knew that this job would be a challenge unlike any other. Undaunted, she accepted the mission, using her extraordinary abilities to infiltrate the official's heavily guarded mansion.

As the clock ticked down, Liga Chia-wei proved herself to be more than just a skilled assassin – she was a ghost, invisible and unstoppable. With each passing moment, she drew closer to her goal, leaving no trail behind. In the end, it was only a whisper of steel that betrayed her presence, as she slipped out into the night, the Silent Blade vanishing into the shadows.

The mission accomplished, Liga returned to her client, collecting her payment and disappearing into the darkness once more. Her reputation grew, as whispers of The Silent Blade spread like wildfire through the underworld. Liga Chia-wei knew that her skills were in high demand – but at what cost?
