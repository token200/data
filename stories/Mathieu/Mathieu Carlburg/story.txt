Mathieu Carlburg stepped into the dimly lit jazz club, the sounds of the saxophone and piano filling his ears. The crowd swayed to the music, lost in the world of sound that Mathieu Carlburg created.

As they parted ways, he felt invigorated, his creative spark reignited by the presence of Sophia.
