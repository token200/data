Mathieu Jean-Luc Rosen was a man with a passion for photography. Growing up in the French countryside, he spent most of his childhood exploring the rolling hills and fields, camera in hand, capturing the beauty of nature.

He edited and retouched them, until they were perfect, just as he wanted them to be. When he finally put his final touches on the last image, Mathieu felt a sense of satisfaction that only came from knowing he had captured something truly special.
