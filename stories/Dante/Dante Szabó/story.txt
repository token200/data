Dante Szabó stepped off the crowded bus and onto the sun-scorched streets of Budapest. As he made his way through the throngs of tourists, he couldn't help but feel a sense of restlessness. It had been months since he'd left his corporate job in New York, chasing after a new sense of purpose.

Dante Szabó had always been driven by a desire to make a difference, but the monotony of office life had slowly drained him. He yearned for something more – something that would allow him to connect with others on a deeper level.
