The pay was substantial, and the risk was enticing – just the kind of challenge that drew Dante Eglīte in.

Dante Eglīte smiled to himself, a plan already forming in his mind. He moved with fluid grace, slipping past the guard like a ghost and into the shadows of the museum. The artifact was within reach, its value beyond measure.
