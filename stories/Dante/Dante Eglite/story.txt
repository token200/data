One fateful night, while on a solo operation in Eastern Europe, Dante received a cryptic message from an unknown source. The message was simple: "The Phoenix has risen." Intrigued, Dante decided to investigate further. He tracked down a lead to an abandoned warehouse on the outskirts of Budapest, where he found himself face-to-face with a group of heavily armed mercenaries.

Without hesitation, Dante sprang into action, taking down the mercenaries one by one. His training and skill proved to be too much for them, leaving him victorious but shaken. As he surveyed the carnage, a figure emerged from the shadows – a young woman with piercing green eyes and raven-black hair.

"Who are you?" Dante demanded, his gun still trained on the mysterious woman.

"My name is Ariana," she replied, her voice husky and confident. "And I'm the one who sent you that message."

Dante's curiosity was piqued. He had been searching for answers about a long-lost brother, who had gone missing under mysterious circumstances. Could this be connected?

Dante's eyes narrowed. He knew that getting involved in such matters would put him in grave danger, but his sense of duty and responsibility drove him forward. With Ariana by his side, Dante set out on a perilous journey to unravel the secrets surrounding his brother's disappearance.

As they walked away from the warehouse, Dante couldn't help but wonder what other surprises lay ahead for him.
