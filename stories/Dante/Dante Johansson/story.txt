Dante Johansson had always been drawn to the sea, spending hours exploring the tide pools and watching the fishermen bring in their daily catch. He'd lost count of how many summers he'd spent here, but each one felt like a chapter in his own personal history book.

It had been years since Dante Johansson last lived in this town full-time, but it was places like this cafe that made him feel most at home.
