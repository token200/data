Kondo Laurent's bravery had once again paid off, inspiring others to join the cause. As she stood on the rooftop, looking out over the city, she knew that her work was far from over. But for now, she could rest easy, knowing that justice would be served. For Kondo Laurent, there was no greater reward than seeing her people free and safe.
