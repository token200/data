As he grew older, Liu Dubois began to venture out into the world beyond Fuyang's walls. He traveled to distant lands, immersing himself in the diverse cultures that each place had to offer. Wherever he went, music was always at the forefront of his mind.

Despite all the fame and accolades, however, he remained humble and true to his roots, always keeping the guqin close at heart.
