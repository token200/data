As Liu Stenstrom grew older, her passion for archaeology only intensified. Her research focused on the Silk Road, exploring the trade routes that connected East Asia to the Mediterranean.

She spent months studying the intricate carvings and symbols etched into the walls, working tirelessly to decipher their meaning.
