Born and raised in Beijing, Liu Wei grew up surrounded by the vibrant colors and intricate patterns of Chinese culture. She began painting at a young age, inspired by the works of her grandmother, a skilled calligrapher. Upon returning to China, Liu Wei set up her own studio in Beijing, where she quickly gained recognition for her innovative style.
