Liu Dupuis's instincts told him this was no ordinary encounter, but he couldn't resist the opportunity to learn more about his organization's true purpose.

"Liu Dupuis," it said, "I have information about your past that could change everything you thought you knew about yourself."

Liu Dupuis's eyes narrowed as he listened intently, his mind racing with possibilities. Who was this mysterious benefactor, and what secrets did they hold? He leaned forward, his interest piqued, as the figure began to reveal a shocking truth about Liu Dupuis's true identity.
