As he gazed upon her pieces, he felt a sudden chill run down his spine. The paintings seemed to pulse with an energy that echoed the turmoil outside.

Their conversations were stilted at first, but as Leo delved deeper into Amalia's world, he found himself drawn to her intensity. She spoke of her art with an unwavering passion, as if it was a physical manifestation of her very soul.
