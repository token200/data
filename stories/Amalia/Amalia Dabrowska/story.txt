But Amalia Dabrowska was more than just a baker - she was a force to be reckoned with. She had a fierce determination and a heart of gold, which earned her the admiration of everyone in town. Amalia chuckled. "Don't worry, I'll help you make a decision. What do you like best: sweet treats or savory goodies?"

As they chatted, Amalia Dabrowska revealed her own love for baking as a child in Poland, where her parents had taught her the secrets of traditional desserts. The girls spent the next hour chatting and laughing together, and by the time Sophie left with a bag full of cookies, Amalia felt like she'd made a new friend.
