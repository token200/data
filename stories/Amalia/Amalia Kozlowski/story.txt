With this new evidence, Amalia Kozlowski pieced together a shocking narrative of deception and betrayal. She confronted Marcus Thompson with the evidence, and he crumbled under the weight of his own guilt. In the end, justice was served, thanks to Amalia Kozlowski's tireless efforts.
