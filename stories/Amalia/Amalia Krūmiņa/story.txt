Amalia Krūmiņa stood out in the crowded streets of Riga, her bright pink hair drawing attention from passersby. Her art was a reflection of this fascination, a call to question the status quo and challenge our assumptions about the world around us.

Her work was a celebration of the country's rich cultural history, as well as its complex and often fraught relationship with its past. Amalia Krūmiņa knew that her art would spark debate and discussion, and she welcomed that.
