Amalia Yun had always been fascinated by the world of art and architecture. Growing up in a small town surrounded by rolling hills and quaint villages, she spent most of her childhood exploring the local streets and admiring the intricate stone carvings that adorned the ancient buildings.

As Amalia Yun entered adulthood, she found herself drawn to the vibrant city life. She threw herself into the project, pouring over texts on eco-friendly materials and innovative design methods.
