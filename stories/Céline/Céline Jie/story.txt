As she began to work on her latest piece, a towering wall in the heart of the city came alive with color and movement. Céline Jie's brushstrokes danced across the canvas, blending bright blues and greens with bold geometric shapes. The crowd that gathered to watch her work couldn't help but be drawn in by her infectious energy.
