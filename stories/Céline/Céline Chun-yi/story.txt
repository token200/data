Under Ma's guidance, Céline Chun-yi's skills continued to flourish. She devoted herself to mastering the subtleties of Tai Chi, delving deep into the spiritual dimensions of the art. Her practice became a daily ritual, one that balanced physical movement with mental discipline and inner peace.

Years passed, and Céline Chun-yi's reputation grew. She traveled far and wide, sharing her unique style with fellow practitioners and captivating audiences with its beauty and power.
