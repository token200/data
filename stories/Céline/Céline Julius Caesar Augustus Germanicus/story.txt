Céline parried every blow, using her knowledge of the Romans' tactics against them. "Well done, young woman," he said, his voice deep and commanding. "You have proven yourself to be one of the greatest gladiators Rome has ever known."

And so, Céline Julius Caesar Augustus Germanicus became a legend in her own right, fighting alongside Emperor Augustus himself as one of his most trusted gladiators.
