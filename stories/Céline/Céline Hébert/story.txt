In the center of the clearing stood an enormous pine tree, its trunk gnarled with age and its branches stretching towards the sky like a giant's arms. She felt a shiver run down her spine as she raised her camera to her eye, capturing the ethereal light that seemed to emanate from within the tree.

Céline Hébert spent the next few days lost in the woods, her lens drinking in the beauty of the park.
