As Céline Bonnet approached the bakery, she noticed a group of suspicious-looking individuals lingering around the scene. She pulled out her notebook and began to take notes, her eyes scanning the crowd for any signs of deceit. Céline Bonnet's curiosity was piqued, and she quickly made her way over to the man, introducing herself as a journalist from the local paper. As Céline Bonnet continued her investigation, she couldn't shake off the feeling that there was more to Jacques' story than he was letting on.
