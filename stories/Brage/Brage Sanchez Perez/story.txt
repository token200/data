Legend had it that the Shadow Wolf was a being of pure darkness, capable of manipulating shadows to move through the world unnoticed. Many believed it to be a myth, but Brage Sanchez Perez was determined to prove them wrong.

He spent months researching and tracking down any possible leads on the creature's existence. Finally, after years of searching, he received a cryptic message from an anonymous source claiming to have seen the Shadow Wolf in the depths of the Amazon rainforest.

Excited by his discovery, Brage Sanchez Perez spent the rest of the night tracking the creature through the forest. Eventually, he cornered the beast in a clearing, and as it turned to face him, their eyes met for a brief moment.
