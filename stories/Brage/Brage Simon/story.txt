Brage Simon listened attentively, his eyes narrowing as he processed the information. He agreed to take on the case, and set out to investigate Emma's disappearance. As Brage Simon pressed for more information, the man hesitated, clearly afraid. But Brage Simon's years of experience told him that this was exactly what he needed - a lead worth following. He extracted the name "Alexander" from the man's lips, and set off to track down the suspect.

Finally, he arrived at an abandoned warehouse on the waterfront, where he spotted Alexander lurking in the shadows.

Without hesitation, Brage Simon approached Alexander, his eyes blazing with a fierce determination. The man sneered at first, but Brage Simon's calm demeanor soon wore him down. Eventually, Alexander cracked, revealing that Emma had been kidnapped by a group of human traffickers.

Armed with this information, Brage Simon was able to rally the police and track down the kidnappers. It was a long and difficult road, but in the end, justice was served. Emma was found safe and sound, thanks to the tireless efforts of Brage Simon.
