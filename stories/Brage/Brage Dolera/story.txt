As a young man, he began to hone his skills on the streets, learning how to pick locks and pockets with ease. He quickly rose through the ranks of the city's underworld, earning the nickname "The Fox" for his cunning and agility.

As Brage Dolera navigated the dark alleys and hidden courtyards of the city, he had a reputation for being one step ahead of his enemies. His charm and wit were matched only by his cleverness, allowing him to talk his way out of even the most precarious situations. But make no mistake, behind that charming facade lay a cold and calculating mind, always on the lookout for the next score.

One night, Brage Dolera set his sights on the city's most secure museum, determined to steal the priceless artifact known as the Starlight Seren. The heist was a daunting task, but one that Brage Dolera accepted with relish. He spent weeks casing the joint, studying the guards' schedules and waiting for the perfect moment to strike.

Finally, the night of the heist arrived, and Brage Dolera slipped into the museum like a ghost. With swift precision, he bypassed the security measures and made his way to the display case. The Starlight Seren sparkled in the dim light, its beauty captivating Brage Dolera's eyes. In that moment, he knew it was worth everything. He snatched the artifact from the case and melted into the night, leaving behind only whispers of his presence.
