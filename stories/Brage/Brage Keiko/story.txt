Despite the risks, Brage Keiko felt drawn to the art collective's message of rebellion and creativity. She spent countless hours poring over their works, trying to unravel the mysteries hidden within. As she got closer to the truth, Brage Keiko found herself increasingly paranoid and isolated. She began to receive strange messages and warnings from unknown sources, urging her to back off the story. But Brage Keiko was undeterred, convinced that she had uncovered something big.
