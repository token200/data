Edward Bonnet's years of experience had honed his skills to perfection, allowing him to remain focused even in the most chaotic of environments. He knew that time was crucial in solving a case like this one, and he couldn't let anything stand in his way.

Edward's eyes locked onto the man's back, his grip on his coat tightening as he hastened to keep up. He knew that this was just the beginning - a long and intricate dance, where every move had to be carefully considered in order to uncover the truth. Edward Bonnet's instincts were his strongest ally, guiding him toward justice with unyielding determination.
