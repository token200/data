Joshua Viljanen was a quiet and introspective artist who found solace in the city's vibrant street art scene. So, he set out to create his own world, one canvas at a time.

Joshua Viljanen spent most of his days exploring the city, taking note of the intricate patterns on street signs, the vibrant colors of market stalls, and the gritty textures of abandoned buildings. He carried a sketchbook with him wherever he went, capturing the beauty in the mundane and turning it into something beautiful.

As his art gained attention, Joshua began to receive commissions from local businesses and individuals who appreciated his unique perspective. People would stop him on the street, admiring his work and asking for recommendations on where to find more of his stuff.
