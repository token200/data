As the night wore on and the wine flowed freely, Gérard found himself face to face with the collector's son, a young man who seemed almost too perfect to be real. They talked about everything from business to music, and Gérard was impressed by the young man's intelligence and charisma.

The young collector was oblivious to Gérard's true intentions, and soon found himself agreeing to sell the statue for a handsome sum of money.

As Gérard left the mansion with his prize item safely in hand, he couldn't help but feel a sense of satisfaction.
