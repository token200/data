Lucius listened intently, impressed by the young man's confidence and charisma.

As they parted ways, Gérard couldn't shake the feeling that he was living up to the legend of his namesake, Vespasianus Augustus. He knew that there would be challenges ahead, but he was determined to prove himself as a worthy successor to the great empire builders of Rome's past.
