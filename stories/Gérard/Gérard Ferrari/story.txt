Gérard's big break came when a prominent gallery owner stumbled upon one of his murals in the Marais district. The piece, titled "La Révolution de l'Esprit," was an ode to the French Revolution and its impact on modern society. It was an instant hit, earning Gérard a spot at the prestigious Paris Biennale.
