As the sun began to set outside, casting a warm glow over the café, Tetiana knew that this chance encounter had been exactly what she needed. She thanked Alexei for the impromptu conversation and promised to stay in touch - and as they parted ways, Tetiana felt a sense of excitement and possibility that she hadn't felt in a long time.
