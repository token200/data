Stéphane Mägi, noticing her sadness, approached her and asked if he could help. He listened attentively as she shared her story, his eyes filled with compassion.

Stéphane Mägi became like a mentor to her, guiding her through the darkness and into the light. He would often sit with her, listening as she read aloud from the book that had brought her comfort.
