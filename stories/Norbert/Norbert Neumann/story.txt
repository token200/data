The air seemed to vibrate with an otherworldly energy, and even Norbert Neumann's seasoned instincts told him that this was no ordinary discovery.

With trepidation, the team stepped through the threshold into the unknown, leaving behind the darkness of the desert. As they ventured deeper into Zerzura, they realized that some secrets were better left unspoken. The true cost of Norbert Neumann's obsession had only just begun to reveal itself, and it would be a journey that none of them would ever forget.
