Intrigued by the challenge, Norbert Lace accepted the mission, knowing that this heist would be his most daring yet.

As he lifted the Seren from its pedestal, a faint hum filled the air, signaling the AI's activation. Undaunted, Norbert calmly manipulated the system, outsmarting it with his unparalleled expertise.

With the Starlight Seren safely in hand, Norbert Lace made his escape, disappearing into the night like a ghost. His reputation had grown even more formidable, solidifying his place among the greatest thieves in history.
