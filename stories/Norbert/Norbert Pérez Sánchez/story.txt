Norbert Pérez Sánchez was a man with a passion for music that knew no bounds. As he grew older, Norbert's love for music only deepened. He began taking lessons on the classical guitar from a young age, and soon became proficient enough to start performing at local festivals and events. As the night wore on, Norbert's music continued to weave its spell, transporting his listeners to a world of beauty and wonder.
