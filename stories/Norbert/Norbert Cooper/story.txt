As they continued to talk, Norbert Cooper's mind began to wander. He thought about his childhood, growing up on a farm surrounded by rolling hills and green pastures. Mrs. Jenkins, sensing Norbert Cooper's nostalgia, asked him to tell her more about his childhood. Norbert Cooper smiled, remembering the countless hours he spent playing in the fields, chasing after butterflies and watching the sunset over the hills. As the afternoon wore on, Mrs. Jenkins thanked Norbert Cooper for his company and left with the sugar she had borrowed. As she walked back to her house, she felt grateful for their friendship and the way Norbert Cooper always knew how to make her feel welcome.

Back in his garden, Norbert Cooper sat among his flowers, feeling content and at peace. He thought about Mrs. Jenkins' kind words and smiled, knowing that he had made a difference in her day.
