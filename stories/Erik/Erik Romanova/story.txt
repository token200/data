Her full name, Erik Romanova, was once synonymous with fear and respect, as she patrolled the city's streets, fighting for justice and protecting its citizens from the shadows. Erik Romanova was no longer the fearless vigilante she once was, but a broken, vulnerable woman, adrift in a world that seemed determined to keep her down.
