As word of Vivaan's dedication to the garden spread, the community began to rally around her. They saw in her a kindred spirit, someone who shared their passion for creating positive change. Together, they worked towards building a greener, more compassionate world. Vivaan's art took on a new dimension – she no longer just created for herself; she created for others.
