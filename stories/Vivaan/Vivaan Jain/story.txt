Vivaan's full name, Vidaan Kumar Jain, echoed in his mind as he navigated through the crowded markets. He had always been close to his sister, Nalini, and wanted to get her something truly special. After hours of searching, he finally stumbled upon a small antique shop tucked away in a quiet alley.

The owner, an elderly man named Mr. Rao, greeted Vivaan warmly and asked what brought him to the shop. Vidaan Kumar Jain explained that it was his sister's birthday and he was looking for something unique and special. Mr. Rao listened intently, nodding his head from time to time, before disappearing into the back room.

As he made his way back to his apartment, Vidaan felt a sense of satisfaction wash over him. Not only had he found the perfect gift for his sister, but he had also connected with someone who shared his passion for history and culture.
