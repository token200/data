As she grew older, Vivaan's love for art only intensified, and she decided to pursue a career as a painter. She spent countless hours honing her skills in local art schools and workshops, experimenting with different techniques and mediums until she found her unique style.

They struck up a conversation about art and music, and before long, they realized that their passions complemented each other perfectly. Lukas invited Vivaan to join him on stage for an impromptu performance, and she accepted without hesitation.

She began to incorporate music into her art, using colors and textures to represent the rhythms and melodies that echoed in her mind.
