As she grew older, Yon Eglitis practiced tirelessly, honing her skills until she became a formidable opponent.

The fight was fierce, the enemy army seemingly endless. But Yon Eglitis fought on, her movements swift and precise as she sliced through the ranks of the dark forces. Her opponents fell before her, their lives extinguished by the blade that was once her father's.
