As the night wore on, Yon Aber's presence drew more and more attention. People began to approach him, drawn by his magnetic personality and the sense that there was more to him than met the eye. But no matter how hard they tried, they couldn't seem to pry any secrets out of him.

Yon Aber smiled again, his smile like a slow-burning fire. "Not at all," he replied. As they talked, their conversation flowed easily, like two pieces of a puzzle fitting together perfectly.
