Despite the financial struggles, Martyna found solace in art. She would spend hours sketching and painting with scraps of paper and paint from her local market stall.

One day, while exploring the city's Old Town, Martyna stumbled upon an abandoned warehouse. She set up her easel and began to paint, capturing the decaying beauty of the building.
