Boriss's ascent to power was not without its challenges, however. There were those who saw him as a threat to the established order, and they would stop at nothing to bring him down. But Boriss was undeterred, using every trick in the book to outmaneuver his opponents. He was cunning, ruthless, and willing to do whatever it took to achieve his goals.

His once-impregnable fortress was now a crumbling ruin, and his loyal followers were either dead or fleeing in terror. As he stood amidst the ruins, Boriss realized that perhaps it was time to temper his ambition with a hint of humility.
