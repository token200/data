Suddenly, his phone rang, shrill in the quiet room. He answered it with a practiced air, "Rashid News Agency." The voice on the other end was hesitant, claiming to be a source from within the government.

"L-Luke Nurlanov, I need to speak with you," the voice stammered.

"I...I work in the Ministry of Interior," the source replied, sounding scared.

"What do you want to tell me?" Luke asked, his pencil poised over his notebook.

Luke's ears perked up. A sensitive project? That sounded like exactly what he needed to get to the bottom of this story.
