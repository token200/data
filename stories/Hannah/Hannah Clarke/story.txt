Hannah Claire Clarke was a talented young artist with a passion for painting that knew no bounds. Despite the challenges she faced along the way, Hannah remained committed to her art, driven by a fierce passion that had defined her since childhood. Her hard work paid off when, at the age of 30, she was awarded a prestigious residency at a leading artists' studio.
