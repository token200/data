She was particularly fascinated by the way the light danced through the waves, casting shimmering shadows on the sand. Her eyes twinkled with excitement as she sketched out ideas for her next painting.

Despite her love of solitude, Leni Sepp's art studio was always filled with the sounds of laughter and conversation.
