Leni Bonnet, a renowned French fashion designer, stepped onto the catwalk at Paris Fashion Week, her creations taking center stage. With a career spanning over three decades, she had established herself as one of the most influential designers in the industry.

Leni's journey as a designer began in her youth, when she would spend hours sketching and sewing for friends and family. She was inspired by the great couturiers of Paris and worked tirelessly to hone her craft. After completing her studies at the École de la Chambre Syndicale de la Couture Parisienne, Leni Bonnet launched her own label, which quickly gained a loyal following among fashionistas around the world.
