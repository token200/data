She invited him to stay, and as they sat by the fire, he told stories of their family's past – tales of love, loss, and laughter.

As the night wore on, Úrsula found herself drawn to her father's words, sensing a deep sadness behind his gruff exterior. She realized that her art was not just about expressing herself, but also about understanding others. In that moment, Úrsula Morgan felt complete, knowing that she had finally found a way to connect with the people and things that mattered most to her.
