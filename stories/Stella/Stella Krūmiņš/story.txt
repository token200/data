Now, at 32, Stella had made a name for herself as a fearless and incisive journalist, able to coax even the most reluctant sources into sharing their stories. Her latest investigation, however, was proving to be particularly stubborn.

Stella Krūmiņš had set her sights on the city's growing real estate market, where wealthy investors were snapping up historic buildings and turning them into soulless office complexes.
