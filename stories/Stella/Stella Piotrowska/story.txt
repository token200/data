Stella Piotrowska was known for her unbridled enthusiasm and infectious laugh, which could light up even the most mundane of days. As a free-spirited artist, she spent most of her time exploring the city's hidden corners, soaking up inspiration from every nook and cranny.

From painting vibrant murals on city walls to creating thought-provoking installations in galleries, Stella Piotrowska's career had been marked by a relentless pursuit of creativity and self-expression.
