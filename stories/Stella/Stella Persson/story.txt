But Stella remained steadfast, refusing to be swayed by the attention or intimidated by those who sought to silence her.

Despite the chaos that surrounded her, Stella persevered, driven by a fierce determination to see justice served. She became a thorn in the side of those she had exposed, but also earned the respect and admiration of her peers.

One evening, as she was working late on a follow-up story, Stella received an unexpected visit from her editor-in-chief.
