"Mats Kovalenko," he replied, setting aside his pen, "what seems to be the matter?"

As the night wore on, Mats Kovalenko decided it was time to pay a visit to his old connections in the intelligence community. He made a few discreet calls, using his skills of persuasion to get them to talk. What he learned would change everything.
