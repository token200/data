Word of Mats' remarkable abilities spread quickly, and soon he was inundated with requests from all corners of Riga. People sought his advice on matters ranging from animal behavior to conservation efforts. Through his tireless work, Mats Pētersons earned a reputation as a champion for the welfare of animals and the environment.
