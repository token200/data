As he grew older, Mats' love for music only deepened. He began to teach himself how to play the guitar and write songs, inspired by the likes of Bob Dylan and The Beatles. His voice was rough around the edges, but it had a raw energy that captivated all who heard him sing.

After high school, Mats set out on his own, traveling from town to town, playing small gigs and honing his craft. He met people from all walks of life, each with their own story to tell. As the night wore on, Mats and Luna found themselves lost in conversation, bonding over their shared love of art and music. It was as if time had stood still, and all that existed was the two of them, suspended in a moment of pure magic.

Mats López Rodríguez knew that his life had changed forever. He had found a kindred spirit, someone who understood him on a deep and fundamental level.
