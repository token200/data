As Francois delved deeper into his research, he became increasingly obsessed with unraveling the secrets of this ancient city. Determined to uncover the truth, Francois set out on a perilous journey through the treacherous jungle terrain, guided by his insatiable curiosity and unwavering determination. As he navigated the dense foliage, he began to feel a sense of presence around him - as if the very spirits of the ancient city were watching his every move.

As he finally reached the heart of the city, Francois Wei-lun felt a surge of excitement and trepidation. What would he find there? Would it be the artifact he had been searching for all this time, or something even more profound?
