Istvan Muller's true intentions remained shrouded in mystery, leaving many to wonder if he was a guardian of ancient wisdom or a servant of unspeakable horrors.

Sophia, now bound to the dark arts by her own curiosity, knew she must seek out Istvan Muller. She would find him in his study, surrounded by the shadows and secrets that only the most intrepid scholars dared to confront. The fate of those who dared to follow in her footsteps hung precariously in the balance, as Istvan Muller's presence loomed like a dark specter on the horizon.
