Born Istvan Pires, the son of a wealthy family, he had always felt stifled by the expectations placed upon him. So, at a young age, he had set out to forge his own path, using his cunning and intelligence to carve a new destiny for himself.

As he grew older, Istvan's skills as a thief only improved, earning him the nickname "The Fox." He became known for his ability to slip in and out of the most secure locations undetected, leaving behind only a whisper of his presence.
