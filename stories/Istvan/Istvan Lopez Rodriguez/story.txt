As he wandered through the crowds, he came across a group of children playing music on makeshift instruments. The joy and laughter emanating from them was infectious, and Istvan couldn't resist capturing their moment.

The resulting photo, titled "La Fiesta de la Vida," became one of Istvan Lopez Rodriguez's most famous works.
