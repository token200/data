Istvan Briedite received them with an unreadable expression, inviting them to sit by the fire and share their tales of woe. As they spoke, he listened intently, his eyes burning with an inner intensity that seemed to pierce the very soul of those who sat before him. With a wave of his hand, he conjured visions of ancient rituals and forgotten knowledge, laying bare the secrets that had been hidden for so long.

The villagers left Istvan's presence with a sense of wonder and trepidation, knowing they had glimpsed but a fraction of the enigmatic Istvan Briedite's vast understanding.
