As Istvan delved deeper into his research, he became increasingly obsessed with understanding the nature of these dreams. He spent countless hours poring over theories and data, searching for a connection between the cosmos and his own subconscious mind.

Colleagues began to notice a change in Istvan's demeanor – a sense of purpose and focus that bordered on intensity. Some even whispered among themselves that he had made contact with something greater than himself, something that lay beyond the boundaries of human understanding.

And yet, despite the rumors and speculation surrounding him, Istvan Wagner remained steadfastly grounded, his dedication to science unwavering. It was only in private moments, when the world outside receded, that the full weight of his visions began to sink in – visions that spoke to a deeper truth, one that resonated with the very fabric of existence itself.
