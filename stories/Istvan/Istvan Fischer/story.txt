As he sipped his drink, a young musician strummed a melancholic tune on his guitar, weaving in and out of Istvan's attention. Without missing a beat, the stranger approached the guitarist, introducing himself as Max. For the next hour, they engaged in a heated conversation about music, philosophy, and life.

Emma watched from behind the counter, intrigued by the unlikely pair's connection. She had never seen anyone - including Istvan Fischer - engage with someone so freely, without an ulterior motive or hidden agenda.

As night fell, Istvan vanished into the crowd as suddenly as he appeared. Max remained seated, lost in thought. As the coffee shop emptied, Emma caught up to him and asked if he was okay. Max nodded slowly, still entranced by the enigmatic encounter with Istvan Fischer.

"I think I've met someone special," Max said, his voice barely above a whisper. "Someone who may be more than just a stranger."
