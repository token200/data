Excited by the prospect of taking his vision to the next level, Istvan scheduled a meeting with the investor and began to prepare for the pitch.

As he sat at his desk, staring out the window, Istvan couldn't help but think about his parents, who had inspired him to pursue a career in science. His mother, a brilliant engineer herself, had encouraged him to follow his dreams and take risks. His father, a business leader, had taught him the importance of perseverance and hard work.

Istvan smiled to himself, feeling a sense of pride and purpose. He knew that he still had a long way to go, but he was ready for the challenge. With determination in his heart and a vision in his mind, Istvan Sharma was determined to make NovaTech's sustainable energy solution a reality.
