One fateful night, Hartwin Taro paid a visit to Blackwood's sleek, high-rise office tower, where he was met with a mixture of hostility and curiosity. The mogul himself emerged from the shadows, his eyes narrowed in challenge.

Hartwin stood firm, his voice steady despite the growing unease. "I'll take my chances," he replied, his gaze never wavering.

As the confrontation escalated, Hartwin's instincts told him that this was a turning point – either he would emerge victorious or face the consequences. With a sense of determination, he pressed on, refusing to back down from the truth, no matter where it might lead.
