Hartwin Fernandez Gonzalez was a man with a passion for music that knew no bounds. One night, after a particularly rousing performance at a local festival, Hartwin was approached by a record label executive who offered him a deal. The young musician was over the moon – this was his chance to share his music with the world.

Hartwin Fernandez Gonzalez threw himself into the recording studio, determined to create something special. He spent hours in the booth, perfecting every note and melody. When he finally emerged with a finished album, he knew that he had created something truly special.
