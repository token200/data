But what few people knew about Urmas Välja was that he wasn't always a master thief. As a young boy, Urmas had to rely on his wits and cunning to survive, often going hungry or begging for scraps just to make it through the day.

One fateful night, when Urmas was just 16 years old, he stumbled upon an opportunity that would change the course of his life forever. As he turned to leave, he heard the sound of Jaan Kikas himself stirring in his bed. Panicked, Urmas dropped the necklace and made a run for it.

He emerged from the mansion just as dawn broke on the horizon, the sun's rays illuminating his sweaty face. From that moment on, Urmas Välja was a man with a mission: to use his newfound skills to outwit the wealthy elite and bring justice to those who had been wronged.

As the years went by, Urmas' legend grew.
