Urmas Fischer's curiosity was piqued, and he carefully examined the box. The intricate carvings seemed to dance in the fading light, as if holding secrets of their own. He turned the box over, studying every detail, before finally discovering a hidden catch.

He spent hours pouring over the code, his mind racing with possibilities. The symbols seemed to shift and change before his very eyes, as if taunting him with their secrets. But Urmas Fischer was undeterred, using his vast knowledge of cryptography to slowly unravel the mystery.

As the night wore on, the room grew darker, but Urmas Fischer's focus never wavered. Finally, after what seemed like an eternity, he cracked the code. The solution revealed a hidden message, penned by a long-forgotten civilization.

Urmas Fischer's eyes widened as he read the words, his mind reeling with the implications. He had uncovered a piece of history that few others would ever lay eyes on. As he sat back in his chair, exhausted but exhilarated, The Collector appeared once more.

"Well done, Urmas Fischer," the stranger said, his voice low and mysterious. "You have proven yourself to be one of the greatest cryptologists of our time. I think it's time we discussed the next challenge."
