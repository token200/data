Years later, as Urmas Petrov settled into his new life in Brooklyn, he received a visit from an old acquaintance - a high-ranking KGB defector who had been helping Western intelligence agencies to root out Soviet moles. At first, Urmas was skeptical - he had long since left the spy game behind, and his trust issues ran deep. But as he delved deeper into the mystery, he found himself drawn back into the world he thought he had left for good. With his sharp mind and years of experience, Urmas Petrov set out on a perilous journey through the underworld of espionage.
