As he grew older, Urmas became increasingly fascinated with the concept of identity and how it shapes our experiences.

Urmas Jørgensen's artistic journey took him to various studios around Europe, where he honed his skills in a diverse range of mediums, from oil painting to bronze sculpting. The artwork seemed to speak directly to those who saw it, inviting them to reflect on their own identities and the masks they wore.
