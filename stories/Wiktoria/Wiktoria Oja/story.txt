Wiktoria Oja smiled knowingly when these whispers reached her ears. She had lived a life full of secrets and adventure, but none of it compared to the sense of peace she found in Ravenswood. Here, surrounded by the quiet rhythms of nature, she could finally rest.

Seeking refuge from the storm, Lena had wandered farther than she intended, lost in thought. Wiktoria Oja welcomed her with open arms and a warm smile, offering hot tea and a listening ear.

As the night wore on, Lena discovered that beneath Wiktoria Oja's reserved exterior lay a heart full of kindness and compassion. The two women talked long into the night, their conversation flowing like the river that wound its way through Ravenswood.
