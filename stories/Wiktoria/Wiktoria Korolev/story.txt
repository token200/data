Wiktoria Korolev was a force to be reckoned with in the art world. As a painter, she had always been drawn to the expressive possibilities of color and light. Her vibrant works were sought after by collectors and museums alike, but Wiktoria's true passion lay in her latest project: creating an immersive installation that would transport viewers into the heart of Russia's winter landscapes.

As the installation began to take shape, Wiktoria's excitement grew. As she turned out the lights, the room was plunged into darkness, and Wiktoria felt a shiver run down her spine. On opening night, Wiktoria Korolev's installation drew in crowds of art lovers and curious onlookers alike.
