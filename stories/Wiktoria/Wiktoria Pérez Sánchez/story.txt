As she delved deeper into her investigation, Wiktoria encountered resistance from those in power who didn't want their secrets exposed. She faced intimidation tactics, threats, and even a lawsuit filed against her by the businessman himself.

Undeterred, Wiktoria continued to push forward, fueled by her determination to uncover the truth. Her tireless efforts finally paid off when she broke the story, revealing widespread corruption and abuse of power.
