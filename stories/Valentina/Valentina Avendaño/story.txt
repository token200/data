Valentina Avendaño's thoughts were elsewhere, however. She had received a cryptic letter earlier that morning, inviting her to meet with an unknown benefactor at midnight. The message was shrouded in mystery, but something about it resonated deep within Valentina Avendaño's soul. Valentina Avendaño's eyes adjusted slowly to the dim light, and when they did, she saw that it was none other than her estranged father, thought to be lost at sea for years. His eyes locked onto hers, filled with tears of regret and apology.
