As a child, Valentina would spend hours watching her father, a master carpenter, work his magic on various projects.

Years went by, and Valentina's passion for woodworking only grew stronger. She began to apprentice with local artisans, learning the techniques and secrets of her trade. Her parents, seeing her dedication and natural talent, encouraged her to pursue her dreams. And so, Valentina Wiśniewski set out to make a name for herself in the world of fine woodcarvings.

As she honed her skills, Valentina's pieces began to gain attention from collectors and connoisseurs alike. Her unique style, which blended traditional Polish techniques with modern flair, quickly gained popularity. People would travel from far and wide to see her creations, each one a testament to her boundless creativity.
