Her sharp mind and quick wit made her a formidable opponent against even the most cunning of culprits.

The thief seemed to be always one step ahead, leaving behind no clues and no signs of forced entry or exit. Intrigued by the challenge, Valentina Jhih-hao accepted the case and began her investigation.

As she delved deeper into the world of high-stakes thievery, Valentina Jhih-hao discovered that the robberies were not just random acts of vandalism, but rather a carefully planned strategy to distract the police while a more sinister plot unfolded. With each passing day, the stakes grew higher, and Valentina Jhih-hao found herself closing in on her quarry.

The chase took her from the neon-lit streets of Shilin to the quiet alleyways of Zhongshan, with Valentina Jhih-hao navigating the city's underbelly with ease. She discovered a hidden safe house in an abandoned warehouse on the outskirts of town, where she finally confronted the mastermind behind the robberies.
