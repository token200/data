Her mother had always encouraged her to pursue her passion for linguistics, and now that she had finally landed a research position at the university, Aija was determined to make the most of it.

As they chatted, Aija's mind began to wander back to her childhood fascination with languages. She remembered spending hours poring over books on ancient tongues, trying to decipher their secrets. Her mother had always been supportive, but now that she was an adult, Aija felt a sense of pride and accomplishment in pursuing her own dreams.

The two friends parted ways, promising to catch up again soon. As Aija walked back to her parents' old cabin on the outskirts of town, she couldn't help but feel grateful for this chance to reconnect with her roots. Her full name, Aija Nemeth, was one that her mother had always been proud of – a nod to her Hungarian heritage and her father's linguistic legacy.

As she settled into her room, surrounded by books and papers, Aija felt a sense of excitement wash over her. This summer was going to be one for the books – or rather, one for the languages.
