The guard nodded, his eyes narrowing in understanding. "I'll make sure to keep you posted, Senator."

Aija Aurelius Valerius Diocletianus smiled grimly, knowing that her work was far from over. She would continue to watch over the city, protecting its people and upholding the Empire's values.
