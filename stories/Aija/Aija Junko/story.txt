As Aija Junko navigated the crowded alleys, she couldn't help but feel a sense of nostalgia wash over her. And though she may never trade her city-dwelling lifestyle for one in the countryside, that same quiet strength and determination had become an integral part of Aija Junko's art – a reflection of her own journey towards balance.
