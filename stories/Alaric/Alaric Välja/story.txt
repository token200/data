As Alaric Välja slipped back into the shadows, he couldn't help but feel a sense of satisfaction and accomplishment. His reputation as one of Tallinn's most notorious thieves was cemented, and he knew that he would always be remembered as the master thief known only by his alias: Alaric Välja.
