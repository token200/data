He spent countless nights scouring dusty flea markets, crumbling libraries, and forgotten attics, always on the lookout for the next great find.

As he walked through the winding streets of the old town, Alaric's eyes scanned the crowded market stalls, searching for that elusive gem that would make his latest acquisition stand out. His full name, Alaric Roux, was whispered among collectors and dealers alike, a reminder that this enigmatic figure had an uncanny knack for tracking down even the rarest of artifacts.
