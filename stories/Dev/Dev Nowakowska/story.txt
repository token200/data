As she browsed the shelves, running her fingers over the spines of worn books, Dev felt a sense of peace wash over her. Dev Nowakowska sat down at a small table by the window, opening a tattered copy of Walt Whitman's Leaves of Grass. As she delved into the poet's words, Dev felt her spirit soar. In that moment, she knew that no matter what the world threw her way, she would always be true to herself and her art.
