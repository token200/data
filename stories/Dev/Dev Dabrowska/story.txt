He was immediately drawn to her confidence and wit, and the two struck up a conversation that lasted long into the evening. But with great passion comes great risk, and soon Dev found herself caught up in a whirlwind romance with Max. She was swept off her feet by his charming smile and infectious laugh, and before she knew it, she was falling deeply in love.
