One fateful evening, while lost in thought at a local coffee shop, Dev overheard a conversation between two strangers. They spoke of a long-abandoned lighthouse, hidden deep within the nearby woods, said to be haunted by the ghost of its former keeper. Intrigued, Dev couldn't shake the feeling that he had to investigate further.

That night, under the light of a full moon, Dev set out on his quest. The wind howled through the trees as he navigated through the dense forest, his flashlight casting eerie shadows on the ground. Suddenly, a gust swept past him, and the beam landed on a dilapidated structure - the lighthouse.

With caution, Dev approached the entrance, noticing strange symbols etched into the doorframe. As he stepped inside, he felt an unsettling chill run down his spine. The air was heavy with the scent of decay, but amidst it all, Dev detected the faintest hint of rosemary - a favorite fragrance of his late grandmother.

The entries spoke of love, loss, and longing, echoing the emotions that often flooded Dev's own heart. In those moments, he felt an inexplicable connection to the long-lost soul.
