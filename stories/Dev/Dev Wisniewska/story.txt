Dev Wisniewska spent countless hours digging through documents and conducting interviews with fighters from all walks of life. He was determined to get to the bottom of the stories that were rarely told, and he wasn't afraid to take risks in order to do so.

One fateful night, Dev received a cryptic message from an anonymous source claiming to have information about the fight club scene. The message read: "Meet me at the old warehouse on 5th and Main at midnight. Come alone."
