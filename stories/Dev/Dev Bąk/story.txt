With a grin, he began to juggle three clubs while reciting Shakespearean sonnets in flawless Polish. The crowd gathered around, mesmerized by the whirlwind of movement and words.

As the morning sun climbed higher in the sky, Dev's performance attracted a young woman with striking green eyes and raven-black hair. As they parted ways, Dev knew he'd found a kindred spirit. He tucked Sofia's phone number into his pocket and promised to meet up again soon.
