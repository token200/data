Yuliana Baktybekova was born in the bustling streets of Almaty, Kazakhstan. Her childhood was marked by the vibrant colors and sounds of the local market, where her family sold traditional clothing and textiles. Her hard work paid off when she won a local design competition, which led to her being approached by a prominent fashion brand.

Yuliana's big break came when she was approached by the brand's creative director, who was blown away by her unique style and vision. He offered her a job on the spot, and Yuliana became one of the youngest designers to join the esteemed label.

Under Yuliana's watchful eye, the brand's designs began to take on a new level of sophistication and elegance.
