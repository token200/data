As she grew older, Yuliana's talents became more refined, and she began to experiment with different mediums and techniques.

Intrigued by her bold colors and energetic brushstrokes, the gallery owner invited her to showcase her art at an upcoming exhibition. Overjoyed at the opportunity, Yuliana eagerly accepted and began preparing for what promised to be a career-defining moment.
