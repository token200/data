Véronique Wei, or Lee Wei as she was known to her friends and family back in Hong Kong, had always been fascinated by the world of art. Growing up in a wealthy household, she was surrounded by masterpieces and collectors, but it wasn't until she stumbled upon a small, hidden gallery in the city that she truly found her passion.

As Lee Wei wandered through the galleries, taking in the works of local artists, she felt a sense of belonging that she had never experienced before. The owner of the gallery, an old man named Mr. Wong, took notice of her and struck up a conversation. They talked for hours about art, philosophy, and life, and Lee Wei found herself feeling seen and understood in a way that she never had before.

From that day on, Véronique Wei became a regular at the gallery, attending exhibitions and talking to Mr. Wong about everything from the latest trends to her own personal struggles. She found solace in his words of wisdom and support, and began to see the world in a new light.
