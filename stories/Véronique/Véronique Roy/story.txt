Véronique Roy was a talented young artist living in Paris. Her paintings were vibrant and full of life, capturing the essence of the city she loved. She spent most of her days working on her latest masterpiece, lost in thought as she brought her visions to life on canvas.

One day, a prominent art critic stumbled upon Véronique's work at a local gallery. He was immediately taken by the raw talent on display, and soon found himself championing Véronique Roy as one of the most exciting young artists to emerge from France in years.
