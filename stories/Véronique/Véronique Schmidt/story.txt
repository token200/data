Véronique Schmidt's art studio was a labyrinthine space filled with strange artifacts and relics. Shelves lined the walls, overflowing with crystals, tarot cards, and other trinkets she had collected on her travels. The air was thick with the scent of incense and the soft hum of candles cast flickering shadows across the room.
