One fateful evening, as the sun dipped below the horizon, casting the city in a warm orange glow, Véronique Romagnoli stood at the edge of a rooftop, gazing out over the urban landscape. Her eyes narrowed, her mind focusing on a single, burning question: what secrets lay hidden beneath the surface of this bustling metropolis?

As the night wore on, Véronique Romagnoli disappeared into the shadows, leaving behind only whispers of a ghostly presence that seemed to haunt the city's hidden corners. Her name became a whispered legend, a mythic figure whose very existence seemed to defy explanation.
