As the night wore on, Arkadiusz's resolve hardened. He vowed to protect Incept's interests while ensuring that its creations were wielded responsibly. The stakes had never been higher, and he was ready to do whatever it took to ensure the company's future.

The next morning, as his team began to stir, Arkadiusz disappeared into the shadows, fuelled by a singular purpose: to safeguard Incept's secrets while navigating the treacherous landscape of technological advancement.
