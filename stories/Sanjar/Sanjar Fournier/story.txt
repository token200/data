Sanjar Fournier smiled and nodded, taking a seat at the counter next to Pierre. As he waited for his coffee, he pulled out his notebook and began to scribble notes, capturing the sounds and smells of the café in all its intricate detail.

Sanjar Fournier shook his head, intrigued by the newcomer's shy demeanor. Sanjar Fournier found himself drawn to her quiet confidence and creative spark.
