Her eyes seemed to hold a world of stories within them, and those who crossed paths with Sanja Larsson couldn't help but be drawn in.

Emma had always been captivated by the idea of meeting someone like Sanja Larsson – someone with secrets and stories that lay hidden behind an enigmatic smile.
