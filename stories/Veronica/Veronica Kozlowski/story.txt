Taking a deep breath, Veronica refocused on her goals and reminded herself of her strengths. She delegated tasks more effectively, trusted her team's instincts, and took calculated risks that paid off in the end.

Her company landed a major win, and Veronica was hailed as a rising star in the industry. Though she still grappled with self-doubt, she knew that with perseverance and confidence, she could conquer even the toughest challenges.
