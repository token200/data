With his eyes scanning the shadows for any sign of trouble, he took down would-be henchmen with swift efficiency, keeping Liv Thompson close by to provide backup.

As they closed in on the museum, Sezim Lundgren could feel the adrenaline coursing through his veins. He knew this was it - the moment of truth. With a deep breath, he led the charge into the museum's heavily guarded entrance, ready to face whatever dangers lay ahead.
