Irene felt a mix of emotions: anger, sadness, and finally, a sense of pride and connection to her family's past. She realized that her parents' secrecy had been a way of shielding her from the harsh realities of their family's history, rather than hiding something shameful.

As she left the archive, Irene knew that her journey was far from over.
