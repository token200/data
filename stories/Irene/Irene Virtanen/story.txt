Now, at 35, Irene was back in Kuusamo for a reason: to help her family's struggling ski resort, Kelo, get back on its feet. Her father had passed away just a year ago, leaving behind a mountain of debt and a dwindling customer base. Irene, with her business degree from Helsinki, was determined to save the resort that had been in her family for generations.

As she made her way through the small town, Irene couldn't help but feel a sense of nostalgia wash over her. The streets were still lined with familiar shops and cafes, the same ones her parents had frequented as children.
