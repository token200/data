As she settled in for another long night of research, Inessa's colleagues began to gather around her, seeking her guidance and expertise.

"Who do we need to talk to next?" one of them asked.

Inessa smiled, her eyes sparkling with intensity. "I've got a few leads to follow up on," she replied. "Let's see where the evidence takes us."
