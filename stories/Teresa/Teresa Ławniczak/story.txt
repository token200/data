As a young girl, Teresa's parents encouraged her to explore her creativity, providing her with an array of art supplies and setting up a cozy little studio in their home. It was there that she discovered her true calling – to create beauty that would touch the hearts of those who saw it.

Despite her success, Teresa remained humble and true to her roots.
