She continued to travel and shoot, always seeking new ways to express herself and tell the stories that mattered most to her. And as she walked through the streets of Madrid one sunny afternoon, camera in hand, it was clear that Teresa Fernandez Gonzalez was still on a mission to capture the beauty of the world - one frame at a time.
