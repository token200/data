The street art scene in her neighborhood was thriving, and she felt proud to be a part of it. Her art had a way of bringing people together, sparking conversations and inspiring new ideas.

Aisha Green's studio was a reflection of her eclectic style – a fusion of bohemian chic and urban edge.
