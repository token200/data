Isaac Marrone was in his element, feeding off the energy of the crowd and channeling it into his performance.

As Echo Fade took their final bow and the crowd erupted into cheers, Isaac Marrone smiled wearily, knowing that it had been a night to remember – one that would stay with him long after the stage lights faded.
