After completing art school, Isaac landed a job as a freelance photographer in the city. He worked tirelessly, taking on assignment after assignment for clients who appreciated his unique eye and skill. His portfolio grew rapidly, and soon he was in high demand.

One day, Isaac received an email from a prominent magazine, offering him a chance to shoot their annual photo spread. Hours later, Isaac had captured everything he needed, and he began to head back to his apartment. As he reviewed his footage, he couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and accomplishment.
