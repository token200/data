Isaac Malinowski listened attentively to each story, his eyes never leaving theirs as he worked his magic. Word of Isaac's healing abilities spread quickly, and soon people were coming from far and wide to seek his help. But despite the attention, Isaac Malinowski remained committed to his work, using his gift to help those in need.
