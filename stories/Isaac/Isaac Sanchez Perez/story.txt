Isaac Sanchez Perez was a man with a passion for art and music. A fellow artist and musician, Lily shared Isaac's passion for creative expression and his desire to challenge the status quo. The two quickly bonded over their shared love of art and music, and soon they were collaborating on projects that blended their talents in innovative ways.

As Isaac Sanchez Perez looked out at the city skyline, he felt a sense of belonging he had never known before.
