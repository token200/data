His colleagues often teased him about his obsession, but Isaac couldn't help it - he was driven by an insatiable curiosity.

One day, while pouring over old texts in the university library, Isaac stumbled upon a cryptic reference to a legendary creature known as the "Włoszczyzna". According to local folklore, the Włoszczyzna was a mysterious being with shaggy hair and eyes that glowed like embers. Intrigued, Isaac became determined to find out if there was any truth behind the legend.

Isaac spent months tracking down leads, conducting interviews, and scouring the countryside for signs of the elusive creature. Finally, after weeks of searching, he received a cryptic message from an unknown source - a single sentence etched onto a piece of paper: "Look to the old oak in the forest".

With a sense of excitement building inside him, Isaac set out into the woods, following the winding path that led him deeper and deeper into the trees. As he pushed aside the branches, he caught sight of a figure standing beneath the ancient oak - its shaggy hair blowing gently in the breeze.

Isaac Wróbel's heart skipped a beat as he approached the creature cautiously. To his amazement, it turned to face him, its eyes glowing softly in the fading light. For a moment, the two simply stared at each other, Isaac unsure of what to make of this strange encounter.

As quickly as it had appeared, the creature vanished into thin air, leaving Isaac Wróbel feeling both exhilarated and bewildered.
