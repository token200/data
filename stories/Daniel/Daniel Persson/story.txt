After completing his degree, Daniel decided to open his own antique shop, where he could share his love for the past with others. He named it "Timeless Treasures" and spent countless hours scouring flea markets, estate sales, and auctions to build a collection of unique pieces.

The collector, an elderly man named Mr. Jenkins, was impressed by Daniel's encyclopedic knowledge of antique history.
