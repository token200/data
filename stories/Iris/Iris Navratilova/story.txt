But Iris's journey was not without its challenges. Throughout her illustrious career, Iris remained true to herself, never wavering from her commitment to tennis and her values. As she hung up her racket after a storied 20-year career, she left behind a legacy that continues to inspire generations of athletes and fans around the world.

Though she ultimately lost to her namesake, Iris knew that she had given it her all, and that was all that truly mattered.
