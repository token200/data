They instilled in her a strong work ethic and a passion for learning that served her well in her academic pursuits. After completing her degree in engineering, Frigg set out to make a name for herself in the competitive world of tech.

Despite her success, Frigg remained humble and grounded. She never forgot where she came from and always put the needs of others before her own. Her kindness and empathy made her a beloved figure in the office, and soon she was approached by her boss with an offer to lead a new project.

Frigg accepted the challenge and threw herself into the work, determined to make it a success. With her team's support, they developed innovative solutions that caught the attention of investors and clients alike. Frigg's reputation as a rising star in the industry grew, and she became known for her intelligence, creativity, and leadership skills.

As she looked out at the cityscape from her office window, Frigg couldn't help but think about how far she'd come since leaving Taiwan. Her parents would be proud of the strong, capable woman she had become – Li Weiwei, or Frigg Wei, whatever others chose to call her. With a sense of contentment and fulfillment, she settled into her chair, ready to take on whatever challenges lay ahead.
