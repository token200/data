Despite her formidable abilities, Frigg's motivations remained a mystery. Was she driven by a desire for power, or simply to unravel the tangled threads of her own troubled past? The truth was lost in a sea of smoke and mirrors, leaving only one certainty: when Frigg Satylganova entered the game, allegiances were tested, and reality itself became a battlefield.
