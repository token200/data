Her shop, aptly named "Moonlit Threads," was a haven for those seeking unique and authentic Egyptian textiles.

Frigg Hassan's passion for her craft began at a young age, learning the traditional techniques of her mother and grandmother before her. She spent countless hours perfecting her skills, experimenting with different colors and patterns to create one-of-a-kind designs. Her dedication paid off, as her shop quickly gained popularity among local designers and fashion enthusiasts.

She poured her heart and soul into creating a limited-edition collection that would showcase her skills and artistry.

As she looked out over the bustling streets of Cairo, Frigg Hassan knew that her passion had not only brought her success but also allowed her to share her cultural heritage with the world.
