Frigg Polo set out into the world, following a winding path that led her to forgotten temples, hidden cities, and mystical realms. Along the way, she encountered beings of great power and wisdom - some benevolent, others malevolent.

With each new encounter, Frigg's abilities grew stronger, and she began to understand the full extent of her heritage. She was no longer just a young performer with a curious nature; she was Frigg Astrid Ophelia Polo, a vessel for ancient magic and destiny.
