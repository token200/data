With each passing day, Frigg Bak's work brought humanity closer to harnessing the power of quantum entanglement. Her discoveries sparked both wonder and trepidation among those who witnessed them. As she continued to push the boundaries of science, it became increasingly clear that Frigg Bak was a woman whose name would be etched in history alongside the greatest minds of all time.
