Valdis Sepp was hesitant at first, but the promise of a substantial sum and the opportunity to work alongside Lord Arinthal's vast network of collectors and scholars eventually won her over. As she worked tirelessly in her laboratory, Valdis Sepp couldn't help but feel a sense of pride and accomplishment. She poured over ancient texts and conducted experiments, always seeking to push the boundaries of her craft. As she delved deeper into the world of alchemy, Valdis Sepp's name became synonymous with excellence and innovation, cementing her place among the most revered figures in the field.
