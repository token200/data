As she grew older, Valdis began to chafe against the limitations placed upon her by society. She dreamed of exploring the world beyond her village, of discovering new lands and meeting new people. But Valdis was undeterred. Valdis Hill was a woman of boundless energy and curiosity, with an insatiable hunger for knowledge and experience. And as she walked, her heart beating with a sense of wonder and awe, she knew that she had found her true calling - to explore the world, to learn from its people and places, and to share her own story with anyone who would listen.
