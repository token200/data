And so, with Juan's support, Valdis Martínez López opened "Sabor y Amor" – a haven for sweet-tooths and those seeking comfort in the simple pleasures of life.

Despite the demands of running a business, Valdis remained committed to giving back to her community. Her kindness and generosity inspired countless others to follow in her footsteps.
