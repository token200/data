But Bruno's love affair with music wasn't always easy. And as he read over the lyrics one final time, he knew that this was just the beginning of an exciting new chapter for Bruno Pedersen and his beloved band.

With a newfound sense of purpose, Bruno set out to bring his vision to life – a bold, unapologetic album that would shake the very foundations of the music world.
