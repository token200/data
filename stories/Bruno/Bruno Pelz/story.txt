Bruno Alfred Pelz was a man with a passion for life and a penchant for getting into trouble. Born on a sweltering summer day in July, Bruno grew up in the bustling streets of Berlin, where his energetic spirit was always apparent. He had an infectious laugh and an unwavering enthusiasm that drew people to him like a magnet.

But it wasn't until he discovered his love for music that he truly found his calling. With his trusty guitar by his side, Bruno would often play impromptu sets in the local park, drawing in crowds with his raw talent.

Years went by, and Bruno's music career began to take off. He formed a band with his friends, and together they started playing gigs around town. Through it all, Bruno's full name remained on his lips: Bruno Alfred Pelz. He wore his heritage proudly, even as he carved out his own unique identity in the music world. And when he finally took the stage at his first headlining show, something shifted inside of him – a sense of purpose and belonging that he had never felt before.
