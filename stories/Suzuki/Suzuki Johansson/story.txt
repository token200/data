As she made her way to the bar, ordering a whiskey on the rocks, Suzuki's mind began to spin with possibilities. What kind of information could be worth risking everything for? And who was behind this mysterious message?

Suzuki Johansson, known only by her handle "Zero Cool" within the collective, had always been drawn to the thrill of the unknown. With her razor-sharp intellect and lightning-fast reflexes, she had built a reputation as one of the best in the business.

The bartender, a gruff but kind-eyed man named Jack, nodded at her as she took her seat. "What's new, Zero?" he asked, pouring her a shot.
