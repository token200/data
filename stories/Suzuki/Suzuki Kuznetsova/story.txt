Maria was captivated by the enigmatic statement and felt compelled to track down Suzuki Kuznetsova. After weeks of searching, Maria finally received an invitation to meet the artist at her Tokyo studio. As she stepped into the space, Maria was struck by the sheer beauty of Suzuki Kuznetsova's work on display.

Each piece told a story of love, loss, and transformation – all woven together with an intricate thread of cultural identity. It was as if Suzuki Kuznetsova had distilled the essence of her own experiences into these works, creating a window into her inner world.
