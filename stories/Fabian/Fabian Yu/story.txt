Fabian Yu was a man with a passion for art and music. Growing up in a small town surrounded by rolling hills and vast farmlands, he had always felt a sense of isolation. But when his parents encouraged him to explore his creative side, Fabian found solace in the world of visual arts.

It was there that he met like-minded individuals who shared his passion for creativity and self-expression. Together, they formed a tight-knit group of artists who encouraged and supported each other's endeavors.

During his college years, Fabian's art gained recognition locally, and he started to receive commissions from private collectors. As Fabian graduated from college, he was filled with excitement and anticipation for the future.
