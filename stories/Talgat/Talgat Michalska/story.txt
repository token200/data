As Talgat Michalska, he had built a reputation as a master thief and con artist, able to talk his way out of (or into) any situation. But with Kasia by his side, he felt invincible – like nothing could touch them, not even the authorities.

"What's that, my dear?" Talgat replied, his eyes locked on hers.

Talgat Michalska grinned, feeling the familiar thrill of anticipation course through his veins.
