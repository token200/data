As the days passed, Talgat Boulanger found himself drawn back to the bakery again and again. He would sit at a small table by the window, watching as Mrs. Suleymanova expertly crafted each loaf of bread. And though he never stayed for long, his presence was always welcome.

Talgat Boulanger knew that his life had taken a few too many twists and turns.
