Talgat smiled back, his eyes twinkling with warmth. "Gracias, Sofia," he replied. "Perhaps one day, you'll join me on the road, and we can weave our own tales of wonder." And with that, Talgat Perez Sanchez disappeared into the fading light, leaving behind a trail of enchantment and possibility.
