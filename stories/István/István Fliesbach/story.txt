As he delved deeper into the mystery, István found himself entwined in a web of intrigue and deception. Despite the whispers, István Fliesbach remained resolute, driven by an insatiable curiosity that fueled his quest for knowledge. The flickering candlelight danced across his face, casting eerie shadows that seemed to writhe like living things.
