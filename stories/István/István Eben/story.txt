He nodded, his dark hair falling across his forehead in a messy swoop. As he sipped his coffee, the door swung open and a young artist, Lili, entered, clutching a sketchbook to her chest. Her eyes locked onto István's, and for an instant, they shared a connection that seemed almost otherworldly.

Lili began to sketch István Eben with bold strokes, capturing his angular features and intense gaze on the page. He watched her work, fascinated by the way her pencil danced across the paper as she coaxed secrets from the blank sheet. As the night wore on, their conversation meandered through topics both personal and philosophical, like threads woven into a rich tapestry.
