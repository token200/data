Sofia took a deep breath and mustered up the courage to approach him, her heart pounding in her chest.

István turned to face her, his eyes narrowing slightly as he sized her up. "Can I help you?" he asked gruffly. Sofia launched into her pitch, extolling the virtues of Sundberg & Sons and its groundbreaking new product line. István listened intently, his expression unreadable.

As they parted ways, Sofia couldn't shake off the feeling that she had only scratched the surface of István Sundberg's enigmatic world.
