As she approached the Cloth Hall, Magdalena Zielińska spotted a familiar face waiting for her on the steps. It was Janek, her long-time friend and fellow historian. They exchanged warm smiles, and Magdalena Zielińska felt a sense of ease wash over her.

"Magdalena Zielińska, I've been searching all over for you," Janek said, his eyes sparkling with excitement. "I just received word that the National Museum is interested in featuring one of your latest discoveries in an upcoming exhibition."

Magdalena's face lit up at the news, and she leaned in to hear more about the proposed exhibition. As they talked, a group of tourists wandered by, camera-clicking as they snapped photos of the historic buildings.
