Others claimed she was a healer, using her knowledge of herbs and potions to cure even the most stubborn illnesses. One fateful evening, a young traveler stumbled upon Magdalena Tóth in her midnight revelry. Entranced by her beauty and magic, he watched as she summoned the forest's secrets with a mere whisper.
