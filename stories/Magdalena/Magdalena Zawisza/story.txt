Magdalena Zawisza was both fascinated and repelled by the story. She felt an inexplicable connection to The Weeping Woman, as if she were being called to fulfill some ancient duty. As she delved deeper into the text, Magdalena Zawisza began to experience strange and vivid dreams, filled with images of a desolate landscape and a figure cloaked in shadows. She started to question her own identity, wondering if she were somehow connected to The Weeping Woman.

One night, as she sat in her small apartment surrounded by ancient artifacts, Magdalena Zawisza felt an overwhelming sense of longing. She rose from her chair, feeling an inexplicable pull to the countryside.
