Days turned into weeks as Kristaps worked tirelessly to gather evidence and build his case. His hard work paid off when he finally secured an exclusive interview with the politician, who ultimately resigned from office due to the scandal. Kristaps Ozolins' reputation as a top-notch journalist was cemented after this success. He went on to work for some of the biggest media outlets in the world, covering politics and power from Washington D.C. to Moscow.
