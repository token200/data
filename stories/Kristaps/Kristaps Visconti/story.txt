But it wasn't until he stumbled upon an ancient tome hidden deep within the family's library that Kristaps truly found his calling.

As he delved deeper into its pages, Kristaps began to unlock secrets of magic he never knew existed. The air around him seemed to shimmer with energy as he practiced the incantations, his eyes flashing with an inner light.

Word of Kristaps Visconti's abilities spread quickly through the magical community. Scholars and mages alike sought out his counsel, hoping to learn from the master himself. But few were willing to test their skills against his own.

"I believe you possess great potential," he said finally, his voice low and measured. "But it is not enough to simply wish for magic to happen. You must first understand the forces you seek to command."
