The night of the show arrived, and Lukas's studio apartment was transformed into a vibrant gallery space. Guests mingled and marveled at his artwork, asking Lukas Mäkinen about his inspiration and creative process. As he walked through the room, taking in the chatter and applause, Lukas felt a sense of pride and fulfillment.
