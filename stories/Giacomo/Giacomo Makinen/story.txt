Born to Finnish and Italian parents in a small town nestled between the rolling hills of Tuscany, Giacomo grew up surrounded by music and passion. His early years were marked by turmoil and heartache, but it was during these dark times that he found solace in the raw emotion of rock music.

As "The Velvet Vagabonds" gained momentum, Giacomo's raw energy and charismatic stage presence drew in fans from far and wide.
