Giacomo Salonen listened intently as Signora Bianchi recounted her sister's last days, her words painting a picture of a desperate search with no leads. He nodded thoughtfully, his eyes narrowing as he processed the information. "Don't worry, signora," he said, "I'll do everything in my power to find your sister."

Giacomo Salonen leaned in close, his ears tuning into every word as he expertly pieced together the fragments of evidence. And when he finally emerged from the café with Luca by his side, he held up a photograph of Signora Bianchi's sister, a triumphant smile spreading across his face.
