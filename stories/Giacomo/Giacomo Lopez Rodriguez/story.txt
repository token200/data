After graduating from Harvard Business School, Giacomo had built a business empire that spanned multiple continents and industries.

As he arrived at the event, he was greeted by a small group of fellow collectors and dealers, all eager to ogle the prized watercolor. Despite the speculation, Giacomo remained calm and collected, his expression betraying nothing. He knew that his reputation as a collector was built on years of hard work and dedication, not some fleeting identity crisis. As he left the mansion, Giacomo Lopez Rodriguez vanished into the night, leaving behind only whispers of his presence.
