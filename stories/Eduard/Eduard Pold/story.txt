Eduard Pold was a man of few words, but when he spoke, people listened. As a former spy and now a retired librarian, Eduard's life had taken many twists and turns. His piercing blue eyes seemed to hold secrets behind them, and his sharp jawline gave him an air of quiet confidence.

Eduard Pold had spent years working in the shadows, gathering intelligence and carrying out missions for a top-secret government agency. Eduard's eyes narrowed as he delved deeper into the book, and for the first time in years, he felt a sense of purpose.
