Kaido's fingers moved deftly across the canvas, as if guided by an unseen force. As Kaido's art gained recognition, he began to attract attention from galleries and collectors. But despite his newfound success, he found it difficult to share his passion with others. His fear of vulnerability held him back, making it hard for him to connect with those around him.

As they spent more time together, Kaido found himself opening up to Sophia in ways he never had with anyone before.
