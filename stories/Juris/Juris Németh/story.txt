Juris Németh's eyes widened as she read through the pages, her mind reeling with implications. Could it be that the clockwork was more than just a mere curiosity? Was it actually a key to unlocking hidden dimensions and realities?

With Dr. Vasco's help, Juris Németh set out to uncover the truth behind her great-grandfather's creation. Together, they poured over the journal, pieced together fragments of information, and began to unravel the mysteries of the ancient clockwork.
