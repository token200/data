With an uncanny ability to slip in and out of shadows unnoticed, she had pulled off some of the most daring heists in recent history. Her methods were always precise, leaving behind no threads to unravel her tracks.

One fateful night, Juris Pei-ling received a message from an unknown source, hinting at a lucrative opportunity that would change the course of her life forever. The note read: "Meet me at the old warehouse on 5th and Main. Come alone." Her curiosity piqued, Juris couldn't resist the allure of the unknown.

Juris took the package, her mind racing with possibilities. She knew that taking on this mission would put her at risk, but the promise of adventure and intrigue was too enticing to resist.
