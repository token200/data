His rugged good looks and quick wit made him a magnet for sources willing to spill secrets, but few knew the truth behind Juris's eyes.

Juris nodded curtly, already mentally mapping out his next move. He had a reputation for being fearless, and this case promised to be the most challenging yet.

As he left the bar that night, Juris couldn't shake the feeling that he was being watched. The streets seemed to narrow, the shadows deepening into darkness that swallowed him whole. But Juris Kazuo was no stranger to danger; he had faced down death countless times before.

The next few days were a blur of late-night interviews and clandestine meetings. Juris followed every lead, from abandoned warehouses to seedy underground clubs, always one step ahead of the cult's enforcers. The closer he got to the truth, the more he realized that nothing was as it seemed - not even his own past.

Juris Kazuo had always thought of himself as a man in control, but this case threatened to unravel everything he thought he knew about himself.
