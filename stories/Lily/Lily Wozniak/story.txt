She was proud of what she had accomplished, and knew that all her late nights and early mornings had been worth it.

When the app was finally launched, Lily Wozniak stood proudly beside Max, watching as users began to download and start using it. It was a moment of pure triumph, and Lily felt an overwhelming sense of satisfaction knowing that she had created something truly remarkable. With her name on the app, she knew that she would always be remembered as a pioneer in the tech world.

As the app's popularity grew, so did Lily Wozniak's reputation.
