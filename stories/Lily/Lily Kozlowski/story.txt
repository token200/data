Lily Kozlowski was a free-spirited artist with a passion for creating vibrant works of art that captured the essence of the world around her. Born and raised in a small coastal town, Lily grew up surrounded by the crashing waves and salty sea air that would later become inspiration for many of her pieces.

As a child, Lily spent hours exploring the tide pools and beaches, collecting seashells and pebbles that she would use to create intricate collages on her makeshift easel. Her love of art was encouraged by her parents, who nurtured her creativity and provided her with endless supplies of paints, canvases, and paper.

As Lily grew older, her talent became increasingly apparent, and she began to hone her skills at a local art school.
