As she strolled along the winding trail, she came across a clearing filled with vibrant wildflowers. She sat down on a nearby rock, took a deep breath, and began to sketch the breathtaking scene before her.

Lily's heart melted at the sight, and she gently set aside her pencils to offer the fawn some fresh berries from her backpack.
