But despite his introverted demeanor, Marcos had a rich inner life, filled with vivid dreams and a deep love of literature.

Little did they know, Marcos was quietly working on a novel of his own – a sweeping epic that explored themes of identity, community, and social justice.

Luna was enchanted by Marcos' quiet intensity, his razor-sharp mind, and his passion for storytelling. She encouraged him to share his writing with the world, and Marcos finally mustered the courage to show her his manuscript. Luna was blown away by the depth and complexity of Marcos Jian's words – she knew at once that he was a writer to watch.

As the months passed, Marcos and Luna grew closer, their bond strengthened by shared readings, debates, and laughter-filled nights spent stargazing. Together, they formed a writing group, where they explored themes of love, power, and social change. Marcos' novel began to take shape, fueled by his newfound partnership with Luna.

When the time came for Marcos Jian to share his work with the world, he was both thrilled and terrified. What if his manuscript wasn't good enough?
