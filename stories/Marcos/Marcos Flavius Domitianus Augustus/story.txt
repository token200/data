Without hesitation, Marcos stepped forward, offering them shelter and protection under his wing.

As he watched the travelers settle into their new home, Marcos felt a sense of purpose wash over him. He was Marcos Flavius Domitianus Augustus, a guardian of the innocent and a champion of justice. And as he looked out upon the vast expanse of the desert, he knew that this was just the beginning of his journey to greatness.

As he listened, Marcos felt his heart swell with empathy and compassion.
