Years went by, and Marcos's passion for art only grew stronger. His enthusiasm was contagious, and soon, people were not only admiring his art but also sharing their own stories and experiences.

They wanted to create a large-scale mural that would celebrate Tavira's cultural heritage and showcase the town's beauty to the world. Marcos was thrilled at the prospect and threw himself into the project, pouring his heart and soul into every brushstroke.
