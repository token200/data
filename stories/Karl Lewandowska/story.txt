His clients often found themselves lost in conversations about escapement mechanisms, gear ratios, and the art of watchmaking. Few people knew that beneath Karl's gruff exterior lay a romantic at heart.

She had been away for years, pursuing her own artistic endeavors in New York City. Now, with a young child of her own, she found herself drawn back to her childhood home.

As Sophia settled into the cozy attic apartment above Karl's workshop, he couldn't help but notice the weariness etched on her face.
