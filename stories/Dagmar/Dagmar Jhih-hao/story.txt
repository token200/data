Blackwood leaned forward, his eyes glinting with interest. "Actually, Dagmar Jhih-hao, I've been investigating a string of high-stakes heists, and your name keeps popping up. I think it's time we had a little chat about what you're really after."

As the night wore on, Dagmar found themselves drawn into a web of intrigue and deception, with Captain Blackwood at its center. But one thing was certain: Dagmar Jhih-hao would stop at nothing to uncover the truth - even if it meant putting everything on the line.
