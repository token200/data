As a child, Dagmar grew up in a small village in Siberia, where he was forced to drop out of school at the age of 15 to work on the family farm. But despite the hardships, Dagmar found solace in bodybuilding and weightlifting. He spent every spare moment training, pushing his body to its limits.

When Dagmar finally decided to pursue a career in professional wrestling, he left his hometown behind and made his way to Moscow. There, he began competing in underground matches, honing his skills and building a reputation as a formidable opponent.
