Despite her success, Dagmar's personal life was far from straightforward. She had a habit of collecting discarded items – broken trinkets, torn photographs, and faded postcards – which she stored away in a secret compartment in her tiny apartment.

As she delved deeper into Yui's world, Dagmar realized that there were forces beyond her control at play – corrupt politicians, greedy corporations, and systemic injustices that needed to be exposed. But as she dug deeper, she also discovered a sense of purpose she had never known before.
