Filippo Martinez Lopez was a man with a passion for photography that knew no bounds. As he grew older, Filippo's fascination with photography only deepened. He spent hours poring over books on art and history, studying the work of masters like Ansel Adams and Henri Cartier-Bresson. He set up his camera and began to shoot, capturing the intricate patterns on the loaf and the joy on the faces of the bakers as they worked.
