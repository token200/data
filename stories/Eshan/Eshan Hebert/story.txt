Eshan Hebert was a free-spirited artist with a passion for exploring the world's most breathtaking landscapes. Born and raised in New Orleans, Louisiana, she grew up surrounded by the vibrant colors and rhythms of jazz music, which would later influence her own unique art style.

As a child, Eshan spent hours sketching the ornate ironwork balconies of the French Quarter, dreaming of one day traveling to distant lands to capture their beauty on canvas. After completing art school in New York City, she set out on an adventure that took her to the rolling hills of Tuscany, the towering mountains of Patagonia, and the vast deserts of Morocco.

Wherever Eshan wandered, she carried a sketchbook and pencils with her, capturing the intricate details of every rock, tree, and sunset she encountered. Her art was a reflection of her boundless curiosity and love for the natural world.
