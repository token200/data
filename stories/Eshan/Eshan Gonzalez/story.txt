He was fascinated by the way light danced through the trees, casting dappled shadows on the forest floor. This early fascination with nature would eventually shape his art style, which blended traditional techniques with modern twists.

As Eshan entered adulthood, he began to focus more on painting, using bold brushstrokes and vivid colors to bring his vision to life. He studied fine art at college, honing his skills and developing a unique voice that was both expressive and emotive.
