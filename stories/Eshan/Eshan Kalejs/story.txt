Eshan Kalejs arrived at the facility under the cover of night, his eyes scanning the perimeter fence for any signs of security cameras or alarms. He found none, and with a quiet efficiency, he scaled the wall and dropped down onto the roof.

From there, he navigated the ventilation system, avoiding guards and making his way to the data drive storage room. As he inserted the drive into his backpack, he could feel the weight of Thompson's money in his pocket - $100,000 was a small price to pay for a job well done.

But as Eshan Kalejs made his escape, he was ambushed by a group of heavily armed men who had been sent to stop him. They were clearly not just any ordinary security team - their uniforms were marked with the insignia of a rival company that Eshan had heard rumors about before.

He fought back with all his might, taking down the attackers one by one. But as he emerged from the facility, victorious but battered, he knew that this job was far from over. He still had to get out of the country without being detected, and then deal with the fallout from Thompson's rival company.

Eshan Kalejs took a deep breath, his mind racing with plans and strategies. He was a man who thrived in the shadows, always staying one step ahead of his enemies.
