Eshan Koval's curiosity was piqued, and he set out to investigate further. He spent hours poring over police reports, conducting interviews with witnesses, and scouring the town for any clues that might lead him to Sarah Jenkins.

Eshan Koval's eyes widened as he pieced together the clues. He realized that Sarah Jenkins' disappearance was just the latest in a long line of eerie occurrences in Ravenswood.
