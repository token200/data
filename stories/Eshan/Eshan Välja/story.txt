As he grew older, Eshan became increasingly restless, yearning for adventure beyond the frozen wasteland of his homeland. One day, Eshan's wanderlust finally led him to the bustling city of Tbilisi, where he hoped to find work as a bodyguard or mercenary. However, it wasn't long before he found himself entangled in a web of intrigue and deception.

As Eshan delved deeper into the world of high-stakes politics and deception, he found himself drawn into a maelstrom of danger and betrayal. With his skills honed to perfection, Eshan fought bravely against overwhelming odds, using every trick in the book to outmaneuver his foes.

Despite being outnumbered at every turn, Eshan Välja refused to back down, employing his cunning and strategic thinking to outwit even the most seasoned opponents. His reputation as a fearless warrior spread far and wide, striking fear into the hearts of those who dared to cross him.
