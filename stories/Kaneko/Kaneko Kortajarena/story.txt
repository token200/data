As a child, she would sneak into the wings of traveling troupes, watching as the actors transformed into their characters with makeup and costuming. She was entranced by the magic of transformation, and soon found herself taking on her own roles in the troupe's performances.

One thing was certain: whenever Kaneko Kortajarena walked into a room, attention followed. Her eyes seemed to bore into those around her, as if searching for secrets or hidden truths. Her movements were fluid and sinuous, like a snake slithering through the grass.
