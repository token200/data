As she grew older, Kaneko's passion for art led her to pursue a career in forgery. However, it wasn't long before Kaneko's skills caught the attention of those with more sinister intentions.

Kaneko Hebert accepted the challenge, pouring every ounce of energy into crafting a masterpiece that would leave Marcus and everyone else speechless. She spent months researching, experimenting with different techniques and mediums until she finally emerged with a work of art that defied expectations.

As she accepted the check for her work, Kaneko couldn't help but think of her parents, who had always encouraged her to pursue her dreams. She smiled, knowing that they would have been proud of her accomplishments.
