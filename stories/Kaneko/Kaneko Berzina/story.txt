Few knew that beneath her reserved exterior lay a soul who wrestled with the very same doubts and fears she sought to capture on canvas. Her friends spoke of a dry wit and a sharp intellect, but even they couldn't fully grasp the labyrinthine mind of Kaneko Berzina.
