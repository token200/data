As she delved deeper into the story, Nadia found herself in increasing danger. Her phone was tapped, her emails were intercepted, and she received threatening messages. But she refused to back down, knowing that her work had the power to bring about change.

Finally, after months of tireless investigation, Nadia Abdykadyrova broke the story, exposing the corruption and bringing the perpetrators to justice. The reaction was swift and severe, with many of those involved facing arrest and prosecution.

Nadia's bravery and tenacity had paid off, but at a great personal cost. She knew that she would have to leave Ashgabat, to protect herself and her family from retaliation.
