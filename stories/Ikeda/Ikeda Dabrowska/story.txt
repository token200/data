The catch: she would have to steal a priceless artifact from the very heart of Krakow Castle.

Ikeda Dabrowska accepted the challenge without hesitation. Under the cover of darkness, she slipped into the castle's labyrinthine corridors, avoiding guards and traps with ease. Her fingers moved with precision, working out the intricate locks on the display case where the artifact lay waiting.

The Patron smiled, knowing that he had secured one of the greatest heists of all time. And Ikeda Dabrowska?
