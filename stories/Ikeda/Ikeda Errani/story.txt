One fateful evening, Ikeda Errani received a cryptic message from an unknown sender, inviting her to meet at the old windmill on the outskirts of town. The note read: "Come alone, and be prepared to face what you left behind."

Ikeda Errani's curiosity was piqued, and she couldn't resist the opportunity to uncover the truth about her troubled past. She made her way to the windmill, her eyes scanning the deserted streets for any signs of danger.

"Who are you?" Ikeda Errani demanded, her hand instinctively reaching for the dagger at her waist.

Ikeda Errani's eyes narrowed, unsure of what to make of this revelation. The old woman began to speak, weaving a tale of Ikeda Errani's early life as a member of a notorious gang, her skills honed by the ruthless leader who had taken her under his wing.

As the night wore on, Ikeda Errani found herself drawn into the old woman's world, forced to confront the traumas and betrayals that she had left behind. The truth about her past began to unravel, thread by thread, until Ikeda Errani was left standing, gasping for breath, as if the very air had been squeezed from her lungs.

When the sun rose over Veritas City, Ikeda Errani knew that she would never be the same again.
