As rumors spread throughout the empire, people began to seek Ikeda's counsel, hoping to find answers to their most pressing questions. Empires rose and fell around him like dust devils, but Ikeda remained steadfast, a beacon illuminating the darkness. In quiet moments, he would sit beneath star-studded skies, reciting aphorisms inscribed upon ancient stones, imbuing those who listened with the weight of eternity.

When asked by an inquisitive youth what lay beyond the horizon, Ikeda's eyes shone like the stars themselves as he whispered: "The threads that weave reality are thin and fragile. We must tend them with care lest they snap, unraveling all we know."
