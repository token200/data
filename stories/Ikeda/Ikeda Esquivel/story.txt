Ikeda's real name was still a closely guarded secret, known only to a handful of individuals who had managed to gain his trust over the years. The catch: the meeting would take place in a remote location, accessible only by a treacherous mountain path.

Ikeda accepted the challenge, eager to prove himself once again as the greatest con artist of his generation. As he made his way up the winding road, he couldn't help but feel a sense of excitement and trepidation. What was this businessman hiding? And what lay in store for Ikeda Esquivel?

As he arrived at the designated meeting spot, Ikeda's instincts told him that something wasn't quite right. The air was thick with tension, and he could sense the weight of secrets being kept behind closed doors. With his quick wit and razor-sharp mind, Ikeda navigated the complex web of deceit and emerged victorious, claiming the artifact as his own.
