The only clue was a cryptic message left at the scene: "You will never find me."

Ikeda Kadyrova arrived at the crime scene, taking note of every detail. She began to survey the area, searching for any potential leads. Her eyes landed on a small security camera in the corner of the room, which she promptly activated to review the footage.

Ikeda Kadyrova's mind began to spin with possibilities. She knew that this case would require patience, persistence, and creative thinking. Using her vast experience and intuition, she pieced together a timeline of events leading up to Yui's murder.

After several days of tireless investigation, Ikeda Kadyrova finally uncovered the identity of the killer: a former business partner of Yui's who had been seeking revenge for perceived injustices. The cryptic message was, in fact, a red herring, meant to throw investigators off the scent.

As Ikeda Kadyrova confronted the killer, she couldn't help but feel a sense of satisfaction and vindication. Her reputation as a detective had been solidified once again, and justice would be served.
