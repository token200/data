As Ikeda Sundberg listened intently to Emma's concerns, her expression softened, revealing a glimmer of warmth beneath her reserved demeanor. "This is for you to keep," Ikeda Sundberg said, as if entrusting it with a precious secret. "Remember, science is not just about numbers and formulas – it's about understanding the mysteries of existence itself."

Emma left Ikeda Sundberg's laboratory that night with a newfound sense of wonder and awe, her mind buzzing with the implications of their conversation. She returned to her own research with renewed purpose, driven by the unyielding curiosity that Ikeda Sundberg had awakened within her.
