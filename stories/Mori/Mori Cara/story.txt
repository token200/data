Mori Cara had walked this path for centuries, her name etched into the annals of Aethoria's history as a champion of justice and balance. As night began to fall, Mori Cara arrived at the Heartwood Glade, where the ancient tree of Aethoria stood tall. The whispers grew louder, a chorus of hope and guidance that only Mori Cara could hear.
