Years passed, and Mori's name became synonymous with discovery and adventure. Her legend grew as tales of her bravery and determination spread throughout the land. And though some said she was haunted by unseen forces, others whispered that Mori Dąbrowska was, in fact, a guardian of sorts - watching over the secrets of Poland's past.
