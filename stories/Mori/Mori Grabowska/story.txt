As a child, Mori would spend hours watching her grandmother weave tales through hand-carved wooden puppets. Her fingers moving with precision, she learned the delicate art of manipulating the puppets' limbs to convey emotions and tell stories.

Years passed, and Mori's passion only grew stronger. She began performing at local festivals and markets, mesmerizing audiences with her ability to evoke entire worlds from the simplest of shadows.
