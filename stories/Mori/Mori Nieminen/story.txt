She spent most of her days tending to the land, coaxing life from the barren soil and nurturing the few plants that managed to survive in such harsh conditions. As Aki listened, she felt a deep connection to Mori Nieminen, a sense that they shared something more than just a love of nature. It was as if they were two sides of the same coin, each one completing the other.
