Victoria Laine stepped out of the crowded airport and into the crisp morning air. Her long, dark hair was tied back in a ponytail, revealing a smattering of freckles across her cheeks. The city's vibrant energy and rich musical heritage were just what she needed to spark some creativity. She was particularly excited to perform at the festival's main stage, where her name would be emblazoned on a giant screen for all to see.

From humble beginnings as a small-town singer, Victoria Laine had worked tirelessly to hone her craft and build a loyal following.
