Victoria Krumins stepped out of her small apartment and into the bustling streets of New Haven. As a successful event planner, she was no stranger to chaos, but today was going to be a particularly big day. She was overseeing the setup for a charity gala that would raise funds for a local children's hospital.

Her years of experience had honed her skills to perfection, and tonight was going to be one for the books.

She was a force to be reckoned with when it came to event planning - everyone knew it, from her most trusted assistants to the caterers and musicians who worked on her events.
