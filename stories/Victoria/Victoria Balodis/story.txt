Victoria Balodis was a writer, struggling to find her voice in the crowded literary scene, but finding solace in the quiet moments like this. She ordered a large cappuccino and took a seat by the window, watching as people rushed to and fro on the sidewalk outside.

Victoria Balodis was captivated by their tales of love, loss, and resilience, feeling a deep sense of connection to these strangers.
