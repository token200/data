As Aitana Handshuhs entered adulthood, her talents became increasingly evident to those around her. Aitana Handshuhs' artistic journey was not without its challenges, however. She had always been drawn to darker themes in her art – death, loss, and the human condition – which sometimes made her work feel heavy-handed or even disturbing to those unfamiliar with it. Yet, for Aitana Handshuhs, these subjects were an integral part of her creative vision.

As Aitana Handshuhs basked in the glory of her newfound success, she never lost sight of her artistic core. She continued to paint with reckless abandon, fueled by a fierce passion that had driven her from the very beginning.
