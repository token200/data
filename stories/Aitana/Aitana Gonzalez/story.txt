Aitana's determination and dogged refusal to back down had already earned her the respect of her colleagues, who saw in her a kindred spirit and fellow seeker of truth. With her quick wit and sharp mind, she effortlessly probed him for information, leaving no stone unturned as she pieced together the puzzle of deceit and corruption.

And so, with each passing moment, Aitana's reputation grew. Her name became synonymous with courage and integrity, inspiring others to follow in her footsteps.
