Aitana's artistic expression soon expanded beyond dance. She discovered a talent for painting, using vibrant colors to capture the essence of her homeland. Her art became an integral part of her identity, reflecting the complexities and nuances of Oaxacan culture.

Years passed, and Aitana Ming-che's artistic talents gained recognition. Critics praised her unique style, which blended traditional elements with modern flair. She showcased her work in galleries across the country, sharing her story with a wider audience.

Despite the challenges, Aitana remained true to her roots. She continued to dance, paint, and share the stories of her ancestors with the world. Her art became a testament to the resilience of her people, celebrating their struggles and triumphs.
