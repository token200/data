The team spent months tracking down the elusive community, braving treacherous terrain and unpredictable weather conditions. Aitana DiCaprio was determined to get as close to the tribe as possible, often finding herself at the forefront of the expedition.

The tribe, in turn, opened up to her, sharing their stories and traditions.
