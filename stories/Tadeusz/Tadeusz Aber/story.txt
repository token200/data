Without hesitation, Sophia followed Aber into the dark recesses of the bookstore, where the shadows seemed to swallow her whole. As Sophia watched in awe, Aber's eyes shone with a light that bordered on madness – a light that spoke of a thousand midnights spent exploring the unknown.
