Tadeusz laughed at such superstitions as he made his way through the winding alleys, finally arriving at the target of his latest scheme - a heavily guarded mansion belonging to the ruthless Countess Szczepańska. With a mischievous glint in his eye, Tadeusz Casanova set to work.

Within minutes, he had bypassed the security measures and slipped into the mansion undetected. The Countess's treasure trove of jewels and gold was within his grasp, but Tadeusz knew that getting out would be the greatest challenge.
