Tadeusz's big break came when he was commissioned by a local gallery to create a mural on the side of a historic building. The project gave him the freedom to express himself in ways he never thought possible. As he worked tirelessly to complete the piece, Tadeusz became completely immersed in his art, losing track of time and forgetting about everything else.

It was a huge opportunity for him – one that could take his career to new heights. But as he pondered the decision, Tadeusz couldn't help but think back to where it all began: in the streets of Vilnius, with his sketchbook and paints.
