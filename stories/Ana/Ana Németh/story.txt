As Ana Németh carefully brushed away the dirt from a particularly intricate stone carving, she felt a shiver run down her spine. Over the next few days, Ana Németh meticulously transcribed and documented every detail from the temple's walls, uncovering secrets that had lain hidden for centuries. She poured over ancient texts and consulted with colleagues back at the university, piecing together a rich tapestry of knowledge about this previously unknown civilization.

Intrigued, Ana Németh decided to conduct her own experiment, using local astronomical data to predict the alignment of celestial bodies during that time.
