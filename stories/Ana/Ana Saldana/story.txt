After completing her studies at the Juilliard School in New York City, Ana set out to make a name for herself in the entertainment industry.

She also lent her voice to the animated film "Rio," playing the sassy and charming Blu.

Throughout her journey, Ana remained humble and grounded, crediting her Puerto Rican heritage for instilling in her a strong sense of pride and identity. Her parents, Enrique and Ascenio Saldana, had always been her biggest supporters, encouraging her to pursue her dreams.
