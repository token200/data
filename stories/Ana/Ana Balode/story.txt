As she made her way deeper into the building, she heard the sound of footsteps echoing through the corridors.

Viktor's expression turned icy, but Ana could see the faintest glimmer of curiosity in his eyes. She knew she had him right where she wanted him. The game was afoot, and Ana Balode was ready to play.

But she also sensed that there was more to this story than met the eye. There were whispers of a deeper conspiracy, one that threatened to bring down not just Viktor Kuznetsov, but the entire city.
