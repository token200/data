Ana Viljanen stepped off the train and onto the platform, her eyes scanning the bustling station. She had just arrived in Helsinki from a long trip abroad, and was looking forward to spending some quality time with her family.

As she made her way through the crowds, Ana couldn't help but feel a sense of relief wash over her. Being on the road for months had been exhausting, both physically and emotionally. She was grateful to be home, surrounded by the people and places she loved.

From hiking through New Zealand to exploring the vibrant streets of Tokyo, she had seen so many incredible sights and met so many fascinating people. But as she drifted off to sleep, she knew that there was no place like home - surrounded by the people and love of her family.
