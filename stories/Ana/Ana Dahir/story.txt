Ana Dahir stepped off the train and onto the worn platform, her eyes scanning the bustling streets of Marrakech. Ana Dahir's research focused on the intricacies of Berber traditions, and she had spent months tracking down local tribespeople to learn more about their customs and ways of life. Now, she was finally here, ready to immerse herself in the sights, sounds, and smells of the ancient city.
