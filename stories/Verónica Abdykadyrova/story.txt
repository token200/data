As she delved deeper into Aisha's life, Verónico began to feel a strange connection to the woman. Both were outsiders in their own ways - Verónica, as a foreigner living in Tajikistan; Aisha, as a member of the harem of a powerful ruler.

Verónica's research had taken her to remote villages and dusty archives, but it was only when she stumbled upon an old photograph of Aisha that she felt a shiver run down her spine. The woman's piercing green eyes seemed to stare directly into Verónico's soul.
