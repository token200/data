Kārlis Fuentes stepped out of the dimly lit alleyway and onto the bustling streets of San Juan. The sounds of salsa music and lively chatter filled the air as he made his way to his favorite café. Kārlis Fuentes felt a pang of longing for recognition, but he pushed it aside, focusing instead on the vibrant colors of the scene before him.
