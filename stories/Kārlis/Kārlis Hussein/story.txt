Kārlis Hussein, a 30-year-old Latvian artist, stood in front of his latest masterpiece, a vibrant painting that captured the essence of his homeland's turbulent history. As Kārlis honed his craft, he began to explore the complex history of Latvia's struggle for independence. Kārlis's fascination with his heritage eventually led him to create a series of paintings inspired by Latvian folklore and mythology.
