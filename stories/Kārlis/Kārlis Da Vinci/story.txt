One fateful day, Kārlis received an unexpected visit from a local nobleman named Baron Hanssen. The Baron, fascinated by Kārlis's artwork, asked for his assistance in creating a grand fresco for the palace. Kārlis, intrigued by the prospect of showcasing his art on such a grand scale, agreed to take on the challenge.

Upon completion, the fresco became a sensation in Riga, drawing visitors from far and wide. People from all walks of life would gather to marvel at its beauty and symbolism. Kārlis's art had indeed captured the essence of humanity, revealing our connection to the world and each other.

As time passed, Kārlis continued to create art that inspired and educated those who witnessed it. His legacy lived on, a testament to his innovative spirit and unwavering dedication to exploring the mysteries of existence.
