As a journalist, Renata Koval had a keen eye for detail and an insatiable curiosity about the world around her. She spent most of her days digging through records, conducting interviews, and pouring over research, all in pursuit of the perfect story.

The barista, a friendly woman with a warm smile, greeted Renata with a nod. "Hey, Renata! Your usual coffee?" she asked, as if reading her mind.

Renata chuckled and shook her head. "Just the usual, thanks." She slid onto a stool at the counter and surveyed the menu, her eyes scanning the options with practiced ease.
