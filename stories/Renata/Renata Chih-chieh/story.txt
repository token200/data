Renata Chih-chieh's passion for weaving was not just a hobby, but a way of life. As she grew older, Renata Chih-chieh dedicated herself to mastering the ancient art of weaving, pouring all her energy and creativity into every thread and stitch.

Lily was fascinated by the stories of Renata Chih-chieh's magical weavings and begged to learn from the master herself. Renata Chih-chieh took Lily under her wing, teaching her everything she knew about the art of weaving.
