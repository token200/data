This moment was hers alone – no obligations, no responsibilities – just a warm breeze rustling her dark hair and the gentle hum of the city's vibrant energy.

Selma González Fernández knew that this fleeting feeling would only last so long; there were always more challenges to face and triumphs to achieve. Yet, as she gazed out at the crowded streets, she smiled to herself, knowing that no matter what lay ahead, she was ready to take on the world – one spark of defiance at a time.
