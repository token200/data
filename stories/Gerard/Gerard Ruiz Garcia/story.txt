She began to sketch Gerard's portrait during one of his performances, capturing the essence of his soulful eyes and rugged charm. When the performance ended, Sophia approached Gerard and introduced herself, introducing him to her art.

Gerard Ruiz Garcia was taken aback by Sophia's talent and enthusiasm. The two quickly discovered a shared love for music, art, and life itself. They spent their days exploring Barcelona's hidden corners, attending concerts, and experimenting with new sounds and styles.
