Gerard Wei, or Li Wei as he was known in his native China, was a man with a passion for life and a thirst for adventure. Born and raised in the bustling streets of Shanghai, Ger grew up surrounded by the vibrant sounds and colors of the city. He spent most of his childhood exploring the narrow alleys and markets, always on the lookout for something new and exciting.

As he entered adulthood, Ger began to feel the call of the unknown. He saved up enough money to embark on a solo journey around the world, determined to see every corner of the globe before turning 30. With a one-way ticket in hand and a backpack full of supplies, Ger set off on an epic adventure that would take him from the Great Wall of China to the beaches of Brazil.
