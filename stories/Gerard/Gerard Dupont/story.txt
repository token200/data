His colleagues often joked that Gerard Dupont's obsession with Bigfoot was borderline fanatical, but to him, it was a matter of life and death.

Without hesitation, Gerard Dupont packed his bags and set off for the remote region, determined to get to the bottom of this enigmatic tale.
