Finley Andersson had been investigating a series of mysterious occurrences at an abandoned warehouse on the outskirts of town. Rumors swirled that it was a hotbed for paranormal activity, and Finley was determined to get to the bottom of it.

As she approached the warehouse, Finley could feel the hairs on the back of her neck standing on end. She had always been skeptical of ghost stories, but there was something about this place that sent shivers down her spine. With a deep breath, she pushed open the creaky door and stepped inside.

Finley's heart racing, she spun around, trying to locate the source of the sound. But there was no one there.
