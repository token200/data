She gasped in awe, her eyes shining with excitement. For a moment, she forgot to breathe, so caught up was she in the wonder of it all.

Finley Astrid Sundberg had done it. She had uncovered the secrets of the abandoned mine, and proved to herself that even the most impossible dreams could come true.
