As he walked down the sidewalk, he snapped away at the colorful murals that adorned the buildings. People passed by him, some admiring his work, others not noticing.

Miguel Bianchi's latest project was a series of photographs showcasing the urban landscape. He had been working on it for weeks, and he was determined to get it just right. As he walked, he took note of the way the light fell on the buildings, casting long shadows across the pavement.
