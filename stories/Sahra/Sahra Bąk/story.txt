She was thrilled at the opportunity to showcase her work to a wider audience and gain exposure to new influences. As she packed her bags for the journey, she couldn't help but feel a mix of excitement and nervousness.

She was determined to push the boundaries of her art even further, experimenting with innovative materials and techniques.
