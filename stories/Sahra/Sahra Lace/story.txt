Sahra Lace was a free-spirited artist known for her vibrant paintings and infectious laugh. One sunny afternoon, Sahra Lace stepped out of her studio and into the bustling streets of the city, searching for inspiration. She wandered aimlessly, taking in the sights and sounds of the crowded sidewalks. As she turned a corner, she stumbled upon a small park tucked away between two towering skyscrapers.
