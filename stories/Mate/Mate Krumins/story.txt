As he began to investigate, Mate Krumins' unorthodox methods quickly became apparent. Mate Krumins quickly paid a visit to the suspect's apartment and found evidence of the stolen gemstone hidden away in a fake rock. The game was afoot, and Mate Krumins was hot on the trail.
