It was here that he spent most of his days, lost in the world of his imagination. His latest project, "The Migration of Dreams," was taking shape – a series of paintings depicting fantastical creatures migrating across deserts, forests, and oceans.

The conversation with his muse was one-sided, but it helped him tap into the creative wellspring. Mateusz Piatek was ecstatic. His big break had finally arrived, and he threw himself into preparing for the exhibition. Weeks of non-stop creativity followed, as he poured all his energy into bringing "The Migration of Dreams" to life.
