Leia Eriksson, the enigmatic and fiery rock star, stood on the edge of the crowded stage, her eyes locked on the sea of expectant faces before her. The dimmed lights cast an otherworldly glow over the sold-out concert venue, as if the very darkness itself was alive and pulsing with energy.
