But Leia's strong will and natural leadership abilities had driven her to continue their fight for freedom.

As she stood over the defeated commander, Leia Valja felt a sense of pride and satisfaction that she had never known before.

But despite her triumphs, Leia Valja knew that the war was far from won. There were still many battles to be fought, many more lives to be lost. And so, with a resolute determination in her eyes, Leia Valja set out once again to rally her troops and lead them into the fray.
