Zhu Lopez Rodriguez and Maria Sanchez's collaboration was unstoppable. They traveled to festivals and clubs across the Caribbean, leaving audiences in awe with their energetic performances. Their signature blend of classic rhythms and modern flair drew in crowds from all walks of life.
