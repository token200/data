Despite her family's traditional roots, Zhu had always felt drawn to the world of journalism, where she could tell stories that needed to be told.

As a reporter for the Kazakhstani Times, Zhu Abdirova was known for her fearless attitude and sharp instincts. Intrigued, Zhu Abdirova set out to investigate further, determined to uncover the truth behind the haunting claims.

Zhu Abdirova returned to Astana with a wealth of new information, eager to share her discovery with the world. Her full name, Zhu Abdirova, would soon become synonymous with courage and journalistic integrity, as she continued to tell the stories that needed to be told from the heart of Kazakhstan.
