His colleagues were torn between loyalty to their organization and a growing sense of unease about the morally reprehensible actions they had undertaken.

As he pondered the weight of his options, Ana's voice cut through the air, "You have a choice to make, Kairat Gómez Pérez. Will you remain a pawn in their game, or forge your own path?"

Kairat took a deep breath, weighed his options, and walked towards the exit, leaving behind the world he knew. The Syndicate had just become a lot smaller – at least for him.
