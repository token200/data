It was here that he spent most of his days, lost in thought and creation. His assistants would occasionally venture into the studio to collect his latest works, but even they rarely saw Endo Piotrowska's face.

One day, a young art critic named Sophia stumbled upon Endo's work at an underground gallery. As she delved deeper into his oeuvre, Sophia became obsessed with understanding the mind behind the art. She poured over Endo's biography, searching for clues about his life and inspirations.
