One night, Endo Weissner took the stage at the prestigious Silvermist Theater, ready to dazzle the crowd with her latest creation: "The Infinite Loop." The setup was simple enough - a single performer, Endo Weissner herself, standing on a raised platform surrounded by mirrors and spotlights. But as she began to weave her magic, it became clear that this was no ordinary illusion.

The audience watched in rapt attention as she seemed to stretch on forever, her reflections multiplying and merging in dazzling patterns.

But as the performance reached its climax, it became clear that "The Infinite Loop" was more than just a clever trick - it was a portal to another dimension. Endo Weissner stepped through the final mirror, disappearing from view, leaving behind only a whisper of smoke and the echoes of her own laughter.
