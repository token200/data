Born into a family of nomadic herders, Endo was raised on the tales of his ancestors and the vast expanse of the Altai Mountains. Endo's most notable expedition began with rumors of a hidden oasis deep within the mountains. Undaunted by the treacherous conditions and daunting distances, Endo Kalmurzaev set out on his journey, driven by an insatiable curiosity.
