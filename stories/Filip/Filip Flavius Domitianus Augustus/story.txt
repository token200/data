Word of her miraculous feat spread far and wide, attracting attention from leaders across the land. Many sought her counsel, and Filip's wisdom earned her a reputation as one of the most respected diplomats in Azura. As she stood on the threshold of adulthood, Filip Flavius Domitianus Augustus knew that her journey was just beginning, and that the world would need all the compassion, empathy, and strength she possessed.
