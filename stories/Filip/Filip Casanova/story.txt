Years later, when Filip Casanova set out on his own, he established himself as one of the most respected detectives in the city. His skills were unmatched, and his reputation preceded him wherever he went.

Filip smiled warmly, his heart swelling with pride. "You're welcome, cara," he replied. "After all, that's what I do best: uncover the truth and bring justice to those who need it most."
