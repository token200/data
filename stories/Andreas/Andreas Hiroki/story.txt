It was here, in these quiet moments, that Andreas found solace from the weight of his discovery. He was no longer just a scholar, but a conduit for the past.

Andreas Hiroki's name became synonymous with bravery and dedication, as he led his team through the jungle and into the heart of Zenith. And as Andreas gazed upon the crumbling walls, he knew that this was just the beginning of a journey that would take him to the very limits of human understanding.
