This fascination only grew stronger as he grew older, leading him to pursue a degree in fine arts.

Critics praised Andreas Hall's ability to capture the essence of human emotion through his work.

Andreas Hall smiled humbly, feeling a sense of gratitude towards his mentor for believing in him from the start.
