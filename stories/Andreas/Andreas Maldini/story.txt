The triumph marked AC Milan's sixth European Cup title, and Andreas Maldini was hailed as a hero by the Rossoneri faithful.

As he hung up his boots following his final season with AC Milan in 2009, Andreas Maldini was showered with accolades and tributes from fans around the world.
