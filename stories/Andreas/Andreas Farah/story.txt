Andreas Farah stepped out into the crisp morning air, feeling invigorated as he gazed out over the city. His parents, though not artists themselves, had encouraged his passion from a young age, providing him with his first camera on his 10th birthday.

Andreas Farah had since honed his craft, studying at some of the top schools and working with some of the most influential photographers in the industry.
