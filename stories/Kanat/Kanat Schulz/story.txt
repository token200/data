Her obsession with the subject had led her to create a functioning time machine, which she had dubbed "Chrono."

She spent hours exploring the ancient ruins, marveling at the ingenuity and skill of the Egyptians who had built such magnificent structures. As she wandered through the crowds, she stumbled upon a young pharaoh named Khafre, who took an immediate liking to her.

Khafre was impressed by Kanat Schulz's knowledge of his people's history and culture, which he attributed to some sort of divine intervention. As the sun began to set, casting a golden glow over the pyramids, Kanat Schulz knew it was time to return to her own era. She bid farewell to Khafre and boarded Chrono, preparing for her journey back home.
