Kanat's success didn't go unnoticed for long; soon after the experiment concluded, he was awarded the coveted National Science Award for his outstanding contributions to the field of astrophysics. As Kanat accepted the award, he couldn't help but think back to his humble beginnings in rural Kazakhstan and the support of his parents who had nurtured his passion for discovery.
