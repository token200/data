His piercing blue eyes seemed to bore into those he met, as if sizing them up for potential threats. With each new discovery, Victor Piatek felt himself drawn deeper into the rabbit hole.

Yet, undaunted by the risks, Victor Piatek persisted, fueled by his unwavering commitment to uncovering the truth for Sarah.
