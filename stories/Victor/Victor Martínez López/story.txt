Despite his unassuming life, Victor had a secret passion - painting. Every night, after completing his chores, he would sneak away to his small studio hidden behind the barn. He set up his easel, mixed his paints, and lost himself in the world of color and light.

Victor's love for art was not just a hobby, but a necessity. As a child, he had been fascinated by the works of Frida Kahlo and Diego Rivera, who often visited their family's farm during the summer months.
