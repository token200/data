Michał Välja sat alone in his dimly lit apartment, sipping on a cup of cold tea as he stared out the window at the grey, rain-soaked streets below. It was a chill that went beyond the temperature, one that had been seeping into his bones for weeks.

Michał Välja knew that art was his solace, but even its comfort couldn't shield him from the doubts that crept into his mind at night. Would he be able to overcome this feeling of disconnection? Only time would tell.
