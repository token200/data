Born into a life of poverty, Michał learned early on to rely on his wit and cunning to survive. He honed his skills on the streets, mastering the art of deception and manipulation. As he grew older, he set his sights on bigger targets – high-stakes heists and elaborate cons that would make even the most seasoned thieves take notice.

But as Michał Hideki made his escape, he couldn't shake the feeling that he'd just set off a firestorm. Though Michał Hideki was known for his quick thinking, this time even he found himself stumped. He vanished into the night, leaving behind only a whisper of his existence – a single, cryptic message scrawled on a forgotten alleyway wall: "Michał Hideki, out."

As the days passed, rumors spread about the mysterious thief's next move. Would Michał Hideki strike again, or had he finally run out of tricks up his sleeve? Only time would tell, but one thing was certain – whenever Michał Hideki walked into a room, excitement and intrigue followed closely behind.
