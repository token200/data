Michał Wiśniewski's days were filled with nothing but the sound of raindrops on the pavement. Days turned into weeks, and Michał Wiśniewski's routine remained unchanged. Yet, with each passing day, the weight of his past began to lift. He found solace in the small acts of kindness he performed for strangers like the young woman. The sound of the raindrops on the pavement no longer brought him sadness, but instead a sense of peace.

One evening, as Michał Wiśniewski sat at the café, a group of children burst through the door, laughing and chattering excitedly. They spotted him and rushed over, eager to meet the "legendary war hero." For a brief moment, Michał Wiśniewski's expression softened, and he smiled, a small, wistful smile that spoke volumes about the man beneath.
