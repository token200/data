Marat Hiroki, known to his friends as Mari, had always been drawn to the world of underground music. As a teenager, he spent countless hours in dingy clubs and bars, soaking up the energy of the scene. Now, as a seasoned journalist, he was determined to shed light on the dark forces that seemed to be operating beneath the surface.

For hours, Marat wandered the streets, gathering clues and piecing together the puzzle. Finally, as the sun began to set, he stumbled upon an abandoned warehouse on the outskirts of town.
