People were drawn to it, but also intimidated by it. It was as if they could see the turmoil brewing beneath his surface, and yet, he remained aloof.

Krzysztof's closest friends – a handful of fellow artists and writers who had discovered him in the early days – would whisper to each other about his hidden vulnerability.

As they talked, she found herself drawn into his world, one that was both fascinating and terrifying. Over the months that followed, Sofia watched as Krzysztof's work began to take on a new depth, as if he was pouring his very soul onto the canvas.
