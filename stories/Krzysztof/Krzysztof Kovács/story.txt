Magda's guidance reignited something within Krzysztof. He began to see the world anew – through the eyes of a child, unencumbered by the constraints of adulthood. His paintings took on a new depth, as if infused with the magic of Magda's own artistry.

As news of Krzysztof Kovács' resurgence spread, galleries clamored for his work, and collectors offered hefty sums for his pieces. Yet, he remained committed to his newfound creative freedom, refusing to compromise on his artistic vision.
