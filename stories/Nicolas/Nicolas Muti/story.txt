It was said that Muti's talents began at a young age, honed in the shadows of Paris's underworld. With a talent for picking locks and an uncanny ability to blend into the crowd, he quickly gained notoriety among those who operated on the fringes of society.

One evening, Muti received a message from an old acquaintance, inviting him to attend a high-society gala at the city's most esteemed museum. The invitation was simple: "Bring something priceless." Intrigued, Muti accepted the challenge.

As he arrived at the mansion, dressed in impeccable attire, Muti began his work. His fingers danced across the display cases, expertly bypassing security measures and leaving a trail of subtle clues in his wake. The night wore on, with guests mingling and laughing as if oblivious to the silent thief among them.
