As he grew older, Nicolas's gift became more apparent. He could make even the most reluctant seedling sprout and thrive with a mere touch of his hand. People from all over the village would come to him for help, seeking advice on how to nurture their gardens and crops. Nicolas was happy to oblige, using his connection to the plant world to guide them.

One day, a group of scientists arrived in the village, searching for someone with Nicolas's extraordinary ability. They were part of a secret organization dedicated to studying the mysteries of nature, and they believed that Nicolas's gift could hold the key to unlocking new technologies and discoveries.

The scientists were impressed by Nicolas's abilities, but they also had a hidden agenda. Nicolas was horrified, realizing that he had been manipulated into helping them without even realizing it.

Despite this betrayal, Nicolas refused to give up on his gift. He continued to use his powers to help those in need, but now with a newfound sense of caution and awareness. He knew that his abilities were a double-edged sword, capable of bringing great good or great harm.

As he worked in the garden, Nicholas Dąbrowska felt the energy of the plants surging through him, connecting him to the natural world. He listened to their whispers, seeking guidance on how to balance his gift with the demands of the modern world. With this newfound understanding, Nicolas vowed to use his powers for the greater good, no matter the cost.

Years went by, and Nicolas's reputation as a guardian of the plant kingdom spread far and wide. People from all walks of life came to seek his advice, and he was always happy to help. Though his journey had been marked by challenges and betrayals, Nicolas Dąbrowska remained steadfast in his commitment to using his gift to protect and nurture the natural world.
