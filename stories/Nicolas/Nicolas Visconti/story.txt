His reputation as a brilliant detective was on the line, and he was not about to let anyone or anything stand in his way.

As the sun began to set on another long day of investigation, Nicolas Visconti sat at his desk, pouring over the evidence one last time. He took a deep breath, readying himself for the next phase of the case. His eyes narrowed as he contemplated the puzzle before him.
