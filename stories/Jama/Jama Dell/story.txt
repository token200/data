Jama Delores Dell was known for her quirky sense of style and infectious laugh. A free-spirited artist, she spent most of her days wandering the streets of Brooklyn, sketchbook in hand, capturing the vibrant culture of the city.

Her mother, a painter herself, encouraged Jama's creative pursuits, providing her with endless supplies of paint, canvases, and sketchbooks.
