Jama's initial spark reignited at the prospect of discovery. She quickly composed herself and began discussing the details with Dr. Patel. For the first time in months, she felt like her mind was engaging with something other than the weight of loneliness that had been bearing down on her.

In this moment, Jama Németh realized that even as she struggled to fill the void within herself, the pursuit of knowledge had become a means of connection to something greater than human understanding.
