But Jama's success didn't go unnoticed by the media, and soon he found himself thrust into the spotlight as one of the league's most promising young players. Despite the attention, Jama remained humble and grounded, crediting his family and coaches for helping him reach the top of his game.

His teammates looked up to him as a leader, and opponents dreaded facing him in the opposing team's box. But Jama never let the pressure get to him; he was always focused on the next match, always looking for ways to improve his game.

The victory was a turning point for the team and cemented Jama's status as a league star. As he lifted the trophy aloft alongside his teammates, the media dubbed him "Jamaal 'Jama' Hébert: The Soccer Sensation." Though Jama blushed at the praise, deep down he knew it was all worth it – he had given everything to achieve success and bring pride to his team.
