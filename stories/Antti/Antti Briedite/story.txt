Antti Briedite was a man shrouded in mystery. Few knew much about his past, but those who did whispered tales of his exceptional skills as a master spy and assassin.

Born into a family of modest means, Antti's early life was marked by hardship and struggle. However, it wasn't long before he discovered his true calling - using his intelligence and athleticism to survive on the streets of 1920s Berlin.

As he honed his craft, Antti caught the attention of a powerful organization known only as "The Syndicate." Impressed by his prowess, they offered him a place among their ranks. And so, Antti Briedite became an agent, using his unique talents to gather intelligence and carry out covert missions.

Years went by, and Antti's reputation grew. He became a ghost, feared and respected by those who knew of his existence. His targets were always high-value, and he never failed to deliver. The Syndicate rewarded him handsomely for his services, but Antti's true loyalties remained unclear.

Antti's curiosity was piqued, and he agreed to meet with her at an abandoned warehouse on the outskirts of town.

As he arrived, Antti spotted Sophia waiting for him in the shadows. Her words were cryptic, but they hinted at a deeper conspiracy within The Syndicate itself. Antti's mind racing, he knew that this could be the break he had been waiting for - a chance to expose the truth and finally reveal his true loyalties.
