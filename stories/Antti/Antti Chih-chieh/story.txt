His movements were honed from years of solitary training under the watchful gaze of the Northern Mountain Sage, a legendary figure said to possess the essence of the elements.

Antti's prowess in hand-to-hand combat was matched only by his mastery of the celestial arts – an ancient form of magic passed down through generations of his family. As he navigated treacherous paths and dodged deadly traps set by rival clans, Antti Chih-chieh seemed to anticipate each danger with uncanny intuition.
