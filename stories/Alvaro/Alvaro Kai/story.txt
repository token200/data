Alvaro Kai stood atop the windswept cliff, his long black hair whipping wildly in the gusts as he gazed out at the vast expanse of the Pacific Ocean. His parents, both native Hawaiians, had taught him the ancient stories of the islands and the creatures that lived within their waters. From a young age, Alvaro had been drawn to the mysteries of the ocean, and he had dedicated his life to unlocking its secrets.
