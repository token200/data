Born to a Latvian mother and a Russian father, he had grown up surrounded by the rich cultural heritage of Eastern Europe. Sarah was a fellow hacker and artist, and the two of them had met online through a mutual acquaintance. As Ethan gazed out at the city below, he knew that Sarah would be the first person to see his latest piece, "Echoes in the Code."
