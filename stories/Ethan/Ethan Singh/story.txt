Ethan Singh was a young and ambitious journalist who had just landed his dream job at a prestigious newspaper in New York City. Born to Indian parents, Ethan had always been fascinated by the world of news and politics, and he was determined to make a name for himself in the industry.

As he sat at his desk, sipping his morning coffee and staring out at the bustling streets of Manhattan, Ethan felt a sense of excitement and purpose wash over him. He was 25 years old, with a sharp mind and a quick wit, and he was ready to take on the challenges of the newsroom.

Ethan's editor, Mrs. Johnson, walked into his office and handed him a folder filled with information on a recent scandal involving a high-profile politician. "This is your assignment for today," she said with a smile. "Get to the bottom of it and find out what really happened."

Ethan's eyes lit up as he took the folder from Mrs. Johnson's hands. He spent the rest of the day pouring over documents and conducting interviews, his mind racing with questions and theories.

As the sun began to set, Ethan sat back in his chair, feeling satisfied with the progress he had made. He knew that there was still a lot more work to be done, but he was confident that he could uncover the truth behind the scandal.

Just then, his phone rang. It was an anonymous source on the other end of the line, offering Ethan exclusive information about the politician's involvement in the scandal. Ethan's heart skipped a beat as he listened to the voice on the other end of the line. "This is big," he said, scribbling down notes furiously.

Ethan Singh had struck gold. He knew that this story could make or break his career, and he was determined to see it through to the end. With his quick wit and sharp instincts, he would uncover the truth behind the scandal and bring it to the attention of the world.
