Ethan María's sources began to dry up, and he found himself facing intimidation and harassment from those who wanted to silence him. But he refused to back down, using every trick in the book to keep his story alive.

Finally, after weeks of tireless work, Ethan had enough evidence to take to the authorities. He presented his findings with confidence, knowing that his story would rock the foundations of power and corruption.
