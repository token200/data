With her keen eye and creative spirit, Bakhyt Kabylbekova had a way of bringing people and places together through her lens.

One sunny afternoon, Bakhyt Kabylbekova received an email from a local museum inviting her to photograph their new exhibit on traditional Kazakh clothing.
