As the night wore on, Bakhyt's exhibition became a sensation, attracting attention from art critics, collectors, and enthusiasts alike. His full name was on everyone's lips, and he knew that this was just the beginning of his journey as an artist.

In the days that followed, Bakhyt Fournier continued to create, inspired by the recognition and support he had received for his work.
