Over steaming cups of coffee, Bakhyt delved deeper into Sofia's disappearance, piecing together the fragments of a town's collective memory. The more she learned, the more convinced she became that this was no ordinary mystery - but one with far-reaching implications for the entire community.

Bakhyt Stensson had always been driven by curiosity and a desire to uncover truth. Now, as she prepared to dive headfirst into the unknown, she couldn't shake off the feeling that Sofia's story held secrets not only about the past, but also about her own family's dark history - one that Bakhyt was determined to unravel.
