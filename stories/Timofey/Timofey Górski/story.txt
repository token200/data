But despite these challenges, Tomi thrived in his own way. He spent most of his days exploring the surrounding countryside, learning about its history and traditions.

As Tomi entered adulthood, he began to make a name for himself in Tyniec. With Kasia's support, Tomi began to slowly rebuild himself, learning to face his demons and find solace in the quiet moments. And though his journey was far from easy, he knew that with friends like Kasia by his side, Timofey Górski could overcome even the darkest of times.
