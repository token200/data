However, Timofey's true love was classical guitar, which he played with such fire and conviction that it seemed to match the rhythm of his brushstrokes.

One evening, as Timofey Almodóvar was working on a particularly intricate piece, he heard a knock at the door. It was his young apprentice, Sofia, who had arrived unannounced with a request for help. She had gotten herself into a bind while trying to complete a commissioned painting and needed someone with Timofey's expertise.

With a sigh, Timofey put down his brushes and set to work on Sofia's predicament. As he guided her through the process, he couldn't help but notice how much she resembled him – same bright blue eyes, same mischievous grin, same love for all things artistic.

The hours flew by as Timofey worked tirelessly alongside Sofia, their conversation flowing easily from art to music to life itself.
