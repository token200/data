As Timofey's reputation grew, so did his client list. He landed high-profile contracts with top brands and magazines, shooting campaigns and editorials around the world. But no matter where his work took him, he always remained committed to capturing the beauty of the world around him.

Timofey was intrigued by the idea and immediately agreed to take on the project.
