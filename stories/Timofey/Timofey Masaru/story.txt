After completing his studies in economics, Timofey decided to leave Russia and pursue a career in the arts. Otsuka-san saw something in Timofey that reminded her of her late husband – a deep understanding of beauty and harmony. As he delved deeper into the world of classical music, Timofey discovered his true calling as a composer.
