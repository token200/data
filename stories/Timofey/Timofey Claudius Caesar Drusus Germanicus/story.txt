He worked tirelessly behind the scenes, forging alliances and negotiating treaties that secured Rome's borders and expanded its territories. His reputation grew, and soon he was being hailed as one of the greatest strategists in Roman history.

One fateful day, Timofey Claudius Caesar Drusus Germanicus received a summons from Emperor Augustus himself. The old leader wanted to discuss a matter of great importance with his most trusted advisor. Timofey arrived at the imperial palace, his heart racing with anticipation.
