Whatever the truth may have been, one thing was certain: Luca Kalejs was a master of his craft.

Luca's instincts told him that this was no ordinary meeting, but he couldn't resist the thrill of the challenge.

There, he found his acquaintance waiting for him - a woman with piercing green eyes and raven-black hair. She introduced herself as Sophia, an art collector with a penchant for rare and valuable artifacts. "I have something that interests you, Luca Kalejs," she said, her voice low and husky. "Something that requires your... particular set of skills."

Luca's curiosity was piqued. What could Sophia possibly have in mind? He leaned forward, his eyes locked on hers, as Sophia revealed the mystery: a priceless artifact hidden somewhere within the mansion's walls. The game was afoot, and Luca Kalejs was ready to play.
