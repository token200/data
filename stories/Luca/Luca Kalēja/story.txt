Luca Kalēja's curiosity was piqued, and he immediately began working on the case. He spent hours pouring over the evidence, analyzing every detail, and searching for any inconsistencies. The evidence pointed towards an inside job, but Luca Kalēja wasn't convinced that this was the complete story. He knew that there were still many loose ends and he needed to dig deeper. With a determined look on his face, Luca Kalēja made a mental note to follow up on every lead and interview every person involved in the case.
