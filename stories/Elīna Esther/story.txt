Elīna's bravery and journalistic skills had earned her a reputation as a leading investigative journalist. Elīna's success had not gone unnoticed, however. The Daily Chronicle offered her a promotion to editor-in-chief, where she would oversee the newsroom and shape the direction of the paper. Elīna was hesitant at first, but ultimately accepted the offer.

She knew that this new role would bring new challenges, but also more opportunities for her to make a difference. With a sense of excitement and trepidation, Elīna Esther stepped into her new role as editor-in-chief, ready to take on whatever came next.
