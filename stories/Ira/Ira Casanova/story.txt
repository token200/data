The following night, Ira found herself strolling through the upscale district, searching for clues. Neon signs reflected off wet pavement as she navigated alleyways, always staying one step ahead of potential witnesses. The game of cat and mouse had begun, and Ira Casanova was determined to uncover the truth.

Ira Casanova smiled faintly, an unsettling hint of amusement dancing at the corner of her mouth.
