As the collectors made their way back to Moscow, Leon Mikhailov watched from afar, his eyes narrowing as he calculated the risks and rewards of his actions. He knew that his involvement in this matter would not go unnoticed for long, but he was willing to take that risk if it meant keeping the painting out of the wrong hands.
