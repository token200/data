Her eyes sparkled with a hint of curiosity, and Leon Mägi felt an inexplicable connection to her. He approached her cautiously, introducing himself as Leon Mägi – his full name, which always seemed to bring a smile to people's faces.

He tucked the note into his pocket, his heart racing with anticipation for their next meeting. As he walked back to his small apartment, Leon Mägi couldn't help but feel that his life had taken an unexpected turn.
