But it wasn't just her family that weighed heavily on her mind – she also thought of her own destiny.

As a woman in Roman society, her options were limited, and she longed to break free from the constraints of her family's legacy. It was a daring idea, one that could get them both into trouble, but also offered the promise of excitement, adventure, and freedom.
