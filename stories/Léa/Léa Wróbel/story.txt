Intrigued, Léa decided to dig deeper and arrange a meeting with Echo at an abandoned warehouse on the outskirts of town. As she approached the entrance, she noticed a group of suspicious-looking men lurking in the shadows. As she left the warehouse, Léa couldn't shake off the feeling that her encounter with Echo was more than just a chance meeting. She felt a deep connection to this enigmatic artist, as if they shared a secret language that only they understood.
