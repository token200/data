The excavation was a success, and Léa's name became synonymous with her groundbreaking research. Dr. Rodriguez took notice of Léa's talent and offered her a permanent position on her team. With this new role, Léa Astrid Stensson continued to push the boundaries of archaeological knowledge, inspiring a new generation of researchers and enthusiasts alike.
