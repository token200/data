Despite his dedication, Jonathan's personal life suffered greatly as a result. His few close friends had long since given up trying to reach him, fearing they would only be met with a distracted ear or a curt dismissal. His family worried about his well-being, but Jonathan couldn't bring himself to slow down now.

One evening, as he was working late on a particularly challenging project, the lights in his studio began to flicker ominously. The air conditioning unit rattled and groaned, threatening to give way at any moment. Suddenly, the entire building plunged into darkness, leaving Jonathan alone and vulnerable.

As he waited for a table, shivering in the chilly evening air, Jonathan couldn't help but feel a sense of disconnection from the world around him. His obsession had isolated him, leaving him without friends or family to rely on.
