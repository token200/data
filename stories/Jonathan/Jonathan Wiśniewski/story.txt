The name, inspired by his Polish heritage and grandmother's nickname, seemed to capture the essence of his artwork: a deep connection to tradition, yet a willingness to push boundaries. Each piece was a testament to his boundless imagination, as if the colors themselves were alive, dancing on the canvas.
