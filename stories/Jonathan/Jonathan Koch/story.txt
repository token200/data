Jonathan Koch, a former astrophysicist, stood at the edge of the Grand Canyon, gazing out at the vast expanse of rugged terrain. His eyes wandered back to his journal, scribbling notes and sketches that captured the beauty of this natural wonder. But as he delved deeper into his research, he found himself feeling increasingly disconnected from the world around him. The isolation was suffocating, and eventually, he made the decision to leave it all behind.

Now, Jonathan Koch spent his days lost in the pages of his own imagination.
