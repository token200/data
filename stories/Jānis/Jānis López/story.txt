Jānis López was an enigmatic figure in the small town of Santanyi, where the Mediterranean sun dipped into the sea and painted the sky with hues of gold and crimson. As a young boy, Jānis had always felt like an outsider, with his mixed heritage and curious nature setting him apart from the rest.

As Jānis López accepted the praise and accolades, his thoughts turned back to his childhood, when the world had been full of wonder and possibility.
