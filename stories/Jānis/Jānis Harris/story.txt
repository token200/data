Jānis slipped on his gloves, feeling the rough texture of the leather as he pulled them up. He was a man of few words, but the weight of his past hung heavy on every step, every move. His name had once been Harris, but that was before the accident, before the scars and the ghosts.

The stranger snorted, but this time, there was no venom to it. Just a deep sadness that spoke of secrets kept and lost.

As the night wore on, Jānis realized that this stranger was not the only one searching for answers – he was searching for himself, too. And in this moment, in the darkness of the alleyway, they found something in common – a shared burden to bear, and a path to follow into the unknown.
