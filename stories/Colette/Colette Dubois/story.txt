The music swirled around Colette as she searched the crowd for any sign of recognition or familiarity - but it was all strangers' faces, their eyes fixed on her with a mix of admiration and curiosity. She knew that look; it was one she'd seen before, in the mirror. It was the gaze of someone who saw right through to the heart of Colette Elara Dubois, the person she pretended to be, but hid from.
