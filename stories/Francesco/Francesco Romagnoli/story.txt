As he grew older, Francesco's love for art only deepened, and he began to study piano and painting in earnest.

As Francesco browsed the selection, Salvatore noticed him and approached. "Welcome, young man," he said, his voice warm and welcoming.

Over the next few weeks, Francesco returned to Salvatore's Music again and again, practicing piano and learning about music theory. Soon, word of Francesco Romagnoli's talents spread throughout Florence, and he was invited to play at local gatherings and events.
