After graduating, Maja spent several years traveling the world, soaking up inspiration from street art in Tokyo, sunsets over the Mediterranean, and the vibrant music scene in New Orleans. Despite her globe-trotting lifestyle, Maja had always been drawn to Portland's quirky charm. She fell in love with the city's eclectic neighborhoods, its passion for sustainability, and its thriving arts community.
