Her passion eventually led her to pursue a career as a historian and preservationist, specializing in the revitalization of old factories into vibrant cultural spaces.

The building had once been home to hundreds of workers who had toiled for decades to produce fabrics and garments. The old textile mill was transformed into a vibrant arts center, complete with galleries, workshops, and performance spaces.
