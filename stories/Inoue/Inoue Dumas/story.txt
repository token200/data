Her love affair with manga began there, and it was only natural that she would one day become a celebrated creator in her own right.

Inoue Dumas's fingers began to twitch, and before she knew it, she had grabbed a pencil and started sketching out rough drafts of characters and scenes. The more she drew, the more ideas began to flow through her mind like water from a faucet turned on full blast.
