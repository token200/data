Inoue Trevisan's path to becoming a legendary warrior was not an easy one, but his dedication and natural talent had earned him a place among the greatest swordsmen of his time.

The air was filled with the clash of steel on steel as he battled imaginary foes, his movements swift and precise. The crowd watched in awe as Inoue Trevisan's sword sliced through the air, leaving trails of sparks in its wake.
