As Inoue Rei, she was determined to make this series a hit.

The story, which followed the journey of a young woman as she navigated the complexities of love and identity, resonated deeply with readers. As Inoue Rei's fame grew, so did her influence. Despite her rising star status, Rei remained humble and dedicated to her craft. She continued to work tirelessly, always striving to improve and push herself creatively. As Inoue Rei, she was a shining example of the power of hard work, determination, and passion, inspiring countless fans around the world with her art and her story.
