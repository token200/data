As an adult, Dorota's love for taxidermy only grew stronger. She enrolled in a prestigious art school in Riga, where she honed her skills as a taxidermist under the guidance of her mentor, Professor Jānis Valters. As the years passed, Dorota Lācis became a household name in the art world, known for her innovative and thought-provoking taxidermy pieces.
