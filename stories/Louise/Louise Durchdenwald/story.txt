Louise Durchdenwald spent most of her free time experimenting with new recipes and flavors in her small bakery, "Sweet Delights." People would come to taste Louise Durchdenwald's creations, not just for the flavor, but also to catch a glimpse of her bright smile and sparkling eyes.

One sunny afternoon, the local farmer's market announced a baking competition, with the grand prize being a year's supply of fresh produce for the winner. Days turned into nights, and Louise Durchdenwald worked tirelessly to perfect her recipe. Louise Durchdenwald presented her masterpiece: a vibrant tart adorned with edible flowers and topped with a crumbly pastry crust infused with the sweetness of local honey.
