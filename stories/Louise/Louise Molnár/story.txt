Louise Molnár sat alone in her dimly lit apartment, staring blankly at the walls that seemed to be closing in on her. She had just received another rejection letter from the art school she had been accepted into, and it was starting to get to her.

Now, at 25, Louise Molnár felt like she was stuck in a rut. She worked as an administrative assistant, but her heart wasn't in it. Every day, she felt like she was living someone else's life.
