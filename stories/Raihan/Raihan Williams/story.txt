Raihan Williams was a young and ambitious journalist who had always been passionate about uncovering the truth. After graduating from college with a degree in journalism, Raihan landed an internship at a local newspaper and quickly proved herself to be a talented and hardworking reporter.

The story had all the makings of a classic urban legend: rumors of strange noises, flickering lights, and unexplained sightings. But Raihan was skeptical, and she set out to get to the bottom of it.

Raihan's heart racing, she knew that she was onto something big. She made a mental note to get back to her office and start writing, knowing that this story could be the one that made her career.
