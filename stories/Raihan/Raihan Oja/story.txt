Her dedication paid off when she was selected for the royal guard, where she quickly rose through the ranks due to her exceptional skills and courage.

One day, the kingdom was threatened by a powerful sorcerer who sought to overthrow the monarchy. The king, desperate for help, called upon Raihan Oja and her fellow guards to defend the realm against this new threat. Raihan, never one to shy away from a challenge, accepted the task with bravery and confidence.

As the battle raged on, Raihan's unique abilities proved invaluable. With her trusty Kusarigama by her side, she fought tirelessly alongside her comrades, taking down sorcerers left and right. Her agility, speed, and mastery of the ancient art allowed her to dodge even the most potent magical attacks.

Despite facing formidable foes, Raihan Oja remained steadfast and focused, drawing upon her training and years of experience to outmaneuver her opponents. She was a true warrior, feared by her enemies and respected by her allies.
