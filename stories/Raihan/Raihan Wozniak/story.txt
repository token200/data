With her trusty equipment in tow, Raihan Wozniak set out on a mission to uncover the truth behind the unexplained phenomena that plagued the rural communities she encountered. Her latest investigation took her to an abandoned asylum on the outskirts of town, rumored to be haunted by the vengeful spirits of former patients.

As she stepped into the musty darkness, Raihan Wozniak felt a shiver run down her spine. She had been warned that this place was not for the faint of heart, but she was determined to uncover its secrets. As her eyes adjusted to the darkness, Raihan Wozniak saw it: a figure standing in front of her, its presence both imposing and ethereal.
