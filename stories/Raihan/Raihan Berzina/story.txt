As the sun began to set outside, casting a golden glow over the city, Raihan finally stumbled upon a tantalizing clue - a small, leather-bound book hidden away in a secret compartment. The pages were yellowed and worn, but as she turned them, Raihan's eyes widened with wonder at the secrets they revealed.
