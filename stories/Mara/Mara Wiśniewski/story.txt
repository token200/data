Finally, after what seemed like hours of walking, Mara caught sight of a small door tucked away between two buildings. The door was unmarked and looked out of place among the ancient stone facades that surrounded it. Without hesitation, Mara pushed open the door and slipped inside.

Mara Wiśniewski felt herself being drawn into the music, her feet moving involuntarily to the rhythm. But she knew she couldn't let her guard down for even a moment. She had to keep her journalist's instincts sharp and her wits about her if she was going to uncover the truth behind this mysterious club.

As she made her way deeper into the room, Mara began to notice subtle hints of a larger story unfolding before her eyes.
