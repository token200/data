Mara Majewski arrived at the hotel just as the sun was setting, casting long shadows across the deserted parking lot. She grabbed her trusty equipment bag and set out to investigate, her heart racing with anticipation.

As she made her way to room 314, Mara Majewski couldn't help but feel a shiver run down her spine. Something about this case felt different – perhaps it was the eerie atmosphere that seemed to cling to every surface like a damp mist. In that moment, Mara Majewski felt an icy presence watching her – a cold, calculating gaze that seemed to bore into her very soul.

Taking a deep breath, Mara Majewski slowly reached for her flashlight, casting a weak beam of light around the room in an attempt to locate the source of the disturbance. Mara Majewski's heart skipped a beat as she stared into those piercing eyes. She knew in that moment that this case was far from over – and that she had just scratched the surface of something truly sinister.
