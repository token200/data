Mara Mercier sat alone in her small apartment, sipping on a cup of lukewarm coffee as she stared blankly at the walls. Mara Mercier thought back to when she first started her career in marketing, full of enthusiasm and determination. She had always been driven to succeed, but over the years, she felt like she'd lost touch with what truly made her happy.
