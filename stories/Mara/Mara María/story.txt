As she haggled with a vendor over the price of a handmade textile, Mara María's infectious laugh caught the attention of a handsome stranger.

He struck up a conversation, asking her about her inspiration and creative process. Mara María was delighted to share her passion with someone who seemed genuinely interested, and before long, they were lost in conversation for hours.

Leo was enchanted by Mara María's story and her love for her heritage. As they parted ways, he promised to visit her studio again soon, eager to see more of her art and meet the incredible woman behind it all - Mariana del Carmen María.
