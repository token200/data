Anton Ozola was a man of mystery and intrigue, with an air of quiet confidence that commanded attention without ever demanding it. Born into a family of modest means, Anton had always shown a keen intellect and a desire to learn, which served him well as he navigated the complex web of international diplomacy.

Anton Ozola was tasked with hosting the American delegate, and he took his role seriously. He welcomed the visitor with a warm smile and a firm handshake, putting him at ease almost immediately.
