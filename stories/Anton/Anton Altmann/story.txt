A professor at the University of Göttingen, he taught generations of students about the wonders of ancient civilizations, inspiring a new generation of scholars to follow in his footsteps.

Despite his success, however, Anton remained humble and kind, always willing to lend a helping hand to those who shared his passion for history. As the years went by, his reputation grew, but he never lost sight of what truly mattered: the pursuit of knowledge and understanding about our shared human heritage.
