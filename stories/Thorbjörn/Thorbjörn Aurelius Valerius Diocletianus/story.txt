Thorbjörn called upon the wisest minds in Azura, gathering scholars and healers to help him find a solution to the people's plight. The villagers' faces lit up with joy as Thorbjörn shared what little food they had, offering shelter and comfort in the darkest of times.

And so, Thorbjörn Aurelius Valerius Diocletianus ruled Azura not just as a warrior-king, but also as a compassionate leader who understood that strength lay not just in arms, but in the hearts of his people.
