The young woman, whose name was Lily, was impressed by Thorbjörn's expertise and work ethic. Thorbjörn Charron, it seemed, had a rich inner life. He spoke of his love for old motorcycles and classic rock music, and the way these things brought him solace in a world that often seemed too loud and chaotic.
