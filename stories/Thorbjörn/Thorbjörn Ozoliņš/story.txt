Despite his mysterious aura, Thorbjörn was kind-hearted and gentle, often putting the needs of others before his own.

As Thorbjörn grew older, he found himself drawn to the arts, enrolling in a local conservatory to study music and composition. The two quickly became inseparable, supporting each other's artistic endeavors and exploring the city's hidden corners together.

One fateful night, while performing at an underground jazz club, Thorbjörn Ozoliņš met a charismatic stranger named Kārlis. Kārlis was a traveling performer with a troupe of street musicians, and he swept Thorbjörn off his feet with tales of exotic lands and mystical traditions. Entranced by the stories, Thorbjörn knew in that moment that he had found his calling – to combine his love of music, art, and magic into a unique form of performance art.

Thus began Thorbjörn Ozoliņš's journey as a performer and artist, weaving together elements of myth and reality to create an immersive experience for his audiences. With Elga by his side, he embarked on a path that would take him from the stages of Riga to the farthest corners of Europe, leaving behind a trail of wonder and enchantment in his wake.
