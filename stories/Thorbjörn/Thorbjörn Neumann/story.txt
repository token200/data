Despite the risks, Thorbjörn Neumann's work gained him a devoted following. People were drawn to his unflinching gaze and his willingness to challenge the status quo. For the first time in his life, he felt truly seen – and truly heard.

Thorbjörn Neumann refused. He knew that by standing firm, he was taking a risk – but it was one he was willing to take. His family's name and wealth would always be tainted by association with his work; better that than sacrifice his integrity.
