Suspicious, Roman Jarv began to investigate, following a trail of subtle clues that led him deeper into the heart of the agency's inner workings.

With calculated precision, he infiltrated the official's inner circle, gathering evidence and waiting for the perfect moment to strike. As the agent lay in wait, his thoughts turned to his troubled past, and the events that had shaped him into the man he was today: Roman Jarv, former soldier and current asset of the agency.
