Her grandmother, a skilled craftswoman herself, had taught Blanca the value of hard work and attention to detail – skills that Blanca now used to great effect in her own career.

As Blanca's reputation grew, so did her client list. She was approached by local businesses and galleries alike, all clamoring for her unique touch on their projects. But despite the demands of her success, Blanca remained committed to using her platform to give back to her community. Blanca was both thrilled and intimidated by the opportunity, knowing that it would put her work in front of a global audience.
