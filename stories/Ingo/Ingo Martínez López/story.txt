With his trusty notebook and pen by his side, Ingo delved deeper into the mystery. He interviewed locals, scoured the asylum's grounds, and even conducted some unconventional experiments of his own. His colleagues often whispered that he was getting too close to the truth, but Ingo Martínez López was undeterred.

With the truth finally out, Ingo returned the actress to her family, shaken but grateful for his services. As he watched her drive away, he couldn't help but feel a sense of satisfaction.
