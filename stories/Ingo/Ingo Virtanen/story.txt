One of Ingo Virtanen's most notable projects was the Kallio Housing Estate in Helsinki, a sprawling complex of apartments and community spaces that blended seamlessly into the surrounding neighborhood. The estate was designed to be self-sufficient, with its own rooftop garden, solar panels, and rainwater harvesting system.
