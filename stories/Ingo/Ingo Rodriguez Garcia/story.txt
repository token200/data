The authorities were powerless against them, but Ingo was on the scene in an instant. Using his powers, he created a massive force field around the city, protecting its inhabitants from the impending destruction.

With his powers, Ingo also created a complex network of bridges and tunnels, allowing him to outmaneuver Dr. Anders and her team. The battle that ensued was intense, with reality itself seemingly warping and bending in response to Ingo's actions.

With his superhero identity safely hidden, Ingo returned to his quiet life as an ordinary man, but one who was always ready to unleash his incredible abilities when duty called.
