Marco Polo's experiences during his travels were so remarkable that he wrote a detailed account of his journeys, known as "Il Milione" (The Travels of Marco Polo). The book was widely read and influential throughout Europe, sparking interest in trade and exploration with Asia.

Despite the risks and hardships he faced on his journey, Marco Polo's travels had a profound impact on European history. He opened up new trade routes, introduced Europeans to Asian cultures, and inspired future explorers like Christopher Columbus.
