Here's a summary of the fictional character Liu Esposito:

Liu is intensely focused when working on a case, but he can also be deeply empathetic and understanding towards those who have been wronged.

As a detective, he is constantly seeking to uncover new leads and connections that can help him solve cases. Outside of work, Liu enjoys long walks in Central Park, trying out new cuisines from around the world (especially Italian and Chinese), and practicing calligraphy in his free time.

**Other Traits:** Liu Esposito has a distinctive sense of style that is both rugged and refined.
