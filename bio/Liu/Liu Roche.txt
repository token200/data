- Eye Color: Bright green
- Hair Color: Long, dark black hair that falls down her back like a dark cloud.

- Gracious and kind-hearted, she accepts gifts and thanks from others with humility.

**Interests:**
- Liu Roche is deeply connected to nature and the outdoors, enjoying strolls through crowded markets and city alleys, exploring hidden courtyards surrounded by ancient trees, and interacting with animals of all kinds.
- She appreciates beauty in every form, from vibrant colors to the sounds of bustling cities.
- Passionate about using her gift to bring people together and make a positive impact on those around her.

**Other Traits:**

- Liu Roche possesses wisdom and kindness that makes her an approachable figure, drawing people to her with her gentle demeanor.
