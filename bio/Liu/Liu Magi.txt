Here is a summary of the personality, physical attributes, and interests of Liu Magi:

**Personality:** Liu Magi is driven by a burning passion to push the boundaries of what is thought possible in the art of illusion. He is renowned for his unparalleled prowess as a weaver of illusions and is sought after by royalty and nobility from neighboring realms. Despite being constantly demanded at critical moments, Liu Magi remains unfazed, demonstrating an unwavering dedication to his craft.

**Physical Attributes:** 

- Height: 6 feet 1 inch (185 cm) - tall and slender, allowing for greater flexibility in his work.
- Eye Color: Emerald green - a striking feature that has come to be associated with Liu Magi's mastery of illusions.
- Hair Color: Dark brown - often worn long and neatly styled, framing his emerald eyes.

**Interests:** 

- Magic, particularly illusion weavings (specializing in elaborate deceptions and disguises)
- The pursuit of artistic expression through his craft
- Mastery of the mystic arts passed down from generations of his esteemed family

Liu Magi's natural affinity for magic, combined with his dedication to honing his skills under Master Lin's guidance, has led him to become one of Aethoria's most revered masters of illusion.
