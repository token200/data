Here is a summary of the fictional character Indrek Yan:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 6 feet 2 inches (188 cm)
- Eye Color: piercing blue, indicative of his bravery and strength
- Hair: dark brown, shoulder-length, often worn in a short braid or tied back with a leather cord

Personality:
Indrek is a fearless warrior with an unwavering commitment to justice. His years of training under his father have instilled in him a strong sense of loyalty and duty, which drives him to protect the realm from threats like Lyraea's dark magic. He is also fiercely independent and has a natural affinity for leadership, as evident in his decisive actions during the battle with the sorceress.

Interests:
- Combat and martial arts: Indrek is renowned for his exceptional swordsmanship and skill in combat.
- Strategy and tactics: As a seasoned warrior, he understands the importance of planning and execution on the battlefield.
- Horses and horse breeding: Indrek has a deep passion for horses, which he inherited from his father. He takes great pride in training and breeding his own mounts.

Other details:
- Age: Late 20s to early 30s
- Education: Trained as a warrior from childhood by his father, a renowned warrior.
- Family: Member of the prestigious Silverwood clan, with a strong sense of family loyalty and duty.
- Personality Traits: Brave, loyal, strategic, independent, and proud of his heritage.
