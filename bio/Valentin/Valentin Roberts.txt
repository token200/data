Here is a summary of the fictional character Valentin Roberts:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 6 feet 1 inch (185 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue
- Hair Color: Dark brown
- Hair Style: Always perfectly styled and maintained
- Facial Features: Chiseled, with high cheekbones and a strong jawline

Personality:
Valentin is a confident and self-assured individual, often bordering on arrogance. However, beneath his tough exterior lies a sense of integrity and moral compass that guides him in making decisions. He has a dry wit and can come across as aloof or unapproachable to those who don't know him, but he is deeply empathetic and willing to go to great lengths to protect the people he cares about.

Interests:
- Former career as a spy
- Private investigation and problem-solving
- Using his skills for good, helping those in need
- Traveling, exploring new places (particularly Europe)
- Staying physically fit and maintaining a high level of mental acuity

Additional Traits:
- Highly intelligent and resourceful, with exceptional research skills
- Experienced in hand-to-hand combat and tactical strategy
- Strong sense of justice and a commitment to fighting against corruption and wrongdoing
- A natural leader, able to inspire confidence and loyalty in those around him
