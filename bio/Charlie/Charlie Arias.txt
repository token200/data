Here is a summary of the fictional character Charlie Arias:

**Physical Attributes:** 
- Height: 6'1"
- Eye Color: Blue
- Hair Color: Dark Brown
- Build: Lean and athletic, with a wiry physique developed from years of physical labor.
- Facial Features: Strong jawline, prominent nose, and piercing blue eyes that seem to bore into those he interrogates.

**Personality:** 
Charlie is a brooding and intense individual with a dry sense of humor. He has a natural affinity for observing the world around him, allowing him to piece together seemingly unrelated clues. As a former cop turned private investigator, Charlie has developed a strong sense of justice and will stop at nothing to uncover the truth.

Despite his tough exterior, Charlie has a soft spot for those in need and is fiercely loyal to those he cares about. His years as a cop have left emotional scars, making him slightly wary of getting close to people or forming attachments.

**Interests:** 
- Boxing: In his younger days, Charlie was an accomplished boxer who found solace in the sport's intensity and physicality.
- Cars: He has a passion for classic American muscle cars and often spends his free time restoring them.
- Music: A heavy metal enthusiast, Charlie can often be heard belting out punk rock anthems while driving down the highway or working on his latest case.

As a PI, Charlie is always on the lookout for clues and is willing to take risks to uncover the truth. His sharp mind and intuitive nature make him an excellent detective, but they also leave him vulnerable to the emotional toll of his work.
