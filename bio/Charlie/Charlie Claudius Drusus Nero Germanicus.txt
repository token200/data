He was deeply committed to the Roman Empire's expansion and growth, and he spent countless hours studying the art of warfare and diplomacy. He also had a deep appreciation for Germanic culture, having been raised by his mother's people, which instilled in him a sense of connection to his heritage.

* Hunting and outdoor activities
* Studying philosophy and the works of great Roman thinkers
* Participating in public ceremonies and events that celebrated Rome's triumphs
