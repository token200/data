Here is a summary of the fictional character Mohamed D'Annunzio:

**Physical Attributes:** Mohamed stands at 5'11" (180 cm) with piercing green eyes, which reflect his Egyptian heritage. His dark brown hair is often styled in a modern, edgy manner, while his warm, olive-toned skin speaks to his Italian mother's influence. He has a lean and athletic build, honed from years of exploring the city on foot or engaging in intense debates with his fellow activists.

**Personality:** Mohamed is a charismatic and confident individual who commands attention without effort. His passion for art and politics drives him to take risks and challenge the status quo, making him both inspiring and intimidating to those around him. He has a quick wit and can be sarcastic at times, but he always means well and is genuinely committed to his vision for Rome's future.

**Interests:** Mohamed is deeply interested in art, architecture, and urban planning, which informs his work on the "Roma Futura" movement. He is also fascinated by technology, innovation, and sustainable development, often incorporating these themes into his art and activism. In his free time, he enjoys attending concerts and festivals, trying new foods and drinks, and exploring the lesser-known corners of Rome.

**Additional Details:** Mohamed's mixed heritage has instilled in him a strong sense of cultural identity and a desire to bridge the gaps between different communities. He is fluent in Italian, English, and Arabic, which serves him well in his work as an artist and activist. Despite his confident exterior, he has a vulnerable side that he only reveals to those closest to him.
