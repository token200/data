Here's a summary of the fictional character Francesco Lewandowska:

Physical Attributes:
- Age: Late 30s to early 40s
- Height: 6 feet 2 inches (188 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue, with an intensity that seems to bore into those he looks at
- Hair: Dark brown, cut short and neatly styled to accentuate his angular features
- Build: Lean and athletic, honed from years of intense physical activity

Personality:
- Calculated and reserved, Francesco exudes confidence and composure under pressure
- A natural strategic thinker, he can analyze situations quickly and make decisive moves
- Emotionally detached, yet this doesn't mean he's unfeeling; he wears his emotions like a suit, choosing when to reveal them to gain an advantage

Interests:
- Mathematics: Francesco has an advanced degree in mathematics, which serves him well in the statistical analysis of poker
- Engineering: He's also an expert engineer, able to apply logical reasoning to complex problems
- Poker: As stated earlier, Francesco is a professional poker player with a reputation for being fearless and calculated.
