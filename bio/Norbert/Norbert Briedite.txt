Here is a summary of the fictional character Norbert Briedite:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 5'10" (178 cm)
- Eye Color: Deep blue, almost indigo
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often worn long and messy
- Build: Lean and athletic, honed from years of physical activity as a fisherman

Personality:
Norbert Briedite is an enigmatic individual with a calm and collected demeanor. He appears to be a quiet and introspective person, preferring to keep to himself and observe the world around him. This unassuming nature allows him to blend into his surroundings and avoid drawing attention to himself.

Interests:
- The sea: Norbert finds solace in the vastness and power of the ocean. He spends much of his free time watching the waves, fishing, or simply standing at the water's edge.
- Solitude: Despite being seen helping others and participating in community events, Norbert values his alone time and often retreats to quiet contemplation.
- History and mystery: Though his past is shrouded in secrecy, Norbert has a fascination with history and the unknown. He may spend hours studying old books or listening to local legends.

Background:
Norbert's past life as "The Order" is marked by covert missions, intelligence gathering, and manipulation of world events. After leaving the organization due to a job gone sour, he reinvented himself as Norbert Briedite, adopting a new identity that has allowed him to start anew in this small seaside town.

Despite his attempt to leave his old life behind, it is clear that Norbert still grapples with secrets and unresolved issues from his past. His enigmatic nature makes it difficult for others to discern what lies beneath his calm exterior, leaving them to wonder about the true depths of his character.
