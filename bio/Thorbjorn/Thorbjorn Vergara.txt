Here's a summary of the fictional character Thorbjorn Vergara:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 5'11" (180 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue, reminiscent of a clear Scandinavian sky
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often styled neatly and tied back when serving in his librarian role
- Build: Lean and athletic, with toned muscles from years of study and research

Personality:
- Quiet and unassuming by day as the town's librarian, Thorbjorn has an air of calm demeanor that puts those around him at ease.
- Passionate about mythology and folklore, he is driven to uncover hidden truths and protect the innocent.
- Brave in the face of danger, he has a strong sense of justice and will not hesitate to stand up for what is right.

Interests:
- Studying ancient mythology and folklore
- Reading and collecting rare books on various subjects
- Martial arts training to stay sharp and prepared for any situation that may arise
- Singing softly to himself when lost in thought, often humming traditional Scandinavian tunes

Background:
Thorbjorn Vergara is a native of Ravenswood and has been its librarian for over 10 years. Few people know about his secret life as a hero who wields the mighty hammer Mjolnir. His dedication to mythology and folklore has led him down a path that few others have taken, and he continues to study and learn in order to protect those around him from threats both magical and mundane.

While not explicitly stated, it's clear that Thorbjorn Vergara is deeply connected to the world of Norse mythology, drawing inspiration from his studies for battle and heroism.
