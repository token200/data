Here is a summary of the fictional character Lucien Ayumi:

- Height: 5'10" (178 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing green
- Facial Features: Sharp jawline

Personality:
Lucien is a complex individual with a quiet intensity. He exudes confidence, which can sometimes be misinterpreted as arrogance. However, his sharp jawline and piercing green eyes suggest that there's more to him than meets the eye. As a freelance journalist, Lucien has developed a knack for uncovering secrets and pushing boundaries, which often puts him at odds with those who prefer to keep things under wraps.

Interests:
- Investigating dark corners of society
- Uncovering corruption and deceit
- Exposing hidden truths through his journalism

Skills:
- Freelance journalism
- Research and investigation
- Interviews and questioning sources

Background:
While not explicitly stated, Lucien's background as a journalist suggests that he may have started out in the field while still in college or shortly after. He seems to have developed a strong reputation for himself as an investigative journalist, which has earned him the respect (and sometimes fear) of his peers.

Relationships:
- Takeshi: A former police detective turned private investigator who has reached out to Lucien about a mysterious string of murders. Their relationship is professional but also seems to hint at a deeper connection.
