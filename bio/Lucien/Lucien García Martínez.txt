Here is a summary of the fictional character Lucien García Martínez:

**Physical Attributes:** 
- Height: 6 feet (183 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing green
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often styled in a sleek and stylish manner
- Facial Features: Chiseled, with high cheekbones and a strong jawline

**Personality:** 
- Charismatic and confident on the surface, with a charming smile that can disarm even the most skeptical individuals
- Highly intelligent and resourceful, with exceptional skills in deception and disguise
- Fiercely independent and self-reliant, but also deeply emotional and vulnerable beneath his tough exterior
- Haunted by the ghosts of his past, which he keeps closely guarded and hidden from prying eyes

**Interests:** 
- High-stakes diplomacy and corporate espionage, as a former CIA operative turned freelance consultant
- Mastering new skills and staying ahead of the game in the world of intelligence gathering and manipulation
- Wine tasting and whiskey sipping (a favorite pastime at his local bar)
- Exploring the complexities of human nature, particularly those with complex and mysterious personalities like Sophia

**Additional Details:** 
- Lucien has a quiet sense of humor and enjoys witty banter with those he trusts
- He is highly analytical and methodical in his approach to problem-solving, often preferring to think things through before acting
- Despite his polished exterior, Lucien has a deep-seated desire for connection and intimacy, particularly after years of living a solitary life as a freelance consultant.
