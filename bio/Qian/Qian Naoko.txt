- History: Her connection to traditional calligraphy stems from learning about it through stories passed down by her grandmother. She seeks to understand the historical significance behind each piece of art she creates.
- Philosophy: Through writing, she explores deeper meanings and emotions in a way that resonates with her.

Qian Naoko was raised under the guidance of an accomplished artist-grandmother who taught her calligraphy at a young age. This early exposure played a significant role in shaping Qian Naoko into the skilled artist she is today.

Values:

- Self-expression: Using writing to convey emotions, tell stories, and connect with others on a deeper level.
- Family legacy: Continuously striving to honor her grandmother's artistic heritage by pushing the boundaries of what is possible within this traditional craft.

These attributes combined make Qian Naoko an embodiment of artistic expression and cultural respect, as she works to share her passion for calligraphy with the world.
