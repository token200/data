Here is a summary of the fictional character Luis Roy:

Physical Attributes:
Luis has an imposing physical presence, standing at 6'2" with a lean yet athletic build. His piercing green eyes seem to bore into those he looks at, giving off an air of intensity and focus. His sharp jawline and chiseled features make him appear ruggedly handsome, adding to his enigmatic aura.

Personality:
Luis exudes quiet confidence and has a reputation for being one of the best private investigators in the business. He is fiercely intelligent and intensely focused on solving cases, often bordering on obsession. His sharp instincts and analytical mind have earned him a spot among the top detectives, but his aloof demeanor and air of arrogance can make it difficult for others to get close to him.

Interests:
As a private investigator, Luis's work takes center stage in his life. He is driven by a passion for solving complex mysteries and uncovering the truth. His expertise in surveillance, interrogation, and forensic analysis allows him to navigate the darker corners of society with ease. When not working on cases, Luis seems to prefer the company of his own thoughts, often spending hours alone in quiet contemplation.

Additional Details:
Luis's past is shrouded in mystery, and he appears reluctant to share any personal details about himself. This air of secrecy contributes to his enigmatic reputation, making it difficult for those who know him to truly understand him. Despite this, there is a sense that Luis is driven by a strong sense of justice, which motivates him to take on cases like Sophia's and fight for the truth, no matter how complex or challenging the situation may be.
