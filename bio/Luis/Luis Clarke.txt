Here is a summary of the fictional character Luis Clarke:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 5'10" (178 cm)
- Eye Color: Hazel
- Hair Color: Dark Brown, often worn in a messy, unkempt style
- Facial Features: Strong jawline, prominent nose, and piercing brown eyes that seem to bore into those he looks at

Personality:
- Intelligent and analytical, with a keen eye for detail
- Passionate about history and the pursuit of knowledge
- Prone to becoming obsessed with his research, often neglecting personal relationships and comforts in favor of uncovering new information
- A bit of a troublemaker, willing to take risks and push boundaries to get to the bottom of a mystery

Interests:
- History, particularly New Zealand's colonial past and the exploration era
- Archival research and manuscript analysis
- The study of ancient cultures and civilizations
- Adventure and exploration, with a sense of restlessness and a desire to uncover secrets that lie hidden beneath the surface
- Mystery solving and puzzle piecing

Background:
Luis grew up in Wellington, New Zealand, with a strong interest in history from an early age. He pursued a degree in history at the University of Auckland, where he honed his research skills and developed a passion for archival studies. After completing his studies, he landed a job as an archivist at the prestigious Wellington Museum, where he spent most of his days surrounded by dusty artifacts and forgotten relics.
