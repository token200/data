In his free time, he feeds stray animals, which brings him joy and a sense of purpose. He also enjoys spending time outdoors, whether it's driving his truck through the woods or simply watching the sunset on a quiet evening.

**Other traits:** Luis has a deep connection to nature, often feeling most at home in the wilderness. His love for stray animals is a source of comfort and companionship for him. Despite his tough exterior, he has a gentle soul that is drawn to those who are lost or in need.
