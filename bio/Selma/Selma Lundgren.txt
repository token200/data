Here's a summary of the fictional character Selma Lundgren:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5'2" (157 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue
- Hair Color: Spiky black hair, often styled in a messy bob
- Build: Petite and athletic, with toned muscles from years of physical training

**Personality:**
Selma Lundgren is a force to be reckoned with, exuding confidence and determination. Her tough exterior hides a complex personality, driven by a deep-seated mistrust of those in power and a burning desire for justice. She's fiercely independent and resourceful, able to think on her feet and adapt to any situation. Selma has a dry sense of humor and can be sarcastic at times, which often catches people off guard.

**Interests:**
- Investigative work and solving puzzles
- Martial arts and hand-to-hand combat
- Guns and firearms (she's an expert shooter)
- Reading and research, particularly on politics and law enforcement
- Solving cryptic codes and decoding messages

**Background:**
As a retired CIA operative turned private investigator, Selma has a wealth of experience in espionage and counter-intelligence. She left the agency after a botched mission went sideways, leaving her with emotional scars and a lingering sense of guilt. Now, she uses her skills to take down corrupt officials and uncover hidden truths, always keeping a close eye on her back.

**Goals:**
Selma's primary goal is to expose the web of corruption that reaches from the FBI to the highest echelons of power. She's determined to see justice served, no matter how high the stakes or who she has to take down along the way.

**Motivations:**
- A sense of justice and a need to make amends for past mistakes
- A desire to prove herself as a capable operative in her own right
- A determination to outsmart those in power who seek to manipulate her

Overall, Selma Lundgren is a complex, driven, and highly skilled individual with a sharp mind and quick reflexes.
