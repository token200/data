Here is a summary of the personality, physical attributes, and interests of the fictional character Selma Aurelius Valerius Diocletianus:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 6 feet 1 inch (185 cm)
* Eye Color: Piercing blue
* Hair Color: Dark brown, often worn long and stylishly arranged
* Build: Lean and athletic, honed from years of military service
* Facial Features: Chiseled jawline, prominent nose, and a small scar above his left eyebrow (inflicted during a battle in his youth)

**Personality:**

* Ambitious: Selma's drive for power and success knows no bounds. He is willing to do whatever it takes to achieve his goals.
* Cunning: A master strategist, Selma has an uncanny ability to outmaneuver his opponents and turn the tide of battle in favor of Rome.
* Ruthless: When necessary, Selma will stop at nothing to eliminate his enemies or eliminate any threats to his power.
* Charismatic: Despite his ruthless nature, Selma has a compelling presence that allows him to win people over to his cause.
* Intelligent: A true thinker, Selma is well-versed in philosophy, politics, and military tactics.

**Interests:**

* Military Tactics: Selma's greatest passion is developing innovative strategies on the battlefield. He is constantly seeking new ways to improve Rome's military prowess.
* Politics: As a master politician, Selma has an unparalleled understanding of the inner workings of the Roman Empire. He knows how to navigate the complex web of alliances and rivalries that defines Roman politics.
* Horses: Selma is an accomplished horseman and has a particular fondness for the majestic stallions that roam the deserts of Rome.
* Reading: In his rare moments of leisure, Selma enjoys devouring books on philosophy, history, and military tactics. His favorite authors include Aristotle and Cicero.

**Hobbies:**

* Hunting: As a seasoned hunter, Selma values the thrill of the chase and has spent countless hours stalking prey across the deserts of Rome.
* Archery: An accomplished archer, Selma finds solace in this quiet pursuit when the demands of power and politics become overwhelming.
* Drinking: Selma is known to enjoy a fine wine or two (or ten) with his closest allies and friends.

**Quirks:**

* A habit of tapping his left foot nervously whenever he is deep in thought or contemplating a new plan
* A tendency to wear the same silver brooch, gifted to him by Emperor Trajan, wherever he goes
* A preference for traveling alone, preferring the company of his thoughts and strategic musings over human companionship.
