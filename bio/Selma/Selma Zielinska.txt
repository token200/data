Here is a summary of the fictional character Selma Zielinska:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
* Eye Color: Hazel with golden flecks
* Hair Color: Dark brown, often styled in loose waves
* Skin Tone: Porcelain-like complexion with a subtle scattering of freckles across her cheeks

**Personality:**

Selma Zielinska is a compassionate and introspective individual who sees the world through the eyes of an artist. She is deeply empathetic and can easily put herself into others' shoes, which allows her to capture the essence of her subjects with remarkable accuracy. Despite her fame, she remains humble and grounded, never losing sight of her artistic vision.

**Interests:**

* Painting from life, using traditional techniques and mediums
* Exploring different eras, cultures, and emotions through her art
* Collecting and studying the faces of people from various walks of life
* Nature and its rhythms (inspiring many of her artworks)
* Philosophy, particularly existentialism and humanism
* Literature and poetry, often reading works by Polish authors
* Conversations with other artists, musicians, and writers to spark creativity

Selma's artistic pursuits have woven a rich tapestry of experiences, emotions, and stories, making her one of the most revered portrait painters of her generation.
