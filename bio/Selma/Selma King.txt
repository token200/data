Here is a summary of the fictional character Selma King:

**Physical Attributes:**
Selma is a petite woman with an unassuming height of approximately 5'2" (157 cm). Her eye color is a bright, sparkling blue that seems to dance with creativity. Her hair is a wild tangle of curly brown locks that often escape her loose braids and ponytails.

**Personality:**
Selma is a free-spirited individual with an infectious passion for life. She is deeply rooted in nature and feels most at home surrounded by the sights, sounds, and scents of the great outdoors. Her creativity knows no bounds, and she is constantly seeking new sources of inspiration to fuel her artistic endeavors. Selma is also fiercely compassionate and dedicated to environmental conservation, often putting the needs of others before her own.

**Interests:**
Selma's primary interests revolve around art, nature, and wildlife preservation. She spends her days exploring the outdoors, collecting materials for her art pieces, and volunteering at local wildlife refuges. Her passion for nocturnal creatures has led to a series of captivating paintings inspired by their unique beauty and behaviors. In addition to her artistic pursuits, Selma is an avid observer of the natural world, constantly seeking new ways to appreciate and protect our planet's precious ecosystems.

**Additional Traits:**
Selma's studio reflects her eclectic style, with vibrant colors and textures woven throughout every inch of it. She has a talent for natural dyes and uses them liberally in her art pieces. Her workspace is often cluttered with paintbrushes, canvases, and half-finished projects, giving testament to her creative energy and spontaneity.
