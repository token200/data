Here is a summary of the fictional character Oliver Jasiński:

**Physical Attributes:** 
- Height: 5'10" (178 cm)
- Eye Color: Deep blue, almost navy

**Personality:**
Oliver is a introspective and creative individual who has been away from his hometown for years. He is struggling to find his place in the world, particularly with regards to his writing career. Despite this, he has a strong sense of nostalgia and belonging for his small coastal town. Oliver is warm and welcoming towards others, as evident from his reunion with Mrs. Kowalska at the bookstore.

**Interests:**
Oliver's interests include literature, writing, and exploring the outdoors. He loves old books and dusty paperbacks, which are a staple of his favorite childhood haunt - the family's old bookstore on Main Street. When he returns to his hometown, Oliver settles back into exploring the local coastline, reconnecting with friends, and helping out at the bookstore.

**Background:** Oliver had spent summers in this small coastal town as a child but moved away after completing university studies in literature. The more he read and wrote, however, the more he realized that home was where his heart was – which led him back to this town he left years ago.
