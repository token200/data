Etienne Esquivel is a driven, intelligent, and fiercely independent private investigator with a reputation for being one of the best in his field. His years of experience have honed his observational skills, allowing him to notice even the smallest details that others might overlook. Despite his tough exterior, he is also fiercely loyal and empathetic towards those who need his help.

Interests:
Etienne Esquivel has a keen interest in solving complex mysteries and unraveling tangled threads of deceit. He is always on the lookout for new cases, no matter how difficult or challenging they may be. When not working, he enjoys smoking cigars, sipping fine wine, and spending quiet evenings at his favorite bookstore, browsing through classic detective novels.

Etienne Esquivel's sharp mind and quick wit make him a formidable opponent in any case that comes his way. His reputation as one of the top private investigators has earned him respect from his peers, but also brings with it pressure to deliver results.
