Here is a summary of the fictional character Etienne Mustermann:

Etienne stands at approximately 6'2" (188 cm) with an athletic yet lean build. His piercing green eyes are his most striking feature, seeming to bore into those he meets as if sizing them up for potential weaknesses. His dark brown hair is often messy and unkempt, framing his chiseled features. Etienne has a strong jawline and sharp cheekbones, giving him a distinctive and brooding appearance.

**Personality:**
Despite his intimidating presence, Etienne possesses a dry wit and a sharp tongue that can cut down even the most confident individual. He is a complex character with a mysterious past as a former spy, having spent years honing his skills in the art of espionage. However, beneath his tough exterior lies a romantic at heart, with a deep love for 19th-century literature and art.

**Interests:**
Etienne's interests are varied but centered around antiques and collectibles. He has a passion for restoring and appraising rare items, using his former espionage skills to analyze and authenticate even the most obscure pieces. Etienne is also an avid reader of 19th-century literature and enjoys spending hours browsing through dusty shelves in Monsieur LaFleur's antique shop.

Overall, Etienne Mustermann is a multifaceted character with a rich inner life, possessing both physical and emotional intensity that makes him a compelling figure to watch.
