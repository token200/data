Despite his affable exterior, Andres is also deeply passionate and emotionally intelligent, able to tap into the emotions of those around him.

Andres is drawn to the stories of others, often finding himself in situations where he's able to meet new people and hear their tales. He has a keen eye for art, music, and literature, and is particularly interested in the ways that these mediums can be used to tell powerful stories about human experience.
