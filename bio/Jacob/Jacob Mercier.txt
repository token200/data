Here is a summary of the fictional character Jacob Mercier:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 6'2"
- Eye Color: Piercing blue
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often worn messy and unkempt
- Facial Features: Sharp jawline, high cheekbones, and a small scar above his left eyebrow (likely obtained during one of his street fighting days)

Personality:
- Jacob is a mysterious individual with an air of confidence that borders on arrogance.
- He has an innate ability to adapt to any situation, often staying one step ahead of those around him.
- A self-proclaimed loner, Jacob prefers the company of strangers over close friends due to past experiences of being betrayed and deceived.

Interests:
- Jacob is drawn to the thrill of the heist and the adrenaline rush that comes with it. He has a natural talent for thievery and con artistry, often finding himself in high-stakes situations.
- Despite his love for deception, Jacob seems to be driven by a deep-seated desire for freedom and self-discovery.
- When not planning or executing heists, Jacob can be found lost in the city's jazz scene, finding solace in the music and the anonymity it provides.

Background:
Jacob grew up on the streets of New Orleans, surviving by his wits and cunning. As a young boy, he learned to fight and defend himself from a tender age. Years went by, and Jacob's reputation as a skilled thief and con artist spread throughout the city. With each new challenge, Jacob's skills improved, and so did his notoriety.

However, beneath his tough exterior lies a complex individual with secrets worth hiding. His past is shrouded in mystery, making it impossible to discern what drives him or what he truly wants from life.
