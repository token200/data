Here is a short summary of the fictional character Wilma Salonen:

**Physical Attributes:** Wilma stands at 5'9" (175 cm) with piercing green eyes and jet black hair that falls in luscious waves down her back. Her features are sharp, angular, and seem chiseled from fine granite.

**Personality:** Wilma is a fiercely determined individual with an intensity that sets her apart. She has a strong sense of self-reliance and can come across as aloof or standoffish to those who don't know her. However, beneath this tough exterior lies a deep-seated passion for engineering and a desire to make a positive impact on the world.

**Interests:** Wilma is deeply interested in physics, mathematics, and engineering, particularly sustainable energy harvesting. She finds solace in tinkering with machines, reading, and learning new skills. Her past experiences have left her with a fascination for fire and flames, which she explores through her work and personal interests.
