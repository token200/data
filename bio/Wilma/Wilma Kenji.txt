Here's a summary of the fictional character Wilma Kenji:

- Eye Color: Warm, honey-brown eyes with subtle golden undertones that sparkle with kindness and warmth.

- Wilma Kenji is a kind-hearted individual who radiates humility and genuine enthusiasm for her craft. She has an infectious smile that puts customers at ease and makes them feel like part of the bakery's family.
- Her dedication to using only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients reflects her respect for tradition and commitment to excellence in every aspect of her work.
- Despite the demands of running a successful business, Wilma Kenji remains grounded and passionate about sharing her love of baking with others.

**Interests:**
- Baking, particularly traditional Japanese desserts, is Wilma Kenji's true passion. She is constantly experimenting with new flavors and techniques to innovate while staying true to her roots.
- Traveling: Although born in Willow Creek, Wilma Kenji has a desire to explore the world, learn about different cultures, and incorporate these experiences into her baking.
- Sharing stories: Over steaming cups of coffee or amidst the warm atmosphere of her bakery, Wilma Kenji loves to share tales of her grandmother's wisdom, family traditions, and the inspiration behind her creations.

Wilma Kenji's dedication to her craft, love for tradition, and passion for sharing the joy of baking have turned her small town bakery into a beloved institution.
