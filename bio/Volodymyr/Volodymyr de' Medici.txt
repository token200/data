Here is a summary of the fictional character Volodymyr de' Medici:

- Eye Color: Dark Brown
- Hair: Jet black, often worn long and unkempt to reflect his more introspective nature.

Volodymyr is an enigmatic figure, with a complex mix of traits that make him both intriguing and mysterious. Despite the weight of his family's legacy, Volodymyr is fiercely independent and longs for freedom from the expectations that come with being a member of the powerful Medici family. His thoughts are often consumed by abstract ideas and theories, making him appear somewhat detached or aloof to those who don't know him.

**Interests:**
- Alchemy: Volodymyr's true passion lies in the pursuit of alchemy, which he believes holds the key to unlocking the secrets of transmutation and the Philosopher's Stone.
- Ancient Texts and Literature: He is deeply fascinated by ancient texts on alchemy, as well as other subjects that deal with spirituality, philosophy, and the nature of reality.
- Personal Freedom and Autonomy: As Volodymyr navigates his life between his family obligations and personal aspirations, he strives to find a balance between these competing desires.

Overall, Volodymyr is an intelligent, introspective character who is driven by his desire for knowledge and self-discovery.
