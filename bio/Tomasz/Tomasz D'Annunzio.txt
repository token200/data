In his free time, Tomasz enjoys exploring the city, sipping fine wines, and attending high-society events, where he can network with influential people and gather information for his next investigation.

**Skills:** Tomasz is an expert interviewer, able to coax even the most reluctant sources into revealing sensitive information. Overall, Tomasz D'Annunzio is a complex and intriguing character, driven by a strong sense of justice and a determination to uncover the truth, no matter what it takes.
