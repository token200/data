Here is a short summary of the fictional character Tomasz Pires:

**Physical Attributes:** Tomasz has a slender build and stands at around 5'9" (175 cm) tall. He has dark brown eyes, which sparkle with creativity whenever he's behind the camera. His hair is a rich, dark brown that often falls across his forehead in a messy swoop.

He is driven by a desire to capture the beauty and essence of the world around him through his lens. Despite his love for old cameras, he's always open to new technology and techniques, which makes him adaptable and enthusiastic in the pursuit of capturing the perfect shot.

**Interests:** As mentioned earlier, Tomasz has a deep appreciation for photography and storytelling through images. He is particularly fascinated by the art of antique camera repair and restoration, and is often on the lookout for rare or unique cameras to add to his collection.
