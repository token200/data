Background:
Basheera Persson lives in seclusion on the outskirts of Ravenswood, surrounded by an aura that shrouds her estate. This sense of mystery has earned her a reputation among the townspeople, who whisper stories of ghostly apparitions and unexplained occurrences emanating from within its walls.

Despite her reclusive lifestyle, Basheera Persson is renowned for her ability to connect with those seeking guidance, often offering profound wisdom and support in times of need.
