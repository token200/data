Here is a summary of the fictional character Dominika Fernandez:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing green
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often styled in a neat and elegant bob
- Skin Tone: Smooth and pale, with a subtle warmth that hints at her Latin American heritage
- Body Type: Lean and athletic, built from years of adapting to limited mobility due to her genetic disorder

**Personality:**
Dominika is a private person who prefers to keep to herself, often coming across as reserved or introverted. However, when she chooses to use her abilities for good, she reveals a brave and selfless side. She has a strong sense of justice and will fiercely protect those in need. Despite her humility about her powers, Dominika is intelligent and resourceful, able to think on her feet and outmaneuver those who would misuse her gifts.

**Interests:**
- Light manipulation (her primary ability)
- Alternative medicine and healing
- Martial arts and self-defense
- Reading and research (especially in the fields of physics, psychology, and philosophy)
- Music (Dominika is an accomplished pianist and enjoys classical music)
- Traveling and exploring new places

These interests reflect Dominika's resourceful and independent nature, as well as her desire to understand and harness her unique abilities.
