- Age: 25
- Height: 5'10" (178 cm)

**Interests:**
- Writing (short stories, essays, poetry)
- Language studies (specifically, Japanese language and customs)
- Literature (19th-century Japanese poetry is a particular favorite)
- Observational documentary photography
- Interviews with people from diverse backgrounds to capture their stories
- Historical research into the city's hidden corners

Sato Abbagnato has found his voice as a writer through his interactions with individuals like the elderly retired schoolteacher, who shares her wisdom and love for literature. His experiences and observations of life in Tokyo serve as a rich source of inspiration for his work, capturing both the vibrancy of urban landscapes and the quiet beauty of human connections.
