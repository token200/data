Here is a summary of the fictional character Ewa Larsson:

**Physical Attributes:**
Ewa stands at 5'8" (173 cm) with striking features. Her hair is dark brown, often pulled back in a practical ponytail for her work as a detective. Her eyes are a piercing blue, a trait that seems to see right through to the truth. She has a strong and angular jawline, accentuated by her sharp cheekbones. Ewa's style is understated yet professional, often dressing in dark-colored coats and trousers that allow her to blend into the shadows.

**Personality:**
Ewa is a determined and fiercely intelligent detective with a strong sense of justice. She is driven by a desire to solve cold cases and bring closure to families who have been left without answers for far too long. Her years of experience have honed her skills as a researcher, interviewer, and strategist, making her one of the best in the business. Ewa's passion and dedication can sometimes be intimidating, but she is always approachable and willing to listen to new ideas. She has a dry sense of humor that helps to break the ice with witnesses and suspects alike.

**Interests:**
Ewa's interests are closely tied to her work as a detective. She loves nothing more than immersing herself in cold cases, pouring over files, and analyzing evidence. In her free time, she enjoys long walks, reading crime novels, and practicing yoga to keep her mind sharp. Ewa is also an avid skier and snowboarder, which explains her initial interest in the Lena Nielsen case – a solo skiing trip had led to her missing, making it eerily personal.

**Background:**
Ewa's background is shrouded in mystery, even within the Stockholm police department. She keeps her personal life closely guarded, preferring to focus on solving cases rather than sharing her own stories. It is clear that Ewa has faced personal struggles and losses in her past, fueling her determination to crack the Lena Nielsen case.

Overall, Ewa Larsson is a complex and intriguing character – driven by a fierce dedication to justice, with a strong sense of purpose that propels her forward even in the most challenging cases.
