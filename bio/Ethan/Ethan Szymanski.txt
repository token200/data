Here's a summary of the fictional character Ethan Szymanski:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
- Eye Color: Dark brown
- Hair Color: Short, dark brown hair that is often styled neatly and tidily
- Build: Lean, athletic build from years of working out to maintain his physical fitness.
- Facial Features: Strong jawline, sharp cheekbones, and a nose slightly crooked, giving him a rugged yet intelligent appearance.

**Personality:**
Ethan is driven by his intense focus on solving mysteries. He's highly analytical and has a knack for spotting patterns that others miss. His dedication to justice can lead him to neglect his personal life at times, but deep down he cares deeply about those around him.
- Ethan values fairness and justice above all else.
- Despite his tough exterior, he has a dry sense of humor and enjoys bantering with his colleagues.
- He is fiercely independent, preferring to rely on himself rather than others in high-pressure situations.

**Interests:**
Ethan's love for detective work is rivaled only by his passion for old movies and novels. In his rare moments of free time, he can often be found reading about famous cases or watching classic whodunits.
- Music: He has an eclectic taste in music, favoring jazz and blues over anything too loud or energetic.
- Cuisine: Ethan enjoys a good steak and fries as much as the next person, but will also opt for lighter meals when working late into the night.
- Hobbies: In his free time, he likes to attend museum exhibits, read books about history, and exercise regularly.

**Additional Traits:**
Ethan's obsession with solving the case has led to some minor physical changes. He now has a few days' worth of stubble and appears slightly sleep-deprived due to working long hours without adequate rest. Despite this, he still manages to maintain his usual composure in high-pressure situations, relying on years of experience to guide him through even the most challenging cases.
