Here's a summary of the fictional character Mila Muti:

**Physical Attributes:**
Mila is a young woman with an athletic yet agile build, honed from her street-smart upbringing in Brooklyn. She stands at approximately 5'6" (168 cm) tall and has long, curly brown hair that often falls in loose waves down her back. Her bright hazel eyes sparkle with wit and intelligence, shining like dark brown stars in the night sky. Mila's smile can light up a room, revealing a small gap between her front teeth and a scattering of freckles across her nose.

**Personality:**
Mila is a quick-witted, resourceful teenager who has learned to rely on herself and her intelligence to navigate life's challenges. She possesses a sharp mind, a silver tongue, and an unwavering determination to protect those she cares about. Mila is fiercely independent and non-conformist, often challenging authority and pushing boundaries in pursuit of justice. Despite the weight of responsibility that comes with her newfound powers, Mila remains humble and empathetic, willing to listen and learn from others.

**Interests:**
Mila's interests revolve around technology, history, and social justice. As a street-smart teenager, she was fascinated by gadgets, machinery, and electronics, which led her to develop skills in fixing appliances, picking locks, and repairing devices. Her discovery of the Mindlink artifact has also sparked a deeper interest in mysticism, ancient civilizations, and the power of thought. Mila is driven by a desire to use her unique abilities to inspire positive change, stand up against injustice, and forge a brighter future for those around her.

**Background:**
Born and raised in Brooklyn's gritty streets, Mila grew up surrounded by poverty, violence, and neglect. Despite these hardships, she found solace in books, technology, and creative expression, which helped shape her into the resourceful and determined individual she is today. With the Mindlink artifact in hand, Mila has embarked on a journey to harness its power for good, using her wit, empathy, and street smarts to outwit The Order and create a better world for all.
