Based on the inspiration from Jorge Luis Borges, I've created a fictional character profile for Tamas Luis Borges:

- Eye Color: Dark Brown with flecks of gold, reminiscent of Jorge Luis Borges' own eyes.
- Hair: Black, often styled in a messy, intellectual look, reflecting his passion for philosophy and literature.

**Personality:**
Tamas is an introspective and curious individual, always seeking to understand the complexities of human existence. His love for Jorge Luis Borges' works has instilled in him a sense of wonder and awe for the mysteries of time, space, and identity. He is fiercely independent and often lost in his own thoughts, which can lead to misinterpretations by those around him. However, this introspection also allows Tamas to observe the world with a keen eye, spotting connections between seemingly unrelated ideas.

2. Literature: He is an avid reader of 20th-century literature, particularly those exploring the intersections of philosophy and fiction.
3. Language: Tamas has a passion for learning new languages, including Spanish, French, and Portuguese, reflecting his love for Borges' works, which often explored linguistic and philosophical themes.

**Habits:**

- Tamas often keeps a journal where he writes down his thoughts, insights, and observations, mirroring Borges' own practice of writing essays and short stories.
- He is known to take long walks at night, lost in thought as he navigates the city's streets, much like Borges himself did in Buenos Aires.
- Tamas has a habit of collecting rare books and manuscripts, which he believes hold secrets to understanding human knowledge.

As Tamas Luis Borges continues his journey of self-discovery, inspired by the writings of Jorge Luis Borges, he becomes increasingly lost in the labyrinthine world of philosophy, literature, and personal introspection.
