Here is a summary of the fictional character Tamas Aurelius Valerius Diocletianus:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 5'10" (178 cm) - a tall and slender figure, reflecting his intellectual and strategic approach to life.
* Eye Color: piercing emerald green, which seemed to gleam with intensity as he studied ancient texts and pondered the complexities of power.

**Personality:**

* Intelligent and analytical, Diocletianus was a voracious learner who devoured knowledge like a sponge. His sharp mind and quick wit made him an asset in debates and diplomatic situations.
* Determined and driven, he felt a sense of duty to forge his own path amidst the turmoil of the empire. He was fiercely independent and non-conformist, often challenging conventional wisdom in pursuit of truth.
* Emotionally introspective, Diocletianus struggled with the weight of history and tradition, seeking to reconcile his own identity with the legacy of his illustrious namesake.

**Interests:**

* Writing and calligraphy - as a novice scribe, Diocletianus took great pride in mastering the art of writing, which he believed would be essential for any aspiring statesman.
* History and politics - his insatiable curiosity drove him to study the ancient arts of governance, diplomacy, and warfare. He saw these disciplines as interconnected threads in the tapestry of power.
* Philosophy and spirituality - Diocletianus was drawn to the mysteries of cosmopolitan politics, which often intersected with philosophical and spiritual inquiry.

**Additional Quirks:**

* A habit of tapping his fingers on ancient texts, a nervous gesture that revealed his excitement when studying new material.
* A fondness for long walks under the desert stars, which allowed him to clear his mind and reflect on the weighty issues at hand.
* A tendency to mutter phrases from ancient texts to himself, as if channeling the wisdom of the past into his own inner voice.

With this summary, we get a glimpse into the character of Tamas Aurelius Valerius Diocletianus - an intellectual and strategic thinker driven by a sense of duty and purpose.
