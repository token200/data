Blanca is 25 years old with an athletic yet slender build. Her physical attributes include long, dark hair (approximately 28-30 inches) and porcelain skin, giving her an ethereal appearance. Her eyes are an striking shade of blue (almost indigo), which complements her dark locks.

Blanca values authenticity and vulnerability, as evident in her relationships with Kostia and others.

Blanca's passions include dance, music, art, and literature. As a professional dancer, she has a deep appreciation for the technical aspects of ballet and enjoys perfecting her craft. Outside of dance, Blanca enjoys practicing yoga to maintain flexibility and relaxation. **Quirks and Habits:**
Blanca has a tendency to analyze her performances and emotions intensely, often overthinking situations. However, she is also learning to be more intuitive and trust her instincts. She values deep conversations and meaningful connections, as seen in her relationships with Kostia and others.
