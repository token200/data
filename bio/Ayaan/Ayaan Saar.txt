Here is a summary of the fictional character Ayaan Saar:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 6 feet (183 cm)
- Eye Color: Deep, piercing blue
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often worn long and messy
- Facial Features: Strong jawline, high cheekbones, and a small scar above his left eyebrow from a childhood accident

Personality:
Ayaan is a introspective and analytical person who is driven by curiosity. He has a strong sense of determination and is willing to take risks to uncover the truth. Despite his tough exterior, he has a soft spot for those in need and will go to great lengths to protect those he cares about.

Interests:
- History: Ayaan is fascinated by the history of Ravenswood and its people. He spends hours researching and exploring the town's abandoned buildings.
- Puzzles and Mysteries: Ayaan enjoys solving puzzles and uncovering mysteries, which is why he is drawn to the family puzzle left behind by his relative.
- Family History: Ayaan is deeply interested in learning more about his family's past and their secrets. He hopes to piece together a history that will help him understand himself better.

Background:
Ayaan grew up in Ravenswood but moved away after high school, where he pursued higher education and started building a career. However, the death of his relative drew him back to Ravenswood, sparking an adventure to uncover the truth about his family's past.

It is worth noting that Ayaan has been shaped by his childhood memories of exploring abandoned buildings with Amira. He shares some traits with her such as a curiosity for the mysteries and history surrounding their town but also reflects different personality aspects to him.
