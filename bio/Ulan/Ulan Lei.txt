Here is a summary of the fictional character Ulan Lei:

However, his innate sense of curiosity and desire for adventure sets him apart from his peers. He is also compassionate and empathetic, as evident in his willingness to share stories of his ancestors' bravery with Aria.

**Interests:** Ulan Lei's primary interest lies in archery and the art of marksmanship, inherited from his family's legacy. He is also fascinated by the spirit world and the mysteries that lie beyond the veil of the living. His connection to the ancient language and mythology further fuels his curiosity about the supernatural forces at work in the world.

**Additional Traits:** Ulan Lei has an exceptional talent for strategy and problem-solving, often using his intuition and instincts to guide him through complex situations.
