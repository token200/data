Anya is also deeply connected to her heritage and culture, valuing the traditions and recipes passed down from her family.

Anya's true passions lie in food, cooking, and sharing meals with others. She loves experimenting with new flavors and techniques, while still honoring traditional Italian recipes. Her nonno's secrets are dear to her heart, and she often finds herself lost in thought, reminiscing about their culinary adventures together. Anya is also deeply invested in building a community around her deli, where people can come together and feel at home.
