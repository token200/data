Here is a summary of the fictional character Ikeda Korhonen:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5 feet 11 inches (180 cm)
- Eye Color: Deep, piercing brown
- Body Type: Muscular and athletic build, with a ruggedly weathered face from years of intense physical training.
- Hair Color: Short, dark brown hair that is often cropped close to the head.
- Facial Features: A strong jawline, prominent nose, and deep-set eyes that seem to bore into those he looks at.

**Personality:**
- Ikeda Korhonen is a rugged and stoic individual who exudes an air of quiet confidence and authority. His years of martial arts training have instilled in him a sense of discipline and self-control.
- Despite his tough exterior, Korhonen has a compassionate and empathetic side that he reveals to those closest to him. He is fiercely loyal to his students and will go to great lengths to protect and support them.
- As a master teacher, Korhonen is patient, encouraging, and demanding of excellence in equal measure.

**Interests:**
- Martial arts, particularly Kyokushin karate
- Teaching and mentoring the next generation of martial artists
- Traveling and exploring new cultures
- Spending time outdoors and connecting with nature

It's worth noting that Korhonen's personality is shaped by his experiences as a martial artist and a teacher. His past struggles have made him a wiser and more compassionate person, while his dedication to his craft has instilled in him a sense of purpose and direction.
