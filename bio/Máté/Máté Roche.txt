Máté is fiercely independent and non-conformist, always staying one step ahead of his adversaries. Máté's interests are diverse and often reflect his eclectic lifestyle. He's an art lover, with a particular fondness for 19th-century European paintings. Máté is also an avid reader of philosophy and history, often seeking out dusty tomes on politics and social justice.
