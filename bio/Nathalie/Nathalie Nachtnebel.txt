Nathalie is fiercely independent and values her freedom above all else, often feeling suffocated by the expectations of others.

Nathalie also has a deep love for history and architecture, often getting lost in the intricate details of Vienna's castle district and hidden courtyards.

In her free time, Nathalie can be found wandering the streets of Vienna, searching for secrets and hidden wonders to inspire her art. She is drawn to the mysterious and the unknown, often feeling a sense of kinship with the enigmatic stranger she met in the castle district – Sabine.
