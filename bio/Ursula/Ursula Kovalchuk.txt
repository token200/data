Ursula's personality is complex and multifaceted, shaped by her tumultuous past and her experiences on the streets of Toronto. She is fiercely independent, having learned to rely solely on herself from a young age. This independence has made her wary of getting too close to people, often causing her to struggle with intimacy and forming meaningful relationships. However, she also has a strong sense of determination and conviction, which drives her to fight for what she believes in.

**Interests:**
Ursula's passion for art is all-consuming, and she spends most of her free time honing her skills as a painter. Her love for art has taken her on a journey of self-discovery, forcing her to confront the darker aspects of her own psyche and the world around her.
