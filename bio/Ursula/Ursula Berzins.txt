Here is a summary of the fictional character Ursula Berzins:

**Physical Attributes:** Ursula stands at 5'8" with an athletic yet slender build. Her hair is dark brown and falls in loose waves down her back. Her eyes are a deep shade of indigo, almost purple, which seem to sparkle with warmth when she smiles. Her skin has a golden undertone, likely due to spending countless hours outdoors.

**Personality:** Ursula exudes warmth, kindness, and compassion. She possesses an empathetic nature that allows her to connect with people from all walks of life. Despite having faced numerous challenges in her past, Ursula's resilience and determination have helped her become a beacon of hope for those around her. Her quiet confidence and gentle demeanor put even the most nervous individuals at ease.

**Interests:** As a skilled baker, Ursula is passionate about experimenting with new flavors and techniques. She finds joy in perfecting recipes, often spending hours in her bakery kitchen creating innovative pastries and desserts. In addition to baking, Ursula enjoys gardening, finding solace in nurturing plants and watching them grow. She also appreciates the art of storytelling, often weaving tales of her own experiences and those of those who sit at her bakery tables.

**Additional Traits:** Ursula has a natural talent for listening and offering words of comfort, which is evident from the way she connected with young Emily after losing her mother. Her empathetic nature makes her an excellent confidante, and people from all over Ravenswood flock to her bakery seeking guidance, solace, or simply a friendly ear.
