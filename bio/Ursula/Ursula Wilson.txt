Here's a summary of the fictional character Ursula Wilson:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 5'8" (173 cm) - a slender yet imposing figure that commands attention
* Eye Color: Piercing Green - a sharp, intelligent gaze that misses nothing
* Hair: Dark Brown, Shoulder-length - often styled in a neat bob or pulled back into a ponytail for maximum impact

**Personality:**

Ursula is a force to be reckoned with, known for her quick wit, sharp tongue, and razor-sharp mind. She has a prickly exterior that hides a soft spot for those in need, making her a beloved figure in the small town of Willow Creek. Despite her tough demeanor, she has a playful side and enjoys pushing people's buttons - especially when it comes to Mr. Thompson's scone recipe.

**Interests:**

* Volunteering at the local soup kitchen with Agnes Jenkins
* Baking (especially scones!) and experimenting with new recipes
* Reading (history and philosophy) in her spare time
* Debating and arguing (often over food or baked goods)
* Laughing at Mr. Thompson's grumblings and enjoying a good pun

**Quirks:**

* Always carries a small notebook and pen to jot down witty observations or clever quips
* Has a stash of her own private recipe cards, complete with notes on her "acquired" recipes
* Can usually be found wearing a signature piece of jewelry, a bold, green-tinted brooch that adds to her already impressive presence

Overall, Ursula Evelyn Wilson is a complex, dynamic character who will stop at nothing to protect those she cares about and enjoy the finer things in life - especially scones!
