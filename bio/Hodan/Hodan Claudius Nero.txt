Here is a summary of the fictional character Hodan Claudius Nero (Hodie):

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing Blue
- Hair: Dark Brown, always impeccably styled and groomed to accentuate his sharp features.
- Build: Lean and athletic, with broad shoulders and a chiseled physique, hinting at his disciplined approach to life and fitness.

**Personality:**
Hodie exudes confidence and charisma, often bordering on arrogance. He is quick-witted and silver-tongued, making him an effective manipulator who can talk his way into (or out of) almost any situation. However, beneath the surface lies a calculating nature that fuels his ambition and strategic thinking, inherited from his illustrious lineage of influential Roman politicians.

**Interests:**
- Politics and Statecraft: Hodie is well-versed in politics and history, reflecting his family's legacy and desire to leave an indelible mark on society.
- Music: As a patron of the arts, he has a deep appreciation for opera and classical music. His admiration for Aurelia Delilah, the beautiful opera singer, brings him both joy and temptation.
- Masquerade Balls and Social Events: Hodie is a natural performer who thrives in high-stakes social situations where wit and charm are essential to success.

**Additional Insights:**
Hodie's calculating nature can be his greatest strength or weakness. While it allows him to outmaneuver opponents, it also risks making him appear manipulative or lacking genuine emotions to those around him.
