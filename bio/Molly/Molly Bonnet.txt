Here's a summary of the fictional character Molly Bonnet:

Physical Attributes: 
- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing green
- Hair Color and Style: Wild, curly brown hair with a mischievous grin

Personality:
- Enigmatic figure in the small coastal town of Ravenswood
- Highly perceptive and empathetic, able to read people and decipher hidden truths and intentions
- Curious and drawn to the unknown, often seeking out eccentric and mysterious inhabitants
- Determined and responsible when it comes to her gifts and using them for good

Interests:
- Spending hours on the beach watching sunsets and listening to whispers of the wind (believing they carry the voices of the dead)
- Investigating mysteries and uncovering hidden truths, particularly when something feels off or out of place
- Seeking guidance and advice from others when needed
- Maintaining a balance between her gifts and everyday life

Additional Details:
- Molly's parents have always warned her against delving too deep into the unknown, but she finds herself drawn to secrets and stories that only she can hear.
- As she grows older, Molly's talents become more apparent, and people begin to seek her out for advice and guidance.
