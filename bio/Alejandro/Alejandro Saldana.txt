Here's a summary of the personality, physical attributes, and interests of the fictional character Zorro:

**Personality:** Zorro is a charismatic and fearless hero with a strong sense of justice. He is cunning, resourceful, and possesses exceptional sword-fighting skills. Despite his bravery, he has a compassionate heart and is fiercely protective of those in need. As Don Diego de la Vega, he balances his dual identities as a wealthy landowner and a masked vigilante.

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
* Eye Color: Piercing blue-green eyes that seem to gleam with mischief
* Hair Color: Dark brown hair that is often worn long and tied back in a ponytail
* Build: Lean and athletic, honed from years of horseback riding and sword fighting

**Interests:** Zorro has a deep love for:

* Horse breeding and riding: He has a special affinity for his trusty steed, Tornado.
* Sword fighting: Zorro is a master swordsman, with a particular fondness for the rapido sword.
* Secret society management: As Don Diego de la Vega, he oversees a network of messengers, spies, and allies who aid him in his fight against injustice.
* Stealth and strategy: He enjoys planning and executing daring heists and battles, often using stealth and cunning to outwit his enemies.

**Additional details:** Zorro's skills extend beyond combat to include martial arts, acrobatics, and disguise. As a wealthy landowner, he has the resources and social standing to move freely among the upper classes while hiding in plain sight as Don Diego de la Vega.
