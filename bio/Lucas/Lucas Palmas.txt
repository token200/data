Here is a summary of the fictional character Lucas Palmas:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 5'10"
- Eye Color: Bright blue
- Hair Color: Dark brown, messy and often unkempt
- Facial Features: Strong jawline, sharp cheekbones, and a small scar above his left eyebrow (likely obtained during one of his many close calls while investigating)

Personality:
- Lucas is driven by an insatiable curiosity and passion for uncovering the truth.
- He is fiercely independent and has a strong sense of self-reliance, often taking on cases that others may deem too risk or challenging.
- Despite his determination and tenacity, Lucas has a soft spot for those in need and will go to great lengths to protect his sources and those he cares about.
- His sharp intellect and research skills serve him well in the pursuit of his craft, but also make him vulnerable to obsession and tunnel vision.

Interests:
- Investigative journalism
- Researching and analyzing documents and evidence
- Interviewing subjects (particularly those on the fringes or with information they're not willing to share openly)
- Photography and storytelling through images
- Uncovering hidden truths and conspiracies
- Learning about local history, folklore, and mythology

Additional Details:
- Lucas has a habit of carrying a small notebook and pen with him at all times to jot down notes and observations.
- He is often plagued by vivid dreams and nightmares that seem to be connected to his investigations.
- Despite the risks and challenges he faces, Lucas feels an unshakeable sense of responsibility to tell the stories that need to be told.
- His ambition and drive have earned him both respect and fear from those in positions of power, leading some to question whether he's truly a journalist at heart or a troublemaker with a penchant for causing chaos.
