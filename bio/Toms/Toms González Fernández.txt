Here is a short summary about the fictional character Tomás González Fernández:

**Name:** Tomás González Fernández
**Age:** Late 20s (25-27 years old)

**Physical Attributes:**

**Interests:**
* Soccer (both as a player and a coach)
* Fitness and training
* Leadership and strategy

As a former professional soccer player turned coach, Tomás brings a unique combination of physical and mental toughness to the game. His experience playing at a high level has given him a deep understanding of the sport, while his coaching background has allowed him to develop innovative strategies and tactics that inspire confidence in his players.

Despite his tough exterior, Tomás has a strong sense of compassion and empathy for those around him, particularly his players and their families. He is driven by a desire to help others reach their full potential, both on and off the field.
