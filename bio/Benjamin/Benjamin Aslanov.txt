Here is a summary of the fictional character Benjamin Aslanov:

**Physical Attributes:** Benjamin Aslanov is a ruggedly handsome man in his late 40s or early 50s. He stands at around 6'1" (185 cm) tall, with a lean and athletic build that speaks to his years as a former KGB agent. His piercing green eyes are a distinctive feature of his face, conveying a sense of intensity and sharpness. His features are chiseled, with a strong jawline and a prominent nose, giving him an air of mystery.

**Personality:** Aslanov is a man of few words, preferring to observe and listen rather than speak. This has earned him a reputation as a quiet and enigmatic individual, whose piercing green eyes can sometimes convey more information than his lips ever could. Despite this, he is fiercely loyal to those he trusts, particularly when it comes to protecting innocent lives. His years of experience as a private investigator have also honed his instincts, making him an expert at reading people and situations.

**Interests:** Aslanov's past experiences as a KGB agent have given him a deep understanding of the inner workings of the Russian government, and he is always on the lookout for opportunities to uncover corruption and bring about justice. He has a strong sense of justice and will go to great lengths to protect those who are in need. When not working on cases, Aslanov enjoys taking long walks along the Moscow River, savoring the city's atmosphere and reflecting on his own experiences.

**Skills:** As a former KGB agent turned private investigator, Aslanov has honed a range of skills that serve him well in his line of work. He is an expert at surveillance, infiltration, and hand-to-hand combat. His years of experience have also taught him how to remain calm under pressure, think on his feet, and make quick decisions in high-stakes situations.

**Motivations:** Aslanov's primary motivation is to uncover the truth and bring about justice, particularly when it comes to protecting innocent lives from those who seek to harm them. He is driven by a strong sense of morality and a desire to see wrongs righted.
