- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
- Eye Color: Bright, expressive brown eyes that sparkle with creativity

- Hair: Long, dark hair often tied up in a messy bun, framing her heart-shaped face

  - Music: She enjoys listening to Indian classical music and contemporary artists like Ravi Shankar and Bon Iver.
  - Nature: Lily finds solace in spending time outdoors, whether it's exploring forests or simply sitting by a river.
  - Family: Her close relationship with her grandmother, Nani, is particularly important to her.

**Goals:** 
- To create art that comes from a place of true passion and authenticity
- To connect with others through her work and share her story
- To stay true to herself and her roots, even as she gains recognition in the art world
