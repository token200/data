Here's a summary of the fictional character Max Petersen:

**Physical Attributes:**
Max is a 25-year-old freelance photographer with a lean and athletic build. He stands at approximately 5'10" (178 cm) tall, which allows him to easily navigate the streets of New York City while carrying his camera equipment. His hair is a dark brown color, often messy and unkempt, and his eyes are a deep shade of blue that seem to hold a warmth and kindness.

**Personality:**
Max has a compassionate and empathetic personality, always willing to lend a listening ear or offer support to those in need. He is driven by a passion for social justice and a desire to use his photography as a tool for change. Max is fiercely independent and self-motivated, having honed his skills through years of freelance work. Despite his tough exterior, he has a soft spot for underdogs and those who are struggling.

**Interests:**
Max's primary interest lies in documentary photography, which allows him to tell the stories of marginalized communities. He is particularly drawn to the lives of those living on the margins, seeking to amplify their voices and shed light on social injustices. When he's not working on a project, Max enjoys exploring the city's hidden corners, searching for new perspectives and subjects to capture.
