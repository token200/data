Here is a summary of the fictional character Max Olsson:

**Physical Attributes:** Max Olsson stands at 5'9" (175 cm) tall, with unruly brown hair and warm, honey-colored eyes that sparkle with curiosity. His nose is slightly pointed, giving him a gentle, inquisitive appearance.

He also has a passion for languages and loves deciphering codes and symbols from different cultures. Additionally, he enjoys reading (especially about history and science) and conducting research in his free time.

**Background:** Max was inspired to become a librarian by his late grandmother, Maria Olsson, who encouraged his love of learning and exploration. He has a deep respect for the past and is driven to uncover its secrets, with his grandmother's wisdom guiding him on his journey.
