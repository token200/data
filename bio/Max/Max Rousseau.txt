Here's a summary of the fictional character Max Rousseau:

**Physical Attributes:** Max is a tall and lean individual, with an unkempt mop of dark brown hair and piercing blue eyes. His facial structure is sharp, giving him an air of intensity and focus. He stands at around 6'1" (185 cm) and has a slender yet athletic build.

**Personality:** Max is a complex and intriguing individual, driven by both his desire for creativity and his need to prove himself. Despite his troubled past with a family of thieves and con artists, he has a strong sense of morality that often puts him at odds with his own ambitions. He is fiercely independent and self-sufficient, having learned to rely on no one but himself in order to survive. Max's determination and focus are unwavering, making him a force to be reckoned with in the high-stakes world of art forgery.

**Interests:** As an artist and forger, Max has a deep passion for art history and the masters, particularly Monet. He is drawn to the challenge and thrill of creating fake artworks that are almost indistinguishable from the real thing. In his free time, he enjoys experimenting with different mediums and techniques, always pushing himself to innovate and improve.
