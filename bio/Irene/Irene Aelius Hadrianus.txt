Here is a summary of the fictional character Irene Aelius Hadrianus:

**Physical Attributes:** 
- Age: Late 20s/Early 30s
- Height: Approximately 5'6" (168 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue, often described as striking and unforgettable
- Hair Color: Dark brown, usually styled in an elegant updo for official occasions

**Personality:** Irene is a confident and determined individual with a sharp mind and quick wit. She has a strong sense of self-awareness, knowing that she doesn't always align with her husband's views or actions but remains committed to her own goals and ambitions.

**Interests:**
- Politics: Irene has a deep understanding of imperial politics and its intricacies. She is well-read on historical events and maintains an extensive network of contacts within the palace.
- History: Irene holds great interest in learning about Rome's past, having spent countless hours studying historical documents and attending lectures by prominent historians.
- Philosophy: She has a philosophical bent, often engaging with intellectuals and scholars to discuss their ideas and perspectives.

**Additional Traits:** 
- Resilience: Despite facing many challenges as the wife of Emperor Trajan, Irene proves remarkably resilient and adaptable.
- Loyalty: Irene is fiercely loyal to her family and brother, particularly Aelius Severus.
