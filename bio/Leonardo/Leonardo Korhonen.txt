Here is a summary of the fictional character Leonardo Korhonen:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
* Eye Color: Piercing blue
* Hair Color: Dark brown, often styled impeccably
* Facial Features: Chiseled jawline, sharp cheekbones, and a small nose

**Personality:**

* Confident and Charismatic: Leonardo exudes an air of superiority, making him a compelling figure in the art world.
* Complex and Multi-Layered: Beneath his polished exterior lies a web of insecurities and fears that drive him to succeed at any cost.
* Ambitious and Risk-Taking: Leonardo is not afraid to take risks, often pushing himself to new heights in both business and personal endeavors.
* Introspective and Analytical: He has a keen eye for value and talent for negotiation, allowing him to navigate the complex world of art dealing.

**Interests:**

* Art History: Leonardo's fascination with art began at a young age, and he spent years studying the works of the Old Masters.
* Painting: Despite his success in art dealing, Leonardo still enjoys painting as a hobby, often finding solace in the creative process.
* Poker and High-Stakes Games: He has become addicted to the thrill of high-stakes poker, which adds an element of excitement and risk to his already driven personality.

**Background:**

* Childhood: Born in a small town in Finland, where he developed his passion for art from an early age.
* Early Life: Raised with a strong work ethic and appreciation for the arts, setting him on the path to become one of the most respected art dealers in Europe.
* Career: Built a reputation as a master of manipulation and deception in the art world, eventually amassing a fortune that rivals some of the wealthiest families in Europe.
