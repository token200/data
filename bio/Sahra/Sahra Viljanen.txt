Personality:
Sahra Viljanen is a determined and resourceful individual who has built her career as a journalist around uncovering the truth. She is fiercely independent and can come across as aloof or standoffish to those who don't know her. However, this exterior hides a complex web of emotions and motivations that she carefully guards. Sahra is driven by a strong sense of justice and will stop at nothing to expose corruption and bring perpetrators to account.

Interests:
- Investigative journalism
- Politics and government
- Psychology and human behavior
- Philosophy and ethics

Skills:
- Exceptional research and analytical skills
- Strong interview techniques
- Proficient in multiple languages, including Finnish, Swedish, and English

Background:
Sahra grew up in a small Finnish town where she became entangled with Juhani, a charismatic stranger who vanished without a trace when she was a teenager. Her past experiences have shaped her into the driven and resourceful journalist she is today.

Weaknesses:

- Her past relationships, particularly those with people like Juhani, leave her vulnerable to emotional manipulation and hurt.

Strengths:
- Unwavering commitment to her work
- Quick thinking and sharp instincts in high-pressure situations
- Ability to navigate complex social dynamics and build strong relationships
