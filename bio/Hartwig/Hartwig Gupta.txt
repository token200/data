He is an avid reader of dusty books and geography texts, often getting lost in the stories and histories they contain. In his younger years, he was drawn to the world beyond his small town in India, sparking a lifelong curiosity about cultures and technologies.

Despite his initial struggles with loneliness, Hartwig's interests have led him to find solace in research and exploration. His love of solitude has slowly given way to a desire for connection, particularly through his relationship with Sophia, an artist who brings light and life into his world.
