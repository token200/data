Here's a summary of the fictional character Saga Szabó:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5'9" (175 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue, reflecting her Hungarian heritage
- Hair: Dark brown, long and flowing like the steppes she rides across
- Build: Lean and athletic from years of horseback riding and sword fighting
- Skin Tone: Weathered and sun-kissed from spending countless hours outdoors

**Personality:**
Saga is a fearless and determined leader, with a strong sense of duty and loyalty to her country. She's fiercely independent but has a deep connection to those around her, particularly her brother József. Her tough exterior hides a vulnerable side, as she struggles to cope with the loss of her loved one. Saga is resourceful and intelligent, able to think on her feet in high-pressure situations.

**Interests:**
- Horse riding and horsemanship
- Sword fighting and hand-to-hand combat
- Gathering intelligence and conducting espionage
- Military strategy and tactics
- Spending time outdoors, particularly on the steppes where she grew up

As a member of the Hungarian cavalry, Saga Szabó is driven by her duty to protect her people and defend her homeland. With courage in her heart and steel in her hand, she's ready to face whatever challenges come her way, always remembering the sacrifices made by those who fought for their freedom before her.
