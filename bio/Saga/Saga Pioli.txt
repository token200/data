She possesses a childlike enthusiasm for discovery and is not afraid to ask tough questions or challenge conventional wisdom. Saga's determination and perseverance make her a formidable researcher and collector.

- Studying ancient civilizations, such as Egypt and Greece
- Exploring Mediterranean history and mythology
- Collecting rare and mysterious objects
- Reading about archaeology and historical events

- She has an innate ability to connect seemingly disparate pieces, recognizing patterns and relationships that others may overlook.
