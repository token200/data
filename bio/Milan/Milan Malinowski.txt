Here is a summary of the fictional character Milan Malinowski:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5'11" (180 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue, with an intense sparkle that seems to bore into those he meets.
- Hair Color and Style: Dark brown, messy, and often unkempt, framing a sharp, angular face.

**Personality:**
Milan is a charismatic and confident individual who embodies an air of confidence that borders on recklessness. He exudes an aura of mystery and authority, making him both feared and revered by those around him. His calm and collected demeanor allows him to remain level-headed even in the most high-pressure situations, giving him an edge over his competitors.

**Interests:**
- Rare Books and Poetry: Milan has a weakness for rare books and poetry, which are said to provide him with solace and inspiration during his missions.
- The Underworld: He is deeply connected to the underworld of New Haven, navigating its hidden corners with ease and relishing the thrill of the chase.

Despite his mysterious nature, there seems to be an underlying passion and curiosity that drives Milan forward. His love for rare books and poetry suggests a creative side, which contrasts with his high-stakes profession as a thief.
