- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)

- She is fiercely devoted to her family and community, always putting their needs before her own.
- Her entrepreneurial spirit and kindness have made her a beloved figure in San Juan de los Ángeles.

- Valdis loves baking and running her own bakery, "Sabor y Amor," where she can share the joy of delicious treats with others.
- She is passionate about giving back to her community and has participated in numerous charity events and volunteered at local schools.
- As a mentorship program leader for young women, Valdis inspires and guides them on their life journeys.

**Additional Details:**
- Valdis's husband, Juan, adores her for her unwavering dedication to family and community.
