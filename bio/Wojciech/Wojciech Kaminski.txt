Here's a summary of the fictional character Wojciech Kaminski:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 6 feet (183 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often worn in a neat, stylishly messy manner
- Facial Features: Strong jawline, prominent nose, and piercing eyes that seem to bore into those he interviews

Personality:
- Confident and reserved, Wojciech exudes an air of quiet authority that commands attention without demanding it.
- Highly analytical and observant, Wojciech's keen mind allows him to piece together seemingly unrelated details into a coherent picture.
- His calm demeanor belies a sharp wit and a quick intellect, making him both effective and intimidating in equal measure.

Interests:
- Investigation and solving puzzles
- Reading literature and learning about history
- Playing chess and other strategy games

Additional Details:
- Wojciech is a late thirties detective with an established reputation for solving complex cases.
- He has an intellectual curiosity that drives him to delve into the world of local business and politics in order to unravel the mystery of his latest case.

Wojciech's calm demeanor, combined with his exceptional analytical skills, allow him to approach even the most seemingly impossible cases with confidence.
