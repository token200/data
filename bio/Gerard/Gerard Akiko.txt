Here is a summary of the fictional character Gerard Akiko:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 6'1" (185 cm) - a tall and imposing figure
* Eye Color: Piercing green eyes with an intense, piercing quality that misses nothing
* Hair: Dark brown, short, and neatly trimmed, often messy from his intense focus on cases
* Build: Lean and athletic, honed from years of rigorous physical training as a Special Forces operative

**Personality:**

* Enigmatic and mysterious, Gerard is a man of few words but speaks volumes with his actions
* Haunted by past traumas, he guards his emotions fiercely and can come across as aloof or detached to those who don't know him
* Highly intelligent and resourceful, Gerard is a master strategist with quick thinking and sharp instincts
* Driven by a strong sense of justice, Gerard will go to great lengths to uncover the truth and bring closure to his clients

**Interests:**

* Investigation and problem-solving are Gerard's greatest passions, whether it's tracking down suspects or unraveling complex mysteries
* Avid reader and researcher, Gerard is always on the lookout for new information and insights that can aid him in his cases
* A lover of whiskey and good music, Gerard has a refined side that few get to see beyond his tough exterior

**Background:**

* Former Special Forces operative turned private investigator, Gerard has seen it all and been through hell and back
* Struggles with emotional scars from past traumas, Gerard has built walls around himself to protect those close to him from getting hurt
