Here is a summary of the fictional character Sofie Sten:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Age: Late 20s
- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
- Eye Color: Dark Brown with hints of green, inherited from her family's Scandinavian roots.
- Hair: Long, curly brown hair that often falls in loose waves down her back.
- Build: Lean and athletic, reflecting her outdoor upbringing in the forest.

**Personality:**
Sofie is a complex and determined individual. She possesses a strong sense of independence and resilience, shaped by her experiences growing up in Ravenswood. Her deep connection to nature and the old gods instills within her a sense of purpose and curiosity about the world beyond her family's traditions. As she navigates the mysterious occurrences in Ravenswood, Sofie displays courage and resourcefulness, able to think on her feet and trust her instincts.

**Interests:**
- Nature and the outdoors: Growing up in Ravenswood has instilled within Sofie a deep respect for the natural world and its secrets.
- Family history and mythology: Her family's dark past and connection to the old gods make Sofie particularly fascinated by exploring the mysteries of her ancestors' lives.
- Mystery and investigation: After years of searching for answers about her family, Sofie is well-equipped to tackle puzzles and unravel enigmas.

**Additional Details:**
Sofie carries a worn leather knife sheathed at her belt, a token from her father who taught her how to defend herself. She has an intricate tattoo on her left shoulder blade, symbolizing her bond with the old gods. Despite her tough exterior, Sofie harbors a deep sense of vulnerability and empathy for those around her, which often drives her actions in pursuit of truth and justice.
