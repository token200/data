Here is a summary of the fictional character Carla Kiss:

**Personality:** Carla is a complex and enigmatic individual with a reputation for being elusive. She has a razor-sharp mind and a dry sense of humor, which she uses to keep people at arm's length. Despite her tough exterior, she is intelligent and perceptive, often sizing others up before deciding whether to engage with them. However, beneath her guarded demeanor lies a deep well of emotional pain and vulnerability, stemming from her traumatic experiences as a former CIA operative.

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 5'8"
* Eye Color: Piercing green
* Hair: Raven-black, cascading down her back like a waterfall of night

**Interests:** Carla is a bookworm who finds solace in literature by the sea. She enjoys watching seagulls soar overhead and sipping wine as the sun dips into the ocean. In her quieter moments, she reflects on her past and contemplates the nature of purpose and redemption.

**Additional Details:** As a former CIA operative, Carla has a wealth of experience and skills that have allowed her to navigate some of the darkest corners of the globe. However, this expertise comes at a steep price, one that she is still grappling with in her present life. Despite her efforts to keep a low profile, strangers will occasionally stumble upon her, sensing an aura of mystery and warning them away with their unsettling gaze.
