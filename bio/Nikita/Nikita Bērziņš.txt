Born into a family of modest means, Nikita was drawn to espionage as a young man for thrills and adventure. This unique approach has made him a valuable asset in his freelance operative career.

- Bringing down corrupt officials and rival agents
- Serving justice, even if it means going against the establishment

Overall, Nikita Bērziņš is a complex and enigmatic figure, driven by a strong sense of justice and a need for adventure. His exceptional skills in espionage and his unwavering commitment to truth make him a formidable opponent in the world of covert operations.
