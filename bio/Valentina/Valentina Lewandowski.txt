Here is a summary of the fictional character Valentina Lewandowski:

Valentina stands at approximately 5'8" (173 cm) with long, curly brown hair and bright hazel eyes. Her sharp features are accentuated by her small nose and full lips, giving her a determined yet approachable look.

Valentina's passions lie in baking, community building, and social justice. She is an avid gardener and envisions a thriving community garden in the outskirts of Willow Creek, where locals can grow their own fruits and vegetables while fostering connections with one another. Her bakery "Sweet Treats" is more than just a business - it's a hub for creativity, connection, and joy.
