- Philosophy and Spirituality: After Takashi's words resonated with him, Ali has become fascinated by the intersection of martial arts and spiritual growth. He devotes time to studying ancient texts, meditating, and exploring the mysteries of the universe.

- Born to a Japanese mother and an unknown father, Ali's early life was marked by survival on the streets of Tokyo's underworld, earning him the nickname "Ghost."
- Raised in a dojo under Takashi's guidance, Ali discovered discipline and purpose through martial arts.
- The cryptic message on the wall sparked Ali's curiosity, leading him down a rabbit hole of research and self-discovery.

Overall, Ali Hiroshi is a complex and intriguing character, driven by a desire to uncover the secrets within himself. His journey from "Ghost" of the underworld to seeker of truth and wisdom has only just begun.
