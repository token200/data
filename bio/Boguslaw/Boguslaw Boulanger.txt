Boguslaw Boulanger is a charismatic and resourceful individual. Boguslaw has a dry sense of humor and enjoys playing pranks on those around him, often leaving subtle clues or hints that suggest his nocturnal exploits. Despite his bravado, he is fiercely loyal to the people of Ashwood and will go to great lengths to keep them safe.
