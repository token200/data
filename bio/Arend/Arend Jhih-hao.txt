Here is a summary of the fictional character Arend Jhih-hao:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 6'2" (188 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing emerald green eyes that seem to gleam with an inner intensity
- Hair: Dark brown, often worn in a simple yet elegant style

Personality:
Arend is a complex and enigmatic figure, whose stoic exterior hides a deep sense of vulnerability. He is driven by a strong sense of justice and honor, which compels him to use his powers for the greater good. Beneath his calm demeanor lies a troubled soul, haunted by the memories of atrocities he witnessed during his training. Despite this, Arend remains resolute in his commitment to protecting the balance between nature and humanity.

Interests:
- Martial arts: Arend is an expert in Tai Chi, which was passed down to him from his father.
- Elemental manipulation: He has devoted himself to mastering the mystical art of harnessing the power of the elements.
- Philosophy and spirituality: Arend seeks guidance in ancient texts and philosophies, seeking wisdom on how to wield his powers responsibly.
- Nature: As a guardian of balance between nature and humanity, Arend is deeply connected to the natural world and values its preservation.

Other details:
Arend is said to possess supernatural abilities that allow him to wield the elements with precision. He is known for his bravery, honor, and unwavering commitment to justice and righteousness. His legend has inspired countless young warriors to follow in his footsteps, making him a beacon of hope in a world where power often comes at the expense of others.
