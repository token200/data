Here is a summary of the fictional character Engel Shu-fen:

**Physical Attributes:**
Engel Shu-fen stands at 5'8" (173 cm) with an athletic yet slender build, allowing her to move effortlessly through crowded streets. Her striking physical presence is complemented by piercing green eyes that seem to see right through you, a trait she inherited from her ancestors. Her raven-black hair is often styled in a neat, high ponytail, framing her heart-shaped face and accentuating her sharp cheekbones.

**Personality:**
Engel Shu-fen exudes an air of quiet confidence and intensity, which can sometimes be misinterpreted as aloofness by those who don't know her. However, beneath her enigmatic smile lies a deep well of compassion and empathy, driving her to solve cases that might otherwise go cold. Her razor-sharp intellect and natural curiosity make her a formidable opponent in the world of criminology. Engel is fiercely independent and non-conformist, often challenging authority when she believes it's necessary.

**Interests:**
Engel Shu-fen has a keen interest in esotericism, particularly ancient Taiwanese folk religions and mysticism. This fascination stems from her family background as street vendors who were influenced by local myths and legends. She is also an avid reader of true crime literature and enjoys puzzles, which she believes have helped her develop exceptional analytical skills.

**Background:**
Engel Shu-fen's upbringing in a humble family of street vendors instilled in her the value of hard work and resilience. Her natural curiosity and aptitude for criminology led her to pursue a career in law enforcement, earning her top honors from the National Police University. As "The Ghost Investigator," Engel has become synonymous with solving cases that baffle others, cementing her reputation as one of Taiwan's most respected detectives.

**Quirks:**
Engel Shu-fen often carries a small notebook and pen with her, jotting down notes and observations during interviews or when investigating crimes. She also has a habit of lighting incense sticks in her office to help her focus and connect with the world around her. Her fondness for esoteric literature and mystical practices is evident in her eclectic fashion sense, often incorporating symbolic accessories into her outfits.
