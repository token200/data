Here is a summary of the fictional character Engel Piotrowski:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
- Eye Color: Hazel with golden flecks
- Hair: Dark brown, often worn in a practical and low-maintenance style that suits her academic lifestyle.

Personality:
Engel is a complex and multi-faceted individual with a mix of introverted and extroverted traits. On the surface, she presents herself as prickly and guarded, using her wit to deflect people who get too close. However, beneath this tough exterior lies a deeply emotional and vulnerable person who has built walls around herself to protect against pain and trauma.

Interests:
Engel's primary interests are centered around history, particularly Poland's turbulent past. She is an avid reader and researcher, often devouring books and documents on the subject. Her love of cats is also well-known among her colleagues, with Dr. Kowalski jokingly referring to them as her "fellow historians."
