Here is a summary of the fictional character Engel Fournier:

**Physical Attributes:** Engel stands at approximately 6'2" (188 cm) with an athletic yet wiry build. His eyes are a piercing blue, reflecting his deep connection to the natural world he inhabits. His hair is dark brown and often unkempt, framing his weathered face. A thick beard covers most of his chin, adding to his rugged demeanor.

**Personality:** Engel Fournier is a complex individual with a soft heart hidden beneath his rugged exterior. He is fiercely independent and has built walls around himself over the years, but once you gain his trust, he becomes a loyal and caring mentor. His love for nature and the creatures that inhabit it drives him to protect and preserve the land. Despite his solitary life, Engel has a deep capacity for compassion, often taking in stray animals and children in need.

**Interests:** Engel's primary interests revolve around living off the land, hunting, and trapping. He is skilled in preserving food through smoking and curing meats, which allows him to survive on his own terms. Additionally, he has a passion for learning about medicinal plants and their uses, often experimenting with concoctions to aid in his survival. Engel also enjoys sharing stories of his adventures and listening to others' tales, particularly those of Sophie, who brought light and companionship into his life.

**Additional Traits:** Engel's years of solitude have made him intensely perceptive, able to read the signs of nature and detect subtle changes in his surroundings. He possesses a quiet confidence and self-reliance that serves him well in his isolated world. Despite his rugged exterior, Engel has a deep appreciation for the beauty of the natural world and its capacity to nurture and heal those around him.
