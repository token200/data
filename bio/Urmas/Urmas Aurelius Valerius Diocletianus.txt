Urmas is an ambitious, driven individual who exudes confidence and determination. His natural intelligence and quick wit make him a formidable opponent in any intellectual or strategic debate. His unwavering dedication to excellence and unshakeable loyalty often put him at odds with those who would seek to undermine his authority.

**Interests:** 
Urmas is deeply fascinated by history, particularly the rise and fall of great empires. He has an extensive knowledge of philosophy, particularly Stoicism, which heavily influenced his worldview.
As a skilled strategist, Urmas enjoys studying military tactics, analyzing successful campaigns and identifying areas for improvement.
He also possesses a passion for literature, often devouring works on politics, history, and ethics to expand his understanding of the world.

**Additional Traits:** 
Urmas is an early riser and begins each day with a ritualistic practice session, focusing on physical exercise and mental discipline. This habit instills him with discipline and focus.
As an astute politician, Urmas has developed remarkable skills in negotiation, able to strike delicate balances between opposing parties and interests.
