Álvaro's reserved nature allows him to observe those around him with precision, often sensing details that others miss. Despite his calm exterior, he has a strong sense of justice and will not hesitate to confront secrets or uncover hidden truths.

**Interests:**
Álvaro is deeply devoted to the world of art, spending most of his days exploring galleries, museums, and studios in search of inspiration. He is particularly drawn to the technicalities of art, finding solace in the intricate details that separate masterpieces from mere creations. Álvaro's love for Kandinsky's work is evident in his own artistic endeavors, though they remain a secret known only to him.
