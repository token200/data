Here is a summary of the fictional character Pēteris Sandström:

Pēteris is a soft-spoken yet fiercely independent individual. He values hard work and dedication to his craft, often putting the needs of others before his own. Despite his reserved nature, he has a deep passion for music and is willing to share his knowledge with those who are willing to learn.

Interests:
- Music: Pēteris has an incredible love for music, particularly the traditional melodies of Riga's Old Town.
- Woodworking: He is skilled in the art of woodworking and takes great care in crafting each instrument.
- Storytelling: Pēteris enjoys sharing the stories behind his creations, allowing listeners to connect with the music on a deeper level.
- Traditional Craftsmanship: He values the traditional methods of luthiery, passed down through generations, and strives to honor this heritage in his work.

Additional Details:
- Age: Late 30s
- Education: Formed an apprenticeship under a respected master luthier, honing his skills over several years.
- Work Environment: His workshop is nestled between cobblestone streets of Riga's Old Town, filled with the scent of wood and music.
