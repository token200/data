Iveta is an enigmatic and calculating leader, embodying the strategic intellect and cunning that defined her ancestors. This dichotomy allows her to navigate complex situations with poise, forging strong relationships with various factions in Aldovia.

Interests:
Iveta has a profound appreciation for the collective knowledge of her ancestors, drawing upon their wisdom to inform her decisions. She is particularly interested in understanding the intricacies of human hearts and motivations, believing that this insight holds the key to maintaining balance within Aldovian society.

Skills:
- Highly skilled diplomat
- Expert strategist
- Proficient in multiple magical disciplines
- Fluent in various languages, including Latin, Aldovian dialects, and several other mystical tongues
