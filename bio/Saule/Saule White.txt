Here's a summary of the fictional character Saule White:

Her dark hair is often styled in a messy bob that accentuates her sharp features. She has a small tattoo of a snake coiled around her right wrist, which she obtained during her street-smart youth.

Her piercing green eyes seem to see right through others, making them feel like they're under scrutiny. Despite this, she exudes an aura of confidence and intelligence that puts those around her at ease. She's fiercely independent and determined, often walking into treacherous situations with a sense of calm determination.

**Interests:** Saule has always been fascinated by the world of mystery and detection. As a child, she devoured detective novels and watched old episodes of Columbo, which sparked her interest in solving puzzles and uncovering secrets. As an adult, she's spent years honing her skills as a detective, studying human psychology, and developing her own methods for solving complex cases.

Saule is also a thrill-seeker who enjoys the rush of adrenaline that comes with taking on new challenges. She's a voracious learner, always seeking to improve her knowledge and stay ahead of the curve in the rapidly changing world of detection.

**Habits and Quirks:** Saule often has a habit of tapping her fingers impatiently when she's deep in thought or trying to piece together a puzzle. She also tends to lose herself in her own thoughts, often neglecting her surroundings and appearing lost in her own world. When working on a case, she's notorious for going without sleep or food, fueled by her insatiable curiosity about the truth.

Despite these quirks, Saule is deeply compassionate and empathetic towards those affected by crime, always seeking to uncover justice rather than personal gain.
