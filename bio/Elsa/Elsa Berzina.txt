Here is a summary of the fictional character Elsa Berzina:

**Physical Attributes:** Elsa Berzina stands at 5'8" (173 cm) tall with striking blue-green eyes that sparkle like the Latvian forests she loves to paint. Her hair is a rich, dark brown color that falls in loose waves down her back. She has a petite yet athletic build, which allows her to move freely and easily as she sets up her easel in the city square.

**Personality:** Elsa is a creative and confident individual who wears her heart on her sleeve. She is passionate about her art and Latvian culture, and this enthusiasm shines through in every brushstroke. Despite struggling with self-acceptance and control issues related to her "magical" powers as a child, Elsa has grown into a poised and compassionate young woman who uses her talents to bring joy to others.

**Interests:** As an artist, Elsa is deeply interested in capturing the essence of Latvian culture through her paintings. She draws inspiration from folklore, mythology, and the natural beauty of Latvia's landscapes. In addition to art, Elsa enjoys spending time outdoors, exploring the forests and coastlines that surround Riga, and appreciating the local music scene.
