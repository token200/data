Alessandro "Ash" Aber is a driven, intelligent, and fiercely independent individual. Ash is also deeply empathetic and can form connections with people from all walks of life, which helps him navigate complex cases. Despite his tough exterior, he has a dry sense of humor and can be witty when the situation calls for it.

Ash's primary interest lies in solving mysteries and unraveling unsolved crimes, particularly those connected to Italian history and culture.
