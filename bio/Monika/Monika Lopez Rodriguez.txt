**Personality:**

Monika is a charismatic and empathetic individual who radiates warmth and kindness. Her sharp wit and infectious laughter make her a beloved fixture in the Olvera neighborhood, where she has built a reputation as a passionate advocate for social justice and community empowerment. She is an active listener, always seeking to understand the stories and struggles of those around her, and is not afraid to confront tough topics with courage and compassion.

**Interests:**

* Art (particularly mural-making and street art)
* Social Justice activism
* Community organizing and building partnerships with local groups
* Listening to and sharing the stories of marginalized voices
* Celebrating Chicana culture and heritage
