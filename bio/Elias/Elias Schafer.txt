Here is a summary of the fictional character Elias Schafer:

**Physical Attributes:** Elias Schafer stands at approximately 6'1" with a lean yet athletic build. His piercing green eyes seem to bore into those he meets, giving him an intense and mysterious presence. His dark brown hair is always neatly trimmed, and he sports a subtle scruff of stubble that adds to his rugged charm.

**Personality:** Elias Schafer is a man of quiet confidence and unyielding determination. He has a reputation for being one of the best private investigators in the business due to his sharp mind and keen instincts. Despite his tough exterior, Elias has a dry sense of humor and can come across as slightly introspective or even aloof to those who don't know him well.

**Interests:** As a former detective turned private investigator, Elias Schafer is naturally drawn to solving complex cases and unraveling mysteries. He is intensely curious and has a passion for gathering clues and piecing together seemingly unrelated information. In his free time, Elias enjoys long walks in the rain, old movies, and whiskey-tasting at local bars.

**Additional Details:** Elias's background as a detective has given him an unwavering commitment to justice and a strong sense of morality. He is fiercely independent and values his alone time above all else. Despite this, he has a deep loyalty to those he cares about, particularly his colleagues and friends like Detective Rachel Kim.
