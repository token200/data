Here is a summary of the fictional character Martina Gómez Pérez:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Age: Late 20s/Early 30s

Martina is also a perfectionist, pouring her heart and soul into every project she undertakes. Her drive and dedication have earned her recognition within the art community, and she continues to push the boundaries of ceramics with innovative techniques.

**Interests:**
- Pottery and ceramics
- Nature and mythology (inspiration for her artwork)
- Traveling and exploring new cultures
- Spending time in her studio, experimenting with new materials and techniques
