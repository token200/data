Here is a summary of the fictional character Endo Lemoine:

**Physical Attributes:** 
- Age: Late 20s/Early 30s
- Height: 5'10" (178 cm)
- Eye Color: Deep, expressive brown eyes that seem to hold a world of emotion within them.

**Personality:** 
- Struggles with vulnerability and opening up about his past due to deep-seated emotional scars.
- Passionate and creative, but stifled by the mundanity of his life.
- Haunted by traumatic events from his early twenties that have left an indelible mark on his psyche.
- Longs for artistic expression, yet fears failure or not meeting expectations.
- Possesses a dry sense of humor and can be introverted at times.

**Interests:** 
- Art (painting, drawing) - the driving force behind Endo's passion and creative fire.
- Music (plays guitar as a hobby, finds solace in melancholic melodies).
- Literature (admirer of writers like Franz Kafka and Edgar Allan Poe who explore themes of isolation and creativity).

**Additional Notes:** 
- Despite his introverted nature, Endo is drawn to people who genuinely care for him, particularly Sophia, with whom he forms a strong bond.
- A lingering sense of guilt and self-doubt stemming from past failures weighs heavily on his conscience, but he's beginning to see a light at the end of the tunnel.
