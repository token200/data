When not tracking down fugitives or taking on bounty work, he's likely to spend time alone, riding through the desert under star-studded skies.

His only other indulgence seems to be collecting rare and exotic plants, which he studies and records in a worn leather journal.

**Motivations:** For Endo del Toro, his primary motivation is justice – not just for others, but also for himself. He's driven by a personal sense of revenge against those who have wronged him or broken the law.
