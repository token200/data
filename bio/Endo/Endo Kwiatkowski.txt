Born to a family of wandering performers and mystics, Endo was raised with an innate sense of wonder and curiosity. Her name "Endo Kwiatkowski" reflects her inner strength and resilience, passed down from her ancestors who navigated treacherous lands and uncertain futures.

As she developed her magical abilities, Endo's fascination with the mysterious arts grew, driving her to explore the unknown and challenge conventional wisdom. Now, as a respected wizard in her own right, Endo continues to forge her own path, weaving magic that resonates with those around her.
