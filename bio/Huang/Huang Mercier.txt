* Height: 5'10" (178 cm)

* Quick-witted and charming, able to think on his feet and talk his way out of most situations
* intensely curious, driven by a desire to uncover the truth and solve the puzzle
* Emotionally reserved, with a tendency to keep people at arm's length due to past betrayals

* Cryptic puzzles and enigmas, which he believes hold the key to understanding the world
* Reading and research, devouring books on everything from forensic science to philosophy
* Martial arts, particularly Tai Chi and Wing Chun, which help him stay focused and centered
