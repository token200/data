She is also deeply interested in spirituality and the intersection of medicine and philosophy. Her dedication to her craft extends beyond the confines of her clinic, as she seeks to use her influence and wealth to establish hospitals and clinics throughout the empire, providing medical care to those who need it most.

**Additional Details:** Halima's full name, while initially seen as cumbersome by some, has become a symbol of her authority and confidence.
