Goals: 
Lucie's ultimate goal is to safeguard the sacred teachings she was given by Master Wong and share them with others, guiding them on their own journeys towards balance and inner peace.

Through her journey as Lucie Chun-yi, it becomes clear that this character embodies a rare blend of physical prowess, spiritual depth, and compassion.
