As someone who has spent their life on the streets, he has a deep appreciation for the beauty in unexpected places.

**Skills:** Alain is an expert thief, able to navigate the city's underworld with ease. He's also resourceful and quick-witted, often using his cunning to outsmart his enemies or extricate himself from sticky situations.

Despite his rough past, Alain has a deep sense of loyalty and justice, which motivates him to help those in need – especially Colette, whom he loves and wants to protect.
