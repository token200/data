However, beneath his composed exterior lies a deeply empathetic person who genuinely cares about others and is driven by a strong sense of justice.

Interests: As a child, Mikołaj was fascinated by foreign lands and cultures, which later became a defining aspect of his career as a diplomat. He has an insatiable thirst for knowledge, with a particular interest in history, geography, languages, and psychology. When not working, Mikołaj enjoys practicing martial arts to maintain physical discipline and mental focus.
