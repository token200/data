Anita Mustermann stands at approximately 5'6" (168 cm) with a sturdy build, reflecting her hardworking nature. Her dark brown eyes sparkle with warmth and kindness, putting those around her at ease. Anita's primary interests revolve around baking and sharing this love with others. When not in the kitchen, Anita can be found exploring the surrounding woods or tending to her small garden, where she grows herbs and flowers used in her recipes.
