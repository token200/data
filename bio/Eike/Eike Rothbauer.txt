Eike Rothbauer's interests are varied and multifaceted, reflecting his background as an archaeologist and his current life as a master thief. As an archaeologist turned con artist, Eike Rothbauer's interests also extend to forensic science, cryptography, and code-breaking. He is a voracious reader, devouring books on psychology, philosophy, and history whenever he has the chance.
