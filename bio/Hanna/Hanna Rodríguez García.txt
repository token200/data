Here is a summary of the fictional character Hanna Rodríguez García:

Physical Attributes:
- Age: 70 years young
- Height: 5'2" (157 cm)
- Eye Color: Warm, hazel eyes with golden flecks, reminiscent of her love for sunshine and gardening.

Personality:
Hanna is a warm, caring, and fiercely protective matriarch who has seen it all. She exudes determination and a strong sense of community, having raised multiple generations and passed down family traditions. Her twinkling eyes convey a wealth of wisdom, compassion, and love for her family's culinary heritage.

Interests:
- Gardening: Hanna spends her days tending to her lush garden, where she cultivates tomatoes and lemons that are the envy of the town.
- Cooking: She is renowned for her "abuela's" secret recipe guacamole and has spent years perfecting her craft. Hanna finds joy in sharing family recipes and traditions with her loved ones.
- Family History: As a keeper of family secrets, Hanna is passionate about preserving their culinary heritage and sharing stories of her ancestors' lives.
- Activism: When faced with the threat to Sofía's kitchen, Hanna springs into action, using her love for tradition and community to rally support and ensure its preservation.

Overall, Hanna Rodríguez García embodies warmth, determination, and a deep love for her family, heritage, and traditions.
