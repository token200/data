Here's a summary of the fictional character Hanna Chia-wei:

Hanna is of Taiwanese descent, but her parents immigrated from mainland China. She has long black hair and expressive brown eyes that light up with excitement when she's investigating a new story. Her height is around 5'6" (168 cm), which allows her to navigate the crowded streets of Taipei with ease.

Hanna is an ambitious, determined individual who is passionate about telling stories that matter. She has a sharp mind and quick wit, which serves her well in her career as a journalist. Despite the challenges she faces, Hanna remains committed to her passion for storytelling and is driven by a desire to share the voices of those who need to be heard.

Interests:
Hanna's fascination with Taiwan's indigenous people stems from a deep appreciation for their culture and history. She spends countless hours researching and interviewing local tribespeople, working tirelessly to bring their stories to life. Her interests extend beyond her work as a journalist, and she enjoys exploring the natural beauty of Taiwan, trying new Taiwanese cuisine, and learning about the island's rich cultural heritage.

Hanna is fluent in Mandarin Chinese, which she learned growing up in Taipei. She also has a working knowledge of the indigenous languages spoken by various tribes in Taiwan, such as Amis and Thao. Her experience living in Taipei and her connections with local communities have given her a unique perspective on the complexities of Taiwanese society.
