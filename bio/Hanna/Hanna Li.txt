Here's a summary of the fictional character Hanna Li:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 5'6" (168 cm)
* Eye Color: Dark brown, almost black
* Hair Color: Jet-black, often styled in a messy bob that frames her heart-shaped face
* Skin Tone: Olive, reflecting her Beijing heritage
* Build: Slender and agile, with toned muscles developed from years of parkour training

**Personality:**

* Hanna Li is confident, resourceful, and calculating. As a skilled thief, she has honed her abilities to think on her feet and stay one step ahead of her opponents.
* She has a quick wit and can be charming when needed, often using her charisma to distract or deceive those around her.
* Despite her tough exterior, Hanna Li has a soft spot for the underdog and is fiercely loyal to those she considers friends.
* Her drive for success and reputation as a master thief fuels her passion, sometimes leading her to take calculated risks.

**Interests:**

* Parkour and martial arts training
* Street art and graffiti
* Antique collecting (especially rare silk fans)
* High-stakes heists and puzzles to solve
* Reading historical texts on Chinese culture and mythology

Hanna Li's interests often intersect with her profession, fueling her fascination with the underworld and its hidden secrets. Her love of parkour and martial arts training keeps her agile and quick-witted, essential skills for a thief like herself.
