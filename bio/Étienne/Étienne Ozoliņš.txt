Étienne Ozoliņš is a complex individual with a rich history and a penchant for mystery. He exudes an air of quiet confidence, tempered by a deep-seated need for connection and belonging. However, upon meeting Sofia, Étienne begins to open up and share aspects of himself that he had long kept hidden.

Interests:

He finds solace in the world around him, particularly when engaging with like-minded individuals who share his passions. The small café where they first met serves as a catalyst for his rediscovery of himself, allowing him to connect with others on a deeper level.
