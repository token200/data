Here is a summary of the fictional character Étienne Piotrowski:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5'10" (178 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue, almost indigo, which seem to sparkle with curiosity and intensity.
- Facial Features: Strong jawline, prominent nose, and piercing eyes, all of which are set off by a wild shock of curly brown hair that often escapes his tidy beard.

**Personality:** Étienne is an introverted yet passionate individual who has dedicated his life to the art of taxidermy. He is meticulous, methodical, and intensely focused on his craft, which can sometimes make him appear aloof or detached to those outside his sphere of interest. However, once you get to know him, he reveals a dry sense of humor and an infectious enthusiasm for sharing his knowledge and stories about the natural world.

**Interests:** 
- Taxidermy, naturally
- Birds, particularly rare species
- Forest ecology and conservation
- Storytelling through art (especially in the context of taxidermy)
- Learning from others, especially apprentices eager to learn under his guidance.
 
Étienne's drive is fueled by a love for storytelling, nature, and human connection. His unique approach to taxidermy allows him to weave compelling narratives that inspire people to care more deeply about wildlife conservation.
