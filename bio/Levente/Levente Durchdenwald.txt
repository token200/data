- Botany: Levente has an extraordinary knowledge of plants and their healing properties.
- Folklore: He is well-versed in the traditional stories and legends of Schnitzelhausen's history.
- Natural History: The creatures of the wild, including animals, insects, and even plants, are of great interest to him.

Overall, Levente Durchdenwald is a complex and multifaceted individual whose reserved exterior hides a deep wellspring of wisdom and knowledge.
