Here's a summary of the fictional character Fabio White:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue
- Hair: Dark brown, often styled in a fashionable, yet effortless manner
- Build: Chiseled and athletic, showcasing his modeling career
- Facial Features: Strong jawline, sharp cheekbones, and a small nose

**Personality:**
- Complex and sensitive soul beneath a charming exterior
- Struggles to connect with others on a deeper level due to past experiences
- Attracted to emotionally unavailable or toxic individuals, often prioritizing their relationships over his own well-being
- Initially guarded and private, but slowly opening up as he gets to know Luna

**Interests:**
- Acting and modeling (his profession)
- Art and philosophy (discovered through the art class with Luna)
- Self-discovery and personal growth
- Exploring new experiences and connections with others, particularly those who see beyond his public persona
