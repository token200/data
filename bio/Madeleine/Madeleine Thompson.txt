As a freelance journalist, she is always on the lookout for unique perspectives and compelling characters to tell their stories. She is particularly drawn to artists, musicians, and loners who inhabit the margins of society, sensing in them a deep wellspring of creative potential.

Her interests extend beyond journalism to include art, history, and self-discovery. Madeleine finds solace in the process of observation, listening to the whispers of forgotten histories etched on weathered wooden signs, and exploring the vast expanse of human experience that her subjects embody.
