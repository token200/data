Kiara is a creative and curious individual with a passion for art history and traditional techniques. Kiara's grandmother had a profound influence on her, instilling in her a love of storytelling and the appreciation of historical context. Her grandmother's stories about Prague's Old Town and its Gothic spires continue to captivate her imagination, sparking creative ideas that inform her own artistic practice.

Kiara is 25 years old, having recently graduated from art school in Prague. She has a background in art history and has worked on several projects involving historical restoration and preservation.
