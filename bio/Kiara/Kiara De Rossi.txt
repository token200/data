Here's a summary of the fictional character Kiara De Rossi:

**Physical Attributes:** Kiara is an Italian-American woman with a fiery personality to match her physical appearance. She stands at 5'6" (168 cm) with curly, dark hair and bright hazel eyes that sparkle with mischief. Her smile can light up a room, and her sharp jawline and full lips add to her striking features.

**Personality:** Kiara is a force to be reckoned with - a passionate and outspoken individual who isn't afraid to speak her mind. She has a quick wit and a sharp tongue that can cut down even the toughest opponents, making her a formidable opponent in any debate. Her fiery personality often gets her into hot water with friends and family, but she can't help it; she's a passionate person through and through.

**Interests:** Kiara is a foodie at heart, especially when it comes to traditional Italian cuisine. She takes great pride in her culinary creations, particularly her signature Neapolitan pizzas, which are infused with love, care, and a dash of sass. Her love for cooking is matched only by her love for animals, particularly strays and misfits. This quirky sense of style extends to her fashion choices, often incorporating vintage finds into her wardrobe.

**Additional Traits:** Kiara has a soft spot for stray animals and takes in a handful of rescue dogs at the pizzeria, making her a beloved figure among locals. Her infectious enthusiasm and wicked sense of humor have won over even the toughest critics, including Leo, the stranger who walks into Bella Vita one fateful evening.

Overall, Kiara De Rossi is a whirlwind of energy, passion, and unapologetic love - a true original who lights up the small town of Willow Creek with her presence.
