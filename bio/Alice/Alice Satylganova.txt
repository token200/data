Despite her impressive credentials, Alice remains somewhat of an outsider among her peers, preferring the company of machines to people.

**Motivations:** Alice's primary motivation lies in unlocking the secrets of human consciousness and creating a new era of intelligence that can benefit humanity as a whole.
