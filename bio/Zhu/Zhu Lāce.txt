Zhu Lāce stands at approximately 5'9" (175 cm) with an athletic yet lean build. His hair is usually short and straight, often worn in a simple style, while his eyes are a deep shade of brown that seem to hold warmth and wisdom beyond his years.

Personality:
Zhu Lāce is known for being wise and compassionate, traits that set him apart from his peers even among the renowned warriors. His selflessness and dedication to protecting those in need have inspired generations to follow in his footsteps. Despite his martial prowess, he remains humble and kind-hearted, always willing to lend a listening ear or offer guidance when needed.

Interests:
As a member of the Zuo clan, Zhu Lāce was well-versed in traditional arts such as calligraphy, poetry, and archery. However, his true passion lay in sharing his knowledge with others, often taking time out from battle to teach and mentor young warriors. He held a deep respect for nature and spent much of his free time meditating among the misty mountains, seeking guidance from within himself.
