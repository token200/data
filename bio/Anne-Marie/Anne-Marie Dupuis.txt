Here is a summary of the fictional character Anne-Marie Dupuis:

**Physical Attributes:** Anne-Marie stands at 5'8" with warm, honey-blonde hair and bright blue eyes that sparkle with kindness. Her smile can light up a room, and her effortless elegance belies her down-to-earth personality.

**Personality:** Anne-Marie is an effervescent individual with an infectious laugh and unwavering kindness. She exudes warmth and hospitality, making everyone feel welcome in her bakery, "Sweet Delights." Despite the chaos that can ensue when dealing with rowdy customers, she remains patient, composed, and genuine.

**Interests:** Anne-Marie's passion for baking is only rivalled by her love of sharing stories, spreading joy, and connecting people. She finds great satisfaction in running her own business, experimenting with new recipes, hosting bake sales for charity, and mentoring young bakers. Outside the bakery, she enjoys taking long walks along the Willow Creek riverbank, reading cookbooks, and practicing yoga to unwind.

In summary, Anne-Marie is a vibrant, kind-hearted baker who embodies the spirit of community, warmth, and generosity. Her bright personality shines through in every interaction, making her beloved by her customers and cherished by those around her.
