Stefan is also meticulous, patient, and dedicated to preserving the history of mechanical devices.

- Restoring old clocks to their former glory
- Studying the intricacies of timekeeping mechanisms
- Sharing knowledge about antique clocks with enthusiasts

Stefan's love for antique clocks is not limited to collecting; he also spends his free time experimenting with new clockmaking techniques and collaborating with fellow collectors to authenticate rare finds. His shop, filled with an assortment of unique timepieces, serves as a haven for those seeking something truly special.

The chance encounter between Stefan and Sophia showcases his ability to connect people through the shared passion of antique clocks, making him a beloved figure in the world of horology.
