Hannah's past experiences have left emotional scars, but they also fuel her drive to protect others.

**Interests:**

- Investigating mysteries and solving crimes (taking on a personal investigation for Emily's disappearance)
- Spending time with close friends like Lily, who supports her in times of need
- Supporting the local community by providing a welcoming space for residents to gather and socialize at "Grab's Grub"
