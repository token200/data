Here is a summary of the fictional character Hannah Gómez Pérez:

Hannah stands at 5'8" (173 cm) tall and has long, dark brown hair that often falls in loose waves down her back. Despite her tough exterior, Hannah has a compassionate heart and is always willing to listen to others and consider different perspectives.

Hannah's interests are centered around social justice, human rights, and investigative journalism. She is particularly passionate about exposing corruption and injustice in her community, and she spends countless hours researching and investigating stories that have the potential to make a real difference. In her free time, Hannah enjoys reading (especially non-fiction and literature from Latin American authors), practicing yoga, and spending time with friends and family.

Hannah was born and raised in a small town in Mexico, where she grew up surrounded by the struggles of everyday people who were often overlooked or marginalized. This experience instilled in her a deep empathy for others and a strong sense of responsibility to use her voice to make a positive impact on the world. She is fluent in Spanish and English, having studied journalism in college and working as an intern at a local newspaper before landing her current job.
