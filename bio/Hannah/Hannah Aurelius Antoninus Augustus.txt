Here is a summary of the fictional character Hannah Aurelius Antoninus Augustus:

**Personality:** Hannah is a free-spirited and ambitious individual who values knowledge and wisdom above all else. She is driven by her passion for writing and literature, and is not afraid to challenge conventional expectations and follow her heart. Despite being born into a life of luxury, Hannah is humble and down-to-earth, with a strong sense of empathy and compassion for others.

* **Build:** Petite but athletic, with a slender figure that belies her sharp intellect and quick wit

**Interests:**

* Writing (particularly poetry and prose)

* Music (Hannah enjoys playing the lyre and singing softly to herself when alone)
* Travel (Hannah longs to explore the world beyond Rome, to learn from different cultures and meet new people)
* Nature (she finds solace in spending time by the Tiber River or wandering through gardens and parks)
