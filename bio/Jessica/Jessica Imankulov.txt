Despite her tough exterior, Jessica has a compassionate side, particularly when it comes to understanding the stories and histories of ancient civilizations.

Jessica's interests revolve around archaeology, history, and the natural world. Jessica also has a passion for photography, using her camera to capture the beauty and majesty of the landscapes she encounters.
