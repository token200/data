Here's a summary of the fictional character Solveig Pelz:

**Physical Attributes:** Solveig is a petite woman with a slender build and an unassuming presence. She stands at approximately 5'2" (157 cm) with long, dark brown hair that falls in loose waves down her back. Her bright hazel eyes sparkle with warmth and kindness, and her small nose is slightly upturned, giving her an endearing appearance.

**Personality:** Solveig is a compassionate and empathetic person who wears her heart on her sleeve. She has a warm smile that puts those around her at ease, and her gentle demeanor makes even the most troubled individuals feel comfortable opening up to her. Despite her own tumultuous past, Solveig is resilient and determined, using her struggles as fuel for her passion in helping others.

**Interests:** Beyond baking, which she finds therapeutic and fulfilling, Solveig has a deep love for cooking, experimenting with new recipes, and exploring the rich flavors of different cultures. She is also an avid listener and values meaningful connections with people from all walks of life. In quieter moments, Solveig enjoys taking long walks through Ravensburg's picturesque streets, observing nature, and collecting rare ingredients for her baked goods.

As the owner of a thriving bakery in Ravensburg, Solveig has created a sanctuary where people can find solace and comfort in the midst of chaos. Her ability to connect with others on a profound level, combined with her passion for baking, makes her an extraordinary individual who leaves a lasting impact on those around her.
