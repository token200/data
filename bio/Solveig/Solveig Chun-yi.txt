**Interests:**

Solveig's interests are centered around using her skills for good, rather than just personal gain. She is driven by a desire to uncover the truth and bring justice to those who have been wronged.

It's worth noting that Solveig's past experiences have left emotional scars, making it difficult for her to open up to others or fully connect with them. However, as she forms connections with like-minded individuals, she is slowly beginning to heal and find a new sense of purpose in her life.
