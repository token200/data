Here is a summary of the fictional character Chang Oja:

**Physical Attributes:** Chang Oja is a young woman with a tall and slender build. She stands at approximately 5'8" (173 cm) and has long, dark brown hair that falls in loose waves down her back. Her eyes are a deep shade of indigo, almost black, but with a sparkle of warmth and kindness. Her skin has a warm, golden undertone from spending countless hours outdoors.

**Personality:** Chang Oja is a compassionate and wise individual, known for her ability to listen carefully to the needs of others and use her knowledge of herbs and medicine to diagnose and treat illnesses. She is humble and grounded, having been chosen by the spirits to be their messenger and continue the teachings of the ancient Mongolians. Despite her importance as a healer and spiritual leader, she remains approachable and down-to-earth, with a strong sense of empathy and connection to those around her.

**Interests:** Chang Oja is deeply interested in traditional Mongolian medicine and spirituality, which has been passed down through generations of shamans in her family. She is also passionate about the natural world and living in harmony with nature, which is reflected in her daily routines of gathering herbs and firewood for ceremonies. Her travels take her across vast expanses of grassland and mountain ranges, where she meets people from all walks of life and learns about their cultures and traditions.

**Other notable traits:** Chang Oja wears a traditional deel, a long robe adorned with intricate patterns and symbols believed to hold spiritual power, which is passed down through generations of Mongolian shamans. She carries a staff made from a sacred tree, said to grant her the power to heal even the most grievous wounds. Her connection to the spirits of the land and her people is strong, and she uses these relationships as guidance in her healing work and spiritual practices.
