Here is a summary of the fictional character Chang Ruiz García:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 5 feet 11 inches (180 cm)
- Eye Color: Deep, piercing emerald green
- Hair: Dark brown, messy, and often unkempt
- Build: Lean and athletic, with broad shoulders and a chiseled physique

Personality:
- Intelligence: Highly analytical and logical, with exceptional problem-solving skills
- Confidence: Confident to the point of being cocky, but with a hint of humility
- Emotional Control: Highly self-controlled, able to keep emotions in check even under pressure
- Determination: Unwaveringly determined to solve cases and bring justice to those wronged

Interests:
- History: Fascinated by the town's history and the evolution of San Juan del Río over time
- Cryptography: Enjoys deciphering codes and ciphers, often spotting patterns that others miss
- Investigation: Thrives on investigation and analysis, often immersing himself in case files and evidence
- Puzzle-Solving: Avidly enjoys solving puzzles and brain teasers outside of his cases

Background:
- Little is known about Chang Ruiz García's past, but rumors suggest a troubled childhood and an early interest in detective work
- Extremely resourceful and able to think on his feet, often coming up with creative solutions to complex problems
- Highly respected by the townspeople for his exceptional skills as a detective, though he prefers to keep a low profile

Quirks:
- Often wears worn leather gloves, which seem to be a good luck charm or a habit formed during one of his early investigations
- Has an affinity for strong, bold colognes and can often be smelled in the vicinity of his office or on the streets where he works
