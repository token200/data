Therese Kalnins exudes an aura of quiet confidence and determination, which has earned her respect among her peers. Therese's passion for forensic science has become all-consuming, fueling her insatiable curiosity about human behavior and motivation. Despite this, Therese maintains a sharp mind and an unwavering dedication to justice, driven by an innate sense of fairness.
