Here is a summary of the fictional character Alarik Harris:

- Height: 6'2" (188 cm)
- Eye Color: Warm Hazel with golden flecks
- Hair Color: Dark Brown, often worn long and messy

Interests:
- Restoring antique furniture and artifacts
- Clockwork mechanisms and their intricate workings
- Music boxes with delicate porcelain ballerinas
- Victorian-era items, particularly music boxes and cabinets
- Exploring history through old documents and photographs

While Alarik may be primarily focused on his craft of restoration, he has an innate curiosity about the world around him, making him a well-rounded individual.
