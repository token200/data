Despite her rising fame, she remains humble and down-to-earth, always staying true to herself and her art. Céline is also deeply introspective, often finding solace in the quiet moments spent walking along the Daugava River or lost in thought.

Interests:
Céline has a deep love for folk music, particularly the songs of Bob Dylan and Joni Mitchell, which inspired her to pick up an instrument at a young age.
