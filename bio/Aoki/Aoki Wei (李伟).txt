Here is a summary of the fictional character Aoki Wei:

**Name:** Aoki Wei (also known as Lee Weiping in Chinese)

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
* Eye Color: Piercing brown eyes with a subtle golden undertone, reminiscent of his Chinese heritage.
* Hair: Dark, wavy hair that often fell across his forehead, adding to his introspective demeanor.
* Build: Lean and wiry, with a quiet strength that belied his intense focus on art.

**Personality:**

* Brilliant and reclusive, Aoki was a private person who kept his personal life out of the spotlight.
* Complex web of relationships and experiences shaped by his Chinese heritage, which he often reflected in his artwork.
* Fiercely loyal and intensely generous to those he considered friends or mentees.
* Struggled with feelings of guilt-ridden relationship with his heritage, as evident in his artwork "Phoenix Rising."

**Interests:**

* Art: Aoki's passion for art was all-consuming, driving him to experiment with different techniques and mediums.
* Calligraphy and woodblock printing (influenced by his summers studying in Japan as a teenager).
* Sculpture (particularly bronze), which became the medium of choice for his enigmatic and thought-provoking works.
* Cultural identity: Aoki's artwork often explored themes of memory, displacement, and cultural identity, reflecting his complex relationships with his Chinese heritage.

Overall, Aoki Wei was a unique individual whose art and personality reflected his fascinating blend of East and West.
