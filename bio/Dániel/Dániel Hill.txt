Here is a summary of the fictional character Dániel Hill:

**Physical Attributes:** 
- Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often styled neatly and neatly trimmed

**Personality:** 
Dániel is known for his quiet strength, unwavering dedication to justice, and confidence that belies his troubled past. He is a hard worker with a strong sense of determination, which has driven him from humble beginnings to become one of Budapest's most successful investigators. His personality is deeply rooted in loyalty and responsibility towards those he cares about, particularly his daughter Lili.

**Interests:** 
Dániel's interests revolve around solving mysteries and uncovering the truth, whether it be a case or simply understanding more about the world around him. As an investigator, he dedicates most of his time to his work, but when free from duty, he enjoys spending quality time with his loved ones, particularly Lili.
