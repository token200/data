Here is a summary of the fictional character Ben Jhih-hao:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5'10" (178 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing brown eyes with a hint of intensity, reflecting his focus and dedication to his work.

**Personality:**
Ben is an introverted and highly driven individual consumed by his passion for geology. His relentless pursuit of knowledge makes him almost oblivious to the world around him, leading others to perceive him as invisible. He possesses unwavering determination and a strong analytical mind, traits that propel him toward groundbreaking discoveries.

**Interests:**
- Geology and Paleontology
- Uncovering Hidden Secrets in Ancient Landscapes

As revealed in the story, Ben's life of discovery takes an unexpected turn when he uncovers evidence suggesting someone or something has tampered with his research. This realization sets off a chain of events that forces him to confront the unknown and question everything he thought was true about his work.

Mei, the enigmatic woman who is watching over Ben, plays a pivotal role in this transformation. She is driven by secrets hidden within the Taiwanese mountains for centuries, which Ben's discovery threatens to expose.
