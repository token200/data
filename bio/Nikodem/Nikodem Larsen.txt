Nikodem's natural leadership skills, strategic mind, and bravery have earned him the respect and admiration of many.

Nikodem Larsen is passionate about justice and freedom. He spends countless hours researching and analyzing the government's tactics, looking for weaknesses to exploit. His interests include:
