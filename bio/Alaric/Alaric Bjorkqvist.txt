Here is a summary of the fictional character Alaric Bjorkqvist:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
- Eye Color: Dark blue, almost indigo
- Hair Color: Gray-brown, often combed neatly to cover thinning patches at the temples
- Build: Lean and athletic, a testament to his years of physical training in the intelligence agency

**Personality:**
- Alaric is a complex individual with an unassuming exterior that belies a deeply emotional and motivated person.
- He has a tendency to internalize his feelings and emotions, which can make it difficult for those close to him to gauge his true state.
- As revealed through his experiences, he has a dry sense of humor and can be quiet in moments of introspection.

**Interests:**
- After being retired from the intelligence agency, Alaric found himself fascinated by art, spending hours wandering through galleries and museums to find inspiration.
- Painting became an integral part of his life as a hobby and creative outlet after Lena encouraged him to explore it further.
- He has a keen eye for detail, which was honed during his years in the intelligence agency where he gathered intel from seemingly insignificant details.
