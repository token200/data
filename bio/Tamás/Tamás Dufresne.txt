Here's a summary of the fictional character Tamás Dufresne:

**Physical Attributes:**
Tamás stands at 5'10" (178 cm) tall and has piercing green eyes. His dark brown hair is often styled in a messy, artistic manner, reflecting his creative upbringing. He has a lean build, honed from years of martial arts training, which he uses to his advantage as The Stargazer.

**Personality:**
Tamás Dufresne is a passionate and curious individual with an insatiable desire for knowledge. As a young boy, he was driven by wonder and excitement, often spending hours gazing up at the stars. This curiosity has carried over into adulthood, fueling his career in physics research. However, beneath the surface lies a thrill-seeker's personality - Tamás loves taking risks and pushing boundaries, both as The Stargazer and in his personal life.

**Interests:**
Tamás's true passion is astronomy and astrophysics. He spends countless hours studying celestial mechanics, learning about black holes, and exploring the mysteries of the universe. In his free time, he enjoys stargazing and attending astronomy lectures. As The Stargazer, Tamás combines his scientific knowledge with martial arts skills to create a unique crime-fighting style.

When not fighting crime or working on research projects, Tamás is likely to be found reading about physics or exploring the city under the cover of darkness, searching for new patterns and connections to use in his crime-fighting endeavors.
