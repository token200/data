Here is a summary of the fictional character Tamás Corberó:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 6 feet (183 cm)
* Eye Color: Dark brown
* Hair Color: Light brown, often worn long and slightly unkempt
* Build: Lean and athletic, developed from years of manual labor in the vineyard

* Humble and unassuming, preferring to focus on the quality of his wine rather than seeking attention for himself
* Intelligent and curious, always seeking ways to improve and innovate in his winemaking techniques
* Emotionally driven by memories of his late grandmother, who first introduced him to viticulture

**Interests:**

* Winemaking and viticulture (obviously!)
* Experimenting with new grape varieties and techniques
* Reading books on wine history, philosophy, and science
* Spending time outdoors, tending to the vineyard and enjoying the beauty of nature
