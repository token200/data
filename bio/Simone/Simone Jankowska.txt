Here is a summary of the fictional character Simone Jankowska:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing green
- Hair Color: Raven-black
- Face Shape: Heart-shaped
- Build: Sleek and athletic, indicating her profession as a detective

Personality:
Simone is intelligent, determined, and fiercely independent. She has a strong sense of justice and will go to great lengths to uncover the truth. Her piercing green eyes seem to hold secrets that only she knows, making her an enigmatic figure. Despite being a renowned detective, Simone is not afraid to show vulnerability and can be moved by those around her, as evident in her reaction when discovering her sister's undercover operation.

Interests:
- Investigating crimes and uncovering the truth
- Solving puzzles and mysteries
- Uncovering corruption and bringing justice to those who have been wronged
- Spending time with family (as revealed in the story, Simone values her relationships with her loved ones)

Background Information:
Simone's sister Sofia is an undercover operative working to bring down corrupt officials. The mysterious informant who sends Simone the cryptic message is Sofia herself, using a clever ruse to get Simone involved in the case.

Skills:
- Exceptional detective skills
- Expert markswoman and hand-to-hand combatant
- Highly intelligent and resourceful

Goals:
- To uncover corruption and bring justice to those who have been wronged
- To protect her family and loved ones
- To continue advancing her career as a renowned detective
