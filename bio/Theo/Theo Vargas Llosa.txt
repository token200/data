**Personality:**

Theo "El Ingeniero" Vargas Llosa is a brilliant and driven individual with an unyielding passion for engineering. He is fiercely dedicated to his work and has an insatiable curiosity about the world around him. As he grew older, however, his intense focus on his craft began to take a toll on his personal relationships and overall well-being. Despite this, Theo remains optimistic and determined, always striving to find a balance between his work and personal life.

**Interests:**

* Engineering: Theo is deeply passionate about engineering and spends most of his free time reading about new technologies and innovations.
* Environmentalism: As a strong advocate for sustainable energy, Theo is committed to reducing Peru's carbon footprint and improving the lives of its citizens through innovative solutions.
* Teamwork: Despite his introverted nature, Theo values collaboration and camaraderie with his colleagues, particularly Sofia, Carlos, and Maria, who form a tight-knit support network that helps him navigate life's challenges.
* Personal growth: As he navigates this turning point in his life, Theo is deeply interested in self-reflection, personal development, and finding healthy ways to manage stress and maintain work-life balance.

**Additional Traits:**

* Resourceful: Theo is known for his creative problem-solving skills and ability to think outside the box.
* Loyal: Once you've earned his trust, Theo will go to great lengths to support and protect his friends and allies.
