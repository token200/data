Interests:
Mikolaj's primary interest is crafting intricate mechanical devices, particularly clockwork mechanisms. He seems to find solace and satisfaction in the precise work of bringing these creations to life. Additionally, he values honesty, hard work, and self-discipline, often putting these principles above personal gain or recognition.

Habits:
Mikolaj's calm demeanor is characterized by moments of quiet reflection, usually accompanied by a subtle smile that hints at his wit and inner strength. Despite being a master craftsman, he doesn't seem to enjoy the spotlight; instead, he prefers to let his work speak for itself.
