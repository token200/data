Here's a summary of the fictional character Dace Smith:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 6'2" (188 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing Blue
- Body Type: Muscular, athletic build honed from years of professional wrestling
- Facial Features: Chiseled jawline, sharp cheekbones, and a nose slightly crooked, giving him a rugged and intimidating appearance.

**Personality:**
- Ruthless and unpredictable in the ring, Dace has no qualms about using underhanded tactics to get ahead.
- Haunted by his troubled childhood, he struggles with anger management and emotional regulation outside of competition.
- Shallow relationships and shallow connections, often leaving him feeling isolated and disconnected from others.
- Despite this, he possesses a fierce determination and unyielding dedication to his craft.

**Interests:**
- Professional wrestling (of course!), where Dace can channel his emotions and frustrations into something tangible.
- Physical training and self-improvement, as he continually seeks to optimize his body and mind for the ring.
- The mystery surrounding "The Patron" and their true intentions drives him forward in the tournament.

**Additional Details:**
- Injuries from past matches have left scarring on his arms, torso, and face, which he sometimes covers with bandages or prosthetics when not competing.
- Dace has a habit of tapping into his emotional turmoil to fuel his performances, often experiencing a heightened sense of adrenaline in the ring.
