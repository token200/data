- Stealth and Strategy: As a skilled thief, Dace enjoys planning and executing complex heists that push her to be one step ahead of her adversaries.
- Collecting Rare Artifacts: With her extensive collection, including the Starlight Seren, Dace is always on the lookout for new pieces to add to her treasure trove.
