Here's a summary of the fictional character Noah Thompson:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5'10"
- Eye Color: Hazel
- Hair Color: Dark Brown with hints of Cinnamon
- Build: Lean and athletic, honed from years of working on the farm.

**Personality:**
Noah is a dreamer at heart, always chasing after some fantastical notion or another. He's fiercely independent and values his freedom above all else. Despite this, he has a strong sense of family loyalty and would do anything to protect those he cares about. Noah is introspective and can come across as reserved, but once you get to know him, he opens up and reveals a dry sense of humor and a deep love for poetry.

**Interests:**
Noah's passions include reading Walt Whitman's poetry, writing in his journal, and exploring the world beyond his small town. He has a deep appreciation for nature and dreams of traveling and experiencing new things. Noah is also drawn to music and often finds solace in the lyrics of old folk songs.

While he may not be content with the traditional farm life that his family expects of him, Noah feels a strong connection to his roots and the land that's been passed down through generations. He hopes to find a way to balance his desire for adventure with his sense of responsibility to his family and community.
