Here is a summary of the fictional character Pauline Morel:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 5'2" (157 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing green
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often worn in a simple yet elegant style
- Face Shape: Heart-shaped with high cheekbones and a pointed chin

Personality:
- Confident and quiet, but with an air of mystery that keeps people at bay
- Intelligent and resourceful, able to navigate complex situations with ease
- Emotionally guarded, having built walls around herself to protect from potential hurt or betrayal
- Intensely loyal to those she cares about, particularly her sister Madeleine

Interests:
- History, particularly World War II and the French Resistance
- Art and antiques, with a particular interest in rare and valuable pieces
- Investigation and research, often delving deep into complex cases to uncover hidden truths
- Physical activity, enjoying long walks through Parisian neighborhoods like Montmartre
- Music, particularly jazz and classical, often found solace in the sounds of these genres

Background:
- Little is known about Pauline's past, but it is clear that she was deeply affected by the loss of her sister Madeleine during World War II
- Her involvement with the French Resistance has become the stuff of legend, sparking rumors and speculation about her role within the group
- Pauline's search for her sister has driven many of her choices and alliances, often leading to complicated relationships and difficult decisions.
