Élise's intelligence and strategic thinking make her a formidable leader, feared by those who oppose her and respected by her allies.

Despite her tough exterior, Élise has a compassionate heart, driven by a strong sense of justice and equality. She is fiercely loyal to those she cares about and will stop at nothing to protect them.

Élise's interests are multifaceted, reflecting her diverse upbringing in the provinces of Illyria and her tutelage under the Roman senator Marcus Tullius Cicero.

* Politics: As a skilled politician, Élise is constantly analyzing the intricacies of Rome's governance and seeking ways to improve its systems and promote social change.
* History: Her Illyrian heritage runs deep, and Élise takes great pride in preserving her family's legacy and honoring their bravery and cunning.
