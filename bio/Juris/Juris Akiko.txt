Here's a summary of the fictional character Juris Akiko:

- Height: 6 feet (183 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing brown
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often kept short and neatly trimmed
- Facial Features: Strong jawline, sharp cheekbones, and a small scar above his left eyebrow (resulting from a past injury)

**Personality:**

Juris Akiko is a man of few words but exudes confidence and authority. His presence commands attention due to his commanding stature, piercing gaze, and an air of quiet intensity. Despite being reserved, he has a strong sense of justice and will not hesitate to intervene when witnessing injustice or harm.

**Interests:**

Juris Akiko is deeply passionate about protecting people, particularly women who have been victimized by violence and harassment. He has a personal connection to this cause due to the loss of someone close in a similar situation. This motivation drives his desire to work as a security consultant, using his skills to ensure clients' safety.

Outside of work, Juris Akiko appears to be a private individual with limited interests beyond his professional pursuits. However, he seems to have an affinity for solitude and introspection, often lost in thought as he navigates the complexities of life.
