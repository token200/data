* Height: 6'2" (188 cm)

As the CEO of his family's fashion label, he has a strong sense of responsibility and vision for the brand's future. Leo is passionate about sustainability and innovation in the fashion industry, often pushing boundaries to create truly remarkable designs.

**Interests:**

* Fashion design and innovation
* Sustainability and eco-friendly practices in fashion
* Italian culture and history
* Music (Leo has a deep appreciation for classic opera and contemporary jazz)
* Traveling and exploring new destinations

Overall, Leo Chiatti is a well-rounded individual with a strong sense of purpose and passion. His dedication to his craft and commitment to making a positive impact on the industry have earned him widespread respect and admiration.
