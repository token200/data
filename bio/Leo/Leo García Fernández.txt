Here's a summary of the fictional character Leo García Fernández:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
- Eye Color: Warm hazel eyes that seem to hold a mix of brown and green hues, a characteristic inherited from his ancestors.
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often styled neatly for professional occasions but can be messy when he's working on projects.

Personality:
Leo is a driven individual with a strong sense of responsibility. He has grown into a successful businessman in his late 30s, yet still retains the curiosity and love for exploration seen in his childhood days. He possesses great determination and conviction, which was evident in his decision to make San Miguel proud and bring its charm to his business ventures.

Interests:
- Business Development: Leo is passionate about expanding small businesses into thriving tourism industries. His drive for innovation has led him to explore various opportunities and invest in ventures that support sustainable growth.
- Family Legacy: The stories shared by his abuela and his mother, Ana, sparked a renewed connection with the town's rich history. He aims to continue this legacy while fostering the next generation of leaders.
- Exploration: Growing up in San Miguel instilled in Leo a deep love for exploration. He spent countless hours exploring the narrow streets as a child, much like he still explores his business ideas today.

In summary, Leo García Fernández combines his professional aspirations with a connection to his family's heritage and his hometown of San Miguel, shaping him into an ambitious leader who wants to bring positive change while respecting tradition.
