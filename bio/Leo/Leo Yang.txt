Here's a summary of the fictional character Leo Yang:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5'9" (175 cm)
- Eye Color: Bright, piercing blue that seems to sparkle with curiosity and intelligence
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often tied back in a messy bun when working on intricate clockwork mechanisms
- Skin Tone: Olive-toned, hinting at Leo's East Asian heritage

**Personality:**
- Inquisitive and fiercely curious, Leo is driven by an insatiable passion for understanding the intricacies of time and the universe.
- His enthusiasm is infectious, drawing people to his shop like a magnet, where he can share his knowledge and love for horology.
- A bit eccentric and quirky, Leo's laughter is soft and soothing, often accompanied by the whirring of machinery in the background.
- He possesses an unwavering confidence that borders on intuition, which guides him as he unravels the secrets of The Whispering Hourglass.

**Interests:**
- Watchmaking and horology are his true loves, with Leo dedicating most of his life to mastering this craft and uncovering its mysteries.
- Astrology and astronomy also captivate him, and he sees parallels between celestial movements and the workings of timepieces.
- The study of ancient languages and mystical traditions fuels his quest for knowledge about The Whispering Hourglass.
- Music – particularly ethereal sounds and whispers – plays a significant role in Leo's connection to The Whispering Hourglass, as if it resonates with an otherworldly harmony.

**Quirks:**
- A habit of muttering to himself when lost in thought or working on complex puzzles
- Leo often gets so engrossed that he forgets about his surroundings and the world outside his workshop
- He has a weakness for rare and unusual clockwork mechanisms, which can lead him down rabbit holes of curiosity
