Here is a summary of the fictional character Finn Klein:

**Physical Attributes:**
Finn Klein is a tall and imposing figure, standing at around 6'2" with a strong build. His eyes are a deep, piercing blue that seem to bore into those he looks at, giving him an air of intensity and authority.

**Personality:** Despite his tough exterior, Finn has a compassionate heart and a soft spot for those who have been marginalized or oppressed. He is fiercely determined and resilient, having grown up on the streets of New Haven after his parents' tragic death. This experience taught him to navigate harsh realities with ease, but also fueled his determination to make a difference in people's lives. Finn is fearless, confident, and unapologetic, making him a formidable detective who will stop at nothing to see justice served.

**Interests:** Finn's interests are deeply rooted in his passion for social justice and helping those in need. He is drawn to cases involving human trafficking, exploitation, and abuse, as he believes that these crimes require special attention and expertise. On a personal level, Finn values honesty, integrity, and fairness, which guides his decisions both in and out of the courtroom.

Overall, Finn Klein is a complex and multifaceted character with a unique blend of toughness and compassion, making him an effective and empathetic detective who will stop at nothing to protect those who cannot defend themselves.
