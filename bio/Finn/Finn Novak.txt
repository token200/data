Here is a summary of the fictional character Finn Novak:

Physical Attributes:
Finn stands at 6'1" with an athletic yet slender build. His piercing blue eyes seem to be his most striking feature, radiating warmth and intelligence. His dark brown hair is often messy and unkempt, adding to his relaxed and approachable demeanor. A small scar above his left eyebrow suggests a past adventure, while his bright smile can light up a room.

Personality:
Finn is known for his fearless attitude and quick wit, which makes him a beloved figure in the community of Ravenswood. Despite his reserved nature, he has a way of drawing people to him with his sharp mind and infectious enthusiasm for books. He is an expert on everything from literature to music, making him a go-to resource for locals and travelers alike.

Interests:
Finn's passion for literature knows no bounds. He spends most of his days surrounded by dusty tomes in his bookstore, "Finn's Fictional World." When not recommending books or engaging in lively debates with customers, Finn enjoys exploring the world of poetry, music, and storytelling. He is particularly fond of classic literature, 1960s folk music, and has been known to serenade himself with guitar on quiet evenings.

Some additional details that were not mentioned in the original story:

* Finn's love for words and language led him to study linguistics in college, which further honed his skills as a book selector and advisor.
* In his free time, Finn enjoys practicing yoga and taking long walks through Ravenswood's picturesque streets, often getting lost in thought and observation.
* Despite his quiet nature, Finn has a secret talent for writing poetry and short stories himself, though he keeps them locked away from prying eyes.
