As he navigates this pivotal moment in his life, Finn is learning to be more confident and open-minded.

Interests: Music has always been a significant part of Finn's life. He spent countless hours teaching himself to play the guitar as a teenager and even formed a band with friends. When he's not working, you can find Finn strumming chords, writing lyrics, or attending concerts. His love for music is genuine, and it brings him immense joy and fulfillment.

As he embarks on this new chapter in his life, Finn is eager to explore different genres of music and collaborate with like-minded musicians. He's drawn to the idea of creating music that has a positive impact on people's lives – making music that touches hearts resonates deeply with him.
