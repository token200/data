Here is a summary of the fictional character Isla Virtanen:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
- Eye Color: Pale blue, reminiscent of the Finnish sky
- Hair: Dark brown, often tied back in a messy bun
- Skin Tone: Porcelain-like complexion with a subtle warmth from spending time outdoors

**Personality:**
Isla is a introspective and creative individual who has learned to navigate her emotions through art. She is haunted by a deep-seated silence that threatens to consume her, but has found solace in expressing herself through fabric, thread, and color. Despite this, she is determined to move forward, weaving the silence into something new and transforming it into a language of its own.

**Interests:**
- Textile art and embroidery
- Family history and traditions
- Nature and the Finnish countryside
- Photography
- Exploring Helsinki's hidden corners and streets

It can be noted that Isla has an artistic side, she is creative and finds her solace in making stuff like textiles.
