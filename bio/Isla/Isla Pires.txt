Despite her tough exterior, Isla has a soft spot for those in need and is not afraid to take risks when it comes to protecting others. Her confidence and self-assurance are traits that serve her well in both her personal and professional life.

**Interests:** Isla's interests are deeply rooted in her experiences on the streets of Rio de Janeiro. She has a passion for pickpocketing and thievery, not out of malice, but as a means to survive and eventually thrive. As she navigated the city's underworld with Luiz, she became fascinated by the art of deception and the thrill of the unknown. Today, Isla is drawn to activities that challenge her skills and test her resolve, such as parkour and urban exploration.
