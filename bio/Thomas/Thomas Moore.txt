Thomas Moore is a driven and meticulous individual with an insatiable curiosity about the unknown. Despite his focus on work, Thomas Moore has a dry sense of humor and can be witty and charming when he wants to be. Thomas Moore's interests are deeply rooted in the paranormal and unexplained phenomena. Thomas Moore comes from a family with a rich history of paranormal investigation. This legacy has driven Thomas to follow in his grandmother's footsteps, and he takes pride in carrying on her work.

Thomas Moore is a skilled researcher and investigator with expertise in various areas of paranormal investigation, including EVP (Electronic Voice Phenomenon) analysis, EMF readings, and thermography. He also possesses excellent communication skills, which enable him to effectively interview witnesses and gather information from those affected by the unexplained events he investigates.

Thomas Moore has a tendency to become completely absorbed in his work, often neglecting his own safety and well-being for the sake of discovery.
