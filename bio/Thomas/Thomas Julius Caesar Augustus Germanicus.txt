- Age: Late 30s/Early 40s
- Height: 6 feet (183 cm)

- Hair: Dark brown, often styled in a dignified, imperial manner.
- Facial Features: Strong jawline, prominent nose, and piercing eyes that convey intelligence and authority.

**Personality:**

Thomas Julius Caesar Augustus Germanicus is a wise, compassionate, and visionary individual. As an accomplished sculptor, he possesses an innate understanding of the human form and its potential for artistic expression. He is fiercely protective of his protégé Marcus, but also encourages him to grow and develop as an artist in his own right. Thomas's greatest asset lies in his ability to connect with others through storytelling and sharing his own experiences, which helps to inspire confidence and loyalty in those around him.

**Interests:**

- Sculpture and Art: Thomas has a deep passion for sculpture and art, evident in his impressive portfolio of works.
- History and Culture: As an Emperor and artist, Thomas has a profound appreciation for Rome's rich history and cultural heritage.
- Storytelling: Sharing tales from his past is one way that Thomas connects with others, conveying valuable life lessons and historical insights through engaging narratives.

- Philanthropy: He was deeply committed to improving the lives of Romans, often using his art to address pressing social issues or celebrate their triumphs.

Through his mentorship of Marcus and his own creative endeavors, Thomas Julius Caesar Augustus Germanicus leaves a lasting legacy as an artist, leader, and pillar of Roman society.
