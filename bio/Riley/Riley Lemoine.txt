Here's a summary of the fictional character Riley Lemoine:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
* Eye Color: Hazel with golden flecks
* Hair: Short, dark brown, often styled in a messy bob that frames her face
* Build: Athletic and toned, with broad shoulders and a lean physique honed from years of combat training and high-stakes missions

**Personality:**

* Unconventional problem-solver, known for thinking outside the box and approaching challenges from unique angles.
* Confident and self-assured, but also fiercely independent and non-conformist.
* Has a dry sense of humor and can come across as sarcastic or aloof to those who don't know her.
* Loyal and dedicated to her colleagues and mission, but also has a strong sense of justice and will not hesitate to take risks when she believes it's necessary.

**Interests:**

* Martial arts and hand-to-hand combat (she is an expert in multiple styles, including kickboxing and Krav Maga).
* Hacking and computer security (she has spent countless hours studying and mastering these skills to stay one step ahead of her enemies).
* Urban exploration and reconnaissance (she loves to explore abandoned buildings and navigate the city's underbelly to gather intel on potential leads).
* Reading and research (she is a voracious reader and researcher, often devouring books on everything from cryptography to psychology to fuel her intelligence gathering efforts).

**Additional Notes:**

* Riley has a scar above her left eyebrow, which she obtained during a mission gone wrong several years ago.
* She is an avid fan of classic rock music and vinyl records, and can often be found listening to Led Zeppelin or The Rolling Stones in her downtime.
* Despite her tough exterior, Riley has a soft spot for stray animals and is often seen with a scrappy little cat named Nova that follows her around the S.H.I.E.L.D. headquarters.
