Despite this, he remains humble and committed to using his powers for the greater good. Magnus is also intensely curious and loves to learn, with a dry sense of humor that can sometimes catch those around him off guard.

- Research: Magnus is driven by a desire to uncover hidden secrets and push the boundaries of what is known. He is always on the lookout for new discoveries and ideas that can help him advance his understanding of magic.
- Exploration: With a love of adventure, Magnus often ventures into uncharted territories in search of inspiration or new knowledge. This has led him to explore mysterious landscapes, encounter magical creatures, and uncover hidden artifacts.

As a result of his laboratory accident, Magnus has developed an appreciation for the unpredictable nature of life and the importance of embracing uncertainty. He continues to innovate and push the boundaries of magic, driven by his insatiable curiosity and passion for discovery.
