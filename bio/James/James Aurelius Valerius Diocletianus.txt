- Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue

- Politics and Governance: He is well-versed in the art of governance, understanding the intricacies of power and diplomacy.

- Education and Social Welfare: He is deeply committed to promoting education and reducing poverty, recognizing their importance in building a just and prosperous society.

**Philosophical Influences:** 
James was heavily influenced by Epictetus' teachings, which resonated with his desire for a life of purpose and meaning. His encounter with the philosopher had a profound impact on his worldview, shaping his values and guiding principles.
