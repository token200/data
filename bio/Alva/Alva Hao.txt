Here is a summary of the fictional character Alva Hao:

**Physical Attributes:** 
- Height: 5'11" (180 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing emerald green
- Hair: Jet black, slicked back and often worn in a messy, piecey style
- Skin Tone: Olive-toned, with a subtle sheen of street-smart weariness

**Personality:** Alva Hao is a cunning and resourceful individual who has honed his skills through years of living on the streets as a pickpocket. He possesses an extraordinary ability to read people and situations, allowing him to blend into his surroundings like a chameleon. This talent for deception is complemented by his quick wit, sharp intellect, and unparalleled expertise in manipulation. Despite his reputation as "The Shadow Weaver," Alva has a dry sense of humor and an underlying vulnerability that makes him more human than he appears at first glance.

**Interests:** 
- History: Alva has a deep fascination with the historical roots of magic and illusionism, often studying ancient texts and seeking out rare artifacts to fuel his understanding.
- Theoretical Physics: As someone who has experienced the power of the Eclipse Chalice firsthand, Alva is intrigued by its potential connections to time itself and spends considerable time pondering the theoretical implications of such a discovery.
- Strategic Planning: A chess player at heart, Alva enjoys outmaneuvering his opponents through careful planning and creative problem-solving.
