Here's a summary of the fictional character Levi Mitchell:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Age: 32
- Height: 6'2"
- Eye Color: Hazel

**Personality:**
Levi is a determined and passionate individual who has found a new sense of purpose in life. After being forced to retire from professional hockey due to an injury, he returned to his hometown of Willow Creek with the intention of running his family's diner. He is fiercely dedicated to his business partner, Rachel, and the community, striving to create a welcoming atmosphere for customers. Levi also has a strong sense of nostalgia and can be nostalgic at times.

**Interests:**
- Running "The Mitchell Munchies" diner
- Coaching minor league hockey (previously)
- Playing hockey in his free time (although he's not as skilled as he used to be due to the injury)

Overall, Levi is a man who has found a new passion in life after facing setbacks and failures. He's determined to make the most of this new chapter and forge a path that brings him fulfillment and a sense of belonging.
