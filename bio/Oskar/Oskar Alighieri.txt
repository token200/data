Here is a summary of the fictional character Oskar Alighieri:

Physical Attributes:
Oskar Alighieri stands at around 5'10" with an unassuming yet refined presence. His hair is dark brown, often neatly trimmed and slightly wavy. His eyes are a deep shade of blue, almost navy in color, which seems to sparkle with curiosity when he talks about the artifacts he's handling. He has a slender yet athletic build, likely developed from years of physical labor maintaining his antique shop.

Personality:
Oskar is a warm and affable individual who radiates passion for his work. His love for antiques is evident in every gesture, every word, and every smile he shares with customers like Sophia. As the owner of an antique shop that's been passed down through generations, Oskar has developed a strong sense of responsibility to honor the stories and histories of the objects he sells.

Interests:
Oskar's primary interest lies in sharing the fascinating tales behind each piece he handles. He is deeply knowledgeable about art history, Italian culture, and family traditions. When not immersed in his work, Oskar enjoys spending time researching and learning more about his ancestors' adventures, as well as reading classic literature to broaden his understanding of human experiences.

Other characteristics:
As a fourth-generation antiques dealer, Oskar carries the weight of his family's legacy with pride. He has inherited not only the business but also Alessandro Alighieri's stories and passion for Italy, which he now shares with new generations through his customers.
