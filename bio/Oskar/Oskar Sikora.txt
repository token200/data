Here is a summary of the fictional character Oskar Sikora:

Physical Attributes:
- Age: Late 30s to early 40s
- Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue, with a sharp intensity that misses nothing

Personality:
Oskar is a man of unwavering dedication and unshakeable confidence. His years of experience have honed his skills to perfection, making him one of the most formidable counter-terrorism experts in Prague. Despite this, he remains humble and introspective, preferring to rely on his instincts and sharp mind rather than seeking external validation.

Oskar is fiercely loyal to his city and its people, and will stop at nothing to protect them from harm. This unwavering commitment drives him forward even in the face of overwhelming danger, earning him the respect and admiration of those around him.

Interests:
- Counter-terrorism strategy and tactics
- Analyzing evidence and piecing together seemingly unrelated threads
- Surveillance and reconnaissance
- Studying security footage and interviewing witnesses

Additional Details:
Oskar is a solitary figure, preferring to work alone rather than rely on others for support. However, he values his partnership with Detective Kate Müller deeply, and the two have formed a formidable team through their shared battles against crime.

Despite his tough exterior, Oskar has a dry sense of humor and can be witty when the situation allows it. He is fiercely independent, but also has a deep respect for tradition and protocol, which serves him well in his work as a counter-terrorism expert.

As the story suggests, Oskar's dedication to his job often leaves him little time for personal relationships or leisure activities outside of work. However, he knows that even in the darkest moments, there is always hope – and it is this optimism that drives him forward, both personally and professionally.
