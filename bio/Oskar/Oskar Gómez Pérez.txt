Here is a summary of the fictional character Oskar Gómez Pérez:

**Physical Attributes:**
Oskar stands at approximately 5'10" (178 cm) with an athletic yet lean build, a testament to his past life on the streets. His eyes are a deep, piercing brown that seem to hold a world of emotion within them. His dark hair is often unkempt and tousled, framing a face that has been weathered by the trials he has faced.

**Personality:**
Oskar's personality is complex and multifaceted, reflecting his tumultuous past and his journey towards redemption. He is fiercely passionate about his poetry and the stories it tells, using his words to give voice to those who have been silenced. His wit is razor-sharp, often catching people off guard with its cleverness and precision. Despite his tough exterior, he has a vulnerable side that he only reveals to those closest to him.

**Interests:**
Oskar's interests are deeply rooted in literature, philosophy, and poetry. He devours books on these subjects, finding solace in the words of other writers who have navigated similar paths. He is particularly drawn to the works of his mother, whose lost book of poetry sparked his own creative journey. When not writing or performing, Oskar enjoys taking long walks through Barcelona's streets and alleys, observing the city's rhythms and patterns.
