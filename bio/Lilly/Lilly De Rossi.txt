With her father Marco's influence, Lilly has become more confident in her artistic endeavors and is now taking on more ambitious projects, including large-scale murals that showcase the town of Willow Creek's unique character.

As a result of her close relationship with Emily and Jack, Lilly has developed a deep appreciation for music and dance, often incorporating these elements into her art. Her love for food and wine has also led to some memorable nights out in Willow Creek, where she shares stories, laughter, and memories with her closest friends.
