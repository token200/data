Here is a summary of the fictional character Aurel Zawisza:

Aurel Zawisza is a master thief with a unique personality that sets her apart from others in the underworld. She has an artistic side, often viewing thievery as a game rather than just a means to survive. Her approach is calculated and precise, making her nearly invisible in crowded market stalls.

Physical Attributes:
- Height: Aurel stands at around 5'8" (173 cm), allowing her to blend in with the crowd while maintaining an air of confidence.
- Eye Color: Her eyes are an striking shade of indigo, almost a dark purple, which seems to hold a secret and allure all who see them.

Interests:
Aurel is driven by two main interests: finding the next treasure to claim as her own, and unraveling mysteries hidden within Krakow's ancient walls. She has a special affinity for objects imbued with history and mystique, like the golden locket she recently acquired. Aurel finds solace in these treasures not just for their monetary value but also for the stories they hold.

Personality Traits:
Aurel is an elusive figure with an air of mystery surrounding her. Her sense of purpose is strong when it comes to stealing valuable items and understanding their histories, as if each treasure holds a hidden key to her own past or future.
