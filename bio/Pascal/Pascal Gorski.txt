Here's a summary of the fictional character Pascal Gorski:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue, almost cerulean in hue
- Hair: Dark brown, messy and unkempt, often falling across his forehead
- Build: Lean and athletic, honed from years of acrobatic thievery and evasive maneuvers
- Facial Features: Chiseled, with high cheekbones, a strong jawline, and a small scar above his left eyebrow (acquired during a particularly perilous heist)

Personality:
- Enigmatic and mysterious, Pascal's past is shrouded in secrecy, which contributes to his allure
- Highly intelligent and resourceful, able to think on his feet and manipulate situations to his advantage
- A natural charmer, with an ability to effortlessly disarm others and extract information from them
- Haunted by a sense of restlessness, as if he's constantly searching for something more

Interests:
- Thievery and con artistry are Pascal's true passions, allowing him to move undetected through the shadows and claim the rewards that few others can access
- Art appreciation: He has a keen eye for beauty in the imperfect, the forgotten, and the overlooked – traits he admires in Aurélie Dupont
- History and mythology: Pascal is fascinated by the darker corners of Parisian history and the legends that have shaped its identity
- Wanderlust: With a sense of wanderer's curiosity, Pascal craves new experiences, hidden secrets, and untold stories

Despite his affinity for the shadows, Pascal Gorski harbors a deep longing for connection, which is why he finds himself inexplicably drawn to Aurélie's vibrant spirit and artistic talents. As their bond grows stronger, he must confront the turmoil within himself, risking everything for the chance at redemption and true love.
