Here is a summary of the fictional character János Borromeo:

**Physical Attributes:** János stands at 5'9" (175 cm) tall, with an athletic build developed from years of intense musical practice. His hair is dark brown and often tousled, framing his heart-shaped face and expressive eyes - which are a deep shade of blue that sparkles like the night sky above Budapest.

**Personality:** János is an intensely passionate individual, driven by his love for music and fueled by his creative spirit. He has an unwavering dedication to his craft, often sacrificing rest and relaxation in pursuit of perfection. Despite his rising fame, he remains humble and grounded, crediting Maestro Sándor's guidance and Éva's influence in shaping his artistic vision.

**Interests:** János is deeply rooted in classical music, with a particular affinity for the works of Bach, Mozart, and Beethoven. As a violinist, he finds solace in the expressive qualities of his instrument, using it to convey emotions that words alone cannot express. He also has an appreciation for the beauty of nature, often finding inspiration in the Hungarian landscapes that surround Budapest.

**Additional Traits:** János is a romantic at heart, with a deep love for poetry and literature that fuels his creativity. When not performing or composing, he enjoys taking long walks along the Danube River, lost in thought as the city's vibrant energy recedes into the distance.
