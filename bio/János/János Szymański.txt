Here is a summary of the fictional character János Szymański:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5'10" (178 cm)
- Eye Color: Bright blue
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often messy and unkempt from years of working with his hands.
- Facial Features: Strong jawline, prominent nose, and a small scar above his left eyebrow from a childhood accident.

**Personality:**
János is a gentle soul with a passion for restoring old cars. He has a calming presence and a soothing demeanor that puts even the most anxious clients at ease. His patience and attention to detail are unparalleled, allowing him to coax life back into even the most temperamental engines. János is also an expert communicator, able to break down complex technical issues into understandable language for his clients.

**Interests:**
- Restoring old cars (his true passion)
- Learning about automotive history
- Collecting rare and vintage parts for his workshop
- Reading classic car repair manuals
- Listening to jazz music while working in his shop

János's love of restoration is evident not just in his work but also in the attention he pays to every detail, from the polishing of chrome trim to the careful calibration of suspension systems. His dedication to preserving automotive history and bringing vintage vehicles back into circulation has earned him a reputation as one of the best car restorers in town.
