Here is a summary of the fictional character Frieda Jasinski:

**Physical Attributes:** Frieda stands at 5'8" with long, curly brown hair and warm hazel eyes. Her features are strong yet soft, giving her an endearing and approachable appearance.

**Personality:** Frieda has a tough exterior that belies her kind and compassionate heart. She is fiercely loyal to those she cares about, as evidenced by her unwavering dedication to Max, her scrappy little terrier companion. Her maternal instincts are strong, which led her to take in Lily, the young woman who had been living on the streets. Frieda's sharp wit and quick thinking make her a formidable problem solver, while her generosity of spirit inspires loyalty and devotion from those around her.

**Interests:** Frieda is deeply passionate about baking and running her beloved bakery in Willow Creek. She takes great pride in perfecting her craft and sharing her creations with the community. Her love of art is also evident in her sketchbook, where she often expresses herself through quick drawings and ideas. Outside of work, Frieda values time spent with friends and family, including Max, who brings immense joy to her life.
