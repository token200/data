Here is a summary of the fictional character Harald Johansson:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 6 feet (183 cm)
- Eye Color: Bright blue
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often messy and unkempt
- Build: Lean, athletic build from years of studying and researching
- Facial Features: Strong jawline, prominent nose, and piercing bright blue eyes that seem to see right through you

**Personality:**
Harald is a driven and determined individual with an insatiable curiosity about his family's past. He has an unrelenting passion for uncovering the truth, which fuels his every move. Despite his reserved nature, Harald exudes a quiet confidence and resilience that allows him to tackle even the most daunting challenges head-on.

**Interests:**
- History, particularly ancient civilizations
- Genealogy and family research
- Reading and studying ancient texts
- Researching local legends and folklore
- Exploring museums and historical sites

Harald's love for history and discovery stems from his desire to understand where he comes from and the secrets that have been hidden in his family's past. With Soren by his side, Harald is poised to embark on a journey of self-discovery that will take him down a path of intrigue, mystery, and possibly even danger.
