Arseny is deeply passionate about flowers and the art of floristry. He enjoys experimenting with new designs and techniques, constantly seeking inspiration from nature. Outside of work, Arseny can often be found tending to his own garden in the countryside or exploring local markets for rare and exotic blooms.
