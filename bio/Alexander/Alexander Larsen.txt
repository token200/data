Here's a summary of the fictional character Alexander Larsen:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 6 feet 2 inches (188 cm)
- Eye Color: Hazel (with a distinctive fleck of gold in each eye)

**Personality:** Alexander is known for his sharp mind and quick wit, but beneath his confident exterior lies a complex web of emotions. He is introspective and empathetic, having developed this quality through his experiences with mental health struggles and his late mother's addiction. As a professor, he exudes compassion and understanding, making him an effective mentor to his students.

**Interests:** Alexander is passionate about psychology, particularly when it comes to understanding the human mind and promoting mental well-being. His interest in his mother's story also drives his research into helping others overcome their struggles with addiction and mental illness.
