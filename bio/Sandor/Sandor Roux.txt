**Personality:**

Sandor Roux is a free-spirited and creative individual, unafraid to challenge the conventional norms of magic. He is fiercely independent and non-conformist, often preferring to think outside the box and forge his own path. Despite this, he has a deep respect for tradition and the wisdom of those who have come before him. Sandor is intensely curious and driven, with an insatiable thirst for knowledge that propels him forward.

**Interests:**

* Magic theory and experimentation
* Ancient artifacts and historical relics
* Cryptography and code-breaking
* Improvisational magic and creative problem-solving
* Stargazing and astrology

Sandor's passion for magic is matched only by his love of discovery and exploration. He finds joy in unraveling the secrets of ancient texts, deciphering cryptic messages, and experimenting with innovative spellcraft. His creative approach to magic has earned him both praise and criticism from his peers at Wysteria Academy, but Sandor remains undeterred, driven by a burning desire to push the boundaries of what is thought possible in the world of magic.

With Professor Orion's guidance, Sandor Roux is poised to unlock new levels of magical understanding and rewrite the textbooks on magic itself. His unorthodox approach has paid off, and he is now ready to take his place among the academy's most esteemed members.
