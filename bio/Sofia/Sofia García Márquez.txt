Here is a summary of the fictional character Sofia García Márquez:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 5'8"
- Eye Color: Dark Brown
- Hair Color: Black with subtle waves
- Skin Tone: Olive-toned with a warm glow, characteristic of her Colombian heritage.

Personality:
Sofia is a compassionate and empathetic individual who has a deep connection to her roots. She is fiercely loyal to her family and community, often putting their needs before her own. Her upbringing in Cartagena instilled in her the importance of resilience, perseverance, and storytelling. Sofia's passion for journalism stems from her desire to amplify marginalized voices and share powerful stories that inspire positive change.

Interests:
- History: Sofia is deeply fascinated by the history of her community and the struggles of women during times of war and economic hardship.
- Social Justice: She is committed to using her platform as a journalist to highlight social injustices and advocate for equality.
- Storytelling: Through her writing, Sofia seeks to preserve the stories of her abuela's community, weaving together historical facts and personal anecdotes to create a narrative that is both powerful and intimate.
- Cultural Preservation: As a proud Colombian-American, Sofia strives to maintain a connection to her cultural heritage through her work and daily life.

Background:
Sofia spent five years away from Colombia, studying in the United States. Upon returning home, she began to reconnect with her roots and rekindle her passion for storytelling. Her experience in Cartagena reignited her love for journalism, allowing her to find a sense of belonging and purpose in her work.
