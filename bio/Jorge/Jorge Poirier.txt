Here is a summary of the fictional character Jorge Poirier:

**Physical Attributes:**
Jorge Poirier stands at approximately 5'10" (178 cm) with a rugged yet athletic build. His eyes are a piercing blue, almost indigo in color, which seem to sparkle with a deep connection to the ocean. His dark brown hair is often wind-swept and mussed from his daily adventures on the water.

**Personality:**
Jorge Poirier is a true embodiment of his name, embodying the strength, resilience, and loyalty that come with being a proud Argentinian fisherman. He exudes an air of quiet confidence, earned through years of experience navigating treacherous waters. Jorge's unwavering commitment to his craft, community, and family sets him apart from others, making him a leader respected by all who know him.

**Interests:**
As a seasoned fisherman, Jorge Poirier has a deep love for the ocean and its rhythms. He is passionate about maintaining the delicate balance of the ecosystem and ensuring sustainable fishing practices that benefit both his community and the environment. Outside of work, Jorge enjoys sharing tales of his adventures with family and friends over a warm glass of wine or a plate of freshly caught seafood. He is also an avid collector of seashells and sea glass, which he displays proudly in his cozy coastal home.

**Additional Details:**
Jorge Poirier has a distinctive scar above his left eyebrow, shaped like a crescent moon, which he obtained during a particularly harrowing storm several years ago. This unique feature is said to bring him good luck on the water. He also has a weakness for traditional Argentine cuisine, particularly empanadas and asado-style BBQ.
