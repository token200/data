Here is a summary of the fictional character Rein Delacroix:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
* Eye Color: Piercing blue
* Hair Color: Dark brown, often worn long and unruly
* Build: Lean and athletic, with a wiry strength developed from years of physical activity during his Resistance days
* Facial Features: Strong jawline, sharp cheekbones, and a small scar above his left eyebrow (resulting from an accident during a mission)

**Personality:**

* Rein is a complex and brooding individual, struggling to reconcile his past actions with his current moral compass. He walks the fine line between light and darkness, often questioning his own motivations.
* He is fiercely independent and resourceful, able to think on his feet and adapt to new situations quickly.
* Despite his tough exterior, Rein has a soft spot for those in need and is deeply compassionate towards others. This is evident in his willingness to help others and protect the innocent.
* He is intensely curious and driven, with an insatiable thirst for knowledge that often leads him into danger.

**Interests:**

* Literature: Rein has a deep love of poetry and philosophy, finding solace in the works of Baudelaire, Nietzsche, and Camus.
* Music: He is an avid listener to classical music, particularly Chopin's nocturnes, which evoke a sense of melancholy and introspection.
* History: As a former Resistance fighter, Rein has a deep respect for history and its ability to shape the present. He is particularly interested in understanding the rise and fall of organizations like Ouroboros.
* Travel: Despite his preference for solitude, Rein has a strong sense of wanderlust and enjoys exploring new cities and landscapes.

**Background:** As mentioned in the short story, Rein was a former French Resistance fighter who lost friends and comrades during the war. He is now grappling with the consequences of his past actions and trying to make amends by reuniting with a long-lost relative involved with the Ouroboros Society.
