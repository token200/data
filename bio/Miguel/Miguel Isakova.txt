Here is a summary of the fictional character Miguel Isakova:

**Physical Attributes:** Miguel stands at 5'10" (178 cm) with a lean yet athletic build. His eye color is a deep brown that seems to hold warmth and kindness, despite his tough exterior. His hair is dark and unkempt, often hidden beneath a messy cap or worn down in loose waves. He has a scattering of tattoos on his arms and torso, each one telling a story about his past life as a street fighter.

**Personality:** Miguel is a complex and multifaceted individual with a soft spot for the downtrodden and oppressed. His years on the streets have hardened him, but also instilled in him a strong sense of justice and compassion. He is fiercely independent and has a quick wit that can be both charming and intimidating. Despite his rough exterior, Miguel has a deep love for art and humanity, which drives his actions and motivations.

**Interests:** Miguel's interests revolve around social justice, art, literature, and philosophy. As a barista at the café, he is passionate about crafting the perfect cup of coffee that brings people together. He is also deeply interested in preserving Moscow's cultural scene, which was threatened by the Soviet regime. In his free time, Miguel enjoys reading and discussing philosophical texts, attending underground art shows, and engaging in heated debates with friends.

**Background:** Miguel's past life as a street fighter has left him with physical scars and emotional scars. He is haunted by memories of violence and trauma, but has found solace in his current work at the café and his interactions with Mr. Petrov. Despite this, he remains fiercely guarded, only opening up to those he trusts.

**Relationships:** Miguel's relationships are complex and multifaceted. He forms a strong bond with Mr. Petrov, who becomes an unlikely ally and friend. His friendship with Sofia, his boss at the café, is also significant, as she provides him with support and understanding in his personal life.
