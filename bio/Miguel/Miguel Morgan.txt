Here's a summary of the fictional character Miguel Morgan:

**Personality:** Miguel is a man of quiet confidence with an air of mystery surrounding him. He has seen his fair share of battles as a former CIA operative, but after leaving the agency, he retreated from the spotlight, preferring a simple life. Despite his tough past, Miguel has a soft spot for those in need and is fiercely protective of Sophia. He is intelligent, resourceful, and determined, with a dry sense of humor that he uses to deflect attention.

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 6'2"
* Eye Color: Piercing green
* Hair Color: Dark brown, often worn messy and unkempt
* Body Type: Lean and athletic, honed from years of military training
* Face: Sharp jawline, prominent cheekbones, and a small scar above his left eyebrow (from a past mission)

**Interests:** Miguel's passions include:

* Music: He is an accomplished guitarist and enjoys playing in the evenings, watching the sun set over the ocean.
* Whiskey: A daily ritual for him, Miguel sips whiskey on his remote coastal property to unwind and clear his mind.
* The outdoors: Miguel loves spending time in nature, often taking long walks along the beach or going on solo hikes in the surrounding woods.
* Investigation: As a former CIA operative, Miguel has developed a keen interest in gathering information and following leads.

**Additional Traits:** Despite his tough exterior, Miguel has a dry sense of humor and can be sarcastic at times. He is fiercely loyal to those he cares about, particularly Sophia, and will stop at nothing to protect her.
