Abdirahman's interests are varied and eclectic, reflecting his cultural chameleonship. His love for acrobatics and juggling allows him to channel his energy into creative outlets, providing a counterbalance to the high-stakes world of espionage. When not operating undercover, Abdirahman enjoys exploring new cities and immersing himself in different cultures, always seeking to refine his skills as a master of identity manipulation.

Despite his many talents, Abdirahman is driven by a deep sense of purpose: he is a keeper of balance, navigating the shadows and light to maintain harmony in a world often torn apart by chaos.
