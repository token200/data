**Interests:**

* Songwriting and composition
* Performing live, both as a solo artist and with her band "Eterna Notte"
* Collaborating with other musicians and artists to create new works
* Exploring her rich heritage and the cultural influences that have shaped her music

**Background:**

Born into a family of artists, Lucía was encouraged from a young age to pursue her passion for music. Her father, Marcello Mastroianni, played a significant role in introducing her to Italian classical composers and shaping her musical tastes. She has since become an iconic singer-songwriter, leading her band "Eterna Notte" to international stardom with hits like "Notte di Luna".
