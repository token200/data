Here is a summary of the fictional character Zhou Tóth:

**Name:** Zhou Tóth (full name: Zhou Tóth)

**Physical Attributes:**

- **Height:** Zhou Tóth stands at approximately 5'8" (173 cm), with an ethereal elegance that belies her powerful sorceress abilities.
- **Eye Color:** Her eyes are a deep, shimmering shade of indigo that seems to hold the very essence of starlight. The color is reminiscent of a moonless night sky, with hints of silver and violet undertones that seem to shift in the light.

**Personality:**

Zhou Tóth is an enigmatic figure, embodying both mystery and wisdom. Her presence commands attention without being overwhelming, as if she holds secrets within herself that only reveal themselves to those attuned to her energy. She exudes a quiet confidence born from years of mastering the mystical arts, yet retains an air of curiosity and playfulness in her demeanor.

Despite her otherworldly nature, Zhou Tóth possesses a deep empathy for others, recognizing hidden potential within each individual and guiding them towards their true purpose. Her smile is as captivating as it is gentle, with an unmistakable warmth that puts those around her at ease. When she speaks, her words are laced with ancient wisdom, tempered by a sense of urgency that drives her to weave reality itself into the tapestry of fate.

**Interests:**

- **Celestial Studies:** Zhou Tóth has spent countless cycles (years) studying the mysteries of the cosmos, seeking knowledge about the birth and evolution of stars, planets, and galaxies. Her maps on the walls of her sanctum reflect a deep understanding of celestial patterns and their influence on reality.
  
- **Magic and Reality Weaving:** As a master weaver of reality, Zhou Tóth dedicates herself to mastering the intricacies of magical manipulation. She explores the boundaries between different dimensions, seeking new techniques to enhance her craft.

- **Apprenticeship and Mentorship:** Having guided several apprentices before Lyra, including a notable sage named Elwira Starweaver, Zhou Tóth takes pride in nurturing talent and guiding her pupils towards their full potential.
