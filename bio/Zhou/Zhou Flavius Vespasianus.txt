Here is a summary of the fictional character Zhou Flavius Vespasianus:

**Physical Attributes:** 
- Height: 6'1" (185 cm) - a tall and imposing figure, reflecting his intimidating presence.
- Eye Color: Piercing brown eyes that seem to see right through you, giving an air of intensity and calculation.
- Skin Tone: Olive-toned complexion, likely a result of his hybrid ancestry.

**Personality:** 
- Zhou is an enigmatic individual with a quiet confidence that commands attention. His mysterious nature makes people both curious and wary.
- He exudes a sense of conviction, unwavering in his pursuit of goals.
- Ruthless efficiency defines his approach to tasks, making him a force to be reckoned with.
- Beneath the surface lies an unyielding drive that pushes him to achieve his own destiny.

**Interests:**
- Zhou is deeply invested in uncovering hidden secrets and piecing together the mysteries of his hybrid existence.
- As a skilled thief and informer, he has an insatiable curiosity about the workings of the Roman Empire's inner circle.
- The pursuit of power and insight drives him to seek out valuable artifacts like "The Eye of the gods."
- Above all else, his desire for self-discovery fuels his unrelenting quest for understanding his own nature.

**Hybrid Origins:** 
Zhou was born from a rare genetic mutation resulting from an ancient pact between a Roman senator's daughter and a powerful alchemist. This hybrid offspring possesses extraordinary abilities that set him apart from humanity, making him both prized and feared in the world of espionage.

**Motivations:**
- To uncover the truth about his past and his unique abilities.
- To protect himself and those he cares for from those who seek to exploit his powers.
- To use his skills to manipulate events from behind the scenes and bring about change - starting with the downfall of Emperor Vespasian.

Overall, Zhou Flavius Vespasianus is a complex figure driven by a singular focus on achieving his own destiny.
