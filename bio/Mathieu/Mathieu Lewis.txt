Here is a summary of the fictional character Mathieu Lewis:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
- Eye Color: Bright Blue
- Hair Color: Dark Brown, often styled neatly and kept short
- Skin Tone: Olive with a subtle tan from spending time outdoors
- Build: Lean and athletic, with broad shoulders and a strong physique

**Personality:**
Mathieu is a driven and introspective individual. As a detective, he is fiercely analytical and methodical in his approach to solving cases. However, beneath his reserved exterior lies a deep sense of empathy and compassion for those affected by crime. His years of experience have taught him to navigate complex webs of human relationships and behaviors.

Despite his professional demeanor, Mathieu has a dry wit and can be sarcastic at times. This tongue-in-cheek humor is often used to deflect attention or ease tension in high-pressure situations. He values intelligence and expertise above all else and is willing to collaborate with others when necessary.

**Interests:**
Mathieu's interests are varied but tend towards the analytical and investigative side of things. Outside of work, he enjoys:

- Reading classic detective novels and true crime stories
- Hiking and outdoor activities in his free time
- Collecting rare books on forensic science and criminology
- Listening to jazz music, particularly Miles Davis

As a native of Willow Creek, Mathieu has a deep connection to the small town's history and culture. He is deeply invested in unraveling its mysteries and bringing justice to those who have been wronged. His determination and attention to detail make him one of the most respected detectives in the department.
