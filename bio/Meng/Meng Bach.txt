Here's a summary of the fictional character Meng Bach:

**Name:** Mei Yuansheng Meng Bach (full name meaning "Dreams of Prosperity")

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5'9" (175 cm) - a slender yet athletic build, reflecting his agility and quick wit.
- Eye Color: Dark brown with flecks of gold, reminiscent of the rich tones of Chinese lacquerware.

**Personality:** Meng Bach is a charismatic and sharp-witted individual. He has an air of confidence and discernment that serves him well in his merchant dealings. Beneath this charming exterior lies a complex and introspective person with a deep love for literature, philosophy, and art. His nickname "Dreams of Prosperity" not only reflects his family heritage but also hints at the imaginative nature of his thoughts and aspirations.

**Interests:** 
- Literature: Meng Bach has a great appreciation for classical Chinese poetry and the works of famous writers.
- Philosophy: He engages in debates about Confucianism, Taoism, and Buddhism, always seeking to deepen his understanding of life's mysteries.
- Art: He is an enthusiast of calligraphy, finding solace in the flowing movements of brushstrokes that mirror the flow of thoughts.
- Merchandising: As a merchant, Meng Bach's interests extend to collecting rare spices, silks, and other valuable commodities that he trades on his own.

**Other notable traits:** Meng Bach has an innate ability to read people and situations, making him an astute negotiator. His enigmatic connection with the Daughter of the Moon also hints at a mystical side to his personality, drawing him into realms beyond the mundane.
