**Personality:**
Amandine is a sharp-witted and determined individual, driven by a strong sense of justice. As a seasoned detective with the Parisian police department, she has developed a no-nonsense attitude and an unwavering commitment to uncovering the truth. Her experience in handling high-pressure situations has honed her instincts and analytical mind, making her one of the best in the business.

Despite her tough exterior, Amandine has a compassionate side, particularly when it comes to victims and their families. She is fiercely loyal to those she trusts and will go to great lengths to protect them. Her keen observational skills also allow her to read people quite well, often picking up on subtle cues that others miss.

**Interests:**
- Investigative journalism
- French history, particularly the cultural heritage of Brittany
- Hiking and outdoor activities in the countryside
- Reading historical novels and mystery fiction
- Learning new languages, including Breton
- Practicing yoga to maintain mental balance

Amandine's fascination with investigative journalism stems from her early days as a rookie detective. She has always been drawn to the thrill of uncovering hidden truths and bringing justice to those who have been wronged.

Her love for French history is deeply rooted in her Breton heritage, which she proudly wears on her sleeve. Amandine often finds solace in hiking and outdoor activities, finding that nature helps clear her mind and refocus her thoughts.

In her free time, Amandine enjoys reading historical novels and mystery fiction, often seeking inspiration from the world of literature to inform her own investigative work. Her interest in learning new languages is a natural extension of her travels and desire to immerse herself in different cultures.

Lastly, Amandine practices yoga as a way to maintain mental balance, recognizing that even the most determined detective needs to unwind occasionally.
