**Interests:**

Peng Kazuo's primary interests revolve around mastering powerful cursed techniques, such as the "Upper-Youth Curse" and "Lower-Grade-Technique." Outside of his training, Peng Kazuo values personal growth, self-discipline, and compassion, recognizing that these qualities are essential for harnessing his abilities effectively.
