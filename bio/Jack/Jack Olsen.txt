Jack Olsen is a complex, driven individual with a strong sense of justice. His years of experience have honed his instincts and made him extremely perceptive, often allowing him to read people and situations quickly. He has an uncanny ability to remain calm under pressure, which makes him a trusted figure in high-stakes investigations.

Jack Olsen's primary interests lie in solving complex crimes, unraveling mysteries, and understanding human behavior. In addition to his exceptional detective skills, Jack Olsen is highly skilled in hand-to-hand combat, marksmanship, and surveillance techniques. His expertise in these areas stems from years of training and experience, making him one of the most formidable private investigators in the city.

Despite his many strengths, Jack Olsen has a few vulnerabilities. He's fiercely self-reliant, which can sometimes lead to him taking on cases that are too complex for even his exceptional abilities.
