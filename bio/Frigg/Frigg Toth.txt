Her emotional landscape is characterized by resilience, courage, and an unwavering commitment to uncovering truth.

- Woodworking and metalwork: Frigg has a natural affinity for these crafts, which are deeply intertwined with her family history.
- Genealogical research: Her quest for understanding the past drives her fascination with the stories of her ancestors and their contributions to society.
- Artisanal skill development: Frigg's ultimate goal is to revive the lost techniques of her ancestors through meticulous practice and innovative approaches.

Frigg Toth's character embodies strength, resilience, and a fierce commitment to learning from her heritage. She holds within herself a blend of curiosity, courage, and determination that propels her forward as she unravels the secrets hidden in her family's past.
