He is fiercely passionate about his work and has a keen eye for detail, which sets him apart from other craftsmen in the region.

- Woodworking: Ibrahim's expertise extends beyond guitar-making to include a wide range of woodworking techniques. He takes great care in selecting the finest woods for his creations.
- Culture: As a proud Tulum resident, Ibrahim has a deep appreciation for the region's history and culture. He draws inspiration from local myths and legends when designing his instruments.

Ibrahim was born and raised in Tulum, where he learned the art of luthiery from his father at a young age. After honing his skills, he became one of the most sought-after craftsmen in the region, creating instruments for musicians around the world.
