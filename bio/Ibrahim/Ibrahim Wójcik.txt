Here is a summary of the fictional character Ibrahim Wójcik:

Physical Attributes:
- Age: Late 30s/Early 40s
- Height: 6'0" (183 cm)
- Eye Color: Bright, warm brown eyes that sparkle with kindness and compassion
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often styled neatly but with a hint of messy texture from his daily work in the kitchen

Personality:
- Ibrahim is an empathetic and caring individual who always puts others before himself. His selfless act at the local market to help an elderly woman sets the tone for his entire character.
- He has a creative and inventive spirit, as evident in his experimentation with various recipes and ingredients to aid patients suffering from food poisoning.
- Ibrahim's humility is quite remarkable; he rises from being an unknown chef to becoming a celebrated healer without seeking recognition or fame.

Interests:
- Cooking: As a professional chef, Ibrahim finds immense joy in creating delicious meals for people. His love for traditional Polish cuisine is evident throughout his recipes and dishes.
- Healing and Wellness: After witnessing the miraculous effects of his cooking on patients with food poisoning, Ibrahim develops an interest in exploring ways to utilize food as medicine, thus leading him into his new role as a healer.
- Helping Others: Being a kind-hearted person from the start, Ibrahim is always looking for opportunities to support those around him and contribute positively to the world.

In summary, Ibrahim Wójcik is a warm-hearted individual with a remarkable journey of discovery that takes him from a humble chef at the market stall to an internationally recognized healer using his culinary skills.
