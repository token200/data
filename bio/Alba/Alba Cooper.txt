Here's a summary of the fictional character Alba Cooper:

**Physical Attributes:**
Alba is a woman with striking features. She stands at 5'9" (175 cm) with an athletic build that belies her sharp intellect and quick wit. Her eyes are a deep, piercing blue that seem to bore into those she meets, as if sizing them up for any potential threat or weakness.

**Personality:**
Despite her tough exterior, Alba has a heart of gold beneath her armor. She is fiercely intelligent, resourceful, and determined, with an unwavering commitment to justice. Her sharp wit and quick tongue make her a formidable opponent in verbal sparring matches, but she is also empathetic and caring towards those who need protection. Alba's strong sense of loyalty and duty drives her to protect the innocent and fight for what is right.

**Interests:**
As a renowned detective, Alba's interests lie primarily in solving complex cases and uncovering the truth. She has a particular interest in understanding human psychology and behavior, which often proves valuable in her investigations. Outside of work, Alba enjoys hiking and exploring new places, perhaps as a way to clear her mind and balance out the intensity of her detective work.

**Additional Details:**
Alba's exceptional skills as a detective are matched only by her unwavering compassion for others. Her sharp intellect and quick thinking have helped her solve some of New Haven's most baffling cases, earning her widespread respect and admiration in the city's law enforcement community.
