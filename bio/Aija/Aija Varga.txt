Aija is a determined and driven individual with a strong sense of justice. She has an unrelenting passion for uncovering the truth, particularly when it comes to cold cases and missing persons. Her past experience as a private investigator has hardened her, making her wary of people but also fiercely loyal to those she cares about.

Aija is intelligent and resourceful, able to think on her feet and navigate complex situations with ease. She is also intensely curious, often driven by a desire to understand the motivations behind people's actions.

Despite her tough exterior, Aija has a deep emotional vulnerability, particularly when it comes to her sister Maria's disappearance. This personal connection fuels her determination to solve the case and bring closure to her family.

**Interests:**

Aija is deeply interested in true crime, cold cases, and missing persons investigations. She spends countless hours researching and studying these topics, always on the lookout for new leads or connections to pursue.

Outside of work, Aija enjoys hiking, reading, and practicing yoga to maintain a healthy balance in her life. She values independence and self-reliance, often preferring to tackle challenges alone rather than seeking help from others.

Aija's investigation into Maria's disappearance has become an all-consuming passion, driving her to push boundaries and take risks that might put her own life at risk.
