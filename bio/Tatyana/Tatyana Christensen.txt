Here's a summary of the fictional character Tatyana Christensen:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
* Eye Color: Hazel with golden flecks
* Hair Color: Dark Brown, often worn in a messy bun or ponytail
* Skin Tone: Pale with a subtle sheen, a testament to her artistic pursuits indoors and outdoors.
* Build: Slender yet athletic, with long fingers that are perfectly suited for painting.

**Personality:**

* Tatyana is a complex and introverted individual who has always felt like an outsider in her small town. She is reclusive and prefers the company of her art to people, but deep down she yearns for human connection.
* Despite her shyness, Tatyana has a dry sense of humor and can be fiercely sarcastic when provoked.
* She is intensely passionate about her art, often pouring all her emotions into each piece.
* As the story progresses, we see a vulnerable side of Tatyana, willing to open up to Lena and sharing her fears and hopes with her.

**Interests:**

* Painting (of course!) - abstract expressionism and mysticism are her bread and butter
* Collecting rare and unusual art supplies, which often take up most of her space in the studio.
* Reading about philosophy, psychology, and spirituality to deepen her understanding of human nature and the world around her.
* Hiking and exploring the outdoors to clear her mind and find inspiration for new pieces.

**Other traits:**

* Tatyana has a tendency to be self-critical and perfectionistic when it comes to her art. She is constantly striving to push herself beyond what she thinks is possible.
* Despite her reclusive nature, Tatyana has a strong sense of loyalty towards those she trusts, like Lena.
* There's an undercurrent of mystery surrounding Tatyana - we catch glimpses of something deeper and more complex beneath the surface of her personality.
