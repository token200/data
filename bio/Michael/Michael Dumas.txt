- Eye Color: Piercing brown eyes that seem to bore into those he interviews
- Hair: Dark, unkempt hair often styled with a hint of messy sophistication

Michael Dumas is charismatic and confident, exuding an aura of calm determination that puts even the most anxious interviewees at ease. His street smarts and resourcefulness are evident in the way he navigates high-stakes situations with poise. Despite the pressure and danger that comes with his line of work, Michael remains principled and driven by a sense of justice, never shying away from difficult stories or confrontation.

**Interests:**
Michael's interests revolve around storytelling, social justice, and the intricacies of human behavior. He has a deep appreciation for the city's vibrant music scene, often attending underground concerts and spoken word performances to find inspiration for his work. His love for jazz music is rivaled only by his love for New Orleans' rich history and culture. When not chasing leads or writing, Michael can be found exploring the French Quarter, sampling beignets at Cafe du Monde, or practicing yoga to calm his mind before diving into a long day of reporting.
