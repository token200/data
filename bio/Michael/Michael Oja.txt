Here is a summary of the fictional character Michael Oja:

Physical Attributes: 
- Height: 5'10" (178 cm)
- Eye Color: Dark brown, almost black
- Hair Color: Light brown, often messy and unkempt
- Build: Lean, with a slightly gaunt appearance due to his introspective nature

Personality:
Michael is an observant and analytical person who has grown accustomed to being overlooked by others. Despite this, he has developed a remarkable capacity for empathy and understanding human behavior. He is fascinated by the complexities of connection and relationships, often feeling like an outsider looking in. However, when someone finally notices him, Michael feels seen and valued.

Interests:
- Philosophy: Michael has a deep interest in philosophical concepts, particularly existentialism and the human condition.
- Poetry: He enjoys reading and writing poetry, finding solace in its ability to capture complex emotions and thoughts.
- Observation: As mentioned earlier, Michael is fascinated by observing people and studying their behavior, mannerisms, and speech patterns.

Other notable traits:
- Perpetual ambiguity: Due to his upbringing and experiences, Michael often feels like an invisible presence, existing on the periphery of people's lives.
- Empathy: Despite feeling like he doesn't quite belong, Michael has developed a remarkable capacity for empathy, allowing him to connect with others in meaningful ways.
- Ambition: Once finding someone who sees him, Michael is eager to form deep connections and find his place in the world.

These attributes make up a complex and intriguing character that embodies the themes of invisibility, connection, and being seen.
