- Despite her privileged upbringing, Mara is humble and down-to-earth, often feeling like an outsider among her peers who are more concerned with wealth and status.

- Architecture: Mara's true calling, where she finds solace in designing structures that blend seamlessly into their surroundings.
- Sustainable Design: Her passion for creating environmentally friendly buildings and spaces that respect the local culture.
- Art: Mara enjoys discussing art with others, particularly Yumi, whom she met at a community center she designed. She sees art as an essential complement to her architectural work.

Background:
- Born into a wealthy family in Tokyo, where expectations are high and pressures can be overwhelming.
- Completed her studies in architecture, leaving behind the family business to forge her own path in Kanazawa, where she discovered her true calling in sustainable design.
