She has a particular interest in human rights and is not afraid to tackle sensitive subjects head-on. When not working on her next story, she enjoys exploring the city of Madrid, attending art exhibitions, and practicing yoga to manage stress.

**Goals:** Mara's ultimate goal is to expose corruption and injustice wherever she finds it, using her skills as an investigative journalist to bring about change. With her reputation in Madrid's press scene now solidified, she sets her sights on uncovering more secrets and telling stories that need to be told.
