Here's a summary of the fictional character Lio Sepp:

**Physical Attributes:** 
- Height: 5'11" (180 cm)
- Eye Color: Hazel with a slight golden tint
- Hair Color: Dark Brown, worn messy and unkempt
- Facial Features: Strong jawline, high cheekbones, and piercing hazel eyes that seem to hold a deep wisdom despite his age.
- Build: Lean and athletic, hinting at his past as a skilled thief.

**Personality:** 
- Lio is an enigmatic individual with layers of mystery surrounding him. He's intensely introspective and can come across as aloof or standoffish to those who don't know him.
- Despite this reserved exterior, he has a deep sense of empathy and understanding that allows him to connect with others on a profound level.
- Lio is driven by a desire for self-discovery and growth. He seeks out experiences that challenge his perspective and push him out of his comfort zone.

**Interests:** 
- Reading: Specifically drawn to the works of Japanese author Kiyoshi Matsukata, whose novels explore themes of existentialism, identity, and personal growth.
- Philosophy: Lio has a strong interest in Eastern philosophies such as Zen Buddhism and Taoism, which resonate with his quest for meaning and balance in life.
- Helping others: He finds fulfillment in assisting those in need and being a source of support to people like Sakura, whom he met on the streets of Harajuku.

**Skills:** 
- As a former thief, Lio possesses exceptional skills in stealth, deception, and manipulation. However, he has worked to suppress these tendencies in favor of more positive pursuits.
- He's highly intelligent and resourceful, able to think critically and come up with creative solutions to problems.
- Physical fitness: Lio is agile and athletic, honed from years of living on the streets and relying on his wits to survive.
