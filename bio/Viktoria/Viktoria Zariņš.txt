Here is a summary of the fictional character Viktoria Zariņš:

Viktoria stands at 5'8" (173 cm) with an athletic yet slender build. Her hair is a rich, dark brown color that falls in loose waves down her back, often tied up in a ponytail when working on projects. Her bright green eyes sparkle with curiosity and intelligence.

- Sharing knowledge and passion with others

Background:
- Born in Riga, Latvia

- Pursued a degree in engineering to further develop her skills
- Founded her own workshop in an abandoned garage where she continues to work on innovative projects
