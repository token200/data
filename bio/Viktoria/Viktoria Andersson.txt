Here is a summary of the fictional character Viktoria Andersson:

**Physical Attributes:** 
- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
- Eye Color: Bright blue
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often worn in a short, chic pixie cut
- Body Type: Fit and athletic, with toned arms from years of outdoor investigation work
- Facial Features: Strong jawline, piercing blue eyes, and a small nose

**Personality:** 
- Viktoria is fearless and determined, driven by her unwavering commitment to uncovering the truth.
- She has razor-sharp instincts and an aggressive questioning style that earns her respect from colleagues and fear from those involved in shady dealings.
- Despite the danger she often faces, Viktoria remains steadfast in her pursuit of justice.
- Her confidence and charisma are undeniable, making her a compelling presence on camera or in interviews.

**Interests:**
- Investigative journalism
- True crime podcasts (she's an avid listener and has even interviewed some guests)
- Reading books on history, economics, and politics to deepen her understanding of the world
- Hiking and outdoor activities in her free time to stay physically active and clear her mind

These details flesh out Viktoria Andersson as a well-rounded character, capturing not only her drive and determination but also her passion for storytelling and her interests outside of journalism.
