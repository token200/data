- Tinkering: He enjoys experimenting with different gears, levers, and mechanisms in his workshop, often finding innovative solutions to complex problems.
- Community protection: As a hero in Schönberg, he feels a strong sense of responsibility to safeguard the town and its residents from harm.

- His years of working with clockwork mechanisms have granted him exceptional hand-eye coordination, agility, and a unique understanding of mechanics that serves him well in combat situations.

Despite his humble demeanor, Dagobert Handschuh is an extraordinary individual whose unassuming nature belies a reservoir of courage, intelligence, and creativity that makes him a true legend in Schönberg.
