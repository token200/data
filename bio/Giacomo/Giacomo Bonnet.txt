He is fiercely intelligent, resourceful, and calculating, always thinking several steps ahead of those around him. Despite his tough exterior, he has a deep sense of vulnerability and sensitivity, which he only reveals to those closest to him.

**Interests:** Giacomo's interests are multifaceted, reflecting his varied past as an artist turned thief. He is passionate about art, history, and music, often collecting rare prints and manuscripts in his spare time. He also has a fascination with the intricate mechanics of art forgery and the world of high-stakes theft. After falling for Isabella's art and her enigmatic presence, he becomes obsessed with uncovering the secrets behind her unique brushstrokes and the mysterious past that surrounds her.
