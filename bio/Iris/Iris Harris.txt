Iris Harris is a young woman with an slender yet athletic build. Her height is around 5'8" (173 cm), which allows her to move with ease and agility. Her features are delicate yet determined, framing a face that radiates an aura of quiet confidence.

She has a natural inclination towards spirituality and the unknown, which draws her to places like antique shops and mysterious artifacts. Her curiosity and sense of adventure make her prone to taking risks, but also allows her to explore hidden corners of the world that others may overlook. Iris is fiercely independent and determined, yet she has a compassionate heart and a strong desire to help others.

Iris's interests are diverse and eclectic, reflecting her broad range of passions. She loves ancient history, mythology, and mysticism, with a particular fascination for the esoteric arts. Her love of magic and the supernatural is evident in her enthusiasm for books on witchcraft and spell-casting. In addition to exploring hidden spaces and collecting rare artifacts, Iris enjoys meditation, yoga, and practicing various forms of divination. She is also an avid reader, devouring books on philosophy, science, and psychology.

As revealed through the ancient tome known as the Collection, Iris has developed remarkable magical abilities, which she can harness to heal others and bring hope to those in need. Her powers are fueled by her own inner light and connection to the natural world, making her a powerful force for good.
