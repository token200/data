- He is empathetic and understands the struggles of mental health issues, inspired by Vincent van Gogh's story.
- Once Samuel discovers his passion for art, he becomes determined to see it through, motivated by a sense of pride and accomplishment.

**Interests:** 

- Art history (specifically, the Renaissance and modern periods)
- Painting and drawing
- Nature observation and appreciation
- Researching techniques used by famous artists, including Vincent van Gogh
