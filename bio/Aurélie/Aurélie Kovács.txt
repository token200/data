- Hair Color: Dark brown with subtle hints of auburn and copper undertones

**Personality:** 
Aurélie is an artistic soul with a rich inner life. She is introspective and emotive, often struggling to balance her creative pursuits with the complexities of the world around her. Despite this, she has an innate optimism that draws people in and inspires them to see beauty in unexpected places.
Despite her natural vulnerability, Aurélie has developed a tough exterior over time, using art as a shield to protect herself from the hurt and disappointment that life can bring. However, with Léon's encouragement and support, she begins to let down her guard and reveal her true self.

**Interests:**
Aurélie is deeply passionate about art, particularly painting and drawing. She loves exploring the city of Paris and capturing its essence on canvas. Music plays a significant role in her life as well; she often listens to classical music while sketching or painting.
Her interests extend beyond the visual arts, however. Aurélie values human connection and meaningful relationships above all else. She is fiercely loyal to those close to her and will go to great lengths to support and care for them.

**Background:** 
Aurélie was born in a small town outside of Paris but has spent most of her life in the city, exploring its winding alleys, charming squares, and artistic communities.
She studied fine art in school, graduating with honors. After art school, Aurélie struggled to find her footing as an artist, feeling uncertain about her place in the world.

Despite these challenges, she persisted, dedicating herself to honing her craft and building a loyal following of fans who appreciate her unique perspective and style.
Her background has shaped Aurélie into the person she is today – introspective, creative, and slightly guarded but ultimately hopeful.
