Here is a summary of the fictional character Anton Kentaro:

Physical Attributes:
Anton Kentaro stands at 6'1" with an athletic build. His hair is dark brown and often unkempt, framing his sharp features. His eyes are a deep, piercing blue that seem to bore into those he looks at. He has a strong jawline and a small scar above his left eyebrow, which he obtained during one of his many adventures as a detective.

Personality:
Anton Kentaro is a man shrouded in mystery himself. He is fiercely independent and non-conformist, having grown up with a sense that there was more to his life than what he knew. This has driven him to become an exceptional detective, using his natural charisma and intuitive skills to unravel the most complex cases.

He is intensely curious and driven by a desire for truth, which often puts him at odds with those around him. Despite this, he has a strong sense of justice and will go to great lengths to uncover the truth, even if it means putting himself in harm's way.

Interests:
Anton Kentaro is an avid reader and collector of rare books on forensic science and cryptography. He is also an expert marksman and enjoys hiking and exploring the outdoors when he's not solving cases.

However, his newfound revelation about his past has left him with a sense of disorientation, leaving him questioning his entire existence and purpose in life.
