Here is a summary of the personality, physical attributes, and interests of the fictional character Anton Alsbach:

His piercing green eyes seem to be the windows to his soul, gleaming with intensity and intelligence. His dark brown hair is always neatly trimmed, revealing a sharp jawline that accentuates his confident demeanor.

He has an effortless charm that puts those around him at ease, but also commands attention and respect. Despite his tough exterior, he possesses a dry sense of humor and a sharp wit, often catching people off guard with his clever remarks.

He has an uncanny ability to replicate the works of famous artists, making him highly sought-after in the black market. Outside of work and forgery, he enjoys collecting rare books on history and philosophy, often spending hours devouring texts on topics ranging from cryptography to alchemy.

In his free time, Anton is a skilled practitioner of parkour and acrobatics, using these skills to navigate the rooftops and alleyways of Ravenswood with ease. He also has a fondness for traditional folk music, often playing the lute or singing old ballads in the evenings after closing up the bakery.

Despite his extensive interests and talents, Anton's true passion remains hidden behind the mask of anonymity, fueling his dual life as a master thief and enigmatic figure in Ravenswood.
