Here is a summary of the fictional character Anton Dufresne:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 5'10" (178 cm)
* Eye Color: Hazel
* Hair: Dark brown, often styled in a messy, unkempt manner
* Build: Lean and athletic, with broad shoulders and a wiry physique

* Martial arts and self-defense
* Philosophy and psychology

* Music and performance (has a passion for jazz)

**Background:**
As a former convict, Anton has spent years honing his skills as an escape artist, using them to survive on the outside. He is haunted by his past and seeks redemption through his latest quest to find "The Collector." Despite his tough exterior, Anton has a deep sense of vulnerability and longing for connection.

**Motivations:**
Anton's motivations are complex and multifaceted. On one hand, he is driven by a desire for freedom and escape from his past. On the other hand, he is drawn to the enigmatic figure of "The Collector" and the promise of discovery and transformation that they offer. Throughout the story, Anton must navigate his own desires and fears in order to uncover the truth about The Collector's identity and intentions.
