- Age: Late 20s
- Height: 5'10" (178 cm)
- Eye Color: Bright Blue, almost an azure hue that complements his artistic genius
- Hair Color: Dark Brown, often styled with a hint of messiness to reflect his creative nature
- Facial Features: Strong jawline, sharp nose, and expressive eyes that convey the intensity of his emotions

**Personality:**
Sebastian is a passionate artist driven by an insatiable curiosity for innovation. Despite being overshadowed by his famous father's legacy at first, he has developed a strong sense of self and independence. He is fiercely determined to prove himself as an artist in his own right. Sebastian's personality can be described as introspective with a quiet confidence, which stems from the knowledge that he has found his own artistic voice.

**Interests:**
- Artistic Expression: Painting, Sculpture, and Architecture
- Historical Research: Study of Leonardo da Vinci's Works and Techniques
- Science and Engineering: Fascination with anatomy, mechanics, and physics as they relate to art and science
- Philosophy: Engages in intellectual discussions on the nature of creativity, perfectionism, and personal growth

**Additional Traits:**
Sebastian is haunted by the weight of expectation left by his father. Despite this pressure, he strives for self-improvement through constant practice and learning from renowned artists.
