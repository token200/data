- Height: 6'2" (188 cm)

- Hair Color: Dark brown, often worn long and unkempt, framing his rugged features

- Haunted by a sense of purpose, seeking validation through his discoveries and contributions to the world of archaeology

- Studying and deciphering ancient languages, texts, and artifacts
- Exploring the mysteries of lost cities and forgotten lore
- Unraveling the history of human civilization, often delving into darker and more complex themes
