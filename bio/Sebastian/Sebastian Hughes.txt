- Age: Late 20s/Early 30s
- Height: 6'1" (185 cm)

- Hair: Dark brown, with hints of chestnut undertones that fall to his shoulders

**Personality:**
Sebastian is a complex and introverted individual with an unyielding need for solitude. He has a reserved demeanor, having grown up moving from place to place, never forming lasting connections or friendships. This upbringing led him to develop a habit of pushing people away before they could get too close. Despite this, he's drawn to those who listen intently and show genuine interest in his life. Sebastian is fiercely independent and non-conformist, with an innate sense of curiosity that drives him to explore the world around him.

**Interests:**
- Reading (especially science fiction novels)
- Exploring nature, particularly dense forests
- Solitude and introspection
- Questioning existence and the meaning of life
