Here's a summary of the fictional character Sebastian Kiss:

**Physical Attributes:**
Sebastian Alexander Kiss is a tall and lean individual, standing at around 6'1" (185 cm). His eyes are an piercing blue color that seems to bore into those he meets. He has dark brown hair that is always impeccably styled, framing his sharp jawline and prominent cheekbones.

**Personality:**
Sebastian is a ruthless and unyielding individual who will stop at nothing to win. Despite this tough exterior, he has a certain charm and confidence that can be both captivating and intimidating. He is highly intelligent and strategic, always thinking several steps ahead of his opponents. However, beneath the surface lies a driven and ambitious person who will do whatever it takes to achieve success.

**Interests:**
Sebastian's primary interest is poker, and he has dedicated himself to becoming one of the best players in the world. He is fiercely competitive and thrives on the rush of adrenaline that comes with high-stakes games. Outside of poker, Sebastian enjoys luxury and fine things in life, often indulging in expensive suits, jewelry, and cars.

Overall, Sebastian Kiss is a force to be reckoned with, both at the poker table and beyond. His intelligence, strategic thinking, and ambition make him a formidable opponent, while his charismatic personality and ruthless competitiveness inspire both respect and fear from those around him.
