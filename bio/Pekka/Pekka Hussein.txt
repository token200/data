He is an avid gardener, finding solace in the simple pleasures of nurturing plants and watching them grow. Pekka also enjoys spending time reading books about history, philosophy, and the natural world.

Physically, Pekka is lean but athletic, with a wiry build that belies his years. He has a scar above his left eyebrow from a sailing accident many years ago, which he got when his boat was caught in a storm off the coast of Australia.

Despite his advanced age, Pekka's spirit remains unbroken, and he continues to be fiercely independent and self-reliant.
