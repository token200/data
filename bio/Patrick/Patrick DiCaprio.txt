Patrick is passionate about collecting and restoring vintage cameras, and his collection has become a reflection of his personality - eclectic, diverse, and uniquely curated. He spends countless hours scouring antique shops, flea markets, and auctions for rare specimens to add to his collection. In addition to his love for photography equipment, Patrick also appreciates the artistry and craftsmanship that goes into camera design.

Patrick comes from a family with a rich history in the art world. His parents were both collectors and dealers who instilled in him a love for rare artifacts from a young age. He has an easy rapport with fellow collectors and is well-respected in his community, often sought out as an expert opinion on rare camera finds.

Despite his refined tastes, Patrick remains humble and grounded. He's always willing to listen and learn from others, whether they're experts in their field or just enthusiastic collectors like himself. His passion for vintage cameras has become a defining aspect of who he is, and he wouldn't have it any other way.
