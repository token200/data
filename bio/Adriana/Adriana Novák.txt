Here is a summary of the fictional character Adriana Novák:

Physical Attributes: 
Adriana Novák stands at approximately 5'8" (173 cm) with striking features. Her eyes are an intense shade of blue, almost indigo, which seem to sparkle with intelligence and wit. Her hair is a rich, dark brown that falls in loose waves down her back, framing her heart-shaped face.

Personality: Adriana's fiery personality is known for its sharp wit, intelligence, and determination. She has a natural ability to think on her feet, making her an exceptional journalist who can ask tough questions without fear. Her confidence and assertiveness make her a force to be reckoned with in the world of journalism.

Interests:
Adriana's passion lies in investigative reporting and uncovering the truth behind hidden stories. She is fiercely curious and has a strong sense of justice, driving her to pursue every lead and follow up on every tip. Her love for storytelling and desire to make a difference have led her to build a reputation as one of the most respected journalists in Prague.

Additional Details:
Adriana comes from an intellectual family where knowledge, critical thinking, and creativity are highly valued. Her upbringing has instilled in her a strong work ethic, discipline, and resilience – all essential qualities for a journalist navigating complex and often treacherous waters.
