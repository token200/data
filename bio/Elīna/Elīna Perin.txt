Interests:
- Elīna's interests are centered around understanding and manipulating the invisible threads of fate that weave together all aspects of reality.
- As someone blessed (or cursed) with visions beyond the veil of everyday perception, she is always on the lookout for ways to better comprehend these mystical energies.
- She has a keen interest in various mysticism practices, seeking to expand her knowledge on how to control and utilize her unique sight.
