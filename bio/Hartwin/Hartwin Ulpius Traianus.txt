- Humble: Despite being a future leader, Hartwin remains grounded due to his father's emphasis on humility.

- Leadership: As he prepares for leadership roles within the Roman Empire, Hartwin is passionate about developing strong leadership skills to unite and inspire his troops.
- Ancient History: With an interest in understanding the cultures of various civilizations, including his own Dacian heritage, Hartwin seeks knowledge on the greatest leaders throughout history.
- Outdoor Activities: Given his upbringing and training under the scorching desert sun, Hartwin is comfortable with outdoor activities such as archery and horseback riding.
