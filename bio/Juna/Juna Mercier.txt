- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
- Eye Color: Hazel

Juna is also determined and driven, as evident from her dedication to documenting the lives of street performers in Montmartre. Her enthusiasm and energy are infectious, making those around her feel inspired and at ease.

**Interests:**
- Photography (particularly street photography and capturing the beauty of everyday moments)

- Travel (exploring new cities and experiencing different cultures)
- People-watching and observing human behavior
- Sharing stories and connecting with others through her photographs

Juna's style is eclectic and bohemian, reflecting her love for art and creativity. She often wears vintage-inspired clothing, carries a worn leather camera bag, and has a fondness for colorful scarves and statement pieces of jewelry. Her apartment in Montmartre is a cozy, cluttered space filled with photographs, books, and mementos from her travels.
