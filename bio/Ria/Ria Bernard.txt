Here's a summary of the fictional character Ria Bernard:

**Physical Attributes:** 
- Age: Late 20s
- Height: 5'6" (168 cm)
- Eye Color: Hazel with golden flecks, reminiscent of the warm colors of Willow Creek
- Hair: Dark brown, often worn in loose waves down her back
- Build: Petite, athletic build developed from years of walking and exploring

**Personality:** 
Ria is a sentimental person who holds onto memories fondly. She's determined but also struggles with feeling unfulfilled and restless. Her time as a journalist has left her with a sense of disconnection, yearning for the simple pleasures in life that she had previously taken for granted.

**Interests:** 
- Journalism (she initially pursued this career path, but is now looking to re-evaluate)
- Cooking and baking (she's developed an interest in these skills from watching Mrs. Thompson at work)
- Photography (Ria has a keen eye for capturing moments and beauty around her)
- Nature and exploration (her time in Oakdale sparked a renewed appreciation for the natural world)

**Background:** 
Growing up in a family of modest means, Ria had always felt a strong connection to Willow Creek. Her trip back to the town after years away is a chance for self-discovery and reconnecting with her roots. The experiences she has during this visit will shape her journey forward.

Ria's character is shaped by her love for memories, her desire for fulfillment, and her growing appreciation for the simple things in life.
