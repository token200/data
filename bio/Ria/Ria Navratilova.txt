Here is a summary of the fictional character Ria Navratilova:

Physical Attributes: Ria stands at 5'9" with an athletic yet toned build. Her long, dark hair is typically tied back in a ponytail, revealing her heart-shaped face and expressive brown eyes that seem to sparkle with determination. She has a small nose ring and a scattering of freckles across the bridge of her nose.

Personality: Ria is a brilliant and fearless detective who exudes confidence and poise. Her sharp mind and quick wit make her one of the most sought-after investigators in Prague. She is fiercely independent and non-conformist, often challenging authority and pushing boundaries to uncover the truth. Despite her tough exterior, Ria has a compassionate heart and is deeply empathetic towards those she helps.

Interests: Outside of work, Ria enjoys practicing martial arts and hiking in the Czech countryside. She is an avid reader and has a particular interest in ancient languages and history. Her love for mystery novels and true crime stories fuels her passion for solving cases and unraveling puzzles.

Background: Born and raised in Prague, Ria comes from a family of law enforcement professionals. Her parents were both respected police officers who instilled in her the importance of justice and fairness. She earned her degree in criminology and psychology and quickly made a name for herself as a top-notch detective.
