**Personality:**
Lev Tzu-chi is a contemplative and enigmatic figure, possessing an aura of calm detachment. His presence seems otherworldly, yet he remains approachable and compassionate. He is intensely focused on his spiritual journey, which has led him to abandon worldly ambitions for inner peace and enlightenment. Despite this, Lev exhibits an uncanny ability to connect with others, as evidenced by his capacity to diagnose rare illnesses and heal the sick.

**Interests:**
Lev's primary interests lie in Buddhism and meditation. He spends most of his time studying ancient texts, practicing yoga, and engaging in introspection to deepen his understanding of the universe. His thoughts often wander into the realms of philosophy, cosmology, and the nature of reality. In addition to his spiritual pursuits, Lev takes an interest in healing and helping others, using his unique abilities to alleviate suffering.

Lev's experiences as a former warrior have given him a deep appreciation for life's fleeting nature, making him particularly empathetic towards those struggling with their own mortality. His actions are guided by a desire to guide others toward enlightenment and self-discovery, rather than personal gain or recognition.
