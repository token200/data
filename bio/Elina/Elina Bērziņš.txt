Here's a summary of the fictional character Elina Bērziņš:

**Physical Attributes:** 
- Age: Late 20s/Early 30s
- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
- Eye Color: Bright Blue, reminiscent of Riga's sparkling blue waters
- Hair Color and Style: Dark Brown, often worn in a loose, effortless bob that showcases her creative spirit

**Personality:** 
Elina is an ambitious, passionate, and determined individual. Her love for art drives her to continually push boundaries and innovate. Elina exudes confidence and creativity, inspiring those around her with her infectious enthusiasm. However, beneath her driven exterior lies a compassionate and empathetic person who genuinely cares about others.

**Interests:** 
Elina is deeply connected to her cultural heritage, often drawing inspiration from Latvian masters like Jānis Petersons and Krišjānis Galenis. Her love for art encompasses traditional techniques blended with modern innovation, pushing the limits of Latvian artistic expression. Elina also finds solace in nature, particularly during winter, as seen in her latest project "Sārdzes Aizeju" (The Dance of Snow).
