Here is a summary of the fictional character Sami Sienkiewicz:

She has a quick wit and sharp instincts, which serve her well in navigating the complex web of supernatural mysteries.

**Interests:** Sami's interests are centered around the mystical and unknown. She is deeply fascinated by ancient mythology, folklore, and mysticism, often spending hours pouring over dusty tomes and listening to tales from old salts. Her grandmother, Babcia, played a significant role in shaping her interest in the supernatural, teaching her about the power of intention and the interconnectedness of all things. As she delves deeper into the hidden world of New Orleans, Sami finds herself drawn to cases involving magic, voodoo, and otherworldly powers.

**Background:** Despite her tough exterior, Sami's heart belongs to her late grandmother, Babcia, who instilled in her a love for the mystical and the unknown. Growing up in a world where magic and reality blurred, Sami has always felt an affinity for the mysterious and the unseen. As she navigates the twists and turns of New Orleans' hidden world, Sami finds herself increasingly drawn to the realm of the unknown, determined to protect those with extraordinary abilities like Akua.

**Quirks:** Sami has a fondness for whiskey, sandalwood incense, and old jazz records. Her quick wit and sharp instincts serve her well in solving cases, but also sometimes get her into trouble.

Overall, Sami Sienkiewicz is a complex, enigmatic figure with a rich inner life and a passion for uncovering the truth about the supernatural mysteries that surround New Orleans' hidden world.
