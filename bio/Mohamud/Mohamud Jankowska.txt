Here's a summary of the fictional character Mohamud Jankowska:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 6'1" (185 cm) - a tall and lean build that speaks to his experiences on the streets.
- Eye Color: Hazel - a warm, earthy brown that seems to hold a deep wisdom.

Personality:
- Mohamud has a mysterious past, but as he forms connections with others, his true nature begins to shine through. He is guarded at first, but ultimately reveals a compassionate and empathetic individual who craves connection.
- Restlessness and wanderlust drive him to explore the city's hidden corners, including Jadwiga's antique shop.

Interests:
- Mohamud develops an intense fascination with antiques, curiosities, and artifacts. He spends hours studying and learning about each item in the shop, feeling a sense of belonging among them.
- As he forms bonds with Jadwiga, he becomes drawn to storytelling, history, and the memories that come alive through old objects.

Background:
- Growing up on the streets of Warsaw leaves Mohamud with a tough exterior, but deep-seated longing for human connection and understanding.
- A life spent surviving by wits and fists gradually shifts as he discovers his love for learning, collecting stories, and preserving memories.
