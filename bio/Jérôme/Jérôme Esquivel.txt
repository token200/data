Jérôme Esquivel stands at around 5'9" (175 cm) with an athletic yet lean build, honed from years of surviving on the streets. This demeanor serves as a shield, protecting those from the painful memories he carries within. Beneath this reserved façade lies a complex web of emotions and desires, driving him to seek connection with others. He is a deeply emotional and sensitive person, yearning for authenticity and understanding.

Jérôme's interests revolve around art, literature, and music, which he discovered on the streets of Montmartre as a young man. His love for these creative pursuits serves as an escape from his troubled past and provides him with solace in times of need. At Le Coeur Volé café, Jérôme finds himself drawn to the intellectual discussions, poetry readings, and artistic performances that fill the space. These experiences spark his own creativity and fuel his desire to explore his emotions and desires.

Jérôme's life on the streets has granted him exceptional skills in disguise and deception, allowing him to adapt quickly to new situations. However, this ability is also a source of anxiety for him, as he often struggles with feelings of disconnection and loss of identity. **Quirks:**
Jérôme has a habit of collecting small trinkets and tokens from places he visits, which serve as mementos of his experiences and reminders of the connections he makes with others. These trinkets often hold sentimental value, reflecting his emotional state at the time.
