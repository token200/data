Here's a summary of the fictional character Ragnar Cooper:

**Physical Attributes:** Ragnar is a tall and imposing figure, standing at around 6'2" with an athletic build. His hair is dark brown, often worn short and neatly trimmed, revealing his prominent jawline and piercing blue eyes that seem to bore into those he meets.

**Personality:** As described in the story, Ragnar is a man of few words but carries significant weight when he does speak. He exudes confidence and authority, earned through his years as a former Marine and successful businessman. However, beneath his tough exterior lies a complex and introspective individual who has been grappling with identity and purpose.

**Interests:** After leaving the corporate world behind, Ragnar finds solace in the physical labor and quiet solitude of the rural landscape. He becomes deeply invested in helping his father restore their family farm, rediscovering the joys of manual work and connecting with nature. Through this process, Ragnar also begins to reconnect with his own past and confront the emotional scars of his childhood.

As he navigates his new life on the Cooper farm, Ragnar starts to explore other interests and passions, including:

* Sustainable farming practices: Ragnar is intrigued by the potential for eco-friendly farming methods to revitalize their land and promote environmental stewardship.
* Family history: As he uncovers hidden stories and secrets from his family's past, Ragnar becomes more interested in understanding the complexities of his own heritage and the experiences that shaped him.
* Spiritual introspection: The tranquility of rural Wisconsin begins to stir a sense of spirituality within Ragnar, as he reflects on his own values, purpose, and connection to the natural world.

**Additional Details:** Despite his newfound interest in sustainable farming practices, Ragnar still has an eye for business acumen. He uses these skills to help improve their farm's efficiency and profitability, leveraging modern technology while preserving traditional methods that align with his family's heritage.
