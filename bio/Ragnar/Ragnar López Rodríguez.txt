Here is a summary of the fictional character Ragnar López Rodríguez:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 5'10" (178 cm)
* Eye Color: Piercing blue, often described as "icy cold"
* Hair: Dark brown, with subtle hints of red undertones that seem to shift in the light
* Build: Lean and athletic, with a wiry physique honed from years of physical training and outdoor survival

**Personality:**

* Quiet confidence: Ragnar carries himself with an air of self-assurance that is both captivating and intimidating.
* Mysterious: Few people know much about his past or motivations, making him all the more enigmatic.
* Calculating: As a master thief, Ragnar approaches every situation with logic and analysis, weighing risks and rewards before acting.
* Charismatic: Despite his tough exterior, Ragnar has a certain charm that allows him to manipulate situations to his advantage.

**Interests:**

* The art of thievery: Ragnar is obsessed with mastering new techniques and staying one step ahead of the law.
* Survival skills: Growing up on the streets taught him how to navigate the urban wilderness and fend for himself.
* High-stakes heists: Ragnar feeds off the thrill of planning and executing daring robberies, often for high-paying clients.
* Solitude: After a lifetime of living by his wits, Ragnar values his independence above all else.

**Other Details:**

* Rumored past: Few know that Ragnar's childhood was marked by poverty and hardship, which taught him to rely on himself alone.
* Network of contacts: Throughout the years, he has cultivated an extensive network of informants and associates who provide crucial information and support.
* A calculated risk-taker: Ragnar is not afraid to take risks when the stakes are high enough – but never without carefully considering the potential consequences.
