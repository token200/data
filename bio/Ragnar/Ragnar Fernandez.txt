Here is a summary of the fictional character Ragnar Fernandez:

Physical Attributes: 
Ragnar stands at 6'2" with an athletic build. His striking features include piercing green eyes and jet-black hair that often falls across his forehead in messy, unkempt layers. He has a strong jawline and defined facial features, giving him a chiseled appearance.

Personality:
Ragnar is a complex and enigmatic individual with a tough exterior and a deep sense of vulnerability. His mysterious past makes it difficult for people to get close to him, but he exudes an air of quiet confidence that commands attention without ever demanding it. As a skilled mercenary, Ragnar has learned to navigate the world's darker corners, and his experiences have left emotional scars that he keeps hidden beneath his tough facade.

Interests:
Ragnar's interests include adventure, self-discovery, and uncovering truth. He is driven by a desire for justice and redemption, which propels him forward even when faced with danger. As he navigates the dark underbelly of the city alongside Sophia, Ragnar becomes increasingly fascinated with his own identity crisis and the secrets that lie beneath his mysterious past.

Ragnar's relationship with Sophia has reignited his passion for life, and he finds himself wanting to unravel the threads of his own identity. He begins to question whether his name is just a superficial label or an integral part of who he truly is.
