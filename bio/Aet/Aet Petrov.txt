**Background:**

Aet's past is shrouded in mystery, but it is clear that he has a deep-seated passion for archaeology and discovery. As a young boy, he was fascinated by tales of his grandfather, a wise and aged historian who sparked Aet's curiosity about ancient civilizations. Over the years, Aet has dedicated himself to unlocking the secrets of lost cities like Zerzura, driven by a sense of wonder and awe at the mysteries that lie beneath the surface. Despite numerous setbacks and disappointments, Aet remains undeterred, always seeking out new leads and opportunities to uncover the truth about these ancient metropolises.
