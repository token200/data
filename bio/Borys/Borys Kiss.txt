Here's a summary of the fictional character Borys Kiss:

* Height: 6'2" (188 cm)
* Eye Color: Piercing blue
* Hair Color: Dark brown, often styled neatly and slicked back to reveal his sharp jawline
* Build: Lean and athletic, with broad shoulders and a chiseled physique

* Borys Kiss is a man of quiet confidence and mystery, with an air that commands attention. He has an unwavering sense of determination and is not afraid to take risks.
* Highly intelligent and perceptive, he uses his exceptional observation skills to read people's body language and detect subtle changes in their demeanor.
* Resourceful and calculating, Borys has a natural talent for puzzle-solving and is skilled at analyzing complex situations.

**Interests:**

* Investigating crimes and solving mysteries
* Reading and learning new information about human behavior and psychology
* Visiting exclusive clubs and social events to gather intel and network with influential people
* Playing high-stakes poker games, albeit only for entertainment purposes (his skills in this area are evident from his ability to track down leads and identify suspects)
* Uncovering hidden truths and unraveling secrets

**Background:**

* Little is known about Borys Kiss's personal life, but it is clear that he has a strong sense of justice and will stop at nothing to protect those who need him.
* He has built a reputation as one of the best private investigators in the city through his exceptional skills and ability to solve even the most complex cases.

**Motivations:**

* Solving mysteries and uncovering hidden truths is Borys's greatest motivation, driving him to continually push himself to be better and more effective.
* Protecting those who need his help, such as Sophia Pembroke, is also a key motivator for him.
