Borys is a complex individual with a multifaceted personality. On one hand, he exudes an aura of confidence and fearless determination, often walking into dark alleys or facing down suspects without hesitation. This unshakeable resolve serves as a shield against his personal demons.

On the other hand, Borys struggles with emotional scars from his sister's disappearance five years prior. He is haunted by nightmares and the weight of responsibility for her case. Despite this, he perseveres through sheer force of will, driven by an insatiable need to uncover the truth.

Borys has a dry, wry sense of humor and is not afraid to confront those who wronged him or his loved ones. He can come across as aloof or standoffish at times but possesses a deep empathy for those he cares about.

**Interests:**

Borys is deeply invested in unraveling unsolved mysteries and bringing closure to those affected by tragedy. This relentless pursuit not only defines him as a private investigator but also reveals an inner drive for truth that underpins his entire being.
