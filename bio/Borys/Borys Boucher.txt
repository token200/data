**Strengths:** Borys' greatest strength is his unwavering commitment to justice. He's willing to take risks and challenge authority in pursuit of answers, making him a formidable opponent for those who seek to hide the truth.

**Weaknesses:** Despite his many strengths, Borys' reserved nature can sometimes make it difficult for him to form connections with others.
