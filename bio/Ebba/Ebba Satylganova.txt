Here is a summary of the fictional character Ebba Satylganova:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing emerald green
- Hair: Dark, wavy, and often styled in loose, ethereal waves that frame her heart-shaped face.
- Skin Tone: Porcelain-like complexion with a subtle sheen, hinting at an Eastern European heritage.

**Personality:**
Ebba is an enigmatic and intense individual, known for her unwavering passion for art. She exudes confidence and charisma in the presence of like-minded individuals, but remains guarded when interacting with those outside her inner circle. Ebba's sharp wit and dark humor often leave people intrigued or intimidated, depending on their reaction to her words.

**Interests:**
- Art (painting, drawing, and collecting rare artwork)
- Gothic architecture and literature
- Dark mythology and the supernatural
- Alternative music and avant-garde performances

Ebba is deeply fascinated by the macabre, the unknown, and the mysterious. Her art reflects these interests, often incorporating elements of gothic elegance, raw emotion, and a touch of the surreal. When meeting new people, Ebba tends to focus on finding those who share her passion for the darker side of life, as she senses that they are "kindred spirits" - individuals who can appreciate her unique perspective on the world.

Despite her reserved nature, Ebba has an undeniable allure and magnetism that draws people in. She is a force to be reckoned with, both in terms of art and personality, leaving a lasting impression on those fortunate enough to encounter her.
