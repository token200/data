Here's a summary of the fictional character Ebba Koval:

**Physical Attributes:**
Ebba Koval stands at 5'8" (172 cm) with an athletic yet slender build, allowing her to move freely and easily while practicing her magic. Her eyes are an striking shade of indigo, reminiscent of the night sky, which seem to sparkle with an inner light when she's channeling her powers.

**Personality:**
Ebba is a determined and brave individual who has faced numerous challenges throughout her life. Despite being underestimated by some due to her sex, she perseveres with unwavering resolve and a strong sense of justice. She possesses a deep connection to nature, which fuels her elemental manipulation abilities and makes her particularly attuned to the emotions and energies of those around her.

**Interests:**
Ebba's interests revolve primarily around magic and the natural world. As mentioned in the story, she has a special affinity for exploring the nearby forest and conversing with ancient trees. She also enjoys pouring over ancient texts, practicing spells, and studying the intricacies of elemental manipulation. Her passion for helping others and protecting the innocent drives her to use her powers for the greater good.

**Other Traits:**
As a nod to her Norse heritage, Ebba's parents named her after the goddess of fertility and prosperity, imbuing her with strong values of hope, abundance, and protection. She is fiercely loyal to those she cares about and will stop at nothing to defend them from harm.
