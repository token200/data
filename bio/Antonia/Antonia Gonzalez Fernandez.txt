Antonia stands at 5'8" (173 cm) with long, dark brown hair and piercing green eyes. Her features are striking, with high cheekbones and a small nose that adds to her determined and focused expression.

Her quick wit and sharp instincts make her a formidable opponent for those who seek to silence her.

She also enjoys reading historical fiction and playing guitar in her free time, finding solace in creative outlets to balance out the demands of her high-stress profession.

Born in Madrid, Spain, Antonia grew up surrounded by the rich culture and history of her native country. Her love for journalism was sparked at an early age, and she pursued it with dedication and passion, studying at the Complutense University of Madrid before beginning her career as a reporter. Throughout her journey, Antonia has faced numerous challenges, but her unwavering commitment to truth and justice has made her a respected figure in the field of journalism.
