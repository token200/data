Here is a summary of the fictional character Antonia Weber:

Physical Attributes: 
Antonia stands at 5'8" with an athletic build. Her piercing green eyes seem to be an unnerving intensity, drawing people into her presence. She has long, dark brown hair that falls in loose waves down her back.

Personality:
Antonia is a force to be reckoned with - perceptive and incisive. She's fiercely intelligent, always seeking the truth behind a person or event. Her piercing gaze can make those around her feel uncomfortable, but also intrigued. Antonia exudes confidence, a quiet authority that commands respect without demanding it.

Interests: 
Her passions include writing, particularly fiction. She finds solace in creating stories, often blending fact with fiction to capture the essence of Ravenswood's history. Antonia is deeply interested in people and their motivations - she believes that everyone has a story worth telling.
