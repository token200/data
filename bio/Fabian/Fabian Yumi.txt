Here is a summary of the fictional character Fabian Yumi:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
* Eye Color: Piercing green
* Hair: Raven-black, falling to his shoulders
* Build: Lean and athletic, with toned muscles from years of martial arts training

**Personality:**

* Enigmatic and mysterious, with an air of quiet confidence
* Driven by a fierce dedication to justice, yet seemingly detached from the world around him
* Charismatic and persuasive, able to inspire loyalty in those who follow him
* Highly intelligent and strategic, always thinking several steps ahead of his opponents
* Haunted by a sense of purpose that drives him to uncover the truth about "The Order"

**Interests:**

* Martial arts, particularly kenjutsu and parkour
* Acrobatics and aerial stunts
* Strategic planning and tactics
* Infiltration and deception ( Fabian has spent years honing his skills in these areas)
* Uncovering secrets and uncovering hidden truths

**Additional Traits:**

* Highly skilled in hand-to-hand combat, sword fighting, and strategy
* Can move with incredible agility and stealth, making him nearly impossible to catch
* Possesses an uncanny intuition that guides him towards the truth
* Loyal only to those he deems worthy of his trust, Fabian is a solitary figure who can be fiercely protective of his allies.

Overall, Fabian Yumi is a complex and intriguing character, driven by a fierce sense of justice and a need to uncover the truth about "The Order".
