* Ancient languages and texts

**Skills:**

* Extensive knowledge of ancient civilizations, languages, and texts
* Expertise in cryptography and code-breaking
* Skilled in hand-to-hand combat and wilderness survival techniques

Vitaliy's insatiable curiosity and passion for discovery drive her to explore the unknown, and her experiences have prepared her for the thrill of uncovering hidden secrets and unraveling the mysteries of the universe.
