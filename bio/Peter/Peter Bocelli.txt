Background:

Growing up in Lucca, Italy, Peter was exposed to music from a young age and began singing as a child. Despite struggling with his own vision impairments – he has low vision due to cataracts that led to permanent scarring in both eyes – Peter continued to pursue his passion for music.

Through his brother's stories and struggles, Peter learned the importance of perseverance, kindness, and humility. He sees himself as an artist who aims to make a difference through his performances, using his voice to inspire people around the world.
