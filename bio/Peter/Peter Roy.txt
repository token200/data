Here's a summary of the fictional character Peter Roy:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often tousled from the wind and sea spray
- Build: Lean and athletic, honed from years of sailing and physical activity

**Personality:**
- Peter is a passionate and driven individual with an unwavering love for the sea.
- He's brave, intelligent, and resourceful, able to think on his feet in emergency situations.
- Despite his accomplishments, he remains humble and grounded, never losing sight of the wonder and awe that the ocean inspires.

**Interests:**
- Sailing (he owns a sturdy sailing boat, the "Maverick's Revenge")
- Marine Biology, particularly Shark Research
- Exploration of the Ocean and its Creatures
- Conservation and Preservation of Marine Ecosystems

**Additional Traits:**
- Peter is an expert navigator and sailor, with extensive experience in navigating open waters.
- He has a deep respect for the ocean's power and majesty, and is not afraid to confront it head-on when necessary.
- His calm demeanor and soothing presence have earned him the nickname "shark whisperer," reflecting his unique ability to connect with even the most fearsome creatures.

Overall, Peter Roy is a complex and fascinating individual, driven by a lifelong passion for the sea and its inhabitants.
