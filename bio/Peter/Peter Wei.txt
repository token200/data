Here is a summary of the fictional character Peter Wei:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5'9" (175 cm)
- Eye Color: Bright, piercing blue
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often worn in a messy, piecey style
- Facial Features: Strong jawline, sharp cheekbones, and a small nose
- Build: Lean and athletic, with a wiry physique that allows him to easily navigate the digital realm

**Personality:**
Peter is an intensely focused and driven individual who throws himself wholeheartedly into his work. His intelligence and resourcefulness make him a formidable opponent in the world of hacking, but they also contribute to his intense competitiveness and occasional detachment from social relationships. Despite this, Peter has a strong sense of justice and uses his skills for good, particularly as a member of Zero Cool.

**Interests:**
- Hacking and cyber security
- Technology and artificial intelligence (his passion project is the AI technology developed by Marcus Thompson)
- Strategy and problem-solving
- Reading about history and philosophy to better understand the implications of his actions
- Playing video games and watching sci-fi movies for entertainment

**Skills and Talents:**
Peter is an expert hacker with unparalleled skills in breaking into secure systems, bypassing firewalls, and creating complex algorithms. He is also a master of disguise, able to adapt his appearance and persona to fit any situation.

Overall, Peter Wei is a brilliant, resourceful, and fiercely independent individual who is determined to make the world a better place through technology and hacking.
