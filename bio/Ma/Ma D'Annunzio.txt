Here's a summary of the fictional character Ma D'Annunzio:

**Physical Attributes:** Ma D'Annunzio stands at an average height of 5'10" (178 cm), with an athletic yet lean build that allows him to move swiftly and effortlessly through shadows. His eyes are an piercing shade of indigo, a unique color that seems to gleam in the dark. His hair is jet black, often kept short and neatly trimmed to maintain his focus on his art.

**Personality:** Ma D'Annunzio exudes confidence and precision, traits honed from years of training under the watchful eye of his mentor, Maestro dell'Ombra. He is a master of calculation and patience, able to wait for hours or even days to observe and gather information before taking action. Despite his skill and reputation, he remains humble and detached, often seeming aloof to those who don't know him.

**Interests:** As a member of the secretive guild, Ma D'Annunzio's primary interest lies in perfecting his art of shadow dancing. He spends countless hours studying the movements of others, observing patterns and anomalies that can be exploited for stealth and deception. He is also fascinated by the mysterious nature of their work, often pondering the intricacies of human psychology and behavior.

**Additional Details:** Ma D'Annunzio's nickname "dell'Ombra" (meaning "of the shadows") reflects his affinity with the night and its secrets. He has a natural affinity for darkness and uses it to his advantage, often appearing most vibrant in the dimmest of light conditions. His movements are fluid and calculated, as if he is one step ahead of everyone around him.
