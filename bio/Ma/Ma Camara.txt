* Storytelling: She has an innate gift for crafting tales that capture the imagination, drawing on mythology, folklore, and personal experiences to weave a rich tapestry of wonder.
* Nature and spirituality: Ma Camara's connection to the natural world is profound, reflecting in her emphasis on the cycles of nature, interconnectedness, and the magic that lies within everyday life.
