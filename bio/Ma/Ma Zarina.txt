**Height:** 5'8" (173 cm)

**Interests:** Weaving, spinning, and textiles are Ma Zarina's greatest passions. She finds joy in creating beautiful fabrics that tell stories of her village and its people. Ma Zarina's love for weaving is not just about creating functional garments, but about preserving tradition, sharing stories, and connecting with others through her craft. Her legacy extends beyond the village, as she strives to inspire future generations of weavers to carry on the art form.
