Here is a summary of the fictional character Jon Cannavaro:

Physical Attributes: 
- Height: 6 feet 2 inches (188 cm)
- Eye Color: Dark brown, almost black
- Hair Color: Short, dark brown hair that's often styled in a messy, unkempt manner

Personality:
- Independent and reserved by nature, preferring to observe the world from a safe distance
- Resourceful and talented individual with a keen eye for detail
- Initially hesitant to open up to others due to past experiences of being shuffled between foster homes
- Has a dry sense of humor and can come across as aloof or standoffish to those who don't know him

Interests:
- Mechanic work, specifically fixing cars and taking them apart to understand how they work
- Classic cars, particularly old American models
- Spending time alone, reading, and exploring the outdoors
- Hobbies are relatively low-key and don't draw much attention to himself

Background: 
- Spent his childhood moving between foster homes, leading to a sense of uncertainty and instability
- Found solace in his work as a mechanic and developed a reputation for being reliable and trustworthy
- Has a mysterious past that he keeps hidden from those who get close to him, including the details about his life before moving to the small town where he currently lives.
