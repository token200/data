Here is a summary of the fictional character Jon Aurelius Valerius Diocletianus:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 6 feet 2 inches (188 cm)
* Eye Color: Piercing blue, almost iridescent
* Hair: Dark brown, often worn in a dignified style befitting his imperial stature
* Build: Lean and athletic, honed from years of martial training and imperial duties

**Personality:**

* Confident and assertive, with an unwavering conviction in the supremacy of the Roman gods
* Intelligent and strategic thinker, with a keen eye for politics and governance
* Loyal to Rome and its people, willing to make difficult decisions for the greater good
* Introspective and self-aware, capable of experiencing moments of mortal humility and awe
* Charismatic leader, able to inspire loyalty and devotion in his subjects

**Interests:**

* Politics and governance, with a passion for streamlining administration and economy
* The imperial cult, with a fervent dedication to the worship of Roman gods
* History and scholarship, with a deep appreciation for the preservation of knowledge
* Military campaigns and conquests, driven by a desire to expand Rome's borders and secure its legacy
* Philosophy and spirituality, exploring the nature of power and mortality

**Other Details:**

* Diocletianus is a member of the esteemed Aurelian dynasty, with a long line of ancestors who have shaped the course of Roman history.
* He has forged unbreakable bonds with key leaders and statesmen, including his closest advisor, Aurelius Marcellinus.
* Despite his imposing figure and strong convictions, Diocletianus is not without vulnerability, having experienced moments of mortality and self-doubt throughout his reign.
