Here is a summary of the fictional character Callum Delacroix:

Physical Attributes: 
Callum Delacroix stands at 6'1" with an athletic yet lean build. His dark brown hair is always perfectly messy, framing his chiseled features and piercing green eyes that seem to see right through you.

Personality:
Callum is a private investigator in his mid-30s, known for being fiercely independent and intensely focused on his cases. He has a dry sense of humor and can come across as aloof or standoffish to those who don't know him. However, once you gain his trust, he reveals a deep sense of empathy and loyalty, which is evident in the way he connects with Colette.

Interests:
As a private investigator, Callum's primary interest lies in solving mysteries and uncovering the truth. He has a keen eye for detail and is highly skilled in forensic analysis. Outside of his work, Callum enjoys exploring new cities, sampling local cuisine, and indulging in fine whiskey.

Additional Traits:
Callum has a deep sense of justice, which motivates him to take on cases that involve vulnerable individuals or those who have been wronged. He is fiercely protective of those he cares about and will stop at nothing to ensure their safety. His years of experience as an investigator have also honed his intuition and ability to read people, making him an exceptional detective.

Background:
Callum's past is somewhat shrouded in mystery, but it is clear that he has a complicated history with his family. The fact that he was hired by Colette's wealthy father to find out what happened to her suggests that there may be more to their connection than meets the eye.
