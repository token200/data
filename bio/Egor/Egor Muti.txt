Here is a summary of the fictional character Egor Muti:

**Physical Attributes:**
Egor Muti stands at an impressive 6'2" with a lean yet athletic build, which reflects his tough and resilient past. His piercing blue eyes seem to hold secrets that only he knows, and they sparkle with a hint of mischief when he smiles. His dark hair is often messy and unkempt, adding to his rough-around-the-edges charm.

**Personality:**
Egor has a commanding presence that draws people in without him even trying. He exudes confidence and self-assurance, but at the same time, there's an air of vulnerability about him that makes you want to get closer. His mysterious past has given him a sense of inner strength and resilience, which he channels into his art.

**Interests:**
Egor is passionate about his art, and it seems to be the driving force behind his life. He's deeply emotional and expressive, often pouring his feelings onto the canvas through bold brushstrokes and vivid colors. He's also fiercely independent and values his freedom above all else. Despite his success, he remains humble and true to himself, using his platform to give back to his community.

**Other Details:**
Egor grew up on the streets of Moscow, where he learned to survive by his wits and his fists. He's a natural-born artist who has honed his skills through years of practice and dedication. His art is his escape, his sanctuary, and his ticket to the world beyond the rough-and-tumble streets of his youth.
