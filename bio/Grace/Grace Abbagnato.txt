Here is a summary of the fictional character Grace Abbagnato:

**Physical Attributes:** Grace has long, curly brown hair and piercing green eyes. Her height is 5'8" (173 cm), which adds to her striking presence on stage.

She is deeply committed to her craft, spending hours perfecting her sound and pouring her heart into every performance. Fans adore her for her raw, emotive vocals and infectious dance moves. She has a strong sense of self-awareness and knows exactly what she wants to achieve in life.

**Interests:** In addition to singing and performing with Electric Storm, Grace enjoys playing the piano. She discovered her passion for music when she stumbled upon an old piano in the backstage area during a show. Since then, playing the piano has become a regular part of her routine, allowing her to express herself and find peace.
