Here is a summary of the fictional character Nora Schäfer:

**Physical Attributes:** Nora stands at around 5'8" (173 cm) with striking features that reflect her East Berlin upbringing. Her hair is dark brown and cut in a practical, no-nonsense style, while her eyes are an expressive shade of hazel. She has a small tattoo of a pen nib on her left wrist, symbolizing her passion for journalism.

Despite her tough exterior, Nora has a compassionate side and is driven by a strong sense of justice.

**Interests:** As a freelance journalist, Nora's work takes up most of her time, but when she's not chasing down leads or researching stories, she enjoys exploring the streets of Berlin, trying out new restaurants and cafes, and practicing yoga to relax. She is an avid reader and has a particular interest in history, particularly the complex and often contentious politics of East Germany.

**Other Details:** Nora's small apartment is cluttered with books, papers, and various gadgets used for her work as a journalist. She is tech-savvy and uses social media to connect with sources and share her stories, but she also values anonymity and discretion in her investigations. Her relationship with her family is complicated due to the trauma of growing up under Stasi surveillance, but she has since found solace in her work and the sense of purpose it gives her.

Overall, Nora Schäfer is a complex and driven individual who will stop at nothing to uncover the truth, even if it means risking her own safety. Her skills as a journalist are matched by her tenacity and determination, making her a formidable opponent for corruption and injustice in Berlin.
