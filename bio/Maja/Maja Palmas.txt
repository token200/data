Here's a summary of the fictional character Maja Palmas:

**Physical Attributes:** Maja is a 28-year-old woman with striking features. She stands at around 5'8" (173 cm) tall, with a lean and athletic build. Her hair is dark brown and shoulder-length, often styled in loose waves that frame her heart-shaped face. Her bright hazel eyes sparkle with intensity, conveying her passion for storytelling. A small tattoo of a pen nib adorns her left wrist, symbolizing her profession.

**Personality:** Maja is a fearless and ambitious individual, driven by a strong sense of justice and a desire to give voice to those who have been marginalized or silenced. Her sharp instincts and quick thinking allow her to navigate the complexities of investigative journalism with ease. She is fiercely independent and determined to uncover the truth, even if it means facing criticism and backlash. Maja's unwavering conviction and strong sense of self make her a compelling presence in the stories she tells.

**Interests:** Maja has a deep passion for storytelling, social justice, and human rights. She finds solace in the words of great journalists like Ida B. Wells and Anna Politkovskaya, who have inspired her to take risks and challenge the status quo. In her free time, Maja enjoys reading novels by Brazilian authors, listening to Afro-Brazilian music, and practicing yoga to center herself before each assignment. Her love for photography is also evident in the way she captures the vibrant colors and textures of the favelas, using them as visual metaphors for the stories she tells.

Despite her tough exterior, Maja has a soft spot for stray animals and often finds solace in their company when working late nights.
