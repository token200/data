Here is a summary of the fictional character Maja Casanova:

**Physical Attributes:**
Maja stands at 5'8" (173 cm) with a slender yet athletic build. Her piercing green eyes are almond-shaped and fringed with thick eyelashes, giving her an striking and captivating gaze. Her raven-black hair falls in loose waves down her back, framing her heart-shaped face. Maja's features are finely chiseled, with high cheekbones and a pointed chin, lending to her regal and sophisticated appearance.

**Personality:**
Maja is a complex and multifaceted individual, hiding behind an icy exterior that she has built over the years. Her sharp wit and piercing gaze can be intimidating, but beneath this façade lies a sensitive and vulnerable person. Maja has a quick wit and can be sarcastic at times, but when she feels comfortable around someone, her true emotions shine through. She is fiercely independent and guarded, having been hurt in past relationships.

**Interests:**
Maja is an actress who is deeply passionate about her craft, spending countless hours rehearsing lines and perfecting her performances. She has a strong affinity for the arts, often incorporating music, dance, or poetry into her work. Maja's interests also extend to philosophy and existentialism, with whom she finds common ground in the works of Friedrich Nietzsche and Jean-Paul Sartre. In addition to these pursuits, Maja is an avid reader and enjoys exploring the world through literature and travel.

**Additional Traits:**
Maja is highly intelligent and resourceful, able to think on her feet and adapt to any situation. Her years in the spotlight have taught her the importance of poise and composure, but this comes at a cost – she has difficulty showing vulnerability or letting her guard down around others. Despite this, Maja possesses a dry sense of humor and can be wickedly funny when given the chance.

Maja's character is multifaceted and intriguing, hiding both strengths and weaknesses beneath her polished exterior.
