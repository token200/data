Here is a summary of the fictional character Teresa Sepp:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5'9" (175 cm)
- Eye Color: Bright blue
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often worn in a ponytail or tied back during competitions.
- Body Type: Lean and athletic, honed from years of rowing.

**Personality:**
- Determined: Teresa is driven by her passion for rowing and will stop at nothing to achieve her goals.
- Resilient: She has faced numerous injuries and setbacks throughout her career but never gives up on herself or her ambitions.
- Humble: Despite her success, Teresa remains grounded and grateful for the support of those around her.
- Inspirational: Her dedication to rowing and inspiring others makes her a role model for many young athletes.

**Interests:**
- Rowing (of course!)
- Fitness and wellness
- Outdoor activities such as hiking and skiing
- Spending time with family and friends, especially those who have supported her throughout her career.
- Philanthropy, particularly in supporting rowing programs for underprivileged youth.
