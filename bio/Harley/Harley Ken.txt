Here is a summary of the fictional character Harley Ken:

- Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue, often described as an intense shade that seems to bore into those he meets.

- Despite the turmoil within, Harley possesses an unrelenting passion for storytelling and justice.

- Research: His keen analytical mind is constantly searching for evidence to back up his theories and expose truth.
- Investigative Journalism: When faced with tips about scandals or wrongdoing, Harley becomes consumed by uncovering the facts and unmasking those responsible.

Given his turbulent past and inner struggles, Harley Ken navigates life's complexities with a mix of determination and self-doubt.
