Here is a summary of the fictional character Harley Gómez Pérez:

- Hair Color: Dark brown hair often styled in a messy, artistic manner

Harley is a passionate and expressive individual who wears his heart on his sleeve. Despite his growing fame, Harley remains humble and true to his roots, always seeking to tell stories that resonate with others. His confidence and charisma draw people in, making him a compelling conversationalist.

- Graffiti art and street art
- Social justice and community activism
- Latin American culture and history
- Music (he is particularly fond of traditional Latin American music)
- Philosophy and poetry
- Traveling and exploring new cities to find inspiration for his art

Harley's interests are deeply intertwined with his artistic expression, as he seeks to use his work to spark meaningful conversations and inspire others to tell their own stories.
