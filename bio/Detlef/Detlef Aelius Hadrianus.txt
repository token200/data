Here is a summary of the fictional character Detlef Aelius Hadrianus:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 5'11" (180 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue
- Hair: Dark brown, often styled with a hint of elegance
- Build: Lean and athletic, honed from years of service in the imperial guard

Personality:
- Detlef is a charismatic leader with an air of quiet confidence, born out of his experiences serving under Emperor Hadrian.
- He possesses exceptional strategic thinking, quick wit, and silver-tongued diplomacy skills acquired during his time as a scribe and advisor to the emperor.
- Detlef is humble yet calculating, often using his enigmatic nature to keep people at arm's length until he reveals his true intentions.

Interests:
- Politics: A natural-born strategist, Detlef has an unrelenting passion for understanding the intricacies of Roman politics and navigating its complex web of alliances and rivalries.
- Negotiation: He excels in high-pressure negotiations, using his silver tongue to achieve mutually beneficial outcomes that often surprise those who underestimate him.
- History: As a mastermind, Detlef has an interest in historical events and how they can inform future strategies.
