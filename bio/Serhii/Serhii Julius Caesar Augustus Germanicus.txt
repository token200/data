- Ambitious, driven to make a lasting impact on history

Interests:
- Military strategy and tactics
- Leadership and diplomacy
- History, particularly the conquests of Julius Caesar and Germanicus
- Architecture and engineering, with a passion for designing grand structures that reflect the glory of Rome
- Hunting and equestrianism, enjoying time spent in nature and honing his skills on horseback

Family Legacy:
Serhii's family name is synonymous with honor, courage, and wisdom. He takes pride in carrying on this legacy, particularly by living up to the expectations set by his illustrious ancestors: Julius Caesar, Augustus, and Germanicus.
