**Interests:** Andrii's singular focus on neuroscience has led him to become an authority on the subject, and his research spans a wide range of topics, including synaptic plasticity, brain function, and consciousness. His interests also extend to reading about various philosophical theories and literary works that explore the human condition.

**Additional Details:** Andrii's appearance may be often mistaken for that of a teenager due to his unkempt hair and glasses, but he exudes an air of authority and confidence that belies his age. He is known to spend long hours locked away in his laboratory, surrounded by rows of humming computers and shelves lined with dusty tomes on neuroscience, where he spends most of his waking hours immersed in his research.

In short, Andrii Bergstrom is a brilliant, enigmatic scientist whose unique blend of intellect, passion, and reclusive nature has captivated the scientific community and set him on the path to potentially revolutionary discoveries about human consciousness.
