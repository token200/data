He is particularly drawn to capturing the stories of people from all walks of life, often focusing on themes of hope, perseverance, and resilience. In his free time, he enjoys exploring new places, trying new foods, and practicing yoga to center himself.

He struggled to find his place in the spotlight as his brother Leo basked in the glory of Hollywood. However, he discovered his own passion for photography at a young age and began to develop his skills through practice and dedication.

**Career:** Roman turned his passion for photography into a career, traveling the world and capturing breathtaking images that tell stories of hope and perseverance. His work has been met with critical acclaim, but more importantly, it has connected people on a deeper level, leaving a lasting impact on those who have seen his photographs.
