Here is a summary of the fictional character Roman Taro:

His eyes are a striking shade of piercing green, a trait that seems to draw people in and command their attention.

Despite this, he has a softer side and is deeply empathetic towards others, which is reflected in his art and interactions with Yuna.

**Interests:** Roman Taro's primary interests are art, design, and self-expression. As a freelance graphic designer by day, he brings his unique perspective to clients' projects. However, it is his nighttime passion for graffiti art that truly sets him ablaze. He loves exploring the city's hidden alleys and secret gardens with Yuna, discovering new wonders and themes to inspire his murals.
