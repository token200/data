Here's a summary of the fictional character Roman Sten:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 6 feet (183 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing blue, with a sharp, intelligent glint
- Build: Lean and athletic, honed from years of physical activity and hand-to-hand combat training
- Facial Features: Strong jawline, prominent nose, and piercing eyes that seem to bore into those he interrogates

**Personality:**
Roman Sten is a rugged and resourceful detective with a sharp mind and an unwavering determination. He's fiercely independent and has a strong sense of justice, which drives him to uncover the truth behind each case. Roman has a natural affinity for reading people and can often detect subtle tells that others might miss. His years of experience have taught him to trust his instincts, but he also recognizes when those instincts need to be challenged.

**Interests:**
Roman is passionate about understanding the darker corners of New Eden's underbelly. He spends hours researching, interviewing witnesses, and scouring crime scenes for clues. Roman is drawn to puzzles and riddles, often seeing them as a way to uncover hidden truths. He has a fascination with ancient mythology and cults, which seems to be connected to his work on the Cult of the Black Depths case.

**Habits:**
Roman tends to pace when thinking through a case, often muttering to himself as he tries to connect the dots. He's known for being fiercely competitive, both in his professional life and personal relationships. Roman has a habit of collecting small artifacts related to cases, which can sometimes be seen in his apartment or office.

**Strengths:**
Roman Sten is an expert interrogator, able to extract valuable information from even the most reluctant sources. He's highly intelligent and resourceful, often thinking outside the box when faced with complex problems. Roman has a natural talent for hand-to-hand combat, which comes in handy during high-pressure situations.

**Weaknesses:**
Roman can be overly self-reliant at times, neglecting his own safety or the advice of those around him. His driven personality can sometimes lead to burnout, causing him to overlook essential details or take unnecessary risks. Roman has a tendency to push people away when they get too close, fearing that they'll become entangled in his cases and risk getting hurt.
