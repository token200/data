Here is a summary of the fictional character Boris Grabowski:

- Height: 6'2" (188 cm) - a tall and imposing figure that commands attention

- Hair: Dark brown and cut short, framing his angular face and adding to his rugged appearance
- Build: Lean and athletic, honed from years of physical training and high-stakes action

- Introspective and brooding, Boris has a dry sense of humor and can come across as aloof or standoffish to those who don't know him
- Loyal and protective once you've earned his trust, he will stop at nothing to defend the people he cares about
- Haunted by his past mistakes and regrets, Boris struggles with feelings of guilt and inadequacy
- A natural strategist and problem-solver, he is always thinking several steps ahead

**Interests:** 
- Mechanic work at the local garage keeps his hands busy and provides a sense of normalcy in his chaotic life
- Reading and research are crucial skills for an ex-spymaster like Boris, who must stay informed about global events and threats
- Martial arts training continues to be essential, even after years of living under cover, as he must remain prepared to face danger at any moment
- A quiet appreciation for art, music, and literature provides a creative outlet for his more emotional side
