**Personality:**

Mats Magi is an enigmatic figure with a commanding presence. He exudes quiet confidence, which commands respect from even the most skilled mages. Despite his impressive stature as the "Master of Arcane Theory," Mats Magi appears humble and approachable, as evident in his warm smile when interacting with Elara. His words are laced with wisdom, conveyed through a conspiratorial tone that suggests he is sharing secrets with someone he trusts.

**Interests:**

Mats Magi's primary interest lies in the study of magical theory and spellcraft. He has dedicated his life to understanding the intricacies of magic, which has led him to write extensively on the subject. His magnum opus, "The Fundamentals of Spellcraft," is a testament to his extensive knowledge and research.

Beyond his academic pursuits, Mats Magi values personal growth and self-improvement. He sees the true power of magic not in its theory but in its application, which suggests that he believes in hands-on learning and experimentation.
