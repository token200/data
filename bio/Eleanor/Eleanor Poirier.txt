Here's a summary of the fictional character Eleanor Poirier:

Physical Attributes: 
- Height: 5'8"
- Eye Color: Warm Hazel

Personality:
Eleanor is a compassionate and empathetic person, shaped by her upbringing in a close-knit community where family ties are deeply valued. She exudes warmth and kindness, having been raised with a strong sense of responsibility towards others. Eleanor's experiences have also made her resourceful and adaptable.

Interests: 
- Nature and outdoors; she has a deep appreciation for the natural world, as evident in her love for exploring the woods behind her childhood home.
- Cooking and food preparation - her grandmother taught her the art of traditional cooking that combines French and local flavors.
- Reading and learning new skills - Eleanor is an avid reader with a passion for self-improvement and expanding her knowledge on various subjects.

Background:
Eleanor grew up in Ashwood, where she was raised by her grandmother after her parents passed away. She went to school, developed friendships, and forged connections that are rooted deep within the local community. When her mother's condition worsened, Eleanor returned home to care for her and find solace in reconnecting with her roots.

Key qualities include:

- Strong sense of family loyalty
- Selfless nature
- Ability to appreciate the value of tradition while being open to change
- Passionate about giving back to her community
