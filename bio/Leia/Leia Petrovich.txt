Here is a summary of the personality, physical attributes, and interests of the fictional character Leia Petrovich:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)

**Personality:**
Leia is a brave and resourceful individual who exudes confidence and determination. She has a strong sense of duty and responsibility, stemming from her upbringing on Alderaan as a member of the royal family. As a leader in the Rebel Alliance, she possesses exceptional strategic thinking and problem-solving skills, which allows her to stay one step ahead of her enemies.

**Interests:**
- Politics and Governance
- Military Strategy and Tactics
- Diplomacy and Negotiation
- Leadership and Team Management

Leia is passionate about fighting for justice and freedom, driven by a strong sense of purpose. She values honesty, integrity, and loyalty, which she expects from herself and others. Despite the dangers and challenges that come with being a Rebel leader, Leia remains steadfast in her commitment to the cause.

Additionally, Leia has a dry sense of humor and can come across as sarcastic at times, particularly when dealing with her close friends like Han Solo. However, beneath her tough exterior lies a compassionate and empathetic person who genuinely cares about those around her.
