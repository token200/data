Here's a summary of the fictional character Leia Grabowska:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 5'8" (173 cm) - slightly above average height
* Eye Color: Bright Blue (a trait inherited from her astronomer parents)
* Hair Color: Dark Brown, often worn in a ponytail or tied back when she's working on cases
* Skin Tone: Fair with a subtle scattering of freckles across her nose and cheeks

**Personality:**

* Leia is a sharp-witted and resourceful individual who excels at problem-solving.
* She has an insatiable curiosity about the world, which drives her passion for astronomy and detective work.
* Despite her analytical nature, Leia has a compassionate side and always puts the needs of others before her own.
* As a guardian of the night sky, she feels a deep sense of responsibility to protect Metropolis from threats both human and supernatural.

**Interests:**

* Astronomy (specifically, celestial mechanics and stargazing)
* Detective work and solving puzzles
* Reading (especially science fiction and detective novels)
* Hiking and exploring the outdoors (a hobby encouraged by her astronomer parents)

Leia's unique blend of scientific expertise, analytical mind, and compassion for others makes her a formidable force in Metropolis as both an astronomer-turned-detective and a guardian of the night sky.
