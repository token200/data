Here's a summary of the fictional character Leia Jhih-hao:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Age: Late 20s/Early 30s
- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
- Eye Color: Hazel with golden undertones, which becomes more prominent when she's excited or concentrating.
- Hair: Dark brown, often styled in a messy bob that frames her heart-shaped face.
- Build: Slender yet athletic, thanks to her freelance journalist lifestyle requiring frequent travel and physical activity.

**Personality:**
Leia is determined and resourceful, with an innate curiosity for uncovering the truth. Her drive stems from a strong sense of justice and compassion, which motivates her to expose corruption and bring accountability to those in power. She's fiercely independent and can come across as aloof or standoffish at times, but beneath this tough exterior lies a deep empathy for those affected by injustice.

**Interests:**
- Investigative Journalism: Leia is passionate about digging up hard-hitting stories that shed light on systemic corruption and inequality.
- Politics: As an active observer of local politics, she's well-versed in the inner workings of government and has a keen eye for spotting patterns of malfeasance.
- Taiwanese Culture: Growing up in Taipei, Leia has a deep appreciation for traditional Taiwanese customs and values. She often incorporates these into her investigative work, highlighting the human impact of corruption on communities.

**Background:**
Leia's background is shrouded in mystery, with few knowing much about her past before she became a journalist. It's rumored that she has connections to some high-profile figures in Taiwan's politics and media circles, which has earned her both respect and suspicion among her peers. Despite this, Leia remains committed to her craft, driven by an unwavering sense of purpose.
