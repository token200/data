Here is a summary of the fictional character Leia Gustafsson:

**Physical Attributes:** Leia is a 5'8" (173 cm) woman with short, dark brown hair and piercing green eyes. Her features are striking, with high cheekbones and a determined jawline.

**Personality:** Leia is a sharp-witted and resourceful private investigator with a no-nonsense attitude. She has a strong sense of self-preservation and is not afraid to stand up for herself, even in the face of danger. Despite her tough exterior, she has a compassionate side and is driven by a desire to help those in need.

**Interests:** Leia's interests include:

* Investigating complex cases and solving mysteries
* Reading crime fiction and true crime books
* Practicing yoga and meditation to stay focused and centered
* Traveling to new places and experiencing different cultures
* Playing guitar and listening to music as a way to unwind

As a private investigator, Leia is fiercely independent and values her autonomy above all else. She has a strong sense of justice and will go to great lengths to uncover the truth and bring those responsible for wrongdoing to account.

**Background:** Leia's past is marked by a complicated history with Marcus Thompson, the wealthy businessman who hired her to find his missing daughter Sophia. Leia had been a babysitter for the Thompson family as a teenager and has since become a trusted confidant and ally to Marcus. However, her relationship with Viktor Petrov, the notorious Russian mobster, suggests that she may have deeper connections to the world of organized crime than initially meets the eye.
