He joined The Consortium at a young age, quickly proving himself as one of the organization's most skilled operatives. **Goals:** Zoltán's ultimate goal is to protect his country and the Western world from threats like the rogue nation he recently infiltrated. He is driven by a sense of duty and loyalty to The Consortium, but also by a desire to prove himself as one of the most skilled operatives in the organization.
