Here is a summary of the fictional character Ben Sanderson:

Physical Attributes:
Ben Sanderson stands at around 5'10" (178 cm) with a lean and worn physique, reflecting his tumultuous past. His eyes are a deep brown, almost piercing in intensity, which seems to hold a world of pain and desperation within them.

Personality:
Ben is a complex and brooding individual, haunted by the ghosts of his past mistakes. He is fiercely independent and has a strong sense of self-preservation, which makes it difficult for him to open up to others. However, he also has a deep-seated desire for redemption and escape from his dark circumstances.

Interests:
Ben's primary interest is snooker, and he is still driven by a passion for the game, even though his professional career was cut short due to addiction. He also has a fascination with mystery and puzzle-solving, which is evident in his attempt to unravel the secrets behind Annette Hargrove's sinister double life.

Other Details:
Ben's sense of style is often disheveled, reflecting his rough and troubled lifestyle. His voice is low and gravelly, carrying an air of weariness that only adds to his enigmatic presence. Despite his tough exterior, there are hints of vulnerability and desperation in his demeanor, making him a deeply human and relatable character.
