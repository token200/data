Here is a summary of the fictional character Sun Vasiliev:

**Name:** Sun Vasiliev (alternatively known as Alexandr Nikolaevich Vasiliev)

**Interests:** As mentioned in the story, Sun's affinity lies in the unknown and the cosmos. He spends most of his time studying ancient Greek philosophy, modern quantum mechanics, and cosmology. He is particularly fascinated by cosmic connections, hidden patterns, and the relationship between the mundane and divine worlds.

While little is known about his private life, it is speculated that he may be a conduit for the universe's hidden knowledge, bridging the boundaries between worlds on quiet nights when the stars shine bright enough to pierce the veil.
