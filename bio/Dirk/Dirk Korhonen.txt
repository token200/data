Dirk Korhonen is known for his unorthodox approach to life. He's a curious and resourceful individual who thrives on finding creative solutions to complex problems. Dirk's passion for tinkering and inventing drives most of his daily activities. He spends countless hours scouring junkyards and salvage yards for discarded materials to repurpose into something new. Dirk is particularly fascinated by ancient technologies, military hardware, and electronic engineering. In his free time, he enjoys reading about historical figures like Olaus Magnus Rönnblom and studying cryptography.

Dirk's latest obsession with the mysterious bike frame has led him down a rabbit hole of discovery, leaving him eager to unravel its secrets and uncover the truth behind Rönnblom's creation.
