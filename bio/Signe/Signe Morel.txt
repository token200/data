Signe is an introspective and creative individual who draws inspiration from the shadows and darkness. Despite her introverted nature, Signe values honesty and openness with those close to her. She's fiercely loyal to her friends and will share her deepest fears and doubts when needed. However, this vulnerability can also make her struggle to open up to others or admit her weaknesses.

- Painting (especially abstract art)
- Exploring the mysteries of life, death, and the supernatural
- Reading and learning about various artistic movements, from Gothic to Surrealism
- Attending underground art exhibitions and alternative gatherings

Signe's creative process is deeply personal and often fueled by her inner turmoil. When she's not painting, Signe can be found lost in books or exploring abandoned places that whisper secrets of the past.
