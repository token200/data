Here is a summary of the fictional character Aanya Salemi:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
* Eye Color: Warm Hazel
* Hair Color: Rich Brown with subtle hints of auburn
* Skin Tone: Porcelain-like complexion with a smattering of freckles across her cheeks and nose
* Body Type: Slender yet athletic build, honed from years of playing guitar and painting

**Personality:**

Aanya is known for her striking features and razor-sharp wit, which has made her an instant hit in Ashwood. She is fiercely determined and confident, with a strong sense of self-worth that was instilled in her by her eccentric artist mother, Lyra Salemi. Aanya's sharp intellect and quick wit have allowed her to navigate the cutthroat music industry with ease, but beneath her tough exterior lies a compassionate heart and a deep sense of vulnerability.

**Interests:**

* Painting: Aanya is an avid painter and has honed her skills under Emma Taylor's guidance. Her art reflects her experiences as an artist and musician.
* Music: As a singer-songwriter, Aanya pours her heart and soul into every performance. She draws inspiration from her own struggles and triumphs, often incorporating themes of love, loss, and self-discovery into her lyrics.
* Photography: In her free time, Aanya enjoys taking photos that capture the beauty of Ashwood's landscapes and its people.

**Quirks and Habits:**

Aanya has a habit of humming melodies as she paints or thinks about music. She is also known to carry around a sketchbook with her at all times, jotting down lyrics and ideas that strike her throughout the day. Despite her fame, Aanya remains humble and kind-hearted, often taking the time to talk to fans and share her story.
