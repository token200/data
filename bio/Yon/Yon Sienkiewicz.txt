Yon's interests are centered around theoretical physics, mathematics, and astronomy. He is particularly fascinated by dark matter and its potential applications. In his free time, Yon enjoys reading scientific texts, attending lectures on cosmology, and stargazing, often lost in thought as he contemplates the vast expanse of the universe.
