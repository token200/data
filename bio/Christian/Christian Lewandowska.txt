- Yoga and meditation
- Music (she plays the guitar and sings)
- Hiking and exploring nature
- Reading literary fiction and poetry

As an artist, Christian is constantly seeking inspiration in the world around her. She draws solace from the natural beauty of the world and finds that it influences her art significantly. Her interests also reflect a desire to stay grounded and connected with herself.

Christian's past experiences have left emotional scars, but she has learned to channel these feelings into her art. Through painting, she can express emotions that are difficult for her to verbalize or put into words.
