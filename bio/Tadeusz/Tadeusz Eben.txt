Tadeusz Eben is an enigmatic figure with a rich history and an air of mystery surrounding him. His past as a spy has instilled in him a strong sense of discipline, independence, and an ability to operate effectively in high-pressure situations.
Eben's nickname, "The Ghost," refers to his uncanny ability to fade into the background, observing without being noticed, making him an elusive figure even among those closest to him. 
Despite this persona, Eben is deeply intelligent and observant, able to pick up on subtle details that others might miss.

Interests:

- Surveillance and Security: Utilizing his expertise in surveillance and security to help clients
- Tactical Analysis: Conducting thorough analysis of scenarios to identify potential threats and vulnerabilities

Background Secrets:
Eben has an extensive background as a spy, with a plethora of skills that have contributed to his reputation. He is also haunted by a case known as "The Architect," which remains unsolved.
A tangled web of half-truths surrounds Eben's identity, making it difficult for others to discern what is real and what is fabricated.

Goals:
Tadeusz Eben seeks justice when the time is right and will pursue mysteries that pique his curiosity. With a strong desire to unravel any new puzzle, he sets out on a path of investigation.
