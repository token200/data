Interests:
- Nurym Junko is deeply interested in learning more about the mystical forces that govern the natural world. She spends many hours studying ancient tomes and seeking guidance from the tribe's spiritual leaders.
- A skilled archer and horseback rider, Nurym Junko enjoys hunting and tracking in the wilderness surrounding her homeland.
