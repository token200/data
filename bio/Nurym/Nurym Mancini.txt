Nurym stands at approximately 5'2" (157 cm) with an athletic yet petite build. Her striking green eyes are almond-shaped and fringed with thick lashes, adding to her mysterious allure. Her jet-black hair is often styled in loose waves, framing her heart-shaped face.

**Interests:**
Nurym's interests revolve around music, composition, and performance. As a classically trained pianist, she has a profound appreciation for the beauty of sound and its ability to evoke emotions. She is also drawn to jazz and pop music, often incorporating these genres into her unique sound. Nurym is a creative soul who seeks inspiration from life's experiences and observations, using them as fuel for her art.

Nurym's modest upbringing in Paris has instilled in her a strong sense of gratitude and appreciation for the artistic community that supported her family. Her talent and passion have earned her widespread recognition, but she remains grounded and true to her artistic vision.
