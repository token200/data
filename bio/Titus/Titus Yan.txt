Here is a summary of the fictional character Titus Yan:

**Physical Attributes:** 
- Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
- Eye Color: Piercing brown eyes with hints of warmth, reflecting his sharp intellect and seasoned detective work.
- Hair Color: Jet-black hair, often messy and unkempt from long hours of investigation.

**Personality:**
Titus is a confident, resourceful, and fiercely independent individual. His years as a renowned detective in Shanghai's underbelly have instilled in him a dry wit, street smarts, and an ability to read people and situations effectively. As he navigates the complex web of crime and deception, Titus exudes a sense of calm determination, never backing down from a challenge.

**Interests:**
Titus is driven by his insatiable curiosity about human nature and the darker aspects of society. He finds fascination in solving mysteries that others deem unsolvable. When not delving into the world of crime, Titus enjoys indulging in his love for fine Chinese cuisine, particularly traditional Shanghai dishes.

**Additional Traits:**
- Highly intelligent, with a deep understanding of psychology and human behavior.
- A skilled smoker, often turning to cigarettes as a coping mechanism during stressful moments.
- Physically fit from years of working out to maintain a healthy lifestyle amidst the grueling demands of his profession.
