- Hair Color: Dark brown, often neatly combed to conceal any noticeable signs of dishevelment.

**Personality:**
Titus Ming is an enigmatic figure, shrouded in mystery. He possesses a commanding presence that demands attention without ever seeming demanding. His piercing blue eyes seem to hold a world of knowledge, as if searching for hidden secrets beneath the surface. Despite his reputation for being involved in clandestine experiments, Titus appears charming and warm, belied by an air of quiet confidence. When engaged on topics he's passionate about, such as his research into human cognition, he exudes intensity, bordering on obsession.

**Interests:**
- Unraveling the secrets of the human mind
- Studying the intricacies of human cognition
- Mapping the subconscious mind
- Delving into realms of dreams and hidden memories within the human psyche

Given his background as a former neurosurgeon who turned to studying human cognition after a near-fatal accident, Titus's fascination with understanding the workings of the human brain is all-consuming. His involvement in clandestine experiments fuels rumors about the nature of his research, but it also showcases his unwavering dedication to uncovering the mysteries of the mind.

While some might view him as brilliant and innovative, others might label him mad or recklessly adventurous, a line Titus seems comfortable walking between brilliance and madness. His laboratory, where he spends most of his waking hours tinkering with machinery and gears that hum softly in the background, is a testament to this divide.
