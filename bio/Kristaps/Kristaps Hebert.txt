Here is a summary of the fictional character Kristaps Hebert:

Physical Attributes:
- Age: 25
- Height: 5'10"
- Eye Color: Warm brown eyes that sparkle with creativity

Personality:
Kristaps is a passionate and expressive individual, deeply rooted in his love for art and music. He has an infectious enthusiasm for sharing his knowledge of the city's cultural heritage and jazz scene. As a result, he is kind, charismatic, and possesses exceptional communication skills. Kristaps' warmth and kindness make him a beloved figure among those who meet him.

Interests:
- Art (painting, drawing, and mixed media)
- Music (jazz, blues, and New Orleans-inspired sounds)
- History and Cultural Heritage of New Orleans
- Meeting new people and engaging in conversations about art, music, and life

Background:
Growing up in a family of musicians exposed Kristaps to the world of jazz at an early age. His love for art developed as he spent hours watching old jazz films with his grandfather. After studying art in college, Kristaps pursued a career in visual arts, gradually building his skills and reputation through various mediums and techniques.
