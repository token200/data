Archie is a complex, dynamic individual with a deep passion for music. He's fiercely loyal to his bandmates and will do whatever it takes to protect them, even if it means sacrificing his own creative vision.

Despite his rough exterior, Archie has a sensitive soul and is deeply empathetic towards those around him. He's intensely emotional and can be prone to mood swings, which often leave his loved ones bewildered. However, he's also fiercely determined and will stop at nothing to achieve his goals.

* Nature: Despite living in the midst of a bustling city, Archie has a deep love for the outdoors. He finds solace in hiking, camping, or simply spending time near the ocean.
