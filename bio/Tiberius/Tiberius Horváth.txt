Here is a summary of the fictional character Tiberius Horváth:

**Physical Attributes:**

* Height: 6'1" (185 cm)
* Eye Color: Piercing blue
* Hair: Dark brown, worn long and unkempt
* Facial Features: Strong jawline, sharp cheekbones, and piercing blue eyes that seem to bore into those he meets.
* Skin Tone: Olive-toned from his Hungarian heritage

**Personality:**

Tiberius is a complex and brooding individual with a taste for politics, adventure, and intrigue. He is fiercely intelligent and intensely curious, always seeking answers to the questions that plague him. His years as an émigré have made him increasingly paranoid and cautious, but also driven by a sense of justice and a desire to uncover the truth.

He has a dry, wry sense of humor and can come across as aloof or standoffish to those who don't know him. However, once you gain his trust, he is fiercely loyal and will go to great lengths to protect those he cares about.

**Interests:**

* Politics and governance
* History, particularly the complex and troubled history of Hungary under various regimes
* Secret societies and clandestine organizations (his involvement with The Assembly has drawn him in deeply)
* Mystery and detection (he is a skilled observer and analyst)
* Philosophy and ideology (he is deeply interested in the nature of power and its impact on society)

**Background:**

Born in Budapest, Hungary, Tiberius was raised in a family of intellectuals and activists. His early life was marked by exposure to revolutionary ideas and activism, which shaped his worldview and sense of purpose. As he grew older, he became increasingly disillusioned with the limitations and corruption within Hungarian politics, leading him to seek opportunities abroad.

Over time, he developed a taste for adventure and politics, becoming involved in various secret societies and clandestine organizations. His involvement with The Assembly has drawn him into a complex web of intrigue and deception, where nothing is as it seems and loyalty is constantly tested.
