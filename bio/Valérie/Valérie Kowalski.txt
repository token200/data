Here is a summary of the fictional character Valérie Kowalski:

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
- Eye Color: Dark brown
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often styled in a neat and professional manner
- Skin Tone: Olive complexion with subtle scars on her cheeks from years of studying

Personality:
- Introspective and analytical, with a sharp mind that can easily become absorbed in complex problems.
- Highly intelligent and resourceful, with an unwavering dedication to uncovering the truth.
- Struggles with emotional intimacy due to a history of prioritizing work over personal relationships.
- Can be fiercely independent and self-reliant, sometimes at the expense of her own well-being.

Interests:
- Forensic psychology and criminology
- Research and reading on psychological profiling and behavioral analysis
- Hiking and solo outdoor activities for stress relief and mental clarity

Other notable traits:
- Valérie has a small tattoo of a quote from Friedrich Nietzsche's "Beyond Good and Evil" on her left wrist, representing her fascination with the darker aspects of human nature.
- She is an avid collector of rare books on psychology and criminology, often finding solace in their musty pages during quiet moments.

Despite her tough exterior, Valérie has a deep sense of vulnerability and empathy for those who have suffered at the hands of crime. Her relentless pursuit of justice drives her to push beyond the boundaries of what is acceptable, making her both brilliant and vulnerable to obsession.
