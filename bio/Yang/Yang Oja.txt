Here's a summary of the fictional character Yang Oja:

**Name:** Yang Oja (meaning "Golden Feather" in the ancient language)

**Physical Attributes:**
- **Height:** 5'8" (173 cm) - a statuesque figure with an athletic build, hinting at her agility and quick reflexes.
- **Eye Color:** Warm golden brown eyes that sparkle like the sun itself, reflecting her radiant personality.

**Personality:**
Yang Oja is a compassionate and empathetic individual who has dedicated herself to healing and helping others. She is driven by a strong sense of purpose and an unwavering desire to alleviate suffering. Her natural talent for herbalism and healing makes her an extraordinary caregiver, earning her the respect and admiration of those around her. Yang Oja's warmth, kindness, and selflessness have made her a beloved figure in Kaito village.

**Interests:**
Yang Oja is deeply passionate about herbalism, botany, and the ancient art of healing. She spends most of her time studying rare plants, experimenting with new remedies, and honing her skills under the guidance of the village elder. Her love for nature and its secrets has led her to explore remote mountains and valleys in search of rare herbs and wisdom.
