Frannie stands at 5'8" (173 cm) with long, dark brown hair often tied up in a loose bun and expressive green eyes. Her skin has a warm, olive undertone due to her Roman heritage. She has a slender yet athletic build, honed from years of exploring the great outdoors.

Despite her tough exterior, Frannie has a soft spot for underdogs and is always willing to help those in need.

Frannie's primary interests lie in botany, exploration, and discovery. She's an avid collector of rare plant species and spends countless hours studying their properties and potential applications. Her love of adventure often leads her to venture into the unknown, seeking out new discoveries that will further her understanding of the natural world.

When not on an expedition or in her laboratory, Frannie enjoys sipping strong coffee at bustling market squares (like the one she was in during the story), reading about history, mythology, and ancient cultures. Born into a wealthy Roman merchant family, Frannie was raised with every advantage. However, her parents' disapproval of her choice of career as a botanist didn't deter her. With their support, she pursued her passion for science and exploration, eventually earning a reputation as one of the leading botanists of her time.
