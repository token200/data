Andrius has a deep appreciation for the Roman gods and their mythology. He often seeks guidance from the wisdom of ancient texts and philosophical treatises on strategy and leadership. When not engaged in his duties as Magister Militum, he enjoys quiet evenings spent with family and close friends, sharing stories of his adventures.
