Here's a summary of the fictional character Andreas Woźniak:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 6'2" (188 cm)
- Eye Color: Warm, honey-brown eyes that seem to sparkle with curiosity
- Hair: A rugged, sandy-blond beard and hair that falls across his forehead in unruly locks

**Personality:**
Andreas Woźniak is a perfectionist and an artisan at heart. He is intensely passionate about restoring old machines and bringing them back to life. His dedication and attention to detail have earned him a reputation as one of the finest restorers in his field.

He is also fiercely independent, having struck out on his own after years of apprenticing with local mechanics. Despite this, he has an inherent sense of fairness and will go to great lengths to help those in need. His willingness to take on the rare 1920s motorcycle project for a wealthy collector showcases his commitment to artistry over profit.

**Interests:**
- Machine restoration and repair
- Tinkering with various contraptions and gadgets
- History of industrial design and innovation
- Classical music (particularly blues and folk)

Andreas' love for machines, tinkering, and restoring them is evident in everything he does. He takes great pride in his work and will go to extraordinary lengths to ensure that every machine he restores reaches its full potential.
