- Reading: In her free time, Wiktoria enjoys reading books on history, politics, and current events. She also keeps up-to-date with the latest developments in science and technology.
- Hiking: Despite being a city girl at heart, Wiktoria finds solace in hiking and exploring nature's beauty. This helps her clear her mind and recharge for the next investigation.

Background:
Wiktoria's upbringing had a significant impact on shaping her into the person she is today. Her parents, both accomplished journalists themselves, instilled in her a strong sense of integrity and a desire to tell stories that needed to be told. This upbringing helped Wiktoria develop her skills as an investigative journalist and set her on the path to becoming one of the most respected and fearless reporters of her time.

Overall, Wiktoria Sharma is a force to be reckoned with in the world of journalism. Her unyielding determination, sharp mind, and quick wit make her a powerful voice for justice and accountability.
