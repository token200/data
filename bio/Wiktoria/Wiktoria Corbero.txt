**Interests:**
Wiktoria's passions lie at the intersection of magic and justice. She is deeply committed to uncovering the truth and bringing wrongdoers to account, particularly when it comes to powerful magical artifacts like the Starheart Crystal. Outside of her academic pursuits, Wiktoria enjoys studying ancient runes, practicing yoga, and exploring the mystical connections between nature and magic.

**Background:** Little is known about Wiktoria's personal history, but it is rumored that she has faced significant challenges in her past, which have driven her to become a leading expert in her field. Despite this, she remains fiercely independent and non-conformist, always challenging authority and pushing the boundaries of what is possible with magic.

Overall, Wiktoria Corbero is a complex and intriguing character, driven by a strong sense of justice and a deep passion for magic.
