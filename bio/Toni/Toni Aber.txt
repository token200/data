Here is a summary of the fictional character Toni Aber:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
- Eye Color: Warm, rich brown eyes that seem to hold a deep intensity
- Hair: Long, curly dark brown hair often worn in loose waves down her back
- Skin Tone: Smooth, sun-kissed complexion with a subtle golden glow

**Personality:**
- Toni is a deeply expressive and empathetic person who wears her heart on her sleeve.
- She exudes confidence and authority, both on and off stage.
- Her passion for music is contagious, drawing people to her like a magnet.
- As the lead singer of Midnight Blue, she has honed her leadership skills, inspiring her bandmates to deliver their best performances.

**Interests:**
- Music (obviously!), particularly jazz and blues
- Performance and audience connection – Toni thrives on being in front of an audience and sharing her music with others.
- Songwriting – despite being well-established as a vocalist, Toni has a secret love for writing original songs and exploring new sonic territories.
- Travel – the city's underground music scene is always full of fresh faces and new inspiration, but when she can, Toni loves to explore beyond its boundaries, immersing herself in other cultures and musical traditions.

Toni Aber's extensive experience, boundless creativity, and unwavering passion for her craft have solidified her status as a jazz icon.
