- Eye Color: Warm, golden brown
- Hair Color: Rich, dark auburn

Personality:
Toni Müller is an assertive yet empathetic individual known for her strong sense of self. She values functionality and style equally in her life and career as a fashion designer. Her demanding nature stems from high expectations she has set for herself, but beneath this façade lies a caring person who genuinely cares about those around her.

Interests:
- Fashion design: Toni's passion is creating beautiful garments that exude modern elegance while honoring the essence of vintage styles.
- High-end fashion industry: As a renowned designer, she spends much of her time keeping up with the latest trends and collaborating with other designers.
- Art and history: Her collection often draws inspiration from classic art movements, historical periods, and cultural influences.
