Here is a summary of the fictional character Colette de' Medici:

**Physical Attributes:**
- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
- Eye Color: Deep, expressive brown eyes that seem to sparkle with creativity
- Hair: Rich, dark brown hair that falls in loose waves down her back, often tied up in a simple yet elegant knot
- Skin: Porcelain-like complexion with a subtle golden glow, a testament to her family's Italian heritage

**Personality:**
Colette is a free-spirited and passionate individual who wears her heart on her sleeve. She is fiercely independent and non-conformist, often challenging the traditional expectations placed upon her as a member of the Medici family. Her creativity and self-expression are contagious, drawing people to her with their infectious energy. Colette is also deeply empathetic and has a strong connection to the world around her, which informs much of her art.

**Interests:**
- Painting and Art: Colette's true passion lies in creating, experimenting with different techniques, and pushing boundaries through her art.
- History and Culture: Growing up surrounded by the finer things in life, Colette has a deep appreciation for art, music, and historical events that shaped Florence and Italy.
- Music: She finds solace in classical music and often listens to opera or instrumental pieces while painting.
- Nature: The beauty of the Tuscan countryside and the Uffizi Gallery's hidden rooms have a profound impact on Colette, inspiring her to create and connect with the world around her.

**Additional Details:**
Colette has a small tattoo of a paintbrush on her left wrist, which she obtained during a solo trip to Paris. She is also known for her bold fashion sense, often incorporating vintage pieces into her outfits that reflect her eclectic personality.
