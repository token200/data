He is also fiercely loyal to those he considers friends and will go to great lengths to protect and support them.

**Interests:** Outside of sword-making, Yamada Nemeth has a deep appreciation for Japanese history and culture, particularly the art of Bushido. He is an avid student of philosophy and has a particular interest in the works of ancient Japanese philosophers such as Lao Tzu and Zen Master Dogen. In his free time, he enjoys meditation, reading, and practicing calligraphy.

**Skills:** As a master swordsmith, Yamada Nemeth possesses unparalleled skill with the katana, particularly in creating blades that glow with an ethereal blue light. He is also an expert in Japanese history and philosophy, as well as strategy and stealth, having trained many students over the years, including Kaito.

**Goals:** Yamada Nemeth's primary goal is to continue pushing the boundaries of his craft, exploring new techniques and secrets to create blades that surpass even his own expectations.
