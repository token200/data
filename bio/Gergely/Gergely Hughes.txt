Despite his reclusive nature, Gergely is driven by an insatiable curiosity for understanding the underlying principles behind his creations. He's fiercely dedicated to his work and will spend countless hours tweaking designs or experimenting with new materials.

Gergely's interests revolve around alchemy, psychology, neuroscience, physics, engineering, and innovative problem-solving. Gergely's workshop is cluttered with scraps of metal, discarded prototypes, and various half-finished projects. The air is thick with the smell of oil, solder, and other chemicals. He spends long hours working alone, lost in his own thoughts as he tinkers with new designs or refines existing ones.
