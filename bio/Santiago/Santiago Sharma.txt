Here is a summary of the fictional character Santiago Sharma:

**Physical Attributes:** Santiago is a 25-year-old man with an athletic build. He stands at approximately 5'10" (178 cm) tall and has dark brown eyes that sparkle with curiosity. His hair is dark brown, often messy and unkempt, framing his chiseled features.

**Personality:** Behind his charming smile and quick wit lies a complex individual with a deep sense of wanderlust and longing for adventure. Santiago is drawn to the unknown, the unexplored, and the unconventional, which sets him apart from others. He is fiercely independent and values his freedom above all else. At the same time, he has a strong desire for connection and belonging, which led him to form a deep bond with Don Pedro.

**Interests:** Santiago is passionate about writing, particularly travelogues and articles about mysticism and ancient cultures. His latest project revolves around exploring the mystical cities of Mexico, where he hopes to uncover hidden histories and secrets. He is also drawn to spiritual practices and has participated in ancient rituals during his time with Don Pedro. When not traveling or writing, Santiago enjoys sipping mezcal, reading about mythology, and practicing yoga.

**Other Details:** As a freelance writer, Santiago values flexibility and freedom above all else. His experiences have made him more introspective and self-aware, allowing him to see the world through new eyes. He continues to be drawn to the unknown and is always seeking new horizons, but he knows that a part of himself will forever remain in Mexico, where ancient mysteries wait to be uncovered.
