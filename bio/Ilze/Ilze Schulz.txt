Here is a summary of the fictional character Ilze Schulz:

Ilze Schulz is a dedicated and driven historian specializing in Latvian culture. She has a passion for uncovering the hidden secrets of Riga's rich history, which motivates her to spend countless hours researching and pouring over dusty archives.

Physical Attributes:
- Height: 5'8" (173 cm)
- Eye Color: Bright blue
- Hair Color: Dark brown, often styled in a neat bob that frames her heart-shaped face

Personality Traits:
- Ilze is an introverted yet fiercely determined individual who can come across as reserved or standoffish to those who don't know her. However, once you gain her trust, she reveals a sharp intellect and an infectious enthusiasm for history.
- Her love for Latvian culture runs deep, instilling in her a strong sense of pride and belonging among her people.

Interests:
- History, particularly the study of Latvian colonization and its impact on local communities
- Ancient languages and scripts (she is proficient in reading and writing the Latin alphabet as well as an understanding of the ancient runes)
- Genealogy and tracing family histories to better understand cultural connections

Ilze's personal drive stems from her deep-seated desire to honor her heritage while also keeping it relevant for modern times.
