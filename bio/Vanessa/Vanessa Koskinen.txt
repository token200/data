- Eye Color: Piercing blue, with a sharp, intelligent glint
- Hair: Dark brown, styled in loose waves that often escape her ponytail
- Build: Slender yet athletic, with toned arms and shoulders honed from years of rock climbing and archaeological expeditions

- Warm and charismatic, with a quick smile that puts even the most skeptical individuals at ease
- Strong sense of justice and compassion, driven by a desire to preserve cultural heritage and protect vulnerable communities

Interests:
- Archaeology: Vanessa's primary focus is on uncovering the secrets of Finland's ancient past, particularly in regards to the Kalevala and its mythology.
- Rock climbing and adventure sports: In her downtime, Vanessa loves to scale vertical walls and explore new outdoor destinations, drawing parallels between the physical challenges she faces in the field and the mental toughness required for archaeological research.
- Linguistics and cultural studies: Vanessa has a deep appreciation for the Finnish language and culture, often incorporating linguistic and historical insights into her research and interpretation of findings.
- Philosophy and spirituality: As she delves deeper into the mysteries of the Amulet of the North, Vanessa begins to explore the philosophical and spiritual dimensions of the artifact's power and significance.

Background:
Vanessa Koskinen is a renowned archaeologist with years of experience in Finland and beyond. Her extensive research has taken her from dusty market stalls to remote ruins, and she is widely respected for her expertise in Finnish history and culture. A warm and engaging presence, Vanessa is well-liked by her colleagues and admired by her peers, who appreciate her tireless dedication to uncovering the secrets of the past.
