As "Mireille Navratilova," she carries on the legacy of her eponymous predecessor by continuing to break down barriers for women's tennis and promote acceptance and inclusivity. Her experiences growing up as a female athlete in a male-dominated sport have instilled in her a strong sense of resilience and determination, qualities that serve her well both on and off the court.
