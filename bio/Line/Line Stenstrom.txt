Line is a creative and empathetic individual, always seeking to capture the essence of those around her. She has a keen eye for detail and is known for her tenacity in pursuit of a great story. Line is fiercely independent and values her freedom above all else, often taking on solo assignments to ensure she can work on her own terms.

Despite her tough exterior, Line has a soft spot for underdogs and those who are overlooked by society. She is drawn to people with a unique perspective or talent, as seen in her connection with Max, the young musician from Times Square.

**Interests:**

Line's primary interests revolve around photography, journalism, and storytelling. She spends countless hours honing her craft, experimenting with new techniques and styles to capture the beauty of the world around her.

Outside of work, Line is an avid traveler, always seeking out new experiences and places to explore. She has a deep appreciation for art, music, and culture, often incorporating these elements into her storytelling.

Line's passion for social justice and human rights also drives much of her creative output, as she strives to use her platform to amplify the voices of those who might otherwise go unheard.
